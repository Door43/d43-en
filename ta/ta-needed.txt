===== Editing Process on tA Pages =====

The following lists are the pages that need editing. The steps through the members of the team will be:

  - Suggested topics arrive from Translation Notes writers.
  - Page is built and tagged with status 'draft jody'.
  - Jody will add content to the pages on her list.
      - Then change the tag from 'jody' to 'kara'
  - Kara will proof the pages, making changes as required.
      - Then change the tag from 'kara' to erika'.
  - Erika will then proof the page
      - Change the tag status on the page to 'review chris'.
      - Change the status to 'review' in the chart above.
  - tA Manager will then do a final check on the page and change the status to 'publish'.
      - Add the link to the Bible chunk.
      - Remove the topic from the above table.
