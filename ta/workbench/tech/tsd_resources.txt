==== translationStudio Desktop v5 ====

The UDB and all of the translationNotes, translationWords and translationQuestions have been included in the translationStudio Desktop program.

Click on the **Check** icon that looks like a clipboard with a check mark.In the far right column there are tabs with the following words:
  * Click on the word "Notes" for the translationNotes. The words or phrases are listed as they appear in the story or in the ULB. Tap on the phrase for help in understanding how to translate into the target language. See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:resources_types]] for more information.
  * Click on the word "Words" for the translationWords. The words are listed as they appear in the story on in the ULB.Tap on the word or phrase for help in understanding how to translate the word into the target language.
  * Click on the word "Questions" for the translationQuestions. The questions are about information as it appears in the story on in the ULB.Read the question and answer it according to the translated text. Tap on the question for the expected answer. If the given answer and the expected answer are the same then the translation might be clear and accurate.
  * Click on the initials "UDB" for the Unlocked Dynamic Bible. It will open at the same spot that you are in the ULB. Read the text. Perhaps the UDB is clearer to understand for this chunk.
