====== Go and Come ======

This module answers the question: What do I do if the word "go" or "come" is confusing in a certain sentence? \\

[[Summary of 100 words or less]]

 
===== Description =====
Different languages have different ways of determining whether to use the words "go" or "come" and whether to use the words "take" or "bring."  For example when English speakers says that they are approaching a person who has called them, they say "I'm coming", while Spanish speakers would say "I'm going." 
 
**Reason this is a translation issue:** If the words "go" and "come" or "take" and "bring" are not translated in the way that is natural in your language, your readers may be confused about which direction people are moving in.



===== Examples from the Bible =====
**Yahweh said to Noah, "__Come__, you and all your household, into the ark** (Genesis 7:1)\\
In some languages, this would lead people to think that Yahweh was in the ark.

**But you will be free from my oath if you __come__ to my relatives and they will not give her to you. Then you will be free from my oath.** Gen 24:41\\
Abraham was speaking to his servant. Abraham's relatives lived far away and he wanted his servant to go to them. 

**When you have __come__ to the land that Yahweh your God gives you possess it and live in it,** (Deuteronomy 17:14) \\ 
Moses and the people were in the wilderness. They had not yet gone into the land that God was giving them.

**Behold, there came a man named Jairus, and he was one of the leaders of the synagogue. Jairus fell down at Jesus’ feet and implored him to __come__ to his house,** (Luke 8:41) \\ 
The man was not at his house when he spoke to Jesus. He wanted Jesus to go with him to his house.  
===== Translation Strategies =====
If the word used in the ULB would be natural and give the right meaning in your language, consider using it. If not, here is another option. \\ 

  - Use the word "go", "come", "take" or "bring" that would be natural in your language.

===== Examples of Translation Strategies Applied ===== 
1. Use the words that would be natural in your language. \\ 
  * **Yahweh said to Noah, "Come, you and all your household, into the ark** (Genesis 7:1)\\
    * "Yahweh said to Noah, "Go, you and all your household, into the ark**
