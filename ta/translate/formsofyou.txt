====== Forms of 'You' ======

===== Singular / Dual / Plural =====

Some languages have more than one word for ‘you’ depending on how many people are being spoken to. If they are speaking to one person, they use the ‘singular’ form of ‘you’. If they are speaking to more than one person, they use the ‘plural’ form of ‘you.’ Some languages have a ‘dual’ form for speaking to two people and some have other forms for speaking to three or four people. 

Watch the video for Singular / Dual / Plural [[http://youtu.be/cPtjzJ2Advk|computer]] or the [[http://youtu.be/AVITfDEk8nc|tablet/phone]].

For more information regarding Singular forms of you [[formsofyou-singular|click here]].

For more information regarding Dual / Plural forms of you [[formsofyou-plural|click here]].


===== Formal / Informal =====

The Formal 'You' is used when addressing someone in authority or someone that a person does not know very well. The Informal 'You' is used when talking with a peer or someone with less authority or a family member or close friend.
