===== Metadata Encoding Software =====

This module answers the question: //What software do you recommend for encoding metadata?//\\
===  ===

  * **[[http://www.mp3tag.de/en/download.html|Mp3Tag]]** - For writing and editing file tags/meta data, Mp3Tag is our recommendation for and intuitive, effective free program that works for mp3 and mp4 files. Lacking in instructions and support. See instructions in Guides.
