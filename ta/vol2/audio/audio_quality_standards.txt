===== Audio Quality Standards =====

**This page answers the question:** ////What are the unfoldingWord standards for audio quality?////

===  ===

Our goal, through collaborative effort of the global Church, is to provide accurate, high-quality, audio recording of Biblical content with listening appeal and usability that will last a lifetime. Rather than being production-focused, we are striving for simplicity and excellence that faithfully communicates the message of His Word and His love for all people, languages and cultures.
