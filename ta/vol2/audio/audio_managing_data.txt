===== Folder and File Management =====

**This page answers the question:** ////Is there a good way to manage folders and files?////

====  ====

Handling files and folders properly is one of the important, detailed tasks in recording and distribution projects. These files represent the incredible amount of work and investment contributed by many people and much care must be given to their naming, organization, storage, backup and formatting for maximum distribution potential according to the diverse needs of listeners. In this module we will discuss key concepts and principles of file management.
