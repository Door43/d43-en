===== Format =====

**This page answers the question:** ////What is the format standard for unfoldingWord audio?////

==  ==

The following are unfoldingWord format standards for recording, uploading/archiving and distribution.

====Recording====

  * WAV, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono


====door43 upload and/or archiving====

  * WAV, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono (high quality, universal standard)
  * FLAC, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono (high quality, do not use without a frame/verse identification alternative solution to embedding markers)
  * MP3, 44.1kHz, 128kbps, mono (low quality, do not use without a frame/verse identification alternative solution to embedding markers)


====Distribution====

  * MP3, 44.1kHz, 128kbps, mono (high quality download)
  * MP3, 44.1kHz, 64kbps, mono (mid quality download)
  * MP3, 22kHz, 32kbps, mono (low quality download)
