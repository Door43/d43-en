===== The Team =====

**This page answers the question:** ////What is involved in preparring a recording team?////

===  ===

Before a recording project can begin, a recording team needs to be formed. There are four essential roles of responsibility in a recording project: the recordist, the narrator, the checker and the project coordinator. While the roles of recordist, narrator and checker are dedicated roles, The responsibilities of project coordinator may be handled by one of the team members or shared by team members.   

Let's look at each of the roles.
