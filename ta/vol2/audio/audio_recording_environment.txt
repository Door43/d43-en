===== The Recording Environment =====

This module answers the question: //What is a recording environment?//\\
====  ====

The recording environment is the physical space where are recording takes place, including all the non-spatial factors and attributes that make up the environment such as climate conditions, ambient noise, human activities, etc.

There are two general recording environments, studio and field, and we will identify some the characteristics of each and discuss some of their advantages and disadvantages in this module.
