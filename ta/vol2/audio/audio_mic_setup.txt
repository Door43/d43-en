===== Mic Setup =====

//This page answers the question:// //How do I properly setup a mic?//

Setting up the microphone properly is a crucial step in the recording process. There is probably no single factor in recording that makes a greater impact on the result of the recording and the amount of work (and degree of difficulty) that will be invested in editing and producing the audio recording.

Setting up a mic is not a difficult task. It is simply a task that requires a small amount of patience and knowing what to do. There are five steps in achieving and maintaining a properly setup mic:

  * Activating the mic
  * Positioning the mic
  * Setting the gain level
  * Ensuring quality signal vs noise ratio with low noise floor
  * Fine-tuning placement with good mic technique by the narrator
