==== Introduction to Resources ====

unfoldingWord has designed several resources to help you as you translate the Bible or Open Bible Stories. All of these resources are available for all projects in Door43, and most are available in translationStudio.

The resources are:
  * Open Bible Stories
  * The Unlocked Literal Bible (ULB)
  * The Unlocked Dynamic Bible (UDB)
  * translationWords
  * translationNotes
  * translationQuestions
  * translationAcademy

=== Things to Know ===

  * Open Bible Stories is a collection of 50 stories from the Bible with pictures that give a very brief overview of its message.
  * Open Bible Stories has its own set of translationNotes and translationQuestions.
  * The ULB puts the form of the original languages of the Bible into modern English to show the structure and style of the original.
  * The UDB is a translation that seeks to present the meaning of the Bible in a way that is simple and easy to understand.
  * The translationWords are important terms that are taken from the OBS and ULB.
  * The translationNotes for the Bible are explanations of the text of the ULB.
  * translationWords are important words to know and should be translated accurately.
  * translationNotes provide additional information on ways to translate into your language clearly and naturally.
  * translationQuestions is a set of questions with answers that you can use to check the translation with others who speak your language.
  * translationAcademy contains information about how to translate, how to check translations, and how to use the unfoldingWord translation tools.
