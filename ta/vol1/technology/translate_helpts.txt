==== Use the Translation Notes in translationStudio ====

All of the Translation Notes and Important Terms have been included in the translationStudio mobile app.
  * As you are reading the source language, swipe from right to left to see the pane that has the Translation Notes and Important Terms for that frame.
  * Choose the "Important Terms" tab. Tap with your finger on one of the Important Terms that is there. Another window will open up with an explanation of that Important Term.
  * Or Choose the Translation Notes tab and read the Notes for that section of text.
  *When you are done with the Translation Notes and Important Terms, swipe from left to right to make the pane go away.
  * Continue with your translation.
