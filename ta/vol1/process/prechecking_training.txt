==== Before Checking ====

It is recommended that you consult the [[en:ta:vol1:checking:toc_checkvol1_2l]] frequently as you check this content. Before you start checking, we recommend that you start working your way through the Checking Manual until you understand what is required at each level. As you work through the checking process, you will need to consult the Checking Manual frequently.
