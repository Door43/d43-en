===== Setting Up a Word Processor =====

**This page answers the question:** //How do I set up a Word Processor?//

In order to understand this topic, it would be good to read:
  * //[[en:ta:vol1:process:platforms|Selecting a Platform]]//

==== Obtaining the Source Text for Open Bible Stories ====

  - Go to https://door43.org/obs-template to get Open Bible Stories Word Template
  - Select your source language (the language from which you will translate) and click "Save". (Note: If you click the "Add all images in the file", the download size will increase greatly.) **The template will download as a docx file.**
  - Open this template in your word processor of choice. 

==== Obtaining translationHelps for Open Bible Stories ====

  * Open Bible Stories translationHelps (translationNotes, translationWords, translationQuestions) is not currently supported for translation in a Word Processor.

==== Obtaining the Source Text & Helps for the Bible ====

  * The Bible and its translationHelps are not currently supported for translation in a Word Processor.
