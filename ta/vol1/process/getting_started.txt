==== How to Get Started ====

Translation is a complicated task and takes organization and a plan. There are many steps that need to be taken to take a translation project from a good idea to completed, checked, distributed, and in use. Here are some of the steps that will need to be taken.

  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:setup_team]]** - Organizing a team of leaders, translators, checkers, and technical people is essential to a successful translation project
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:platforms]]** - Selecting a translation platform where the translation will be written
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:pretranslation_training]]** - Learning good translation theory, principles, and methodology
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:upload_merge]]** - Getting your translation online where it can be published
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:prechecking_training]]** - Learning good checking theory, principles, and methodology
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:required_checking]]** - Learning how to check to Levels 1, 2, and 3
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:publishing_process]]** - Learning how to publish your translation to unfoldingWord
  * **[[en:ta:vol1:process:intro_publishing]]** - Learning how to distribute content so the church can use it
