===== Translating in Door43 =====

This module answers the question: How can I translate in Door43?\\
Before you start this module have you learned about:
  * [[en:ta:vol1:process:pretranslation_training|Training Before Translation Begins]]
  * [[en:ta:vol1:process:setup_door43|Setting Up Door43]]

==== Using Translation Helps ====

  - Once the source text is set up on Door43 (for help see [[en:ta:vol1:process:setup_door43]]), then you are ready to start translating. 
    * It is strongly recommended that you consult the translationNotes and translationWords while doing your translation
  - For the best way to use translationNotes in Door43, please read [[en:ta:vol2:tech:translate_helpd43]]

==== Editing a page ====

  - Login to Door43
  - Navigate to the page you want to edit. 
  - Click on the pencil icon on the right side of the page. (If you don't see a pencil, you probably have not logged in.)
  - Change whatever is needed.
  - Be sure to save your work (see below)

==== Saving My Work ====

  - Before you close the edit page, you need to save your work. (If you close the tab or browser or navigate to another page before saving your work, the edits you made will be lost.)
  - Click the "Save" button. A revision of that page will be saved. 

==== Sharing Your Work ====

Sharing your work is easy on Door43. 

  * Online: Anybody can navigate to the pages you have worked on and see your work. 
  * Download: You may also download each page by clicking on one of the three icons at the bottom right part of the page. Downloads are available in .txt, .html, and .odt formats. These downloads can be shared with others offline.
