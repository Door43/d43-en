==== Updating tK ====

Sometimes you may need to update the keyboard.

   * Open the translationKeyboard application by tapping on the tK icon found on the Android screen.
   * Tap on the 'Update Keyboard' button.
   * Wait for the update to complete.
   * Close the tK application.
