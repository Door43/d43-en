===== Install unfoldingWord on an Android Device =====

This module answers the question: How do I install unfoldingWord on an Android device?\\
Before you start this module have you learned about:
  * [[en:ta:vol1:intro:uw_intro|The unfoldingWord Project]]

==== How to Install uW app ====

The instructions for installing unfoldingWord are below. You can also watch the __[[https://door43.org/_media/en/ta/tech/installing_translationstudio_-_android_v2.mp4|Install translationStudio]] (https://door43.org/_media/en/ta/tech/installing_translationstudio_-_android_v2.mp4)__ video to understand the process of selecting and installing an app on your device.

**Note:** Installing requires an Internet connection.

   * Tap on the **Google Play Store **  icon on the Android device. Google Play Store opens.
   * To search for the app, tap the magnifying glass at the top right of the window.
   * Tap **Search Google Play**  and type unfoldingWord (do not use spaces).
   * Tap the magnifying glass or close the keyboard.
   * When the Play Store has found the program, tap on the **uW**  icon.
   * Tap **Install**.
   * Tap **Accept**  to use the default settings of the app.
   * The app will download and then install. Once the program is installed, tap **Open**.
