===== Update unfoldingWord on an Android Device =====

This module answers the question: How do I update unfoldingWord on an Android device?\\
Before you start this module have you learned about:
  * [[en:ta:vol1:tech:uw_app|unfoldingWord Mobile App]]

==== How can I get new content? ====

To update the content of the unfoldingWord app:
  - Go to the opening screen:
  - Tap on the cloud icon found in the middle of the top light green bar.
  - An Internet connection is necessary for an update to happen.
  - There will be a moving line during the update process.
