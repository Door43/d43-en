===== Upload Your Work =====

This module answers the question: How do I upload my work?\\
Before you start this module have you learned about:
  * [[en:ta:vol1:tech:ts_share|Backup and Share Your Work]]

When you work in translationStudio, your text is saved every five minutes to your device. Your work is not saved to Door 43 until you publish it. See [[en:ta:vol1:tech:ts_publish]] for more information.
