===== Enable translationKeyboard on an Android Device =====

**This page answers the question:** //How do I enable translationKeyboard on an Android device?//

In order to understand this topic, it would be good to read:
  * //[[en:ta:vol1:tech:tk_install|Install translationKeyboard on an Android Device]]//

==== Enabling tK ====

   * Open translationKeyboard and tap the **Enable keyboard **  button at the top of the screen. This will open the Settings window on your device.
   * Select the checkbox in front of translationKeyboard and tap **OK **  to accept the notifications about this app.
   * Tap the **Home **  icon at the bottom of the screen to exit the Settings window.
