===== Find the Resources in translationStudio =====

**This page answers the question:** //How do I find Resources in translationStudio?//

In order to understand this topic, it would be good to read:
  * //[[en:ta:vol1:tech:ts_create|Create and Edit Text]]//
  * //[[en:ta:vol1:translate:resources_intro|Introduction to Resources]]//

==== translationStudio v2.3 ====

  * When editing - click on the word 'Resources' on the top bar.
  * Tap the word 'Notes' for the translationNotes.
  * Tap the word 'Words' for the translationWords. 

==== translationStudio v3 ====

  * Click on the icon to view the panel.
  * Choose the heading of 'Terms' for links to the information about translationWords.
  * Choose the heading of 'Notes' for the translationNotes.
