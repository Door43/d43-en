====== oak ======

===== Definition: =====

An oak is a tall tree with a large trunk and wide spreading branches that provide plenty of shade.

  * Oak trees have strong, hard wood that was used to build ships and to make farming plows, oxen yokes and walking sticks for elderly people.
  * The seed of an oak tree is called an acorn.
  * The trunk of certain oak trees could be as wide around as 6 meters.
  * <font 14.399999618530273px line-height: 15.680000305175781px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Oak trees were symbolic of long life and had other spiritual meanings. In th Bible, they were often associated with holy places.</font>
