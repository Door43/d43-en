====== oak ======

===== Definition: =====

An oak is a tall tree with a big trunk and was often associated with sacred places in the Old Testament.

  * An oak tree has full green leaves that spread out widely.
  * Oak is considered a strong, hard wood.  This attribute is likely connected to its religious significance.
  * The seed of an oak tree is called an acorn.
