====== profit, profitable ======

===== Definition: =====

Profit is gaining in addition to what one already owns. If something is profitable, it is expected to add wealth and abundance to the person who does it.

  * 2 Timothy 3:16 says that all Scripture is profitable for helping us to instruct and correct others. In this sense, 'profitable' means that it is good for us to do, because it makes us better servants for God.
  * I Corinthians 10:23 says that all things for Christians are lawful, but not all things are profitable. In this verse, 'profitable' means something that we do that makes things better.
