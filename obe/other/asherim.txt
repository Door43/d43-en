====== Asherim, Asherah ======

===== Definition: =====

Asherah was a Canaanite godess of the sea during the times of the Exodus and entry into the promised land.  Asherim were wooden idols representing Asherah, which were sometimes located at the altars of Baal.

  * The Isrealites were commaned to destroy the asherims of the Canaaites in the books of Exodus and Deuteronomy.
