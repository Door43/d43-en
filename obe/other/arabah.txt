====== Arabah ======

===== Facts: =====

Arahah is the name of a large region consisting of three distinct areas: the Jordan Valley, the Dead Sea, and the territory from the Dead Sea to the Gulf of Aqabah. This barren region was traversed by the Isrealites on their journey from Egypt.
