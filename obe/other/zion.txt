====== Zion, Mount Zion ======

===== Definition: =====

Zion was a Jebusite fort conquered by King David in the Old Testament. He named it the "City of David."

  * Zion later came to mean the temple in Jerusalem, the city of Jerusalem, and the nation of Israel.
  * In the New Testament, Zion refers to Christians, the Church and the heavenly Jerusalem.
  * Mount Zion was also Mount Moriah, the mountain to which Abraham and Isaac traveled.
