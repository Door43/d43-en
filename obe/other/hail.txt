====== hail ======

===== Facts: =====

To call out to someone, or call attention to something.

  * When Judas betrayed Jesus he called out to Him "hail master", and then kissed Him.
  * The soldiers who placed a crown of thorns on Jesus' head called out to Him "hail King of the Jews" as a mockery.
  * In the Bible hail can also refer to round, frozen pellets of ice which fall from the sky.
