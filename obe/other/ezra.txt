====== Ezra ======

===== Facts: =====

Ezra was an Israelite priest who recorded the history of Israel's return from captivity in Babylon.

  * Ezra led a group of Babylonian exiles back to Jerusalem in about 458 BC.
  * Ezra was appalled at marriages between Jews and pagan women there, had them dissolved and re-established the Law.
  * Ezra recorded Jewish history from 536 to 432 BC in the biblical books of Ezra and Nehemiah.
  * There are two other men named Ezra mentioned in the Old Testament.
