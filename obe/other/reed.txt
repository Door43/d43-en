====== reed ======

===== Facts: =====

An aquatic plant which grows at the bank of a river or stream.

  * Reeds can also be called a "rush" or a "bulrush" or "cat tail".
  * Moses was placed in a basket in the Nile River, amongst the reeds where he was found by Pharoah's daughter.
