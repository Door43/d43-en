====== Phinehas ======

===== Facts: =====

A priest descended from Aaron who was a faithful supporter of Yahweh in Northern Israel before the Exile.

  * Phinehas slaughtered an Israelite man and Midianite woman who had offended Yahweh.
  * Phinehas was overseer of the gatekeepers and on several occasions accompanied armed forces to eradicate the Medianites.
  * He was the leader of a group of ten chiefs who went to tribes East of the Jordon to charge them with building a pagan altar.
