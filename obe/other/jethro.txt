====== Jethro ======

===== Facts: =====

Jethro is the father of Zipporah, the father-in-law of Moses.

  * Moses encountered Jethro on his initial flight from Egypt when he met Jethro's seven daughters who were sheperding at a well.
  * Later, when Jethro encounted Moses with the Israelites after their exodus from Egypt he proclaimed that Yahweh was greater than all gods.  (Exodus 18:11)
