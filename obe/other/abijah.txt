====== Abijah ======

===== Facts: =====

Several different men in the Old Testament were named Abijah.

  * One Abijah was a son of Samuel who led the people of Israel at Beersheba.  He and his brother Joel were corrupt leaders and the people used this as a reason to demand Samuel to appoint a king to rule them instead.
  * During the time of King David, one of the temple priests was named Abijah.
  * Abijah was a king of Judah who reigned from 915-913 BC. He was the son of King Rehoboam,
  * One of the sons of King Jeroboam was also named Abijah. He died at a young age.
  * The chief priest who returned with Zerubbabel to Jerusalem from the Babylonian captivity was named Abijah.
