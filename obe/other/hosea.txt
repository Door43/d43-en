====== Hosea ======

===== Facts: =====

An Old Testament minor prophet of Israel in the 8th century B.C.

  * Hosea was told by God to marry a prostitute named Gomer and love her while she strayed from him. This was a picture of God's love for Israel.
  * Hosea prophesied against the Northern Kingdom, warning them to repent of their growing idolatry.
