====== bear ======

===== Facts: =====

A large mammal common in Israel during biblical times, the animal was a varitey of the brown bear.

  * The bear was used in the OT as a symbol of strength,and both human and divine anger.
  * A bear is one of the four beasts that comes out of the ocean in Daniel's vision.
