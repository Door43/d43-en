====== bear ======

===== Facts: =====

A large four legged mammal common in Israel during biblical times.

  * With it's sharp claws and large paws, the bear was used in the Old Testament as a symbol of strength.
  * A bear is one of the four beasts that comes out of the ocean in Daniel's vision.
  * While tending sheep King David fought a bear and defeated it.
