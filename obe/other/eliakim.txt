====== Eliakim ======

===== Facts: =====

Another name for Jehoiakim King of Judah

  * Jehoiakim reigned as King over Judah around 600 B.C.
  * Eliakim was also the name of the Governor of the palace under King Hezekiah from 715 to 686 B.C.
