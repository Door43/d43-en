====== bold, boldness ======

===== Definition: =====

Someone who is bold is willing to face danger with confidence. Boldness is the state of an individual who possesses the courage to do something even though it is dangerous or difficult.

  * In the New Testament, Christ's disciples were frequently bold in speaking and preaching about Him because the Jewish leaders rejected Christ.
  * The boldness of Christ's early disciples speaking the good news of His redeeming death on the cross was responsible for the growth of Christianity in Israel and nearby countries.
