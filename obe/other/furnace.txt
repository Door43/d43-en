====== furnace ======

===== Facts: =====

A large stone or brick oven used for various purposes.

  * In the Bible some furnaces were used for smelting or refining purposes, or some for vitrifying pottery.
  * Furnaces can also be those constructed for baking bread.

(See also: other)
