====== sweep, swept ======

===== Facts: =====

Used figuratively in the Bible to describe a broad, swift military movement.

  * Isaiah prophesied that the Assyrians would sweep into through into Judah.
  * Habakkuk prophesied that the Chaldeans would sweep through and collect captives as they went.
