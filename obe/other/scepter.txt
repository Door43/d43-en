====== scepter ======

===== Definition: =====

An ornamental staff or wand held by a ruler.

  * The scepter was a symbol of royalty and authority which added to the decorum of a King or Monarch.
  * In the Old Testament, God's scepter is described as a scepter of righteousness.
  * In prophecy, Christ was foretold to be a symbolic scepter that would rise out of Israel to rule over all nations.

(See also: other)
