====== Geshur ======

===== Definition: =====

Geshur was a small kingdom located between Israel and Aram during the time of King David.

  * David sealed an alliance with Geshur by marrying Maacah, the daughter of its king.
  * Maacah bore David a son, Absalom.
  * Geshur may have been eventually assimilated into the kingdom of Damascus.
