====== House of David ======

===== Facts: =====
The "House of David" is another way of speaking about the bloodline, or lineage of King David. 

  * David's son Solomon was the first descendant in the "House of David."
  * Another way of saying this is "the descendants of David."
  * Because Jesus was from the lineage of David he was part of the "House of David."
