====== Rimmon ======

===== Facts: =====

The name of several Biblical locations.

  * A Levitical city of Zebulun mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6.
  * A town in the southern portion of Judah mentioned in Joshua 15.
  * Rimmon-Parez was an unknown location in the Judean wilderness.
  * A rock cliff mentioned in Joshua 20.
