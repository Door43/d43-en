====== loins ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "loins" refers to the part of the body of an animal or person that is between the lower ribs and the hip bones.

  * The idiom "gird up the loins" refers to preparing to work hard. It comes from the custom of tucking the bottom of one's robe into a belt in order to move with ease.
  * The loins are referred to as a source of strength.
  * When referring to human beings, this term is often translated as "waist."
  * The term "loins" is also often used to refer to a certain part of an animal that was sacrificed or used for food.
