====== horsemen ======

===== Definition: =====

Horsemen were men who rode horses into battle.

  * In scripture, horses are usually associated with chariots rather than cavalry.
  * Israel did not maintain a large contingent of horsemen.
  * Isreal considered the horse as a pagan luxury which placed too much emphasis on power for victory rather than on Yahweh.
