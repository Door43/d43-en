====== Laban ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Old Testament Laban was the uncle and also father-in-law of Jacob about 1950 BC.

  * Jacob lived with Laban's household in Padan Aram and managed his sheep and goats as a condition of marraiage to his daughters.
  * Jacob's preference was for Laban's daughter Rachel to be his wife.
  * Laban tricked Jacob and made him marry his oldest daughter Leah first before giving Rachel to him as his wife.
