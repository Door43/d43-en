====== Hananiah ======

===== Facts: =====

Hananiah was a wise Hebrew youth who was taken into Babylon during the captivity period.

  * Hananiah was assimilated into Babylonian culture as a royal servant due to his excellent character and God-given abilities.
  * Hananiah was renamed Shadrach by the Babylonian King. He refused to eat the King's food which was prohibited by Jewish law.
  * There are other lesser known men named Hananiah in various Old Testament passages. The different men must be kept separated by larger context through specific events.
