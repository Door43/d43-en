====== Samson ======

===== Facts: =====

Samson was a one of the judges, or deliverers, of Israel. He was from the tribe of Dan.

  * God gave Samson amazing, superhuman strength which he used to fight against Israel's enemies the Philistines.
  * Samson was put under a vow to never cut his hair and to never drink wine or any other fermented drink.
  * When he gave into the pleading of his treacherous wife, Delilah, and told her the secret of his strength, she cut his hair so that he would lose his strength.
  
  * His Philistine wife Delilah betrayed him and cut his hair so that he lost his strength.
  * the Philistines were able to easily capture and enslave him. they gouged out his eyes.
  * But as his hair grew back, God gave him back his strength allowing him to pull down the temple of the false god Dagon, killing many Philistines.
