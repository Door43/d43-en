====== Ahijah ======

===== Facts: =====

Ahijah is the name of numerous Old Testament characters.

  * Ahijah was the name of a priest in the time of Saul and was present with Saul's army at Gibeah where he was responsible for the Ark of the Covenant.
  * Ahijah was the name of one of David's "Mighty Men."
  * Under the reign of Soloman, Ahijah, son of Shisha, was a scribe.
  * Ahijah was the name of a prophet from Shiloh who was an opponent of King Jeroboam of Israel. He prophesized the division of the kingdom in a dramatic roadside pantomine before Jeroboam during which he tore his garment into twelve pieces.
  * A man named Ahijah was father of Baasha, a king of Israel.
