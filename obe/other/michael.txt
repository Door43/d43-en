====== Michael ======

===== Facts: =====

A Hebrew name meaning "Who is like God?"

  * The name of several characters in the OldTestament including a forbear of the Levitical singer, Asaph, a Manasseh tribesman who deserted to David at Ziklag, one of the sons of King Jehoshaphat of Judah, and the father of Zebadiah who had accompanied Ezra out of Babylon.
  * A heavenly archangel mentioned in both Testaments who was portrayed as the patron angel of Israel. He led Israel against the Persians as well as leading the armies of Israel in the final battle against the forces of evil according to Daniel.  He also sometimes is depicted as an intercessor for the people before God.  He is one of the priviledged angels who alowed to stand beside the throne of God.
