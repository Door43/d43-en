====== shield ======

===== Definition: =====

A shield is a protective covering worn on one's arm while in battle. Shields take a variety of forms, but often are circular or oval in shape, and constructed of sturdy materials to withstand an enemy attack by arrow or sword.

  * Metaphorically, God is said to be the shield of the believer.
  * Faith is also the believer's shield, allowing him or her to withstand the attacks of the Devil.

(See also: other)
