 ====== bow and arrow ======

===== Definition: =====

A bow is a curved stick, bone or antler strung with a cord used by an archer to shoot shafts called arrows at an enemy or prey.

  * Bow and arrow were commonly the weapon  used by nomads, hunters, and warriors.
  * The term "arrow" was often used as a symbol of violence or divine judgment.
