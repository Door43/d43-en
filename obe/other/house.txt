====== house ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "house" is often used figuratively in the Bible to refer to a person's ancestors, descendants or other relatives. It can also refer to all the people who live together in a house, including servants.

  * Sometimes the term "house of God" or "house of Yahweh"  is used to refer to God's temple.
  * "God's house" can also refer to God's people or God's kingdom (Hebrews 3).
  * The phrase "house of Israel" refers to the entire nation of Israel and could be translated as the "people of Israel"
  * Depending on the context, different ways that "house" could be translated include, "household" or "people." or "family" or "descendants" or "temple" or "dwelling place."
