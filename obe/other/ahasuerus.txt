====== Ahasuerus ======

===== Facts: =====

Ahasuerus was a Persian king whose greatest claim to fame was his wife, Esther.

  * He ruled over 127 provinces from Ethiopia to India from his throne in Susa.
  * He also may have been known as Xerxes.
