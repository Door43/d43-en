====== Cyrus ======

===== Facts: =====

Cyrus was a Persian king who founded the mighty and expansive Persian empire in about 550 BC through military conquest.

  * Cyrus conquered Babylon which led to the release of the Israelites held in exile.
  * Cyrus' edict of tolerence led his predecessor, Darius, to allow the rebuilding of the Jerulsalem temple after the exile.
