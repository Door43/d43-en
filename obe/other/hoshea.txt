====== Hoshea ======

===== Facts: =====

Hoshea was Joshua, the successor of Moses.

  * Hosheah was the son of Nun from the tribe of Ephraim.
  * Moses changed Hoshea's name to Joshua before sending him out to spy out the land of the Canaanites.
