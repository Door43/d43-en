====== Hoshea ======

===== Facts: =====

Hoshea was the name given to several prominent men in the Old Testament.

  * The original name of Joshua, later changed to Moses.
  * One of David's officers who was over the Ephraimites,
  * The Hebrew name of the prophet Hosea.
  * The last king of Israel (732 - 724 BC) "He did what was evil in the sight of the Lord."
