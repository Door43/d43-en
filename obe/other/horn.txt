====== horn, horns ======

===== Facts: =====
Horns are permanent hard growths on the heads of many types of cattle, sheep, and goats. Horns are also another name for a trumpet or other type of instrument blown with a person's mouth.
 
Text
  * In the Bible animal horns were used as instruments as well as containers for liquids to store and drink from.
  * Sometimes in the Bible horns are used as a symbol of strength, power, authority, and royalty.
