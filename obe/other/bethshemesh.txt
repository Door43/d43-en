====== Beth Shemesh ======

===== Facts: =====

A city in the tribe of Judah's territory approximately 30Km West of Jerusalem.

  * Beth Shemesh was taken from the Canaanites during Joshua's conquests.
  * Beth Shemesh was a city that was set aside as a dwelling for priests from the tribe of Levi.
  * When the Philistines returned the captured Ark of God, Beth Shemesh is the first city where it stopped.
