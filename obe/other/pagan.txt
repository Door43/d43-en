====== pagan ======

===== Definition: =====

A non-christian belief system characterized by the worship of nature and hedonistic tendencies .

  * Pagans have practiced such rituals as sexually-based worship and human sacrifice.
  * Pagan belief systems are often polytheistic, pantheistic and immoral.
