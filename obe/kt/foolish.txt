====== foolish ======

===== Definition: =====

A person who is "foolish" is senseless or unwise in his behavior and decisions. It is the adjective form of the noun "fool", which refers to a person who often makes bad or wrong choices.

  * A "fool" or foolish person is someone who does not obey God; this is often contrasted to the wise person, who obeys God.
  * The book of Proverbs in the Old Testament gives many descriptions of what a fool is like.
  * In the Psalms, David describes a fool as a person who does not believe in God, who ignores all the evidence of God in his creation.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Foolish can be translated as "to lack understanding" or "senseless."  Many words similar to "foolish" have harsh negative connotations, but "fool" does not.
