====== unjust, unjustly, injustice ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "unjust" and "unjustly" refer to treating people in an unfair and even harmful manner.

  * An "injustice" is something that is done that the person did not deserve. It also refers to unfair treatment of people.
  * Injustice means that some people are treated badly while others are treated well.
  * Someone who is acting in an unjust way is being "partial" or "prejudiced" because he is not treating people equally well.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "unjust" could also be translated as "unfair" or "partial" or "prejudiced," depending on the context.
  * Sometimes "unjust" can be translated in a similar way to "unrighteous."
  * The phrase "the unjust" could be translated as "the unjust ones" or "unjust people" or "people who treat others unfairly" or "unrighteous people" or "people who disobey God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:nominaladjective]])
  * The term "unjustly" could be translated as, "in an unfair manner" or "wrongly" or "unfairly" or "rightly."
  * Ways to translate "injustice" could include, "wrong treatment" or "unfair treatment" or "acting unfairly."
