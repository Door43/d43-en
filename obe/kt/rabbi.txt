====== Rabbi ======

===== Definition: =====

A term of respect and honor usually conveyed upon a teacher of the Mosaic law.

  * As used in the New Testament there is no implication of an official status or title.
  * Both John the Baptist and Jesus were called "rabbi" at times by their disciples.
