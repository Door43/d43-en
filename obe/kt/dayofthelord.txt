====== day of the Lord, day of Yahweh ======

===== Description: =====

The phrases "day of Yahweh" and "day of the Lord" refer to times of judgment for Yahweh's enemies.

  * The word "day" in this phrase refers to a "time" or "occasion" that is longer than 24 hours.
  * The term "day of Yahweh" is used in the Old Testament to refer to times when Yahweh punished his enemies or his people in his wrath.
  * The term "day of the Lord" in the New Testament often refers more specifically to the time when there will be a final judgment of all who have rebelled against Yahweh and rejected Christ. The term "Lord" probably refers to the "Lord Jesus" in these contexts.
  * This final time of judgment and resurrection called the "day of the Lord" is also known as the "last day". It will begin when the Lord Jesus comes back to judge sinners, and will continue until his rule over all creation is permanently established.
