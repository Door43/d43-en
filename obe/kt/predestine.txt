====== predestine, predestined ======

===== Definition: =====

To predestine is to ascribe events to a preplanned origin in which chance is not a factor.

  * God is said to have predestined certain things in Romans 8.
  * Simply put, it is the idea that our fate is predetermined by God who knows everything.
