====== exhort, exhortation ======


===== Definition: =====


To exhort is to give a strong encouragement to do something. Usually exhortation is verbal communication to convince others to follow righteousness and not sin.


    * The New Testament encourages us to exhort each other to do right.
    * We are to exhort using gentle and kind words, not harshly or abruptly.
