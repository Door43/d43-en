====== bind, bond ======

===== Definition: =====

To bind something is to tie it or fasten it together securely. Something that is tied or joined together is a bond. This can be a literal bond such as chains or a figurative bond such as the bond of friendship between two people.

  * The term "bonds" refers to anything that binds, confines or imprisons someone. It usually refers to chains, fetters or ropes that keep a person from being free.
  * In Bible times, bonds such as ropes or chains were used to attach prisoners to the wall or floor of a stone prison.
  * A bondservant was another word for a slave.
  * The term "bond" is used figuratively to refer to something, such as sin, that controls or enslaves someone.
  * On the positive side, the terms "bind" and "bond" are also used figuratively to talk about a close relationship between people in which they support each other emotionally, spiritually and physically.
