====== confidence, confident ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "confidence" refers to being sure that something is true or certain to happen. Usually this word is translated as "hope" in English translations, with the meaning of waiting expectantly for something that is sure to happen.

  * Being "confident" means believing in God's promises and acting with the assurance that God will do what he has said. This term can also have the meaning of acting boldly and courageously.
  * Often the term "confidence" refers especially to the certainty that all believers in Jesus have of someday being be with God forever in heaven.
  * The phrase, "have confidence in God" means to fully expect to receive and experience what God has promised.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "confident" could be translated as "assured" or "very sure."
  * The phrase "be confident" could also be translated as "trust completely" or "be completely sure about" or "know without a doubt that something will happen."
  * Depending on the context, ways to translate "confidence" could include, "complete assurance" or "sure expectation" or "faithful expectation."
