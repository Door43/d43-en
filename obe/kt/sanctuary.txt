====== sanctuary ======

===== Definition: =====

A sanctuary is an earthly or heavenly sacred or holy place.

  * In the Old Testament the reference is usually to a tabernacle, the temple or even Jerusalem itself.
  * In the New Testament the reference is more broadly used to include both pagan and Jewish temples.
  * In the NT the word is also personalizd to denote the Body of Christ as well as the spirit-filled life of the believer.
