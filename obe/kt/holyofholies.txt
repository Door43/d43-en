====== holy of holies, most holy place ======

===== Definition: =====

Inside the Jewish temple the holy of holies was the innermost chamber where the Ark of God and His presence dwelled.

  * Israel's official high priest was the only person allowed into the holy of holies, once per year to administer the blood of the sacrifice on Israel's day of atonement.
  * The holy of holies represents the inner access that Jesus has to the presence of God where Jesus' own blood is the only acceptable sacrifice for mankind's sin.
