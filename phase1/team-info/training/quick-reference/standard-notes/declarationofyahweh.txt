====== declaration of Yahweh, declaration of the Lord ======

===== Description =====

This phrase occurs often in prophesy, and needs to be distinguished from "the word of Yahweh." The phrase appears in several forms including:

  * declaration of Yahweh
  * declaration of Yahweh of Hosts
  * declaration of the Lord
  * declaration of the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts
  * Yahweh's declaration

===== Standard Notes =====

The first occurrence in a book should include the translation alternates, and following occurrences in the same book can refer back to the original explanation:

  * **declaration of Yahweh of Hosts** - "what Yahweh of Hosts has declared" or "what Yahweh of Hosts has solemnly said"
  * **declaration of Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:xx:xx|x:x]].

  * **declaration of Yahweh** - "what Yahweh has declared" or "what Yahweh has solemnly said"
  * **declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:xx:xx|x:x]].

  * **declaration of the Lord** - "what the Lord has declared" or "what the Lord has solemnly said"
  * **declaration of the Lord** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:xx:xx|x:x]].

  * **declaration of the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts** - "what the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts has declared" or "what the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts has solemnly said"
  * **declaration of the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:xx:xx|x:x]].
