====== Son of [something] ======

===== Definition: =====

"Son of" is an idiom used to describe a person's behavior or character.


===== Standard Notes: =====

An example from [[en:bible:notes:act:04:36|Acts 4:36]]
  * **Son of Encouragement** - The apostles used this name to show that Joseph was a person who encouraged others. "Son of" is an idiom used to describe a person's behavior or character. AT: "Encourager" or "one who encourages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
