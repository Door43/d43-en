====== joy ======


===== Definition: =====

Joy is a feeling of delight or deep satisfaction that comes from recognizing that something is very good or from a sense of well-being.

  * Joy can be translated as: gladness, happiness, or delight. 
  * Joyful can describe how a person feels and can be translated as: glad, happy, or delighted.
  * Having joy does not depend on pleasant circumstances. God can give people joy even when very difficult things are happening in their lives.ning.
