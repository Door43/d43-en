===== Ishmael Was Born [05-02] =====

So** Abram married Hagar**. Hagar had a baby boy, and Abram named him **Ishmael**. But **Sarai became jealous of Hagar**. When Ishmael was thirteen years old, **God** again spoke to Abram.
