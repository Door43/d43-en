\v 1 Thank Yahweh, because he does good things for us;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 2 Thank God, the one who is greater than all other gods;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 3 Thank the Lord who is greater than all other lords;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\b
\q1
\v 4 He is the only one who performs great miracles;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 5 He is the one who by being very wise created the heavens;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 6 He is the one who caused the ground to rise up above the deep waters;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 7 He is the one who created great lights in the sky;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 8 He created the sun to shine in the daytime;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 9 He created the moon and stars to shine during the nighttime;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\b
\q1
\v 10 He is the one who killed the firstborn males in Egypt;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 11 He led the Israelite people out of Egypt;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 12 With his strong hand he led them out;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\b
\q1
\v 13 He is the one who caused the Red Sea to divide;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 14 He enabled the Israelite people to walk through it on dry land;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 15 But he caused the king of Egypt and his army to drown in it;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\b
\q1
\v 16 He is the one who led his people safely through the desert;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 17 He killed powerful kings;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 18 He killed kings who were famous;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 19 He killed Sihon, the king of the Amor people-group;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 20 He killed Og, the king of Bashan region;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 21 He gave their lands to us, his people;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 22 He gave those lands to us people of Israel, who serve him;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\b
\q1
\v 23 He is the one who did not forget about us when we were defeated by our enemies;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 24 He rescued us from our enemies;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\q1
\v 25 He is the one who gives food to all living creatures;
\q2 his faithful love for us endures forever.
\b
\q1
\v 26 So thank God, who lives in heaven, for all that,
\q2 because his faithful love for us endures forever!
