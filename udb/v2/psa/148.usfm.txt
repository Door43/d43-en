\q1 Praise him, you who are in heaven;
\q2 praise him from way up in the sky!

\v 3 Sun and moon, you also praise him!
\q2 You shining stars, you praise him!

\v 4 You highest heavens, praise him!

\q2 and he commanded that they should be there forever.

\v 11 You kings on this earth and all the people that you rule,
\q2 you princes and all other rulers,

\v 12 you young men and young women,
\q2 you old people and children, everyone, praise Yahweh!

\q1
\v 13 I want them all to praise Yahweh

\q2 in order that we, his people,
