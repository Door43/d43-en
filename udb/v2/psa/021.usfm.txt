\c 21
\s1 A psalm written by David for the choir director
\b
\q1
\v 1 Yahweh, I, your king am glad because you have caused me, Or: him to be strong.
\q2 I rejoice from my, Or: his enemies.
\q1
\v 2 You have given me desired
\q2 and you have not refused to do what I requested you to do.
\qr (Think about that!)
\q1
\v 3 In answer to my to succeed and prosper.
\q2 You placed a gold crown on my head.
\q1
\v 4 I to live for a long time,
\q2 and that is what you gave me life.
\q1
\v 5 I am to defeat my, Or: his enemies;
\q2 you have given me.
\q1
\v 6 You will bless me forever,
\q2 and you have caused me to be joyful in your presence.
\q1
\v 7 Yahweh, you are God Almighty,
\q2 and I trust in you.
\q1 Because you faithfully love me,
\q2 disastrous things will never happen to me.
\b
\q1
\v 8 You will enable me enemies
\q2 and all those who hate me.
\q1
\v 9 When you appear, you will throw them into a fiery furnace.
\q1 Because you are angry with them, you will swallow them up;
\q2 the fire will burn them up.
\q1
\v 10 You will remove their children from this earth;
\q2 their descendants will all disappear.
\q1
\v 11 They planned to harm you,
\q2 but what they plan will never succeed.
\q1
\v 12 You will cause them to run away [IDI]
\q2 by shooting arrows at them.
\b
\q1
\v 13 Yahweh, show us that you are very strong!
\q2 When you do that, while we sing we will praise you because you are very powerful.
