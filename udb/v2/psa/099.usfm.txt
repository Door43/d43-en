\c 99
\q1
\v 1 Yahweh is the supreme king,
\q1 so all the people-groups should tremble in his presence!
\q1 He sits on his throne in the temple above the statues of winged creatures,
\q2 so the earth should quake!
\q1
\v 2 Yahweh is a mighty king in Jerusalem;
\q2 but he is also the supreme ruler of all the people-groups.
\q2
\v 3 So they should praise him because he is very great;
\q2 and he is holy!
\b
\q1
\v 4 He is a mighty king who loves what is just, Or: right;
\q2 he has acted justly and fairly  in Israel.
\q1
\v 5 Praise Yahweh our God!
\q2 Worship him in front of the Sacred Chest in his temple ,
\q2 where he rules people.
\q2 He is holy!
\b
\q1
\v 6 Moses and Aaron were two of his priests;
\q1 Samuel also was someone who prayed to him.
\q1 Those three cried out to Yahweh to help them,
\q2 and he answered them.
\q1
\v 7 He spoke to Moses and Aaron from the cloud that was like a huge pillar;
\q2 they obeyed all the laws and commandments  that he gave to them.
\b
\q1
\v 8 Yahweh, our God, you answered your people
\q2 when they cried out to you to help them;
\q1 you are a God who forgave them for the sins that they had committed,
\q1 even though you punished them for the things that they did that are wrong.
\q1
\v 9 Praise Yahweh, our God,
\q2 and worship him at the temple on his sacred hill;
\q2 it is right to do that because Yahweh, our God, is holy!
