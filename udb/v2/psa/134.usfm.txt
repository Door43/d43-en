\c 134
\s1 A psalm written for people going up to the temple to worship
\q1
\v 1 All you people who serve Yahweh,
\q2 who stand up and serve him at night in his temple,
\q1 come and praise him!
\q1
\v 2 Lift up your hands to pray to him in the temple
\q2 and praise him!
\b
\q1
\v 3 And I hope that Yahweh, who created heaven and the earth,
\q2 will bless you from where he lives in the temple on Zion Hill!
