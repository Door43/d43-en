\c 70
\s1 A psalm written by David for the choir director, asking God to help David
\q1
\v 1 God, please save me!
\q2 Yahweh, come quickly to help me!
\q1
\v 2 Humble those who are happy about my troubles.
\q2 Chase away those who are trying to kill me.
\q1
\v 3 I hope that you will cause them to become dismayed and ashamed
\q2 because you have defeated them.
\q1
\v 4 But I hope.
\q2 I hope,
\q1 "God is great!"
\b
\q1
\v 5 As for me, I am poor and needy ;
\q2 so God, come quickly to help me!
\q1 Yahweh, you are the one who saves and helps me,
\q2 so please come quickly!
