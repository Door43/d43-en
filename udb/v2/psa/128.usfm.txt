\c 128
\s1 A psalm written for those going up to the temple to worship
\q1
\v 1 Happy are those of you who have an awesome respect for him
\q2 and do what he wants you to do [IDI].
\q1
\v 2 You will be able to earn the money that you  need to buy food;
\q1 you will be happy and you will be prosperous.
\q1
\v 3 Your wife will be like a grapevine that bears many grapes ;
\q2 she will give birth to many children.
\q1 Your children who sit around your table
\q1 will be like a strong olive tree that has many shoots growing up around it .
\q1
\v 4 Every man who has an awesome respect for Yahweh
\q2 will be blessed like that.
\b
\q1
\v 5 I wish
\q1 and that you will see the people of Jerusalem prospering every day that you live!
\q2
\v 6 I wish that you will live many years
\q2 with the result that you will have grandchildren and be able to see them.
\b
\q1 I wish that things will go well for people in Israel!
