\s1 The Israelites accepted Yahweh's agreement
\p
\v 1 Then Yahweh said to Moses. While you are still some distance from the top of the mountain, prostrate yourselves on the ground and worship me.
\v 2 Moses, I will allow you alone to come near to me. The others must not come near, and the rest of the people must not come up the mountain."
\p
\v 3 Moses went and told the people everything that Yahweh had said and all that he had commanded. The people all replied together, saying, "We will do everything that Yahweh has told us to do."
\v 4 Then Moses also set up twelve stones, one for each of the Israelite tribes.
\v 5 He also selected some young men. They burned sacrifices to Yahweh and they also sacrificed some cattle that were not burned completely, for the purpose of restoring fellowship with other people.
\v 6 Moses against the altar.
\v 7 Then he had written everything that Yahweh had commanded in the agreement that he had made, and he read it aloud, while all the people were listening. Then all the people said, "We will do all that Yahweh has told us to do. We will obey everything."

\v 8 Then Moses the agreement that Yahweh has made with you when he gave you all these commands."

\v 11 God did not harm  because of their having seen him. They saw God, and they ate and drank together! Then they all went down the mountain.
