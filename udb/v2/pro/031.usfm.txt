\s1 Wise sayings from Lemuel
\p
\v 1 These are sayings that God gave to Lemuel's mother, and which his mother taught him:
\q1
\v 2 You are my son; I gave birth to you ;
\q2 you are the son that God gave me in answer to my prayers.
\q1
\v 3 Do not exhaust all your energy having sex  with women to whom you are not married,
\q2 with women who have ruined kings by having sex with them.
\b
\q1
\v 4 Lemuel, kings should not be constantly drinking wine
\q2 or greatly desire to drink other strong drinks.
\q1
\v 5 If they do that, they forget the laws that they have made,
\q2 and they do no not do what is right for poor people.
\q1
\v 6 Give strong drinks to those who are dying
\q2 and to those who are greatly distressed.
\q1
\v 7 If they drink, they will forget that they are poor,
\q2 and they will not think about their distress anymore.
\b
\q1
\v 8 Speak  to defend people who are unable to defend themselves;
\q2 speak to encourage others to do what is right for those who are helpless.
\q1
\v 9 Speak  and try to cause judges to decide matters fairly, Or: justly;
\q2 try to cause others to do for poor and needy people what should be done for them.

\q2 and because of her, he has everything that he needs .
