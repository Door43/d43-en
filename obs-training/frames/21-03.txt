====== A Prophet Like Moses [21-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-21-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]** **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:promise|promised]]** **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:moses|Moses]]** that in the future he would **raise up another [[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:prophet|prophet]] like Moses**. This was another promise about the **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]** who would come at some later time.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **raise up another prophet like Moses** - That is, “appoint another prophet like Moses” or, “cause another prophet to come who would be like Moses.”
  * **prophet like Moses** - To be like Moses, the future prophet would need to have great authority from God to lead and rescue his people.
