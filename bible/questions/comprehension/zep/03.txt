====== Zephaniah Chapter 03 ======

===== Translation Questions and Answers =====

Q? What are the princes of the violent city like?

A. The princes of the violent city are like roaring lions. [3:3]

Q? When will Yahweh dispense justice?

A. Yahweh will dispense justice from morning to morning. [3:5]

Q? What did Yahweh hope the people would do?

A. Yahweh hoped the people would fear him and accept correction. [3:7]

Q? What decision has Yahweh made about the nations?

A. Yahweh has decided to assemble the nations and pour out his anger and wrath on them. [3:8]

Q? What will Yahweh then call the people to do?

A. Yahweh will call the people to serve him standing shoulder to shoulder. [3:9]

Q? How will the remnant of Israel change after this?

A. The remnant of Israel will no longer commit injustice and speak lies. [3:13]

Q? Why should Israel sing and cheer?

A. Israel should sing and cheer because Yahweh has taken away their punishment. [3:14]

Q? Who will Yahweh rescue from those who have mistreated Israel?

A. Yahweh will rescue the lame and gather together the outcast. [3:19]

Q? Why will all the nations respect and praise Israel?

A. All the nations will respect and praise Israel when they see that Yahweh has restored them. [3:20]
