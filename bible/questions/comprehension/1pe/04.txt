====== 1 Peter Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? With what did Peter command the foreigners, the chosen ones, to arm themselves?

A. He commanded them to arm themselves with the same intention that Christ had when he suffered in the flesh [4:1].

Q? Why did the Gentiles speak evil about the foreigners, the chosen ones?

A. They spoke even about the foreigners, the chosen ones, because they did not participate in sensuality, passion, drunkenness, carousings, wild parties, and in abominable idolatries like the Gentiles [4:3-4].

Q? Who is God ready to judge?

A. God is ready to judge both the living and the dead [4:5].

Q? Why were the foreigners, the chosen ones, to be of sound mind and have fervent love for one another?

A. They were to do those things because the end of all things was coming, and for the sake of their prayers [4:7].

Q? Why were each of the foreigners, the chosen ones, to use the gifts they had received to serve one another?

A. They were to use their gifts so that God may be glorified through Jesus Christ [4:10-11].

Q? Why were the foreigners, the chosen ones, told to rejoice if they experienced the sufferings of Christ or were insulted for the name of Christ?

A. Because they were blessed if they were insulted [4:12-14].

Q? Why were the foreigners, the chosen ones, not to suffer as a murderer, a thief, an evildoer, or a meddler?

A. Because it was time for judgment to begin with the household of God [4:15-17].

Q? Why should the ungodly person and the sinner obey God's gospel?

A. Because even the righteous man is saved through difficulties [4:17-18].

Q? How were those who suffered according to the will of God to act?

A. They were to entrust their souls to the faithful Creator while they did good [4:19].
