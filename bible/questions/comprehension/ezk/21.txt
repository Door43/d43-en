====== Ezekiel Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Against whom did Yahweh tell Ezekiel to speak?

A. Yahweh told Ezekiel to speak against the sanctuaries; against the land of Israel [21:2].

Q? Who did Yahweh say he would cut off from the land of Israel?

A. Yahweh said he would cut off both the righteous and wicked person from the land of Israel [21:3-4].

Q? What did Yahweh tell Ezekiel to do before the eyes of the people?

A. Yahweh told Ezekiel to groan before the eyes of the people [21:6].

Q? Why did Yahweh tell Ezekiel to act this way before the eyes of the people?

A. Yahweh told Ezekiel to act this way to show what it will be like for them when Yahweh brings the sword against them [21:7].

Q? Why did Yahweh say the sword would be sharpened and polished?

A. The sword would be sharpened and polished in order to engage in great slaughter [21:9-10].

Q? On whom has that sword come?

A. That sword has come on Yahweh's people and the leaders of Israel [21:12].

Q? What will come to rest after the great slaughter?

A. After the great slaughter, Yahweh's fury will come to rest [21:17].

Q? What were the destinations of the two roads assigned for the king of Babylon?

A. The two destinations were Rabbah of the Ammonites, and Judah and Jerusalem [21:20].

Q? How would the king of Babylon determine which road to take?

A. The king of Babylon would obtain a prophetic message from divination to determine which road to take [21:21].

Q? What would the king of Babylon accuse Israel of in order to beseige them?

A. The king of Babylon would accuse Israel of violating their treaty in order to beseige them [21:23].

Q? What did Yahweh tell the wicked ruler of Israel?

A. Yahweh told the wicked ruler of Israel to remove his turban and crown, because the crown would no longer exist [21:25-27].

Q? When did Yahweh say he would restore the crown in Israel?

A. Yahweh said he would restore the crown in Israel when one comes who has a right to it [21:27].

Q? What did Yahweh say would happen to the Ammonites?

A. Yahweh said that the Ammonites would be slaughtered and would not be remembered [21:28,32].
