====== Ezekiel Chapter 13 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? From where were the prophets of Israel getting their prophesies?

A. The prophets in Israel were getting their prophecies from their own minds and spirits [13:2-3].

Q? What had the false prophets failed to do?

A. The false prophets had failed to repair the breaks in the wall around the house of Israel [13:5].

Q? What were the false prophets saying, even though Yahweh had not sent them?

A. The false prophets were saying, "Such and such is Yahweh's declaration," even though Yahweh had not sent them [13:6-7].

Q? What declaration did Yahweh make against the false prophets of Israel?

A. Yahweh declared that his hand was against the false prophets, and they would not be enrolled in the record of the house of Israel [13:8-9].

Q? What message were the false prophets saying to the people?

A. The false prophets were saying, "Peace!" where there was no peace [13:10,16].

Q? What did Yahweh say he would do with the wall the false prophets were building?

A. Yahweh said he would break down the wall the false prophets were building [13:10-14].

Q? What will the people know when Yahweh annihilates the false prophets?

A. The people will know that the Lord is Yahweh [13:14].

Q? What were the women false prophets doing to the lives of the people?

A. The women false prophets were killing people who should live, and preserving the lives of those who should not [13:18-19].

Q? What were the women false prophets using to ensnare and trap the people?

A. The women false prophets were using magic charms to ensnare and trap the people [13:18,20].

Q? What did Yahweh say he would do for his people who are trapped?

A. Yahweh said that he would let his people who are trapped go free [13:20-21].

Q? What are the false prophets doing to the righteous and the wicked person?

A. The false prophets are discouraging the righteous and encouraging the wicked person [13:22].

Q? What did Yahweh say the false prophets would no longer have?

A. Yahweh said the false prophets would no longer have false visions or continue to make false predictions [13:23].
