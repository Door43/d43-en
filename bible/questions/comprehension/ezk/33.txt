====== Ezekiel Chapter 33 ======

===== Translation Questions and Answers =====

Q? What does the watchman do for the people of the land?

A. The watchman looks for the sword coming on the land, and blows his horn to warn the people. [32:3]

Q? What happens if the people do not pay attention to the watchman?

A. If the people do not pay attention, the sword kills the people and each one's blood is on his own head. [32:4-5]

Q? What happens to the watchman if he does not warn the people?

A. If the watchman does not warn the people, his blood will be required by Yahweh. [32:6]

Q? Who did Yahweh make a watchman for the house of Israel?

A. Yahweh made Ezekiel a watchman for the house of Israel. [32:7]

Q? In what does Yahweh say he does not delight?

A. Yahweh says he does not delight in the death of the wicked. [32:11]

Q? What does Yahweh call the wicked to do?

A. Yahweh calls the wicked to repent so they will live. [32:11]

Q? What does Yahweh say will happen to a righteous person who then commits injustice?

A. Yahweh says the righteous person who then commits injustice will die in the wickedness that he has committed. [32:12-13]

Q? What does Yahweh say will happen to a wicked person who repents and does what is just and right?

A. Yahweh says the wicked person who repents and does what is just and right will surely live. [32:14-16]

Q? According to Yahweh, whose ways are not fair?

A. According to Yahweh, the ways of the people of Israel are not fair. [32:17]

Q? How does Yahweh say he will judge each person of the house of Israel?

A. Yahweh says he will judge each person according to his way. [32:20]

Q? What message did the fugitive bring to Ezekiel?

A. The fugitive brought the message that the city of Jerusalem had been captured. [32:21]

Q? What were the people of Israel saying about the land that Abraham inherited?

A. The people of Israel were saying the land that Abraham inherited was their possession. [32:24]

Q? Why does Yahweh question whether or not the people of Israel should really possess the land?

A. Yahweh questions whether or not they should possess the land because they had depended on their swords and had done disgusting things. [32:25-26]

Q? What does Yahweh say he will do to the land because of the things the people of Israel have done?

A. Yahweh says he will turn the land into a desolation and a horror because of the things the people of Israel have done. [32:28-29]

Q? What will the people of Israel do with the words of Ezekiel?

A. The people of Israel will listen to the words of Ezekiel, but they will not obey them. [32:30-32]

Q? What will the people of Israel know when everything comes to pass?

A. The people of Israel will know that a prophet has been among them when everything comes to pass. [32:33]
