====== Numbers Chapter 26 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh tell Moses and Eleazar to do after the plague?

A. Yahweh told them to count all the people of Israel from twenty years old and up, by their tribes, who were able to go to war. [26:1-2]

Q? What did Moses and Eleazar tell the people Yahweh had commanded them to do?

A. They told the people they needed to count the people from 20 years old and up, who came out of the land of Egypt. [26:4]

Q? Who was the firstborn of Israel?

A. The firstborn of Israel was Reuben. [26:5]

Q? How many men did the clans of Reuben's descendants number?

A. The clans of Reuben’s descendants numbered 43,730 men. [26:7] 

Q? Who were the sons of Eliab, descendant of Reuben, and what did his sons do?

A. Eliab's sons were Nemuel, Dathan and Abiram. Dathan and Abiram rebelled against Yahweh by following Korah who challenged Moses and Aaron. [26:9]

Q? What happened to all the followers of Korah that became a warning sign?

A. The earth swallowed them up and fire devoured 250 men. [26:10]

Q? Whose line did not die out?

A. Korah’s line did not die out. [26:11] 

Q? Who was listed next of Israel's sons, and how many did they number?

A. The clans of Simon was the next of Israel's sons; they numbered 22,200 total men. [26:12 - 14]

Q? What are the names of the clans of the descendants of Gad and the number?

A. The clans are by Zephon the Zephonites, by Haggi the Haggites, by Shuni the Shunites, by Ozni the Oznites, by Eri the Erites, by Arod the Arelites which numbered 40,500 men total for these clans. [26:15 - 18]

Q? What happened to Judah's sons Er and Onan?

A. They died in the land of Canaan. [26:19]

Q? What are the names of the clans of the other descendants of Judah and the total number?

A. The clans are by Shelah the Shelanites, by Perez the Perezites, by Zerah the Zerahites, and the descendants of Perez by Hezron the clan of Hezronites, by Hamul the clan of Hamulites which numbered 76,500 men total. [26:19 - 22]

Q? What were the names of the clans of Issachar and how many were numbered?

A. The clans of Issachar were by Tola the Tolaites, by Puvah the Punites, by Jashub the Jashubites, by Shimron the Shimronites and the total number was 64,300 men. [26:23 - 25]

Q? What were the names of the clans of Zebulun and how many were numbered?

A. The clans of Zebulun were by Sered the Seredites, by Elon the Elonites, by Jahleel the Jahleelites and they numbered 60,500 men. [26:26 - 27]

Q? What were the names of the clans of Joseph's descendants?

A. The clans of Joseph's descendants were Manasseh and Ephraim. [26:28]

Q? Who were the descendants of Manasseh?

A. The clan of the Machirites by Machir and the Gileadites by Gilead. [26:29]

Q? Who were the descendants of Gilead?

A. The clan of the Iezerites by Iezer, the clan of the Helekites by Helek, the clan of the Asrielites by Asriel, the clan of the Shechemites by Shechem, the clan of the Shemidaites by Shemida and the clan of the Hepherites by Hepher. [26:30 - 32]

Q? How many daughters did Zelophehad have although he had no sons?

A. Zelophehad son of Hepher had no sons and had five daughters who were Mahlah, Noah, Hoglah, Milcah, and Tirzah. [26:33]

Q? How many were numbered from the clans of Manasseh?

A. The men numbered 52,700 men. [26:34]

Q? What were the names of the clans of Ephraim's descendants and what was their number?

A. The clans of Ephraim's descendants were the clan of Shuthelathites by Shuthelah, the clan of the Becherites by Becher, the clan of the Tahannites by Tahan, the descendants of Shuthelah were Eranites by Eran which numbered 32,500 men.[26:35 - 37]

Q? What were the clans of Benjamin's descendants?

A. These are the clans of Benjamin's descendants, Belaites by Bela, Ashbelites by Ashbel, Ahiramites by Ahiram, Shuphammites by Shephupham, Huphamites by Hupham and Bela's sons clans were Ardites by Ard, Naamites by Naaman and they numbered 45,600 men. [26:38 - 41]

Q? What were the names of the clans of the descendants of Dan and what were their numbers?

A. The descendants of Dan were Shuhamites by Shuham and they numbered 64,400 men. [26:42 - 43]

Q? What are the clans names of the descendants of Asher and what is their number?

A. The clans of Asher's descendants were the Imnites by Imnah, the clan of Ishvites by Ishvi, the clan of Berites by Beriah and the descendants of Beriah the clan of Heberites by Heber, the clan of MMalchielites by Malchiel and Asher's daughter Serah, which numbered 53,400 men. [26:44 - 47]

Q? What are the clans of the descendants of Naphtali and what is their number?

A. Naphtali's descendants clans names are Jahzeelites by Jahzeel, the clan of Gunites by Guni, the clan of the Jezerites by Jezer, the clan of the Shillemites by Shillem and their number was 45,400 men. [26:48 - 50]

Q? What was the complete count of the men of the people of Israel?

A. The complete count of men from among the people of Israel ws 601,730. [26:51]

Q? What did Yahweh speak to Moses about the land?

A. Yahweh said to divide the land among these men as an inheritance according to the number of their names. [26:52 - 53]

Q? How will the land be divided for their inheritance?

A. The clans who are smaller in number according to the number of men who were counted will be given a smaller inheritance and the larger clans according to the number of men who were counted will be given a larger inheritance. [26:54]

Q? What way will be used to divide the land?

A. The land will be divided by random lots from among their ancestor's tribes with the larger clans getting a larger inheritance of land and the smaller clans getting a small inheritance of land. [26:55 - 56]

Q? What clans were the Levites?

A. These were the clans: the Gershonites by Gershon, the Kohathites by Kohath, the Merarites by Merari, the Libnites,the Hebronites, the Mahlites, the Mushites and the Korahites.[26:58]

Q? Who were the children of Jochebed, a descendant of Levi who was born in Egypt. amd Amram?

A. Their children were Aaron, Moses, and their sister Miriam. [26:59]

Q? What sons did Aaron have?

A. Aaron's sons were Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar. [26:60]

Q? What happened to Nadab and Abihu when they offered unacceptabble fire before Yahweh?

A. Nadab and Abihu died when they offered unacceptable fire. [26:61]

Q? What number of males one month old and up were not counted among Israel's descendants and why?

A. 23,000 males one month old and up were not counted for they had no land inheritance among the people of Israel. [26:62]

Q? Who did Moses and Eleazar not count as they counted the people of Israel in the plains of Moab by the Jordan at Jericho?

A. There was no man counted that had been counted by Moses and Aaron when the descendants of Israel were counted in the wilderness of Sinai. [26:63 - 64]

Q?Why didn't Moses and Aaron count these descendants?

A. Yahweh had said those people would die in the wilderness except Caleb and Joshua. [26:65]
