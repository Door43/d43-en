====== Numbers Chapter 15 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What do the offerings at the feasts produce for Yahweh?

A. The offering at the feasts produce a sweet aroma for Yahweh. [15:3]

Q? What drink offering were the people to bring with their burnt offering or with the sacrifice each lamb?

A. The people were told to prepare wine as the drink offering with their burnt offering or with the sacrifice each lamb. [15:5]

Q? What grain offering were they to bring with the offering of a ram?

A. With the offering of a ram, they were to bring flour mixed with oil. [15:4]

Q? Who was told to follow those specific laws and decrees about offerings?

A. All who were native-born Israelites had do same laws and decrees. [15:13]

Q? How was the law and decree to apply to the foreigner who  stayed with the people of Israel?

A. The same law and decree must apply to them and to the foreigner who was staying with them. [15:16]

Q? How long does Yahweh command the people to raise up an offering from the first dough?

A. He says that they must give a raised offering throughout the people’s generations. [15:21]

Q? What kind of sin is without the community's knowledge?

A. An unintentional sin is without the community’s knowledge. [15:24]

Q? For whom does the priest need to make attonement?

A. The priest must make atonement for all the community of the people. [15:25]

Q? What must be done to the person who does anything in defiance?

A. The person who does anything in defiance must be cut off from among his people. [15:30]

Q? What has the person who has broken Yahweh's commandment done with his word?

A. They have despised Yahweh's word and broken his commandment. [15:31]

Q? Where did the community stone the man found gathering wood on the Sabbath?

A. The community brought him outside the camp and stoned him to death. [15:36]

Q? What are the people not to do as they carry out Yahweh's commandments?

A. They are told to carry them out so that they do not follow after their own heart and eyes. [15:39]

Q? What does Yaweh say he did to become the people of Israel's God?

A. Yahweh said that he brought them out of the land of Egypt, to become their God. [15:41]
