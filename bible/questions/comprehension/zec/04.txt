====== Zechariah Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? In brief what did was Joshua shown? What did he see?

A. Joshua saw a gold lampstand with a bowl on top with seven lamps and two olive trees by the bowl. [4:2-3]

Q? What did the the seven lamps "mean"?

A. The seven lamps are the eyes of Yahweh that roam over the whole earth. [4:7]
