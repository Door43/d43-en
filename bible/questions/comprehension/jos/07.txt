====== Joshua Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Yahweh's anger burn against the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh's anger burned against the people of Israel because Achan took some things for himself that were dedicated to destruction. [7:1]

Q? What did the spys that were sent by Joshua to Ai report back to Joshua?

A. The spys that were sent to Ai reported to Joshua that only a small army could take Ai because there were few people there. [7:3]

Q? What happened to the smaller army of 3,000 men who attacked Ai?

A. The smaller army which attacked Ai was driven away by the men of Ai and 36 were killed. [7:5]

Q? What did Joshua do when he learned of the defeat of his army at Ai?

A. When Joshua learned of the defeat of his army at Ai he tore his garments, put dust on his head, and lay before the ark. [7:6]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua why his army was defeated at Ai?

A. Yahweh told Joshua that his army was defeated because Israel had sinned by stealing some of the things dedicated for destruction. [7:11]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua to do?

A. Yahweh told Joshua to get up and consecrate the people to give over the things dedicated for destruction. [7:13]

Q? What did Yahweh say would happen to the one who had stolen the things dedicated to destruction?

A. Yahweh said that the one who had stolen the things dedicated to destruction would be burned as well as all he has. [7:15]

Q? What did Achon tell Joshua that he had taken?

A. Achon told Joshua that he had taken a beautiful coat, 200 shekels of silver and a bar of gold. [7:21]

Q? Where did Achon tell Joshua that he had hid the things he had taken?

A. Achon told Joshua that he had hidden the things he had taken in the ground in the middle of his tent. {7:21]

Q? What happened to Achor?

A. Achor, his family, his oxen, donkeys, sheep and tent were brought to the Valley of Achor where they were burned and stone. [7:24-25]

Q? What happened to Yahweh's burning anger?

A. Yahweh's burning anger was turned away. [7:26]
