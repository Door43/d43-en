====== Joshua Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Jabin, king of Hazor, do when he heard about the victory of the Israelites at Gibeon?

A. When Jabin, king of Hazor, heard about the Israelites victory he sent many kings from  the region to bring their armies to wage war with Israel. [11:1-5]

Q? What did their number seem like?

A. Their number seemed like the sand of the seashore. [11:4]

Q? Where did the battle take place?

A. The battle took place at the waters of Merom. [11:6}

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua to do?

A. Yahweh told Joshua to hamstring their horses and burn their chariots. [11:6]

Q? What did Joshua do to Hazor and its king?

A. Joshua struck the king with a sword and burned Hazor. [11:10-11]

Q? What did Joshua do with the rest of the cities that had waged war with Israel?

A. Joshua captured the kings and destroyed them but he did not burn their cities. [11:12-13]

Q? What had Moses commanded Joshua to do?

A. Moses had commanded Joshua to kill every human being. [11:14]

Q? Did Joshua do all that Moses had command hi to do?

A. Yes. Joshua did all that Moses had command him to do. [11:15]

Q? What one city made peace with the Israelites?

A.  Gibeon was the only city to make peace with Israel. [11:19]
