====== Joshua Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What were the two tribes that made up the tribe of Joseph?

A. The tribe of Joseph was made up of the tribes of Manasseh and Ephraim. [14:4]

Q? Who came to Joshua to ask for Hebron as an inheritance?

A. Caleb came to Joshua and ask for Hebron as an inheritance. [14:6-13]

Q? How old was Caleb when he came to Joshua to ask for Hebron as an inheritance?

A. Caleb was eighty-five years old when he came to Joshua to ask for Hebron as an inheritance. [14:10]
