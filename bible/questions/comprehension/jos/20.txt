====== Joshua Chapter 20 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua to say to the people?

A. Yahweh told Joshua to say to the people to appoint the cities of refuge. [20:2]

Q? What is a city of refuge?

A. A city of refuge is city where someone who has unintentionally killed a person can go to escape anyone who seeks to avenge the blood of a person who was killed. [20:3]

Q? To whom and where would the person who killed another explain his case?

A. The person who had killed another will stand at the city gate and explain his case to the elders of that city. [20:4]

Q? What were soome of the cities selected by the Israelites as cities of refuge?

A. The Israelites selected Kedesh, Shechem, Kiriath Arba, Bezer and Ramoth as cities of refuge. [7-8]

Q? What would the accused person do in the city of refuge to avoid being killed by the one who wanted to avenge the shed blood?

A. The accused person would first stand before the assembly. [20:9]
