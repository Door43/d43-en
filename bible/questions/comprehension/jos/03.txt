====== Joshua Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the officers tell the people to follow when it appeared being carried by Levite priests?

A. The officers told the people to follow the ark of the covenant, carried by the priests from the Levites. [3:3]

Q? Why did the priests tell the people to stay about 2000 cubits behind the ark of the covenant?

A. The people were told to stay behind the ark so they could see which way to go since they had never gone that way before. [3:4]

Q? What did Joshua tell the people that Yahweh was going to do among them on that day?

A. Joshua told that people that Yahweh was going to do "wonders" among them that day. [3:5]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua to tell the priests when they got to the Jordon River?

A. Yahweh told Joshua to tell the priests to stand still in the Jordon River. [3:8]

Q? What did Joshua tell the people would happen when the soles of the priests carrying the ark touched the Jordon River?

A. Joshua told the people when the priests carrying the ark touched the river the waters would be cut off. [3:13]

Q? Where did the people cross the Jordon River?

A. The people crossed the Jordon near Jericho. [3:16]

Q? What did the priests who carried the ark of the covenant do while the people crossed over?

A. The priests who carried the ark of the covenant remained in the middle of the Jordon River on dry ground while the people crossed over. [3:17]
