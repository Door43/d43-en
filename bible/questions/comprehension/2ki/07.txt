====== 2 Kings Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Elisa say will happen to the captain who did not believe him?

A. Elisha said the captain will not eat any of the flour or barley. [7:2]

Q? Why did the four men with leprousy go to the army of the Arameans?

A. They went to the army of the Arameans because they said that they might die if they go into the city, stand where they were, or go to army the Arameans. [7:3]

Q? Why did the Aramean army think the king of Israel hired the kings of the Hittites and Egyptians to come against the Arameans?

A. The Aramean army thought the king of Israel hired the kings of the Hittites and Egyptians to come against the Arameans because they heard a noise of chariots, and a noise of horsesâ€”the noise of another large army. [7:6]

Q? After the men with leprousy carried plunder away from the army of the Arameans, what did they realize they should do?

A. After the men with leprousy carried plunder away from the army of the Arameans, they realized they should go and tell the king's household. [7:8-9]

Q? What did the king think was the reason the Arameans left their camp?

A. The king thought they had gone out of the camp to hide themselves in the fields. Then when the Israelites came out of the city, the Arameans will take them alive, and get into the city. [7:12]

Q? Of what was the road full?

A. The road was full of clothes and equipment that the Arameans had cast away in their hurry. [7:15]

Q? What happened to the captain who had not believed Elisha?

A. The king had ordered the captain to be in charge of the gate, and the people trampled him down in the gateway. [7:17-18]
