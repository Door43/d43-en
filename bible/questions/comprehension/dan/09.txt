====== Daniel Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who was Darius?

A. He was the son of Ahasuerus who was king over the realm of the Babylonians. He was a descendant of the Medes. [9:1]

Q? What did Daniel observed by studying Yahweh's word to Jeremiah the prophet?

A. He observed that Jerusalem's abandonment would end after seventy years. [9:2]

Q? How did Daniel seek the Lord God?

A. Daniel sought the Lord God with prayer and requests, with fasting, wearing sackcloth, and sitting in ashes. [9:3]

Q? What did Daniel say about the Lord God in his prayer?

A. Daniel said God was great and awesome and who keeps covenant and faithfulness with those who love him and keep his commandments. Daniel said that to the Lord God belongs righteousness. Daniel said compassion and forgiveness belong to the Lord God. [9:4,7,9]

Q? What did Daniel say Israel had done with respect to God and his prophets?

A. Daniel said Israel had sinned, done wrong, acted wickedly, rebelled, turned aside from God's commands and decrees, not listened to God's prophets, committed treachery, not obeyed Yahweh's voice, and transgressed Yahweh's law, [9:5-10]

Q? How did Yahweh confirm the words he spoke against Israel and their rulers?

A. Yahweh confirmed his words by bringing on Israel and their rulers a great disaster. [9:12]

Q? What did Daniel say that Israel had not done to beg God for mercy?

A. Daniel said they had not turned away from their iniquities and had not paid attention to God's truth. [9:13]

Q? What did Daniel give to the Lord God as reasons the Lord God should forgive Israel

A. Reasons Daniel gave as to why the Lord should forgive Israel: 1.Because of the Lord's righteous deeds. 2. For the Lord's own sake. 3. Because of God's great mercy. [9:16-19]

Q? When did Gabriel come to Daniel?

A. Gabriel came to Daniel at the time of the evening sacrifice while Daniel was praying. [9:21]

Q? Why did Gabriel come to Daniel?

A. Gabriel came to give Daniel insight and understanding, [9:22].
