====== Psalms Chapter 071 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the writer tell Yahweh he wants him to do?

A. The writer wants him to rescue him, make him safe, turn his ear to him and save him. [71:2]

Q? What does the writer want Yahweh to be to him?

A. The writer wants Yahweh to be his rock and fortress. [71:3]

Q? What hand does the writer want to be rescued from?

A. The hand of the wicked, and the unrighteous and cruel man. [70:4]

Q? How long has he trusted in Yahweh?

A. Since he was a child. [71:5]

Q? How do many people see the writer?

A. They see him as an example having God as his strong refuge. [71:7]

Q? What does the writer do with his mouth all day?

A. He fills his mouth with praise and honor all the day. [71:8]

Q? When the writer is old and his strength fails what does he tell God not to do?

A. To not throw him away or abandon him. [71:9]

Q? What do the enemies say as they are plotting together about the writer?

A. They say, "God has forsaken him; pursue and take him, for these is no one to save him" [7:10-11]

Q? What does the writer want God to do to those who are hostile to his life.

A.

Q? What will the writer tell about his hope in Yahweh?

A. His mouth will tell of his rightousness, salvation. and the mighty acts of the Lord. [71:14-16]

Q? When will the writer declare God's wonderful deeds?

A. From his youth through old age and into the next generation. [71:17-18]

Q? Who will revive us and bring us up again from the depths?

A. God who has done great things. [71:19-20]

Q? What does the writer,with his harp, give thanks to God for?

A. He thanks God for how he increased his hono, turn and comforted him and for God's trustworthiness. [71:21-22]

Q? What does the writer use his liips and tongue to proclaim?

A. He proclaims God's righteousness. [71:23-24]
