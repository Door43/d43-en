====== Psalms Chapter 036 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does sin speak as in the heart of the wicked man?

A. Sin speaks as an oracle in the heart of the wicked man and there is no fear of God in his eyes. [36:1]

Q? How does the wicked man live?

A. He lives in the delusion that his sin will not be discovered and be hated. [36:2]

Q? What are the wicked man's words like?

A. His words are sinful and deceitful. [36:3]

Q? What does the wicked man not want to be?

A. He does not want to be wise and to do good. [36:3]

Q? When does he plan ways to sin?

A. The wicked man plans ways to sin while he lies in bed. [36:4]

Q? What kind of way does he set out on and what does he not reject?

A. The wicked man sets out on an evil way, he does not reject evil. [36:4]

Q? Whose covenant faithfulness reaches to the heavens and his loyalty reaches to the clouds?

A. Yahweh's covenant faithfulness and his loyalty. [36:5]

Q? What is Yahweh's justice like?

A. His justice is like the highest mountains and the deepest sea. [36:6]

Q? Who preserves both mankind and the animals?

A. Yahweh preserves both mankind and the animals. [36:6]

Q? Who takes refuge under the shadow of God's wings?

A. Humanity takes refuge under the shadow of God's wings. [36:7]

Q? How will humanity be satisfied with the richness of the food of God's house, and where will God let them drink from?

A. Humanity will be abundantly satisfied, and they will drink from the river of God's precious blessings. [36:8]

Q? With whom is the fountain of life, and in whose light will we see light?

A. God is the fountain of life and in his light we will see light. [36:9]

Q? What is David asking God to do?

A. David is asking God to extend God's covenant faithfulness fully to those who know God, and for God to extend his defense to the upright of heart. [36:10]

Q? What is David asking God not to let happen?

A. David is asking God not to let the foot of the arrogant man come near him, and the hand of the wicked to drive him away. [36:11]

Q? Who have fallen, are knocked down and not able to get up?

A. The evildoers have fallen, are knocked down and not able to get up. [36:12]
