====== Psalms Chapter 032 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does Yahweh bless man?

A. Yahweh forgives man's transgression and coveres sin. He reckons no guilt or deceit. [32:1-2]

Q? What happened to David when he remained silent?

A. His bones wasted away and he groaned all day long. God's hand was heavy on David and his strenghth withered. [32:3-4]

Q? What does God do when David acknowledges his sin?

A. God forgives the guilt of David's sin. [32:5]

Q? What should godly people do?

A. They should pray to Yahweh, so the surging waters will not reach them. [32:6]

Q? How does God surround David?

A. God surrounds him as a hiding place, a guard from trouble and with the songs of victory. [32:7]

Q? What promises does Yahweh make to David?

A. Yahweh promises he will instructs him and teach him in the way which he should go and keeps his eye on David. [32:8]

Q? What will people who trust in Yahweh experience?

A. They will feel Yahweh's covenant faithfulness surrounding them. [32:10]

Q? How do the upright in heart show they are glad in Yahweh?

A. The uprightinheart rejoice and shout for joy. [32:11]
