====== Psalms Chapter 025 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does David ask Yahweh for?

A. David asks God to not let him be humiliated; not let his enemies rejoice over him. [25:2]

Q? What does David request of Yahweh?

A. David requests Yahweh to make known to him Yehweh's ways. "Teach me your paths, guide me into your truth", [25:4-5]

Q? What does David ask Yahweh to remember?

A. Remember Yahweh's acts of compassion and of covenant faithfullness, but not to remember the sins of David's youth or his rebelliousness. [25:6-7]

Q? What does Yahweh do because he is good and fair?

A. He teaches sinners the way, he guides the humble with justice and he teaches the humble his way. [25:8-9]

Q? How are the paths of Yahweh idenified?

A. They are made from covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness. [25:10]

Q? Why does David want his sin pardoned?

A. For the sake of Yahweh's name. [25:11]

Q? How does David describe the man of Yahweh?

A. He is a man who fears Yahweh, The lord will instruct him in the way he should choose. His life will go along in goodness; and his descendants will inherit the land. [25:12-13]

Q? What does Yahweh do for those who follow him?

A. He counsels and makes his covenant known to them. [25:14]

Q? Why does David keep his eyes on Yahweh?

A. Because Yahweh will free David's feet from the net. [25:15]

Q? When David is alone and afflicted what does he request of Yahweh?

A. David asks Yahweh to turn toward him and have mercy on him. [25:16]

Q? When David's heart is troubled what does he ask of Yahweh?

A. David asks Yahweh to see his affliction and his toils; and forgive his sins and see his enemies who hate him and are crule to him. [25:17-18-19]

Q? Why does David take refuge in Yahweh?

A. Because Yahweh protects and rescues David so that he is not humiliated. [25:20]

Q? What preserves David?

A. David's integrity, uprightness and hope in Yahweh. [25:21]

Q? What is David's request to God?

A. To have God rescue Israel from all of its troubles.[25:22]
