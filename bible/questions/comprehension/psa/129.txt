====== Psalms Chapter 129 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How long has Israel been under attack and not defeated?

A. Israel has been under attack and not defeated since it's youth. [129:]

Q? What did the plowers do on Israel's back?

A. The plowers plowed on Israel's back and made their furrows long. [129:]

Q? What has righteous Yahweh done for Israel?

A. Yahweh has cut the ropes of the wicked. [129:]

Q? What does the writer want to happen to those who hate Zion?

A. He wants for them to all be put to shame and turned back, and to wither like grass before it grows up. [129:5-6]

Q? What does the writer not want those who pass by to say?

A. He does not want them to say, "May the blessing of Yahweh be on you;

we bless you in the name of Yahweh." [129:8]
