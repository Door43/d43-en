====== Psalms Chapter 054 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? David says that strangers have risen up and pitiless men have sought his soul, but what have they not set before them?

A. "The strangers have not set God before them." [54:3]

Q? What does David say that the Lord sustains?

A. "The Lord is the one who sustains my soul." [54:4]

Q? For what is David saying that God will repay his enemies?

A. "He will repay the evil to my enemies." [54:5]

Q? Why does David say that he will give thanks to the name of Yahweh?

A. "I will give thanks to your name, Yahweh, for it is good." [54:6]
