====== Psalms Chapter 024 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who founded the earth?

A. Yahweh founded it upon the seas and established it on the rivers. [24:1]

Q? Who will stand in Yahweh's holy place?

A. Those who have clean hands and a pure heart; who have not lifted up their soul to what is false, and who have not sworn deceitfully. [24:3-4]

Q? Who will receive a blessing from Yahweh?

A. The generation of those who seek him, those who seek the face of the God of Jacob. [24:5-6]

Q? Why are the gates to be lifted up?

A. So that the King of glory may come in. [24:7]
