====== Psalms Chapter 098 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the marvelous things Yahweh has done?

A. The marvelous things are that Yahweh's right hand and hoiy arm have given the people victory, he has made known his salvation and has openly showed his justice to all the nations. [98:1-2]

Q? What does Yahweh call to his mind?

A. Yahweh calls to mind his covenant loyalty and faithfulness for the house of Israel. [98:3]

Q? Who will see the victory of our God?

A. All the ends of the earth will see the victory of our God. [98:3]

Q? What should be the response of the house of Israel to Yahweh's provision?

A. The house of Israel should shout for joy and burst into song, singing Yahweh's praises. [98:4]

Q? What instruments should the house of Israel use to sing praises and make a joyful noise before Yahweh?

A. The house of Israel should use the harp, trumpets, and the sound of the horn. [98-5-6]

Q? How does the writer encourage Yahweh's creation to respond to his coming?

A. The writer says to let the sea shout and everything in it, let the world shout and those who live in it, let the rivers clap their hands and let the mountains shout for joy. [98:7-8]

Q? How will Yahweh judge the world?

A. Yahweh will judge the world with righteousness and the nations with fairness. [98:9]
