====== Psalms Chapter 076 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where does the God of Judah choose to live?

A. His dwelling place is in Zion. [75:1-2]

Q? As he descends from the mountains what does God look like?

A. God shines brightly and reveals his glory. [76:4]

Q? What happened when the God of Jacob angrily rebuked his enemies?

A. Their horses and riders died, everyone became afraid, and no one stood in God's sight. [76:6-7]

Q? What happened when God arose to execute judgment?

A. The earth was afraid and silent, and he saved the oppressed people. [76:8-9]

Q? What should all people do for Yahweh their God?

A. They all should make vows, keep the vows, and bring him gifts. [76:11]
