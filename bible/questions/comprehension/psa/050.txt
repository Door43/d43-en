====== Psalms Chapter 050 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Asaph uses what three names of God when he says God has spoken and called the earth?

A. The Mighty One, God, Yahweh, has spoken and called the earth. [50:1]

Q? God calls to the heavens above and to the earth in order to do what?

A. He calls to the heavens above and to the earth so that he may judge his people. [50:4]

Q? Asaph says that God's people will not be reproved for what?

A. God says, "I will not reprove you for your sacrifices." [50:8]

Q? Why does God say that he will take no bull or goat from his people?

A. God says that every beast of the forest is mine, and the cattle on a thousand hills. [50:10]

Q? What does Asaph say to offer and to pay to God?

A. Offer to God the sacrifice of thanksgiving and pay your vows to the Most High. [50:14]

Q? What does Asaph say that the wicked hate and throw away?

A. God says that the wicked hate instruction and throw away his words. [50:17]

Q? What does God say that the wicked think about him when God keeps silent?

A. You thought that I was someone just like yourself. [50:21]

Q? When God says that he will tear the wicked in pieces, who does he say will come help them?

A. God says, "I will tear you in pieces, and there will be no one to come to help you." [50:22]

Q? Who are the people that God shows "God's salvation"?

A. God shows his salvation to whoever offers a sacrifice of thanksgiving and plans his path in the right way. [50:23]
