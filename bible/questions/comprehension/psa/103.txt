====== Psalms Chapter 103 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the two phrases David uses at the beginning to describe his whole life and person?

A. "Bless Yahweh, my soul, and all that is within me." [103:1]

Q? Among the list of benefits, what does David say Yahweh crowns you with?

A. "He crowns you with covenant faithfulness and acts of tender mercy." [103:4]

Q? What does Yahweh do for all who are oppressed?

A. Yahweh does acts of justice for all who are oppressed. [103:6]

Q? What did Yahweh make known to the descendants of Israel?

A. "He made knownâ€¦ his deeds to the descendants of Israel." [103:7]

Q? David says that Yahweh is merciful, gracious and patient, but what other trait does David describe as being great?

A. "Yahwehâ€¦has great covenant loyalty." [103:8]

Q? David says that Yahweh does not deal with us in what way?

A. "He does not deal with us as our sins deserve." [103:10]

Q? In what special way does Yahweh have compassion on those who honor him?

A. "As a father has compassion on his children, so Yahweh has compassion on those who honor him." [103:13]

Q? David says the days of man are like grass which disappears, but the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh lasts how long?

A. "The covenant faithfulness of Yahweh is from everlasting to everlasting on those who honor him." [103:17]

Q? Where has Yahweh established his throne?

A. "Yahweh has established his throne in the heavens." [103:19]

Q? What is called to bless Yahweh which has great strength and obeys his word?

A. "Bless Yahweh, you angels of his, who have great strength and obey his word." [103:20]

Q? As David closes this psalm, are there any creatures he does not include in his call to Bless Yahweh?

A. "Bless Yahweh, all his creatures, in all places of his kingdom; bless Yahweh, my soul." [103:22]
