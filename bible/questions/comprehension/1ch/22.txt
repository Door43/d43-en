====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 22 ======

===== Translation Questions and Answers =====

Q? After David declared where the house of Yahweh was to be built, what did he order his servants to do?

A. He ordered them to find the foreigners living in the land of Israel and give them jobs as stonecutters to build God's house. [22:1-2]

Q? What preparations did David make for building the house of Yahweh before his death?

A. He provided large amounts of iron, bronze, and cedar. [22:3-5]

Q? Why did David tell Solomon that he was commanding him build the house of Yahweh?

A. Yahweh told David that he would not be the one to build the house because of all the blood that was shed. [22:6-8]

Q? How did Yahweh tell David he would do for his son?

A. He would give him rest from all his enemies on every side, and would establish Solomon's kingdom over Israel forever. [22:9-10]

Q? How did David tell Solomon to carefully observe?

A. He told Solomon to carefully obey the statutes and the decrees that Yahweh gave to Moses concerning Israel. [22:13]

Q? What did David tell Solomon to do with all of the materials that David had prepared for Yahweh's house?

A. He told Solomon to add more to it. [22:14]

Q? What did David tell Solomon to get up and to do?

A. He told Solomon to get up and build the sanctuary of Yahweh God. [22:19]
