====== Apostrophe ======


===== Description =====

In apostrophe, a speaker turns his attention from the people he is speaking to and makes an exclamation to someone who is not there with them. He could even speak to an imaginary person or a thing or idea as if it were a person (See [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]].)

=== Luke 13:34 ===

"Jerusalem, Jerusalem, who kills the prophets and stones those sent to you. How often I desired to gather your children the way a hen gathers her brood under her wings, but you did not desire this. "

  * **(1) Jerusalem, Jerusalem**  - **(3)**  Jesus spoke as if the people of Jerusalem were there listening to him. He said this twice to show how sad he was for them. **(5)** (See: Apostrophe)
