====== Ambiguity ======


===== Description =====

Ambiguity happens when a single word or phrase can have more than one meaning. 


===== Template =====

The following information is either required or optional in the notes:

  - **(Required)**  Write the text from the ULB that has the ambiguity in bold
  - (Optional)  Give a clarifying translation
  - **(Required)**  State the meaning of the ambiguity
  - **(Required)**  Suggest alternate ways to translate the text, with or without the ambiguity
  - **(Required)**  Add TA Link: (See: Ambiguity)

  * **(1) ULB text**  - **(3)** Text meaning. **(4)** Alternate translation suggestion. **(5)** (See: Ambiguity)
