===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ships** - These are ships capable of traveling on large bodies of water.
  * **Tarshish** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **They built the ships** - Jehoshaphat and Ahaziah did not build the ships, rather, their workers built them. AT: "Their workers built the ships" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Ezion Geber** - Translate the name of this place as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:08:17|8:17]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Eliezer ... Dodavahu** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Mareshah** - Translate this man's name as you did in  [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:11:05|11:08]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **The ships were wrecked so that** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The ships became ruined and" or "The ships wrecked so that" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they were not able to sail** - "no one was able to sail them"
