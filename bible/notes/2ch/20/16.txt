===== translationNotes: =====

  * **go down against** - AT: "go to" or "go to battle"
  * **pass of Ziz** - This is a narrow valley between two mountains southeast of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **rescue of Yahweh** - God will fight the battle and rescue the people
