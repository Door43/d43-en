===== translationNotes: =====

  * **set himself to seek** - This is an idiom. AT: "determined himself to seek" or "devoted himself to seek" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **to seek Yahweh** - This speaks of seeking Yahweh's advice as if he were looking for Yahweh. AT: "to seek Yahweh's advice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He proclaimed a fast** - The word "fast" can be expressed as a verb. AT: "He proclaimed that all the people should fast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Judah gathered** - This refers to the people of Judah. AT: "The people of Judah gathered" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
