===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to sing and to praise** - They were singing to Yahweh. AT: "to sing and to praise Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Yahweh set men** - This means that Yahweh caused some of the enemy soldiers to ambush other enemy soldiers. AT: "Yahweh set some enemy soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **ambush** - a surprise attack
  * **Mount Seir** - Translate the name of this place the same as in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:20:10|20:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **They were defeated** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They defeated them" or "They defeated those they had ambushed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to completely kill them and destroy them** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. The second intensifies the first. AT: "to completely annihilate them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **When they had finished with** - This refers to after they had destroyed them. AT: "When they had finished killing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
