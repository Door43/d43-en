===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He rebuilt** - Manasseh did not do the building and construction himself, rather, he commanded his workers to do it. AT: "He commanded them to rebuild" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he commanded Judah** - Here "Judah" refers to the people who live there. AT: "he commanded the people of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
