====== 2 Chronicles 33 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== King Manasseh ==

Manasseh angered God more than any other king of Judah. He worshiped the sun, stars and many foreign gods and even sacrificed his sons to the sun. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:falsegod]])
