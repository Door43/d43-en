===== translationNotes: =====

  * **cried out to heaven** - Here "heaven" represents Yahweh. AT: "cried to Yahweh for help" or "pleaded to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **shame-faced** - "embarrassed" or "ashamed" 
  * **the house of his god** - "the temple of his god"
