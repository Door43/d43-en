===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gave them rest on every side** - Here "rest" means "peace." AT: "gave them peace with all the neighboring countries" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] )
  * **in the sight of all nations** - AT: "so that all the surrounding people-groups knew" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
