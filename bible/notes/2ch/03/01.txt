===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then Solomon began to build ... He prepared ... He began ... that Solomon laid** - The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. AT: "Then Solomon's workers began to build ... They prepared ... They began ... that Solomon's workers laid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Mount Moriah** - This is the name of a mountain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ornan the Jebusite** - "Ornan" is the name of a man. "Jebusite" is the name of a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the second day of the second month** - This is the second month of the Hebrew calendar. The second day is near the middle of April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the fourth year** - "in the 4th year" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the narrator begins to explain the dimensions and the design of the temple.
  * **sixty cubits ... twenty cubits** - "60 cubits ... 20 cubits." A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "27.6 meters long ... 9.2 meters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
