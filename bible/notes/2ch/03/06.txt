===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He decorated ... He also overlaid ... he carved** - The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. AT: "Solomon's workers decorated ... They also overlaid ... they carved" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **precious stones** - Stones that were beautiful, highly valued, and used for decorations.
  * **Parvaim** - This is probably the name of a region. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **beams** - A beam is a long heavy piece of wood used to support a building.
