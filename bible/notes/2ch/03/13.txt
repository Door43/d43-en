===== translationNotes: =====

  * **twenty cubits** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "9.2 meters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **He made ... he fashioned** - The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. AT: "Solomon's workers made ... they fashioned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **blue, purple, and crimson wool** - Translate these words as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:02:06|2:7]].
  * **fine linen** - "high quality cloth" or "the best cloth"
