  * **Hezekiah ... gave the assembly ... bulls and ... sheep as an offering** - Hezekiah gave the animals to the people as an offering to the Lord for the people to eat.
  * **one thousand bulls ... seven thousand sheep ... one thousand bulls ... ten thousand sheep and goats** - "1,000 bulls ... 7,000 sheep ... 1,000 bulls ... 10,000 sheep and goats" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
