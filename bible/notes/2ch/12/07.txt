===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When Yahweh realized that they had hunbled themselves**  - "they" refers to the king and the other Israelite leaders.
  * **They have humbled themselves**  - The king and other leaders are symbolic of all the people of Israel. (See: metonymy)
