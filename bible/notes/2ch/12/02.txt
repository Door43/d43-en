===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It happened in the fifth year of King Rehoboam, that ** - AT: "In the fifth year of King Rehoboam's reign," (See: Idiom; See: Metonymy)
  * **He came with twelve hundred chariots**  - "He" refers to Shishak, king of Egypt.
  * **Soldiers without number**  - AT: "A great number of soldiers" (See: Idiom)
