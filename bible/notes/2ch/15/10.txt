===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So they gathered together** - "They" here refers to the tribes of Judah and Israel that were with Asa.
  * **the third month** - This is the third month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of May and the first part of June on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]])
  * **third ... fifteenth** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]]
  * **seven hundred ... seven thousand** - "700 ... 7,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
