===== translationNotes: =====

  * **All Judah rejoiced** - Here the word "all" is a generalization. The phrase means that  people from all over Judah rejoiced. AT: "People all over Judah rejoiced" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **with all their heart** - The idiom "with all their heart" means "with complete devotion." AT: "with complete devotion" or "wholeheartedly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he was found by them** - This can be stated in active form. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:15:03|15:4]]. AT: "they found him" or "he permitted them to find him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
