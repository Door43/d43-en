====== 2 Chronicles 15 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The section about King Asa continues in this chapter.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Promise ==
The people promise to serve the Lord. This is something they will ultimately fail to do. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:promise]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

The phrase "seek Yahweh" means "wanting to believe and obey Yahweh." This is a common way to describe proper worship of Yahweh. It is possible the meaning of this idiom is about a desire to properly worship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
