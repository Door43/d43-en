===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The Spirit of God came on Azariah** - This is an idiom which means that the spirit of God influenced Azariah and enabled him to prophesy. AT: "The spirit of God gave Azariah the ability to prophesy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Oded** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **he will be found by you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you will find him" or "he will permit you to find him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
