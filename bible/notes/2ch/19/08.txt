===== translationNotes: =====

  * **heads of the ancestral houses** - Here "heads" is a metaphor for the most important part, and "houses" represents families. AT: "leaders of the ancient families" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for carrying out judgment for Yahweh** - The abstract noun "judgment" can be translated using the verb "to judge." AT: "for judging the people for Yahweh" or "for judging on Yahweh's behalf" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **for the sake of disputes** - "for settling disputes" or "for resolving arguments"
  * **You must serve in reverence for Yahweh** - The abstract noun "reverence" can be translated as a verb. AT: "You must serve while fearing Yahweh" or "You must serve while respecting Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **with your whole heart** - Here "heart" represents the whole person. AT: "with your whole being" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
