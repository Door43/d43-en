===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He made ... he placed ... He made ... He made** - Here "he" refers to Solomon. The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. AT: "Solomon's workers made ... they placed ... They made ... They made" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **that were made from the instructions for their design** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "according to the instructions for their design" or "according to how Solomon instructed his workers to design them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **on the right hand ... on the left** - "on the right side ... on the left side" or "on the south side ... on the north side"
  * **one hundred basins** - "100 basins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **basins** - a shallow bowls used for washing
