===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Josiah took away all** - Josiah would have commanded his workers to do this. AT: "Josiah commanded his workers to take away all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the disgusting things** - These are the idols that were repulsive to God. AT: "the disgusting idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **For all of his days** - Josiah's life is represented by his "days." AT: "For all of his life" or "As long as Josiah was alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
