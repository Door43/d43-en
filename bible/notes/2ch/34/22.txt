===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Huldah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shallum ... Tokhath ... Hasrah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **keeper of the wardrobe** - This refers to Shallum. Possible meanings are 1) the person who took care of the clothing that priests wore in the temple or 2) the person who took care of the king's clothing.
  * **the Second District** - "the 2nd District" or "District 2" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **they spoke with her in this way** - "they had the following conversation with her"
