===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stood in his place **- AT: "stood at the entrance to the temple" (See: Implicit Information - vague reference)
  * **"to walk after" **- This means "to follow"
  * **with all his heart and all his being, **- AT: "completely," or "completely and with all of his heart," (See: Doublet)
  * **all those who were found in Jerusalem and Benjamin **- AT: "all of the people from Jerusalem and the tribe of Benjamin" (See: Metonymy)
  * **to stand by the covenant. ****The inhabitants of Jerusalem acted in obedience to the covenant of God, **- AT: "to act in obedience to the covenant of God," (See: Parallelism)
