===== translationNotes: =====

  * **four hundred cubits in distance** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "400 cubits in distance" or "a length of 180 meters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **with Obed Edom** - Here "Obed Edom" refers to this man's descendants. AT: "under the care of the descendants of Obed Edom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the king's house** - "the royal palace"
