===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - King Solomon continues praying.

  * **So listen to the requests of your servant and of your people Israel** - Solomon speaks of himself as "your servant" to show that he respects Yahweh. This can be stated in first person. AT: "So listen to my requests and the requests of your people Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **when you listen, forgive** - "when you hear our prayers, please forgive our sins"
