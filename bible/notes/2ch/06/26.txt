===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When the skies are shut up and there is no rain**- - AT: "When you do not allow any rain to fall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **confess your name**- With their words and by their actions, they honor God and His authority. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **turn from their sin**  - AT: stop their evil ways"
  * **direct them to the good way in which they should walk**  - AT: "show them how to live righteously"
