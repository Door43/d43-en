===== translationNotes: =====

  * **has carried out the word that he had said** - This is an idiom. AT: "has done exactly what he said he would do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I have arisen in the place of David my father** - Height is a metaphor for power. AT: "I have gained the power that David my father had" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I sit on the throne of Israel** - The throne is a metonym for the activity of the one who sits on the throne. AT: "I rule over Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for the name of Yahweh** - Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh's name represents himself. AT: "for Yahweh" or 2) Yahweh's name represents his reputation. AT: "for the reputation of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in which is Yahweh's covenant, which** - The stone tablets on which Yahweh had written the terms of the covenant are spoken of as if they were the covenant itself. AT: "in which are the tablets on which Yahweh wrote the terms of the covenant that" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
