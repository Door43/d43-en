===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let your eyes be open, and let your ears be attentive**- (See: Doublet)
  * **ark of your strength**-The ark which was in the temple contained the tablets of the ten commandments representing God's power and might  
  * **Let your priests, Yahweh God, be clothed with salvation**-The priests should show that they are saved by Yahweh God by the way they live and serve God's people.  (See: Metaphor)
