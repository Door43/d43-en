===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Asa slept with his ancestors, dying in** - The phrase "slept with his ancestors" is a euphemism for dying. AT: "Asa died during" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **the forty-first year** - "the 41st year" or "year 41" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **which he had dug out for himself** - Asa would have had his workers prepare his tomb for him. AT: "which his workers had dug out for him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **bier** - A bier is a table on which a dead body is placed at a funeral.
  * **sweet odors and various kinds of spices prepared by skilled perfumers** - Putting plants that smell good with the dead body was one of the burial customs of the people of Israel. AT: "sweet smelling plants prepared by people skilled in this custom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **prepared by skilled perfumers** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that skilled perfumers had prepared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
