====== 2 Chronicles 16 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The section about King Asa is completed in this chapter.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Asa did not trust Yahweh ==
When Baasha was building Ramah to blockade him, Asa asked the king of Aram to help him instead of trusting God to help him. This was sinful because he disobeyed Yahweh. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:trust]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])  

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metonymy ==

The phrase "the eyes of Yahweh run everywhere" is a metonym. It means Yahweh is "looking everywhere" for something or someone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
