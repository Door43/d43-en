===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **seventy thousand of them to carry loads**- 70,000 men who were not Israelites by birth were assigned to carry the cedar logs that had floated down the Mediteranean Sea from Tyre to Joppa, overland to Jersusalem for the building of the temple.
