===== translationNotes: =====

  * **censer** - This is a special pan or bowl used for burning incense.
  * **behold, he had become leprous** - The word "behold" here shows that the priests were surprised by what they saw. 
  * **They quickly drove him out of there** - "They quickly forced him out of there" or "They quickly made him leave" 
  * **Yahweh had struck him** - This speaks of Yahweh making him sick as if Yahweh had hit him. AT: "Yahweh had made him ill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
