===== translationNotes: =====

  * **120 talents** - A talent is a unit of weight equal to about 33 kilograms. You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "about four thousand kilograms of gold" or "about four metric tons of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **No greater amount of spices ... was ever given to him again** - The verb "was ever given" can be expressed in active form. AT: "No one ever gave more spices than the queen of Sheba gave to King Solomon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
