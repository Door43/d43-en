===== translationNotes: =====

  * **first and last** - The phrase "first and last" represents the whole time of Solomon's reign. AT: "from the beginning to the end of his reign" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **are they not written ... Jeroboam son of Nebat?** - The author uses the question to remind the readers that other people had written about Solomon. It can be expressed in active form AT: "they are written ... Jeroboam son of Nebat" or "someone has written about them ... Jeroboam son of Nebat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **The History of Nathan the Prophet ... The Prophecy of Ahijah the Shilonite ... The Visions of Iddo the Seer** - These are the names of some writings that existed when 2 Chronicles was written, but they do not exist now.
  * **He slept with his ancestors** - This was a polite way to tell about him dying. AT: "He died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
