===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Beth Horon the Upper and Beth Horon the Lower** - These were two cities in Judah. The upper city was on top of a hill and the lower city was in a valley. AT: "Higher Beth Horon and Lower Beth Horon" or "Beth Horon of the Hill and Beth Horon of the Valley" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Baalath** - This was a city in Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **all the lands under his rule** - "all the lands that he ruled"
