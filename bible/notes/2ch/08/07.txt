===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their descendants who were left after them in the land** - "after those people died, their descendants who remained in the land"
  * **forced laborers** - "slaves" 
  * **which they are to this day** - This phrase adds information about the descendants of those that Solomon had made into forced laborers. AT: "and their descendants are still forced laborers even now" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
  * **to this day** - This refers to the time when the book of 2 Chronicles was written. See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:05:09|5:9]].
