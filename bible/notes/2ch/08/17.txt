===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ezion Geber and then to Elath on the coast** - These are towns along the Sea of Reeds. They had ports where people who had ships could stop. AT: "the towns of Ezion Geber and Elath, on the coast of the Sea of Reeds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Ophir** - This is the name of a place. Its location is not known. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **450 talents of gold** - "four hundred and fifty talents of gold." You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "about sixteen thousand kilograms of gold" or "about fifteen metric tons of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
