===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **People from all the tribes of Israel came after them**  - the people from the tribes belonging to the northern kingdom who still served the Lord God fled to Judah
    * **for they walked in the path of David and Solomon**  - they followed the Lord (but only for three years) as David and Solomon had done
