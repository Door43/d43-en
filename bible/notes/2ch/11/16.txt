===== translationNotes: =====

  * **People from all the tribes of Israel came after them** - The people from the tribes belonging to the northern kingdom who still worshiped the Lord God fled to Judah.
  * **set their hearts to seek Yahweh** - AT: "who desire to worship Yahweh"
  * **they walked for three years in the way of David and Solomon** - AT: "they followed the example of David and Solomon for three years"
