===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Rehoboam lived in Jerusalem and built cities** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped Rehoboam build these cities. AT: "Rehoboam lived in Jerusalem and had his workers build cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Etam, Tekoa ... Zorah, Aijalon** - All of the names in this list refer to cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
