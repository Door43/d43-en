===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Mahalath ... Maacah** - These are the names of women. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Rehoboam took Maacah** - This idiom means that he married her. AT: "Rehoboam married Maacah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Abijah, Attai, Ziza, and Shelomith** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **eighteen wives and sixty concubines ... twenty-eight sons and sixty daughters** - "18 wives and 60 concubines ... 28 sons and 60 daughters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
