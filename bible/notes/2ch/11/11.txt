===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He fortified the fortresses** - Here the word "fortresses" refers to fortified cities. That Rehoboam "fortified the fortresses" means that he built up and strengthened the defenses of these fortified cities. AT: "He strengthened the defenses of the fortified cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **made them very strong** - "made the cities very strong"
  * **Judah and Benjamin belonged to him** - Rehoboam was in control of the tribes of Judah and Benjamin.
