===== translationNotes: =====

  * **was strengthened in his rule** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "gained complete control over his kingdom" or "ruled powerfully over his kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the translationWord page about Yahweh concerning how to translate this.
  * **God was with him** - Here "was with him" is an idiom. AT: "God supported him" or "God helped him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
