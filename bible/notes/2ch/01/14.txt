===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **silver and gold to be as common...as stone**- Stones are everywhere in Jerusalem.  (See: Simile)
  * **cedar wood as common as...sycamore trees**- Cedar trees were very good trees for building and had to be brought into Israel from Lebanon.  They did not grow in Israel.  Sycamore trees grow in Israel in the lowlands but are not as strong for building.
