====== 2 Chronicles 35 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the end of the story of King Josiah. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Passover ==
Josiah organized a large Passover celebration and people shared their animals with those without animals to sacrifice. The celebration of Passover was a sign of proper worship by the people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:passover]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sign]])
