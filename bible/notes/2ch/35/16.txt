===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the entire service of Yahweh was carried out** - This refers to everything associated with the preparation, the sacrifice, and the worship of Yahweh during the Passover. The word "service" can be expressed as a verb and this can be stated in active form. AT: "they did everything that needed to be done to serve Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **kept the Passover** - This is an idiom. AT: "observed the Passover" or "celebrated the Passover" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **then the Festival of Unleavened Bread** - The missing words may be added. AT: "then kept the Festival of Unleavened Bread" or "then celebrated the Festival of Unleavened Bread" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
