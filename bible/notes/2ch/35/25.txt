===== translationNotes: =====


  * **to this day** - This means the day on which the writer wrote. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:05:09|5:9]].
  * **behold** - This word is used here to add emphasis to what is said next.
  * **the songs of lament** - This was an ancient scroll of funeral songs.
