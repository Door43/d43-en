===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Adoniram** - Adoniram is the name of a man. In the Hebrew text of this sentence, his name was written as Adoram. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who was over the forced laborers** - Being over people represents having authority to tell them what to do. AT: "who was in charge of the forced laborers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **stoned him to death with stones** - "killed Adoniram by stoning him"
  * **to this day** - This refers to the general time when the author wrote this book. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:05:09|5:9]]. AT: "even now" 
  * **against the house of David** - Here "the house of David" represents the kings who were descendants of David. AT: "against the kings descended from David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
