===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **Hezekiah commanded storerooms to be prepared** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Hezekiah commanded the priests and Levites to prepare storerooms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Konaniah ... Shimei ... Jehiel, Azaziah, Nahath, Asahel, Jerimoth, Jozabad, Eliel, Ismakiah, Mahath, and Benaiah** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **were managers under the hand of Konaniah and Shimei his brother** - Here "hand" represents power or control. The phrase "under the hand" is an idiom that means to be under someone's authority. AT: "were managers whom Konaniah and Shimei his brother supervised" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the official over the house of God** - Here "over" is an idiom that means to be in charge of. AT: "the official in charge of everyone who served in the house of God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
