===== translationNotes: =====
  * **to seek his God** - Seeking Yahweh represents choosing to know, worship, and obey him. AT: "to obey his God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he performed it with all his heart** - Here the word "heart" refers to a person's will and desire. AT: "he performed it with all his will" or "he was completely committed to what he did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
