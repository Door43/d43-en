===== translationNotes: =====

  * **thank offerings** - These were offerings of thanksgiving.
  * **the house of Yahweh** - The word "house" represents the temple. AT: "the temple of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who had a willing heart** - Here the word "heart" represents the person who was willing. AT: "who were willing" or "who desired" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
