===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses list the names of the Levites who began to do the work that Hezekiah had ordered them to do. These are all men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **the Levites arose** - Here the word "arose" is an idiom that means that the Levites began to act. AT: "the Levites began to work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
