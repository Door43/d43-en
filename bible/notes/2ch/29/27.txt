===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the song of Yahweh began also** - The phrase "the song of Yahweh" refers to people singing a song to Yahweh. AT: "the people began to sing a song to Yahweh also"
  * **until the burnt offering was finished** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "until the priests finished offering the burnt offering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
