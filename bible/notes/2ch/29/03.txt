===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **carry away the filthiness** - AT: "remove uncleanliness" or "remove the dirtiness"
