===== translationNotes: =====

  * **become a lying spirit in the mouth of all his prophets** - Here the word "spirit" refers to the attitudes of the prophets and the words "the mouth" represents what they will say. AT: "cause all his prophets to speak lies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
