===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They took Beth Shemesh** - Taking a city is an idiom that means to conquer or to capture the city. AT: "The Philistines captured Beth Shemesh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Beth Shemesh, Aijalon, Gederoth, Soko ... Timnah ... Gimzo** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
