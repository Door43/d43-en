====== 2 Chronicles 05:11-12 ======

===== translationNotes: =====


  * **It came about that** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way of doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **Asaph, Heman, Jeduthun** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **sons and brothers** - "sons and other relatives" (UDB)
  * **cymbals** - two thin, round metal plates that are hit together to make a loud sound (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
