===== translationNotes: =====

  * **began to reign** - "became king of Judah"
  * **forty years** - "40 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Zibiah** - This is a woman's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh** - The word "eyes" here represents seeing, and seeing represents judgment. Yahweh saw and approved of Joash's actions.  See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:14:01|14:1]]. AT: "what Yahweh judged to be right" or "what Yahweh considered to be right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all the days of Jehoiada, the priest** - This idiom means "as long as Jehoiada, the priest, was alive." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Jehoiada took for him two wives** - "Jehoiada chose two wives for Joash"
