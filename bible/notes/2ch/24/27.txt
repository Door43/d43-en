===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the accounts about his sons** - "the record of what his sons did"
  * **the important prophecies that were spoken about him** - "the important things the prophets spoke about him"
  * **see, they are written** - Here "see" alerts the reader to pay special attention to the information that follows.
  * **the commentary on the book of the kings** - This is a book which no longer exists.
