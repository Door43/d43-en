===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in his place** - "after his father"
  * **strengthened himself against Israel** - Jehoshephat prepared the army for battle. AT: "prepared the army to be able to battle Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **He placed forces** - "He places soldiers"
  * **garrisons** - "military encampments." These are set up for the people's protection.
