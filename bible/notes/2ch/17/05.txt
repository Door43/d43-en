===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh established the rule in his hand** - The phrase "his hand" represents Jehoshaphat's control. AT: "Yahweh enabled him to completely control his kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **honor in abundance** - The word "honor" may be expressed as a verb. AT: "was greatly honored" or "the people greatly honored him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **His heart** - Here the king is represented by his "heart" to emphasize his will and desires. AT: "He" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
