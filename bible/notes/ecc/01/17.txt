===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **applied my heart **- This can also be explained as directed my mind.
  * **to know wisdom, and to know madness and folly **- This can also be explained that if you know wisdom you also know foolishness. See Translate: Doublet
  * **striving after wind. **- This can also be explained as trying to reach something that is not possible.
  * **much grief  **- This can also be explained as great sadness.
