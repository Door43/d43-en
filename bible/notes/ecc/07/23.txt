===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Wisdom is far off and very deep.**  AT: "It is very hard to find wisdom. It is well hidden."
  * **Who can find it?**  - AT: "No man can find it." (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **I turned my heart**  - AT: "I decided" (See: Synecdoche)
  * **to understand that evil is stupid and that folly is madness**  - These are saying the same thing. (See: Doublet)
