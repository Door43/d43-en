===== translationWords: =====


===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When times are good ... when times are bad** - The word "times" is an idiom for "things happening." AT: "When good things happen ... when bad things happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **live happily in that good** - "be happy about those good things"
  * **both to exist side by side** - The phrase "side by side" is an idiom that means "this one" and "this one." AT: "both to exist" or "there to be both good and bad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **anything that is coming after him** - Possible meanings are 1) "anything that happens in the future" or 2) "anything that happens to him after he dies."
