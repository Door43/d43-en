===== translationNotes: =====

  * **every word that is spoken** - This can be written in active form. AT: "everything that people say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you know yourself** - "you yourself know." Here "yourself" is used to emphasize the phrase "you know." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **in your own heart** - Here a person's thoughts are represented by their "heart." AT: "in your own thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
