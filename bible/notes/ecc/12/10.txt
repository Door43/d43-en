===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **vivid**  - "understandable" or "clear" or "expressive"
  * **like goads. . .Like nails** - "Goads and nails" are used to make a picture of making sharp points with words.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **goad** - a long stick with a point on the end, used to prod an animal to move
  * **by one shepherd** - The writer pictures himself as a shepherd who takes great care with his animals. H (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metaphor]])
