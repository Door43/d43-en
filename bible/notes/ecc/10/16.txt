===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **if your king is young** - This means that the king is inexperienced or immature.
    * **begin feasting in the morning** - This implies that the leaders are more concerned with having a good time than with leading the nation.
    * **the land is happy** - AT: "the people living in the land are happy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
