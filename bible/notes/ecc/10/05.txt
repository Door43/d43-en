===== translationNotes: =====

  * **under the sun** - This refers to things that are done on earth. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ecc:01:01|1:3]]. AT: "on the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Fools are given leadership positions** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Rulers give positions of leadership to fools" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **successful men are given low positions** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they give low positions to successful men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **low positions** - This is an idiom. AT" "unimportant positions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **successful men walking like slaves on the ground** - This speaks of successful men walking like slaves walk, because slaves were usually forced to walk and were not permitted to ride. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
