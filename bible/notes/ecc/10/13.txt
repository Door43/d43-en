===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As words begin to flow from a fool's mouth** - A fool's speech is represented by his "mouth." AT: "As a fool begins to speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **at the end his mouth flows with wicked madness** - A fool's speech is represented by his "mouth." AT: "as he finishes talking, he speaks wicked madness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **multiplies words** - This is an idiom. AT: "keeps on talking" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **what is coming** - "what will happen in the future" 
  * **Who knows what is coming after him?** - The writer asks this question to emphasize that no one knows what will happen in the future after one's death. This question can be written as as statement. AT: "No one knows what is coming after him." or "No one knows what will happen after he dies." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
