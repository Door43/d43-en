===== Translation Notes: =====
Some proverbs

    * **wisdom provides an advantage for success** - A wise person would have sharpened his blade and would not have had to work so hard.
    * **before it is charmed** - AT: "before the snake charmer charms it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
