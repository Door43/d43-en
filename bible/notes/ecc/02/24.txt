===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God's hand** - Here God is represented by his "hand" to emphasize how he provides for people. AT: "from God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **For who can eat or who can have any kind of pleasure apart from God?** - The author uses this rhetorical question to emphasize that no pleasure can be had without God's provision. This question can be written as a statement. AT: "For no one can eat or have any kind of pleasure apart from God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
