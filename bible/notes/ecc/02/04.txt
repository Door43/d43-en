===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I built houses for myself and planted vineyards** - The writer probably told people to do the work. AT: "I had people build houses and plant vineyards for me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **gardens and parks** - These two words share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **to water a forest** - "to provide water for a forest"
  * **forest where trees were grown** - AT: "forest where trees grew" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
