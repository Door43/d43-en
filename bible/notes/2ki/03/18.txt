===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is an easy thing in the sight of Yahweh** - Here Yahweh's "sight" refers to what he thinks. AT: "Yahweh considers this as an easy thing for him to do" or "This is an easy thing for Yahweh to do" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **fortified city** - A fortified city is well protected from enemies, by using things such as high walls or good locations.
