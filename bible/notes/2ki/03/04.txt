===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He had to give to the king of Israel**  - "Every year he was forced to give... to the king of Israel" (UDB); The reason Mesha had to give these things to the king of Israel is because his kingdom was controlled by the king of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to mobilize all Israel for war**  - AT: "to prepare the people of Israel for war" or "to get the people of Israel ready to go to war"
