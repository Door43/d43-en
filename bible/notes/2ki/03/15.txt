===== translationNotes: =====

  * **And it came to pass** - "And it happened that" 
  * **harpist** - someone who plays the harp
  * **the hand of Yahweh came upon Elisha** - Here Yahweh's "hand" refers to his "power." AT: "the power of Yahweh came upon Elisha" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **trenches** - A trench is a long ditch that workers dig in the ground, where water can collect. 
  * ** this river valley will be filled with water** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will fill this river valley with water" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
