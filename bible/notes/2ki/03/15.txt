===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **harpist**  - A harpist is someone who plays the harp.
    * **hand of Yahweh**  - Here "hand" is used to represent power.  
    * **full of trenches**  - A trench is a long hole that is dug in the ground, where water can pool.
