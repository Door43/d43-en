===== Translation Notes: =====

The narrative continues to summarize Yahweh's judgement on Israel.

  * **Yahweh had testified...by every prophet** - Yahweh spoke through the prophets.
  * **Turn from your evil ways** - "Stop doing the evil things you have been doing"
  * **I sent to you by my servants the prophets** - The prophets were sent by Yahweh to remind the people of God's Laws and to obey them.
