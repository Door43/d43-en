===== Translation Notes: =====

The narrative continues to summarize Yahweh's judgement on Israel.

  * **not right against Yahweh** - "not right before Yahweh"
  * **the tower of the watchman** -  the smallest kind of town
  * **fortified city** - a large city, well protected from enemies
