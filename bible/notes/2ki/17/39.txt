===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The summary now ends with an appeal to worship only Yahweh and a description of the sins of the people.

  * **They would not listen** - "Listen" is a metaphor for paying attention and acting on the command. AT: "They did not obey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **these nations feared Yahweh** - These nations feared Yahweh only to the point of appeasing him the same way they treated their own gods.
  * **up to this day** - "and have continued ever since." The phrase "this day" refers to the time period in which the writer lived.
