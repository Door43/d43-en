===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **might of your enemies** - "from your enemies" or "from the power of all your enemies" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They would not listen** - "They did not obey" 
  * **these nations feared Yahweh** - These nations feared Yahweh only to the point of appeasing him the same way they treat their own gods.
