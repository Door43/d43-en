===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The summary of Yahweh's judgment on Israel includes how Judah also fell into idolatry.


  * **Judah** - The location "Judah" is metonymy for the people that live there. AT: "the people of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he afflicted them** - "Yahweh punished the Israelites"
  * **gave them into the hand of those who would take the possession as spoil** - "Hand" is metonymy for control, power or authority. AT: "handed them over to those who robbed them of their property" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **until he had cast them out of his sight** - "his sight" is metaphor for attention and caring. AT: "until he got rid of them all" or "until they were no longer in his presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
