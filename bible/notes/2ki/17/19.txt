===== Translation Notes: =====

The summary of Yahweh's judgement on Israel includes leading Judah into Idolatry.


  * **Judah** - "the people of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he afflicted them**  - "Yahweh punished the Israelites"
  * **gave them into the hand of those who would take the possession as spoil**  - AT: "handed them over to those who robbed them of their property"
  * **until he had cast them out from his sight**  - AT: "He got rid of them all" (UDB) or "until they were no longer in his presence"
