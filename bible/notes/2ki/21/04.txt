===== translationNotes: =====


  * **General Information:** - The story of King Mannaseh's reign continues.

  * **It is in Jerusalem that my name will be forever.** - AT: "Jerusalem is where I will forever make known who I am." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for all the stars of heaven** - AT: "for the people to worship all the stars of heaven"
  * **He offered his son as a burnt offering in the fire** - AT: "He burned his son to death as an offering to his gods."
  * **consulted** - "asked for information from"
  * **evil in the sight of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:03:01|3:2]]
