===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Edom revolted from under** - "Edom rebelled against"
  * **the hand of Judah** - Here the word "hand" refers to the control of Judah, and "Judah" refers specifically to the king of Judah. AT: "the control of the king of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they set a king over themselves** - "they appointed a king to rule over themselves"
  * **Then Jehoram crossed over** - What was "crossed over" can be stated clearly. AT: "Then Jehoram crossed the enemy lines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
