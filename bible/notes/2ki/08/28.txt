===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ahaziah went with**   - "Ahaziah and his army went with" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Joram, the son of Ahab**  - "Joram, the son of Ahab, and his army" 
  * **to fight against Hazael, king of Aram **- "to fight against the army of Hazael, king of Aram" 
  * **The Arameans wounded Joram** - AT: "During the battle with the Arameans, Joram was injured"
  * **that the Arameans had given him at Ramah** - "he received during the battle with the Arameans at Ramah"
