===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to the king** - This refers to the king of Israel.
  * **for her house and for her land** - While the woman was gone her home and property were sieged. She is begging for them to be returned to her. AT: "for her property to be returned to her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
