===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ben Hadad** - The name of the king of Aram. His name means "son of Hadad." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **attacked Samaria** - The king and his army attacked Samaria. AT: "they attacked Samaria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **a donkey's head was sold for** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "a donkey's head cost" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **eighty pieces of silver** - "80 pieces of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the fourth part of a kab** - This can be written in modern measurements. AT: "a fourth part of a liter" or "a quarter of a liter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **the fourth part** - "the 4th part." This is one part out of four equal parts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
  * **dove's dung for** - It is understood that this refers to the cost of the dung. AT: "dove's dung was sold for" or "dove's dung cost" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]]) 
  * **was passing by on the wall** - "walking on top of the city wall" 
  * **my master** - The woman referred to the king by this name to show him respect.
