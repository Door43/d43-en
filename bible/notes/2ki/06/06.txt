===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the man of God said** - "So Elisha, the man of God, asked"
  * **He then cut off a stick, threw it in the water, and made the iron float** - God uses Elisha to perform a miracle. The ax head rises to the surface of the water and it stays there so the prophet can pick it up.
  * **made the iron float** - "caused the iron to float" 
  * **the iron** - "the ax head." The ax head was made of iron.
