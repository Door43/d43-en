===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the king** - This refers to the king of Aram.
  * **the man of God** - "Elisha" or "Elisha the man of God"
  * **behold** - The word "behold" here shows that the servant was surprised by what he saw.  
  * **had risen early and gone outside, behold** - "got up early in the morning and went outside, and he saw" 
  * **And his servant said to him** - The servant went back inside the house to tell Elisha what he had seen. AT: "The servant went back inside and said to Elisha," (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **those who are with us are more than those who are with them** - "To be with someone" in battle means to fight for their side. AT: "those who are on our side in the battle are more than those who are on their side" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
