====== 2 Kings 24 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

These last two chapters are the final defeat of Judah and the beginning of their exile to Babylon.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===
== God's punishment ==
God punished Judah because of their sin. The Babylonians conquer Jerusalem and carry away all the craftsmen, soldiers and wealthy. They make Zedekiah the king. He was not truly a king because he lacked absolute authority. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
