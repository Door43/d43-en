===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Nehushta, Elnathan**  - Nehushta is the name of a woman. Elnathan is the name of a man.(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **He did what was evil in the sight of Yahweh**  - "Jehoiachin did what Yahweh said was evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **he did all that his father had done**  - "just as his father had done"
