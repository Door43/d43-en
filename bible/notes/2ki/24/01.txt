===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In Jehoiakim's days**  ​- AT: "During the time that Jehoiakim ruled Judah"
  * **attacked Judah** - AT: "attacked and defeated Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Jehoiakim became his servant for three years. **- "as a result, Jehoiakim was required to pay much tribute to King Nebuchadnezzar." (UDB)​
  * **This was in conformity with the word of Yahweh** -  ​AT: "This was according to the word of Yahweh"
