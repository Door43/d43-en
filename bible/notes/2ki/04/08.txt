===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she urged him to eat food with her** - AT: "she asked him to come to her house to have a meal"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **passed by** - "traveled through Shunem"
  * **See, now I realize** - "Now I understand" 
  * **who is always passing by** - "who travels by regularly"
