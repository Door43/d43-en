===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the man of God** - This refers to Elisha. AT: "Elisha, the man of God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **live with your sons on the rest** - This means to use the money to buy the things that they need, such as food and clothing. AT: "use the rest of the money for you and your sons for what you need to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
