===== translationNotes: =====

  * **are they not written in The Book of the Events of the Kings of Judah?** - This question is used to remind the reader that these things are recorded. See how this phrase is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:08:22|8:23]]. AT: "they are written in the book of the History of the Kings of Judah." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **They made a conspiracy against Amaziah in Jerusalem** - A conspiracy is a secret plan to do harm to someone or something. AT: "Some people in Jerusalem plotted against Amaziah" 
  * **Lachish** - This is a city in southwestern Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **but they sent men after him to Lachish** - The men who made the conspiracy sent other men to follow Amaziah to Lachish.
