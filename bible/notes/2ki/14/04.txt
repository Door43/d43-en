===== Translation Notes: =====

Scripture continues to describe the rule of Amaziah, King of Judah. 

  * **But the shrines were not taken away** - AT: "But he did not remove the places of pagan worship."
  * **It came about that as soon as his rule was well established** - AT: "And so, as soon as Amaziah had securely established his rule,"
  * **he killed the servants who had murdered his father, the king** - AT: "he killed the leaders who had murdered his father, the king."
