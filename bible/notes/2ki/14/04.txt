===== Translation Notes: =====

Scripture continues to describe the rule of Amaziah, King of Judah. 

  * **But the high places were not taken away** - AT: "But he did not remove the high places, the shrines of pagan worship that were built upon the hills."
  * **As soon as he was completely in control of his kingdom** - AT: "And so, as soon as Amaziah securely established his royal rule and kingly authority,"
  * **he executed the servants who had murdered his father, the king** - AT: "he put to death the leaders who had murdered his father, the king."
