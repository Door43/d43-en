===== Translation Notes: =====

This is what happened after the death of King Amaziah.

  * **They brought him back on horses** - AT: "They brought Amaziah's body back on horses," 
  * **All the people of Judah took Uzziah, who was sixteen years old, and made him king in place of his father Amaziah** - AT: "The people of Judah took the 16 year-old Uzziah and made him king after his father, Amaziah."
  * **Eloth** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **restored it to Judah** - AT: "returned it to Judah."
