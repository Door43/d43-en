===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all the men of Judah and all the inhabitants of Jerusalem** - This is a generalization. AT: "many other people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **from small to great** - This merism includes everyone in between. AT: "from the least important to the most important" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **He then read in their hearing** - "Then the king read aloud so that they could hear"
  * **that had been found** - In [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:22:08|22:8]] it states that Hilkiah found the book. This can be translated in active form. AT: "that Hilkiah had found" or "that they had found" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
