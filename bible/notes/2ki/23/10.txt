===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Topheth ... Ben Hinnom** - place names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **put his son or his daughter into the fire as a sacrifice to Molech** - "put his son or his daughter in the fire and burn them as an offering to Molech"
  * **He took away** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people, perhaps Hilkiah and "the priests under him" ([[:en:bible:notes:2ki:23:04|23:4]]), might have helped Josiah do this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the horses** - Possible meanings are 1) real horses or 2) statues of horses.
  * **had given to the sun** - Here "given" represents being dedicated for worship. AT: "had used to worship the sun" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Nathan-Melek** - a man's name (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
