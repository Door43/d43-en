===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Keep the Passover**  - "You must celebrate the Passover"
    * **the days of the judges who ruled over Israel** - This refers to the nation of Israel before it was divided. 
    * **the days of the kings of Israel or Judah** - This refers to the kingdoms of Israel and Judah that formed when the nation of Israel split in two.  
    * **Such a Passover celebration had never been held from the days of**  - "This kind of Passover festival had not been held since the time of"
