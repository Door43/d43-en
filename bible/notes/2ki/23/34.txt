===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jehoikim taxed the land** - "Jehoikim collected taxes from the people who owned land"
  * **people of the land** - "people of the land of Judah." Possible meanings are 1) "the people who lived in the land of Judah" or 2) "the richest and most powerful of those who lived in Judah."
