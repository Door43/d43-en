===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wept** - "cried" (UDB)
  * **My father, my father** - Elisha was not the king's literal father. King Joash used this term as a sign of respect.
  * **and the horsemen** - AT: "and the drivers of the chariots" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_explicit]])
