===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The anger of Yahweh burned against Israel** - AT: "Then Yahweh had an intense anger against Israel,"
  * **handed him over to Hazael king of Aram, and to Benhadad the son of Hazael** - Here "him" refers to Israel.  AT: "allowed Hazael king of Aram and Benhadad, his son, to defeat the Israelites in battle."
  * **implored Yahweh** - "prayed to Yahweh,"
