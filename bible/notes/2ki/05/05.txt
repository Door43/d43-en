===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will send a letter** - The king is going to give the letter to Naaman to take with him to the king of Israel. AT: "I will send a letter with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **ten talents of silver, six thousand pieces of gold** - "10 talents of silver, 6,000 pieces of gold." This can be written in modern measurements. AT: "340 kilograms of sliver, 6,000 pieces of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **took with him ten ... clothes** - AT: "took with him ten ... clothes, which were gifts for the king of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **six thousand** - "6,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
