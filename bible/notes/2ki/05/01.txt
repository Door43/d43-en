===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in his master's view** - "sight." The king's "view" represents what he thinks about something. AT: "in the king's opinion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **because by him Yahweh had given victory to Aram** - Here "Aram" refers to the Aramean army. AT: "because through Naaman, Yahweh had given victory to the Aramean army"
  * **The Arameans had gone out** - Here the "Arameans" refer to the Aramean soldiers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **raiding in bands** - "in small groups attacking." This means to go out attacking an enemy in small groups.
