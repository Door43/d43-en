===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the letter**  - This refers to the letter that the king of Aram sent with Naamum to the king of Israel.
  * **he tore his clothes**  - Because of his distress, he ripped his clothes. (See: Making Implicit Information Explicit)
  * **"Am I God, to kill and to make alive, that this man wants me to cure a man of his leprosy?"- ** AT: "Does the king of Aram think I am God, with the power over death and life? He wants me to cure this man of his leprosy, but I can not do that." (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **It seems he is seeking to start an argument with me.**- The king of Israel could not believe the request by the king of Aram for him to heal Naamum was the real reason the letter. So, he made this statement. AT: "He is looking for an excuse to start a fight with me."
