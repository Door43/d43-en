===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jeroboam reigned over Israel in Samaria for six months** - Samaria is the city that he lived in when was king of Israel. AT: "Jeroboam lived in Samaria and reigned over Israel for six months"
  * **Zechariah son of Jeroboam** - This Jeroboam was the second king of Israel that had that name. He was the son of King Jehoash.
  * **He did what was evil** - "Zechariah did what was evil" 
  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - The sight of Yahweh represents Yahweh's judgment. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:03:01|3:2]]. AT: "what was evil in Yahweh's judgment" or "what Yahweh considers to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He did not depart from the sins of Jeroboam son of Nebat** - Departing from sins represents refusing to do those sins. AT: "Zechariah did not refuse to commit the sins of Jeroboam son of Nebat" or "He sinned as Jeroboam son of Nebat had sinned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Jeroboam son of Nebat** - This Jeroboam was the first king of the ten northern tribes that made up the kingdom of Israel. 
  * **who had caused Israel to sin** - Here the word "Israel" represents the people of the kingdom of Israel. AT: "who had caused the people of Israel to sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
