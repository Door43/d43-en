===== translationNotes: =====

  * **went up from there to Bethel** - The phrase "went up" is used her because Bethel is higher in elevation than Jericho. 
  * **Go up** - The young boys wanted Elisha to go away from them and expressed this by saying "go up." AT: "Go away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **baldhead** - A bald person does not have any hair on their head. The young boys were mocking Elisha for having a bald head. 
  * **forty-two of the boys** - "42 of the boys" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
