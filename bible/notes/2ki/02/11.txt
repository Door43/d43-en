===== translationNotes: =====

  * **behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **a chariot of fire and horses of fire** - Here the phrase "of fire" means that these were surround by fire. AT: "a chariot surrounded by fire pulled by horses surrounded by fire" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_possession]])
  * **went up by a whirlwind into heaven** - "was carried into the sky by a whirlwind." Translate the word "whirlwind" the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:02:01|2:1]].
  * **My father, my father** - Elisha is calling Elijah his respected leader.
  * **tore them into two pieces** - People would often tear their clothes as a sign of great sadness or grief. AT: "ripped them in two pieces to show his great sadness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
