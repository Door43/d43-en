===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **healed these waters** - Yahweh made the water become good and pure again.
  * **there will be no more death or unfruitful land** - AT: "there will be no more death or problems with crops caused by this water": or "this water will not cause death or crop failure any more" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
