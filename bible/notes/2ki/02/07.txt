===== translationNotes: =====

  * **fifty of the sons** - "50 of the sons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the sons of the prophets** - This does not mean that they were the sons of prophets, but rather, that they were a group of prophets. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:02:03|2:3]]. AT: "the group of men who were prophets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **stood opposite them** - This means to be standing facing them. AT: "facing them" or "stood watching them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **cloak** - outer piece of clothing used as a covering
  * **The river divided on both sides so that the two of them walked over on dry ground** - "The water of the Jordan River opened up so there was a dry path for Elijah and Elisha to cross over to the other side"
  * **on both sides** - "to the right and the left." This refers to the right and the left of where Elijah struck the water.
