===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **urged Elisha until he was ashamed** - The sons of the prophets kept asking Elisha until he felt bad about saying no. 
  * **Did I not say to you, 'Do not go'?** - AT: "I told you that you should not go, because you would not find him!" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
