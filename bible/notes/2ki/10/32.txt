===== translationNotes: =====

  * **began to cut off regions from Israel** - "began to cause the territory controlled by Israel to become smaller"
  * **regions** - "areas of land"
  * **Hazael defeated** - Here "Hazael" refers to himself and his army. AT: "Hazael and his army" or "King Hazael's Aramean army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Hazael** - See how you translated this king's name in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:08:07|8:8]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **from the Jordan eastward** - "from the land east of the Jordan" 
  * **Aroer ... Bashan** - These are all names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the Arnon** - "the Arnon River" This is the name of a river. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
