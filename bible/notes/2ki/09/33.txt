===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Throw her down** - Jehu was telling the eunuchs to throw Jezebel out of the window.
  * **So they threw Jezebel down** - The eunuchs threw Jezebel out of the high window and she died when she hit the ground. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **Jehu trampled her under foot** - This means that he drove his horses over her body. AT: "Jehu's horses that were pulling his chariot trampled her body under their feet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **See now to** - The phrase "see now to" means to give your attention to whatever is specified. AT: "Now go to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for she is a king's daughter** - Since Jezebel was the daughter of a king, it was necessary to bury her properly. AT: "because she is a king's daughter and therefore should be buried properly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
