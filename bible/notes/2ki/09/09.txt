===== Translation Notes: =====

The young prophet is continuing to speak to Jehu, whom he had just anointed as king over Israel.

  * **I will make the house of Ahab like the house of Jeroboam the son of Nebat and like the house of Baasha the son of Anhijah** - This is a message from Yahweh through the young prophet.  He says that Ahab and his family will be destroyed as were Jeroboam and Baasha and their families before him.
  * **The dogs will eat Jezebel**  - AT: "Dogs will eat the dead body (UDB: corpse) of Jezebel"
