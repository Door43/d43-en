===== translationNotes: =====

  * **turned his chariot and fled** - "turned his chariot around to try to flee"
  * **treachery** - trickery or deception
  * **with his full strength** - "with all of his strength" or "with all of his power"
  * **he sank down in his chariot** - Joram died from being shot with arrow. AT: "Joram fell down dead in his chariot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
