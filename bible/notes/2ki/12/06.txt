===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why have you not repaired anything in the temple?**  - AT: "You should have been repairing the temple!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Now take no more money** - "From now on, take no more money for your own use" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **but take what has been collected for repairs of the temple** - AT: "but take the money that has been collected and use it for repairs of the temple"
  * **to those who can make the repairs**  - AT: "to pay workers who know how and will make the repairs." 
  * **consented**  - "agreed"
