===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **weighed out**  - AT: "counted out" or "given out" 
  * **into the hands of the men**  - "to the men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **who took care of**  - AT: "who were working on the repairs of"
  * **carpenters **- people who build and repair things made of wood
  * **masons **- people who build with stone
  * **for buying timber and cutting stone**  - "to use for buying wood and stone to cut"
