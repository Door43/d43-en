====== 2 Kings 12 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is the end of the story of the revival in Judah under Joash.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Temple ==
Joash repaired the temple. In many ways, the temple represents the relationship between the people and Yahweh. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])
