===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **took it**  - AT: "defeated and took control of it"
    * **his fathers**  - not his actual father, but previous kings of Judah (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor]])
    * **Then Hazael went away from Jerusalem**  - The gifts Joash gave Hazael convinced him not to attack Jerusalem.
