===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **did not pay to make for it** - AT: "was not spent to make"
  * **silver cups, lamp trimmers, basins, trumpets, or any gold or silver furnishing** - These are items that would have been used by the priests for various temple tasks, such as sacrifices or festivals.
