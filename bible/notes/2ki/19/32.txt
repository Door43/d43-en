===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of the message from Yahweh, spoken through the prophet Isaiah to King Hezekiah.  It uses parallelism throughout. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **nor shoot an arrow here** - "Arrow" is metonymy representing all tools of warfare and destruction. AT: "nor do any fighting here" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **or build up a siege ramp against it** - "and they will not even build up high mounds of earth against the city wall to enable them to attack the city" 
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **for my own sake and for my servant David's sake** - "for the sake of my own reputation and because of what I promised to King David, who served me well"
