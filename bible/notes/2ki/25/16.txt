===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the sea** - "the large bronze basin"
    * **cubit**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
    * **a capital of bronze**  - "a bronze piece with many designs"
    * **latticework**  - a design made of crossed strips which may look like a net (UDB) 
    * **pomegranates** - Pomegranates are a red colored fruit the size of an orange.
