====== 2 Kings 25 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is the final defeat of Judah and the beginning of their exile to Babylon. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Zedekiah's revolt ==
Zedekiah revolted against the Babylonians, so they came and completely destroyed Jerusalem, tear down the wall and take the rest of the people except the very poorest to Babylon as slaves.
