===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nebuzaradan** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:25:08|25:8]].
  * **Riblah** - This is the name of a place. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:25:06|25:6]].
  * **put them to death** - This is a polite way of saying "killed them." It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people might have helped the king do this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **In this way, Judah went out of its land into exile** - "So Judah was taken into exile out of its land"
  * **Judah went out of its land** - Judah, the name of the people group, is a metonym for the people themselves. AT: "the people of Judah went out of their land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
