===== translationNotes: =====


  * **in the thirty-seventh year** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the twelfth month, on the twenty-seventh day of the month** - This is the twelfth month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-seventh day is near the beginning of April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Evil Merodach** - This is the name of a man, not a description of him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
