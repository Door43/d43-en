===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all the fortified cities of Judah**  - "all the cities in Judah that had walls around them" (UDB) A fortified city is one that has had walls built around it to protect it from enemy attack.
  * **sent word**  - "sent a message" (UDB) (See: Merism) or (See: Idiom)
  * **I have offended you**  - "What I have done was wrong" (UDB) King Hezekiah is saying this to king Sennacherib.
  * **Withdraw from me**  - AT: "Take your army out of my cities." (See: Making Implicit Information Explicit)
  * **Whatever you put on me I will bear**  - AT: "I will pay you whatever you demand of me"
  * **talent**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalmoney|biblical money]])
