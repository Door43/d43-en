===== Translation Notes: =====


  Rabshakeh continues to talk to the people, giving them a message from the king of Assyria.  In this part of his message he uses several rhetorical questions to make them think about their situation.  
  
    * **Hamath, Arpad, Sepharvaim, Hena, Ivvah**  - (See: Translate Names)  These are all names of places.
    * **out of my hand**  - AT: "out from my control"
    * **from my power**  - "from me" (See: Metonomy)
