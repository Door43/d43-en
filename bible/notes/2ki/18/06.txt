===== Translation Notes: =====

The story of King Hezekiah's reign continues.

  * **he held on to Yahweh** - AT: "Hezekiah stayed loyal to Yahweh." or "Hezekiah remained faithful to Yahweh." 
  * **and wherever he went he prospered** - AT: "and wherever Hezekiah went he was successful." 
  * **fortified city** - city with a wall all around it
