===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Eliakim ... Shebna ... Joah ... Asaph** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who was over the household** - "who managed the king's palace"
  * **the recorder** - "the history keeper"
  * **chief commander** - and is the translation of the Hebrew; some see this as a personal name, "Rabshakeh"
