===== Translation Notes: =====

This is the end of the message from Yahweh to King Josiah through the prophetess Huldah.

  * **See, I will gather you to your ancestors**  - AT: "Listen, you will die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **and your eyes not see all the disaster **  - AT: "and you will not experience any of the disaster" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
