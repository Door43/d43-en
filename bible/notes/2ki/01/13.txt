===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **implored him** - "begged him"
  * **these fifty servants of yours** - AT "my fifty soldiers" (UDB)
  * **be precious in your sight** - AT: "be of worth to you" or "be spared from death"; The captain is asking Elijah to be kind and spare his life and the lives of the fifty men.
