===== Translation Notes: =====

This is what King Ahaz did after he returned from Damascus and visited the new altar he had ordered to be built.

  * **He made his** - "King Ahaz made his" 
  * **the bronze altar** ​- This was the altar that the people of Israel had made according to God's instructions.
