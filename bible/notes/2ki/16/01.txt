===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the seventeenth year of Pekah son of Remaliah** - It can be stated clearly that this is the seventeenth year of his reign. AT: "In year 17 of the reign of Pekah son of Remaliah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Pekah ... Remaliah** - These are men's names. Pekah was the king of Israel. See how you translated them in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:15:25|15:25]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh his God** - The eyes represent seeing, and seeing represents thoughts or judgment. AT: "what was right in Yahweh's judgment" or "what Yahweh his God considers to be right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **as David his ancestor had done** - David had done what is right.
