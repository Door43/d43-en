===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the portable stands** - "the movable stands" or "the carts." These stands had wheels on them so they could be moved around.  
  * **he also took down the sea** - "he also removed the large bowl." The "sea" was a huge basin or water bowl that was made of bronze.   
  * **because of the king of Assyria** - Why they did this can be stated explicitly. AT: "to please the king of Assyria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
