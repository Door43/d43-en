===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So Isaiah said to Hezekiah** - Why Isaiah spoke can be stated clearly. AT: "So, because Isaiah knew Hezekiah had been foolish to show the men all his valuable things, Isaiah said to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the word of Yahweh** - "the message of Yahweh" 
  * **Look, the days are about to come when** - "Listen to me, someday there will come a time when"; "Look" is used to draw attention to what Isaiah is about to tell Hezekiah.
  * **the days** - "Days" is synecdoche to refer to an undefined span of time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
