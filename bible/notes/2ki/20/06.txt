===== Translation Notes: =====


The message from Yahweh to King Hezekiah through the prophet Isaiah continues. 

    * **fifteen years** - 15 years (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **from the power of the King of Assyria** - "from the King of Assyria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **lump of figs** - "paste made of boiled figs" (UDB) 
    * **they did so and put it on his boil"** - AT: "Hezekiah's servants did this and put the paste on Hezekiah's sore"
