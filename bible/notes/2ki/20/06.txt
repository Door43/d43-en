===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The message from Yahweh to King Hezekiah through the prophet Isaiah continues. 

  * **fifteen years** - 15 years (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **from the hand of the king of Assyria** - "Hand" is metonymy for power, authority and control. AT: "from the control of the king of Assyria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **lump of figs** - "paste made of boiled figs" 
  * **They did so and put it on his boil** - "Hezekiah's servants did this and put the paste on Hezekiah's sore"
