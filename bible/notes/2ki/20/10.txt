===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **it is an easy thing for the shadow to go forward ten steps**  - "It is easy to cause the shadow to move forward ten steps, because that is the normal (or natural) thing for it to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]])
    * **the stairway of Ahaz**  - AT: "the steps that had been built for King Ahaz"
