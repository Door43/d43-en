===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for the day of Yahweh is approaching;**  - "for the awesome day of Yahweh's judgment is near;" or "because it will soon be the time when Yahweh will judge and punish people." (See UDB)
  * **for Yahweh has prepared the sacrifice and consecrated his guests.**  - AT: "for Yahweh has prepared for a great slaughter (or 'killing') of the people of Judah."
  * **It will come about on the day of Yahweh's sacrifice,**  - AT: "On that day of judgment,"
  * **that I will punish the princes and the king’s sons, **- AT: "I will punish the officials and the king's sons (or 'family')." These words refer to all the leaders of Judah.
  * **and everyone dressed in foreign clothes, **- AT: "and everyone who follows pagan customs and worship false gods."
  * **all those who leap over the doorsill,**  - (See Idiom) AT; "all those who follow pagan customs," or "all those who avoid stepping on the doorsill (or 'threshold') of the pagan temple,"  Not stepping on the threshold of the temple when entering was a pagan custom of those who worshipped the false god Dagan.
  * **those who fill their master’s house with violence and deceit!'**  - AT: "and those who lie and do violent things in their pagan temple!'"
