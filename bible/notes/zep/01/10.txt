===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verses 1:2-18 refer to Yahweh's judgment. Verses 1:4-16 describe Yahweh's judgment on the people of Judah.
  * **General Information:** - In verses 1:8-13, Yahweh is speaking. He alternates between using first person and speaking about himself in the third person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]]) 

  * **on that day** - "on the day of Yahweh." Translate this phrase as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:zep:01:07|1:9]].
  * **Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. AT: "what Yahweh has declared" or "what Yahweh has solemnly said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **Fish Gate** - The Fish Gate was one of the gates in the Jerusalem city wall.
  * **wailing from the Second District** - "Mourn loudly from the Second District." The Second District was a newer part of Jerusalem.
  * **a great crashing sound** - This refers to the sound of buildings collapsing. This can be stated explicitly. AT: "a loud sound of buildings collapsing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **from the hills** - This refers to the hills surrounding Jerusalem.
  * **for all the merchants will be ruined; all those who weigh out silver will be cut off** - These two phrases refer to the same people and are used to emphasize that business will be destroyed. AT: "for those who buy and sell goods will be killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **those who weigh out silver** - This refers to merchants. Before coins were used, people weighed out silver or gold as payment for things they bought. 
  * **cut off** - Destroying is spoken of as if it were cutting something off from what it was a part of. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zep:01:01|1:3]]. AT: "destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
