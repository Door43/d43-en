===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh's Declaration** - AT: "what Yahweh has declared” or "what Yahweh has solemnly said”
  * **Fish Gate**  - The Fish Gate is one of the gates in the surrounding walls that allowed people to enter and leave Jerusalem.
  * **wailing from the Second Quarter**  - AT: "Mourn loudly from the Second Quarter." The Second Quarter was a newer part of Jerusalem.  
  * **from the hills**  - AT: "from the hills around Jerusalem."
  * **Wail, inhabitants of the Market District** - "Mourn loudly, all who live where goods are sold." 
  * **for all the merchants will be ruined; all those who are weighted down with silver will be cut off.**  - AT: "for those who sell goods and are rich will be killed."
