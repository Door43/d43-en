===== Translation Notes: =====
   * **walk about like blind men,** **poured out like dust,** and **their inner parts like dung** - These three phrases indicate comparisons to show what they have in common.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]) 
   * **The fire of Yahweh's wrath**- This refers to the power of Yahweh's anger.  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
