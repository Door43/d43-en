===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh's declaration** - This means what Yahweh has solemnly said.
  * **to plunder** - This means to take the property of others often by conquering them in war. 
  * **to gather the nations, to assemble the kingdoms** - These two phrases mean the same thing and emphasize that Yahweh will judge all of the nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **my anger, all my fierce wrath** - The words "anger" and "fierce wrath" mean basically the same thing and emphasize the intensity of Yahweh's anger. AT: "my very fierce wrath." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **so that all the land will be devoured by the fire of my anger** - AT: "so that the fire of my anger will devour all the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **devoured by the fire of my anger** - Yahweh will destroy the land completely, like fire completely destroys the things that it burns. (See: [[: en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
