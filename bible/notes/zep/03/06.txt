===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I**  - "I" in v6-7 refers to Yahweh.
  * **"I have destroyed nations;**  - '"I have destroyed many nations;"
  * **their fortresses are ruined. **- AT: "their walls and towers are damaged too much to repair."
  * **I have made their streets ruins, so that no one passes over them.**  - AT: "I have made rubble of (or "have destroyed") their streets and no one has survived to walk on them."
  * **Their cities are destroyed so that there is no man inhabiting them.**  - AT: "No one is alive in the cities that are destroyed."
  * **I said, 'Surely you will fear me!**  - AT: "When you hear what I have done, surely my people will listen to my warnings and follow my instructions!"
  * **Accept correction**  - AT: " Heed my warnings and follow my way"
  * **and do not be cut off from your homes by all that I have planned to do to you!'"**  - AT: "and do not lose your homes because you do not repent and I punish you as I said I would."'
  * **But they were eager to begin each morning by corrupting all their deeds.**  - AT; "but no, instead of repenting, each day they eagerly sin in all they do."
