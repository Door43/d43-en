===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have heard the taunts of Moab and the reviling of the people of Ammon when they taunted my people **- AT: "I have heard the ridicule and insults of Israel by Moab and Ammon" The pronoun "I" refers to Yahweh in all places it is used in v8-9.
  * **and violated their borders.**  - AT: "and have seen their violations of the border."
  * **Therefore, as I live—this is the declaration of Yahweh of hosts, God of Israel,**  - AT: "So now, as surely as I live,"
  * **Moab will become like Sodom, and the people of Ammon like Gomorrah;**  - See (Simile) AT: "I will destroy Moab and Ammon as I destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah. See (UDB)
  * **a weedy place and a salt pit, deserted forever! **- AT: "Their land will only grow stinging nettles (or "weeds"). There will be salt pits, and total desolation."
  * **But the remnant of my people will plunder them,**  - AT: "The people of Judah who have survived will take all their valuable things"
  * **and the remainder of my nation will inherit from them!**  - AT: "and the people of Judah will occupy (or "take") their land!"
