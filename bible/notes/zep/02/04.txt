===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They will drive out Ashdod at noon, and they will uproot Ekron!**- The people will be quickly and completely be forced from their cities.  
  * **Yahweh has spoken against you ** - AT: "Yahweh has spoken judgment against you"
  * **until no inhabitant remains!** - AT: "until no one is there!"
