===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have returned to Jerusalem with mercies**  - AT: "I have come back to Jerusalem and will show mercy to Judah."
  * **My house will be built within her**  - AT: "My temple will be built inside the city of Jerusalem's walls."
  * **"and the measuring line will be stretched forth over Jerusalem!" **- AT: "and Jerusalem will be surveyed before rebuilding"
  * **Again call out,**  - "Again shout loudly,"
  * **My cities will once again overflow with goodness**  - AT: "The cities of Israel will once again be prosperous"
  * **Yahweh will again comfort Zion**  - "Yahweh will encourage the people of Israel."
  * **once again choose Jerusalem. **- AT: "choose Jerusalem as his precious possession."
