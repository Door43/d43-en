===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the twenty-fourth day of the eleventh month, which is the month of Shebat** - "Shebat" is the eleventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-fourth day is near the middle of February on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Berechiah ... Iddo** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **myrtle trees** - a kind of small tree with colorful flowers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
