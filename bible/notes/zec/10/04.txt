===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will come the tent peg**  - See (Metaphor) "will come leaders that will keep the people loyal to their community (or "Judah")"
  * **become like warriors**  - See (Simile) AT: "They will be mighty in battle"
  * **who trample their enemies into the mud of the streets in battle; **- See (Metonomy) AT: "who defeat their enemies triumphantly (or "overwhelmingly")."
  * **they will make war, for Yahweh is with them,**  - AT: "Yahweh is with them as they make war,"
  * **and they will shame those who ride warhorses.**  - AT: "and they even (or "easily") defeat the enemy that is on horses."
