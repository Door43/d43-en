===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **be in the streets of Jerusalem** - "be living in Jerusalem"
  * **in his hand because he has grown so old** - Having the opportunity to grow old is a sign of peace and prosperity.
  * **And the streets of the city will be full** - The public areas of the city will be full of people in their normal activities.
