===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh of Hosts came to me and said, **- AT: "Yahweh gave me another message for the people.
  * **"Yahweh of Hosts says this: **- "He says (or "said"):"
  * **I am passionate for her with great zeal!'**  - AT "I am consumed with passion (or "love") for Zion (or Jerusalem)!"
  * **Thus says Yahweh,**  - "Yahweh says,"
  * **'I have returned to Zion **- AT "'I am returning to Zion"
  * **will live in the midst of Jerusalem,**  - "will live in Jerusalem"
  * **City of Truth **  (and) **the Mountain of Yahweh of Hosts - **  See (How to Translate Names)
  * **the holy mountain!**  - "the Holy Mountain!"  Some manuscripts capitalize the title.
