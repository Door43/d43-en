===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then the word of Yahweh of hosts came to me** - "Me" refers to Zechariah.
  * **The fasts of the fourth month** - The Jews mourned during a part of the fourth month of the Hebrew calendar because this is when the Babylonians broke through the walls of Jerusalem. The fourth month is during the last part of June and the first part of July on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **fifth month** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:07:01|7:3]].
  * **seventh month** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:07:04|7:5]].
  * **tenth month** - The Jews mourned during a part of the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar because this is when the Babylonians began their siege against Jerusalem. The tenth month is during the last part of December and the first part of January on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
