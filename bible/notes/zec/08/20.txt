===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **'People will again come**  - "People will come to Jerusalem again"
  * **even those who are living in many different cities.**  - AT: "from many different cities and nations."
  * **The inhabitants of one city will go to another and say,**  - AT: "The people of one city will go to another city and say,"
  * **'Let us quickly go to beg for the favor of Yahweh and to seek Yahweh of Hosts!**  - AT: "'Let's go together to Jerusalem to worship Yahweh and request him to bless us!" (UDB)
  * **I**** myself will also go!'**  - "I am going!" or "I am determined to go!"
  * **Many people and mighty nations will come to seek Yahweh of Hosts in Jerusalem and beg for the favor of Yahweh!"**  - AT: "Many people from many nations will come to Jerusalem to seek Yahweh and ask for His blessing."
