===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will grasp the hem of your robe** - "will grasp your clothes to get your attention" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Let us go with you** - "Please let us travel to Jerusalem with you"
  * **God is with you** - "God is with you people"
