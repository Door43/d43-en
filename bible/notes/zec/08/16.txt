===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that you must do**  - "You" refers to the people of Judah.
  * **Speak truth, every person with his neighbor** - AT: "Tell the truth to every person"
  * **Judge with truth, justice, and peace in your gates** - AT: "Judge disputes justly in your places of judgment so that people can live in peace with each other"
  * **nor be attracted to false oaths** - AT: "do not approve when people tell lies in court cases"
