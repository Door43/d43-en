===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as in former days;**  - "as in the past;"
  * **I will be with the remnant of this people **- AT: "Now I will bless these people" or "Now I will treat these people kindly"
  * **this is Yahweh of Hosts' declaration.**  - AT: "Yahweh declares (or "says")."
  * **sown the seed of peace**  - See (Metonym) AT: "The people will have peace within themselves."
  * **will give its produce**  - AT See (Metonym)  AT: "will yield (or "produce") a large harvest of grapes"
  * **earth will give its produce.**  - AT: "crops will yield (or "produce") a good harvest."
  * **The skies will give their dew**  - "It will rain"
  * **the remnant of this people**  - AT: "the people in Judah at this time"
  * **all these things as an inheritance.**  - AT: "all these blessings as an inheritance."
