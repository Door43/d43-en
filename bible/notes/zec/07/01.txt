===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When King Darius had been ruler for four years** - "in the fourth year since Darius became king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **on the fourth day of Kislev (which was the ninth month)** - "Kislev" is the ninth month of the Hebrew calendar. The fourth day is near the end of November on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Sharezer and Regem Melech** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]).
  * **they said, "Should I mourn ... years?** - The word "they" refers to Sharezer and Regem Melech.
  * **in the fifth month** - The Jewish people fasted during a part of the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar because this is when the Babylonians destroyed the temple in Jerusalem. The fifth month is during the last part of July and the first part of August on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
