====== Zechariah 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter starts occurs about two years after the previous chapter and is written in prose. 

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of 7:4-14, which is an extended quotation.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Fasting ==
This chapter's topic is different. The author discusses fasting. These people were asking the same questions as those from previous generations. The Israelites were asking these same questions before they were exiled from their land.
