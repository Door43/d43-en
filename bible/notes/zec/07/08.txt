===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Let each man do this** - The word "this" refers to how a person should judge.
  * **widow** - a woman whose husband has died
  * **orphan** - a child whose parents have died
  * **foreigner** - someone who travels from his own land to a different land
  * **you must not plot any harm in your heart** - "you must not make plans to do evil"
