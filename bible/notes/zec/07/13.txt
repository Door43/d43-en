===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It happened that when he called, they did not hear.**  -  "When Yahweh called, the people would not listen."
  * **storm**  - See (Metaphor)  This is a very strong wind that spins and travels, scattering debris all around.  As used here, "whirlwind" describes the way Yahweh will scatter His disobedient people into many different and far away nations, driving them away from their land.
