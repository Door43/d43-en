===== translationNotes: =====


  * **the covenant was broken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the covenant ended" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **knew that Yahweh had spoken** - It is implied that those watching knew Yahweh was giving them a message through the breaking of the staff. AT: "knew that Yahweh had given them a message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **thirty pieces of silver** - It is implied that this was very little pay for the shepherd. AT: "only thirty pieces of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **thirty pieces** - "30 pieces" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
