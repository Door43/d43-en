===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Heldai, Tobijah, and Jedaiah ... Jehozadak** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Then take the silver and gold, make a crown** - AT: Then use the silver and the gold to make a crown. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Joshua son of Jehozadak** - This is not the same man who was Moses' helper; this is the high priest from the book of Haggai.
