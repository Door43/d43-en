===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tell him**  - The angel instructs the prophet Zechariah to inform the newly crowned king, Joshua son of Jehozadak.
  * **his name is Branch **- The angel of Yahweh pronounces a new name for the new king Joshua. "Branch" is the limb of a tree that connects the main trunk to smaller branches.
  * **he will grow up where he is **- First the king Joshua will grow to adulthood and then he will move to Jerusalem (where he will build the temple).
  * **its splendor**  - "its magnificence" or "its treasures"
  * **the counsel of peace**  - "peace" or "wise advice to be peaceful" (will succeed).
  * **between the two**  - The king will serve as both king and priest.
