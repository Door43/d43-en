===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The crown will given** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You will give the crown" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Heldai, Tobijah, Jedaiah ... Hen** - These are the names of men. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:06:09|6:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **for Hen son of Zephaniah as a memorial** - Some modern versions interpret this phrase as "as a memorial to the generosity of the son of Zephaniah" or "as a memorial to the one who is gracious, the son of Zephaniah." Also, some modern versions interpret the name "Hen" as meaning the name "Josiah."
  * **those who are far off** - This refers to the Israelites who remain in Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **so you will know** - The word "you" is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **truly listen to the voice of Yahweh your God** - Here to "listen" means to obey and the word "voice" represents the words that Yahweh speaks. AT: "truly obey what Yahweh your God says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
