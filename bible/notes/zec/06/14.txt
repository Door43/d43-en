===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a crown will be placed**  - This could be written as, "The builders will put a crownâ€¦" (See: Passive.)
  * **to the generosity**  of - "to the great gifts of" or "to the giving spirit" or "to remember how generous" (the son of Zephaniah was).
  * **those who are far off**  - This refers to the Israelites who remain in Babylon.
  * **so you will know**  - This "you" refers to both the prophet himself and the people.
  * **has sent me to you**  - "has sent me, the prophesying angel to you, Zechariah the prophet"
  * **truly listen**  - Listening truly is demonstrated by obedience after receiving the message.
