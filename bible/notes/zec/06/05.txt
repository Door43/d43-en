===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **said to me**  - The angel in his vision speaks to the prophet Zechariah.
  * **four winds**  - In some languages, the "four winds" has a symbolic or idiomatic meaning. (See: Idioms.)
  * **their station before the Lord**  - This implies that the winds have their beginning in heaven or in the presence of God.
  * **(special or general note) **- In most languages, a wind is described by its origin. Thus, the black horse would be the southern wind, the white horse, the eastern wind, etc.
