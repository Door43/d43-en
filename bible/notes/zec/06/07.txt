===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sought to go**  - "intended to go" or "meant to go"
  * **roam over**  - "spread over" or "wander over" (although wander may not fit in with the idea that they had a plan).
  * **they left for all the earth**  - As winds, the four horses could spread over every part of the earth.
  * **the ones**  - "the (black) horses"
  * **they have brought my spirit's rest**  - The rest (or peace or calmness) of God's spirit is carried by the horses like a gift from God to the northern country.
