===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will appear to them**  - AT: "will be seen in the sky"
  * **will be like lightning bolts**  - The Israelites sometimes thought of lightning bolts as arrows shot by God.
  * **blow the trumpet**  - "blow the ram's horn" The horns of rams were made into horns to communicate and signal.
  * **will advance with the whirlwinds from Teman**
 - The Israelites sometimes thought of God as traveling on violent storms coming from the south.
  * **Then they will be as full of wine as the bowl that holds the blood of the animals priests kill at the altar**  - AT: " They will shout and celebrate the battle as though they were drunk" (See: simile
  * **and they will be filled like basins**  - See ( Simile) AT: "and they will be just as full of wine as the altar basins"
  * **like the corners of the altar**  - AT: "and the corners of the altar are full of blood"
