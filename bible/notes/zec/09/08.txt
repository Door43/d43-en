===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will camp around my land** - God is speaking about himself as if he were an army that was protecting his land. AT: "I will protect my land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **For now** - "For at that time"
  * **I will watch over my land with my own eyes** - Here "my own eyes" represent Yahweh's personal attention. AT: "I will personally watch over my land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
