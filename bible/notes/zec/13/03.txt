===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his father and mother who bore him** - The phrase "who bore him" describes "his father and mother" in order to express surprise that someone's own parents would treat him in this way.  
  * **You will not live**  - AT: "You must die"
  * **will pierce** - Stab him to kill him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
