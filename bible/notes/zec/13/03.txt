===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to prophesy** - It is implied that these are false prophecies. AT: "to prophesy falsely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **his father and mother who bore him** - The phrase "who bore him" describes "his father and mother" in order to express surprise that someone's own parents would treat him in this way. AT: "his own father and mother" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
  * **You will not live** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "You must die"
  * **you speak lies in the name of Yahweh** - Speaking "in the name of God" means speaking with his power and authority, or as his representative. AT: "you claim to speak for Yahweh but you speak lies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will pierce** - "will stab and kill him"
