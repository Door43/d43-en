===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the one who stretched out the sky,**  - "the one who created the sky"
  * **laid the foundation of the earth,**  - "put all the pieces of the earth in place,"
  * **fashions the spirit of mankind within him**  - AT: "creates the human spirit".
  * **Behold**  - "Pay attention"
  * **burdensome stone against all the people. **- AT: "an unmovable obstacle."
  * **Anyone trying to lift it will hurt themselves very much,**  - "Anyone that tries to move it will hurt themselves very much,
