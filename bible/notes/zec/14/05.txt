===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the description of the final war for the city of Jerusalem and of how God will save her.

  * **you will flee** - Here "you" is plural and refers to the people of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **between Yahweh's mountains** - This refers to the mountains created after the Mount of Olives split in half.
  * **Azal** - This is the name of a town or village east of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **You will flee just as you fled** - Here "You will" refers to the people of Jerusalem. But, "you fled" refers to their ancestors since this describes an event that happened many years earlier. AT: "You will flee just as your ancestors fled" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the days of Uzziah, king of Judah** - Here "in the days" is an idiom that refers to the time when Uzziah was king. AT: "when Uzziah was king of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the holy ones** - This probably refers to God's angels.
