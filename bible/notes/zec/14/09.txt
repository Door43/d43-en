===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the description of the final war for the city of Jerusalem and of how God will save her.

  * **there will be Yahweh, the one God, and his name alone** - "there will only be one God, Yahweh, who will be worshiped." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
  * **Arabah ... Geba ... Rimmon** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jerusalem will continue to be elevated** - This refers to the altitude of the city of Jerusalem, about 760 meters above sea level.
  * **she** - Jerusalem and other cities were often spoken of as if they were women. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **will live in her own place** - "will live where she has always lived"
  * **the Benjamin Gate ... the first gate ... the Corner Gate** - These are names of various gates in the city wall of Jerusalem.
  * **the Tower of Hananel** - This refers to a strong point in the city defenses on the wall. It was probably built by a man named Hananel.
  * **the king's winepresses** - This probably refers to the place where wine was made for the royal family.
