===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This chapter describes the final war for the city of Jerusalem and how God will save her.

  * **your plunder will be divided in your midst** - AT: "your enemies will take all of your possessions and divide it for themselves while you watch" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I will gather every nation against Jerusalem for battle** - AT: "I will cause many nations to attack Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **the remainder of the people will not be cut off from the city** - AT: "your enemies will allow the remaining people to stay in the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
