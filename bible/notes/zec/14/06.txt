===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the description of the final war for the city of Jerusalem and of how God will save her.

  * **living waters** - This normally means running or flowing water, rather than still or stagnant water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the eastern sea** - This refers to the Dead Sea, which is east of Jerusalem.
  * **the western sea** - This refers to the Mediterranean Sea.
