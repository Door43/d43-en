===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Lord, show us the Father** - The "Father" is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **Have I been so long with you, and you still do not know me, Philip?** - This remark appears in the form of a question to add emphasis to Jesus' words. AT: "Philip, I have been with you disciples already for a very long time. You should know me by now!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Whoever has seen me has seen the Father** - To see Jesus, who is God the Son, is to see God the Father. The "Father" is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])  
  * **how can you say, 'Show us the Father'?** - This remark appears in the form of a question to emphasize Jesus' words to Philip. AT: "So you really should not be saying, 'Show us the Father!'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
