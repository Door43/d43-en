===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jesus said to her** - "Jesus asked her"
  * **Sir, if you have taken him away** - Here the word "him" refers to Jesus. AT: "If you have taken away the body of Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **tell me where you have put him** - "tell me where you have put it" 
  * **I will take him away** - Mary Magdalene wants to get Jesus' body and bury it again. AT: "I will get the body and bury it again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
