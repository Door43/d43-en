===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that day, the first day of the week** - This refers to Sunday.
  * **Peace be to you** - This is the common greeting.
  * **he showed them his hands and his side.** - "he showed them the wounds on his hands and his side" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
