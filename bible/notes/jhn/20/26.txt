===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his disciples** - The word "his" refers to Jesus.
  * **while the doors were closed** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "when they had locked the doors" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **Peace to you** - This is a common greeting that means "May God give you peace" .
  * **unbelieving** - "without belief" or "without faith"
  * **but believe** - Here "believe" means to trust in Jesus. AT: "put your trust in me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
