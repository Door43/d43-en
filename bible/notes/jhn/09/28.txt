===== Important Terms: ===== 

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we are disciples** - In both verses "we" refers to the Jewish leaders. In both cases, the word "we" is exclusive and does not include anyone except for the Jewish leaders themselves. (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **this man** - refers to Jesus
