===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they called the man** - Here, "they" refers to the Jews. ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:09:16|9:18]])
  * **this man** - This refers to Jesus.
  * **that man** - This refers to the man who had been blind.
  * **Whether he is a sinner, I do not know.** - AT: "I do not know if he is a sinner or not."
