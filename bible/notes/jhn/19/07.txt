===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **The Jews answered him** - Here "Jews" is a synecdoche for the Jewish leaders who opposed Jesus. AT: "The Jewish leaders answered Pilate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **he has to die because he claimed to be the Son of God** - Jesus was condemned to death by crucifixion because he claimed he was "the Son of God."
  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
