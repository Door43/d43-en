===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **However**  - "Just to prove that Jesus was truly dead,"
  * **pierce**  - To thrust a spear, javelin, sword, or any such weapon through a person's body.
  * **out came blood and water**  - this was proof that Jesus' lungs and heart had stopped, and proved Jesus' death.
  * **The one who saw this** -verses 34 and 35 are separate from the story, the writer (John the Apostle) is telling us that he was there and we can trust what he wrote.
  * **has borne witness**  - "has carried the message"
