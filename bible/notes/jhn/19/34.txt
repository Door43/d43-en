===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who saw this** - This sentence gives background information to the story. John is telling readers that he was there and that we can trust what he has written. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **has borne witness, and his witness is true** - To "bear witness" means to tell about something that one has seen. AT: "has told the truth about what he has seen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **so that you also may believe** - Here "believe" means to put one's trust in Jesus. AT: "so that you also may put your trust in Jesus." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
