===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jesus explains why the voice spoke from heaven.


  * **Now is the judgment of this world** - Here "this world" is a metonym that refers to all the people in the world. AT: "Now is the time for God to judge all of the people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Now will the ruler of this world be thrown out** - Here "ruler" refers to Satan. You can translate this in an active form. AT: "Now is the time when I will destroy the power of Satan who rules this world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
