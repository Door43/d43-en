===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. Jesus enters Jerusalem and the people honor him as a king.

  * **a great crowd** - "a great crowd of people"
  * **Hosanna** - This means "May God save us now!"
  * **Blessed** - This expresses a desire for God to cause good things to happen to a person.
  * **comes in the name of the Lord** - Here the word "name" is a metonym, which means authority and power. AT: "comes as the representative of the Lord" or "comes in the power of the Lord" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
