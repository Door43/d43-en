===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hosanna**  - means  "May God save."
  * **comes in the name of the Lord**  - Coming in the name of someone means to be sent with their authority and power, or be their representative and messenger. AT: "comes as the representative of the Lord" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
