===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verse 33 John tells us background information about what Jesus said about being "lifted up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **when I am lifted up from the earth** - Here Jesus refers to his crucifixion. You can translate this in an active form. AT: "when people raise me high on a cross" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will draw everyone to myself** - Through his crucifixion, Jesus will provide a way for everyone to trust in him. 
  * **He said this to indicate what kind of death he would die** - John interprets Jesus' words to mean that people will crucify him. AT: "He said this to let the people know how he would die" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
