===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer makes a note for background information.

  * **when Jesus was glorified**  - AT: "when God glorified Jesus."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **they remembered that these things had been written about him**  - John, the writer, interrupts here to give the reader some background information about what the disciples later understood. (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background|Writing Styles - Background Information]])
