===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus is continuing to speak to his disciples about going to Bethany.

  * **for your sakes** - "for your benefit"
  * **who was called Didymus** - Didymus is a male name that means "twin."  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
