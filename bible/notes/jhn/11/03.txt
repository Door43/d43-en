===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sent word to Jesus** - "sent a message to Jesus"
  * **love** - Here "love" refers to brotherly love or love for a friend or family member. This is natural human love between friends or relatives.
  * **death** - This refers to physical death.
  * **for the glory of God so that the Son of God may be glorified by it."** - Jesus, as "the Son of God," receives the same glory as "God." God the Son is equal to God the Father and God the Spirit.
