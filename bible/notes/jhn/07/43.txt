===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So there arose a division in the crowds because of him** - The crowds could not agree about who or what Jesus was. 
  * **but no one laid hands on him** - To lay hands on someone is an idiom which means to grab him or to hold onto him. AT: "but no one grabbed him to arrest him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
