===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. Jesus is in Galilee speaking to his brothers. These verses tell about when this event occurred. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **After these things** - "After he finished speaking with the disciples" (See: [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:06:66|6:66-71]]) or "Some time later"
  * **traveled** - "walked"
  * **the Jews were seeking to kill him** - Here "the Jews" is a synecdoche for "the Jewish leaders." AT: "the Jewish leaders were making plans to kill him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Now the Jewish Festival of Shelters was near** - "Now the time for the festival of the Jews was near" or "Now it was almost time for the Jewish festival of Shelters"
