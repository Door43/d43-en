===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jesus is now teaching the Jews in the temple.

  * **How does this man know so much?** - The remark appears in the form of a question to emphasize the Jewish leaders' surprise that Jesus has so much knowledge. AT: "He cannot possibly know so much about the scriptures!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **but is of him who sent me** - "but comes from God, the one who sent me"
