  * **This is the one who is coming after me.** - "He will preach to you after I am gone."
  * **I am not worthy to untie the strap of his sandal **- John is saying that he does not deserve to do the most unpleasant work of a servant. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
