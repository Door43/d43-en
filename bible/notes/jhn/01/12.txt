===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **believed on** - This means to ?believe in Jesus?. It means to trust him as Savior and live in a way that honors him.
  * **his name** - The phrase "his name" refers to Jesus' identity and everything about him. AT: "Jesus" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he gave the right** - "he gave them the authority" or "he made it possible for them"
