===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Which one of you convicts me of sin?** - "None of you can convict me of sin." Jesus asks this question to emphasize that he is sinless. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **why do you not believe me?**  - "You have no reason for not believing in me."  Jesus uses this question to rebuke the Jewish leaders for their unbelief. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
