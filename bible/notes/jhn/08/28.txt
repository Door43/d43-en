===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **When you have lifted up** - "When you lift up." This had not happened yet.
    * **As the Father taught me**  - "In the same way that the Father taught me"
    * **He who sent me** - The word "he" refers to the Father.
    * **As Jesus was saying** - "While Jesus was saying"
