===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **die in your sin** - "die while you are still sinful" or "you will die while you are sinning"
  * **you cannot come** - "you are not able to come"
  * **Will he kill himself, he who said**  - This can be translated as two separate questions. "Will he kill himself? Is that why he said"
