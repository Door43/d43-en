===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues speaking to all those listening to him ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:06:32|6:32]]).

    * **true food ... true drink** - Receiving Jesus in faith provides everlasting life the same way food and drink nourish the physical body. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
