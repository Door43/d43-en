===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Truly, truly** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].
  * **it is my Father who is giving you the true bread from heaven** - The "true bread" is a metaphor for Jesus. AT: "The Father gives to you the Son as the true bread from heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **my Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **gives life to the world** - "gives spiritual life to the world"
  * **the world** - Here the "world" is a metonym for all of the people in the world who trust in Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
