===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus now continues speaking to the crowd and the Jewish leaders.

  * **Truly, truly** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].
  * **Not that anyone has seen the Father, except he who is from God** - Although no human who is alive on earth has seen God the Father, Jesus, the Son of God, has seen the Father.
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **he who believes has eternal life** - God gives "eternal life" to those who trust in Jesus, the Son of God.
