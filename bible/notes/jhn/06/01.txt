===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. Jesus has traveled from Jerusalem to Galilee. A crowd has followed him up a mountainside. These verses tell the setting of this part of the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **After these things** - The phrase "these things" refers to the events in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:05:01|5:1-46]]. 
  * **Jesus went away** - It is implied in the text that Jesus traveled by boat and took his disciples with him. AT: "Jesus traveled by boat with his disciples" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **A great crowd** - "A large number of people"
  * **signs** - This refers to the miracles that are used as evidence that God is the all-powerful one who has complete authority over everything.
