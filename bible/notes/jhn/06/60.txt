===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues speaking to all those listening to him ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:06:32|6:32]]).

  * **who can accept it?** - "no one can accept it." or "it is unacceptable." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Does this offend you?** - AT: "I am surprised that this offends you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **offend you** - "cause you to give up your faith" or "disgusts you"
