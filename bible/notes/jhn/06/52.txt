===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues speaking to all those listening to him ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:06:32|6:32]]).

  * **Truly, truly** - Translate this as you did in [[en:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].
  * **eat the flesh of the Son of Man and drink his blood** - Receiving the Son of Man in faith is like receiving food and drink. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
