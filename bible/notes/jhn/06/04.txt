===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The action in the story begins in verse 5. 
 
  * **Now the Passover, the Jewish festival, was near** - John briefly stops telling about the events in the story in order to give background information about when the events happened. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **But Jesus said this to test Philip, for he himself knew what he was going to do** - John briefly stops telling about the events in the story in order to explain why Jesus asked Philip where to buy bread. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **for he himself knew** - The reflexive pronoun "himself" makes it clear that the word "he" refers to Jesus. Jesus knew what he would do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
