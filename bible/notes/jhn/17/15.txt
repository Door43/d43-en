===== translationNotes: =====

  * **world** - In this passage, the "world" is a metonym for the people who oppose God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **keep them from the evil one** - This refers to Satan. AT: "protect them from Satan, the evil one" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]]) 
  * **Dedicate them to yourself in the truth. Your word is truth** - Here "dedicate them" means to set them apart and prepare them for a special purpose. AT: "Make them your own people by teaching them your words, which are true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]])
