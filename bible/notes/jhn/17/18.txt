===== translationNotes: =====

  * **into the world** - Here into "the world" is a metonym that means to the people who live in the world. AT: "to every person and place throughout the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **they themselves will also be dedicated to you in truth** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "they may also dedicate themselves truly to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
