===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The part of the story from the previous chapter continues. Jesus had been speaking to his disciples, but now he begins to pray to God.

  * **he lifted up his eyes to the heavens** - This is an idiom that means to look upward. AT: "he looked up to the sky." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **heavens** - This refers to the sky. 
  * **Father ... glorify your Son so that the Son will glorify you** - Jesus asks God the Father to honor him so that he can give honor to God. 
  * **Father ... Son** - These are important titles that describe the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **the hour has come** - Here the word "hour" is a metonym that refers to the time for Jesus to suffer and die. AT: "it is time for me to suffer and die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **all flesh** - This refers to all people.
