===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]]) 
  * **where I am** - Here "where I am" refers to heaven. AT: "with me in heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **so that they will see my glory** - "so that they will see my greatness" 
  * **before the creation of the world** - Here Jesus refers to the time before creation. AT: "before we created the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
