===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Father, that which you have given to me—** - "Father, the ones that you have given to me—" 
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]]) 
  * **I desire that they also may be with me where I am** - Here "where I am" refers to heaven. AT: "I want them to be with me in heaven." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **so that they may see my glory** - "so that they may see my greatness" 
  * **before the foundation of the world** - Here Jesus refers to the time before creation. AT: "before we created the world." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
