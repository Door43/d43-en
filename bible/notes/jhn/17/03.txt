===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is eternal life ... know you, the only true God, and ... Jesus Christ** - Eternal life is to know the only true God, God the Father and God the Son.
  * **that they know you** - This is an idiom that means to experience God, rather than just knowing things about God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the work that you have given me to do** - Here "work" is a metonym that refers to Jesus' entire earthly ministry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Father, glorify me ... with the glory that I had with you before the world was made** - Jesus had glory with God the Father "before the world was made" because Jesus is God the Son. AT: "Father, give me honor by bringing me into your presence as we were before we made the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
