===== Translation Notes: =====

 
  * **how will you believe if I tell you** - in both places "you" is singular.
  * **how will you believe if I tell you?** - "You certainly will not believe if I tell you about heavenly things." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **heavenly things** - spiritual things.
  * **heaven** - This means the place where God lives.
