===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **member** - a part of a group
    * **Jewish Council** - The Jewish Council is called the "Sanhedrin." This was the most significant of all Jewish councils.
    * **we know** - Here "we" is exclusive, referring only to Nicodemus and the other members of the Jewish Council.
