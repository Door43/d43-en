===== translationNotes: =====

  * **How can these things be?** - This question adds emphasis to the statement. AT: "This cannot be!" or "This is not able to happen!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Are you a teacher of Israel, and yet you do not understand these things?** - This question adds emphasis to the statement. AT: "You are a teacher of Israel, so I am surprised you do not understand these things!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Truly, truly** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].
  * **we speak** - When Jesus said "we," he was not including Nicodemus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
