===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Sir** - The man said this to show respect to Jesus.
    * **when the water is stirred up** - "when an angel stirs up the water." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]). An angel would come and agitate the water and the first person who got down into the water after the water was disturbed would be healed of whatever disease that person had. 
    * **another steps down before me** - "someone else always enters the water before me." This pool had steps like a staircase above and into the water in the basin of the pool. 
    * **get up** - "stand up"
    * **take up** - "pick up"
