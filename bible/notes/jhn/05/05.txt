===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verse 5 introduces the man lying beside the pool to the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]])

  * **was there** - "was at the Bethesda pool" ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:05:01|5:1]])
  * **an invalid** - "paralyzed" 
  * **thirty-eight years** - "38 years." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **he realized** - "he understood" or "he found out"
  * **he said to him** - "Jesus said to the paralyzed man"
