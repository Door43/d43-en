===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For as the Father has life in himself, thus he has given to the Son also to have life in himself** - The word "for" marks a comparison. The Son of God has the power to give life, just as the Father does. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples|Translating Son and Father]])
  * **Father...Son of Man** - These are important titles that describe the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples|Translating Son and Father]])
  * **life** - This means spiritual life.
  * **the Father has given the Son authority to exercise judgment** - The Son of God has the authority of God the Father to carry out judgment.
