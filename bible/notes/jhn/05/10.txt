===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the Jews said to him** - The Jews (especially the leaders of the Jews) became angry when they saw the man carrying his mat on the Sabbath. 
  * **It is the Sabbath** - "It is God's Day of Rest"
  * **He who made me healthy** - "The man who made me well"
