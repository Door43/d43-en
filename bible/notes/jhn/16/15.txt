===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All things...the Father has are mine...the Spirit will take things of mine** - God the Father, Son, and Spirit all own the universe together. Together they are the one and only God. 
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **In a little while** - "Soon" or "Before long"
