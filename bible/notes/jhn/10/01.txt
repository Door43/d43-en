===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues to speak to the Pharisees. This is the same part of the story which began in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:09:35|9:35]]. 
  * **General Information:** - Jesus begins to speak in parables. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **Truly, truly** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].
  * **sheep pen** - This is a fenced area where a shepherd keeps his sheep.
  * **a thief and a robber** - This is the use of two words with similar meanings to add emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
