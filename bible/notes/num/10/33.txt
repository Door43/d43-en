===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They journeyed** - "They" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **the mountain of Yahweh** - This refers to Mount Sinai. AT: "Mount Sinai, the mountain of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The ark of the covenant of Yahweh went before them** - The group of Levites carrying the ark of the covenant went before the people of Israel as the traveled. AT: "Men carried the ark of the covenant of Yahweh before them as they traveled" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **by daylight** - "every day" or "during the day"
