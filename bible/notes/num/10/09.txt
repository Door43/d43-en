===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When you go to war ... oppresses you** - Yahweh is speaking to Moses and uses the word "you," but he is actually referring the people of Israel going to war. AT: "When the people of Israel to war ... oppresses Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **then you must sound an alarm with the trumpets** - Here Yahweh again speaks to Moses using the word "you" but actually wants Moses to have the priests blow the trumpets. AT: "then you must command the priests to sound an alarm with the trumpets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **call you to mind** - This phrase "call to mind" means to remember. AT: "remember you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
