===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When you blow a loud signal** - Here the word “you” is plural and refers to the priests. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **They must blow a loud signal for their journeys** - Here the word "They" refers to the priests and the word "their" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **be a regulation for you** - "be a rule for you." Here the word "you" is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
