===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in front of you** - "while you are present." This means that Moses was to be there with the priest when he blew the trumpets. 
  * **the leaders, the heads of the clans of Israel** These two phrases refer to the same group of people, the second phrase is used to describe the first phrase. AT: "the leaders, who are the heads of the clans of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **When you blow a loud signal** - Here "you" is plural and refers to the priests. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
