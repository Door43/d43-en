===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the second year** - This is the second year after Yahweh brought the Israelites out of Egypt. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the second month, on the twentieth day of the month** - This is the second month of the Hebrew calendar. The twentieth day is in the first part of May on western calendars. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the cloud was lifted** - AT: "the cloud rose up" (UDB) or "Yahweh lifted the cloud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh's command given through Moses** - AT: "the command that Yahweh had given through Moses" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
