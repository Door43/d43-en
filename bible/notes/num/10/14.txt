===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The camp under the banner of Judah's descendants** - This camp includes the three tribes under the division of Judah: Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun. 
  * **went out first** - "went out 1st." The phrase "went out" means that they packed up their camp and began moving. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Nahshon son of Amminadab** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:7]].
  * **Nethanel son of Zuar** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:8]].
  * **Eliab son of Helon** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:9]].
