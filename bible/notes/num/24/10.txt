===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Balak's anger was kindled** - The increase in Balak's anger is spoken of as if it was a fire starting to burn. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Balak became very angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **struck his hands together** - This was a sign of great frustration and anger.
