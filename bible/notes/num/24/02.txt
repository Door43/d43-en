===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He raised his eyes** - AT: "He looked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Spirit of God came on him** - This mean's God's Spirit took control of him to prophesy.
  * **son of Beor** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:num:22:05|22:5]].
  * **whose eyes are wide open** - This means he has received the spiritual power to know and understand God's will. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
