===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He raised his eyes** - Here "raised his eyes" is an idiom that means to look up. AT: "He looked up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Spirit of God came on him** - This mean's God's Spirit took control of him to prophesy.
  * **He received this prophecy** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God gave him this prophecy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Balaam son of Beor** - This is the king of Moab. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:22:05|22:5]].
  * **whose eyes are wide open** - This idiom means he sees and understands clearly. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
