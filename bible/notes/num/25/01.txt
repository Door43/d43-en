===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Israel** - 
  * **Shittim** - This is the name of a place in Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **bowed down** - 
  * **Peor** - Peor was the name of a mountain. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:23:28|23:28]].
  * **Yahweh's anger was kindled** - The increase in Yahweh's anger is spoken of as if it was a fire starting to burn. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh became very angry." See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:num:21:19|21:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
