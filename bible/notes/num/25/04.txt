===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all the leaders of the people** - AT: "all the leaders of the people who are guilty of idolatry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to expose them in the daylight** - This means that the leaders of Israel will kill these people during the daytime and they will leave their dead bodies out where all the people can see them.
  * **to Israel's leaders** - AT: "to Israel's leaders who were not guilty of idolatry"
