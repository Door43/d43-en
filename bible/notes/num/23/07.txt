===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Arak**  - The UDB uses the word Aram.
  * **How can I curse those whom God has not cursed? - ** Balaam used this question to express amazement that someone would want him to go against the will of God.  AT:  It would not be right to curse someone who was acceptable to God.  (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **How can I defy those whom Yahweh has not defied? - ** Balaam used this question to express amazement that someone would want him to go against the will of God. AT:  It would not be right to defy someone who was acceptable to God.  (See Rhetorical Question)
