===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rose up against Moses **- This means they conspired against Moses or rebelled against Moses.
  * **two hundred and fifty**  - "250" (See: Translate numbers)
  * **assembled​ themselves together**  - AT: "came together".
  * **confront​ **- This means to challenge or question the authority of.
  * **reserved for Yahweh**  - AT: "set apart for Yahweh".
  * **lift up yourselves ****above the rest**  - AT: "act as if you are more important than the rest"
