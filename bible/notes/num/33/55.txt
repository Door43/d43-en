===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **like objects in your eyes and thorns in your sides**  - This means that the people allowed to stay on their land will cause trouble for the Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
