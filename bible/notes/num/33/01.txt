===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **by their armed groups** - This means that each tribe had their own men, who were armed, to protect them. AT: "by their military group" or "by their armies"  
  * **as commanded by Yahweh** - AT: "as Yahweh commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **departure after departure** - AT: "from one place to another place"
