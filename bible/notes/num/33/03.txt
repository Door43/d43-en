===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses lists the places the Israelites went after they left Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **during the first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on western calendars. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **leaving on the fifteenth day of the first month** - This is near the beginning of April on western calendars. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the people of Israel left openly, in the sight of all the Egyptians** - "the Israelites left in plain view of the Egyptians"
  * **their firstborn** - "their firstborn son" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **for he also inflicted punishment on their gods** - This means that when Yahweh caused the plagues in Egypt, he proved that he was more powerful than all the false gods that the Egyptians worshiped.
