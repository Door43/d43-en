===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the fortieth year after** - Here "fortieth" is the ordinal number for forty. AT: "40 years after" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the fifth month, on the first day of the month** - This "fifth" is the ordinal number for five. This "first" is the ordinal number for one. This is the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar. The first day is near the middle of July on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
