===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **you must offer a sacrifice made by fire, a burnt offering** - AT: “you must burn an offering on the altar” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **mixed with oil** - AT: "which you have mixed with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **ephah** - An ephah is 22 liters. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
