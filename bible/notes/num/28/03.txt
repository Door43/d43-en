===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **the offering made by fire** - This can be stated in active form. "the burnt offering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **mixed with one-fourth of a hin of beaten oil** - This can be stated in active form. "which you have mixed with one-fourth of a hin of pure olive oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a tenth of an ephah** - This can be written in modern measurements. AT:  "2 liters" or "a tenth of an ephah (which is about 2 liters)" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **one-fourth of a hin** - This can be written in modern measurements. AT: "a liter" or "one-fourth of a hin (which is almost 1 liter)" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **beaten oil** - "pressed oil." This refers to oil that has been pressed out of olives.
