===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **the offering made by fire**  - AT: “the burnt offering"
  * **year...day** - (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]])
  * **mixed with a fourth of a hin of beaten oil** - AT: "which you have mixed with a fourth of a hin of pure olive oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **ephah** - An ephah is 22 liters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
  * **hin** - A hin is 3.7 liters.
