===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **the first month, on the fourteenth day of the month ... the fifteenth day of this month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. The fourteenth day and fifteenth day are near the end of March and the beginning of April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **comes Yahweh's Passover** - "you must celebrate Yahweh's Passover"
  * **a feast is to be held** - "you must have a feast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **bread without yeast must be eaten** - "you must eat bread without yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
