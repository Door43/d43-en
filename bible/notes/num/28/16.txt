===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **the first month, on the fourteenth day of the month ... the fifteenth day of this month** - "the 1st month, on the 14th day of the month ... the 15th day of this month." These months refers to the Hebrew calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **comes Yahweh's Passover** - "you must celebrate Yahweh's Passover"
  * **a feast is to be held** - This can be stated in active form. "you must have a feast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **bread without yeast must be eaten** - This can be stated in active form. "you must eat bread without yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the first day** - "the 1st day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **there must be a holy assembly to honor Yahweh** - The phrase "a holy assembly" means the people gather together to worship Yahweh. Worshiping Yahweh is a holy event. AT: "you must gather together to worship and honor Yahweh"
