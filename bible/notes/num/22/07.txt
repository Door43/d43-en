===== translationNotes: =====

  * **payment for divination** - The abstract noun "divination" can be stated as an action. AT: "money to pay Balaam to curse Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **They came to Balaam** - You may prefer to say "They went to Balaam" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **spoke to him Balak's words** - "told him the message from Balak"
  * **Balak** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:22:02|22:2]].
  * **I will bring you** - Balaam's report is spoken of as if it were something that he would carry to the messengers. AT: "I will tell you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
