===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Am I not able to honor you**  - AT: "I am able to pay you money for coming to me."
  * **Did I not send men to you to summon you**  - Balak is expressing amazement that Balaam took so long to come, since he sent men to bring Balaam.  AT: "I sent men to summon you." (See: Rhetorical Question).
  *  **Am I not able to honor you**
 - Balak is expressing amazement that Balaam took so long to come, since Balak is able to pay Balaam for coming. AT: "I am able to pay you money for coming to me." (See: Rhetorical Question).
