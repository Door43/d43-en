===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the first month** - "in the 1st month." This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It marks when God brought the people of Israel out of Egypt. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the second year** - "the 2nd year" or "year 2" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **after they came out from the land of Egypt** - Here "they" refers to the people of Israel. Also, the phrase "came out" means to leave. AT: "after they left the land of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Let the people ... at its fixed time of year** - The word "fixed" means "previously set." This means that this is when the observe it every year. AT: "Let the people ... at the time of year they currently observe it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **On the fourteenth day** - "the 14th day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **On the fourteenth day ... at its fixed time of year** - "On the fourteenth day ... and observe it, for this is the time you do celebrate it every year" 
  * **follow all the regulations, and obey all the decrees** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined to emphasize that they needed to obey the commands. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
