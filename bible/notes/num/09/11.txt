===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the second month on the fourteenth day** - This is the second month of the Hebrew calendar. The fourteenth day is near the end of April on Western calendars. (See: en:bible:notes:luk:12:27 and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **at evening** - "at sunset"
  * **with bread without yeast** - "with bread made without yeast"
  * **bitter herbs** - These are small plants that have a strong and usually bad taste.
  * **break a bone of animals** - This refers to the lamb that they are eating.
