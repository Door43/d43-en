===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All the gold of the offering that they gave to Yahweh—the offerings from the commanders of thousands and from the captains of hundreds—weighed** - AT: "All the gold which the commanders of the army gave to Yahweh weighed"
  * **16,750 ** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **shekel** - A shekel is 11 grams. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalmoney|biblical money]])
  * **as a reminder of the people of Israel for Yahweh** - The gold will remind the people that Yahweh gave them victory. It will also remind Yahweh that the people fulfilled his revenge on the Midianites.
