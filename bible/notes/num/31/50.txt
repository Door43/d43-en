===== Translation Notes: =====
The officers of the army continue speaking to Moses.
  * **armlets and bracelets, signet rings, earrings, and necklaces** - These are all types of jewelry that people wore.
  * **the gold and all the articles of craftmanship** - AT: "all the articles of gold" or "all the golden jewelry"
  * **to make atonement for ourselves before Yahweh** - AT: "to thank God for saving our lives"
