===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Avenge the people of Israel against the Midianites** - Yahweh held the Midianites responsible for convincing the Isrealites to worship idols.
  * **be gathered to your people** - This means Moses' spirit will go to same place as his relatives who died before him. AT: "join your relatives who have also died"
