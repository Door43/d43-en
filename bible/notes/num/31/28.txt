===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.
  * **Then levy a tax to be given to me from the soldiers who went out to battle** - AT: "Collect a tax from the soldiers' plunder and give it to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **every five hundred** - "every 500" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **from their half** - "from the soldiers' half"
  * **to be presented to me** - AT: "which he will present to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
