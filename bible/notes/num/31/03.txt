===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Arm some of your men for war** - "Give weapons to some of your men"
  * **go against Midian and carry out Yahweh's vengeance on it** - "go fight a war against the Midianites and punish them for what they did to us"
  * **a thousand ... twelve thousand** - "1,000 ... 12,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Israel's thousands and thousands of men** - "Israel's many men"
  * **one thousand were provided from every tribe for war** - This can be stated in active form. "every tribe sent 1,000 men to war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **twelve thousand men in all** - All 12 tribes sent men, including the tribe of Levi. They also sent 1000 men into battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
