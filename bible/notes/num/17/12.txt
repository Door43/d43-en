===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We will die here. We will all perish!** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Must we all perish?** - The people ask this question out of fear. AT: "We are afraid that we will all perish." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
