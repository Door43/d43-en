===== translationNotes: =====

  * **selected from each of the ancestral tribes** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom Moses selected from each of the ancestor's tribes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the tent of the covenant decrees** - The phrase "the covenant decrees" refers to the tablets that the decrees were written on. AT: "the tent where the tablets with the covenant decrees written on them are stored" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the tent of the covenant decrees** - The phrase "the covenant decrees" refers to the box that held the tablets that the covenant decrees were written on. AT: "the tent of the covenant box" or "the tent that the covenant decrees are in" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
