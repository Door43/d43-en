===== translationNotes: =====

  * **then whatever came out of her lips** - Here what the woman said is referred to as something that came out of her lips. AT: "then whatever she said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will not remain in force** - This phrase means that her vows will not remain in effect, that is, she will be not required to fulfill them. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:num:30:06|30:6]]. AT: "she will not be obligated to fulfill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
