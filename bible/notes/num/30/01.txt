===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When anyone makes a vow to Yahweh, or swears an oath to bind himself with a promise** - These two sentences have similar meanings. They are combined for emphasis. AT: "When anyone promises Yahweh he will do something." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **swears an oath to bind himself with a promise**  - "promises to do something"
  * **he must not break his word. He must keep his promise to do everything that comes out of his mouth** - These two sentences have similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. AT: "he must do everything he said he would do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
