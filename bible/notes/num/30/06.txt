===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If she is married when she makes her vows** - "If she gets married after she makes her vows"
  * **speaks rashly with her lips and binds herself** - AT: "promises something without thinking carefully about it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **then her vows will remain in force. The promise by which she has bound herself remains in force** - AT: "Then she must keep every vow and promise she has made" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
