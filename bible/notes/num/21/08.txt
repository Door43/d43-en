===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **everyone who is bitten** - AT: "everyone whom a snake bites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **if he looked at the bronze snake, he survived** - Here the word "he" refers to "any person" whom a snake bites.
