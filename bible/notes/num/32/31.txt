===== translationNotes: =====

  * **We will cross over armed** - You can make it clear that they will cross the Jordan. AT: "We will cross over the Jordan ready to fight" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **our possessed inheritance** - The land that the people were to receive as a permanent possession is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they were to obtain. AT: "the portion of land that we will possess" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **will remain with us** - This idiom means refers to ownership. AT: "will be ours" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
