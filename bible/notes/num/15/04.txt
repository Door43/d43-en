===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a tenth of an ephah** - An ephah is a unit of volume equal to about 22 liters. AT: "about 2 liters" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
  * **a fourth of a hin** - A hin is a unit of volume equal to about 3.7 liters. AT: "about 1 liter" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
