===== translationNotes: =====

  * **That person must be cut off** - See how you translated this idea in [[:en:bible:notes:num:09:13|9:13]].
  * **His sin will be on him** - This is an idiom. Here "sin" refers to punishing him because of his sin. AT: "I will consider him responsible for his sin" or "I will punish him because of his sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
