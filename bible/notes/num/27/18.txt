===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Take Joshua son of Nun, a man in whom my Spirit lives** - Yahweh's Spirit is in Joshua, meaning that Joshua obeys Yahweh and follows his commands.
  * **lay your hand on him** - This is a sign of dedicating someone to God's service.
  * **and command him before their eyes to lead them**  - AT: "and in front of all of them command Joshua to lead the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
