===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must put some of your authority on him**  - "You" refers to Moses.
  * **He will go before Eleazar the priest to seek my will for him**  - "He" refers to Joseph, and "him" refers to Joseph.
  * **Urim - **  This word is used as Urim and Thummim meaning to cast lots.
  * **both he and all the people of Israel with him, the whole community **- "All the people of Israel" and "the whole community" mean the same group, and they are used together for emphasis.  AT:  "both he and the entire nation of Israel." (See: Doublet)
