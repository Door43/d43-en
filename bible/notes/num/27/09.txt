===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues God's law about who will receive land if a man does not have any sons.

  * **be a law established by decree for the people of Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "be a law that all the people of Israel must obey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **has commanded me** - Here "me" refers to Moses.
