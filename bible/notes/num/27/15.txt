===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the God of the spirits of all humanity** - Possible meaning are 1) Here "spirits" refers to all people" or 2) the reference to "spirits" refers to God having given life and breath to all people. AT: "the God over all of humanity" or "the God who gives breath to all people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a man over the community** - To be "over" a group of people means to have authority to lead them. AT: "a man to lead the community" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a man who may go out and come in before them and lead them out and bring them in** - This is an idiom that means a person who will lead all the people and also lead the army into battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **so that your community is not like sheep that have no shepherd** - This is a simile that means without a leader the people will wander and be helpless. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
