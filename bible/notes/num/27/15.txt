===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the God of the spirits of all humanity** - AT: "the God over all of humanity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a man who may go out and come in before them and lead them out and bring them in** - This means a person who will lead all the people and also lead the army into battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **so that your community is not like sheep that have no shepherd**  - This means without a leader the people will wander and be helpless. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
