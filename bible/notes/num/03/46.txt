===== translationNotes: =====

  * **for the redemption of** - The noun "redemption" can be translated with the verb "redeem." AT: "to redeem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]) 
  * **first-born people of Israel** - "first-born sons of Israel"
  * **five shekels** - A shekel is a unit of weight equal to about 11 grams. AT: "about 55 grams of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]]) 
  * **who exceed the number of the Levites** - This means that there are 273 more firstborn males among the other tribes of the Israelites than there are total number of Levite males. 
  * **You must use the shekel of the sanctuary as your standard weight** - This means that the shekel must weight the same as those in the sanctuary. AT: "You must use the weight of the shekels in the sanctuary as your stand weight" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **twenty gerahs** - "20 gerahs." A gerah is a unit of weight equal to about .57 kilograms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **the price of redemption that you paid** - Here the word "price" refers to the shekels that Moses collected. AT: "the money that you collected for their redemption" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
