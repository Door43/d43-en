===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must give** - "You" refers to Moses.
  * **They are wholly given** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I have given them entirely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but any foreigner who comes near** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit.  AT: "but any foreigner who comes near the tabernacle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **must be put to death** -This can be stated in active form.  AT: "you must kill" or "must die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
