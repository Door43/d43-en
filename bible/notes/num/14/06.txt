===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Nun...Jephunneh** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who were some of those sent** - AT: "who were some of those whom Moses sent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **tore their clothes** - Tearing one's clothes was a gesture that indicates that the person is very troubled and is mourning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **flows with milk and honey** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:num:13:27|13:27]].
