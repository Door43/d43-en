===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Joshua and Caleb continue speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **We will consume them as easily as food** - Joshua and Caleb speak of destroying their enemies as if they were to eat them. AT: "We will destroy them as easily as we can eat food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Their protection will be removed from them** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will remove their protection from them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Their protection** - The abstract noun "protection" can be stated as "protect." AT: "Anyone that might protect them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
