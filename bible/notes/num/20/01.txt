===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It marks when God rescued the Israelites from the Egyptians. The first month is during last part of March and the first part of April on the Western calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
