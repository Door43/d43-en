===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the wilderness of Sin** - The word "Sin" here is the Hebrew name of the wilderness. It is not the English word "sin." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_transliterate]])
  * **the first month** -  This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It marks when God rescued the Israelites from the Egyptians. The first month is during the last part of March and the first part of April on the Western calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]])
  * **and was buried** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and they buried her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
