===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why have you brought Yahweh’s community into this wilderness to die here, we and our animals?**  - The people ask this question in order to rebuke Moses. AT: "You should not have brought Yahweh's community into this wilderness to die here, we and our animals." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **And why did you make us come up out of Egypt to bring us to this horrible place?**  - AT: "You should not have made us come up out of Egypt to bring us to this horrible place." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
