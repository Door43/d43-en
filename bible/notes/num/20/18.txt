===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You may not pass...to attack you** - Here the word "you" is singular and refers to Moses, who represents the people of Israel. AT: "Your people may not pass...to attack them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will come with the sword** - AT: "I will send my army" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the people of Israel** - This phrase refers to the Israelite messengers.
