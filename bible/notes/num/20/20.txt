===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the king of Edom came against Israel with a strong hand with many soldiers** - Here the hand represents the king's powerful army. AT: "the king of Edom sent a strong army of many soldiers to attack Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **cross over their border** - Here "their" refers to the Edomites.
