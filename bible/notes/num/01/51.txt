===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When the tabernacle is to be set up** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "When it is time to set up the tabernacle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **must be killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "must die" or "you must kill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **near the banner** - A banner is a large flag.
  * **his armed group** - "his military division"
