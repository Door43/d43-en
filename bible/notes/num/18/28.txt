===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **must make a presented offering** - AT: "must present an offering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **give his presented offering to Aaron** - Here the word "his" refers to Yahweh.
  * **Out of all the gifts you receive, you must make** - AT: "From every offering that you receive from the people, you must take 10 percent to offer to Yahweh as your own offering to Yahweh"
  * **that have been given to you** - AT: "that the people of Israel have given to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
