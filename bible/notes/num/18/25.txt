===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Your presented offering must be considered by you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must consider the offering that you present" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **as your inheritance** - God speaks of what Aaron and his descendants will receive as if they will inherit it. AT: "as your portion of what I give to all Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
