===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **assemble the whole community **- "gather the whole community"
  * **offer the Levites**  - AT: "present the Levites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **as if they were raised high before me**  - He compared the Levities to the offerings to show that they were dedicated to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **on the behalf of the people of Israel**  - AT: "as a gift from the people of Israel"
