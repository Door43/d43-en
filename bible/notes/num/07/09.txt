===== translationNotes: =====

  * **none of those things** - This refers to the carts and oxen.
  * **Kohath** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:17|3:17]].
  * **theirs would be the work** - "their work would be"
  * **the things that belong to Yahweh** - You can make explicit the implicit meaning of what belongs to Yahweh. AT: "the things that Yahweh reserved for the tabernacle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
