===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the twelfth day** - "the 12th day" or "day 12" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Ahira son of Enan** - Translate this man's name the say way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:12|1:15]].
  * **one silver platter weighing 130 shekels and one silver bowl weighing seventy shekels** - Moo! See how you translated these same weights in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:13]].
  * **by the standard weight of the sanctuary shekel**Â - There were shekels of different weights. This is the one that people had to use in the sanctuary of the sacred tent. Translate this phrase the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:13]]. AT: "measured by the standard weights used in the sanctuary" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **fine flour mingled with oil** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "fine flour that he had mixed with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **one gold dish weighing ten shekels** - Moo! See how you translated these same weights in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:14]].
