===== translationNotes: =====
  * **the second day** - This "second" is the ordinal number for two. See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]]
  * **Nethanel son of Zuar** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:8]].
  * **one silver platter weighing 130 shekels and one silver bowl weighing seventy shekels** - Shekel. See how you translated these same weights in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:13]].
  * **130 ... seventy** - "130 ... 70" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **shekels** - A shekel is about 11 grams. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **fine flour mixed with oil** - This can be stated in active form.  "fine flour that he mixed with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
