===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the second day** - "the 2nd day" or "day 2" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Nethanel son of Zuar** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:8]].
  * **one silver platter weighing 130 shekels** - If necessary, these weights can be written in modern measurements. See how you translated these same weights in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:13]]. AT: "one silver platter weighing nearly one and a half kilograms" or "one silver platter weighing one kilogram and 430 grams" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]]) 
  * **one silver bowl weighing seventy shekels** - "one silver bowl weighing 70 shekels." If necessary, these weights can be written in modern measurements. See how you translated these same weights in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:13]]. AT: "one silver bowl weighing nearly eight tenths of a kilogram" or "one silver bowl weighting 770 grams" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **by the standard weight of the sanctuary shekel**Â - There were shekels of different weights. This is the one that people had to use in the sanctuary of the sacred tent. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:13]]. AT: "measured by the standard weights used in the sanctuary" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **fine flour mixed with oil** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "fine flour that he had mixed with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
