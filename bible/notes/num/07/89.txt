  * **he heard his voice speaking to him** - Here "his voice" refers to Yahweh. AT: "he heard Yahweh speaking to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **from above the atonement lid ... from between the two cherubim** - These two phrases describe the same location. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **ark of the covenant decrees** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:num:04:05|4:5]].
  * **He spoke to him** - "Yahweh spoke to Moses"
