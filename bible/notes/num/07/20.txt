===== translationNotes: =====

  * **one gold dish weighing ten shekels** - If necessary, this can be written in modern measurements. See how you translated these same weights in [[:en:bible:notes:num:07:12|7:14]]. AT: "one gold dish weighing one tenth of a kilogram" or "one gold dish weighting 110 grams" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]]) 
  * **that were a year old** - "that were each one year old"
  * **This was the sacrifice of Nethanel son of Zuar** - "This was what Nethanel son of Zuar presented"
  * **Nethanel son of Zuar** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:8]].
