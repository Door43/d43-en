===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a statute, a law** - These two words share similar meanings. AT: "a statute of the law" or "a legal statute" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **bring to you** - Here "you" is singular and refers to Moses.
  * **flaw or blemish** - These two words mean basically the same thing and emphasize that this animal is to have no imperfections. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
