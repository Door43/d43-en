===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then he must wash his clothes** - Here "he" refers to Eleazar the priest. 
  * **he will remain unclean** - Being unacceptable to God or unfit to do any sacred work is spoken of as not being clean. "(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
