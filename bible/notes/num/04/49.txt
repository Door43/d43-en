===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At Yahweh’s command** - "As Yahweh commanded"
  * **he was assigned** - Here the word "he" refers to each man whom Moses counted.
  * **they obeyed what Yahweh had commanded them** - Here the words "they" and "them" refer to Moses and Aaron.
