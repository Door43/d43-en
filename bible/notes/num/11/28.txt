===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stop them **- AT: "tell them to stop prophesying"
  * **Are you jealous for my sake?**  - AT: "Do you wish you had the same ability as I have?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **that he would put his spirit on them all **- In this sentence, "he" and "his" refers to God's spirit.
