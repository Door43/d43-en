===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stop them** - AT: "tell them to stop prophesying"
  * **Are you jealous for my sake?** - Moses uses this question to rebuke Joshua. AT: "Do not be jealous for my sake." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **jealous for my sake** - This means that Joshua was worried that Eldad and Medad posed a threat to Moses' authority.
