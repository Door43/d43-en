===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Where can I find meat to give to all this people?** - This is a rhetorical question and can be expressed in a statement. AT: "I cannot possibly find enough meat to give to all these people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **They are too much for me** - This is an idiom. AT: "This responsibility is too difficult for me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
