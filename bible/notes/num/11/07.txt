===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **coriander seed **- Means a plant used for flavoring.
  * **resin **- Means a yellowish-sticky substance.
  * **ground it in mills **- Means they made it into a floury substance.
  * **beat it in mortars **- Means they crushed it in a large bowl.
