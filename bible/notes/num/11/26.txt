===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Spirit also rested on them **- AT: “The Spirit of God took control of them”
  * **Their names were written on the list. **- AT: "The list contained their names." (See: passive)
  * **list **- A record Moses kept of those chosen by God, such as Eldad and Medad.
