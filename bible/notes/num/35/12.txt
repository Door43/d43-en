===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the avenger** - This refers to a close relative that seeks vengeance by killing the accused man.
  * **so that the accused man will not be killed without first standing trial before the community** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "so that no one will kill the accused man before the community is able to judge him in court" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
