===== translationNotes: =====

  * **plains** - a large area of flat land
  * **give some of their own shares of land to the Levites** - The Levites did not receive land by lot like the other tribes. Each tribe gave some of their land to the Levites.
  * **pastureland** - an area of land where animals feed on grass
