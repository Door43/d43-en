===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The leaders of Israel are counting the men, 20 years old and older, according to their tribes and families. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **was the firstborn of Israel** - Here "Israel" refers to the man also known as Jacob.
  * **From his son** - The word "his" refers to Rueben. 
  * **43,730 men** - "forty-three thousand seven hundred and thirty men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
