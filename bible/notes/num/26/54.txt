===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.
  * **give more inheritance** - AT: "give more land as an inheritance"
  * **who were counted** - AT: "whom the leaders of Israel counted" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the land must be divided** - AT "you must divide the land"
  * **by random lots** - God would show his will by how sticks fell on the ground. AT: "as I show  you how"
  * **it will be divided** - AT "you will divide it"
  * **distributed to them** - AT: "and you must distribute the land to them"
