===== Translation Notes: =====
This is a list of the Levite clans. Moses counts the Levites separately from the other tribes because they did not receive any land. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **Gershon...Kohath...Merari...Amram** - See how you translated these names in [[en:bible:notes:num:03:17|3:17-19]].
