===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a list of the Levite clans. Moses counts the Levites separately from the other tribes because they did not receive any land. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **counted clan by clan** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that the leaders also counted clan by clan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Gershon ... Kohath ... Merari ... Amram** - See how you translated these men's names you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:17|3:17-19]].
  * **She bore to Amram their children** - "She and Amram had children"
