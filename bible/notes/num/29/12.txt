===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **the fifteenth day of the seventh month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The fifteenth day is near the beginning of October on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]])
  * **a sacrifice made by fire** - "you must burn it on the altar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **thirteen ... fourteen** - "13 ... 14" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **a year old** - "each a year old"
