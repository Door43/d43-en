===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **the fifteenth day of the seventh month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The fifteenth day is near the beginning of October on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltime]])
  * **a sacrifice made by fire** - "you must burn it on the altar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **thirteen...fourteen** - "13...14" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **a year old** - "each a year old" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]])
