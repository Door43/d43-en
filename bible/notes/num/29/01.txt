===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **the seventh month, on the first day of the month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The first day is near the middle of September on western calendars. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **It will be a day when you blow trumpets** - The trumpets were rams' horns with a small hole near the tip of the horn. The priests blew the trumpet to begin a worship service. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
