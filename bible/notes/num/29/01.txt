===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **month...day** - (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]])
  * **It will be a day when you blow trumpets** - The trumpets were rams' horns with a small hole near the tip of the horn. The priests blew the trumpet to begin a worship service. (See: symbolic action)
