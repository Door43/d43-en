===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Heshbon ... Jazer** - These are names of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **twelve cities in all** - "12 cities in total" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the casting of lots** - This refers to a method of making decisions, often done with the belief that God will decide the outcome. AT: "choosing by chance"
