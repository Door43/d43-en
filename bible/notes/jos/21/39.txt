===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Heshbon and Jazer**  - names of cities (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * ** twelve cities in all**  - Refers to the entire list beginning in verse 34. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **casting of lots**  - AT "choosing by chance" or "randomly assigning." A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome.
