===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **by the hand of Moses**  - This is a figure of speech referring to Moses by only his hand. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **clans of the Kohathites**  - Kohath was a son of Levi. (See: [[en:obe:other:levite]])
