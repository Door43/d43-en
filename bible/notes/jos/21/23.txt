===== translationNotes: =====

  * **From the tribe of Dan, the clan of Kohath was given Eltekeh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The tribe of Dan  gave to the clan of Kohath Eltekeh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the clan of Kohath** - The priests in this group were descendants of Levi's son Kohath. A portion of them were also descendants of Aaron, Kohath's grandson. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:21:20|21:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Eltekeh ... Gibbethon ... Aijalon ... Gath Rimmon** - These are names of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **four cities** - This refers to the number of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
