===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Eleazar ... Nun** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **They said to them** - "The Levites said to them"
  * **Yahweh commanded you by the hand of Moses** - The phrase "by the hand of" is an idiom that means that Yahweh used Moses to deliver his command. AT: "Yahweh told Moses to command you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
