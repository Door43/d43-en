===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Arba had been the father of Anak** - This is background information about the name the man who founded the city of Kiriath Arba. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **Anak** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **hill country** - An area of land with natural elevations, smaller than mountains.
  * **pasturelands** - An area covered with grass or plants suitable for the grazing of livestock or cattle.
  * **fields of the city** - Areas of open land, usually, planted with crops, belonging to and surrounding the city.
  * **villages** - Small communities, usually smaller than a town.
