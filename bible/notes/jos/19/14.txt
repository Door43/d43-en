===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Neah,Hannathon, Iphtah El, Kattah, Nahalal, Shimron, and Idalah**  - names of cities. "El" is a Hebrew form of "God" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[en:obe:kt:god]])
  * **border**  - the edge or boundary of something
  * **twelve cities**  - the number of cities beginning with verse 10. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
