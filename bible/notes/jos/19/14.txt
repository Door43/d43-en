===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hannathon ... Iphtah El ... Kattath ... Nahalal ... Shimron ... Idalah ... Bethlehem** - These are names of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Iphtah El** - The word "El" is a Hebrew form of "god."
  * **Bethlehem** - This is not the same "Bethlehem" that is south of Jerusalem in Judah. The name means "house of bread." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **border** - the edge or boundary of something
  * **twelve cities** - "12 cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
