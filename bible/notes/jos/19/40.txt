===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The seventh casting of lots** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:19:01|19:1]].
  * **The seventh** - number seven in a list (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **The territory of its inheritance** - The land that the tribe of Dan received is spoken of as if it was an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "The territory of land that the tribe of Dan received as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Zorah, Eshtaol, Ir Shemesh, Shaalabbin, Aijalon, and Ithlah** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
