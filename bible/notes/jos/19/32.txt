===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sixth casting**  - The number six in a series. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **casting of lots**  - A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome.
  * **Heleph, Zaanannim, Adaminekah, Jabneel, Lakkum, Aznothtabor, and Hukkok**  - Names of cities. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
