====== Joshua 19:5-7 ======


===== TFT: =====

===== Important Terms: =====

 

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Ziklag**  - Translate this the same as in chapter 15:31 and also throughout 1 & 2 Samuel, 1 & 2 Chronicles and Nehemiah.
    * **thirteen cities**  -  the number of cities included with Simeon's land (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
