===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The fifth casting of lots** - Translate this phrase in the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:19:01|19:1]].
  * **The fifth** - number five in a list (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **it was given to their clans** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua gave it to their clans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Helkath, Hali, Beten, Achshaph, Allammelek, Amad, and Mishall ... Shihor Libnath** - These are the names of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
