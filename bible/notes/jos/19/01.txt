===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **second casting of lots** - coming after the first in time or order (See: [[:en:bible:notes:jos:18:05|Joshua 18:6]])
  * **casting of lots** - A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome.
  * **in the middle of** - AT: "completely surrounded by"
