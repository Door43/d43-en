===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This was the inheritance of the tribe of Issachar** - The land and cities that the tribe of Issachar received are spoken of as if they were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "This was the land and the cities that the tribe of Issachar received as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **it was given to their clans** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua gave it to their clans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
