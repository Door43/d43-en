===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Rabbith, Kishion, Ebez, Remeth, Engannim, Enhaddah, Bethpazzez, and Shahazumah**  - the names of cities and brder places (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Tabor**  - A mountain. Translate the same way as in Judges 4:6, 12, 14, 8:18, 1 Samuel 10:13, 1 Chronicles 6:77, Psalm 89:12, Jeremiah 46:18 and Hosea 5:1. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **sixteen cities**  - The total number of cities beginning in verse 18. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]) 
    * **the Jordan**  - A short name for the Jordan River. (See: [[en:obe:other:jordanriver]])
