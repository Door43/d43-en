===== Translation Notes: =====
Joshua tells Israel what Yahweh is about to do

  * **cross over**  - "cross over" means to go to the opposite bank of the river. AT: "travel from this side to the opposite side" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
