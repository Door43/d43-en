===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Moses gave an inheritance to the half tribe of Manasseh** - The land that Moses assigned to the half tribe of Manasseh is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that he gave to them as a permanent possession. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **half tribe of Manasseh** - Only half of the tribe received this land because the other half received land on the other side of the Jordan River.
  * **It was assigned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Moses assigned it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Mahanaim ... Jair ... Ashtaroth ... Edrei** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **These were assigned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Moses assigned these" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Machir** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
