===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **hill country**- a region of land with elevated areas smaller than mountains
    * **Beth Shean, Jezreel**  - names of places or locations (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

    * **they are strong**  - Refers to their warring ability. (See: [[en:obe:kt:power]])
