===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **in the morning**  - AT: during the time from sunrise to noon
    * **present yourselves**  - AT: come before (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
    * **destruction**  - the act or process of destroying or ruining someone or something to the point that it no longer exists
    * **burned**  - AT: destroyed by fire
    * **broken**  - AT: "fractured" or "damaged"
    * **disgraceful**  - AT: shameful
