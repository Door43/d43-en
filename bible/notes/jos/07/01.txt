===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the things that were set apart for destruction** - "the things that God had said they must set apart to him by destroying them"
  * **Achan ... Karmi ... Zabdi ... Zerah** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Yahweh's anger burned** - "anger" and "burned" indicates intensity, not that fire is present. AT: "Yahweh's anger burned like a fire" or "Yahweh was very angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
