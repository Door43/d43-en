===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Achan**  - This is a male name. Translate this the same way you did in [[en:bible:notes:jos:07:01|Joshua 7:1]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Valley of Achor** - "Achor" means "disturbance" or "trouble", this could signify a place of judgement for Achan and all his family. AT: "Valley of trouble"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
