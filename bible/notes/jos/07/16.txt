===== Translation Notes: =====
Joshua follows Yahweh's command to bring Israel before Yahweh.

  * **Zabdi ... Carmi ... Zerah**  - men's names (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Achan** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[en:bible:notes:jos:07:01|7:1]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zerahites** - "descendants of Zerah"
