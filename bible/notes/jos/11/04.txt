===== Translation Notes: =====
All the Canaanite kings attack Joshua and the nation of Israel.
    * **like the sand on the seashore**  - This is deliberate exaggeration. AT: "a very great many" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
    * **Merom**  - This is the name of a place. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
