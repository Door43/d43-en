===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Joshua continues quoting what Yahweh said about his dealings with his people.

  * **Balak ... Zippor** - men's names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **you** - The word "you" is plural throughout this speech and refers to the entire nation of Israel. See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
  * **out of his hand** - Here "hand" refers to power. AT: "enabled you to overcome him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
