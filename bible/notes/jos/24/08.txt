===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Joshua continues quoting what Yahweh said about his dealings with his people.

  * **the Jordan** - This is a short name for the Jordan River.
  * **gave them into your hand.** - This is a figure of speech using the hand to speak of the entire nation. AT: "empowered you to conquer them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
