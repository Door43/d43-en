===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Reubenites** - These are the descendants of Reuben.  
    * **Gadites**  - These are the descendants of Gad.  
    * **half tribe of Manasseh**  - They are called a half tribe because the other half of the tribe received an inheritance in the land of Canaan.
