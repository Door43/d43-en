===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Enaim**  - This is the name of a city. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Jarmuth**  - This is the name of a city. Translate the same way as in [[en:bible:notes:jos:10:03|Joshua 10:3]].
    * **Lachish**  - An important city. Translate the same way as in [[en:bible:notes:jos:10:03|Joshua 10:3]].
    * **Eglon**  - This is the name of a city. Translate the same way as in [[en:bible:notes:jos:10:03|Joshua 10:3]].
    * **Gezer**  - A Canaanite city-state in the foothills of the Judaean Mountains.
