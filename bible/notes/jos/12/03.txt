===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Sihon**  - Name of defeated Amorite king. Translate the same way as in Numbers 21:21, 23, 26-29, 34, 32:33, Deuteronomy 1:4, 2:24, 30-32, 3:2, 6, 4:46, 29:7, 31:4, Joshua 2:10, 9:10, 5, 13:10 and 21. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Og**  - Name of defeated king of Bashan. Translate the same way as in Numbers 21:33, 32:33, Deut 1:4, 3:1, 3, 4, 10, 11, 13, 4:47, 29:7, Joshua 2:10, 9:10, 13:12, 30, 31, 1 Kings 4:19, Nehemiah 9:22, Psalm 135:11 and 136:20. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **the Rephaim**  - name of a people group (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **the Arabah**  - Translate the same way as in Deuteronomy 3:17, 4:49, Joshua 3:16, 15:6, 61, 18:18, 22, 2 Kings 14:25 and Amos 6:14. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and )
    * **Sea of Chinneroth**  - Translate the same way as in Joshua 11:2 and 1 Kings 15:20
    * **Bethjeshimoth**  - Translate the same way as in Joshua 13:20 and Ezekiel 25:9.
    * **Mount Pisgah**  - Translate the same way as Numbers 21:20, 23:14, Deuteronomy 3:17, 27, 4:49, 34:1 and Joshua 13:20.
    * **Ashtoroth**  - Translate the same way as in Deuteronomy 1:4, Joshua 9:10, 13:12, 31 and 1 Chronicles 6:71.
    * **Edrei**  - Translate the same way as in Numbers 21:33, Deuteronomy 1:4, 3:1, 10, Joshua 13:12, 13:31 and 19:37.
    * **Salecah and Maacathites**  - names of places or locations (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Heshbon**  - Name of a place. Translate the same way as in Numbers 21:25-28, 30, 34, 32:3, 37, Deuteronomy 1:4, 2:24, 26, 30, 3:2, 6, 4:46, 29:7, Joshua 12:2, 5, 13:10, 17, 21, 26, 27 and 21:39. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
