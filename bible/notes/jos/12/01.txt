===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **these are the kings**  - Refers to the listing of kings continuing through verse 24.
    * **the Jordan**  - A short name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:En:obe:other:jordanriver]])
    * **Arnon**  - Translate name the same way as in Deuteronomy 2:24, 36, 3:8, 12, 4:48, Joshua 13:9, 16 and 2 Kings 10:33 (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Hermon**  - Translate the same way as noted in [[en:bible:notes:jos:11:16]]
    * **Arabah**  - Translate the same way as in  Deuteronomy 3:17, 4:49, Joshua 3:16, 12:3, 15:6, 61, 18:18, 22, 2 Kings 14:25 and Amos 6:14.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Heshbon**  - Translate the same way as noted in [[en:bible:notes:jos:09:09]].
    * **Aroer**  - Translate the same way as in Numbers 32:34, Deuteronomy 2:36, 3:12, 4:48, Joshua 13:9, 16, 25, Judges 11:26, 33, 1 Samuel 30:28, 2 Samuel 24:5, 2 Kings 10:33, 1 Chronicles 5:8, 11:44, Isaiah 17:2 and Jeremiah 48:19.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Jabbok**  - Translate the same way as in Genesis 32:22, Numbers 21:24, Deuteronomy 2:37, 3:16, Judges 11:13 and 22.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Sihon**  - The name of a king. (See note at [[en:bible:notes:jos:09:09]])
