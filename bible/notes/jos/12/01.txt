===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the writer begins to provide background information. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **these are the kings** - This refers to the list of kings that continues through verse 24.
  * **Arabah ... Aroer** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sihon ... Heshbon** - See how you translated these words in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:09:09|9:10]].
