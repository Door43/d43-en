===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Sin** - This is the name of the wilderness area. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **from the end of the Salt Sea, from the bay that faces to the south** - "from the bay that faces south at the end of the Salt Sea." These two phrases refer to the same location. The second phrase clarifies the point at which the southern border begins.
  * **bay** - smaller part of the sea that extends into the land
  * **that faces to the south** - "that turns toward the south"
