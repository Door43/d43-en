===== translationNotes: =====

  * **let themselves be defeated before them** - "let themselves be defeated before the people of Ai." The phrase "before them" represents what the people of Ai would see and think. AT: "let the people of Ai think that the Israelites were defeated" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **let themselves be defeated before them** - The phrase "be defeated" can be stated in active form. AT: "let the people of Ai think that they had defeated the Israelites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **before them ... they went after ... they were drawn away** - The words "them" and "they" here refer to the army of Ai.
  * **they fled ... go after them** - The words "they" and "them" here refer to the army of Isreal.
  * **All the people who were in the city were called together** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The city leaders called all the people in the city together" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **All the people who were in the city** - The writer speaks in a general way about all the people, but "all the people" refers only to those who could fight. AT: "all the people in the city who could help chase the army of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **left it open** - "left the city gates open"
