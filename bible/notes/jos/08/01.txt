===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Do not fear... do not be discouraged**  - These phrases are similar and are combined for emphasis. Here Yahweh is telling Joshua that he is again on the side of Israel . (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **given into your hand**  - The word "hand" here represents the ownership or control by the nation of Israel. AT: "given the nation of Israel control of" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
