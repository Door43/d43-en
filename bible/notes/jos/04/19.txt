===== translationNotes: =====

  * **came up out of the Jordan** - This refers to when Israel crossed the Jordan River on dry ground.
  * **on the tenth day of the first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the end of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **The twelve stones that they took out of the Jordan** - Each tribe was to take one stone from the Jordan River so Joshua could build a memorial of the crossing event. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
