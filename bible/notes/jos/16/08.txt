===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Tappuah ... Kanah** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **This was the inheritance of the tribe of Ephraim** - The land that Ephraim possessed is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "This was the land that the tribe of Ephraim received as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **assigned to their clans** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "which Joshua assigned to their clans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the cities that were chosen** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the cities that Joshua had chosen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **within the inheritance of the tribe of Manasseh** - The land that the tribe of Manasseh possessed is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "within the land that the tribe of Manasseh had received as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
