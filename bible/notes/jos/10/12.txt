===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Sun, be still at Gibeon, and moon, in the Valley of Aijalon** - Joshua is praying that Yahweh would make the progression of time stop on this day. This emphasizes the power of Yahweh and shows his glory. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **Valley of Aijalon**  - This is the name of a place. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
