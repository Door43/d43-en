===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Jarmuth**  -  A town in the low country of Judah. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Lachish**  -  Is generally regarded as the second most important city in the southern kingdom of Judah. 
    * **Eglon**  - A town of Judah in the low country, known as the "city of palm trees". 
    * **Hoham, Piram, Japhia, and Debir**  - These are male names of kings (See:  [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Come up to me** - AT:"travel to were I am"
