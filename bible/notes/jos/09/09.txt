===== Translation Notes: =====
The Gibeonites continue to deceive Joshua and the nation of Israel.

  * **the Jordan**  - This is a shortened name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Sihon**  - This is the name of the defeated Amorite king. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Heshbon**  - This is the name of the royal city of the nation of Moab. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Og**  - This is the name of the defeated king of Bashan. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ashtaroth**  - This is the name of a city known for worshiping the goddess of the same name. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
