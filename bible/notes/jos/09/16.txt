===== Translation Notes: =====
The nation of Israel finds out that Gibeon tricked them. 

    * **third day**  - This refers to number three in order. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **Chephirah** - one of the cities of the Gibeonites.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Beeroth**  - The name of a place. 
    * **Kirjath Jearim** - Name means city of woods or forests.
