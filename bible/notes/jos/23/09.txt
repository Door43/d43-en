===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **to stand before you**  - Here "stand" represents holding ground in a battle; "you" refers to the entire nation of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **single**  - "one" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **thousand**  - "1,000" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
