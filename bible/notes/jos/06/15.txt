===== Translation Notes: =====
Joshua and Israel march around Jericho - Day 7
  * **people** - refers to the people of Israel 
  * **given to you**  - The word "you" refers to the entire nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
