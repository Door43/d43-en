===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the narrator tells us why the gates of Jericho are closed and locked up. 
  * **I have handed over to you Jericho, its king, and its trained soldiers** - Giving the city and its king and people to the people of Israel represents making Israel able to defeat them. AT: "I am making you able to defeat Jericho, its king, and its trained soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
