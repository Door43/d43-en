===== Translation Notes: =====
The Israelite spies continue to talk to Rahab.

    * **"if you do not do this"**  -  This expresses a condition for the promise the spies had made to Rahab. The word, "this," refers to "tie this scarlet rope in the window" in [[en:bible:notes:jos:02:18|Joshua 2:18]] (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
