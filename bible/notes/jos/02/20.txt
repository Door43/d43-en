===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The two Israelite spies continue to speak to Rahab about their promise to her.
  * **General Information:** - The spies required Rahab to remain silent about their visit or they would be free from their oath to protect her family.
 
  * **if you speak** - "You" refers to Rahab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])
  * **May what you say be done** - Rahab agreed to their terms of the oath to protect her family.
