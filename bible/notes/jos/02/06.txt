===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But she had taken them ... on the roof** - This is background information and explains how she had hidden the men in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:02:04|2:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **the roof** - The roof was flat and strong, so people could walk around on it. 
  * **flax** - a plant that is grown for its fibers, which is used in making cloth
  * **So the men pursued them** - The men pursued the spies because of what Rahab had told them in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:02:04|2:5]].
  * **fords** - places where a river or other body of water is shallow enough for people to get to the other side by walking through it
