===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the roof** - The roof was flat and strong, so people could walk around on it. 
  * **flax**  - a plant that is grown for its fibers, which are used in making cloth
  * **ford**  - a place where a river or other body of water is shallow enough for people to get to the other side by walking through it
