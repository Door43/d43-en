===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will run** - "He" refers to the person who unintentionally killed someone.
  * **Then they will take him** - "they" refers to the elders and "him" refers to the person who unintentionally killed someone.
  * **live among them**  - Refers to the city as a whole, not to the elders only.
