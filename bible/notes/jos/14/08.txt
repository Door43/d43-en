===== translationNotes: =====

  * **made the heart of the people melt with fear** - Making the people very afraid is spoken of as if it were making the hearts of the people melt. AT: "made the people very afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I completely followed Yahweh** - Being loyal to Yahweh is spoken of as if it were completely following Yahweh. AT: "I remained loyal to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the land ... will be an inheritance for you and for your children forever** - The land that Caleb and his descendant would have is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they would receive as a permanent possession. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the land on which your foot has walked** - Here "your foot" represents Caleb. AT: "the land on which you have walked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
