===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **hill country** - an extended portion of land filled with naturally occurring raised areas, lower than mountains
    * **that day** - a certain point in time
    * **Anakim**  - a people group (See: [[ en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **great,fortified cities**  - having walls around them for protection
    * **drive them away** - force them to leave the area (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
