===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the north shoulder of Beth Hoglah** - Land that is in the form of a slope or ridge is spoken of as if it were a shoulder. AT: "the north slope of Beth Hoglah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Beth Hoglah** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **This was the inheritance of the tribe of Benjamin** - The land that the tribe of Benjamin received is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "This was the land that the tribe of Benjamin received as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **it was given to each of their clans** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua gave it to each of their clans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
