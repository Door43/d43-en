===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and they conquered the land** - They had conquered the people who lived in the land before they set up the tent of meeting. AT: "after they had conquered the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_events]])
  * **whose inheritance had not been assigned** - The land that the tribes would receive is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they would receive as a permanent possession. This can be stated in active form. AT: "to whom Joshua had not assigned land as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
