===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All scripture has been inspired by God**  - "God has spoken all the scriptures by his Spirit" (UDB) or "all scripture is God-breathed," produced by the Spirit of God. God told people what to write.
  * **profitable**  - "useful" or "beneficial"
  * **conviction**  - "pointing out error"
  * **correction**  - "fixing errors"
  * **training**  - "discipling" or "bringing up"
  * **competent**  - "complete"
