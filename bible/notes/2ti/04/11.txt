===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he is useful to me in the work** - Possible meanings are 1) "he can help me in the ministry" or 2) "he can help me by serving me."
  * **cloak** - a heavy garment worn over clothes
  * **Carpus** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the books** - This refers to scrolls. A scroll was a type of book made of one long sheet of papyrus or leather. After writing on a scroll or reading it, people rolled it up using rods on the ends
  * **especially the parchments** - This may refer to a specific type of scroll. AT: "especially those made from animal skins"
