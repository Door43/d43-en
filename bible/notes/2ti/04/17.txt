===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the Lord stood by me** - Paul is speaking as if the Lord had physically stood with him. AT: "the Lord helped me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **so that, through me, the proclamation might be fully fulfilled** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "so that I was able to speak all of the Lord's message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I was rescued out of the lion's mouth** - Paul is speaking about danger as if he had been threatened by a lion. This danger could have been physical, spiritual, or both. AT: "I was rescued from great danger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
