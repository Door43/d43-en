====== 2 Timothy 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The ULB has chose to indent the lines of 2:11-13 to set it apart from the rest of the text as the "trustworthy saying." This is not necessary to duplicate in translation but it may be helpful.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== "We will reign with him" ==
It is unknown how this will work, but the faithful Christian are said to reign with Christ in the future. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==
Paul uses several different [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphors]] to illustrate what the Christian life is like.
