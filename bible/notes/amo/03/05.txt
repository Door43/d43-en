===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Can a bird fall in a trap on the ground when no bait is set for him?**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
    * **Will a trap spring up from the ground when it has not caught anything?**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
    * **Will the trumpet sound in a city and the people not tremble?**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
    * **Will disaster come on a city and Yahweh has not sent it?**  - This is a Rhetorical Question to emphasize the absolute truth of what God is going to say later in verses 7-8.
