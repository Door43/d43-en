===== translationNotes: =====

  * **this is what Yahweh says, the God of hosts, the Lord** - "this is what Yahweh, the God of hosts, the Lord says"
  * **Wailing will be in all the squares** - "People will wail in all the town squares"
  * **Wailing** - long, loud, sad cries
  * **the squares** - broad open places in the town where people gather 
  * **the mourners to wail** - The phrase "they will call" is understood from the beginning of the sentence. AT: "they will call the mourners to wail" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **I will pass through your midst** - God speaks of punishing the people as if he were to come and punish them while walking through the group of them. AT: "I will come and punish you" or "I will punish you"
