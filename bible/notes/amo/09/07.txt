===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **brought up Israel out of the land of Egypt** - Here the man "Israel" stands for the whole nation of "Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Are you not like the people of Ethiopia to me, people of Israel?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] with obvious answer yes.   The idea is that Israel had stooped themselves to the abominations of Ethiopia.
    * **Did I not brought up Israel out of the land of Egypt, and the Philistines from Crete, and the Arameans from Kir?**  - This is also a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] with obvious answer yes.
    * **the house of Jacob**  - The people or nation of Israel (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
