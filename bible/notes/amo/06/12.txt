===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Do horses run on the rocky cliffs?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and the answer is obviously "no".
    * **Does one plow there with oxen?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and the answer is obviously "no".  See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]
    * **Yet you have turned justice into poison**  - This means perverted justice (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]))
    * **and the fruit of righteousness into bitterness**  - This implies that good is being punished or condemned (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]))
