===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I hate his fortresses**  - God hates that the nation of Israel is pridefully trusting in its own military might and the strength of the fortified walls around their cities in lieu of righteously honoring and trusting in God (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
