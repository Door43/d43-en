====== Amos 08 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is written in poetic form and it shows the way Yahweh's people are sinning and
how he is going to respond. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:peopleofgod]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== "This is the declaration of Yahweh" ==

This phrase is used to introduce prophecy. It highlights what God is proclaiming. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]])
