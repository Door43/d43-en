===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues his message of judgment on the people of Israel.
  * **General Information:** - The word "you" in these verses refers to the people of Israel.

  * **Look** - This alerts the reader to pay attention to what follows. AT: "Listen" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you"
  * **I will crush you as a cart that is full of grain can crush someone** - Yahweh compares his judgment on the people of Israel to crushing them with something very heavy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **The swift ... the strong ... the mighty** - These adjectives refer to people in general. AT: "Swift people ... strong people ... mighty people" or "The strong person ... the strong person ... the mighty person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
