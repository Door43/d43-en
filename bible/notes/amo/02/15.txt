===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The archer will not stand** - "The archer will run away"
  * **the fast runner will not escape** - "the fast runner will be captured" 
  * **the horseman will not save himself** - "the horseman will die" 
  * **flee naked** - Possible meanings are 1) "run away without his weapons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]]) or 2) "run away wearing no clothes."
  * **declaration of Yahweh** - Translate these words as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:amo:02:11|2:11]].
