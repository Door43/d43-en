===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and a book of remembrance was written before him for those who feared Yahweh** - This can mean 1) the Israelites wrote a book so they would remember what they had promised and the names of people who fear Yahweh (see UDB) or 2) Yahweh caused someone in heaven to write a book with the names of people who fear Yahweh.
  * **book of remembrance** - book to help people remember important things
  * **esteemed his name** - respected him (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
