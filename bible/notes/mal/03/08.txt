===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **Would a person rob God?** - This expression implies that the idea of robbing God is very wicked. AT: "A man should certainly not rob God!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **How have we robbed you?** - This question implies that the people do not think they have robbed God. AT: "We have certainly not robbed you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **You are cursed with a curse** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I have certainly cursed you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
