===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **as in the days of old, and as in ancient years** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize that the offering was once pleasing to Yahweh. AT: "as it was in the past" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
  * **oppress the hired worker in his wages** - "do not pay the hired worker the wages due him"
  * **turn away the foreigner from his rights** - "deny foreigners living in the community the rights that the local people have"
