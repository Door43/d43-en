===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The declaration of the word of Yahweh to Israel by the hand of Malachi.-**  (Metonymy) " Yahweh spoke to Israel through Malachi."
  * **But you say, "How have you loved us?" -**  This question indicates doubt, or questions the truth of God's words.
  * **declares Yahweh. - **"says Yahweh."
  * **"Yet I have loved Jacob, - **  Yahweh chose Jacob to bless**.**
  * **but Esau I have hated. - **  Yahweh rejected Esau because of his sin.
  * **I have made his mountains an abandoned devastation, -**  "I have made his high lands unfit to live in,"
  * **and I have made his inheritance a place for the jackals of the wilderness." - **  "and I have made his inherited lands a desert where only jackals (or 'desert animals' or 'wild dogs') can live."
