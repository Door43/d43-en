===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh, if only - This expresses great desire.**
  * **there were one of you who would shut the temple gates,-**  The gates of the temple courtyard were open most of the time and were closed only by temple servants when it was necessary.
  * **so that you might not light fires on my altar in vain! -**  The word 'light' here refers to making a fire. "so that you might not make fire and sin by offering worthless sacrifices."
  * **accept any offering -**  "accept any worthless (or 'unacceptable' or 'evil' or 'sinful') offering"
  * **from your hand. -**  (An Idiom or Metonymy) "from you." or "that you bring to me."
  * **For from the rising of the sun to its setting my name will be great among the nations; - **"My name is honored from morning until night in many nations,"
  * **in every place incense will be offered to my name, -**  "and in these places incense will be offered to my name,"
  * **and also a pure offering. - **'pure' can mean "genuine" or "true" or "acceptable."
  * **But you are profaning it when you say the Lord's table is polluted, and its fruit, its food is to be despised. -**  "You are reviling (or 'dishonoring' or 'sinning against') me when you say that it is all right to defile the Lord's table with unacceptable offerings."
