===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Oh, if only** - This expresses great desire.
  * **so that you might not light fires on my altar in vain** - Here "light fires on my altar" stands for offering sacrifices on Yahweh's altar. AT: "so that you might not make fires to burn offerings that I will not accept" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **from your hand** - Here "your hand" stands for "you." AT: "from you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **from the rising of the sun to its setting** - This double expression means "everywhere." It is parallel to "among the nations" and "in every place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
  * **my name will be great among the nations** - Here "my name" stands for "I." AT: "I will be honored in other nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my name** - Here "name" stands for "reputation," "honor." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in every place incense will be offered to my name** - This may be expressed in active form. AT: "in these nations people will offer incense to me in order to worship me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **incense will be offered to my name** - This action is an act of worship.
  * **to my name** - Here "name" stands for Yahweh. AT: "to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **its fruit, its food** - Possible meanings are 1) "the meat sacrificed on the altar from animals whose other parts the priests should eat" or 2) "the meat sacrificed on the altar."
