===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If Edom says, - **"If the people of Edom say," The people of Edom are descendants of Esau.
  * **"We are beaten down, but we will return and build the ruins; - **'beaten down' could mean "destroyed," or "ruined," or "conquered." 'build the ruins' could mean "try to rebuild," and 'ruins' could mean "cities" or "buildings" or "houses."
  * **but I will throw down; -**  (Metonymy) "I will destroy it,"
  * **and men will call them -**  "and men of other nations will call them"
  * **country of wickedness,' - **"country of evil men that are wicked,"
  * **Your own eyes will see this, - **"People of Israel, you will see this happen,"
