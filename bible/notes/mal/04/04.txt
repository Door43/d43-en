===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Remember the teaching of my servant Moses that I gave him** - The abstract noun "teaching" can be stated as "taught." AT: "Remember what I taught my servant Moses" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Remember** - Here "Remember" stands for "Think about" and, at the same time, "Obey."
  * **Horeb** - This is another name for Sinai.
  * **all Israel** - Here "all Israel" is a reference to all the people in the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **the statutes** - These are the laws that God gave Israel for all time.
  * **the rulings** - These are legal decisions meant to make clear how the general statutes apply to everyday life.
  * **the coming of the great and fearful day of Yahweh** - Here the occurrence of this day is spoken of as if it were coming. AT: "before the great and fearful day of Yahweh happens" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **the great and fearful day of Yahweh** - This expression refers to any time in which Yahweh acts decisively. 
  * **He will turn the heart of the fathers to the children, and the heart of the children to their fathers** - Here changing how people think is spoken of as if it were turning their hearts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
