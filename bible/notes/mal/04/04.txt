===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Remember the law of my servant Moses, - **"Remember the law that I gave to my servant Moses."
  * **the statues and rules that I commanded him at Horeb for all Israel. -**  "Obey the statutes and decrees (or "rules") that I gave him for all of Israel at Mount Sinai." Horeb is the same mountain as Sinai.
  * **See, I will send you Elijah the prophet before the great and terrible day of Yahweh comes. -**  "Before that great and terrible day of Yahweh arrives, I will send the prophet Elijah to you."
