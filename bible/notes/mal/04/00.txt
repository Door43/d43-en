====== Malachi 04 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Last days ==
Although the Jews may have hoped these prophecies referenced a time in the near future to them, the prophecies of this chapter exclusively relate to the last days. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]] and [[:en:obe:kt:lastday]])
