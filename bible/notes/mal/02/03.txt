===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and I will spread dung on your faces** - "I will most certainly treat you with disrespect" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will be taken away with it** - "they will throw you on the dung pile with it"
  * **Levi** - Yahweh speaks of the tribe of Levi (see UDB) as though they are one person. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
