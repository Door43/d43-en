===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And now,you priests, this command is for you. - **"Listen you priests, this warning is for you."
  * **"If you will not listen, and if you will not take it to heart to give glory to my name," says Yahweh of Hosts, -**  (Figure of speech. See Discussion.)"If you do not heed my warning and do not give deep thought to my command, and give honor to my name," says Yahweh of Hosts,"
  * **"then I will send a curse on you, and I will curse your blessings. - **"then I will send a curse on you, and turn around (or 'stop') the blessings you are already receiving."
  * **Indeed, I have cursed them already, because you do not take my command to heart. - **  (Figure of Speech. See line 2 above) "In fact, I have already begun to turn your blessings into curses because you do not listen to me and follow my commands." or "and honor my name."
