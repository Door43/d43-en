===== translationNotes: =====

  * **lay it on your heart** - "make it very important" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **give honor to my name** - Honor is spoken of as if it were an object that can be given to someone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **I will send a curse on you** - A curse is spoken of as if it were an object that could be sent upon someone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
