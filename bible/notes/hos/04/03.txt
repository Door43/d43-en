===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the land is drying up** - This expression refers to drought, when no rain falls for a long time.
  * **wasting away** - becoming weaker and dying because of sickness or no food
  * **are being taken away** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "are dying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
