===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking about Israel.

  * **They feed on the sin of my people** - When people sinned, they would offer sacrifices so God would forgive them. The priests were allowed to eat these sacrifices. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they are greedy for more of their wickedness** - The priests want the people to sin more so that the people will offer more sacrifices that the priests may eat.
  * **It will be the same for the people as for the priests** - "The people and the priests will be punished in the same way"
  * **their practices** - "their habits" or "their conduct"
