===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **Sow righteousness for yourselves, and reap the fruit of covenant faithfulness** - Righteousness and covenant faithfulness are spoken of as if they were crops that could be sown and harvested. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Break up your unplowed ground** - When the ground is "unplowed" it is not ready to be planted. Yahweh means he wants the people to repent so they can start doing what is right. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **You have plowed wickedness; you reaped injustice** - Wickedness and injustice are spoken of as if they were crops that could be sown and harvested. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **You have eaten the fruit of deception** - The result of deception are spoken of as if they were a food that could be eaten. AT: "You now suffer the consequences of having deceived each other" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
