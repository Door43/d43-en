===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Samaria's king will be destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Assyrians will destroy the king of Samaria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **like a chip of wood on the surface of the water** - This means the king of Samaria will be as helpless as a small piece of wood that waves of the water toss back and forth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **The shrines of wickedness—the sin of Israel—will be destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Assyrians will destroy Israel's wicked shrines where they sinned against Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
