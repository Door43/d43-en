===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like a chip of wood on the surface of the water **- The king of Samaria is compared to a chip of wood. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **chip**  - a small piece that has been broken off of something larger
  * **The shrines of wickedness - the sin of Israel - will be destroyed** - The shrines the Israelites built to their idols were wicked in God's sight. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
