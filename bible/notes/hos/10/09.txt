===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.

  * **days of Gibeah **- See how you translated this phrase in __09:09__.
  * **there you have remained.**  - //there you have remained//  can be made explicit: "it is as if you have been living there ever since." (UDB) (see: Explicit and Implicit information)
  * **Did not war overtake the evildoers in Gibeah?**  - The result of the evil done in Gibeah was a war that killed many people. (See:  rhetorical questions)
