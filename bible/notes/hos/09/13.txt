===== Translation Notes: =====
Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **I have seen Ephraim, just like Tyre, planted in a meadow** - AT: "The nation of Israel was once beautiful and pleasant like the city of Tyre, like a tree that someone plants in a meadow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **but Ephraim will bring out his children** - AT: "but the people of Israel will bring out their children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Give them, Yahweh—what will you give them?** - Hosea uses a question to emphasize that he wants Yahweh to give the people of Israel what they deserve. AT: "This is what I ask you, Yahweh, to give them:" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **a miscarrying womb** - To "miscarry" means that a pregnancy ends too early and the baby dies.
