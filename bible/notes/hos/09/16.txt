===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking in verse 16.

Hosea, the prophet, is speaking in verse 17.

  * **Ephraim is diseased; and their root is dried up; they bear no fruit. **- Ephraim is being compared to a vine that does not bear fruit because its roots have dried up. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
