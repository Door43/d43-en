===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **like a lion ... like a leopard ... like a bear ... like a lion ... like a wild beast** - These are wild animals that attack and kill other animals. Yahweh continues to emphasize how he will destroy his people for their sins. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like a bear that is robbed of her cubs** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "like a bear attacks an animal that takes her cubs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
