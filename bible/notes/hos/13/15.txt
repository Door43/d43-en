===== Translation Notes: =====

Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **an east wind will come; the wind of Yahweh** - This means that Yahweh will judge Israel soon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Ephraim's spring will dry up, and his well will have no water** - The northern kingdom of Israel is referred to as a tree or a plant that always needs water to live. AT: "The people of Israel will meet disaster" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **his storehouse** - This refers to all of the peoples' possessions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
