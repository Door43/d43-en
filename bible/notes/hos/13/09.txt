===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **Where now is your king, that he may save you in all your cities? Where are your rulers, about whom you said to me, 'Give me a king and princes'?** - Yahweh uses these questions to tell Israel that when they rebel against him, no king or ruler can help them. Only Yahweh can save them from destruction. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
