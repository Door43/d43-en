===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **but it was only so they might be cut off** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "but the result will be that I will destroy the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **"He has thrown away your calf, Samaria"** - Possible meanings are 1) this is the prophet speaking (see UDB) or 2)  Yahweh is emphasizing his own importance by speaking of himself as if he were speaking of someone else. AT: "I myself have thrown away your calf, Samaria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]]) 
  * **thrown away** - "rejected" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **your calf** - The people made an idol that looked like a calf and worshiped it, so Yahweh is speaking of their worship as if it were the calf itself. AT: "you because you worship your calf idol" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **My anger burns against these people** - "I am very angry with these people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **For how long will they stay impure?** - Yahweh asks this question to express his anger about his people being impure. "I am angry with these people because they have no desire to be pure." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
