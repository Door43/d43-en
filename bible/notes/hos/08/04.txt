===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **but it was only so they might be cut off** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "but the result will be that I will destroy the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cut off** - That is, destroy (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Your calf has been rejected, Samaria** - Possible meanings are 1) this is the prophet speaking (see UDB) or 2)  Yahweh is speaking.  AT: "I myself have rejected your calf, Samaria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]]) 
  * **Your calf** - King Jeroboam the Second of Israel (See [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:12:28]]) had made two golden idols that looked like calves and set them up so the people of his kingdom would worship it. Yahweh is speaking of their worship as if it were the calves themselves. AT: "You because you worship your calf idol" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **My anger is burning against these people** - Anger is often spoken of as if it were a fire. AT: "I am very angry with these people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **For how long will they stay guilty?** - Yahweh asks this question to express his anger about his people being impure. "I am angry with these people because they have no desire to be innocent." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
