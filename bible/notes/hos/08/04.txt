===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.

  * **but it was only so they might be cut off** - This will be the result of idol worship, not the purpose of idol worship. AT: "but as a result of making idols, the people will be cut off"
  * **they might be cut off** - Possible meanings are 1) "they will perish" or 2) "I will send them away"
  * **your calf** - AT: "your idol that you made to look like a calf" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **My anger burns against these people** - AT: "I am very angry with these people"
  * **For how long will they stay impure?**  - Yahweh asks this question to express his anger about his people being impure. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
