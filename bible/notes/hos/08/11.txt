===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.

  * **these altars have become a sin to him.**- //These altars have become a sin to him //  can be made explicit: "These altars have become places all across Ephraim where the people commit terrible sins against me." (UDB) (see: Explicit and Implicit information)
  * **I could write down my Law for them ten thousand times**  - This means that Yahweh has already given Israel his Law and expectations through the prophets many, many times. (see: Hyperbole)
  * **but they would view it as something strange to them.**  - //but they would view it as something strange to them//  can be made explicit: "they would refuse to obey them.They would say they had never heard of them." (UDB) (see: Explicit and implicit information)
