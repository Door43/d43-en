===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.

  * **Israel is swallowed up** - The tribes of Israel have been taken captive by their enemies and taken away to other lands. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **like a wild donkey all alone**  - Israel is compared to a wild donkey, an animal that was known to be headstrong and unruly (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Ephraim has hired lovers for herself**- AT: "The people of Israel have tried to pay other nations to protect them." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
