===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **For with hearts like an oven** - This means like a fire burns in an oven, these people have strong evil desires within them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Their anger smolders** - The word "smolders" means something is burning slowly without a flame. AT: "Their anger grows slowly and quietly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **it burns high like a flaming fire** - AT: "it gets very intense" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **They all are hot as an oven** - This compares their anger to the heat that comes from an oven. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
