===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to Hosea about what he will do for Israel.

  * **I will plant her for myself in the land** - AT: "I will take care of the Israelite people as a farmer plants his own land and takes care of his crops" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Lo Ruhamah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hos:01:06|1:6]].
  * **Lo Ammi** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hos:01:08|1:9]].
  * **Ammi Attah** - AT: "my people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
