===== Translation Notes: =====

God is speaking to Hosea about what he will do to Israel.

  * **On that day will I make a covenant for them with the beasts in the fields, with the birds in the sky, and with the crawling things on the ground. **- Yahweh will protect Israel from all the possible dangers from the environment in which they are living. THis symbolizes the peace that will surround Israel when they worship Yahweh. (see: Metaphor)
  * **I will drive away the bow, the sword, and the battle from the land, and I will make you lie down in safety**. - Yahweh will keep Israel's enemies away from them; there will be no more war; the people will live in safety. (see: Metaphor and Synecdoche)
