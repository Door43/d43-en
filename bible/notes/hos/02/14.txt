===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking about Israel.

  * **So I am going to win her back** - AT: "I, Yahweh, will bring her back to me"
  * **and the Valley of Achor as a door of hope** - Yahweh is waiting for his people to confess that they have sinned by worshiping idols. This is Yahweh's hope for his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **She will answer me there as she did in the days of her youth, as in the days that she came out of the land of Egypt** - Yahweh hopes that the nation of Israel will repent and again choose to worship him as their God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
