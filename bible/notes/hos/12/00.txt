====== Hosea 12 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jacob ==

There is a parallel between Jacob and the northern kingdom of Israel. In the same way Jacob returned to Yahweh, the northern kingdom should [[:en:obe:kt:repent|repent]] of their idolatry and turn back to Yahweh.
