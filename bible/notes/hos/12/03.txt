===== Translation Notes: =====

Hosea the prophet is speaking about Jacob the patriarch.

  * **In the womb Jacob grasped his brother by the heel**  - He wanted to take his brother's place so he tried to keep his brother from being born first. (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **He struggled with the angel and won.**  - Jacob fought with an angel so that the angel would bless him. (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
