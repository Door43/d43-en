Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **So his Master will leave the guilt of his blood on him** - As shown in the UDB translation, this phrase is saying that these people did many sins against their Master and he is not going to try get rid of them. (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
