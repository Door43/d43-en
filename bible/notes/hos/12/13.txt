===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **by a prophet** - That is, Moses.
  * **bitterly** - The anger that the people have caused in Yahweh is extremely great. 
  * **So his Lord will leave his blood on him** - Here "blood" refers to the guilt incurred by people who murder others. God will not forgive their sins. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will turn back on him his disgrace** - The idea of making someone suffer the results of his own actions is spoken of as if those actions were objects that were thrown back at him. AT: "will make him suffer from his own disgraceful actions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metaphor]])
