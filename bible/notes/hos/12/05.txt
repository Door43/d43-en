===== Translation Notes: =====

Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **"Yahweh" is his name to be called on** - The meaning for "called on" can mean "prayed to" or "worshipped" (UDB). 
  * **Keep covenantal faithfulness and justice** - One must obey God's law and do justly. (UDB).
