===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **The people who live in his shade will return** - This means the people of Israel will be strong enough to help anyone who comes to them for help. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they will revive like grain** - The people of Israel will sustain themselves with food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and blossom like vines** - The people of Israel will sustain themselves with drink. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **His fame will be like the wine of Lebanon** - AT: "People everywhere will know about the nation of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **What more have I to do with idols?** - This question emphasizes that the people of Israel will not worship idols any more. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **a cypress whose leaves are always green** - A "cypress" is a tree whose leaves stay green all year. This represents Yahweh and his blessings on Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **from me comes your fruit** - Here "fruit" represents every good thing that comes from Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
