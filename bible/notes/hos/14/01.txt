===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **for you have fallen because of your iniquity** - Here "fallen" means they have lost God's favor and blessing and are now experiencing misery because of their sin. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Take with you words of confession** - AT: "Confess your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the fruit of our lips** - "lips" refers to what people say. AT: "our words and songs of praise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
