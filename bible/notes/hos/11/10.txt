===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking about when he will restore his people.

  * **They will walk after me, Yahweh** - "They will live their lives following my commands" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will roar like a lion** - This means Yahweh will call for his people, and they all will hear home and return to their land. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **They will come trembling like a bird...like a dove** - This means they will come home quickly like a bird returns to its nest. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **I will make them live in their homes** - "I will let them live in their own homes in the land of Israel again"
  * **the declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hos:02:12|2:13]].
