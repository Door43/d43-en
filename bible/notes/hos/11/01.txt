===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking of caring for Israel like a parent cares for a child.

  * **When Israel was a young man** - Yahweh is speaking about the people of Israel as if they were a young man. Yahweh is referring to when he first started his relationship with the nation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I called my son out of Egypt** - "son" refers to the people of God the Father. AT: "I led my son out of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The more they were called, the more they turned away** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The more I called them to be my people, the more they refused me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
