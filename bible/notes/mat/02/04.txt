===== Translation Notes: =====

Herod was troubled by the news of the birth of Jesus.

  * **in Bethlehem of Judea**  - this can be translated "in the town of Bethlehem of Judea."
  * **this is what was written by the prophet**  - This can be stated in active voice as "this is what the prophet wrote." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **written by the prophet **- this can be translated "written by the prophet Micah"
  * **and you, Bethlehem **- "You who live in Bethlehem" (UDB)
