===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of what happened when Jesus was born as the King of the Jews.

  * **But when he heard **- "But when Joseph heard"
  * **his father Herod **- This is Archelaus' father.
  * **he was afraid to go there **- The "he" refers to Joseph.
  * **he would be called a Nazarene **- The "he" refers to Jesus.
