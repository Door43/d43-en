===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After they** - "After the learned men"
  * **they had seen in the east** - "they had seen come up in the east" or "they had seen in their country"
  * **went before them** - "guided them" or "led them"
  * **stood still over** - "stopped over"
  * **where the young child was** - "the place where the young child was staying"
