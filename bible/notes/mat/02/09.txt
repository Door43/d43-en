===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of what happened when Jesus was born as the King of the Jews.

  * **After they heard the king **- "then" (UDB) or "after the learned men heard the king"
  * **went before them**  - Alternative translation: "guided them."
  * **stood still over **- Alternative translation: "stopped over."
