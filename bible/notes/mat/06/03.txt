===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus instructs on giving.

  * **But when you give alms**- The word "you" refers to the crowd that Jesus is talking to.
  * **left hand know what your right hand**  - This does not refer to your physical left or right hand but rather doing an action out of a pure motive. (see: metaphor)
  * **your alms may be given in private**  - This phrase is saying give to the poor without other people knowing.
