===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - All instances of "you" and "your" are plural. However, Jesus is telling them what will happen to them as individuals if each person does not forgive others. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **trespasses** - "wrongs" or "sins"
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
