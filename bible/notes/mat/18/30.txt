===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues his story illustrating Peter's forgiveness of his brother's sins against him.
  * **the first servant refused; instead, he went and threw him into prison **- The author uses irony to highlight that the first servant did the opposite of what was expected of him considering the mercy that his master had just shown to him.
  * **threw him into prison **- <font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>the first servant</font>    did this to the one who owed him the small amount of money (See the UDB for pronoun clarity.)
