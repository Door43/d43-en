===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach about repentance and forgiveness.

  * **to listen to them** - listen to the witnesses ([[:en:bible:notes:mat:18:15|18:16]])
  * **let him be to you as a Gentile and a tax collector** - "treat him as you would treat a Gentile or tax collector"
