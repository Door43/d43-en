===== Translation Notes: =====

To illustrate forgiveness, Jesus continues telling the story about a man who owed lots of money.

  * **fell down, bowed down**  - probably down on his knees, and bowed his head down
  * **before him**  - before the king (See UDB)
  * **released him**  - instead of making him and his family slaves, he let him go
  * **forgave him the debt**  - let the debt be gone
