===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus uses a parable to teach about repentance and forgiveness. 

  * **one hundred denarii** - "100 denarii" or "one hundred days' wages" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
  * **grasped** - "took hold of" or "grabbed" (UDB)
  * **fell down...Have patience with me, and I will repay you** - Translate this as you translated "fell down...have patience with me, and I will pay you" in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:18:26|18:26]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
