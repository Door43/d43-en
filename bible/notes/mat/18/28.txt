===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable to his disciples. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **one hundred denarii** - "100 denarii" or "one hundred days' wages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **He grasped him** - "The first servant grasped his fellow servant"
  * **grasped** - "took hold of" or "grabbed" 
  * **fell down** - This shows that the servant approached the king in the most humble way possible. See how you translated these phrases in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:18:26|18:26]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
