===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus concludes his story (or parable) about forgiveness that he started when Peter asked how often he should forgive his brother.
  * **His master was angry **- The master was angry because the first servant treated his fellow servant unfairly.
  * **handed him over to the torturers until he would pay all that was owed**  - This could be translated "handed him over to the torturers for the rest of his life because this debt could never be repaid."
