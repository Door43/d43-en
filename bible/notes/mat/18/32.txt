===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable to his disciple. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **Then that servant's master called him** - "Then the king called the first servant"
  * **you implored me** - "you begged me"
  * **Should you not have...you?** - The king uses a question to scold the first servant. AT: "You should have...you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
