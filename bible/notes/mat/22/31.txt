===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues talking to the Sadducees about marriage.
  * **have you not read**- Jesus said this to the Sadducees (See: Rhetorical question.)
  * **what was spoken to you by God **- This can be translated as "what God spoke to you." (See: Passive Voice)
  * **God, saying**  - This is a quote within a quote. "God said to Moses that he (God) was the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob." (See: Indirect quote.)
  * **When the crowds heard this **- "this" refers to what Jesus taught about the resurrection.
  * **they were astonished**  - This could be stated in the active voice "his teaching astonished them."
  * **astonished**  â€‹ - Astonished means that they marveled at his teaching. (See "marvel" in Key Terms.)
