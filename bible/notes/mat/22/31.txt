===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues talking to the Sadducees about the resurrection.

  * **have you not read...Jacob'?**- Jesus reminds the Sadducees of what scripture says. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **what was spoken to you by God **- "what God spoke to you" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **God, saying, 'I am ...Jacob'?**  - This is a quote within a quote. "God said to Moses that he (God) is the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob." (See: Indirect Quote)
  * **I am the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob** - It is very important to keep this in the present tense.
  * **When the crowds heard this **- "When the crowds heard what Jesus taught"
  * **they were astonished**  -  "his teaching astonished them." (See: Active/Passive)
