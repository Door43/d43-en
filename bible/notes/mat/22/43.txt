===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus has asked the Pharisees a question about the Christ--the Messiah.

  * **How then does David in the Spirit call him Lord**  - Jesus uses a question to make a point. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **The Lord said to my Lord** - They understood that David in the first "Lord" was referring to himself and in the second "Lord" was referring to the Messiah. (Psalm 110:1)
  * **at my right hand **- The "right hand" is often used to indicate a place of honor. (See: Metonymy)
  * **until I make your enemies your footstool** - "Until I conquer your enemies" (See: Idiom)
