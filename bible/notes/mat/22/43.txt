===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to question the religious leaders about the Messiah.

  * **at my right hand **- The "right hand" is often used to indicate a place of honor. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **until I make your enemies your footstool** - "Until I conquer your enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
