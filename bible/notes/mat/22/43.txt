===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **How then does David in the Spirit call him Lord**  - Jesus uses a question to make a point. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **The Lord said to my Lord**  - The first Lord refers to God the Father and the second Lord refers to the Messiah. David spoke of the Messiah as the Lord. (Psalm 110:1)
  * **at my right hand **- "at a place of honor" (See: Metonymy)
  * **Till I put your enemies underneath your feet**  - "Until I conquer your enemies" (See: metonymy)
