===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The kingdom of heaven is like **- Jesus tells a parable about the kingdom of heaven (See: Simile)
  * **marriage feast **- "a meal that celebrates a wedding"
  * **those who were invited**  - This can be translated in the active voice "the people who received the invitation." (See: Passive/Active)
