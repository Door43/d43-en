===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus tells a parable about a marriage feast to rebuke the religious leaders and to illustrate their unbelief.

  * **The kingdom of heaven is like** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:13:24|13:24]]. 
  * **those who were invited** - To make it clear who is being called, you may want to use an active verb. AT: "the people the king had invited." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
