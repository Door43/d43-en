===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them **- "them" refers to the Pharisees and others who were sent.
  * **Whose image and name are these **- Jesus knew whose name is on the coin. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **image**  - "picture"
  * **things that are Caesar's**  - refers to the taxes or any other responsibilities they might have to the Roman government.
  * **things that are God's**  - refers to giving God what he deserves
  * **When they heard it **- "it" refers to the answer Jesus gave.
