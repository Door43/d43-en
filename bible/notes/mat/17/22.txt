===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples in Galilee.

  * **they stayed** - "the disciples and Jesus were staying"
  * **The Son of Man will be delivered over** - AT: "Someone will deliver the Son of Man over" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they will kill him** - "the authorities will kill the Son of Man"
  * **he will be raised up** - "God will raise him up" or "he will come back to life again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
