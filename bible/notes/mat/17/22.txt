===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **While they**  - The word "they" refers to the disciples.
  * **The Son of Man will be given up into the hands of people **- This can be translated as " Someone will give up the Son of Man into the hands of the people". (See Passive)
  * **Son of Man -**  Jesus was referring to himself.
  * **hands of people **- This can be translated as "control of those who are in authority". (See metonymy)
  * **and they will kill him**  - The word "they" refers to the authorities.
  * **he will be raised up **- "God will raise him up".
