===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **While they**  - The word "they" refers to the disciples and Jesus.
  * **The Son of Man will be delivered over into the hands of people **- "Someone will give the Son of Man into the hands of the people." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **into the hands of people **- This can be translated as " into the control of those who are in authority." (See: Metonymy)
  * **and they will kill him**  - The word "they" refers to the authorities.
  * **he will be raised up. **- "God will raise him up." or "He will come back to life again." (See: Active/Passive)
