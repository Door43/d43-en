===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **While he**  - "he" refers to Peter
  * **overshadowed**  - (See UDB)
  * **there was a voice out of the ****cloud**  They heard God speaking about Jesus from inside the cloud. (UDB) - implied information
  * **When the disciples heard it**  - "it" refers to the voice of God out of the cloud.
  * **they fell on their face**  - the disciples with Jesus "fell face down" or "bowed with their face on the ground"
  * **and were very afraid**  - “were terrified.”
