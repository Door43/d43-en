===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus healing a boy who had an evil spirit.

  * **how long will I have to stay with you? How long must I bear with you?** - Jesus is unhappy with the people. AT: "I am tired of being with you! I am tired of your unbelief and corruption!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
