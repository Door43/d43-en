===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **how long will I have to stay with you? **- (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **How long must I bear with you? **- These rhetorical questions don't expect an answer. They express Jesus' extreme displeasure with the people. (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **Bring him here to me**  - "him" refers to the sick, demon possessed boy and "me" refers to Jesus.
  * **rebuked him **- Here "him" refers to a demon that possessed the boy.
  * **out of him **-"out of the boy"
