===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **John his brother**  - John, the brother of James (See: UDB)
  * **He was transfigured **- "He" refers to Jesus.
  * **He was transfigured**  - "He transfigured his appearance" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **transfigured**  - Jesus' appearance was completely changed.
  * **like the sun**  - It tells how brightly Jesus' face shone or glowed. (See: Simile)
  * **garments**  - Jesus' clothing
  * **as light**  - It describes the beauty and brightness of Jesus' clothing. (See: Simile)
