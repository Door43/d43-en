===== Important Terms: =====

  * Behold
  * Moses
  * Elijah

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold there appeared to them**  - "them" refers to the disciples, Peter, James and John.
  * **Lord, it is good for us**  - "us" refers to Peter, James and John/
  * **I will make** - "I" refers to Peter.
