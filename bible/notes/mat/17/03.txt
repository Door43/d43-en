===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold **- this word alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows. It could be translated as "suddenly."
  * **there appeared to them**  - "them" refers to the disciples who were with Jesus; Peter, James, and John.
  * **answered**  - Peter is not responding to any question that was asked. It could be translated as "In his excitement Peter said."
  * **Lord, it is good for us**  - "us" could refer to the disciples, or to everyone there.
  * **three shelters**  - huts or booths; probably intended as some worship or memorial tents for the occasion.
