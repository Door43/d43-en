===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they**  - Peter, James, John, and Jesus
  * **epileptic **- a sick boy who becomes unconscious and shakes. From Jesus' actions we know that he was also demon possessed.
  * **I brought him **- "I" refers to the man and "him" refers to the man's son.
  * **your disciples **- "your" refers to Jesus.
  * **but they could not cure him**  - "they" refers to the disciples of Jesus and "him" refers to the man's son.
