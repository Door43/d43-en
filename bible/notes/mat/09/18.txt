===== Translation Notes: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Jesus was answering the question about fasting.</font>

  * **these things to them **- This refers to the answer Jesus gave John's disciples about fasting.
  * **Jewish official **- "Jewish leader"
  * **bowed down to him **- This is a way someone would show respect in Jewish culture.
  * **come and lay your hand on her, and she will live**  - This is a way of saying that the Jewish official believed Jesus had the power to bring her back to life.
  * **his disciples**  - This refers to Jesus' disciples.
