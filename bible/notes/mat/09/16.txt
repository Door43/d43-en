===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to answer the question asked by the disciples of John.

  * **People do not sew a patch of unshrunken cloth on an old garment **- This is a metaphor that means that people who know only the old traditions are not eager to accept new ones. (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])
  * **garment **- "clothing"
  * **the patch **- "the piece of new cloth"
