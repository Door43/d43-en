===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus had compassion on the worried and confused crowd.

  * **pray to the Lord of the harvest**  - That is, pray to the Lord. He is in charge of the harvest. This harvest refers to people who come to faith in God by believing the good news of Jesus. (See: Keyword Harvest)
  * **send forth laborers**  - This "laborers" refers to God's workers.
  * **for his harvest **- The harvest is the people who believe and are added to his kingdom. Jesus used farming terms because the people knew about farming. (See: Metaphor)
