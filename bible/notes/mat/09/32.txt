===== Translation Notes: =====

The two blind men shared the news that that their eyes were opened.

  * **mute man**  - a person who is not able to talk
  * **the crowd marveled at it**  - The people were full of wonder because it was a miracle.
  * **he throws out**  - The "he" refers to Jesus.
