===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus responds to Peter's statement that Jesus is the Son of God.

  * **keys of the kingdom of heaven** - the ability to open the way for people to become the people of God the way a servant would welcome guests into a house (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **bind on earth...loosed in heaven** - to declare people forgiven or condemned as it is also done in heaven (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
