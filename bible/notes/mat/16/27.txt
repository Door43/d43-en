===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I say to you**  - Jesus is speaking to the disciples.
  * **some of them who are standing here**  - Here Jesus was referring to some of the people he was speaking to. This could be translated as "some of you standing here now."
  * **will not taste death**  - "will not experience death" or" will not die"
