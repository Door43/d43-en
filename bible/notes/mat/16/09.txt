===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues to warn his disciples about the Pharisees and Sadducees.

  * **Do you not yet perceive or remember...you gathered up?** - Jesus uses a question to rebuke the disciples. AT: "Surely you remember...you gathered up!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **five thousand...four thousand** - "5,000...4,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Or the seven loaves...you took up?** - "Do you also not remember the seven loaves...you took up?" Jesus uses a question to rebuke his disciples. AT: "Surely you also remember the seven loaves...you took up!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
