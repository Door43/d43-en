===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This begins an encounter between Jesus and the Pharisees and Sadducees.

  * **tested him** - Here "tested" is used in a negative sense. AT: "challenged him" or "wanted to trap him."
  * **When it is evening** - "When the sun is setting"
  * **fair weather** - This means clear, calm, and pleasant weather.
  * **for the sky is red** - As the sun is setting, Jews knew that if the color of the sky changes to red, it is a sign the next day will be clear and calm.
