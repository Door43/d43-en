===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus arrived at a new place where the Pharisees and Sadducees began to test him.

  * **When it is evening **- This is the time of day when the sun is setting.
  * **tested him**  - The pronoun "him" refers to Jesus.
  * **them**  and **you **- Both the pronouns, "them" and "you", refer to the Pharisees and Sadducees.
  * **fair weather**  - clear and calm, pleasant
  * **heaven is red**  - the sky is bright and clear with a red sunset.
