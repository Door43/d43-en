===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **May his blood be on us and our children!** - "Yes! We and our descendants will gladly be responsible for executing him!" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
