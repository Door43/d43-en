===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **whom they forced to go with them so that he might carry his cross**  - Could be translated: "whom the soldiers forced to go with them so that he might carry Jesus's cross."
  * **they gave him wine to drink**  - could be translated "They gave Jesus wine to drink"
