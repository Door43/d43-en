===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins the account of when Jesus was crucified.

  * **As they came out**  - "As they came out of Jerusalem" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **whom they forced to go with them so that he might carry his cross**  - "whom the soldiers forced to go with them so that he could carry Jesus' cross"
  * **place called Golgotha**  - "place that people called Golgotha"
  * **gall**  - the bitter yellow liquid that bodies use in digestion
