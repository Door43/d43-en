===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the events after the burial of Jesus.

  * **a guard**  - a group of Roman soldiers that had between 4 and 16 members
  * **sealing the stone**  - They placed the stone so that it could not be moved and put on it the seal of the Roman Caesar that must not be broken.
  * **placing the guard**  - soldiers stood in front of the stone at the tomb
