===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the crucifixion and death of Jesus.

  * **one of them**  - Possible meanings: 1) one of the soldiers or 2) one of those who stood by and watched
  * **sponge** - sea animal harvested and used to take up and hold liquids that can later be pushed out
  * **gave it to him**  - "gave it to Jesus"
