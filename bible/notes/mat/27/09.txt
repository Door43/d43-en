===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The author quotes Old Testament scripture to show that Judas' suicide was a fulfillment of prophecy.

  * **Then that which had been spoken by Jeremiah the prophet was fulfilled** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "This fulfilled what the prophet Jeremiah spoke" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the price set on him by the people of Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the price the people of Israel set on him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the people of Israel** - This refers to those among the people of Israel who paid to kill Jesus. AT: "some of the people of Israel" or "the leaders of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **directed me** - Here "me" refers to Jeremiah.
