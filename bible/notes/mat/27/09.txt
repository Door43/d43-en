===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Judas killed himself.

  * **Then that which had been spoken by Jeremiah the prophet was fulfilled, saying** - "The prophet Jeremiah spoke this prophecy, and it had come true; he had said" (See Active/Passive)
  * **people of Israel **  - the Israelite religious leaders (See: Metonymy)
  * **directed me**  - directed "Jeremiah the prophet" (27:9)
