===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then when Judas - **  This indicates that the story is moving forward and that the scene has changed.
  * **thirty pieces of silver**  - This was the price that the chief priests and elders paid Judas to betray Jesus.
  * **innocent blood**  - Jesus didn't deserve to die
