===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **washed his hands **- refers to a custom: symbolic hand washing meant Pilate was not responsible for this judgment.
  * **innocent of the blood**  - may be translated "I am not responsible for this innocent man's death"
