===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the crucifixion and death of Jesus.

  * **cried**  - "called out" or "shouted"
  * **Eli, Eli, lama sabachthani** - Translators usually leave these words in their native Hebrew. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
