===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.

  * **Now**  - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline so the writer can give information to help the reader understand what happens beginning in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:27:17|27:17]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **the feast**  - the feast during which the Passover ([[:en:bible:notes:mat:26:01|26:2]]) was celebrated
  * **prisoner chosen by the crowd** - AT: "prisoner whom the crowd had chosen" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **notorious**  - well known for doing something bad
