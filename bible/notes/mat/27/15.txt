===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline so the writer can give information to help the reader understand what happens beginning in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:27:17|27:17]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **the feast** - This is the feast for the Passover celebration.
  * **prisoner chosen by the crowd** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "prisoner whom the crowd would choose" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they had a notorious prisoner** - "there was a notorious prisoner"
  * **notorious** - well known for doing something bad
