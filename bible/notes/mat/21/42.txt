===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them**  - Or, "Jesus said to the chief priests and elders"
  * **this was from the Lord**  - Or, "This wisdom is from the Lord"
  * **did you never read in the scriptures- **  a rhetorical question: Jesus wanted them to think about the scripture he was about to talk about (see rhetorical question)
  * **the stone - **  the word stone that was rejected by the builders and the word stone that was made the head of the corner is a metaphor for Jesus (see: metaphor)
  * **the head of the corner -**  or "cornerstone". See Key Terms for cornerstone.
