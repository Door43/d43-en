===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his parables**  - "Jesus' parables"
  * **they saw that he was speaking about them.**  - the chief priests and Pharisees understood that Jesus was speaking about them.
  * **they tried to lay hands on him,**  - the chief priests and Pharisees tried to arrest Jesus so they could kill him
