===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to travel to Jerusalem with his disciples.

  * **Bethphage** - a place in ancient Israel, which is about 2 km from the modern city of al-Eizariya.
  * **donkey** - a four-footed animal between about 36 and 50 inches (90 to 130 centimeters) tall; a miniature horse mostly used as a carrier of loads.
  * **a colt with her** - "a young male donkey with the mother"
  * **Untie them** - "remove the ropes of the donkey and its colt"
  * **The Lord** - Jesus is referring to himself. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **has needs of them** - "needs the donkey and the colt"
