===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **say I to you**  - Jesus was speaking to the chief priests and elders
  * **the kingdom of God will be taken away from you and be given to a nation that will bring forth its fruits- **  This is an idiom which means God will no longer let the Jewish people have God's favor but He will give it to a non-Jewish people who will accomplish God's purpose. (See: Idiom)
  * **the stone- **  the stone is a metaphor for Christ being solid as rock. (See: Metaphor)
