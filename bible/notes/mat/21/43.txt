===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to explain the parable.

  * **I say to you**  - Jesus was speaking to the chief priests and elders.
  * **cultivates its fruits**  - "will do what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **its fruit**  - "the kingdom of God's fruits"
  * **whoever falls on this stone**  - "the one who stumbles over this stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **on whomever it falls**  - "on whomever judgment falls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
