===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But what do you think **- This is a rhetorical question. Jesus does not expect them to tell him, and he does not need them to tell him. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **A man had two sons **- Jesus is now introducing a parable about two sons and a father which he wants them to think about.
