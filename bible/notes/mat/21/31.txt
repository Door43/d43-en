===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They said **- "The chief priests and elders said"
  * **Jesus said to them**  - the pronoun "them" refers to the chief priests and elders
  * **John came to you**  - John came and preached to the religious leaders and the common people.
  * **in the path of righteousness **  - John showed how people should respond to God and live.
  * **And you, when you saw that happen**  - "you" refers to the Jewish religious leaders who watched John
