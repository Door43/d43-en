===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **their garments **- "their" refers to the two disciples who brought the animals.
  * **garments on them**  - probably some outer garments, or long coats on the donkey and the colt.
  * **and Jesus sat there **- This refers to Jesus who sat on the clothing that was put on the donkey. Or, Jesus sat on the clothing that was put on the donkey
  * **spread their garments on the road**  - in honor of the rider
