===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hosanna**  - a Hebrew word that means "save us," but came to be an exclamation of praise to God.
  * **Son of David**  - an heir to the great king David, usually referring to the Messiah or Anointed One of God.
  * **Blessed is he**  - The pronoun refers to Jesus.
  * **comes in the name of the Lord**  - "comes as God's representative and with his authority" (See: Metonymy)
  * **all the city was stirred **- This may refer to people and leaders who did not yet believe in Jesus. That is why they asked the question.
  * **all the city was stirred**  - "When Jesus entered Jerusalem, turmoil came over the whole city. People asked, "Who is this?" (See: Active/passive)
  * **all the city**  - "all the people of the city" (See: Metonymy)
  * **The crowds answered **- "The crowd that was already following him" (UDB)
