======  Matthew 08 General Notes ======


=== Formatting in this chapter ===

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Miracles ==

Jesus miracles in this chapter appear to emphasize his [[:en:obe:kt:authority|authority]] over things that are beyond human control. They also show that the proper response to a miracle was to worship Jesus.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

There are many different figures of speech in this chapter. It was also common for Jesus to use analogies and metaphors in his teaching. Jesus' way of teaching was intended produce faith and to be applicable to many different situations.
