===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Here the scene shifts to a different time and place and tells of Jesus healing another person.

  * **came to him and asked him** - Here "him" refers to Jesus.
  * **paralyzed** - One who is unable to move because of disease or stroke.
  * **Jesus said to him** - "Jesus said to the centurion"
  * **I will come and heal him** - "I will come to your house and make your servant well"
