===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus healing several people.

  * **him ... him** - Jesus
  * **paralyzed**  - "unable to move" because of disease
  * **Jesus said to him, "I will come and heal him."**  - "Jesus said to the centurion, "I will come to your house and make your servant well."
