===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **were demon-possessed - **  (passive voice) Could be translated "Demons possessed the man." (See: Passive/Active)
  * **he drove out the spirits with a word**  - Jesus spoke one word and the demons (evil spirits) had to leave the man because Jesus has authority over the demons.
  * **In this way was fulfilled**  - This could be stated in the active voice:** "**Jesus fulfilled the prophecy that God told the prophet Isaiah to speak to Israel.
  * **had been spoken **- (passive) Can be written in active voice: "(what) Isaiah had said" (See: Passive/Active)
  * **He himself**  - Jesus
  * **took our sickness and bore our diseases **- (Doublet) "freed people from being sick and made them well" (See: Doublet) This is from Isaiah 53:4.
