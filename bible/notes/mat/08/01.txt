===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus has finished his teaching to the crowds on the hillside.

  * **if you are willing **  - this could be translated "if you want to" or "if you desire". The leper believes that Jesus has the power to heal him if he wants to.
  * **you can make me clean**  - <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>this could be translated</font>    "you can heal me"
  * **immediately**  - "right away"
  * **he was cleansed**  - (passive phrase) This means that Jesus cleansed (or "healed") the leper. (See: Passive/Active)
