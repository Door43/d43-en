===== Translation Notes: =====

This concludes the account of Jesus calming a storm.

  * **them **  - The pronoun "them" refers to the disciples.
  * you - The pronoun "you" refers to the disciples as Jesus is speaking directly to them.
  * **Why are you afraid**  - This is a rhetorical question to express his disbelief. Jesus is not expecting any answer. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **you of little faith**  - "you" is plural, inclusive of all the disciples. Jesus is saying the disciples do not believe that he has the power to keep them safe in the storm.
  * **rebuked the winds and the sea**  - "commanded" or "demanded" the winds and the sea to be still or calm.
  * **What sort of man is this, that even the winds and the sea obey him? **- This is a rhetorical question to show the surprise of the disciples. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **the winds and the sea obey him**  - This is a personification. The natural elements are described as characters that respond like people. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]])
