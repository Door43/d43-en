===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are you afraid?**  -  Jesus is not asking for information. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **you of little faith**  - "You" is plural, inclusive of all the disciples. Jesus is saying the disciples do not believe that he has the power to keep them safe in the storm.
  * **rebuked**  - This word usually means "to criticize," but in this case, Jesus is commanding the waves and the sea to be still or calm.
  * **the winds and the sea obey him**  -  Here the natural elements are described as characters that respond like people. (See: Personification)
  * **What sort of man is this, that even the winds and the sea obey him? **- <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The disciples were showing surprise. (See: Rhetorical Question)</font>
