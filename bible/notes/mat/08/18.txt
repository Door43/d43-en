===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The foxes have holes and the birds have nests**  -  "no place to live" (See: Doublet)
  * **no place to lay my head **- "no place to sleep". (See: Idiom)
  * **he gave instructions**  - "he told them what to do"
  * **wherever **- "to any place"
