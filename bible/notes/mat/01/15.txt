===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The author concludes Jesus' genealogy, which began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:01|1:1]].

  * **Mary, by whom Jesus was born** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Mary, who gave birth to Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **who is called Christ** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom people call Christ" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **fourteen** - "14" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **deportation to Babylon** - Use the same wording you used in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:09|1:11]].
