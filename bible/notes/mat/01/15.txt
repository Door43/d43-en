===== Translation Notes: =====

The list of Jesus' ancestors continues. Use the same wording that you used in Matthew [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:01|1:2-3]].

  * **Mary, by whom Jesus was born **- This could be translated in the active voice as "Mary, who gave birth to Jesus." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **deportation to Babylon**  - Use the same wording you used in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:09|1:11]].
