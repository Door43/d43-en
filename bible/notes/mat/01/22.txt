===== Translation Notes: =====

The virgin Mary conceived Jesus by the Holy Spirit to fulfill the prophecy concerning Jesus birth.

  * **All this happened to fulfill -**  "All this happened as a result of" (UDB)
  * **what was spoken by the Lord through the prophet**  - This could be stated in the active voice as: "what the Lord told the prophet Isaiah to write long ago" (See: Passive/Active)
  * **the virgin will conceive and give birth to the son **- This is from Isaiah 7:14
