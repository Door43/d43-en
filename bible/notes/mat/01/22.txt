===== Translation Notes: =====

Matthew quotes a prophecy that the birth of Jesus would fulfill.

  * **what was spoken by the Lord through the prophet**  - This could be stated in the active voice as "what the Lord told the prophet Isaiah to write long ago." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
  * **the virgin will conceive and give birth to a son**  - This verse is a direct quotation from [[en:bible:notes:isa:07:13]].
