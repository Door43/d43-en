===== Translation Notes: =====

The list of Jesus' ancestors continues. Use the same wording that you used in Matthew [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:01|1:2-3]].

  * **after the deportation**  - Use the same wording you used in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:09|1:11]].
  * **Shealtiel the father of Zerubbabel**  - Shealtiel was actually Zerubbabel's grandfather (see UDB).
