===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to explain to his disciples the meaning of his parable in verse [[:en:bible:notes:mat:15:10|11]].

  * **to us** - "to us disciples"
  * **passes** - "goes"
  * **latrine** - a polite term for the place where people buried body waste
