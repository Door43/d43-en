===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus healing the daughter of a Canaanite woman.

  * **she came** - "the Canaanite woman came"
  * **the children's bread...the little dogs** - "what properly belongs to the Jews...the Gentiles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
