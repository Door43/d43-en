===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lest **- "for fear that" This could be translated as "so that they do not faint."
  * **they faint**  - fall down because of their weakness
  * **deserted place**  - a place that is a long way from any village where they could buy food
  * **to satisfy so large a crowd**  - verse 38 says there were 4,000 men, plus women and children
