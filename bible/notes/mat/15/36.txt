===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He**  - "Jesus"
  * **them**  - the loaves of bread and the fish
  * **they **- "the disciples"
  * **Those who **- "The crowd of people"
  * **the region**  - "a part of the country"
  * **Magadan **- sometimes referred to as Magdala
