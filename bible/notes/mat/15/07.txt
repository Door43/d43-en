===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You **- The word "You" refers to the Pharisees and scribes.
  * **well did Isaiah prophesy**  - This could be tranlated as "Isaiah told the truth in this prophecy."
  * **he said**  - This could be translated as "when he told what God said."
  * **This people honors me with their lips**  - This could be translated as "this people say nice things about me."
  * **but their heart is far from me**  - This could be translated as "but they do not honor me in their hearts" or "but they do not love me."
  * **far from me**  - an idiom for how much they have separated themselves from God. (See: Idiom)
  * **They worship me in vain**  - This could be translated as "Their worship has no effect on me" or "They only pretend to worship me."
  * **the commands of people**  - "the rules that people make up."
