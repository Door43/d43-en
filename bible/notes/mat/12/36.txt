===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues an account of the Pharisees' saying that Jesus healed a man by the power of Satan.

  * **you **- Pharisees
  * **day of judgment**  - This is the day each of us will stand before God to be judged.
  * **give an account**  - "to give answer for" or "be judged by."
  * **idle - **"lazy," "wasteful," or "useless"
  * **they**  - "people"
  * **by your words you will be justified**  - "God will justify you by your words" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **by your words you will be condemned **- "God will condemn you by your words." (See: Active/Passive)
