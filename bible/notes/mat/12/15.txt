===== Translation Notes: =====

This account explains how the actions of Jesus fulfilled one of the prophecies of Isaiah.

  * **this** - "the pharisees' plot to kill him".
  * **he withdrew ** - "he left".
  * **followed him** - went after Jesus
  * **them** - The pronoun "them" refers to the people Jesus healed
  * **not to make him known ** - "not to tell anyone about him"
  * **him ** - The pronoun "him" refers to Jesus.
