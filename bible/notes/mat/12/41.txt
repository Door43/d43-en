===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The men of Nineveh will**** stand up in judgment - **  Jesus continues his metaphor of the prophet Jonah, whose preaching of coming judgment to Nineveh brought repentance..
  * **this generation**  - This refers to the people who are living during the time of Jesus' preaching.
  * **someone greater**  - This refers to the importance of the person who is greater than Jonah. When Jesus said this, he was talking about himself.
