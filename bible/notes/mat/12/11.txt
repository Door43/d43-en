===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to respond to the Pharisees who criticized him for healing a man on the Sabbath.

  * **What man would there be**â€¦ This begins a long question. (See: [[en:ta:translate:RhetoricalQuestion]])
  * **them** - The pronoun "them" refers to the Pharisees
  * **you ** - The pronoun "you" Refers to the Pharisees
  * **he ** - The pronoun "he" Refers to the man in Jesus' example here.
  * **grasp hold of it and lift it out ** - This refers to pulling the sheep out of the hole.
  * **lawful to do good on the Sabbath** - something may be "unlawful" but there are times when doing good is more important and therefore "lawful."
