===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus is continuing to speak to the people.

  * **you -**  Pharisees
  * **sin and blasphemy -**  "deep disrespect for God
  * **every sin and blasphemy will be forgiven**  - This can be written in active voice: "God will forgive every sin and blasphemy." (See-Passive)
  * **but**  - "except"
  * ** will be forgiven -**  This refers to "any word spoken against the Son of Man."
  * ** will not be forgiven - ** This refers to "any word spoken against the Holy Spirit". Jesus was saying that they could slander him as a man, but not the Holy Spirit of God.
  * **that which -**  "the world to come". This refers to the new heavens and new earth which Christ will create in the future.
