===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues an account of the Pharisees saying that Jesus healed a man by the power of Satan.

  * **for a tree is known by its fruit ** - "people know a tree by its fruit." (See: [[en:ta:translate:Active]])
  * **a tree is known by its fruit** - Jesus is likening the Pharisees to bad trees with bad fruit. (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])
  * **You ** - The pronoun "you" refers to the Pharisees.
  * **offspring of vipers** - Jesus compares the Pharisees to poisonous snakes. (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])
  * â€‹**how can you say good things?** - This is a rhetorical question to show that he is not expecting any good from them. (See: [[en:ta:translate:RhetoricalQuestion]])
  * **"For out of the content of the heart the mouth speaks"**  - This means that what a man does or says, reflects what he believes, thinks, and feels, or the kind of person he truly is.
  * **good treasure **- "righteous thoughts" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **evil treasure** - "evil thoughts" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
