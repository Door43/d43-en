===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins an account of the Pharisees saying that it was by the power of Satan that Jesus healed a man.

  * **someone blind and mute ** - "someone who could not see and could not talk".
  * **All the crowds were amazed** - "All the people who had seen Jesus heal the man were greatly surprised"
