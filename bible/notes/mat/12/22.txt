===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins an account of the Pharisees saying that Jesus healed a man by the power of Satan.

  * **someone blind and mute ** - "someone who cannot see and cannot talk".
  * **Then someone blind and mute, possessed by a demon, was brought to Jesus** - "Then someone brought a blind and mute person, possessed by a demon, to Jesus." (See: [[en:ta:translate:Active]])
  * **All the crowds were amazed** - "This healing caused the crowds to be amazed." (See: [[en:ta:translate:Active]]).
  * amazed - "greatly surprised"
  * son of David - Jesus' ancestor was the Jewish King David.
