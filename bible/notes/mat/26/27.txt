===== Important Terms: =====

  * blood
  * covenant (Note: the KT //new covenant//  is closest in meaning here)
  * forgiveness
  * sins
  * fruit of the vine (wine)
  * Father
  * kingdom

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gave it to them **- The pronoun "them" refers to the twelve disciples.
  * **blood of the covenant **- This refers to a new covenant being put into effect when Jesus' blood was shed.
  * **is poured out **- This is an expression that means “poured out in death” or “will soon flow out of my body" (UDB) or "will flow out of my wounds when I die.”
  * **fruit of the vine**
