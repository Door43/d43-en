===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **took a cup**  - a cup with contents they were to drink (See: Metonymy)
  * **gave it to them **- The pronoun "them" refers to the disciples.
  * **this is my blood**  - probably a metaphor, not literally his blood (See: Metaphor)
  * **blood of the covenant **- This refers to a new covenant being put into effect when Jesus' blood was shed on the cross.
  * **is poured out **- This is an expression that means “poured out in death” or “will soon flow out of my body" (UDB) or "will flow out of my wounds when I die.”
  * **fruit of the vine **- "wine" (or "fermented fruit juice" or "fruit juice" if wine is not known)
