===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **chief priests **- See explanation of priests under the Important Term "priest."
  * **palace **- A large, expensive house or group of buildings, in this case the home of the high priest. The chief priests and elders were in the courtyard (open area) of the palace.
  * **stealthily **- "by deceit" or "secretly"
  * **a riot does not arise among the people **- This passive phrase can be translated actively if necessary as in the UDB. (See: Active or Passive)
