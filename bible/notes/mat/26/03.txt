===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - These verses give background information about the Jewish leaders' plot to arrest and kill Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **were gathered together** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "came together" or "met together" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Jesus stealthily** - "Jesus secretly"
  * **Not during the feast** - What the leaders did not want to do during the feast can be made more clear. AT: "We should not kill Jesus during the feast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the feast** - This is the yearly Passover feast.
