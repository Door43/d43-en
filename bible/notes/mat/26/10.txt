===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why are you troubling this woman?** - Jesus asks this question as a rebuke of his disciples. AT: "You should not be troubling this woman!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Why are you** - All occurrences of "you" are plural and refer to the disciples. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **the poor** - This can be stated as an adjective. AT: "poor people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
