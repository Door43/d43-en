===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus praises the woman who anointed him before his death.

  * **Why are you troubling this woman?** - AT: "You should not be troubling this woman!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **You ... you ... you** - the disciples
