===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus praying in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **He went away** - "Jesus went away"
  * **unless I drink it** - "unless I drink this cup of suffering"
  * **their eyes were heavy** - "they were very sleepy" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
