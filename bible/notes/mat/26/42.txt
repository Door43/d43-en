===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He went away** - "Jesus went away"
  * **a second time ... third time** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **My Father** - This is an important title for God that describes the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **if this cannot pass away unless I drink it** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "if I must drink it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **if this** - Here "this" refers to the cup and the contents within it. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:mat:26:39|26:39]])
  * **unless I drink it** - "unless I drink from it" or "unless I drink from this cup of suffering"
  * **your will be done** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "may what you want happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **their eyes were heavy** - This is an idiom. AT: "they were very sleepy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
