===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples as they walk to the Mount of Olives.

  * **fall away **- Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:26:30|26:31]].
  * **before the rooster crows** - AT: "before the sun comes up"
  * **rooster** - a bird that calls out loudly around the time the sun comes up
  * **crows** - the sound a rooster makes
