===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Peter said to him**  - "Peter said to Jesus"
  * **fall away **- “abandon you” or “run away” or “give up my faith in you”
  * **Truly I say to you **- This can be translated “I tell you the truth.”
  * **cock **- "rooster"
  * **all the disciples**  - "all the other disciples"
