===== Important Terms: =====

  * disciples
  * Son of Man
  * betrayed, betrays
  * sinners

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Are you still sleeping and taking your rest? **- This is a rhetorical question that could be written as a statement as in the UDB.
  * **the hour is at hand**  - This is a figure of speech that means "the time has come" and refers to a specific moment in time."
  * **is being betrayed into the hands of sinners **- This can be written as an active phrase as in the UDB.
  * **into the hands of sinners**  - "is being handed over to sinful people" (See: Synecdoche)
