===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus praying in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **the hour is at hand**  - "the time has come"
  * **the hands of sinners**  - "sinful people" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Look** - "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you."
