===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Peter answered him** - "Peter answered Jesus"
  * **when Peter saw the wind** - Here "saw the wind" means he became aware of the wind. AT: "when Peter saw that the wind was tossing the waves back and forth" or "when he realized how strong the wind was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
