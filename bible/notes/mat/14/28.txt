===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus miraculously walking on the surface of the Sea of Galilee.

  * **Peter answered him **- "Peter answered Jesus"
  * **command me to come to you upon the water **- "tell me to walk on the water to you" (UDB)
  * **climbed down from the boat**  - **"**got out of the boat" (UDB)
  * **he became afraid**  - "he was afraid"
  * **began to sink**  - This means to fall below the water.
