===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Herod killed John the Baptist.

  * **Herod's birthday came **- "a birthday celebration for Herod took place" or "the birthday of Herod was celebrated"
  * **in the midst **- The implied information is that the daughter of Herodias danced in the midst of the guests who were there to participate in the birthday celebration. (See: Implicit and Explicit Information)
  * **pleased Herod **- "Herod found pleasure in her dance"
  * **In response **- Herod responds favorably to the dancing of the daughter.
