===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins an account of how Herod killed John the Baptist while Jesus was ministering in Galilee.

  * **About that time **- "In those days" or “While Jesus was ministering in Galilee.”
  * **Herod the tetrarch**  - This refers to Herod Antipas, who was the ruler of one-fourth of Israel.
  * **heard the news about Jesus **- "heard reports about Jesus" or "heard about the fame of Jesus"
  * **He said **- "Herod said"
  * **This is John the ****Baptist**  - "Jesus is John the Baptist"
  * **Therefore these ****powers are at work in him **- This can be translated with an active verb as in the UDB. (See: Active or Passive)
