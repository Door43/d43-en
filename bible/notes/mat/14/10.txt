===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Herod killed John the Baptist.

  * **He sent and beheaded John in the prison **- "He" refers to Herod. Most likely, Herod did not do this personally but ordered someone to do this for him (see UDB). Your translation may have a need to reflect this.
  * **beheaded John **- "killed John by cutting off his head"
  * **his head was brought on a platter and given to the girl **- This may be translated with an active verb if necessary: "someone brought his head on a platter and gave it to the girl." (See: Active or Passive)
  * **platter**  - This is a large plate.
  * **girl **- This refers to the daughter of Herodias. The Greek word means "little girl" or "young, unmarried girl."
  * **his disciples**  - "the disciples of John"
  * **came **- "went to the prison" (UDB)
  * **took up the corpse**  - "took up the dead body" or "took up his dead body"
  * **buried it**  - "buried him"
  * **they went **- "the disciples of John left"
  * **told Jesus **- The implied information is that they told Jesus what had happened to John the Baptist. (See: Implicit and Explicit Information)
