===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus miraculously walking on the surface of the Sea of Galilee.

  * **"O you of little faith" **- "You who have little trust in God"
  * **why did you doubt**  - This is a rhetorical question. Jesus wanted Peter to think about why he had not trusted Jesus to keep him from sinking. (See: Rhetorical)
