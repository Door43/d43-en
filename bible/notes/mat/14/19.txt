===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Jesus fed the crowds who followed him to the isolated place.

  * **loaves **- These are specific amounts of baked bread. This could be translated as “whole breads” or "loaves of bread."
  * **and looking**  - “while looking” or “after looking”
  * **blessed**  - "thanked God for them" (UDB)
  * **up to heaven**  - This refers to looking up, toward the sky. The Jews believed that heaven was located above the sky.
  * **They all ate**  - "The people all ate"
  * **and were filled**  - "and were satisfied" or "until they were no longer hungry" (UDB)
  * **Then they took up what remained **- "Then the disciples took up what remained."
  * **they took up**  - "they gathered up"
  * **Those who ate**  - The implied information is "Those who ate the bread and the fish." (See: Implicit and Explicit Information)
