===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus miraculously walking on the surface of the Sea of Galilee.

  * **the forth watch**  - This means between 3:00 and 6:00 in the morning. (UDB)
  * **he was walking on the sea**  - "Jesus was walking on top of the water"
  * **they were terrified**  - "the disciples were very afraid"
  * **It is a ghost**  - "It is a spirit"
