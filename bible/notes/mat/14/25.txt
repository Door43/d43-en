===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the fourth watch** - The fourth watch is between 3 a.m. and sunrise. AT: "Just before dawn"
  * **walking on the sea** - "walking on top of the water"
  * **they were terrified** - "they were very afraid"
  * **ghost** - a spirit that has left the body of a person who has died
