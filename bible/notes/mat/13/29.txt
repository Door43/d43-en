===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell a large crowd of people different parables that describe what the kingdom of God is like. These verses conclude the parable of the weeds.

  * **The landowner said** - "The landowner said to his servants"
  * **I will say to the reapers, "First gather up the weeds and tie them in bundles to burn them, but gather the wheat into my barn"** - You can translate this as an indirect quote: "I will tell the reapers to first gather up the weeds and tie them in bundles to burn them, then gather the wheat into my barn." (See: [[en:ta:translate:quotation|]])
  * **First gather up the weeds** - "Pull up the weeds first"
  * **my barn** - A barn is a farm building that can be used for storing grain.
