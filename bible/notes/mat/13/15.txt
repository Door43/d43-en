===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hearts have become dull, and their ears are heavy of hearing, And their eyes they have closed**  - Represents that they are not willing to understand and act and see. (See parallelism.)
  * **And I should heal them**  - This quote from Isaiah is referring to God healing the unbelief of the nation of Israel, Jesus is referring to the crowd's unbelief.
