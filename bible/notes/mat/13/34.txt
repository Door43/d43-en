===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell a large crowd of people different parables that describe what the kingdom of God is like.

  * **All these things Jesus said to the crowds in parables; and without parables he said nothing to them** - The order "said...parables...parables...said" is meant to emphasize that he spoke to them in parables.
  * **All these things** - This refers to what Jesus taught beginning at [[:en:bible:notes:mat:13:01|13:1]].
  * **without parables he said nothing to them** - "he taught them nothing except by parables." Alternate translation: "everything he said to them he said in parables." (See: [[en:ta:translate:hyperbole|]] and [[en:ta:translate:litotes|]])
  * **what had been said through the prophet when he said **- This can be translated with an active verb: "what God told one of the prophets to write long ago" (UDB). (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **when he said** - "when the prophet said" 
  * **things that were hidden** - This can be translated with an active verb: "things that God has kept hidden".(See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **from the foundation of the world** - "since the beginning of the world" or "since God created the world."
