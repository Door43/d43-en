===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All these things I have obeyed **- The young man claims to have kept all of these commandments.
  * **what do I still need**  - "is there something else I need to do"
  * **wish**  - This could be translated as "want."
  * **to be perfect**  - This could mean "to be pleasing to God."
  * **go, sell what you have, and give it to the poor**  - Jesus gives him a command to do something that would please God and make him more like God â€“ to give up his possessions to those in need.
  * **treasure in heaven **- This refers to eternal rewards for any person who believes and obeys God.
  * **sorrowful**  - This means that the man was sad.
  * **great possessions**  - much wealth
