===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they were very astonished**  - " the disciples were amazed at what Jesus said"
  * **be saved**  - “to be saved from sin and live forever with God”
  * **With people this is impossible **- Jesus stresses that people cannot possibly save themselves, but only God can save them.
  * **possible with God**  - "possible for God to do"
  * **we have left everything **  - This refers to “all our wealth” or “all our possessions.”
