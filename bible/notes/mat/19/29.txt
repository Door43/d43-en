===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who has left houses... **- This refers to anyone who has given up everything in this life to serve, honor and obey God.
  * **will receive a hundredfold**  - He will be rewarded by God and live forever with Him in heaven.
  * **many who are first now**  - Many who are first in the world's eyes, such as those who are rich and those who rule others, will one day be last in the kingdom of God.
