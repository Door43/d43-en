===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who has left houses...**
 - sacrifices made in this life to serve, honor and obey God's instructions are rewarded at the end of things, when we live forever with Him.
  * **many who are first now...** - wealth, power and authority in this life won't buy, command or require eternal wealth, power and authority in heaven.
