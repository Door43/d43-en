  * **It is easier for a camel to go through a needles eye -  ** It is impossible for a camel to fit throught the eye of a needle. Therefore, it appears that Jesus may have ben using a hyperbole to say "It is extremely difficult for a rich man to be saved." (See Hyperbole)

  * **needles eye - ** The needle's eye is the hole in a sewing needle through which the thread is passed.
