===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pharisees came to him **- "Pharisees came to Jesus"
  * **Have you not read **- Jesus asked this to scold the Pharisees. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **the beginning**  - This refers to the creation of the first man and woman described in the Old Testament.
