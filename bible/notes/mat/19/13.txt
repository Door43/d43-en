===== Translation Notes: =====

  *
  * **lay his hands on them and pray - **  It was the tradition in Jewish culture to receive a special gift from God when a godly man touched them and prayed for them.
  * **for to such as these -**  This means to those who are like little children, with open and believing hearts
  * **rebuked**  - The disciples expressed strong disapproval of the parents who were bringing their babies to Jesus.
  * **little children**  - This is a different word than “infants” which was used for the babies that the people were bringing. “Little children” were somewhat older than “infants.”
  * **Welcome the little children**  - The picture here is of a person opening their arms and a child running into the arms of the person.
  * **do not forbid them**  - This could be translated “do not hinder them” or “do not prevent the little children” from coming to Jesus.
  * **S****uch as these belongs**  - This could also be translated “belongs to people who are like these little children.”
