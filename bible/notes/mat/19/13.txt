===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **some little children were brought to him**  - This can be translated with an active clause “some people brought little children to Jesus.” (See: Active or Passive)
  * **he would lay his hands on them and pray**  - This can be translated as “he would put his hands on them and pray” or “he would touch the children and pray for them.”
  * **rebuked them**  - "scolded the people who were bringing the children to Jesus"
  * **Permit the little children**  - "Let the little children come to me" (UDB)
  * **do not forbid them to come to me**  - This can be translated as “do not stop them from coming to me” or “do not tell them that they must not come to me.”
  * **for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such ones **- This can be translated as “the kingdom of heaven belongs to people who are like these” or “only people who are like little children can go into the kingdom of heaven.”
