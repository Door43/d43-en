===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus begins to teach about riches in the world and rewards in heaven.

  * **Behold** - The writer is bringing a new person into the story. Your language may have a way of doing this.
  * **good thing** - thing that pleases God
  * **Only one is good** - "God alone is completely good"
