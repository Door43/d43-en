===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold **- This means to look or see.
  * **good****thing**  - "something that pleases God"
  * **what good thing must I do**  - “what do I need to do” or “what is required of me”
  * **that I may have eternal life**  - This could be translated as "that I may live forever with God."
  * **Only one is good**  - "God alone is completely good.”
