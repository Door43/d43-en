===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues what the authorities told the soldiers to do.

  * **the governor** - Pilate ([[:en:bible:notes:mat:27:01|27:2]])
  * **did as they had been instructed** - "did what the priests had told them to do" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **today** - the time Matthew wrote the book
