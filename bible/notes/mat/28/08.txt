===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The women** - "Mary Magdalene and the other woman named Mary"
  * **Behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows. Your language may have a way of doing this.
  * **Greetings** - This is an ordinary greeting, much like "Hello" in English.
  * **took hold of his feet** - "got down on their knees and held onto his feet"
  * **my brothers** - This refers to Jesus' disciples.
