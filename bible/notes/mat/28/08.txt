===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the resurrection of Jesus from the dead.

  * **The women **- "Mary Magdalene and the other woman named Mary"
  * **Behold** - The writer is telling the reader that something surprising is about to happen. Your language may have a way of doing this.
  * **took hold of his feet **- "got down on their knees and held on to  his feet"
  * **my brothers **- Jesus' disciples
