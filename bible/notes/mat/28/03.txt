===== translationNotes: =====

  * **His appearance** - "The angel's appearance"
  * **was like lightning** - This is a simile that emphasizes how bright in appearance the angel was. AT: "was bright like lightning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **his clothing as white as snow** - This is a simile that emphasizes how bright and white the angel's clothes were. AT: "his clothing was very white like snow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **became like dead men** - This is a simile that means the soldiers fell down and did not move. AT: "fell to the ground and lay there like dead men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
