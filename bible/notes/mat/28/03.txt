===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues theÂ account of the resurrection of Jesus from the dead.

  * **His appearance **- "The angel's appearance"
  * **was like lightning**  - "sudden" (See: Simile)
  * **white as snow**  - "extremely bright" (See: Simile)
  * **snow**  - white particles of frozen water that falls from the sky in cold weather when temperatures are zero degrees C or lower.
  * **like dead men**  - "paralyzed" or "unable to move" the way people appear to be when they are dead. (See similes)
