===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus meeting with his disciples after his resurrection.

  * **Teach them to obey**  - "Teach the disciples to obey"
  * **and see**  - "and behold"
  * **I am with you always**  - Jesus is speaking to his disciples. (See: Forms of you)
