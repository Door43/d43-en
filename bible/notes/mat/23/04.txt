===== Translation Notes: =====

JesusÂ continues to warn his followers not to be like the religious leaders.

  * **they bind heavy burdens that are difficult to carry **- This phrase compares heavy burdens to many rules imposed by the Pharisees. "They place on you many rules which collected together are difficult to follow." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they themselves will not move a finger** - "They will not give the least amount of help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for they make their phylacteries wide, and enlarge the borders of their garments ** - The religious leaders often made their phylacteries larger so they would appear to have even greater devotion to Yahweh.Likewise, they made the tassels on the hem of their cloths longer, to also draw attention to their devotion to Yahweh. 
  * **phylacteries** - These were small leather boxes containing portions of Scripture that they wore on their arms or foreheads to remind them to keep the law.
