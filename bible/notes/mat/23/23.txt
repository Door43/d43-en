===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Woe to you**  - This is a way of saying, "How terrible it will be for you." It lets the hearers know that God is angry with them. See how you translated this in __23:13__.
  * **mint, dill, and cumin **- These are different spices.
  * **You blind guides**  - These people are not physically blind. Jesus is comparing spiritual blindness to physical blindness. (See: Metaphor)
  * **you who strain out a gnat but swallow a camel**  - Jesus uses these examples from nature to show them their actions about obeying God's law. He was showing them they were unwilling to swallow a small insect (or insignificant rules of God) but were ignoring the very big animals (or rules of God) (See: Metaphor)
  * **gnat**  - a very small flying insect
  * **camel**  - a very large animal in desert regions
