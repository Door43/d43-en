===== Important Terms: =====

Jesus continues to rebuke religious leaders

  * woe
  * scribes
  * Pharisees
  * hypocrites
  * tombs
  * uncleanliness
  * righteous
  * iniquity

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like whitewashed tombs**  - Whitewashed refers to a thin coat of cheap paint. Jesus uses a simile of painted graves for the scribes and Pharisees. (See: Simile)
  * **outwardly appear righteous to men**  - Jesus means that they do outward actions to make people think they are good.
