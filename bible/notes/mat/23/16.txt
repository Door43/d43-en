===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus is talking to the Jewish religious leaders.
  * **blind guides**  - (See: Metaphor)
  * **Woe to you**  - This is a way of saying, "How terrible it will be for you." It lets the hearers know that God is angry with them.
  * **bound to his oath**  -This could be translated "must do what he promised to do."
  * **You blind fools****â€¦?**  - Jesus uses this question to point out something they already know. (See: Rhetorical Question)
