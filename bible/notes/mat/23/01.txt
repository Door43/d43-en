===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the beginning of a new part of the story that runs through [[:en:bible:notes:mat:25:44|25:46]], where Jesus teaches about salvation and the final judgment. Here he begins to warn the people about the scribes and Pharisees.

  * **sit in Moses' seat** - Here "seat" represents the authority to rule and make judgments. AT: "have authority as Moses had" or "have authority to say what the law of Moses means." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **whatever** - "anything" or "everything"
