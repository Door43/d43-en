===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You offspring **- "you" refers to Pharisees and Sadducees
  * **You offspring of poisonous snakes**
 - This is a metaphor that could be translated as "You people are evil like poisonous snakes".
  * **yourselves **- Pharisees and Sadducees
  * **we**  - Pharisees and Sadducees
  * **I **- Jesus
  * **children**  - descendants of Abraham
