===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the end of the part of the story about John the Baptist and describes what happened after he baptized Jesus.

  * **After he was baptized** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "After John baptized Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **the heavens were opened to him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Jesus saw the sky open" or "God opened the heavens to Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **coming down like a dove** - Possible meanings are 1) this is simply a statement that the Spirit was in the form of a dove or 2) this is a simile that compares the Spirit coming down upon Jesus gently, the way a dove would. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a voice came out of the heavens saying** - "Jesus heard a voice from heaven." Here "voice" refers to God speaking. AT: "God spoke from heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Son** - This is an important title for Jesus, the Son of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
