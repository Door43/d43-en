===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues his parable about a landowner paying his workers.

  * **each of them** - "each of the workers hired at the eleventh hour"
  * **a denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
  * **they thought that** - "the workers who were hired first thought that"
