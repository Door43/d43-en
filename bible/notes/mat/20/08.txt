===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is continuing the story of the landowner and the workers.
  * **the eleventh hour**  - This can be translated as "near the end of the day."
  * **each of them**  - This is referring to the workers who were hired at the eleventh hour.
  * **they thought that **- The "they" is speaking about the workers who were hired first.
