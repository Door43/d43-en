===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is continuing the story of the landowner and the workers.
  * **When they received**  - This is speaking about the workers who were hired first.
  * **property owner**  - "landowner" or "vineyard owner"
  * **you have made them equal to us**  - "Them" is referring to the workers hired at the eleventh hour, and "us" is referring to the workers who were hired first.
  * **equal**  - This word means that two or more things, or people, are the same in some way.
  * **we who have borne the burden of the day and the scorching heat **- This can be translated as "we who have worked all day in the hot sun."
