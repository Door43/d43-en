===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is continuing the story of the landowner and the workers.
  * **one of them**  - This is the landowner speaking to the complaining first workers.
  * **I do you no wrong**  - This means that the landowner is being fair. This can be translated as "I am being fair to you."
  * **Did you not agree with me for one denarius**  - This rhetorical question means that the worker and the landowner had agreed on the wages. This can be translated as "we already agreed on one denarius." (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **it is my pleasure to give to these**  - Could be translated "it pleases me to give these wages" or "giving these wages pleases me"
