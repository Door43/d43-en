===== translationNotes: =====
  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **one of them** - "one of the workers who had worked the longest"
  * **Friend** - Use a word that one man would use to address another man whom he is politely rebuking.
  * **Did you not agree with me for one denarius?** - The landowner uses a question to rebuke the workers who were complaining. AT: "We already agreed that I would give you one denarius" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **one denarius** - This was the daily wage at that time. AT: "one day's wages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
