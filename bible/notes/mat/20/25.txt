===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the rulers of the Gentiles subjugate them**  - "any top officials of the nations put people under their rule"
  * **their important men**  - The pronoun "their" is speaking about the rulers who have other men to whom they give authority.
  * **exercise authority over **-** ** <font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"have control over"</font>
  * **them**  - "them" here is referring to the people being ruled.
  * **wishes**  - "wants" or "desires"
  * **among you**  - The pronoun "you" refers to the disciples.
