===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is continuing the story with the blind men.
  * **called to them**  -  the pronoun "them" refers to the blind men.
  * **What do you wish me to do for you**  - "you" is speaking about the blind men.
  * **Wish**  - "want"
  * **that our eyes may be opened**  - This idiom means that the blind men wanted to be able to see. This can be translated actively by saying "make us see." (See: Idiom)
  * **being moved with compassion**  - This can be made active by saying "having compassion" or "moved with compassion for them."
