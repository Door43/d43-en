===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she bowed down before him**  - The mother of the sons of Zebedee came and showed respect to Jesus by bowing to him.
  * **What do you wish**  - Jesus is asking the mother of the sons of Zebedee what she wants. This can be translated as "What do you want me to do?"
  * **one at your right hand, and one at your left hand**  - This is saying that the mother wanted her son's places to be on either side of Jesus, in places of importance or authority. This can be translated as "one on each side of you in places of authority in your kingdom." (See: Metonymy)
