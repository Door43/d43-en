===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is continuing the story of the landowner and the workers.
  * **Again he went out**  - This is referring to the landowner.
  * **Again he went out about the sixth hour **- This can be translated as "He went out about the middle of the day."
  * **ninth hour **- This can be translated as "between the middle of the day and sunset."
  * **eleventh hour**  - This can be translated as "near the end of the day."
