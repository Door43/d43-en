===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is continuing the story of the landowner and the workers.
  * **He went out again**  - This is referring to the landowner of the vineyard.
  * **He went out again about the third hour**  - This can be translated as "He went out again between sunrise and the middle of the day."
  * **standing idle**  - "not having any work"
  * **So they went to work**  - "They" here is speaking about the workers.
