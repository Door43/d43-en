===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **He went out again** - "The landowner went out again"
  * **the third hour** - The third hour is around nine in the morning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **standing idle in the marketplace** - "standing in the marketplace not doing anything" or "standing in the marketplace with no work to do"
  * **marketplace** - a large, open-air area where people buy and sell food and other items
