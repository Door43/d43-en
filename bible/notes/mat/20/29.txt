===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As they went**  - Speaking about the disciples and Jesus.
  * **Followed him**  - "Him" here is Jesus.
  * **blind men**  -This means that the men could not see. 
  * **When they heard that Jesus was passing by**  -This can be translated "When they heard Jesus walking by them".
  * **They cried out all the more**  - The blind men were crying out.
