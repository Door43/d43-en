===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As they went**  - This is speaking about the disciples and Jesus.
  * **followed him**  - The word "him" here is Jesus.
  * **Behold**  - This word can be translated "This happened."
  * **blind men**  - This means that the men could not see.
  * **when they heard that Jesus was passing by**  - This can be translated "when they heard that Jesus was walking by them."
  * **Lord, Son of David**  - This refers to the blind men recognizing the authority Christ had.
  * **They cried out all the more**  - The blind men were shouting more than they were before. This can be translated "They shouted louder."
