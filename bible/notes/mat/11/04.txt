===== Translation Notes: =====

This concludes the response of Jesus to the disciples of John the Baptist.

  * **report to John ** - "tell John"
  * **stumbling in me** - This is a figure of speech that could be translated as "taking offense over me" or "stumbling over me." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
