===== translationNotes: =====

  * **What will be the sign of your coming and of the end of the world** - Here "your coming" refers to when Jesus will come in power, establishing God's reign on earth and bringing this age to an end. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Be careful that no one leads you astray** - "Be careful that you do not believe anyone who lies to you about these things"
  * **many will come in my name** - Here "name" refers to "in the authority" or "as the representative" of someone. AT: "many will claim that they have come as my representative" or "many will say they speak for me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
