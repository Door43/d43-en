===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues to talk with his disciples about his coming and the end times.
  * **This generation will not pass away**  - This means before this generation in the end times, not the disciples' own generation, dies or perishes.
  * **pass away**  -"die" or "perish" (See: euphemism)
  * **until all these things will have occurred.**  - "until God causes all these things to happen." (See: Active/Passive) (See: UDB)
  * **The sky and the earth will pass away, but my words will not pass away**  - Even when the sky and earth don't exist any more, Jesus' words will exist eternally. (See: Litotes)
  * **The sky and the earth will pass away,**  - "The sky and the earth will perish." (See: Euphemism)
