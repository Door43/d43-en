===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Truly I say to you** - "I tell you the truth." This adds emphasis to what Jesus says next.
  * **this generation will not pass away** - Here "pass away" is a polite way of referring to death. AT: "the people living today will not all die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **until all of these things will have happened** - "until God causes all these things to happen"
  * **Heaven and the earth will pass away** - "Heaven and the earth will no longer exist"
  * **my words will never pass away** - Here "words" refers to what Jesus has said. AT: "what I say will always be true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
