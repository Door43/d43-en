===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * **this generation will not pass away** - "the people living today will not all die" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **until all of these things will have occurred**  - AT: "until God causes all these things to happen"
  * **Heaven and the earth will pass away**  - "Heaven and the earth will no longer exist"
