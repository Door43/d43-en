===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This generation will not pass away**  - This means before this generation (the generation in the end times that he has been talking about) dies. (See: euphemism)
  * **until all these things will have been accomplished**  - All these things will happen. (See: passive) (See: UDB)
  * **The sky and the earth will pass away, but my words will not pass away**  - Jesus' words will exist eternally. (See: Litotes)
