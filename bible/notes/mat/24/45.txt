===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples how to get ready for his return.

  * **So who is the faithful, wise servant, whom his master...?** - "So who is the faithful, wise servant? He is the one whom his master" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **give them their food** - "give the people in the master's home their food"
