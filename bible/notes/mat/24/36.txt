  * Jesus continues to talk to his disciples about his return and the end times.
  * **that day and hour no**** one knows **- No one knows when all these things that Jesus has been discussing (his return and the end times) will take place.
  * **day and hour **- "time"
  * **nor the Son,**  - "not even the Son,"
