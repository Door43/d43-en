===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his disciples what will happen before he comes again.

  * **immediately**  - "right away"
  * **those days**  - the days described in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:24:32|24:23-28]]
  * **the sun will be darkened**  - "God will make the sun dark" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **the powers of the heavens will be shaken **- "God will shake things in the sky and above the sky" (See: Active/Passive)
