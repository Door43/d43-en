===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues Jesus' teaching on the hill to his followers.

  * **it -**  This refers to the light of the lamp.
  * **lamp**  - This is a small bowl with a wick and olive oil for fuel. The important thing is that it gave light.
  * **your light -**  (See UDB)
  * **your good deeds -**  (See UDB)
