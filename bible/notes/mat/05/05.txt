===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the meek** - "the gentle" or "those who do not rely on their own power"
  * **they will inherit the earth** - "God will give them the entire earth"
  * **hunger and thirst for righteousness** - This metaphor means a person should strongly desire to do what is right. AT: "desire to live right as much as they desire food and drink" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they will be filled** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will fill them" or "God will satisfy them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the pure in heart** - "people whose hearts are pure." Here "heart" refers to a person's desire. AT: "those who only want to serve God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they will see God** - Here "see" means they will be able to live in God's presence. AT: "God will allow them to live with him"
