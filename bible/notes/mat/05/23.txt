===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you - **  Jesus was talking to a group of people about what might happen in their individual lives.
  * **offering your gift**  - "giving your gift"
  * **be reconciled**  - This is a passive phrase meaning "come into agreement with." (See: Passive)
