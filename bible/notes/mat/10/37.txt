===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his apostles about the persecution they will endure as they work for him; this began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:10:16|10:16]].

  * **He who ** - This can also be translated "Whoever" or "The one who" or "Anyone who" or "People who" (see UDB).
  * **loves ** - The word for "love" here refers to “brotherly love” or “love from a friend.” It can also be translated "cares for" or "is devoted to" or "is fond of."
  * **me** - The pronoun "me" in these verses refers to Jesus.
  * **is not worthy of me ** - This can also be translated "does not deserve to belong to me" or "is not worthy to be my disciple" or "is not worthy to belong to me."(see UDB).
  * **He who does not pick up his cross and follow after me ** - When a person picks up his cross, that person is willing to follow Jesus even to the point of being killed, as Jesus was killed. (See: Metaphor)
  * **pick up** - "take up"
  * **He who finds his life will lose it** - The meaning here is that if a person puts his physical life as more important, his life will be useless.
  * **finds his life ** - “saves his life” or "keeps his life"
  * **loses his life** - "gives up his life"
  * **for my sake** - “because he follows me” or “on my account" or "because of me"
