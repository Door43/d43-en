===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his apostles about the persecution they will endure as they work for him; this began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:10:16|10:16]].

  * **Do not think ** - "Do not suppose" or "You must not think"
  * **a sword ** - This metaphor can stand for 1) violent death (see "cross" in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:10:37|10:38]]) or 2) strife that causes division. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **set ** - "turn" or "divide" or "separate"
  * **a man against his father ** - "a son against his father"
  * **A man's enemies** - "A person's enemies" or "A person's worst enemies"
  * **those of his own household ** - "members of his own family"
