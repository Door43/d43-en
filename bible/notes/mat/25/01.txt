===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then the kingdom of heaven will be like **- This begins a parable, comparing the kingdom of heaven to the wise and foolish virgins. (See: Parable)
  * **virgins **- A noun meaning "unmarried girls" (UDB) or "young women" with an emphasis on not having had sexual relations.
  * **lamps **- These could have been either oil lamps or torches. (A torch was made by wrapping some kind of cloth onto the end of a stick or pole and soaking the cloth with oil. When the torch was lit, it would burn for a while until the oil was gone.)
  * **Five of them **- The pronoun "them" refers to five of the virgins.
  * **For when the foolish**- "For when the foolish virgins" or "For when the foolish girls" (UDB)
  * **containers of oil**  - "flasks of oil" or "jars of oil"
