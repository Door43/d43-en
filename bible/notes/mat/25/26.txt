===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell a parable about faithful and unfaithful servants.

  * **You wicked and lazy servant** - "You are a wicked and lazy servant"
  * **I reap where I have not sowed** - “I gather garden food from gardens in which someone else sowed the seed”
  * **and harvest where I have not scattered** - “and I collect garden food from 
  * **scattered**  - The implied information is that seeds were scattered or sown.
  * **bankers **- These are persons or businesses who keep money for others. The bankers have use of the money while in the bank, and the owner of the money will receive their money back with interest.
  * **received back my own ** - "received back my own money" (See: Implicit Information)
  * **with interest**- "with some more money as well"
