===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues his parable.
  * **bankers **- These are persons or businesses who keep money for others.  The bankers may have use of the money while being kept, and the owner of the money may receive back their money with interest.
  * **received back my own **- "received back my own money"
  * **with interest **- "with some more money as well"
