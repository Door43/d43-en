===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **trimmed their lamps** - "adjusted their lamps so they would burn brightly"
  * **The foolish said to the wise** - These nominal adjectives can be stated as adjectives. AT: "The foolish virgins said to the wise virgins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **our lamps are going out** - This is an idiom. AT: "the fire in our lamps is about to burn out" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
