===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus begins here to tell his disciples how he will judge all people at the end of the age.)
  * **the Son of Man**  - Jesus was referring to Himself.
  * **When the Son of Man comes in his glory **- This describes Jesus coming in splendor and radiance.
  * **sit on his glorious throne**  - "sit on his royal throne"
  * **Before him**  - "In front of him"
  * **Before him will be gathered all the nations**  - This can be expressed actively as "All the nations will gather together before him" (See: Active or Passive)
  * **all the nations **- This is a metonym for all people from every nation. (See: Metonymy)
  * **He will place**  - "The Son of Man will place"
  * **as a shepherd separates the sheep from the goats**  - This is a simile that distinguishes two distinct groups of people. (See: Simile)
  * **goats - **  Goats are medium-sized four-footed mammals that are similar to sheep, often domesticated or herded like sheep.
  * **He will place the sheep on his right hand, but the goats on his left**  - Jesus will place one group of people at his right hand but the others at his left.  Being placed at the king's right hand rather than at the left hand would be the place of greater favor or honor.
