===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues his parable of the talents that highlights the kingdom of heaven.)
  * **I have made two more talents **- “I have earned two more talents.”
  * **Well done**  - “You have done well” or “You have done right.” Your culture might have an expression that a master (or someone in authority) would use to show that he approves of what his servant (or someone under him) has done.
  * **the joy of your master**  - "the delight of your master" or "enter into my joy" The master was speaking about himself.
