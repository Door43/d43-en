===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus teaching His disciples.

  * **to the dogs - **  Dogs represents wicked people who do not value holy things.
  * **pearls**  - round precious rare gems which are valuable, used here to mean some object or idea which is valuable. (See: Metaphor)
  * **your pearls**  - The "your" in these verses refers to people who have the pearls.
  * **of the hogs - **  Hogs represents wicked people who do not value holy things.
