===== Translation Notes: =====

Continuing beatitudes sermon, Jesus teaching His disciples.

  * **pearls**  - are small, usually white/ off white, round gem like objects produced by shelled creatures (oysters) in the sea. Somewhat rare and bright. Used for jewelry.
  * **your pearls**  - "your" in these verses refers to people who have the pearls
