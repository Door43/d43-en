===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:05:01|5:1]]. 

  * **like a foolish man who built his house upon the sand** - Jesus continues the simile that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:07:24|7:24]]. He compares those who do not obey his words to foolish house builders. Only a fool would build a house on a sandy place where rain, floods, and wind can sweep the sand away. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile|]])
  * **fell**  - Use the general word that describes what happens when a house falls down.
  * **and great was its fall**  - The rain, floods, and wind completely destroyed the house.
