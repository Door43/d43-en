===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues to teach his disciples in his Sermon on the Mount, which began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:05:01|5:3]].
  * **General Information:** - Jesus is talking to a group of people about what might happen to them as individuals. The instances of "you" and the commands are plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **Do not judge** - It is implied here that "judge" has the strong meaning of "condemn harshly" or "declare guilty." AT: "Do not condemn people harshly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you will not be judged** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will not condemn you harshly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **For** - Be sure the reader understands the statement in 7:2 is based on what Jesus said in 7:1.
  * **with the judgment you judge, you will be judged** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will condemn you in the same way you condemn others" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **measure** - Possible meanings are 1) this is the amount of punishment given or 2) this is the standard used for judgment.
  * **it will be measured out to you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will measure it out to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
