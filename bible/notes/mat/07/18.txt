===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:05:01|5:1]]. He is continuing to use the metaphor of fruits for the words and works of prophets that begin in  [[:en:bible:notes:mat:07:15|7:16]].

  * **Every tree that does not yield good fruit is cut down and thrown into the fire**  - This metaphor refers to false prophets who will be thrown out. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **By their fruits you will know them**  - This  metaphor refers to the deeds of the prophets that will make them known as good or bad.
