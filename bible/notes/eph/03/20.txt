===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul concludes his prayer with a blessing.
  * **General Information:** - The words "we" and "us" in this book continue to include Paul and all believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])

  * **Now to him who** - "Now to God, who"
  * **everything far, far above** - God can do far more than we could ever ask or imagine.
