===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul praises God in his sufferings and prays for these Ephesian believers.

  * **we...our** - Paul includes the readers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **access with confidence** - AT: "access into God's presence with confidence" or "freedom to enter into God's presence with confidence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **These are your glory** - The Christians in Ephesus should be proud of what Paul is suffering in prison. The words "your glory" is synecdoche for the honor that others will give them. AT: "For these things people will honor you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
