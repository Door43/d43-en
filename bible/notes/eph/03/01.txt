===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul refers back to the oneness of Jews and Gentiles and the temple of which believers are now a part to make clear the hidden truth about the church to believers.
 
  * **Because of this** - "Because of God's grace to you"
  * **the prisoner of Christ Jesus** - "the one whom Christ Jesus has put in prison"
  * **the task of the grace of God that he gave me for you** - "the responsibility that God gave me to bring his grace to you"
