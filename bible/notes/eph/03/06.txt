===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the Gentiles are fellow heirs...** - This is the hidden truth Paul began to express in [[en:bible:notes:eph:03:03|3:5]]. The Gentiles who receive christ will also receive the inheritance like the believing Jews.
    * **fellow members of the body** - Paul continues to use the analogy of the physical body in describing the believers in Christ.
    * **For this I became a servant** - AT: "I am now serving God to spread the gospel" (see [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
