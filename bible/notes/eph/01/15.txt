===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because of this** – This can be translated “For this reason”.​
  * **those set apart for him** - The Greek word from which this phrase comes means “morally blameless” or “consecrated” or “holy ones”. The pronoun “him” refers to the Lord Jesus. This word is often translated as “saints”.
  * **you** – This pronoun includes all believers in the church at Ephesus
