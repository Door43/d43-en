===== translationNotes: =====

  * **we were chosen** - The word "we" is includes of all believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **we were chosen** - God chose us. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **inheritance** - The Christian believers are presented here as God's own people. They are spoken of as if they were something that he inherited. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])   
  * **We were the first** - Here Paul refers to the Jewish believers, not the believers at Ephesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **the plan of the one** - the plan of God
  * **the purpose of his will** - "the plan of God" or "the desire of God"
