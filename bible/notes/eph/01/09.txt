===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hidden truth of the plan** - "secret of his plan" (See: UDB)
  * **according to his desire that he set forth in Christ** - "as he was pleased to reveal Christ" (See: UDB)
  * **when the times are fulfilled** - "God planned that at the time that he appointed" (See: UDB)
  * **for the completion of his plan** - "for his plan to be completed"
  * **his plan, he will** - "God's plan, God will"
  * **under Christ** - “under Christ’s rule”
