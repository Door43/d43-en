===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **subjected ** - “put” (UDB) or "placed". 
  * **feet ** - This is a metonymy for authority or power, so God has put all things under Christ’s power. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **head over all things in the Church, which is his body** - This is a metaphor for the church, where Christ is to the church what the head is to a body. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]]) The people belonging to a church are like the different parts of Christ's body. (UDB)
