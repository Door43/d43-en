===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in his beloved one we** - "in God's beloved one, Jesus Christ, we" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **we have redemption** - The word "we" refers to all believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **riches of his grace** - Paul speaks of God's grace as if it were material wealth. AT: "greatness of God's grace" or "abundance of God's grace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He made this grace abound for us in all wisdom and understanding** - Possible meanings are 1) God in his wisdom and understanding gave us abundant grace or 2) God in his abundant grace gave us wisdom and understanding.
  * **He made this grace abound** - Paul speaks of God's grace as if it were objects that could be increased in number. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
