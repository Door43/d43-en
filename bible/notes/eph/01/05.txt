===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **destined us in advance for adoption **– This could be translated as “determined in advance to adopt us”.
  * **adoption **– This word in the Greek refers to being placed in God’s family in the position of an adult son or daughter rather than just a child.
  * **he **– This pronoun is referring to God in both verses.
  * **beloved one **– This is referring to Jesus Christ.
