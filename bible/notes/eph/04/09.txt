===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He ascended** - "Christ went up"
  * **he also descended** - "Christ also went down" or "Christ also came down"
  * **into the depths of the earth** - Possible meanings are 1) "into the lower regions, the earth" or 2) "into the lower parts, the earth."
  * **he might fill all things** - "he might completely fill everything" or "all might be filled with him"
