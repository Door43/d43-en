===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul finishes his instructions on what believers should not do and ends with what they must do.

  * **Put away all bitterness, rage, anger** - "Put away" is a metaphor for not continuing to have certain attitudes or behaviors. AT: "You must not allow these things to be part of your life: bitterness, rage, anger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **rage** - intense anger
  * **Be kind** - "Instead, be kind"
  * **tenderhearted** - "gentle" or "full of compassion"
