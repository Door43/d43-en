===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **one body** - All believers in the family of God are like the various members of a human body. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **one Spirit** - There is one Holy Spirit.
  * **called in one** - AT: "specially chosen in one" or "appointed in one"
  * **in one confident expectation** - AT: "in one sure hope"
  * **Father of all...and in all** - AT: "Father of everything...over everything...through everything...in everything"
