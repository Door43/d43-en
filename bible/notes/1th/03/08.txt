===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For now we live** - The word "we" refers to Paul, Silvanus, and Timothy but not the Thessalonian believers. AT: "We are very encouraged." (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you stand firm** - AT: "you are trusting very much" (UDB) (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **For what thanks can we give to God for you, for all the joy that we have before our God over you?** - This rhetorical question expresses gratefulness. AT: "We cannot thank God enough for what he has done for you! We greatly rejoice over you when we pray to our God!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
  * **Night and day** - "Frequently" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **very hard** - "fervently"
  * **see your face** - "visit you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
