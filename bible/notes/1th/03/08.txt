===== translationNotes: =====

  * **we live** - This is an idiom that expresses living a satisfied life. AT: "we are very encouraged" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **if you stand firm in the Lord** - To "stand firm" is an idiom meaning to continue being faithful. AT: "if you continue to trust in the Lord" (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **For what thanks can we give to God for you, for all the joy that we have before our God over you?** - This rhetorical question can be expressed as a statement. AT: "We cannot thank God enough for what he has done for you! We greatly rejoice over you when we pray to our God!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
  * **before our God** - Paul speaks as if he and his companions were physically in God's presence. He is probably referring to the activity of praying. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **Night and day** - Here an exaggeration is used to show how often Paul prays. AT: "Frequently" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **very hard** - "fervently"
  * **see your face** - "visit you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
