===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you became** - This pronoun refers to the believers in the Thessalonian church. (see: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
  * **imitators ** - This noun comes from the verb to imitate, which means to act like or to follow.
  * **us** - This pronoun refers to Paul, Silvanus, and Timothy.
  * **received the word ** - "welcomed the teaching" or "accepted the teaching"
  * **in much tribulation ** - "during a time of great suffering"
  * **Achaia ** - This is an ancient district in what is present day Greece.
