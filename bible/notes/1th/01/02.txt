===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We always give thanks to God**  - AT: "We give thanks to God frequently" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **We always** - The word "we" refers to Paul, Silvanus, and Timothy but not the Thessalonian believers. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **we make mention of you in our prayers**  - "we pray for you"
  * **We remember without ceasing**  - "We frequently remember"
  * **work of faith**  - "acts of faith" or "your work for God because you trust in him" (UDB)
  * **patience of confidence**  - "endurance of confidence"
