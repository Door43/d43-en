===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We always give thanks to God ** - This is a hyperbole. It can be translated as "we give thanks to God frequently" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]]), The pronoun "we" refers to Paul, Silvanus and Timothy.
  * **for all of you ** - This pronoun refers to the believers in the Thessalonian church. (see: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
  * **we make mention of you in our prayers** - "we pray for you"
  * **We remember without ceasing** - This is a hyperbole. It can be translated as "we frequently remember" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **work of faith ** - "acts of faith" or "your work for God because you trust in him" (UDB)
  * **patience of hope ** - "endurance of hope"
