===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **abstain**  - stay away from
  * **sexual immorality**  - sexual activity that opposes God's will for believers. Including, but not limited to adultery, fornication, and homosexuality.
  * **you**  - the believers in Thessalonica
  * **know how to possess **  - know how to find or acquire
  * **his own wife**  - she alone will belong to him and he alone will belong to her
  * **in holiness and honor**  - his acquisition of a wife should be done in a pure and honorable way
  * **lustful passion**  - with impure and selfish desires and cravings
  * **Gentiles who do not know God**  - unbelievers
  * **no man **- no human being whether male or female
  * **transgress and wrong**  - a doublet
  * **brother**  - a believer in Christ
  * **the Lord is an avenger**  - He will punish the one who transgressed and will defend the one who was wronged
  * **forewarned you**  - the believers had already been told about the consequences of sexual sin
