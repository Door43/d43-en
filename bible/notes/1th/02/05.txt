===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul tells the Thessalonian believers that his conduct was not based in flattery, greed, or self glory.

  * **use words as flattery** - "speak to you with false praise"
  * **nor as a pretext for covetousness** - You may need to supply the words omitted by the ellipsis. AT: "nor did we use words to hide an evil desire for your money" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **could have claimed privileges** - "could have insisted you give us money"
