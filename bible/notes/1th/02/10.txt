===== translationNotes: =====

  * **holy, righteously, and blamelessly** - Paul uses three words that focus on how they behaved in a very good way toward the Thessalonian believers.
  * **a father with his own children** - Paul compares how a father teaches his children to behave to how Paul's team exhorted and encouraged the believers to walk in a manner that is worthy of God.
  * **we exhorted and encouraged you** - The words "exhorted" and "encouraged" express how passionately Paul's group encouraged the Thessalonians. AT: "we strongly encouraged you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **into his own kingdom and glory** - The word "glory" describes the word "kingdom." AT: "into his own glorious kingdom." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
