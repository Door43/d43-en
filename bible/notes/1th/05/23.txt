===== translationNotes: =====

  * **make you completely holy** - This refers to God making a person sinless and perfect in his sight.
  * **your whole spirit, soul, and body** - This is a parallelism where the terms "spirit," "soul," and "body" have similar meanings and are used for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **be preserved without blame for** - "be kept from sinning until"
  * **Faithful is he who calls you** - "He is faithful who calls you"
  * **the one who will also do it** - "he will be helping you"
