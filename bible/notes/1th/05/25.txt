===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul gives his closing statements.

  * **brothers** - Here "brothers" means fellow Christians.
  * **I request of you by the Lord** - "I ask you, as if the Lord were speaking to you"
  * **that this letter be read** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that you read this letter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
