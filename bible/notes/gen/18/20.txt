===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the cries from Sodom and Gomorrah** - This could be translated as "the outcry against Sodom and Gomorrah." This refers to people crying out to God to punish the people of Sodom and Gomorrah.
  * **their sin is so grave** - "their sin is so serious" or "they have sinned so much"
  * **I will now go down there**  - "I will now go down to Sodom and Gomorrah"
  * **go down there and see** - "go down there to find out" or "go down there to decide"
  * **as wicked as the outcry suggests, which has come to me**  - "as wicked as the outcry that has come to me suggests." This can also be translated as "as wicked as I have heard."
  * **if not** - "If they are not as wicked as the outcry suggests"
