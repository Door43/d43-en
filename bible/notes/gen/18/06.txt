===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **three bags** - "three measures" of flour. This would be enough flour for a very large meal for many people.
  * **flat bread**  - This bread was probably cooked quickly on a hot stone. It may have been flat bread or round like small loaves or rolls.
  * **he hurried to prepare it** - The word "he" refers to the servant.
  * **to prepare it**  - This can also be translating more specifically as "to cut it up and roast it."
  * **curds** - This refers to the solid part of fermented milk. It may have been yogurt or cheese.
  * **the calf that had been prepared**  - "the roasted calf"
