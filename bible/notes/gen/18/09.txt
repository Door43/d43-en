===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They said to him** - "Then they said to Abraham"
  * **He said, "I will certainly return to you** - The word "He" refers to the Lord. This is the one that Abraham called "Lord" in [[en:bible:notes:gen:18:03|18:3]]. 
  * **and see** - The word "see" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows. 
  * **in the springtime** - This can be translated as "this same season comes next year" or "about this time next year" (UDB).
  * **behold** - This can be translated as "look" or "listen" or "pay attention to what I am about to tell you."
  * **in the tent doorway** - "at the opening of the tent" or "at the entrance of the tent"
