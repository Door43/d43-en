===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He spoke to him**  - "Abraham spoke to Yahweh"
    * **if there are forty found there**  - This means "if you find forty righteous people in Sodom and Gomorrah."
    * **He replied** - "Yahweh replied"
    * **I will not do it for the forty's sake**  - This means "I will not destroy the cities if I find forty righteous people there."
    * **thirty** - "thirty righteous people" or "thirty good people"
    * **Look, I have undertaken to speak** - This means "Pardon me for deciding to speak." The phrase "Look" here draws attention to the surprising information that follows.
    * **twenty**  - "twenty righteous people" or "twenty good people"
    * **bold words**  - explanation
