===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have enough**  - This can be made more explicit: "I have enough property" (see [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]).
  * **if I have found favor in your sight**  - "if we are on good terms"
  * **my gift from my hand**  - "this gift from me"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **I have seen your face, and it is like seeing the face of God**  - This is either an expression of amazement and wonder ("Seeing your face is as wonderful as seeing the face of God") or of humility ("I am humbled before you as I am humbled before God").
  * **God has dealt graciously with me**  - "God has treated me very well" or "God has blessed me very much"
  * **Thus Jacob urged him, and Esau accepted it.**  - It was customary for these people to refuse a gift first, but then to accept the gift before the giver was offended (see [[en:bible:notes:gen:23:03|Gen 23:3-18]]).
