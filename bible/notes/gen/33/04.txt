===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **meet him**  - "meet Jacob"
    * **hugged his neck**  - "hugged him around his neck"
    * **they wept**  - This can be made more explicit: "they cried because they were happy to see each other again." (See [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]).
    * **he saw the women and children**  - "he saw Jacob's women and children"
    * **The children whom God has graciously given your servant**  - The phrase "whom God has graciously given" tells us something about "the children." You could also translate this: "God has graciously given these children to your servant." (See [[:en:ta:translate:sentencestructure#clause]]).
    * **God has graciously given** - "God has freely given" or "God has given because he favors"
    * **your servant**  - This is a polite way of referring to the speaker. Here it refers to Jacob. You can also translate this as "me" (see [[en:ta:translate:123person#examples_from_the_bible]]).
