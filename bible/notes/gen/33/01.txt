===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold**  - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to a surprising new part of the story.
  * **four hundred men**  - "400 men" (See [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Numbers - Translating Unknowns]]).
  * **female servants**  - "servant wives." This refers to Bilhah and Zilpah.
  * **bowed** - Here the word "bow" means to bend over to humbly express respect and honor toward someone.
