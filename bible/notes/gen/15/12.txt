===== translationNotes: =====
"
  * **Abram fell sound asleep** - This is an idiom. AT: "Abraham slept deeply." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
   * **deep and terrifying darkness** - "extreme darkness that terrified him"
  * **overwhelmed him** - "surrounded him"
  * **strangers** - "aliens" or "foreigners"
  * **and will be enslaved and oppressed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and the owners of that land will enslave your descendants and oppress them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
