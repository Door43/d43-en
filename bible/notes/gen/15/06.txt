===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am Yahweh that brought you out of Ur**  - By stating who he is and what he had done, Yahweh is confirming his power to give Abram what he promised.
  * **how will I know**- Abram is asking for more proof that Yahweh will give him a child. By asking again, Abram is expressing some doubt.
  * **He counted it to him as righteousness**  - God "considered Abram righteous because of his belief."
