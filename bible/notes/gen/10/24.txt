===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Arpachshad** - Arpachshad was one of Shem's sons.
  * **Peleg** - Translators may add a footnote note that says: "The name Peleg means 'division.'" 
  * **the earth was divided** - This can be translated as "the people of the earth divided themselves" or "the people of the earth separated from one another."
