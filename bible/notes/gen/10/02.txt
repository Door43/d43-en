===== translationNotes: =====

  * **From these the coastland peoples separated and went into their lands** - "Javan's sons and descendants separated and moved to the coastlands and islands"
  * **coastland peoples** - This refers to people who lived along the coast and on the islands.
  * **their lands** - "their homelands." These are the places that the people moved to and lived in. 
  * **every one with its own language** - "Each people group spoke its own language" or "The people groups divided themselves according to their languages"
