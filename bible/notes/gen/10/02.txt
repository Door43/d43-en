===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **From these**  - "from the sons of Javan"
  * **coastland peoples**  - This refers to people who lived along the coast and on the islands.
  * **their lands**  - "their homelands." These are the places that the people moved to and lived in.
  * **From these the coastland peoples separated and went into their lands**  - This can also be translated as "Javan's sons and descendants separated and moved to the coastlands and islands."
  * **every one**  - "every people group"
  * **every one with its own language**  - This can be translated as "Each people group spoke its own language" or "The people groups divided themselves according to their languages".
