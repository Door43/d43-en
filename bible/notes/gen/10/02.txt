===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **From these** ​ - "from the sons of Javan"
  * **coastland peoples**  - this means "people living on the coast," including islands.
  * **their lands**  - this means "their homelands," the places they were later found.
  * **with its own language** ​ - this means "speaking its own language" or "divided by its own language".
  * **clans - **  alternate: "families"
