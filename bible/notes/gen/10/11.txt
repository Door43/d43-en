===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he went forth into Assyria**  - "Nimrod went into Assyria"
  * **Mizraim became** - The list of Noah's descendants continues.
  * **Mizraim** - Mizraim was one of Ham's sons. His descendants became the people of Egypt. Mizraim is the Hebrew name for “Egypt.”
