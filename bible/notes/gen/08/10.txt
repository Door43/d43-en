===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  - The word "behold" here tells us to pay attention to the important information that comes next.
  * **freshly plucked**  - "freshly picked" or "newly picked"
  * **He waited another seven days** - "He waited a second seven days"
  * **She did not return again to him**  - If people would not understand, you could  state the reason explicitly: "She did not return again to him because she found a place to land." (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
