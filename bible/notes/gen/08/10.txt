===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  - The word "behold" here tells us to pay attention to the important information that comes next.
  * **freshly plucked**  - "freshly picked" or "newly picked"
  * **she did not return again to him**  - you could make this more explicit: "she did not return again to him because she found a dry place to land" (See [[:en:ta:discourse:implicitexplicit|Implicit and Explicit Information]]).
  * **he waited another seven days **- or "he waited a second seven days"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
