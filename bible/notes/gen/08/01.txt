===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **considered**  - This could be translated as "remembered" or "turned his thought to."
  * **all the other animals**  - This could be translated as "all the domestic animals." Together with the wild animals the text talks about all the animals in the boat.
  * **the fountains of the deep **- This refers to underground water sources.
  * **the windows of heaven**  - This is figurative language referring to the source of rain falling from the sky.
  * **the fountains… and the windows…were closed**  - this can be translated either as "Yahweh closed the fountains…and the windows" or "the fountains…and the windows closed up".
