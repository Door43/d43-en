===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **her foot … she returned … and brought her **- The word "dove" is feminine in the author's language. You could translate these phrases with the pronouns "it … its … it" or "his … he … him" depending on how you refer to a dove. "And she returned to him on the boat" could alternatively be translated "and it (the dove) returned to Noah on the boat."
  * **rest her foot**  - this means "to land or perch on something to rest from flight"
  * **He / Him **- the masculine pronouns in these verses continue to refer to Noah i.e. "Noah sent out a dove","Noah stretched out his hand", etc…
  * **the face of the ground**  - This could alternatively be translated as "surface of the ground."
