===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On the first day of the month**  - this could be translated "In the tenth month, on the first day of the month." Or, "on the first day of the tenth month"
  * **came to rest**  - "landed" or "stopped on solid ground"
  * **Ararat region **- This is a region is eastern Turkey.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **appeared**  - this can be made more explicit as "appeared above the surface of the water" (see [[:en:ta:discourse:implicitexplicit|Implicit and Explicit Information]]).
  * **Jewish Year (UDB)**- The Jewish calendar, unlike our calendar, is a lunar calendar. The 7th month of the Jewish calendar comes in about the September/October time frame. This is when the Jewish new year is celebrated.
