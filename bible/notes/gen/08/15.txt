===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **God said**  - This could be translated as "Then God said" as in the UDB.
  * **Come out**  - This could be translated as "go out."
  * **all flesh**  - This refers to all the different kinds of animals.
  * **abound **- This could be translated as "swarm".
  * **be fruitful and multiply**  - this can be translated "reproduce greatly" or "have many offspring". This is the same as the command God gave to the first animals and people in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:28|Gen 1:28]].
  * **be fruitful **- "have offspring" or "produce young."
  * **multiply**  - "become many."
