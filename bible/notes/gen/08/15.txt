===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Come out** - AT: "Go out"
  * **every living creature of all flesh** - "every kind of living creature." The word "flesh" is a figurative way to refer to the "living creature" and emphasize that they are alive.
  * **abound** - AT: "swarm"
  * **be fruitful, and multiply** - This is the same command God gave to the first people in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:28|1:28]]. AT: "reproduce greatly" or "have many offspring." 
  * **be fruitful** - "have offspring" or "produce young"
  * **multiply** - "increase greatly in number" or "become many"
