===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **built an altar to Yahweh**  - "built an altar dedicated to Yahweh" or "built an altar for worshiping Yahweh".
  * **clean animals â€¦ clean birds **- "Clean" means that God allowed these animals to be used in sacrifice. Some animals were not used for sacrifices and were called "unclean."
  * **burnt offering **- After the animal was killed, Noah burned the whole animal as an offering to God. Because God does not have a body and does not eat, only the smoke goes up to God.
  * **Yahweh smelled the pleasing aroma**  - the author describes Yahweh in human terms. This means "Yahweh liked the offering" or "Yahweh appreciated the offering".
  * **curse the ground**  - this refers to the cursing of the ground because of Adam and Eve's disobedience earlier in the story (see [[:en:bible:notes:gen:03:17|Gen 3:17]]).
  * **because of mankind**  - this can be made more explicit: "because mankind is sinful" (see [[:en:ta:discourse:implicitexplicit|Implicit and Explicit Information]]).
  * **the inclination of their hearts is evil from childhood**  - "from their earliest years they tend to do evil things"
  * **inclination of their hearts**  - "their tendency" or "their habit"
  * **from childhood** - This refers to an older child and can be translated as "from their youth."
  * **pleasing aroma**  - This describes the smell of roasted meat that is likable.
  * **While the earth remains**  - "while the earth lasts" or "as long as the earth exists"
  * **seed time**  - "the season for planting"
  * **summer and winter**  - this refers to two major times in the year.
  * **summer**  - the hot, dry time of the year
  * **winter**  - the cool, wet time of the year
  * **will not cease**  - this can be made more explicit: "will not cease to exist" or "will not cease to occur".
