===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about at that time that Judah**  - This introduces a new part of the story that focuses on Judah in 38:1-30. Alternate translation: "Soon after, Judah" or "At that time, Judah" (UDB)
  * **a certain Adullamite, whose name was Hirah**  - "a man named Hirah in the town of Adullam" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **whose name was Shua** - "Shua is a canaanite woman who married Judah.
