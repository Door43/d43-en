===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **She became pregnant** - "Judah's wife became pregnant"
  * **He was named Er** - This can be written in active form. AT: "His father named him Er" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Er ... Onan ... Shelah** - These are the names of Judah's sons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **called his name** - "named him"
  * **Kezib** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
