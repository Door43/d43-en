===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Binding his young horse ... to the choice vine** - It is implied that the vines are so full of grapes that the master does not mind that his donkey eats them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Binding his young horse** - The word "his" refers to Judah's descendants, or to the ruler in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:49:10|49:10]]. 
  * **he has washed** - Often in prophecy events that will happen in the future are described as something that has already happened in the past. This emphasizes that this event will certainly happen. AT: "he will wash" or "they will wash."
  * **His eyes will be** - "their eyes will be." AT: "His eyes are" or "their eyes are."
  * **as dark as wine ... as white as milk** - "darker than wine ... whiter than milk"
