===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Issachar is a strong donkey** - Here "Issachar" refers to his descendants. They are compared to a donkey. This emphasizes that other people will use the descendants of Issachar to do hard work. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He sees a good resting place and the pleasant land** - "He saw a resting place that was good and that the land was pleasant"
  * **He will bend his shoulder to the burden**  - "He bent his shoulder to the burden"
  * **He will bend his shoulder to the burden**  - "He will work very hard for the task."
  * **a servant for the task**  - "a slave for forced labor"
