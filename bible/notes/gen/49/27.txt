===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Benjamin is a hungry wolf** - This refers to Benjamin and his descendants. Jacob compares them to a hungry wolf. This emphasizes that they will be fierce warriors. Alternate translation: "The descendants of Benjamin will be like hungry wolves." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
