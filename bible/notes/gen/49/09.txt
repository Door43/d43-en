===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Judah is a lion's cub** - Jacob speaks about Judah as if he were a lion's cub. Jacob is emphasizing Judah's strength. AT: "Judah is like a young lion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **My son, you have gone up from your victims** - "You, my son, have come back from eating your prey"
  * **like a lioness** - Jacob also compares Judah to a female lion. The lioness is the primary hunter and protector of her cubs. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Who would dare to awaken him?** - Jacob uses a question to emphasize how terrifying Judah is to other people. AT: "No one wants to wake him up." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
