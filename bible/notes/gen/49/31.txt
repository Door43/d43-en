===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jacob continues speaking to his sons.

  * **were purchased** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Abraham purchased" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from the people of Heth** - "from the Hittites"
  * **finished these instructions to his sons** - "finished instructing his sons" or "finished commanding his sons"
  * **he pulled his feet into the bed** - Jacob was sitting on the bed. Now, Jacob turns and puts his feet in the bed so he can lie down.
  * **breathed his last** - This is a polite way of saying a person died. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **went to his people** - This means that after Jacob died, his soul went to the same place as his relatives who died before him.
