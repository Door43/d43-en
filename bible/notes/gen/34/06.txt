===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hamor ... went out to Jacob** - "Hamor ... went to meet Jacob"
  * **The men were offended** - "The men were outraged"
  * **They were very angry ... should not have been done** - This can be stated as a direct quotation spoken by Jacob's son, as in the UDB. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **he had disgraced Israel** - Here the word "Israel" refers to every member of Jacob's family. Israel as a people group was disgraced. AT: "he had humiliated the family of Israel" or "he had brought shame on the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **forcing himself on Jacob's daughter** - "assaulting Jacob's daughter"
  * **for such a thing should not have been done** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "for he should not have done such a terrible thing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
