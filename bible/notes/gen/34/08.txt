===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hamor spoke with them** - "Hamor spoke with Jacob and his sons."
  * **loves your daughter** - Alternate translation: "loves her and wants to marry her" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **give her to him as a wife** - In some cultures, the parents decide whom their children will marry.
  * **Intermarry with us** - "Allow marriages among your people and ours"; "to intermarry is to marry a member of a different racial, social, religious, or tribal group"
