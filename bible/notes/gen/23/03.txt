===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rose up and went from his dead wife** - AT: "got up and left the body"
  * **the sons of Heth** - "the descendants of Heth" (UDB). AT: "the Hittites." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **among you** - AT: "here" or "in your country"
  * **Please grant me a property** - AT: "Sell me some land" or "Allow me to buy a piece of land"
  * **my dead** - This means "the dead body of a person that I love." AT: "my wife's body" or "my wife" or "my loved one."
