===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Please, my lord, listen to me**  - "Hear me, my lord" or "listen to me, kind sir."
  * **A piece of land worth four hundred shekels of silver**  - "Why worry over a piece of land worth only four hundred shekels of silver?"
  * **what is that between me and you?**  - "Between you and me, it does not matter."
  * **Bury your dead**  - "Go bury your dead."
  * **Abraham weighed out to Ephron**  - "Abraham weighed out for Ephron"
  * **in the hearing of the sons of Heth**  - "in the presence of the Hittites"
  * **according to the standard measurement of the merchants**  - "using the standard measurement of weight that merchants use"
