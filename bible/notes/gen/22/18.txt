===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel of Yahweh continues speaking to Abraham.

  * **all the nations of the earth will be blessed**  - "I, the Lord, will bless all the people living everywhere." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **you have obeyed my voice**  - God said "my voice" to refer to what he had said earlier. AT: "you have obeyed what I said" or "you have obeyed me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Abraham returned**  - Only Abraham was named because he was the father, but it was implied that his son went with him. AT: "Abraham and his son went back." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **young men**  - "servants" (UDB)
  * **they departed**  - "they left that place"
  * **he lived at Beersheba**  - Only Abraham was mentioned because he was the leader of his family and servants, but it was implied that they were with him. AT: "Abraham and his people stayed in Beersheba."
