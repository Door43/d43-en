===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behind him was a ram caught in the bushes**  - or "one ram was caught in the bushes." The Hebrew words for "one" and "behind" look almost identical. The Masoretic text reads "behind" but most other manuscripts read "one."
  * **went and took** meaning: Abraham went over to the ram and took it. As an alternate, you may substitute  the UDB's words or use, "went to the ram, took it," 
  * **Yahweh will provide**  - or "Yahweh sees"
  * **On the mountain of Yahweh it will be provided**  - "On the mountain of Yahweh, he will appear"
