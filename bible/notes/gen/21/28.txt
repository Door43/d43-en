===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Abraham set seven female lambs of the flock by themselves** - "Abraham separated seven female lambs from the flock"
  * **seven** - "7" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **What is the meaning of these seven female lambs that you have set by themselves?** - "Why have you separated these seven lambs from the flock?"
  * **you will receive** - "you will take"
  * **from my hand** - Here "hand" stands for Abraham. AT: "from me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **it may be a witness** - The word "it" refers to the gift of seven lambs.
  * **so that it may be a witness for me** - The abstract noun "witness" can be stated as "to prove." AT: "to prove to everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
