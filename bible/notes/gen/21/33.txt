===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a tamarisk tree** - This is an evergreen tree that can grow in the desert. It can be stated more generally. AT: "a tree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **the eternal God** - "the God who lives forever"
  * **many days** - This stands for a longer period of time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
