===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **God opened her eyes, and she saw**  - "God caused Hagar to see" or "God showed Hagar" (UDB) The phrase "opened her eyes" is an idiom that means to "make her aware of." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **the skin**  - "the bottle made of skin" or "the bag"
  * **the lad** - "the child" or "the boy" or "Ishmael"
  * **God was with the lad**  - "God guided the boy" or "God blessed the boy"
  * **he grew** - "he grew stronger"
  * **an archer**  - "very skilled at using a bow an arrow" 
  * **got a wife**  - "found a wife"
