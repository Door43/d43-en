===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he called that place** - "Abraham called that place"
  * **Beersheba** - Translators may add a footnote saying "Beersheba can mean either "well of the oath" or "well of seven."
  * **they both** - "Abraham and Abimelech"
  * **Phicol** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:21:22|21:22]].
  * **returned to the land of the Philistines** - "went back to the land of the Philistines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
