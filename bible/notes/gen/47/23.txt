===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and you will plant**  - "that you might sow"
  * **At the harvest,  you shall give a fifth to Pharaoh, and four parts shall be your own** - "For every five plants that produces food, you must give one to Pharaoh. You may keep the other four for yourselves."
