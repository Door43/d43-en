===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May we find favor in your eyes** - Here "eyes" stands for thoughts or opinions. AT: "May you be pleased with us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **find favor** - This means that someone is approved of by someone else. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the land of Egypt** - "over the land of Egypt" or "throughout the land of Egypt"
  * **to this day** - This means to the time that the author was writing this.
  * **one-fifth** - See how you translated "a fifth" in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:47:23|47:24]].
