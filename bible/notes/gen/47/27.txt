===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They were fruitful and multiplied greatly** - The words "fruitful" and "multiplied" say basically the same thing for emphasis. Alternate translation: "They gave birth to very many children there" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
