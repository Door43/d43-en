===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Then Joseph settled his father and his brothers** - "Then Joseph took care of his father and his brothers"
    * **the land of Rameses** - This is another name for the land of Goshen. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **according the number of their dependents** - "according to how many people were in their families"
