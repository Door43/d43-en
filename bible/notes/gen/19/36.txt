===== translationNotes: =====

  * **were pregnant by their father** - "became pregnant by their father" or "conceived children with their father"
  * **He became** - "He is"
  * **the Moabites of today** - "the Moab people who are now living"
  * **of today** - The word "today" refers to the time when the author of Genesis was living. The author was born and wrote this many years after Lot's family lived and died.
  * **Ben Ammi** - This is a male name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the people of Ammon** - "the descendants of Ammon" or "the Ammon people"
