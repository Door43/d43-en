===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the men of the city, the men of Sodom**  - "the men of the city, that is, the men of Sodom" or just "the men of the city of Sodom"
  * **both young and old**  - This means "men of all ages" and refers to the men of Sodom surrounding the house. This could be translated "from the youngest to the oldest."
  * **that came in to you**  - "that came in to your house" or "that entered unto you."
  * **have relations with them**  - "have sex with them" (UDB)
