===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lot went out**  - This could be translated "So Lot went out."
  * **sons-in-law**  - They had legally promised to marry his daughters, but the marriages had not yet taken place. In the culture of that day, there was a long waiting period between the engagement ceremony, which was legally binding, and the actual marriage.
  * **Get going**  - "Go now"
  * **so you are not swept away**  - or "or else you will be swept away."
  * **swept away**  - "destroyed" or "wiped out"
