===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Lot went out** - "So Lot left the house"
  * **his sons-in-law, the men who had promised to marry his daughters** - The phrase "the men who had promised to marry his daughters" explains what "sons-in-law" means here. AT: "the men who were going to marry his daughters" or "his daughters' fianc√©s" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **When the morning dawned** - "Just before the sun came up"
  * **Get going** - "Go now"
  * **you are not swept away in the punishment of the city** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "so Yahweh does not also destroy you when he punishes the people of this city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **not swept away in the punishment** - God destroying the people of the city is spoken of as if a person were sweeping away dust. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **of the city** - Here "city" stands for the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
