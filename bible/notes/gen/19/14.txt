===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lot went out**  - This could be translated "So Lot left the house."
  * **his sons-in-law, the men who had promised to marry his daughters**  - The phrase "the men who had promised to marry his daughters" explains what "sons-in-law" means here. It could also be translated as "the men who were going to marry his daughters" or "his daughters' fianc√©s"
  * **Get going**  - "Go now"
  * **you are not swept away in the punishment of the city**  - "so Yahweh does not also destroy you when he punishes the the people of this city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
