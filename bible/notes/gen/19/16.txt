===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But he lingered**  - "But Lot kept waiting." The pronoun "he" refers to Lot.
  * **So the men grabbed**  - "So the two angels grabbed"
  * **was merciful to him**  - or "had compassion on him."
  * **they had brought them out**  - "the two men had brought Lot's family out"
  * **Flee for your lives!**  - "Run to save your lives!" or "Flee for your life!" (singular)
