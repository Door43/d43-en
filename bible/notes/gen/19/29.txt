===== Translation Notes: =====

Verse 29 is a summary of of this chapter.

  * **God called Abraham to mind** - "God remembered to be merciful to Abraham." This tells why God rescued Lot.
  * **out of the midst of the destruction** - AT: "away from the destruction" or "out of the area that was being destroyed" or "away from danger"
