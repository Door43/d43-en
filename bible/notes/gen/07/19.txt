===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The waters forced themselves higher and higher on the earth** - This can also be translated as "The waters came more and more powerfully on the earth" or "The water totally overwhelmed the earth."
  * **the whole heaven**  - "the entire sky."
  * **fifteen cubits** - "six meters." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:06:13|6:15]].
