===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So every living thing ... was wiped out** - If necessary, this can be stated in active form. AT: "So every living thing ... perished" or "So the flood completely destroyed every living thing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **They were all destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God destroyed them all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from the earth** - "so they were no longer on the earth"
  * **those with him** - "the people and animals that were with him"
  * **were left** - "remained" or "lived" or "remained alive" (UDB)
  * **The water dominated the earth** - "The deep water covered all the earth" or "The waters remained at full flood on the earth" (UDB)
