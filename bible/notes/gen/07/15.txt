===== Translation Notes: =====

Verses 13-18 repeat for a third time and give more detail about how Noah went into the ark with his family and the animals in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:07:01|7:1-5]]. This is not a new event.

  * **Two of all flesh**  - This refers to the animals.
  * **in which was the breath of life**  - AT: "that lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **male and female of all flesh**  - AT: "male and female of every kind of animal"
  * **after them**  - AT: "after they entered the ark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
