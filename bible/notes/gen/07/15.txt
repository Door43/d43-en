===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verses 13-18 repeat for a third time and give more detail about how Noah went into the ark with his family and the animals in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:07:01|7:1-5]]. This is not a new event.

  * **Two of all flesh** - Here "flesh" represents animals. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in which was the breath of life** - Here "breath" represents life. AT: "that lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **came to Noah** - The word "came" can be translated as "went." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **of all flesh** - Here "flesh" represents animals. AT: "of every kind of animal" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **after them** - The full meaning can be stated explicitly. AT: "after they entered the ark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
