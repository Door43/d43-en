===== translationNotes: =====

  * **moved upon** - "moved about" or "roamed"
  * **all the swarming creatures that swarmed upon the earth** - This refers to all the animals that move around on the ground in large groups.
  * **All beings in whose nostrils was the breath of the spirit of life** - "all beings that had the breath of life in their noses." The "nostrils" represent the whole person. This included animals and humans. AT: "every creature that breathed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the breath of the spirit of life** - The words "breath," "spirit," and "life" all represent the power that causes people and animals to be alive. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all those of the dry land** - "all creatures that lived on dry land." This tells something more about the creatures that had the breath of life. AT: "and who lived on dry land."
  * **died** - This refers to physical death.
