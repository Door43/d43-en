===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **moved**  - "moved about" or "roamed"
  * **all the swarming creatures that swarmed upon the earth**  - This refers to all the animals that move around on the ground in large groups. It can also be translated as "all the animals that creep around on the earth."
  * **All beings in whose nostrils was the breath of the spirit of life**  - The could also be translated as "all beings that had the breath of life in their noses" or "every creature that breathed." This included animals and humans.
  * **all those of the dry land**  - "all creatures that lived on dry land." This tells something more about the creatures that had the breath of life. It can also be translated as "and who lived on dry land."
