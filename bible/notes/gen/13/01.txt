===== translationNotes: =====

  * **went up from** - "left" or "departed from"
  * **went into the Negev** - The Negev was a desert region in southern Canaan, west of Egypt. This can be made explicit. AT: "went back to the Negev desert" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Abram was very rich in animals, in silver, and in gold** - "Abram had many animals, much silver, and much gold"
  * **animals** - "livestock" or "cattle"
