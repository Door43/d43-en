===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lived**  - "settled" or "stayed"
  * **the land of Canaan**  - This could also be translated as "the land of the Canaanites."
  * **he set up his tents as far away as Sodom**  - Possible meanings are 1) "he set up his tents near Sodom" (UDB) or 2) "he moved his tents around in an area that reached all the way to Sodom."
