===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **east**  - or "in the direction of the east" or "eastward."
  * **the whole plain of the Jordan** - this refers to the flood-plain of the Jordan River.
  * **well-watered** - meaning: enough clean water to support populations of livestock and people
  * * **like the garden of Yahweh, like the land of Egypt** - alternatively: "like the garden of Yahweh or like the land of Egypt".
  * **garden of Yahweh** - this refers to the Garden of Eden ([[:en:bible:notes:gen:02:07|Gen 2:8]])
  * **This was before Yahweh destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah** - This anticipates a part of the story that will be told later in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:19:23|Gen 19:23-25]].
  * **the relatives** - "the kinsmen" or "the families". This refers primarily to Lot and Abram.
