===== translationNotes: =====

  * **heard it** - "heard what they were saying"
  * **from their hand** - The phrase "their hand" refers to the brothers' plan to kill him. AT: "from them" or "from their plans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Let us not take his life** - The phrase "take his life" is a euphemism for killing someone. AT: "Let us not kill Joseph" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **Shed no blood** - The negation can be placed on the verb. Also, "shedding blood" is a euphemism for killing someone. AT: "Do not spill any blood" or "Do not kill him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **lay no hand upon him** - This means not to harm or injure him. AT: "do not harm him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that he might rescue him** - This can be translated as a new sentence: "Rueben said this so that he might rescue Joseph"
  * **out of their hand** - The phrase "their hand" refers to the brothers' plan to kill him. AT: "from them" or "from their plans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **to bring him back** - "and return him"
