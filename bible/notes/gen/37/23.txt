===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about that when**  - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **they stripped him of his tunic** - "they stripped off his tunic" or "they tore his tunic from him"
  * **his tunic, the tunic he wore that reached his hands and ankles**  - The repetition here reminds the reader about the special coat that Israel gave Joseph (see [[en:bible:notes:gen:37:03]]). You could also translate it: "his tunic. This was the tunic that he wore, the one that reached his hands and ankles."
  * **that reached his hands and ankles**  - "that was full length with long sleeves"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
