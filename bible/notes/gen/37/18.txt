===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They saw him from a distance** - "Joseph's brothers saw him while he was far away"
  * **they plotted against him to kill him** - "they made a plan to kill him"
  * **this dreamer is approaching** - "here comes the one who has the dreams"
  * **Come now, therefore, **- "So now"
  * **wild animal** - "dangerous animal" or "ferocious animal"
  * **has devoured him** - "has eaten him"
  * **We will see what will become of his dreams** - Alternate translation: "That way we will make sure his dreams do not come true."
