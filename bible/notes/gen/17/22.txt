===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When he had finished talking with him** - "When God had finished talking with Abraham"
  * **God went up from Abraham** - "God left Abraham"
  * **every male among the men of Abraham's household** - "every human male in Abraham's household" or "every male person in Abraham's household." It refers to human males of all ages: babies, boys, and men.
