===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He told all these things to them**  - "He told them everything that God had told him"
  * **What have you done to us?**  - Abimelech used this rhetorical question to accuse Abraham. It can also be translated as “You have done something bad to us!” or “Look what you have done to us!”  (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **to us** - The word "us" here is exclusive and does not include Abraham and Sarah. If your language uses an exclusive form of "we" or "us," use it here (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **How have I sinned against you** - Abimelech used this rhetorical question to remind Abraham that he had not sinned against Abraham. It can also be translated as "I have not sinned against you." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **that you have brought on me and on my kingdom a great sin** - This can be translated as "that you should make me and my kingdom guilty of such a terrible sin."   
  * **You have done to me that which ought not to be done.**  - "You should not have done this to me."
