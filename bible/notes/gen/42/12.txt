===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said to them** - "Joseph said to his brothers"
  * **No, but you have come to see the undefended parts of the land** - "No, you have come to find out where we are not guarding our land"
  * **twelve brothers** - "12 brothers" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the youngest is this day with our father** - "today the youngest is with our father"
  * **and one brother lives no longer** - "and one brother is no longer alive"
