===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a new part of the story. 
  * **Why do you look at one another?"** - Jacob uses a question to scold his sons for not doing anything about the grain. Alternate translation: "Do not just sit here!" (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **Lest harm may perhaps happen to him**  - "Something bad might happen to him"
