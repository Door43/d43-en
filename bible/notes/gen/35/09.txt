===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came from Paddan Aram** - See Genesis [[en:bible:notes:gen:25:19|25:20]] for how you translated "Paddan Aram."
  * **blessed** - Here "bless" means to pronounce a formal blessing on someone and to cause good things to happen to that person.
  * **but your name will no longer be called Jacob** - "but your name will no longer be Jacob"
