===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When Jacob came from Paddan Aram** - It can be made explicit that they were in Bethel. AT: "After Jacob left Paddan Aram, and while he was in Bethel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **blessed** - Here "bless" means to pronounce a formal blessing on someone and to cause good things to happen to that person.
  * **but your name will no longer be called Jacob** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "but your name will no longer be Jacob" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
