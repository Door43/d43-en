===== translationNotes: =====

  * **rose up from** - "set out from"
  * **in the carts** - "Carts" are wagons with two or four wheels. Animals pull the carts. See how you translated "carts" in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:45:19|45:19]].
  * **they had accumulated** - "they had acquired" or "they had gained"
  * **He brought with him** - "Jacob brought with him"
  * **his sons' sons** - "his grandsons"
  * **his sons' daughters** - "his granddaughters"
