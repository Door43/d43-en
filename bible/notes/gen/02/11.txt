===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pishon**  - This is the only time this river is referred to in the Bible. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]]).
  * **the whole land of Havilah**  - "the whole land called Havilah." It was somewhere in the Arabian Desert.
  * **where there is gold**  - This is a relative clause that describes Havilah. <font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Some languages would not use a relative clause for this.</font>    It cana also be translated as "There is gold in Havilah." (See the section on clauses in [[:en:ta:vocabulary:sentencestructure]])
  * **bdellium**  - This resin comes from a tree and smells nice. A resin is sticky stuff that comes out of some trees and can burn. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
  * **the onyx stone**  - "onyx stones." Onyx is a certain kind of beautiful stone.
