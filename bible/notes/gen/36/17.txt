===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Genesis 36:17-19**  - Esau, Anah, Reuel, Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, Mizzah, Jeush, Jalam, and Korah are male names. Basemath, Oholibamah are female names. Land of Edom is a place name. (see: [[en/ta/translate/translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
