===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were the descendants of Esau (also called Edom)** - "These are the descendants of Esau, who is also called Edom." This sentence introduces the account of Esau's descendants in Genesis 36:1-8. AT: "This is an account of the descendants of Esau, who is also called Edom." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Adah ... Oholibamah** - These are names of Esau's wives. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Elon the Hittite** - "Elon the descendant of Heth" or "Elon a descendant of Heth." This is the name of a man. See how you translate this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:26:34|26:34]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anah ... Zibeon ... Nebaioth** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Hivite** - This refers to a larger group of people. See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:10:15|10:17]].
  * **Basemath** - This is the name of one of Esau's wives. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:26:34|26:34]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Nebaioth** - This is the name of one of Ishmael's sons. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:28:08|28:9]].  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
