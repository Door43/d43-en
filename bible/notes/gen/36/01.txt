===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These were the descendants of Esau (also called Edom)** - "These are the descendants of Esau, who is also called Edom." This sentence introduces the account of Esau's descendants in Genesis 36:1-8. Alternate translation: "This is an account of the descendants of Esau, who is also called Edom."
  * **Adah...Oholibamah** - These are names of Esau's wives. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Elon the Hittite** - See how you translate this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:26:34|26:34]].
  * **Anah ... Zibeon ... Nebaioth** - These are names of men. 
  * **Hivite** - This refers to a larger group of people. See how you translated it in [[en:bible:notes:gen:10:15|10:17]].
  * **Basemath** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:26:34|26:34]].
  * **Nebaioth** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:28:08|28:9]].
