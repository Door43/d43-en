===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Leah conceived again**  - "Leah became pregnant again"
  * **and bore a sixth son to Jacob.**  - "and gave birth to a sixth son for Jacob."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **She called his name Zebulun**  - "So she called him Zebulun" or "So she named him Zebulun." Zebulun means "honor."
  * **Afterwards she bore a daughter**  - "Afterwards she gave birth to a daughter"
  * **and called her name Dinah.**  - "and named her Dinah."
