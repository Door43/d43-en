===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Leah conceived again** - "Leah became pregnant again"
  * **bore a sixth son to Jacob** - "gave birth to a sixth son for Jacob"
  * **She called his name Zebulun** - Translators may also add a footnote that says: "The name Zebulun means 'honor.'"
  * **called her name Dinah** - This is the name of Leah's daughter. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
