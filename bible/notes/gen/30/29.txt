===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jacob said to him** - "Jacob said to Laban"
  * **how your livestock have fared with me** - "how well your livestock have done since I started taking care of them"
  * **For you had little before I came**  - "your herds were small before I worked for you"
  * **and it has increased abundantly**  - "but now your wealth has greatly increased"
  * **Now when will I provide for my own household also?**  - "Now when will I take care of my own family?" This can be translated as a statement: "Now I want to take care of my family."
