===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Bilhah conceived** - This is the name of Rachel's female servant. See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:gen:30:03|30:3]].
  * **bore Jacob a son ** - "gave birth to a son for Jacob"
  * **God has listened to me**  - This can be translated as "God has judged me"
  * **heard my voice **- Here "my voice" is a synecdoche and can be translated as "heard me." (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
  * **she called his name** - "Rachel called his name" 
  * ** called his name Dan** - Translators may also add a footnote that says "The name Dan means 'he judged.'"
