===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When Leah saw that**  - "When Leah became aware that"
  * **she took Zilpah, her servant, and gave her to Jacob as a wife**  - "she gave Zilpah, her servant, to Jacob as a wife."
  * **Zilpah** - This is the name of Leah's female servant. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **bore Jacob a son.**  - "gave birth to a son for Jacob"
  * **This is fortunate!**  - "How fortunate!" or "What good luck!"
  * **called his name Gad**  - Translators may also add a footnote that says “The name Gad means 'fortunate.'”
