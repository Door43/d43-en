===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Laban said to him** - "Laban said to Jacob"
  * **If now I have found favor in your eyes** - The phrase "in your eyes" refers Jacob's thoughts or opinion. The idiom "find favor" means that someone is approved of by someone else. Alternate translation: "If I have found favor with you" or "If you are pleased with me" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **wait** - "please stay"
  * **I have divined** - "I have discovered by my own spiritual practices"
  * **for your sake** - "because of you"
  * **Name your wages** - This can be made more explicit. AT: "tell me how much I have to pay to keep you here" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]).
