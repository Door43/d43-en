===== Important Terms: =====
 

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **My integrity will testify for me later on**  - "My integrity will speak for itself later on, because" or "My reliability will prove itself later on"
    * **integrity** - "reliability" or "honesty"
    * **Every one that**  - This can be made more explicit: "Every one of my flock that" (see [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]).
    * **and black among the sheep**  - "or not black among the sheep"
    * **if any are found with me, will be considered to be stolen**  - You can make this active: "if you find any of those among my possessions, you can consider it stolen" (see [[:en:ta:translate:active]]).
    * **found with me**  - "found among my possessions"
    * **Let it be according to your word**  - "it will be as you say" or "we will do what you have said" [UDB]
