===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bilhah ... conceived again** - "Bilhah ... became pregnant again"
  * **bore Jacob a second son** - "gave birth to a second son for Jacob"
  * **With mighty wrestlings have I wrestled with my sister** - The phrase "wrestlings have I wrestled" is an idiom used for emphasis. It is also a metaphor that speaks of Rachel's attempts to have a child like her sister as if she were having a physical fight with Leah. AT: "I have had a great struggle to have children like my older sister, Leah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **have prevailed** - "I have won" or "I have succeeded"
  * **called his name Naphtali** - Translators may also add a footnote that says "The name Naphtali means 'my struggle.'"
