===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After Rachel had borne Joseph** - "After Rachel gave birth to Joseph"
  * **and let me go** - "so I can go"
  * **you know the service I have given you** - "you know that I have served you enough." Jacob is reminding Laban of their contract ([[:en:bible:notes:gen:29:26|Gen 29:27]]).
