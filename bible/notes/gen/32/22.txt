===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his two women servants** - "his two servant wives." This means Zilpah and Bilhah.
  * **ford** - a shallow place in a river that is easy to cross
  * **Jabbok** - This is the name of a river. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **all his possessions** - "all that he had"
