===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **O God of my father Abraham, and God of my father Isaac, Yahweh**  - This does not refer to different gods, but God as he is known in three different ways, and can be translated: "O Yahweh, God of my father Abraham and my father Isaac."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **I will prosper you**  - "I will make you prosperous" or "I will give you good fortune" or "I will do good to you" or "I will treat you well."
  * **faithfulness that you have done for**  - "faithfulness that you have shown to"
  * **faithfulness **- "trueness."
  * **your servant**  - This is a polite way of saying "me."
  * **two camps**  - This refers to the splitting of Jacob's group in two in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:32:06|Gen 32:7]].
