===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **deliver**  - "rescue" or "save"
  * **from the hand of my brother, from the hand of Esau**  - "from the hand of my brother, Esau"
  * **the hand of**  - "the power of" or "the control of"
  * **I am afraid of him, that he will**  - "I am afraid that he will"
  * **attack me, the mothers with the children**  - The word "me" here includes everyone under Jacob's leadership: "attack my people, even the mothers and children."
  * **make you prosper **- "do good to you" or "treat you well."
  * **which cannot be numbered for their number**  - "which you cannot count because of their number"
