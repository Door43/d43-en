===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hip** - "thigh socket." This refers to the place where the upper leg bone connects to the hip.
  * **Jacob's hip was dislocated as he wrestled with him** - Alternate translation: "The man injured Jacob's hip as he wrestled with him" or "The man caused Jacob's thigh bone to pull away from the hip socket." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
