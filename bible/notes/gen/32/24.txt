===== translationNotes: =====

  * **until daybreak** - "until dawn" (UDB)
  * **hip** - "thigh socket." This is the place where the upper leg bone connects to the hip.
  * **Jacob's hip was dislocated as he wrestled with him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The man injured Jacob's hip as he wrestled with him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for the dawn is breaking** - "the sun will rise soon"
  * **bless** - Here "bless" means to pronounce a formal blessing on someone and to cause good things to happen to that person.
  * **I will not let you go unless you bless me** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "Absolutely not! You must bless me first, then I will let you go" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
