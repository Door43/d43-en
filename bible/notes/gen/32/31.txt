===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That is why to this day** - This marks a change from the story to background information about the descendants of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **ligaments of the hip** - This refers to the muscle that connects the thigh bone to the thigh socket.
  * **hip joint** - "thigh socket"
  * **while dislocating** - "while striking"
