===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He said** - "Jacob replied"
  * **brought it to me** - This is an idiom meaning that God caused it to happen. AT: "helped me to succeed while hunting" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **whether you are my true son Esau or not** - "if you are really my son Esau"
