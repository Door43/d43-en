===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said** - "Jacob replied"
  * **How is it that you have found it so quickly** - "How did you find it so quickly"
  * **brought it to me**- "caused it to happen in front of me" or "granted me success"
  * **you are my true son Esau** - "you are really my son Esau"
