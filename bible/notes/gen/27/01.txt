===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his eyes were dim so that he could not see**  - The phrase "eyes were dim" is an idiom which means Isaac could no longer see well. This could be translated as "nearly blind." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:idiom|Idiom]])
  * **he called Esau, his older son and said to him**  - "he called to Esau, his oldest son, saying"
  * **He said to him**  - "And Esau replied"
  * **Here am I**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:22:01|22:1]]
  * **He said**  - "Then Isaac said"
  * **see here**  - The phrase "see here" adds emphasis to what follows. It could also be translated "listen carefully."
  * **I am old. I do not know the day of my death.**  - "I have grown old and do not know when I will die."
