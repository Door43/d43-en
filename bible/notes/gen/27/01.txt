===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his eyes were dim** - This speaks about being nearly blind as if the eyes were a lamp and the light has nearly gone out. AT: "he was nearly blind" or "he was almost blind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He said to him** - "And Esau replied" 
  * **Here I am** - "I am here" or "I am listening." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:22:01|22:1]].
  * **He said** - "Then Isaac said"
  * **See here** - The phrase "see here" adds emphasis to what follows. AT: "Listen carefully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **I do not know the day of my death** - It is implied that Isaac knows he will die soon. AT: "I may die any day now" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])  
  * **death** - This refers to physical death.
