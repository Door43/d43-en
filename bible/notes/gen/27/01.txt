===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his eyes were dim** - This means that Isaac could no longer see well. AT: "he was nearly blind" or "he was almost blind" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **He said to him** - "And Esau replied" 
  * **Here I am** - AT: "Yes?" or "What is it?" or "I am listening." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:22:01|22:1]]
  * **He said** - "Then Isaac said"
  * **See here** - The phrase "see here" adds emphasis to what follows. AT: "listen carefully"
  * **I am old. I do not know the day of my death** - AT: "I have grown old and do not know when I will die"
  * **death** - This refers to physical death.
