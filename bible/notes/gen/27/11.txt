===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am a smooth man**  - "I am a man with smooth skin" or "I am not hairy"
  * **Perhaps my father will touch me**  - "What if my father touches me?"
  * **and I will seem to him as a deceiver**  - This can be a new sentence: "He will know that I am deceiving him."
