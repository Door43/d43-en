===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am a smooth man** - AT: "I am a man with smooth skin" or "I am not hairy"
  * **and I will seem to him as a deceiver** - AT: "and he will think that I am a liar" or "he will know that I am deceiving him"
  * **I will bring a curse upon me and not a blessing** - AT: "Then because of what I do, he will curse me and not bless me"
