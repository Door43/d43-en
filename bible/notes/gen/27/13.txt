===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let any curse fall upon me**  - "Let your curse be on me, my son"
  * **obey my voice** - Rebekah said "my voice" to refer to what she was saying. This could be translated as "obey me." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **got the kids **  - "got the baby goats"
  * **made delicious food, just as his father loved**  - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:gen:27:03|27:04]].
