===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jacob went to his father** - "Jacob came to his father"
  * **He said** - "And his father replied"
  * **Here am I** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:22:01|22:1]].
  * **I have done as you commanded me**  - "I have done what you told me to do"
  * **that your soul might bless me**  - Jacob uses the word "soul" to refer to his father, Isaac. This could be translated as "so that you may bless me." (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
