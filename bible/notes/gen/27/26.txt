===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and he smelled**  - "and Isaac smelled"
  * **the smell** - "the scent"
  * **and blessed him**  - "then he blessed him" This refers to the formal blessing a father pronounces on his children.
  * **that Yahweh has blessed** - Here the word "blessed" means that Yahweh has causes good things to happen to the field and it has become fruitful.
