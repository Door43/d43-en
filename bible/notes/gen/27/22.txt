===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Jacob went over to Isaac his father** - "Jacob approached Isaac his father"
  * **The voice is Jacob's voice** - Isaac is equating Jacob's voice with Jacob himself. AT: "You sound like Jacob" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **but the hands are the hands of Esau** - Isaac is equating Esau's hands with Esau himself. AT: "but your hands feel like Esau's hands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
