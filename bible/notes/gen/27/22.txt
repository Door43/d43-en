===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jacob went over to Isaac his father**  - "Jacob approached Isaac his father"
    * **The voice is Jacob's voice** - "The voice sounds like Jacob's voice"
    * **but the hands are the hands of Esau**  - "but the hands are Esau's hands" or "but the hands feel like Esau's hands"
