===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaac continues giving instructions to his older son Isaac.

  * **your weapons**  - "your gear" or "your equipment"
  * **your quiver**  - "your quiver of arrows" or "your arrows." A quiver is a case for holding arrows. 
  * **hunt game for me** - "hunt a wild animal for me"
  * **Make delicious food for me, the sort that I love** - The word "delicious" refers to something that tastes very good. This could be translated as "Cook for me the tasty meat that I love."
