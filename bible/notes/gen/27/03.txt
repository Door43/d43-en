===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your weapons**  - "your gear" or "your equipment"
  * **your quiver**  - A quiver is a case for holding arrows. This could be translated as "your quiver of arrows."
  * **your...you** - In verses 3-4 the pronouns "your" and "you" are singular and refer to Esau. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-singular]])
  * **me...I** - In verses 3-4 the pronouns "me" and "I" refer to Isaac. 
  * **Make delicious food for me, the sort that I love** - "Make for me the food that I love"
