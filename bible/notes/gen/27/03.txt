===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaac continues giving instructions to his older son Isaac.

  * **your weapons** - AT: "your gear" or "your equipment"
  * **your quiver** - A quiver is a case for holding arrows. AT: "your quiver of arrows" or "your arrows"
  * **hunt game for me** - AT: "hunt a wild animal for me"
  * **Make delicious food for me, the sort that I love** - The word "delicious" refers to something that tastes very good. AT: "Cook for me the tasty meat that I love"
  * **bless** - In Bible times, a father would often pronounce a formal blessing on his children.
