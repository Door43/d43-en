===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you…your**  - Here these pronouns are singular and refer to Jacob. But the blessing also applies to Jacob's descendants. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **Be master over your brother**  - "Become a master over your brothers"
  * **And may your mother’s sons bow down to you**  - "And your mother’s sons will bow down to you"
