===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bow down** - This means to bend over to humbly express respect and honor toward someone.
  * **you...your** - Here these pronouns are singular and refer to Jacob. But the blessing also applies to Jacob's descendants. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **Be master over your brothers** - "become a master over your brothers"
  * **and may your mother's sons bow down to you** - "and your mother's sons will bow down to you"
