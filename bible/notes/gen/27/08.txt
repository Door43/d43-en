===== Translation Notes: =====

Rebekah continues to speak to her younger son Jacob.

  * **obey my voice as I command you** - Rebekah said "my voice" to refer to what she was saying. AT: "obey me and do what I tell you" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will make delicious food from them for your father, just like he loves** - See how a similar sentence was translated in [[en:bible:notes:gen:27:03|27:4]].
  * **You will take it to your father** - Possible meanings are 1) "Then you will bring it to your father" or "Then take it to your father."
  * **so that he may eat it, so that he may bless you** - The word "bless" refers to the formal blessing a father pronounces on his children. AT: "and after he eats it, he will bless you"
  * **before his death** - "before he dies"
