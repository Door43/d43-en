===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **obey my voice as I command you**  - Rebekah said "my voice" to refer to what she was saying. This could be translated as "obey me and do what I tell you." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **I...me** - In 27:8-10 these pronouns refer to Rebekah.
  * **you** - In 27:8-10 the word "you" is singular and refers to Jacob. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-singular]])
  * **Go to the flock, and bring me two good kids from the goats**  - "Go to the herd, and bring me two baby goats"
  * **I will make delicious food from them for your father, just as he loves**  - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:gen:27:03|27:04]].
  * **You will take it to your father**  - "Then you will bring it to your father"
  * **that he may eat, so that he may bless you**  - "and after he eats it, he will bless you"
  * **before his death **- "before he dies"
