===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is not he rightly named Jacob?**  - This is a rhetorical question that emphasizes Esau's anger at Jacob. This could be translated as a statement: "Jacob is certainly the right name for my brother." (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **Jacob** - Translators may also add a footnote that says “The name Jacob means 'he grasps the heel.' This is a way of saying 'he deceives.'”
  * **my birthright**  - "my right as firstborn to inherit most of our father's wealth"
  * **What more can I do for you, my son?**  - This is a rhetorical question used for emphasis. This could be translated as a statement: "There is nothing else I can do for you."
