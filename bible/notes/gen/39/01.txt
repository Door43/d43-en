===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Joseph was brought down to Egypt**  - This can be translated with an active verb: "The Ishmaelites had taken Joseph to Egypt." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **He was a prosperous man**  - "He became a prosperous man"
  * **He was in the house**  - "He lived in the house" or "He worked in the house" (UDB)
  * **his Egyptian master**  - Joseph was now Potiphar's slave.
