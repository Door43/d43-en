===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These are the events concerning Isaac, Abraham’s son**  - This sentence introduces the account of Isaac's descendants in Genesis 25:19-35:29. It could be translated as “This is the account of the descendants of Isaac, Abraham's son."
  * **forty** - "40" (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]]) 
  * **when he took as his wife**  - "when he married"
  * **Bethuel**  - This is the name of a man. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **Syrian**  - This could also be translated as "Aramean"
  * **Paddan-aram** - This is the name of a land.
  * **the sister of Laban the Syrian**  - "and also the sister of Laban the Aramean"
