===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were the events concerning Isaac, Abraham's son** - This sentence introduces the account of Isaac's descendants in Genesis 25:19-35:29. AT: "This is the account of the descendants of Isaac, Abraham's son" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **forty years old** - "40 years old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **when he took as his wife Rebekah** - "when he married Rebekah"
  * **Bethuel** - Bethuel was Rebekah's father. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:22:20|22:22]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Paddan Aram** - This was another name for the region of Mesopotamia, which about the same location as modern Iraq. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
