===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **said to her**  - "said to Rebekah"
  * **Two nations are in your womb** - This is poetic language that means the two children will start two separate nations. This could be translated as "Two nations will come from the twins within you."
  * **And two peoples will be separated from within you** - This can be translated with an active verb: "And those two nations will separate from each other even while you are giving birth to them." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
