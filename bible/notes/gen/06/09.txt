===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These are the generations of Noah**  - This refers to what follows. The section of Genesis that follows tells about Noah and his sons and what they did.
  * **walked with God**  - Walking is a metaphor for living. “Noah walked with God” means that he had a close relationship with God. Translators may add a footnote that explains the meaning. Or it could be translated as “lived in union with God.”
  * **Noah became the father of three sons**  - "Noah had three sons"
  * **Shem, Ham, Japheth**  - Translators may add the following footnote. “The sons are not listed in the order that they were born in.”
