===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The earth** - Possible meanings are 1) this is a metonym that represents the people who lived on the earth or 2) "The earth itself." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **was corrupt** - The writer speaks the way he would speak of food that has become rotten. AT: "was rotten" or "was completely evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **before God** - Possible meanings are 1) "in God's sight" or 2) see how "the presence of Yahweh" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:04:16|4:16]].
  * **and it was filled with violence** - The writer speaks of violence as if it were something that could be put into a container and of the earth as a container. AT: "there were very many violent people on the earth" or "because it was full of people who did evil things to each other" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **all flesh** - Here "flesh" represents human beings. AT: "all people"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **had corrupted their way** - How a person behaves is spoken of as if it were a way or road. AT: "they stopped living the way God wanted" or "they behaved in an evil way" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
