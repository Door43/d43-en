===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He dreamed** - "Jacob had a dream"
  * **set up on the earth** - "standing on the earth"
  * **heaven** - This refers to the place where God lives.
  * **Behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **Abraham your father** - "Abraham your ancestor" or "Abraham your forefather"
