===== translationNotes: =====

  * **vowed a vow** - "made a vow" or "solemnly promised God"
  * **If God will ... then Yahweh will be my God** - Jacob is speaking to God in the third person. This can be stated in the second person. AT: "If you will ... then you, Yahweh, will be the God that I will worship" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **on this road on which I am walking** - This stands for Jacob's journey to find a wife and to return home. AT: "on this journey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will give me bread to eat** - Here "bread" stands for food in general. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **to my father's house** - Here "house" stands for Jacob's family. AT: "to my father and the rest of my family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a sacred stone** - This means that the stone will mark the place where God appeared to him and it will be a place where people can worship God. AT: "God's house" or "God's place"
