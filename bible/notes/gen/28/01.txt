===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will not take**  - "Do not take"
  * **of the Canaanite women **- "from the daughters of Canaan"
  * **Arise, go **- "Go right away"
  * **take a wife**  - "take for yourself a wife"
  * **one of the daughters **- "from the daughters"
  * **your mother’s brother**  - "your uncle" or "your maternal uncle"
