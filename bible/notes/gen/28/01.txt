===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must not take** - "Do not take"
  * **Arise, go** - "Go right away"
  * **Paddan Aram** - This was another name for the region of Mesopotamia, which about the same location as modern Iraq. See how this was translated in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:25:19|25:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **house of** - This refers to a person's descendants or other relatives. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Bethuel** - Bethuel was Rebekah's father. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:22:20|22:22]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **your mother's father** - "your grandfather"
  * **one of the daughters** - "from the daughters"
  * **your mother's brother** - "your uncle"
