===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they brought the gifts which were in their hand** - Here "hand" stands for the entire person. AT: "the brothers brought the gifts they had with them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **bowed down before him** - This is a way of showing honor and respect. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
