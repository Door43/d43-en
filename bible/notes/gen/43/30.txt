===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hurried to go out of the room** - "hurried out"
  * **for he was deeply moved about his brother** - The phrase "deeply moved" refers to a strong feeling or emotion a person gets when something important happens. Alternate translation: "for he had strong feelings of compassion for his brother" or "for he had strong feelings of affection for his brother."
  * **saying** - "and said"
