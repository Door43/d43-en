===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The men were afraid** - "Joseph's brothers were afraid"
  * **they were brought to Joseph's house** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they were going into Joseph's house" or "the steward was taking them into Joseph's house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **It is because of the money that was returned in our sacks the first time we were brought in** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The steward is bringing us into the house because of the money that someone put back in our sacks" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that he may seek an opportunity against us. He might arrest us** - This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "He is waiting for the opportunity to accuse us, so that he might arrest us"
  * **we came down** - It is common to use the phrase "came down" when speaking of traveling from Canaan to Egypt.
