===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The places in 14:1-2 are all independent cities. (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **It came about** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **in the days of** - "in the time of"
  * **they made war** - "they went to war" or "they started a war" or "they prepared for war"
