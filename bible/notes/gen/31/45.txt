===== translationNotes: =====

  * **pillar** - This means that a large stone was simply set up on its end to mark the place where this important event happened.
  * **made a pile** - "stacked them on top of each other"
  * **Then they ate there by the pile** - Eating a meal together was a part of making the covenant with one another. The full meaning of this may be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Jegar Saha Dutha** - Translators may add a footnote that says: "The name Jegar Saha Dutha means 'heap of witness' in Laban's language." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Galeed** - Translators may add a footnote that says: "The name Galeed means 'heap of witness' in Jacob's language. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
