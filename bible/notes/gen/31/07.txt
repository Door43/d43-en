===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ULB Gen 31:7; has deceived me** - "has lied to me" or "has not treated me fairly"
  * **ULB Gen 31:7; my wages** - "what he said he would pay me"
  * **ULB Gen 31:7; ten** - "10" (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **ULB Gen 31:8; The speckled animals** - "the animals with little spots"
  * **ULB Gen 31:8; the ringstreaked**  - "the animals with stripes"
  * **ULB Gen 31:9; In this way God has taken away the cattle of your father and given them to me**  - "This is how God gave your father's cattle to me."
