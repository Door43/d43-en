===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ULB Gen 31:4; Jacob sent and called Rachel and Leah to the field to his flock**  - "Jacob sent for Rachel and Leah and told them to meet him out in the field with the flocks."
  * **ULB Gen 31:5; and said to them**  - This can be a new sentence: "He said to them"
  * **ULB Gen 31:5; I see your father’s attitude toward me has changed**  - "I have noticed your father is no longer pleased with me"
  * **ULB Gen 31:6; You know that it is with all my strength**  - "you" here refers to both Rachel and Leah (because "you" is feminine plural). It also adds emphasis. It could be translated as, "Both of you certainly know that it is with all my strength"
