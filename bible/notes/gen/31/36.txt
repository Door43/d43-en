===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He said to him** - "Jacob said to Laban"
  * **What is my offense? What is my sin, that you have hotly pursued after me?** - The phrases "What is my offense" and "What is my sin" mean basically the same thing. Jacob is asking Laban to tell him what he did wrong. AT: "What have I done wrong that you should pursue me like this?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **hotly pursued after me** - Here the word "hotly" means Laban urgently chased Jacob intending to capture him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **What have you found of all your household goods?** - "What have you found that belongs to you?"
  * **Set them here before our relatives** - Here the word "our" refers to Jacob's relatives and includes Laban's relatives. AT: "Lay anything you have found in front of our relatives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **they may judge between us two** - Here "two of us" refers to Jacob and Laban. The phrase "to judge between" means to decide which person is right in a dispute. AT: "they may judge between the two of us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
