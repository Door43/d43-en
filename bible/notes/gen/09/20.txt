===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **farmer**  - "man of the soil"
  * **became drunk**  - "drank too much wine."
  * **uncovered**  - The text is not specific about what was uncovered as Noah lay drunk. Because of his sons' reactions, it is presumed that certain parts of his body were exposed, and that was shameful.
