===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **farmer**  - "man of the soil"
  * **became drunk**  - "drank too much wine."
  * **uncovered**  - The text is not specific about how much of Noah's body was uncovered as he lay drunk. His sons' reactions show us that it was shameful.
