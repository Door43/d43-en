===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gave them provisions for the journey** - "gave them what they needed for traveling"
  * **he gave each man changes of clothing** - Each man received a set of clothes except for Benjamin who received 5 sets of clothes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **three hundred pieces** - "300 pieces" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **ten donkeys ... and ten female donkeys** - The donkeys were included as part of the gift. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
