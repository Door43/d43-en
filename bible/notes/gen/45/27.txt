===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **They told him** - "They told Jacob"
    * **all the words of Joseph that he had said to them** - "everything that Joseph had said to them"
    * **the spirit of Jacob their father revived** - The word "spirit" refers to Jacob. Alternate translation: "Jacob their father recovered" or "Jacob their father became very excited." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
