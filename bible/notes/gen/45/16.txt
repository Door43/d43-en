===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The news of the matter was told in Pharaoh’s house: "Joseph’s brothers have come."** - Alternate translation: "Everyone in Pharaoh's palace heard that Joseph's brothers had come." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

  * **and you will eat the fat of the land**- "and you will eat the best food in the land"
