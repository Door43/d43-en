===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the man**  - "the servant" (UDB)
  * **has not forsaken his covenant faithfulness and his trustworthiness**  - "has not stopped showing his covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness" or "has continued to be faithful and trustworthy"
  * **covenant faithfulness**  - This is faithfulness because of the covenant that God made with Abraham. It can be translated as "faithfulness because of his covenant” or "faithfulness" or "kindness because of his covenant."
  * **relatives**  - "family" or "clan"
