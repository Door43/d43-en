===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But if**  - Abraham is answering the servant's hypothetical question from [[:en:bible:notes:gen:24:05|24:5]]. Abraham thinks this is an unlikely, even impossible, situation. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hypothetical|Hypothetical Situations]])
  * **is not willing to follow you**  - "refuses to come with you"
  * **you will be free from this oath of mine**  - "you will be released from the oath you made to me"
  * **swore to him **- "made an oath with him"
  * **this matter**  - "Abraham's request"
