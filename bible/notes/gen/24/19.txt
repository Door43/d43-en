===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will draw water**  - "I will get water"
  * **camels**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:24:10|24:10]].
  * **So she hurried and emptied her pitcher**  - "So she quickly emptied her pitcher"
  * **the trough**  - "the animals' water trough" (UDB). A trough is a long open container for holding water for animals to drink.
