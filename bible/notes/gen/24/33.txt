===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They set** - Here, the word "they" refers to Laban's family members or to the household servants.
  * **set food before him** - "gave food to the servant"
  * **said what I have to say** - "spoken my words" or "told you why I am here"
  * **he has become great** - Here the word "he" refers to Abraham.
  * **become great** - "become very wealthy"
  * **He has given** - The word "he" refers to Yahweh.
