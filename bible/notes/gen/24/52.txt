===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their words** - "Laban and Bethuel's words." Here "words" stands for what they said. AT: "what Laban and Bethuel said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **he bowed himself down** - Bowing down before God is an expression of worship to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **articles of silver and articles of gold** - "silver and gold items" or "things made of silver and gold"
  * **precious gifts** - "expensive gifts" or "valuable gifts"
