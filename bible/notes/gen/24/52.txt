===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **their words**  - "Laban and Bethuel's words" or "what Laban and Bethuel said"
  * **he bowed himself down** - Bowing down before God is an expression of worship to him.
  * **articles of silver and articles of gold** - "silver and gold items" or "things made of silver and gold"
  * **precious** - "expensive" or "valuable"
