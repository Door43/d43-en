===== Translation Notes: =====

Abraham's servant continues speaking to Rebekah's family.

  * **bore a son to my master** - "gave birth to a son"
  * **My master made me swear, saying** - "My master made me swear, and he said." AT: "My master made me swear that I would do what he told me, and he said."
  * **from the daughters of the Canaanites, in whose land I make my home** - "from these Canaanites that we live among"
