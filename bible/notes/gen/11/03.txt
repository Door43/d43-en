===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **burn them thoroughly **  - "bake them completely". Bricks made out of clay were heated in an oven to make them harder and stronger just as they still are today.
  * **tar**  - a sticky substance that comes up from the ground.
  * **mortar**  - a thick substance used to stick or join bricks together
  * **let us make a name for ourselves**  - "let us make our reputation great"
  * **name**  - "reputation"
  * **if we do not**  - This can be made more explicit: "if we do not build a city to live in" (see [[:en:ta:discourse:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]]).
  * **face**  - "surface"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
