===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now these were the descendants of Terah** - This sentence introduces the account of Terah's descendants. Genesis 11:27-25:11 tells about Terah's descendants, particularly his son Abraham. AT: "This is the account of Terah's descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Haran died in the presence of his father Terah** - This means that Haran died while his father was still living. AT: "Haran died while his father, Terah, was with him" or "Haran died while his father, Terah, was still living" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
