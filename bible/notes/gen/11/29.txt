===== translationNotes: =====

  * **took wives** - "married wives"
  * **Iskah** - This is a female name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Now** - This word is used to introduce new information about Sarai that will become important in later chapters.
  * **barren** - This term describes a woman who is physically unable to conceive or bear a child. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
