===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **brought his sack down**  - "lowered his sack"
  * **the oldest…the youngest**  - The word "brother" is understood. Alternate translation: "the oldest brother…the youngest brother." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis|Ellipsis]])
  * **and the cup was found in Benjamin's sack**  - This can be translated as a new sentence. Alternate translation: "The steward found the cup in Benjamin's sack." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **Then they tore their clothes**  - The word "they" refers to the brothers. See how you translated the similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:37:29|37:29]].
  * **and returned**  - "and they returned"
