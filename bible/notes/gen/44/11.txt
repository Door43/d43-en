===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brought his sack down** - "lowered his sack"
  * **the oldest ... the youngest** - The word "brother" is understood. AT: "the oldest brother ... the youngest brother" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **youngest, and the cup was found in Benjamin's sack** - This can be translated as a new sentence and in active form. AT: "youngest. The steward found the cup in Benjamin's sack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Then they tore their clothes** - The word "they" refers to the brothers. Tearing clothes was a sign of great distress and sorrow. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]]) 
  * **returned** - "they returned"
