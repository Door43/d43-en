===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The morning dawned** - "The morning light shown"
  * **the men were sent away, they and their donkeys** - "the men, with their donkeys, were sent on their way"
  * **follow after** - "pursue"
  * **Why have you returned evil for good?**  - "Why have you repaid evil for good?"
  * **You have done evil in what you have done**  - "What you have done is evil"
  * **"this cup"**  - "this"; the word "this" refers to the "cup" or "drinking bowl"
