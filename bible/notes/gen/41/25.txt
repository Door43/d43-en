===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The dreams of Pharaoh are the same** - It is implied that the meanings are the same. AT: "Both dreams mean the same thing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **What God is about to do, he has declared to Pharaoh** - Joseph speaks to Pharaoh in the third person. This is a way of showing respect. It can be stated in the second person. AT: "God is showing you what he will soon do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **seven good heads** - The words "of grain" are understood. AT: "seven good heads of grain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
