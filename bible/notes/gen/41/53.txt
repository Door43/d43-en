===== translationNotes: =====


  * **in all lands** - In all the surrounding nations beyond Egypt, including the land of Canaan.
  * **but in all the land of Egypt there was food** - It is implied that there was food because of Joseph's plan to store food during the seven good years. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
