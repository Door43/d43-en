===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **there is none so discerning** - "there is no one as discerning"  
  * **discerning** - See how you translated this word in 41:33.
  * **You shall be over my house** - "You will have authority over my kingdom" 
  * **according to your word will all my people be ruled** - "you will rule over my people" (See: en:ta:translate:active)
  * **Only in the throne** - "Only in my role as king"
  * **See** - "Look" or "Pay attention"
  * **I have put you over all the land** - "I have given you authority over my kingdom"
