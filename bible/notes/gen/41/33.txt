===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now let Pharaoh look for a man discerning and wise, and put him over the land of Egypt. Let Pharaoh do this: let him appoint overseers over the land.**  - These are repeated phrases to emphasize to Pharaoh the importance of this. an alternate translation could be "Pharoah must find a wise leader and place in charge of Eqypt's production, and storage. (see: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
  * **Let Pharaoh do this: let him appoint**  - "Let Pharaoh proceed to appoint"
