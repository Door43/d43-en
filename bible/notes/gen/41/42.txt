===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He had him ride in the second chariot which he possessed. **  - It is likely that no one but Pharaoh had ridden in this spare chariot. Placing Joseph in it while introducing him to Egypt in his new role was a brilliant job of positioning Joseph for success.
