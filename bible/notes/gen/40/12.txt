===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is the interpretation of it** - "Here is what the dream means"
  * **Within three days** - "In three more days"
  * **will raise your head** - Alternate translation: "will raise up your head to take away your shame"
  * **and restore you to your office** - "and he will allow you to serve him again"
