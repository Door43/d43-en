===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is the interpretation of it** - "Here is what the dream means"
  * **The three branches are three days** - "The three branches represent three days"
  * **Within three days** - "In three more days"
  * **will lift up your head** - Here Joseph speaks of Pharaoh releasing the cupbearer from prison as if Pharaoh were causing him to lift up his head. AT: "will release you from prison" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **restore you to your office** - "will give you back your job"
  * **just as when** - The missing words may be added. AT: "just as you did when" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
