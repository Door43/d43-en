===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jacob kissed Rachel** - This is a greeting kiss between two relatives of opposite gender who have never met before. If your language has an affectionate first time greeting of a relative that is a kiss , use that. If not, use what is appropriate.
  * **her father’s relative**  - "related to her father"
