===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **conceived**  - "became pregnant"
  * **bore a son**  - "gave birth to a son"
  * **she called his name Simeon**  - The name Simeon sounds the the Hebrew work for "heard."
  * **she said**  - "Leah said"
  * **Yahweh has heard that I am unloved**  - "Yahweh has heard that I am disliked" or "Yahweh has heard that I am hated"
  * **my husband be attached to me**  - "my husband embrace me"
