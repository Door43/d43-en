===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jacob said to them**  - This can be made more explicit: "Jacob said to the shepherds"
  * **and, look there, Rachel his daughter is coming with the sheep**  - "Now look! Rachel his daughter is coming with the sheep"
  * **Laban the son of Nahor **- "Laban a descendant of Nahor" Note: Laban was a grandson of Nahor. Hebrew often uses the word "son" to refer to descendants.
