===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the evening, Laban took Leah his daughter**  - "But in the evening, Laban took Leah his daughter"
  * **went to bed with her**  - "had sex with her" - This implies that they were married.  See Explicit and Implicit Information
  * **behold**  - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **it was Leah**  - This can be made more explicit: "it was Leah who was in bed with him"
  * **What is this you have done to me?**  - "What have you done to me?"
