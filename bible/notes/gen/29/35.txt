===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **She conceived again** - "Leah became pregnant again"
  * **bore a son**  - "gave birth to a son"
  * **this time will I**  - "this time I will"
  * **she called his name Judah** - Translators may also add a footnote that says "The name Judah means 'praise.'"
