===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is not our custom to give** - "In our family we do not give"
  * **Complete the bridal week of this daughter**  - "Finish celebrating Leah's bridal week"
  * **and we will give you the other also** - The full meaning can be made explicit: "and next week we will give you Rachel also." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
