===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his sister's son** - "his nephew"
  * **embraced him** - "hugged him"
  * **kissed him** - This is a greeting kiss between 2 male relatives who have never met before. If your language has an affectionate first time greeting of a male relative that is kiss, use that. If not, use what is appropriate.
  * **Jacob told Laban all these things** - "then Jacob told Laban everything he told Rachel"
  * **my bone and my flesh** - This phrase means they are directly related. Alternate translation: "my relative" or "a member of my family." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
