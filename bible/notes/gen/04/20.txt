===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He was the father of those who lived in tents**  - Possible meanings are 1) He was the first person to live in a tent" or 2) "He and his descendants lived in tents."
  * **who lived in tents who have livestock**  - This refers to people who both live in tents and also take care of cattle.
  * **He was the father of those who play the harp and pipe**  - Possible meanings are 1) "He was the first person to play the harp and pipe" or 2) "He and his descendants played the harp and pipe."
  * **Tubal Cain, the forger of tools of bronze and iron**  - "Tubal Cain. He made bronze and iron tools" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **iron**  - a very strong grey metal
