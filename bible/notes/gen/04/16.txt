===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Nod**  - Translators may add a footnote saying "The word Nod means 'wandering.'"
  * **had marital relations** - “had sex with.” Your language may have a way of saying this politely. Some languages say “knew” or “slept with.” (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]]). See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:04:01|4:1]].
  * **He built a city**  - "Cain built a city"
