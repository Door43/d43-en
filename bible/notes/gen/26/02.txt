===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now Yahweh appeared** - Here "Now" introduces Yahweh into this part of the story.
  * **appeared to him** - "appeared to Isaac"
  * **Do not go down to Egypt** - It was common to speak of leaving the promised land as "going down" to another place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for to you and to your descendants, I will give all these lands** - "for I will give all these lands to you and your descendants"
  * **I will fulfill the oath that I swore to Abraham your father** - "I will do what I promised Abraham your father I would do"
