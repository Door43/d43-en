===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a new part of the story.
  * **a famine happened** - AT: "there was a famine" or "there was another famine." See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:26:01|12:10]].
  * **in the land** - Which land this refers to can be stated explicitly: "in the land where Isaac and his family lived" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **that had been in the days of Abraham** - The phrase "in the days" here refers to when Abraham lived. AT: "that had happened during Abraham's life" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
