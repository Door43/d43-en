  * **Now a famine happened**  - "Now there was a famine" or "Now there was another famine."
  * **in the land**  - "in the land where Isaac and his family lived"
  * **besides the first famine that had been in the days of Abraham**  - referring to the earlier famine, Genesis 12:10
  * **Abimilech**  - it is not known whether this is the same Abimilech that Abraham, Isaac's father knew.
