===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now**  - This word is used here to mark a new part of the story.
  * **a famine happened**  - "there was a famine" or "there was another famine"
  * **in the land**  - The land this refers to can be made explicit: "in the land where Isaac and his family lived." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]])
  * **that had been in the days of Abraham**  - The phrase "in the days" here refers to when Abraham lived. This can be translated as "that had happened during Abraham's life." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:idiom|Idiom]])
