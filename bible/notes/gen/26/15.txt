===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in the days of Abraham his father** - The phrase "in the days of" refers to the time when a person was alive. This could be translated as "when Abraham, his father, was living." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
    * **the Philistines stopped them and filled with earth**  - "the Philistines filled them with dirt"
    * **much mightier than we**  - "much stronger than we are"
    * **So Isaac departed** - "So Isaac and his household departed." Only Issac is mentioned because he is the leader, but his family and servants were with him. (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
