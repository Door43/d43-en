===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will multiply your descendants** - “I will cause your descendants to increase again and again” or “I will cause your descendants to be very many”
  * **like the stars of heaven** - This compares Isaac's descendants with the stars to emphasize the number of descendants Isaac will have. It can be translated as "beyond what anyone can count." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **all the nations of the earth will be blessed** - This can be translated with an active verb: "I will bless all the nations of the earth." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **obeyed my voice** - God uses "my voice" to refer to what he said. This can be translated as "obeyed what I said" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **my instructions, my commandments, my statutes, and my laws** - This can be translated as "all that I declared and all the laws that I gave him." (UDB)
