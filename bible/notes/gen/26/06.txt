===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So Isaac settled in Gerar** - "So Isaac and his family settled in Gerar." Only Isaac is mentioned because he is the leader of the family, but his whole family was with him. (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **He feared to say**  - "He was afraid to say"
  * **to get Rebekah**  - "so they can take Rebekah"
  * **He saw, behold, Isaac** - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows. This could be translated as "And, he was surprised to see that Isaac."
  * **was caressing**  - "was flirting with" or "was fondling."
