===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **multiply your descendants** - AT: "will cause your descendants to increase greatly" or "will cause your descendants to be very many"
  * **for my servant Abraham's sake** - "for my servant Abraham" or "because I promised my servant Abraham that I would do this" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Isaac built an altar there** - Why Isaac built an altar can be made explicit. AT: "Isaac built an altar there to sacrifice to Yahweh" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **called on the name of Yahweh** - "prayed in the  name of Yahweh" or "worshiped Yahweh." See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:12:08|12:8]].
