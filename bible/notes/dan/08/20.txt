===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In these verses, the angel explains to Daniel the symbolic meaning of the things he saw in his vision. Each of the animals and horns actually represented human rulers and kingdoms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])

  * **two horns—they are** - "two horns—they represent"
  * **The large horn between his eyes is** - "The large horn between his eyes represents"
