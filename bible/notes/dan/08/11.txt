===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **It became great, even as great as the divine commander of the army**  - "The goat's horn raised itself up as high as the divine commander of the army"
    * **The regular burnt offering was taken away from him**  - "It took away the regular burnt offering"
    * **and the place of the sanctuary was polluted**  - "and the it made the place of the sanctuary unclean"
    * **The horn will throw truth to the ground** - This means that the horn will ignore truth and godliness (See: Personification)
