====== Daniel 08 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The vision of the ram and male goat  ==

Daniel saw Greece overthrowing Media-Persia and then breaking up into four kingdoms. One kingdom stopped the temple worship for a while and then it was restored.
