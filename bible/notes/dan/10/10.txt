===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said to me** - Here the word "He" refers to the man in Daniel's vision.
  * **man who is greatly loved** - AT: "man whom God greatly loves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for I have been sent to you** - AT: "for God sent me to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
