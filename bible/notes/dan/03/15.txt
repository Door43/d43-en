===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Blazing furnace" - **  See note in 3:6
  * **"Who is the god who is able to rescue you from my hands?" - **  This is a question that does not need an answer. The king is threatening the three men. It means, "No god will be able to save you." (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **"You will be thrown into the  blazing furnace" - **"My soldiers will throw you into the fire" (See: Active)
