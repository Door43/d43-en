===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Did we not throw three men tied up into the fire?** - "We threw three men tied up into the fire, right?"
  * **The brilliance of the fourth is** - "Man four is giving off a very bright light" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **like a son of the gods** - The gods were believed to shine brightly with light.
