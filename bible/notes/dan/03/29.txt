There is no other god who can rescue people like this!'"

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **any people, nation, or language ... must be torn apart, and that their houses must be made into rubbish heaps** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "my servants will tear apart any people, nation, or language ... and make their houses into piles of garbage" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **speaks anything against the God** - "speaks words that do not respect the God" 
  * **must be torn apart** - "must have their bodies torn apart" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
