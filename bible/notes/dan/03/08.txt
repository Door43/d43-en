===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used to mark a break in the main story line. Here the writer tells about some new people in the story.
  * **King, live forever!** - This was a common greeting to the king.
  * **the horns, flutes ... and all kinds of music** - See how you translated this list in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:03:03|3:5]].
