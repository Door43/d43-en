===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Whoever does not fall down and worship must be thrown into a blazing furnace** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Soldiers will throw into a blazing furnace anyone who does not lie down on the ground and worship" (See: [[(en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **blazing furnace** - This is a large room where people burn things.
  * **affairs** - "matters" or "business" 
  * **Shadrach ... Meshach ... Abednego** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:01:06|1:7]].
  * **pay no attention to you** - "do not pay attention to you"
