\v 1 King Nebuchadnezzar made a golden statue. It was about twenty-seven meters tall and almost three meters wide. He set it up in the plain of Dura in the province of Babylon.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sixty cubits tall and six cubits wide**  - "about 27 meters tall and almost three meters wide" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **Plain of Dura**  - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **provincial governors, regional governors, local governors**  - These are ranks of authority over different sizes of territory.
  * **treasurers**  - These officials are in charge of money.
  * **the statue that he had set up**  - AT: "the statue that Nebuchadnezzar commanded his men to set up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
