===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the horns** - Horns are a symbol of power and represent powerful leaders.
  * **Three of the first horns were wrenched out by the roots**  - AT: "The little horn tore out three of the first horns" or "Someone tore out three of the first horns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a mouth that boasted about great things** - AT: “the horn had a mouth and spoke boastfully about great things” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
