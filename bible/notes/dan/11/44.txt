===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is still about the king of the North.

  * **he will go out with great rage** - AT: "he will be very angry and will go out" or "he will become very angry and will go out with his army" 
  * **the beautiful, holy mountain** - This refers to the hill in Jerusalem where God's temple was. See how you translated similar phrases in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:09:15|9:16]] and [[:en:bible:notes:dan:09:20|9:20]].  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
