===== Translation Notes: =====
This is still about the king of the North.


  * **he will go out with great rage** - AT: "he will be very angry and will go out" or "he will become very angry and will go out with his army" 
  * **the mountain of the beauty of holiness** - This refers to the hill in Jerusalem where God's temple was (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]). AT: "the beautiful, holy mountain." See how you translated similar phrases in [[en:bible:notes:dan:09:15|9:16]] and [[en:bible:notes:dan:09:20|9:20]].
