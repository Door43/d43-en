===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In Daniel 11:1 through 12:4, the one who was speaking to Daniel in chapter 10 tells him what is written in the book of truth. This is as he said he would do in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:10:20|10:21]].

  * **In the first year of Darius** - Darius was the King of the Medes. "The first year" refers to the first year that he was king. AT: "In the first year of the reign of Darius" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Three kings will arise in Persia** - "Three kings will rule over Persia"
  * **a fourth will be far richer than all the others** - "after them a fourth king will come into power who will have more money than the three before him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **power** - Possible meanings are 1) authority or 2) military power.
  * **he will stir up everyone** - "he will cause everyone to want to fight"
