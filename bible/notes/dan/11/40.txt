===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the king of the South** - AT: "the army of the king of the South" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The king of the North** - AT: "the army of the  king of the North"
  * **like a storm** - The violence of the army is compared to the destruction caused by a strong storm. AT: "violently" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **He will invade** - AT: "The king of the North will invade"
  * **like a flood** - AT: "it will come as quick as a flood with its destruction" [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]); See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:dan:11:10|11:10]]
  * **the glorious land** - referring to land of Israel; See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:dan:08:09|8:9]]
  * **many will stumble** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:dan:11:14|11:14]]
  * **escape from his hand** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:dan:08:03|8:4]]
