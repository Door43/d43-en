===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the king of the South**  - AT: "the army of the king of the South" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **The king of the North**  - AT: "the army of the king of the North"
  * **like a storm**  - The violence of the army is compared to the destruction caused by a strong storm. AT: "violently" or "violently like a storm" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **He will invade**  - AT: "The king of the North will invade"
  * **like a flood**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:11:10|11:10]]
  * **the glorious land**  - This refers to land of Israel. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:08:09|8:9]]
  * **many will stumble and fall **- AT: "many Israelites will die." See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:11:14|11:14]]
  * **will escape from his hand**  - “will escape from him” or “will escape from his power” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
