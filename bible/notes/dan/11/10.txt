===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel continued to explain to Daniel what was going to happen.

  * **will overflow like a flood **- AT: "the army will advance and cannot be stopped" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]).
  * **fortress** - a place difficult to attack
  * **all the way to his fortress** - It is not clear to whom the "his" refers.
