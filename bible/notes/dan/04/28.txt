===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verses 19-33 use the third person to describe the punishment of Nebuchadnezzar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])

  * **All these things ... in Babylon** - The information in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:04:28|4:28-29]] has been rearranged so its meaning can be more easily understood. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_versebridge]])
  * **Twelve months** - "12 months" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Is this not the great Babylon ... for the glory of my majesty?** - Nebuchadnezzar asks this question to emphasize his own glory. This can be translated as a statement. AT: "This is the great Babylon ... for the glory of my majesty!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **for the glory of my majesty** - "to show people my honor and my greatness"
