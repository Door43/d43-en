===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tree that you saw​** - This vision of the tree is explained in 4:11.
  * **this tree is you, king, - **  "this tree represents you, king,"
  * **Your greatness has grown and reaches to the heavens, and your authority reaches to the ends of the earth.** -   (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Your greatness has grown** - AT: "You have become more powerful" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Your greatness...reaches to the heaven... your authority reaches to the ends of the earth** - He is saying the king is very great and his authority very widespread. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
