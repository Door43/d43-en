  * **He shouted and said** - It can be made clear that the holy messenger was speaking to more than one person. AT: "He shouted to some people and said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Let the animals flee ... from its branches** - "The animals will be free to flee from under it and the bird will fly away from its branches"
