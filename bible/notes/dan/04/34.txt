===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and my sanity was given back to me** - AT: “and my sanity came back to me.” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

There are two examples of parallelism in this verse. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **I praised the most high God, and I honored and glorified the one who lives forever** - AT: "I praised and worshipped the Most High, and I honored him, the one who lives forever." (UDB)
  * **For his reign is an everlasting reign, and his kingdom endures from all generations to all generations** - AT: "He rules forever. His kingdom will never end."
