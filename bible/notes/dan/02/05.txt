===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This matter has been settled** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I have already decided what to do about this matter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **your bodies will be torn apart and your houses made into rubbish heaps** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will command my soldiers to tear your bodies apart and to make your houses into rubbish heaps" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you will receive gifts from me** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will give you gifts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
