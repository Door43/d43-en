===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the second year** - "In year two" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **he had dreams** - "Nebuchadnezzar had dreams"
  * **His mind was troubled** - Here "mind" refers to his thoughts. AT: "His thoughts disturbed him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and he could not sleep** - His troubled thoughts prevented him from sleeping. AT: "so that he could not sleep"
  * **Then the king summoned the magicians** - "Then the king called the magicians"
  * **the dead** - "people who had died"
  * **they came in** - "they came into the palace"
  * **stood before** - "stood in front of"
