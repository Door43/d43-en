===== translationNotes: =====

  * **fell on his face** - This symbolic act showed that the king was honoring Daniel. AT: "lay down with his face on the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **an offering be made and that incense be offered up to him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "his servants make an offering and offer up incense to Daniel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Truly your God** - "It is true that your God"
  * **the God of gods, the Lord of kings** - "greater than all the other gods, and King over all other kings" 
  * **the one who reveals mysteries** - Translate "the one who reveals mysteries" as in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:02:29|2:29]].
  * **to reveal this mystery** - "to reveal the mystery of my dream"
