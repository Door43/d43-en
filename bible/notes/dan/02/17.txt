===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his house** - This is Daniel's house.
  * **what had happened** - AT: "about the king's decree"
  * **He urged them to seek mercy** - "He begged them to pray for mercy"
  * **so that he and they might not be killed** - AT: "so that the king would not kill them" or "so that the king's bodyguard would not kill them" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
