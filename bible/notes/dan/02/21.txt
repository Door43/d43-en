===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses are also part of Daniel's prayer.

  * **he removes kings** - "he takes away kings' authority to rule"
  * **and places kings on their thrones** - The idea of kings being on their thrones refers to them ruling. AT: "and makes new kings rule over their kingdoms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the light lives with him** - "the light comes from where God is"
