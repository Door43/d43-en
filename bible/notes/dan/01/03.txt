===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ashpenaz** - This is the chief official. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **nobility** - This is the highest social class.
  * **without blemish** - These two negative words together emphasize a positive idea. AT: "who appeared perfect" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **filled with knowledge and understanding** - This is an idiom. This means they knew much and could organize and use that information. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **king's palace** - This is the large house or building where the king lives.
  * **He was to teach them** - "Ashpenaz was to teach them"
  * **delicacies** - special, rare, good foods
  * **trained** - "taught skills"
