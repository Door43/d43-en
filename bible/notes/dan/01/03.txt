===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ashpenaz**  - This is the name of the chief official (See: Translate Names)
  * **his chief official**  - This is the person in charge.
  * **nobility**  - These are members of a high social class.
  * **young men without blemish **- These are men that did not have any unwanted physical marks on their body.
  * **filled with knowledge and understanding**  - This means that they know a lot and can organize and use that information. (See: Idiom)
  * **king's palace**  - This is where the king lives.
  * **He was to teach them - **"He" refers to Ashpenaz, the chief official.
  * **delicacies**  - The food that was prepared for the king.
  * **trained for three years -**  AT: "instructed for 3 years"
