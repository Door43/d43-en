===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The king spoke with them** - The king spoke with the "four young men" ([[:en:bible:notes:dan:01:17|1:17]]).
  * **among the whole group there were none to compare with Daniel, Hananiah, Mishael, and Azariah** - AT: "Daniel, Hananiah, Mishael, and Azariah pleased him much more than anyone else in the whole group did"
  * **Daniel, Hananiah, Mishael, and Azariah** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:01:06|1:6-7]].
  * **ten times better** - AT: "much better" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
