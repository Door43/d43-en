===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brought a plan before the king** - "presented a plan to the king"
  * **may you live forever!** - This was a normal way to greet a king.
  * **for thirty days** - "for 30 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **whoever makes a petition** - "whoever makes a request"
  * **that person must be thrown into the den of lions** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "your soldiers must throw that person into the den of lions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **den of lions** - This may refer to a room or pen where lions were kept.
