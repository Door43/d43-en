===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they brought in Daniel** - "his soldiers went and got Daniel"
  * **lions' den** - This may refer to a room or pit where lions were kept. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:06:06|6:7]].
  * **May your God ... rescue you** - The king is expressing his desire for God to save Daniel.
  * **rescue you** - "save you from the lions"
