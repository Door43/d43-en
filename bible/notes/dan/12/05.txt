===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The angel has finished speaking, and Daniel goes on to tell what he saw next in this vision that began in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:10:04|10:4]].

  * **How long will it be to the end of these amazing events?** - This refers to the time from the beginning to the end of the events. AT: "How long will these amazing events last?"
  * **these amazing events** - At the time when the angel spoke to Daniel, none of the events in this vision had happened. This definitely refers to the the events in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:12:01|12:1-4]], and may possibly also include the events in the vision from chapter 11.
  * **the man clothed in linen** - This refers to the man who has been speaking to Daniel, not to one of the men who was standing beside the river.
