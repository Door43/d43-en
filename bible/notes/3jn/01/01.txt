===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The elder**  - This is referring to the writer of this letter, the apostle John.
  * **Gaius**  - This is a fellow believer to whom John is writing this letter.
  * **brothers **- "fellow believers."
  * **bore witness**  - This could be translated "told what they saw."
  * **my children**  - This is a metonymy which means "Those that John helped believe in Christ." (See: Metonymy)
  * **walk in truth**  - "That you are behaving in a manner that is consistent with God's truth" or "in a way that matches God's truth." (UDB)
