===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of John's letter to Gaius. He gives some final remarks and closes with a greeting.

  * **I do not wish to write them to you with pen and ink** - John does not wish to write these other things at all. He is not saying that he would write them with something other than pen and ink.
  * **face to face** - "together" or "in person"
  * **May peace be with you** - "May God give you peace"
  * **The friends greet you** - "The friends here greet you"
  * **Greet our friends there by name** - "Greet each of the believers there for me"
