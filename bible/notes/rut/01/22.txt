===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So Naomi and Ruth...** - This is a summary of statement. English marks this by the word "so". you need to determine how your language marks concluding or summary statements and do the same.
  * **returned out of the country of Moab**  - That is, "returned from the country of Moab".
  * **at the beginning of the barley harvest**  - This can also be translated as, "when the farmers were just beginning to harvest their barley crop".
