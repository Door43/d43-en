===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh**  - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the note on the Important Term page concerning how to translate this.
  * **She had heard in the region of Moab**  - That is, "while Naomi was living in Moab she heard".
  * **had helped his people in need**  - This is literally, “visited his people”. It means that God paid attention to his people, and he saw their need and provided for them. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:idiom|idiom]])
  * **went out from the place**** where she was**  - This can also be translated as, "left the place in Moab where she had been living". (See: implied information)
  * **daughters-in-law**  - the women who married Naomi's sons.
  * **they walked down the road**  - "they walked along the road".
