===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **what man does this young woman belong to?** - That is, "Whose daughter is that young woman?". He was asking whose family she belonged to.
  * **supervising** - This can also be translated as, "in charge of" or "managing".
  * **house** - That is, "hut" or "shelter". This was a temporary shelter or garden hut that provided shade from the sun.
