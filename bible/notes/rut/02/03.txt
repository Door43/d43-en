===== translationNotes: =====

  * **She happened to come** - Ruth was not aware that the field she picked to glean in belonged to Naomi's relative Boaz.
  * **Behold, Boaz** - The word "behold" alerts us to the important event of Boaz arriving at the field. Your language may also have a specific way of introducing important events or characters.
  * **came from Bethlehem** - The fields were an unspecified distance outside of Bethlehem.
  * **bless you** - "give you good things" or "make you happy"
