===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **left off his loyalty to the living and the dead** - Boaz remembered his obligations to Naomi as a family member and has shown favor and kindness to them in their need.
  * **kinsmen-redeemers**- A kinsman-redeemer was a close male relative that could redeem a childless widow by marrying her and having a child with her. He would also reacquire the land his relatives had lost due to poverty and redeem family members who had sold themselves into slavery.
