===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **glean**  - "Glean" means to pick up grain missed by the harvesters after they are finished with their job.
  * **ears of grain**  - The "ears of grain" are that part of the plant that contains the cereal.
  * **kinsman**  - A "kinsman" is a relative, one who belongs to the same clan.
  * **a mighty man of wealth**  - means that Boaz was prominent in his community, having wealth and a good reputation. "A man of wealth" could be translated as "a worthy man" or "a man of good character" or "a prominent man".
