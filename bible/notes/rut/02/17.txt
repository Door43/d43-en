===== translationNotes: =====

  * **beat out** - She separated the edible part of the grain from the hull and stalk, which is thrown away.
  * ** ears of grain** - This refers to the eatable part of the grain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **about an ephah of barley** - "about 13 kilos of barley" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **She lifted it up and went into the city** - It is implied that Ruth carried the grain home. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **her mother-in-law saw** - "Naomi saw"
