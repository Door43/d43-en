===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let me find favor in your sight** - "let me receive grace from you" or "please accept me."
  * **comfort** - That is "encouragement" or "consolation."
  * **kindly** - Boaz's favor has touched and encouraged Ruth's heart and mind.
