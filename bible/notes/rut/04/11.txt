===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like Rachel and like Leah**  - These were the two wives of Jacob. They and their two nurses bore the twelve sons of Jacob, the ancestors of the tribes of Israel.
  * **Perez and Judah **- Judah, and his son Perez after him, were the receivers of the covenant blessing. It was Judah and Perez's offspring who would become the kings of Israel.
  * **Tamar **- Like Ruth, Tamar was widowed without children. Judah performed the role of raising up children for her.
  * **who built up the house of Israel **- who built up the people of Israel by bearing children.
