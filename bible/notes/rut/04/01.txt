===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gate** - "gate of the city" or "gate of Bethlehem." This was the main entrance to the walled town of Bethlehem. There was an open area by the gate that was used as a meeting place to discuss community matters.
  * **the near kinsman** - This was the closest living relative to Elimelek.
  * **elders of the city** - "leaders of the city"
