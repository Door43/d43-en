===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **heart was merry**  -
  * **uncovered his feet**  -
  * **lay down**  -
  * **mother-in-law **- the mother of a woman's husband
  * **his heart was merry **- means "he felt contented" or "he was satisfied" or "he was in a good mood."
  * **she came softly **- means "she sneaked in" or "she tiptoed in" so no one would see or hear her.
