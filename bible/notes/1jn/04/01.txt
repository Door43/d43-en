===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **do not believe every spirit**  - This could be translated "Do not believer everything you hear" or "Do not trust every person that tells you something."
  * **test the spirits**  - This could be translated "Compare God's word, the Bible, to what a person is saying."
  * **into the world**  - This refers to the world system, which is ruled by Satan.
  * **false prophets**  - false teachers
  * **Jesus Christ has come in the flesh**  - This could be translated "Jesus , the Son of God, took on human form ."
