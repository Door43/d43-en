===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not love the world**  - This is a metonymy that refers to "the world system, ruled by evil desires." (See: Metonymy)
  * **the lust of the flesh...****and the vain glory of life**  -this is a metaphor which means "the things done apart from the will of God, the things seen that do not please God, and the things that we brag about."  (See: Metaphor)
  * **The world and its desire is passing away**  - This could be translated "All that we can see and touch will be destroyed sometime in the future."
