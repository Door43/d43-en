===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **commits sin**  - "continues to sin" (UDB)
  * **does lawlessness**  - "is refusing to obey God's law" (UDB)
  * **You**  - Here "you" is plural and refers to the people John is writing to. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural|Forms of 'You' - Dual/Plural]])
  * **Christ was revealed** - This can be translated with an active verb: "Christ appeared" or "the Father revealed Christ." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **remains in him**  - "belongs to him"
  * **has neither seen him or known him**  - This says the same thing in two different ways for emphasis. This can be translated as "has never truly believed in him." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
