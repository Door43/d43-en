===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we are of the truth**  - This could be translated as "we are living according the the way Jesus taught us."
  * **assure our heart**- In 3:19-22 the word "heart" refers to a person's conscience or the part of a person's thinking through which God makes him aware that he is doing something sinful. This can be translated as "we will not feel guilty in the presence of God." (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **God is greater than our heart** - This could be translated as "we know God is a better judge than our heart."
