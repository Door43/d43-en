===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These things**  - "This letter."
  * **written to you **- "You" is a pronoun which refers to all believers. (See: Pronoun)
  * **according to his will **  - This could be translated "anything he (Jesus) approves of." (UDB)
  * **petitions**  - "Whenever we ask him (Jesus) for something." (UDB)
