===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The words "him" and "He" refer to Jesus Christ.

  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus that describes his relationship to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **has given us understanding** - "has enabled us to understand the truth" 
  * **life** - Here "life" stands for the right to live forever by God's grace and love. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1jn:01:01|1:1-2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **He is ... eternal life** - This stands for the thought that Christ gives us eternal life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Children** - John was an elderly man and their leader. He used this expression to show his love for them. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1jn:02:01|2:1]]. AT: "My dear children in Christ" or "You who are as dear to me as my own children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
