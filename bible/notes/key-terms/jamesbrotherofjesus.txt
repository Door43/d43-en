====== James (brother of Jesus)======


===== Facts: =====


James was a son of  Mary and Joseph, and one of Jesus' half-brothers.

    * Before Jesus was raised from the dead, James did not believe that Jesus was the Messiah.
    * After Jesus was raised from the dead, James became a leader of the church in Jerusalem.
    * James wrote a letter to the Christians who were living in other countries. That letter is in the Bible and is called "James."
