====== Lydda, Lod ======

===== Facts: =====

Lydda is the name of a city in the New Testament located about 18 km southeast of Joppa. The name of this city in the Old Testament was Lod, and that is what it is called in modern Israel as well.

  * In the Old Testament, the city of Lod was occupied by the descendants of Benjamin.
  * In the New Testament, the city of Lydda was where Peter healed a paralyzed man named Aeneas.
