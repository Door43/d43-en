====== council ======

===== Definition: =====

A council is a group of people who are called together to discuss, give advice, and make decisions about important matters.

  * A council can be a group that is organized in an official and somewhat permanent way, or it can refer to a group of people that come together temporarily to handle a certain situation.
  * The Jewish Council was also known as the Sanhedrin, and its members were Pharisees and Sadducees.
  * Paul was brought before a Roman council when he was arrested for teaching the gospel.
  * The word "council" can also be translated as "assembly".
