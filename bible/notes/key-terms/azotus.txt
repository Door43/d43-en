====== Azotus, Ashdod ======

===== Facts: =====

Azotus is the Greek name for the Old Testament city of Ashdod. It was one of the main cities of the Philistines and was located in southern Canaan, near the coast of the Mediterranean Sea.

  * Although Ashdod was part of the land that God had given to the tribe of Judah, it still remained under Philistine control.
  * The temple of the Philistine's false god Dagon was located in Ashdod..
  * Ashdod is most well-known as the place where the ark of the covenant was first taken after the Philistines stole it from the Israelites. God severely punished the Philistines until they returned the ark.
  * In the New Testament, Azotus is one of the cities where the evangelist Philip preached the gospel.
