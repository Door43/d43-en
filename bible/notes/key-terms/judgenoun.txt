====== judge (the person) ======

===== Definition: =====

A judge is a person who decides what is right or wrong in civil, legal or spiritual matters. A judgment is a careful decision that is made about a matter.

  * In the Bible, God is often referred to as a judge because he is the one perfect judge who makes the final decisions about what is right or wrong.
  * After the people of Israel entered the promised land and before they had kings, God appointed judges to lead them in times of trouble. Often the judges rescued the Israelites from their enemies.
  * To judge is to decide whether something is good or bad, right or wrong. It can also mean to criticize or condemn. "Judgment" often refers to punishment for wrong behavior.
