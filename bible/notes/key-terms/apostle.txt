====== apostle ======

===== Definition: =====

The apostles were men sent by Jesus to preach about God.

  * The word "apostle" means, "someone who is sent out for a special purpose."
  * Jesus chose twelve apostles from among his followers, and gave these apostles special responsibilities. The apostles had a closer relationship to Jesus than his other disciples did.
  * By God's power, the apostles were able to heal people and make demons leave people.
  * The word "apostle" can also be translated as, "someone who is sent out" or "sent out one."

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciple]], the twelve, the eleven)
