====== disobey, disobedient ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "disobey" means to not obey what someone in authority has commanded or instructed. A person who does this is being "disobedient."

  * A person who does something he was told not to do is disobeying.
  * To disobey also means to refuse or fail to do something that was commanded.
  * The term "disobedient" is also used to describe the character of someone who habitually disobeys or rebels.
  * A "disobedient people" could be translated by "people who keep on disobeying,"
  * To "disobey" is the opposite of to "obey."

(See also: authority, [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:evil|evil, wicked]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:sin|sin, sinful, sinner]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:obey|obey]] other)
