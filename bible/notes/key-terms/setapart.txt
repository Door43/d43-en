====== set apart, chosen ======

===== Definition: =====

"Set apart" is the placement of somebody or something away from others into an exclusive group. "Chosen" refers to somebody or something as picked out, selected, or preferred.

  * The Israelites were selected by God as His Chosen People in the Old Testament.
  * Individual people today are chosen by God to become His children through faith in Christ.

(See also: other)
