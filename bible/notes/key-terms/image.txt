====== image, graven image ======

===== Definition: =====

An image is something that is like something else, in appearance or in essence. A "graven image" is another name for an idol.

  * A graven image or carved image is a metal or wooden object that has been made to look like an animal, person, or thing for the purpose of worshiping a false god.
  * Sometimes just the word "image" is used to refer to the carved idol.
  * The term "image" could also be translated as "statue" or "engraved idol" or "carved religious object."
