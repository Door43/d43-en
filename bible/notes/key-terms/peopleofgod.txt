====== people of God ======

===== Definitions: =====

In the Bible, "people of God" refers to people whom God has called out from the world to have a special relationship with him.

* God's people are chosen by him and are set apart from the world to live in a way that is pleasing to him. He also calls them his children.\\
* In the Old Testament, “people of God” refers to the nation of Israel because it was chosen by God and set apart from among the other nations of the world to serve and obey him.\\
* In the New Testament, “people of God” especially refers to those who believe in Jesus and are called the Church.
