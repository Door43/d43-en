====== member, body part ======

===== Definition: =====

A dual usage in the New Testament: used as a reference to organs of the body; and the body as a whole.

  * Literal usage as to eyes and hands in Matthew.
  * Metaphorical use as to followers of Christ being "members" of the body of Christ in I Cor. 12.

(See also: other)
