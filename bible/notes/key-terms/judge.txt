====== judge, judgment ======

===== Definition: =====

To judge is to decide whether something or someone is legally or morally right or wrong. This decision is called a "judgment."

  * To judge can also mean to condemn something or someone.
  * When God judges sin, it means he condemns it as evil.
  * The "judgment of God" may express his decision to condemn something or someone as sinful.
  * The "judgment of God" also often refers to his punishment of sinners.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:justice|justice]])
