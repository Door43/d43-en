====== enslave, hold in bondage ======

===== Definition: =====

To "enslave" is to place someone under control of another. To "hold in bondage" is to keep someone under the control of another.

  * To "enslave" also means to take away a person's freedom. To maintain their lack of freedom is to "hold in bondage."
  * Another word for "bondage" is "slavery."
  * As human beings, we are born in sin and are enslaved to sin until Jesus frees us from its control and power.
  * When a person becomes a Christian, there will still be temptations to sin but he is no longer a slave to sin. Through God's power he can choose to obey God and not give in to the temptation.
  * The Christian life is a battle between the continuous temptation of sin (being a slave of sin) and being obedient to God (being a slave of righteousness).

(See also: servant, slave, slavery other)
