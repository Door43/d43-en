====== Titus ======

===== Facts: =====

Titus was an early church leader who was mentored/trained by the apostle Paul

  * Titus was given the job of appointing church leaders in Crete
  * One New Testament book is a letter that was written to Titus by Paul.
  * As a Gentle church leader, Titus was not required to be circumcised to meet with Jewish leaders in Jerusalem.
