====== Tarsus ======

===== Facts: =====

Tarsus is an ancient and prosperous city located on the Tarsus River near the southeast Mediterranean shore in the Roman province of Celicia in Asia Minor,

  * Tarsus was the birthplace of Paul the Apostle.
