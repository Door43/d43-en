====== bewitched ======

===== Definition: =====

The literal meaning of "bewitched" is to cast a demonic spell on someone.

  * As it is used in the Bible, to be "bewitched" is an idiom meaning to be "deceived" or to be "led astray from the truth."
  * It implies that someone has lost the power of reasoning or that he is not thinking clearly.
  * Ways to translate "bewitched you" could include "deceived you" or "tricked you" or "led you astray."
