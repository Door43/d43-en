====== seed ======

===== Definition: =====

A seed is the part of a plant that gets planted in the ground to reproduce more of the same kind of plant.

  * "Seed" is often used figuratively to refer to a person's offspring or descendants.
  * In English the word "seed" can be plural, referring to more than one descendant.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:descendant]])
