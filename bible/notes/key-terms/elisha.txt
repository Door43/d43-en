====== Elisha ======

===== Facts: =====

Elisha was a prophet in Israel during the reigns of kings Ahab, Ahaziah, Jehoram, Jehu, Jehoahaz, and Jehoash.

  * <font 13.888888359069824px line-height: 13.194443702697754px; background-color: rgb(238, 238, 238);/Lato, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 13.888888359069824px; line-height: 13.194443702697754px; background-color: rgb(238, 238, 238);>God told the prophet Elijah to anoint Elisha as prophet.</font>
  * Before Elijah was taken to heaven, Elisha asked to receive a double portion of Elijah's spirit.
  * Elisha worked many miracles, including the healing of Naaman of Syria and the resurrection of a Shunammite woman's son.
