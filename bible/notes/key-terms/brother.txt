====== brother ======


===== Definition: =====

A brother is a male who shares at least one biological parent with another person.

  * Jewish men considered themselves to be brothers because they were all descendants of Abraham.
  * Men who believe in Jesus are brothers because they all have God as their Father.
  * In a similar manner, women who believe in Jesus are also called sisters because they all have God as their Father.
  * The local leadership of the early church were sometimes referred to as "the brothers."
