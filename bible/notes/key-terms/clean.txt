====== clean, cleanse ======


===== Definition: =====


To be clean is to be free from dirt or stain. It is often used metaphorically to mean, "to be free from sin or unholiness."

    * "Cleanse" is the process of making something "clean." 
    * In ancient Israel, only certain foods, animals, and hygiene practices were considered clean for use or eating.
    * A person could be cleansed from an illness or condition that made him ceremonially unclean.
    * In biblical usage, "clean" can also refer to moral purity.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:holy|holiness]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:unclean|unclean]])
