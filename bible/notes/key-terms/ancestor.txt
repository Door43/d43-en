====== ancestor ======


===== Definition: =====


An ancestor is a person born more than one previous generation earlier in a family line.


    * An ancestor is someone's great-grandparents and all earlier grandparents.
    * "Ancestor" is mostly used to to refer to a relative in the distant past.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:descendant|descendant]])
