====== rest ======

===== Definition: =====

To rest is to cease the work that is at hand and to put it to an end, either permanently or temporarily.

  * At the end of the creation account God "rested" or "put an end" to the work he was doing, i.e. creation.
  * God set aside the seventh day for resting from work. This day is called the "Sabbath".

(See also: Sabbath)
