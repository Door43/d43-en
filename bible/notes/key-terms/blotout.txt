====== blot out ======

===== Definition: =====

To "blot out" means to completely get rid of something or someone.

  * "Blot out" means to remove something completely.
      * It can be used in a positive sense, as when God "blots out" sins by forgiving them and choosing not to remember them. One way to translate this could be, "erase" or "wipe out" or "get rid of."
      * It can be used in a negative sense, as when God "blots out" a people group, destroying them because of their sin.
  * The term "blot out" could also be translated as "completely destroy."
