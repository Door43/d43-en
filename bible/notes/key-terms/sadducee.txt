====== Sadducee ======

===== Definition: =====

Sadducees, one of two main sects of priests, were a political Jewish group of priests during the time of Jesus Christ who supported Roman rule and who did not believe in the resurrection.

  * Jesus spoke in opposition to these two groups because of their selfishness and hypocrisy.
