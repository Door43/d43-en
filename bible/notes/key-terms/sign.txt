====== sign ======

===== Definition: =====

A sign is an object, event, or action that communicates a special meaning. Signs often prove that something is true.

  * God made a rainbow as a sign to remind people that he would never again destroy all life with a worldwide flood.
  * <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The miracles done by the prophets and apostles were signs that proved they were speaking God's message.</font>
  * God commanded the Israelites to circumcise their sons as a sign of his covenant with them.
  * An angel told shepherds a sign that would help them know which baby in Bethlehem was the newborn Messiah.
  * The miracles that Jesus performed were signs that proved he was truly the Messiah.
  * Judas kissed Jesus as a sign to the religious leaders that Jesus was the one they should arrest.
  * Depending on its context, "sign" could be translated as: signal, symbol, mark, evidence, proof, or gesture.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:miracle|miracle, wonder, sign]], apostle, Messiah, covenant, circumcise, circumcision other)
