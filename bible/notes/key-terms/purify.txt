====== pure, purify, purification ======

===== Definition: =====

<font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To be "pure" means to have no flaw or to have nothing mixed in that is not supposed to be there. To purify something is to cleanse it and remove anything that contaminates or pollutes it.</font>

  * <font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In regard to Old Testament laws, "purify"and "purification" refer mainly to the cleansing from things that make an object or a person ritually unclean, such as disease, body fluids, or childbirth.</font>
  * <font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Old Testament also had laws telling people how to be purified from sin, usually by the sacrifice of an animal. This was only temporary and the sacrifices had to be repeated over and over again.</font>
  * <font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the New Testament, to be purified often refers to being cleansed from sin. The only way that people can be completely and permanently purified from sin is through repenting and receiving God</font>    's forgiveness, through <font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>trusting in Jesus and his sacrifice.</font>
  * <font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The term "purify" could be translated as "cleanse from all contamination" or "get rid of all sin."</font>
