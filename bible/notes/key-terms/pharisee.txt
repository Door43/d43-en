====== Pharisee ======


===== Definition: =====


The Pharisees were a Jewish sect of religious leaders at the time of Jesus's life.


    * The Pharisees were characterized by a rigid adherence to the Law of Moses. They also strictly adhered to the oral law, an uninspired tradition developed alongside of the Old Testament.
    * In Jesus' day, the Pharisees were the primarily teachers of the Jewish Law and traditions.
    * The Pharisees were the epitome of legalism because of the importance they placed on rituals and man-made traditions.
