====== Ananias the high priest ======

===== Facts: =====

Ananias was the high priest during the time of Paul's last trip to Jerusalem.

  * Paul was arrested in Jerusalem for disturbing a Jewish crowd who had become very angry when he told them that God had sent him to preach to the Gentiles.
  * Ananias ordered bystanders to hit Paul in the face when he was taken to the Jewish council the day after his arrest.
  * A few days later, Ananias and the council elders testified against Paul at a hearing with the Governor Felix in Caesarea.
