====== foreigner ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "foreigner" refers to a person living in a country that is not his own. In the Old Testament it especially refers to anyone who came from a different people group than the people he was living among.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A foreigner is also a person whose language and culture is different from your own.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The apostle Paul writes the Ephesians that before they knew Christ, they were "foreigners" to God's covenant.</font>
  * An older word for "foreigner" is "alien."
  * Sometimes "foreigner" is translated as "stranger," but make sure it does not mean "someone that you don't know."
