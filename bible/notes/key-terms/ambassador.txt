====== ambassador ======

===== Definition: =====

An ambassador is a diplomatic person who represents his or her country in dealings with a foreign nation.

  * An "ambassador" should exemplify the attitudes, actions, values and customs of their nation or country.
  * In the Bible, the Apostle Paul said that he was an ambassador for Jesus Christ.
  * Christians are called to be "ambassadors" for Jesus Christ since they represent him in this world.
