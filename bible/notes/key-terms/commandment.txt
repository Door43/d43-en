====== commandment ======


===== Definition: =====

A commandment is a particular order or rule that tells how we are to behave and live our lives. Commandments are given by someone who has authority, and those who are under that authority must obey them.

    * A commandment may say what people must do (“You must honor your father and mother”) or what they must not do (“You must not steal. 
    * Words with a similar meaning are “law,” “command” and “regulation."
