====== anoint, anointed ======


===== Definition: =====


To anoint someone or something sets that person or object apart for service to God. Anointing is accomplished by rubbing or pouring oil on a person or object as part of a ceremony.


    * In Old Testament times, kings and priests were anointed to begin their service.
    * Objects to be used for holy service were anointed prior to their use.
    * The titles "Messiah" and "Christ" mean "anointed one."
    * In the early church, anointing was also used as a ritual for the sick.
