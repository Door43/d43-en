====== elect, chosen, Chosen One ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "elect" means "chosen." It is sometimes translated as "God's chosen people".

  * God "chose" or "elected" people to love him and serve him and to bear spiritual fruit.
  * God's "elect" (that is, God's "chosen ones") refers to God's people who have been saved by him and who have been made his children.
  * In a few Old Testament passages, the term "elect" or "chosen one" is used to refer to the nation of Israel as God's chosen people.
  * In the New Testament, the term "God's elect or "God's chosen ones" refers to people who have been saved by God through faith in Jesus.
  * Some English Bible versions use the phrase "my elect" in one Old Testament verse where God is talking about his Messiah; other versions translate this as "my chosen one" instead (Isaiah 42:1).
