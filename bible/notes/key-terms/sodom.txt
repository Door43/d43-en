====== Sodom ======


===== Definition: =====


Sodom was a city in the southern part of Canaan. Sodom and its twin city, Gomorrah, were destroyed by God because the people who lived there were especially evil.


    * The area around Sodom was very well-watered and produced abundant crops.
    * Abraham's nephew, Lot, chose to live in the city of Sodom.
    * The most significant sin that has historically been identified with Sodom is male homosexuality.
