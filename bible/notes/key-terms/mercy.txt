====== mercy, merciful ======

===== Definition: =====

"Mercy" means helping people who are in need, especially when they are in a lowly or humbled condition.

  * A person is merciful when he treats someone kindly instead of harming him.
  * It also means to forgive someone who has done something wrong against us.
  * We show mercy when we help people who are in great need, especially when they are not able to help themselves.
  * God is merciful to us, and he wants us to be merciful to others.
  * To "show mercy" could be translated as, "treat kindly" or "be compassionate toward".
