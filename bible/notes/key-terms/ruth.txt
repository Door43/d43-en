====== Ruth ======

===== Facts: =====

Ruth was a Moabite woman who married an Israelite man when his family moved to her country.

  * She is noted for her loyalty and hard-working servant heart.
  * This woman's name means "friendship," perhaps a clue to the reader of the book of Ruth that she will become a special friend to Naomi.
  * Ruth was a Moabite woman who became the great-grandmother of one of Israel's greatest kings.
