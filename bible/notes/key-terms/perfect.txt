====== perfect, complete, mature ======

===== Definition: =====

The Biblical terms "perfect," "complete," and "mature" all refer to the goal of Christian growth.

  * Being perfect and mature as a Christian does not imply we are sinless, but rather obedient.
  * The New Testament book of James states that trials and patience produce completeness and maturity.
  * Study of the scripture and obedience to it brings spiritual maturity by making believers more like God.
