====== godly, godliness ======

===== Definition: =====

"Godly" is used to describe a person who acts in a way that shows what God is like. "Godliness" refers to godly actions and character.

  * Godly character in a person is seen by qualities such as love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, and self control.
  * Godliness is the fruit or outward evidence that a person has the Holy Spirit and is submitting to him.
