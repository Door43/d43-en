====== heir ======

===== Definition: =====

An heir is a person who legally receives property or money that belonged to a person who died.

  * In Bible times, the main heir was the eldest son, who typically received most of the property of his father.
  * The Bible also uses "heir" in a figurative sense of Christians receiving spiritual benefits from God as their spiritual father.
  * As God's children, Christians are said to be joint heirs with Jesus Christ.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|inherit, inheritance]])
