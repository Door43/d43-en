====== heir ======

===== Definition: =====

An heir is legally the recipient of an individual's inheritance upon death.

  * The first heir is normally a surviving spouse, followed by the person's children.
  * As God's children, Christians are said to be joint heirs with Jesus Christ.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|inheritance]])
