===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The young woman's brothers continue to speak among themselves.

  * **If she is a wall ... If she is a door** - The little sister ([[:en:bible:notes:sng:08:08|8:8]]) has very small breasts that either have not grown or are very small. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **we will build on her a tower of silver ... we will adorn her with boards of cedar** - The brothers decide to decorate the little sister with silver and cedar, symbols of riches, so that she will be more likely to attract a good husband. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **will adorn her** - "will decorate her"
