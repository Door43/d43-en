===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If she is a wall…silver. - **  Possible meaning: If the young girl has firmness of character and acts like a wall to protect herself from sexual encounters, the brothers will talk about her beauty and give her silver which is a sign of holiness.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **If she is a door,…cedar.**  - Possible meaning: If the young girl opens herself (like a door) to immorality, the brothers would put up a strong barrier (cedar boards) to guard and protect her.
