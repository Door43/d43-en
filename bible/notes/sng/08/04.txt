===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **daughters of Jerusalem's men - **  The women of the palace are attending to the woman as she prepares for the wedding night.
  * **making love** - "having sex" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **until it is over** - The woman is expecting to be alone with her husband while they have sex without anyone disturbing them.
