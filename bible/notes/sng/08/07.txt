===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Surging waters cannot quench love nor can floods sweep it away**- Not even a flood can stop the fire of their great love. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **If a man gave all his possessions...the offer would utterly be despised. **- AT: "The man who thinks he can buy love by offering wealth to a woman will be disappointed because love can only be freely given."
  * **utterly be despised** - "completely hated"
