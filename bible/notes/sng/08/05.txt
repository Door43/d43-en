===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **​Who is this who is coming…leaning on her beloved? **- The women who are attending the bride are remembering the time when she was a child. Since the time from childhood to adulthood seems to pass quickly, the women are asking who this grown up person is.  (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **I awakened you under the apricot tree.**  - The woman is saying to the man that she remembers when they became lovers. She aroused his feelings for her, and she responded to his lovemaking. (See: Euphemism)
  * **​there your mother conceived you **- This could mean that the woman remembers the time when they met, close to home. It may also mean that the woman is comparing their first lovemaking to the beginning of a new life. (See: Metaphor)
  * **there she gave birth to you, she delivered you - **  The woman is emphasizing the wonderful result of beginning a new life together. (See: Doublet)​
