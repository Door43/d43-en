===== Translation Notes: =====

The lover continues to praise the woman.
  * **How beautiful is your love** - "Your love is wonderful"
  * **my sister, my bride** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:sng:04:09|4:9]].
  * **How much better is your love than wine** - "Your love is much better than wine." See how you translated "your love is better than wine" in [[en:bible:notes:sng:01:01|1:2]].
  * **the fragrance of your perfume than any spice** - At: "the smell of your oils is better than any spice." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **fragrance...perfume** - See how you translated these words in [[en:bible:notes:sng:01:01|1:3]].
  * **spice** - 



  * **perfume** - a sweet smelling liquid made to put on the body in small amounts
  * **spice** - dried plants or seeds to add flavor in cooking 
  * **Your lips, my bride, drip honey; honey and milk are under your tongue** - He is not saying honey is actually dripping from her lips. This is a word picture to say how desirable she is to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **fragrance** - a pleasing smell
  * **garments** - "pieces of clothing"
  * **Lebanon** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:sng:03:08|3:9]].
