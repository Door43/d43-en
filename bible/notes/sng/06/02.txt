===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My beloved has gone down to his garden** - "The one who loves me has come to me, I, who am like his garden" (UDB) (See: Metaphor, Euphemism)
  * **to the beds of spices **  - He has come to enjoy my physical attractions which are like spices (See: Metaphor, Euphemism)
  * ** to graze in the garden and to gather lilies ** - "to enjoy cuddling (and) embracing me and kissing my lips, which are like lilies" (UDB) (See: Metaphor, Euphemism)
  * **lilies** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:sng:02:16|2:16]]
  * **he grazes among the lilies with pleasure **- "He has pleasure in kissing my lips, like sheep enjoy grazing" (UDB) (See: Metaphor).
