===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **prince's daughter** - Though the woman was not born into a royal family, the way she looks and acts cause her to seem to be the daughter of a prince. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **curves of your thighs** - The word "thighs" refers to the upper part of a woman's legs.
  * **are like jewels,
the work of the hands of a master craftsman**  - The shape of the woman's thighs are like a beautiful precious stone which has been carved to a perfect shape by a skilled workman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])​
