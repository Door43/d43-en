===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The woman continues to speak to the man.

  * **rise early** - "get up early" or "wake up early"
  * **have budded** - "have begun to bloom"
  * **blossoms** - flowers when they are open
  * **are in flower** - "have flowers open on the plant"
  * **I will give you my love** - "I will make love with you"
