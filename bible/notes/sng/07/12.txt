===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon's wife has invited him to go into the nearby countryside.

  * **Let us rise early to go to the vineyards** - The husband and wife have begun living together to share the experiences which are the result of love and devotion to one another.
  * **There I will give you my love** - The wife offers a lifetime of physical and emotional love unselfishly to her husband.
