===== Translation Notes: =====

The woman continues to speak to her beloved.

  * **mandrakes** - This is the name of plants that give off a strong but pleasant scent. The scent is slightly intoxicating and exciting which increases the desire to make love. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **give off their fragrance** - "produce their scent"
  * "Mandrakes" are plants with fist-sized pale orange fruit that stimulates and is sometimes associated with making one able to have children.
  * **at the door** - "At the door" means (1) "close by" or (2) a part of daily life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **choice fruits, new and old** - In their married life the couple are celebrating what they knew in the past and what they will know about each other in the future--all aspects of a loving relationship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **stored up for you** - "heaped up for you" or "protected for you"
