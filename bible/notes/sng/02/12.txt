===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The flowers have appeared** - "People can see flowers"
  * **in the land** - "all over this land"
  * **pruning** - cutting back branches of plants so they will bear more fruit
  * **and the singing of birds** - "and for birds to sing"
  * **pruning** - cutting off branches from a plant so that it will produce more fruit or look better 
  * **the sound of the doves is heard** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "people can hear the sound of doves" or "the doves are cooing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The fig tree ripens her green figs** - The tree is spoken of as if it were actively causing its fruit to ripen. This is a collective singular and can be translated as a plural. AT: "The figs on the trees are becoming ripe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **vines are in blossom** - "vines are flowering" or "vines have flowers"
  * **they give off** - The word "they" refers to the blossoms on the vines.
  * **fragrance** - "sweet smell"
  * **my love** - "you whom I love." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:sng:01:09|1:9]]
