===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As the apricot tree is among the trees of the forest, so is my beloved among the young men** - AT: "Of all the other men, this man is the one who loves me; he is like the finest apricot tree that grows among the trees of the forest"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **I sit down under his shadow with great delight** - She is happy for him to be important. 
  * **shadow** - a dark shape that appears on a surface when something or someone blocks the light
  * **and his fruit is sweet to my taste** - She is referring to sex. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **He brought me to the banqueting hall** - This is the room where they made love. 
  * **and his banner over me was love** - This refers to a sweet sense of tender, protective love; a safe covering which showed to whom she belonged.
  * **banner** - a large strip of cloth with a design
