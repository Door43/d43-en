===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **daughters of Jerusalem's men** - "young women of Jerusalem"
  * **by the gazelles and the does of the fields **- These animals of the field are shy and timid, but experience absolute freedom.
  * **the gazelles** - animals that look a lot like deer and are fast, slender, and shy
  * **of the fields** - "that live in the countryside"
  * **will not interrupt our lovemaking
until it is over** - "will not interrupt us as we make love until we are finished"
  * **interrupt** - "disturb" or "bother"
