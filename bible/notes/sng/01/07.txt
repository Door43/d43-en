===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **whom my soul loves** - The woman is telling the man that she not only loves him with her physical body but also with her spiritual soul.
  * **where do you feed your flock? Where...noontime?** - Because of her great love for the man, she wants to know where he is and what he is doing. She wants to be with him. This is repeated to emphasize it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
  * **Why should I be like someone who wanders beside the flocks of your companions?** - The woman does not want to be with the man's friends. She wants to be with him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **wanders** - to go here and there to different places without purpose
  * **companions** - "friends" or "co-workers"
