===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **feed your flock** - "graze your flocks"
  * **rest your flock** - "have your flock lie down"
  * **Why should I be like some who wanders beside the flocks of your companions** - AT: "Tell me so that I will not need to wander around among the flocks of your companions when I am looking for you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **wanders** - "goes all around"
  * **companions** - "friends" or "co-workers"
