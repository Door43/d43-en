  * **most beautiful among women** - "you who are the most beautiful of all women"
  * **follow the tracks of my flock** - "follow along behind the flock"
  * **tracks** - marks of the hooves of the flock on the ground
  * **pasture your young goats** - "graze your young goats" or "let your young goats eat"
