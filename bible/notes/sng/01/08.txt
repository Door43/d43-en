===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If you do not know,...follow the tracks of my flock. **- The man is explaining how the woman can find him easily.  He is not trying to avoid her or deceive her because he loves her as much as she loves him.
  * **pasture your young goats near the shepherd's tents **- The man is explaining that he wants the woman to be close to him.  He is not trying to hide anything from her.
