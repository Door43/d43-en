===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I compare you, my love, to a mare among Pharoh's chariot horses** ​- The man tells the woman how valuable and beautiful she is to him.  In that culture in that time, horses were a sign of wealth and power.  The man is comparing the woman to an important possession owned by a powerful ruler. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Your cheeks...silver studs** - The man tells the woman that she is so beautiful that he wants to give her valuable expensive gifts of gold and silver jewelry to match her beauty.
