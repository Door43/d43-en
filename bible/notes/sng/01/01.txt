===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Song of Songs, which is Solomon's **- This book of the Bible is made up of poems which are in the style of the writings of Solomon.
  * **Oh, that you would kiss me **- Since the kiss is an important expression of physcial love between a man and a woman, the woman asks the man to kiss her.
  * **your love is better than wine **- Love is better than wine because kisses show intense desire and are a source of rejoicing. (See: Metaphor)
  * **anointing oils - **  ​The act of anointing with special, sweet smelling oils was used to set people apart from others. The woman is saying that she is pleased that the man has set himself apart from others to be her faithful lover. (See List of Important Terms)
  * **your name is ****like flowing perfume, so the young women love you**.**
 - **  The woman realizes that her lover has a good reputation which is attractive to other young women. (See Metaphor)
  * **let me celebrate your love; it is better than wine.**  - Since wine is used during special occasions, the woman is comparing the happiness of those special times to the happiness she feels being in love with the man. (See: Metaphor)
  * **It is natural for the other women to adore you.**  - Because the woman is in love with the man, she can understand why other women may be attracted to him.
