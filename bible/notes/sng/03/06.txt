===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like a column of smoke **- Smoke rises from its source into the air. A column is an object that starts from the ground and gives support to another piece of a building. Smoke looks like a column as it rises. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **perfumed with myrrh and frankincense **- "with smells of myrrh and frankincense." "Perfume" is usually a liquid that smells very nice, often made from flowers and put on different areas of the body.
  * **powders** - a fine dust made by crushing something solid, for example, crushing dried flower petals to make perfume
  * **sold by merchants**  - "merchant" is a person who buys and sells or trades items for money or other items.
  * **it is the portable litter **- The word "portable" means easily moved from one place to another. 
  * **litter** - The word "litter" refers to a chair or bed, sometimes with curtains around it, that can be moved by men or animals.
  * **sixty warriers surround it, sixty of Israel's soldiers **- This is repeated for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **sixty warriors surround it**  - A "warrior" is a brave man who fights for and defends his country, village, or leader.
  * **surround** -  "are all around"
