===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **watchmen**  - the men who have the job of keeping guard of the town at night to keep the people safe
  * **bedroom**  - a room for sleeping 
  * **the one who had conceived me** -- "the one who had gotten pregnant with me" or "the one who carried me in her belly." This refers to her mother.
