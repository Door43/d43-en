====== Song of Solomon 05 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Verses 2-7 describe a dream the woman had. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Beauty ==
The woman is described as the epitome of beauty in ancient Israel. Not all cultures share the same standards of beauty. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==
In the ancient Near East, it was common to describe a woman using metaphors involving animals. In many cultures today, this can be considered offensive. Different metaphors of beauty are used in different cultures. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== "My sister, my bride" ==
The woman described is not the sister of her husband. They are not related. Instead, this is a reference to a woman who is a fellow Israelite.
