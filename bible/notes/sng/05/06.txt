===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** My heart sank ** - This literally means her heart fell or dropped to the bottom, as in water. However, this is a picture of her great disappointment.  The Hebrew text says my soul failed me. (See: Metaphor)
  * ** I became despondant ** . - AT: "I was so full of saddness that it felt like my spirit left me" (See: Metaphor)
