===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The watchmen** - those who watch and guard the city at night
  * **found me** - The word "me" refers to the young woman.
  * **struck me** - "beat me" or "hit me"
  * **wounded me** - "injured me"
  * **the guards on the walls** - "the men who guard the walls"
  * **cloak** - an outdoor overgarment that people wore over their other clothing on their upper body when they went out in public.
