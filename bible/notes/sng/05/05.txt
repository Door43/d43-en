===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I got up to open the door for my beloved** - Possible meanings are 1) the young woman got out of bed in order to let her beloved into the house or 2) "I prepared myself in order to open up myself sexually to my beloved." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **my hands...my fingers** - The words "hands" and "fingers" likely refer to the private parts of a woman.
  * **with moist myrrh** - "with liquid myrrh"
