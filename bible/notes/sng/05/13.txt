===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **beds of spices** - a garden or field where spices are grown (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **yielding aromatic scents** - "bringing good smells"
  * **His lips are lilies** - She is not saying his lips are actually lilies. Lilies are plants that have sweet smelling white or colorful bell-shaped flowers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
