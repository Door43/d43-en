===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Daughters of Jerusalem's men** - These words refers to all the women of Jerusalem and seems to be a loving term. 
  * **I plead with you** - "I beg you" or "I charge you"
  * **sick because of love** - Her love is so deep that she feels physically sick. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
