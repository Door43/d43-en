===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Daughters of Jerusalem's men **  - AT: " Daughters of Jerusalem "
  * **Daughters of Jerusalem **- refers to all the women of Jerusalem, and it is used in both the Old and New Testaments.
  * ** I plead with you   ** - AT ** : " ** I charge you"  (See Discussion Notes)
  * **if you find my beloved, please tell him **- (See: Doublet)
  * **sick because of love **  - love so deep that one feels physically sick (See: Simile)
