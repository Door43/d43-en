===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His arms are rounded gold set with jewels; **- AT: "His hands are like rods of gold set with precious stones." Rods of gold represent purity and strength. (See: Metaphor)
  * **his abdomen is ivory **  - AT: "His abdomen is as bright as ivory" (See: Simile)
  * Ivory - a hard white substance that comes from the tusk of elephants or other large animals and represents strength.
  * Sapphires - A blue or purple gem stone.
