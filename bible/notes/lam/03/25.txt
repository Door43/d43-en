===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the one who waits for him** - Possible meanings are 1) "all those who depend on him" (UDB) or 2) "the one who waits patiently for him to act."
    * **the life that seeks him** - This phrase means basically the same thing as the previous phrase and is used for emphasis. AT: "the person who seeks him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **bear the yoke** - This phrase refers to enduring hardship and suffering. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **laid it upon him** - Here the word "it" refers to the yoke.
    * **put his mouth in the dust** - This is a posture of mourning. AT: "bown down with his mouth on the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
