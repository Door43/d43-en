===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **call to mind my affliction** - AT: "remember my affliction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **wormwood and bitterness** - The two words share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **I bow down within myself** - The expression refers to depression and despair. AT: "my soul melts within me" or "I am in despair" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
