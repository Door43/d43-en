===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **My eye brings severe pain to my life** - AT: "What I see brings me much pain." (See: Metonymy)
    * **all the daughters of my city**  - This is a Semitic way of referring to the inhabitants of the city, in this case, Jerusalem. (See: Idiom)
    * **they have destroyed my life in a well**  - Possible meanings are 1) "they have dropped me into a water well in order to cause me to drown in the water" (see UDB) or 2) "they have put me into the grave" (See: Metaphor)
    * **have placed a stone over me**  - Possible meanings are 1) "have covered the shaft with a stone so that I cannot escape" (see UDB) or 2) "have put me in the grave and thrown rocks over my body". (See: Metaphor)
    * **I have been cut off**  - AT: "I am about to die!" (See: Idiom)
