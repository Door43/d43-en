===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My eyes cause me grief** - AT: "What I see causes me much pain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all the daughters of my city** - Possible meanings are 1) the women of Jerusalem or 2) all the inhabitants of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They cast me into a pit** - AT: "They have killed me by drowning me in a well." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **have placed a stone over me** - Possible meanings are 1) "have covered the well with a stone" or 2) "have thrown stones at me."
  * **I have been cut off** - AT: "I have been killed" or "I am drowning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
