===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Who is it that speaks and it happens when the Lord has not commanded it?**  - This emphasizes that things only take place if the Lord commands it. AT: "Whatever people say should happen, it will only happen if the Lord commands it will happen." (See: Rhetorical question)
    * **Do not both calamity and success come out of the the mouth of the Most High?**  - This emphasizes that good and evil come from God. AT: "We should not be surprised if good or bad things happen, since we know that both may come from God." (See: Rhetorical question)
    * **come out of the mouth of** - This refers to words that come from God's mouth. (See: Metonymy)
    * **How does any person alive complain—any man over his sins' punishment?**  - This means that it is ridiculous to complain when God punishes them, because their sin deserves it. AT: "People should not complain when God punishes them; their sins deserved it." (See: Rhetorical question)
