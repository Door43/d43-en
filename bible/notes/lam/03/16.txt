===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He crushed ... he has pushed me** - are two expressions for God humbling the author. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **My soul is deprived of peace** - The author is no longer experiencing joy or pleasure in his life.
  * **My endurance has perished** - Some versions translate, "My glory has perished," but here "glory" stands for endurance or strength. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
