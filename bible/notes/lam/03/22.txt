===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is by the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh that we are not destroyed** - AT: "It is because of the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh that we do not perish" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **your faithfulness** - The writer speaks to Yahweh.
  * **Yahweh is my inheritance** - "Because Yahweh is with me, I have everything I need"
  * **I have said to myself** - AT: "I sincerely declare" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
