===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **we are not finished off** - AT: "we are not completely destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **Great is your faithfulness!** - Here the word "your" refers to Yahweh.
    * **Yahweh is my portion** - "Yahweh is my inheritance"
    * **declares my life** - AT: "I declare" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
