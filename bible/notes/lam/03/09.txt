===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He has blocked my path with walls of hewn stone**  - God has prevented him from escaping, as would a man who blocked a road. (See: Metaphor)
  * **hewn stone**  - Pieces of rock that someone has cut into regular shapes in order to build a stronger wall.
  * **every way I take is crooked**  - The man tries to run away, but there is no straight, easy road to follow. (See: Metaphor)
  * **He has turned aside my paths.**  - The man tries to follow a safe path, but God prevents him and makes him take a dangerous and desolate path.
