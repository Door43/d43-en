===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will return a recompense to them, Yahweh, according to what their hands have done** - AT: "They have made me suffer, Yahweh, so now you will make them suffer"
  * **return a recompense to them** - "pay them back"
  * **what their hands have done** - AT: "what they have done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **heart, your curse upon them** - AT: "heart. You will set your curse upon them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **You will set fear in their heart** - AT: "You will make them deeply afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **from under the heavens** - AT: "from wherever they are on earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
