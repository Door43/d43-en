===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **You have covered yourself with a cloud** - This means that Yahweh has made himself impossible to approach. (See: Metaphor)
    * **you** - this refers to Yahweh.
    * **You have made us castoffs and refuse**  - "You have thrown us away as if we were trash." (See: Metaphor)
    * **opened their mouth mockingly**  - This refers to people opening their mouths in order to say insulting things. (See: Metonymy)
