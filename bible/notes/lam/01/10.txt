===== translationNotes: =====

  * **has put his hand on** - This is an idiom. AT: "has taken possession of" or "has stolen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **precious treasures** - This refers to their valuable possessions.
  * **She has seen** - The word "She" refers to Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the nations** - This refers to people from various nations, not the entire population of those nations. AT: "people from the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **you had commanded** - The word "you" refers to Yahweh.
