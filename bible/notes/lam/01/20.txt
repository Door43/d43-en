===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my innermost parts churn about** - The word "churn" means to toss violently, normally in a circular rotation.
  * **My heart is turned within me** - AT: "My heart is broken" or "I am very sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I have grievously rebelled** - "I have rebelled greatly"
  * **at home it is like death** - Possible meanings are 1) "at home everyone is dying" or 2) "at home is like a grave."
