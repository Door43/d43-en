===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Zion has spread her arms wide** - Zion is like a person that lifts up her arms to ask for help from other people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **should be his adversaries** - Here the word "his" refers to Jacob.
  * **like a menstrual rag** - See how you translated this phrase in [[en:bible:notes:lam:01:08|1:8]].
