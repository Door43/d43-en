===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He has stretched out the measuring line**  - This refers to a rope of a certain length which was used to measure distance. It is as if the Lord measured the walls of Jerusalem so that not a single bit would remain. AT: "He has used a length of rope to measure every bit of the wall." (See: Metaphor)
    * **has not withheld his hand from destroying the wall**  - The hand symbolizes the destruction that he causes. "He has deliberately destroyed the wall." (See: Litotes; See: Synecdoche)
    * **he has made the ramparts lament and the walls become feeble**  - The ramparts (outer, defensive walls) and the inner main walls of Zion are like a person who laments and grows weak. (See: Personification)
