===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He has overthrown** - "He has destroyed" 
  * **to rejoice over you** - This means that the enemy rejoiced because they defeated them. AT: "to rejoice over defeating you" 
  * **he has lifted up the horn of your enemies** - Here "horn" (that is, an animal horn) represents strength. AT: "he has increased the power of your enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
