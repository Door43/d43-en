===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the grinding house**  - The invaders have forced young men to grind grain. This was done by putting the grain between two huge discs of stone, and animals or slaves would walk around in a circle, moving the upper stone. 
    * **youthful men stagger beneath logs**  - The Babylonians force young men to carry large bundles of wood, and they stagger under the weight.
    * **They have removed the elders from the city gate**  - Older men of the ancient East would sit near the gate or entrance of the city. They would offer their opinions to people who came and went, and it was a place of honor. (See: Metaphor)
    * **vigorous men from their music**  - Young men should be happy and playing and listening to music. The invaders have made them sad and unable to enjoy that music. (See: Metonymy)
