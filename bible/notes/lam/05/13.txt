===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **vigorous men** - men at the strongest time of their lives
    * **the grinding house** - the place where slaves would grind grain
    * **youthful men** - men who are still growing strong (see UDB)
    * **elders** - men who are no longer "vigorous men"
    * **the city gate** - where the elders would give legal advice, but also where people would meet socially
    * **their music** - part of social life at the city gate
