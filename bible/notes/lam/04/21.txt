===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Rejoice and be glad, daughter of Edom living in the land of Uz**  - The speaker addresses Edom as if it were physically present. (See: Apostrophe)
  * **daughter of Edom**  - This is a way of saying the people of the nation of Edom. (See: Idiom)
  * **the cup will also pass to you**  - The cup full of wine is a symbol of God's judgment, which people will drink. AT: "God will judge you next". (See: Metaphor) (See: Synecdoche)
  * **You will become drunk and strip naked**  - When God judges Edom, they will become like a drunk person who is shamed by appearing naked in public. (See: Metaphor)
  * **Your guilt is ended, daughter of Zion**  - The speaker addresses Zion as if it were physically present. (See: Apostrophe)
  * **daughter of Zion **- This is a way of saying the people of Zion, or Jerusalem. (See: Idiom)
  * **He will yet uncover your sins**  - "Uncover" is a symbol of revealing their sins so that others can see what wrong they have done. (See: Metaphor) (See: UDB)
