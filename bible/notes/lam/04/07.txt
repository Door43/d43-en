===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **her leaders used to shine like snow and were white as milk**  - In Israel the rich had clear, pale skin, because they spent time indoors and kept clean. (See: Simile)
  * **her**  - This refers to Jerusalem.
  * **leaders**  - These are the noble men of Jerusalem.
  * **more ruddy than coral**  - Coral is a red-colored calcium that is formed by sea creatures. The red color refers to the healthy skin of the leaders.
  * **their form like sapphire**  - Sapphire is a blue gem. It is not clear why the leaders of Jerusalem are "blue" in color. (See: Simile)
  * **darkness blackens their appearance**  - Because they cannot wash and live outside, exposed to the sun, they are darker in appearance.
  * **​their skin clings to their withered bones **  ​- The leaders are dehydrated and their skin and bones are unhealthy.
  * **as dry as wood **- The leaders' skin has become as dry as wood due to lack of adequate food and water. (See: Simile)
