===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They now wander as blind men in the streets** - The prophets and priests wander aimlessly in the streets as if they cannot see where they are going. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **They are defiled by that blood** - AT: "that blood that they have shed has defiled them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they cry out** - Here the word "they" refers to people whom the prophets and priests meet.
  * **they wander abroad** - Here the word "they" refers to the prophets and priests.
