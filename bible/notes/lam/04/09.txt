===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Those slain by the sword** - AT: "Those whom enemy soldiers have killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **those slain by hunger** - AT: "those who starve to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **pierced by hunger** - Hunger pierces and kills as if it is a sword. AT: "whom hunger has pierced" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **waste away** - This refers to people losing body weight because of slow starvation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **hunger for the crops of the field **- The people hungered for crops that did not exist.
  * **The hands of the compassionate women** - AT: "Compassionate women" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the crushing of the daughter of my people** - See how you translated this phrase in [[en:bible:notes:lam:02:11|2:11]].
