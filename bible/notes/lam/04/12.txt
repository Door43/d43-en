===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **adversary and enemy** - These two words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **sins of her prophets, and the iniquities of her priests** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **who have shed the blood of the righteous** - AT: "who have murdered the righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
