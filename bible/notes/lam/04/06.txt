===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **iniquity **- The word used here can mean either "sin" or "punishment for sin."
  * **who was overthrown in a moment** - AT: "whom God destroyed in a moment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **though no hands seized her** - God destroyed Sodom without the help of human soldiers. AT: "though no human soldiers captured her"
