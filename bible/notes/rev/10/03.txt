===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then he shouted** - "Then the angel shouted"
  * **the seven thunders spoke out** - The thunder is described as if it were a person who could speak. AT: "the seven thunders made a loud noise" or "the thunder sounded very loudly seven times"
  * **seven thunders** - Thunder occurring seven times is spoken of as if it were seven different "thunders."
  * **but I heard a voice from heaven** - The word "voice" refers to the speaker. It is not the angel. AT: "but I heard someone speaking from heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
