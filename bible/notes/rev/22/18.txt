===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I testify** - Here "I" refers to John.
    * **If anyone adds to them...if anyone takes away** - This is a strong warning to not change anything about this prophecy.
    * **written about in this book** - AT: "which I have written about in this book" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
