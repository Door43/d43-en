===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - John gives his final remarks about the book of Revelation. 

  * **I testify** - Here "I" refers to John.
  * **the words of the prophecy of this book** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:22:06|22:7]].
  * **If anyone adds to them ... If anyone takes away** - This is a strong warning to not change anything about this prophecy.
  * **written about in this book** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "which I have written about in this book" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
