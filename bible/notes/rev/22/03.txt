===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There will no longer be any curse** - Possible meanings are 1) "There will never be anyone ... there that God will curse" or 2) "There will not be anyone there who is under God's curse."
  * **his servants will serve him** - Possible meanings of "his" and "him" are 1) both words refer to God the Father, or 2) both words refer to both God and the Lamb, who rule together as one.
