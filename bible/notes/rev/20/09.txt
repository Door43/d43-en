===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They went** - "Satan's army went"
  * **the beloved city** - This refers to Jerusalem. 
  * **fire came down from heaven and devoured them** - Here John speaks of fire as if it were alive. AT: "God sent fire from heaven to burn them up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  * **The devil, who deceived them, was thrown** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God threw the devil who had deceived them" or "God's angel threw the devil, who had deceived them," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **lake of burning sulfur** - "the lake of fire that burns with sulfur" or "place full of fire that burns with sulfur." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:19:19|19:20]].
  * **where the beast and the false prophet had been thrown** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "where he had also thrown the beast and the false prophet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **They will be tormented** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will torment them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
