===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses are a break from John's account of his vision. Here he give a warning to the people reading his account.

  * **If anyone has an ear** - Being willing to listen is spoken of as having an ear. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:06|2:7]]. AT: "Let the one who is willing to listen, listen" or "If you are willing, listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **If anyone is to be taken** - This expression means that someone has decided who should be taken. If needed, translators may state clearly who decided it. AT: "If God has decided that someone should be taken" or "If it is God's will that someone should be taken" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **If anyone is to be taken into captivity** - This can be stated in active form. The noun "captivity" can be stated with the verb "capture." AT: "If it is God's will for the enemy to capture a certain person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **into captivity he will go** - The noun "captivity" can be stated with the verb "capture." AT: "he will be captured" or "the enemy will capture him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **If anyone is to be killed with a sword** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "If it is God's will for the enemy to kill a certain person with a sword" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **with a sword** - A sword represents war. AT: "in war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he will be killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the enemy will kill him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Here is a call for patient endurance and faith for those who are holy** - "Those who are holy must endure patiently and be faithful"
