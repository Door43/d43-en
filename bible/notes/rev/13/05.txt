===== translationNotes: =====


  * **The beast was given ... It was permitted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God gave the beast ... God permitted the beast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **The beast was given a mouth that could speak proud words and blasphemies** - Being given a mouth refers to being allowed to speak. AT: "The beast was allowed to speak proud words and blasphemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **forty-two months** - "42 months" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **to speak blasphemies against God** - "to say disrespectful things about God"
  * **blaspheming his name, the place where he lives, and those who live in heaven** - These phrases tell how the beast spoke blasphemies against God.
