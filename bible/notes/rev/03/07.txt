===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **key of David **  - The "key" means spiritual authority or power. (See: Metaphor)
  * **I know what you have done **- AT: "I know the things you do". See Rev. 3:1
  * **open door**  - The "open door" refers to the opportunity to enter into the Kingdom of God. (See: Metaphor).
