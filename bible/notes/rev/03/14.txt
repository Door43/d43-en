===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Laodicea **  - This is the name of the city. (See: translate names)
  * **The words of the Amen **- (See: personification).
  * **the ruler over the creation of God **- "ruler" refers to Jesus. (See: Implicit-Explicit).
  * **I know what you have done **- AT: "I know the things you do". See Rev. 2:2.
  * **cold nor hot **- This represents 2 extremes.
  * **lukewarm**  - This refers to being ineffective or having no conviction.
