===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who conquers** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:06|2:7]]
  * **will be clothed in white garments** - This can be translated with an active verb. AT: "will wear white garments" or "I will give him white clothes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **speak his name** - He would not simply say the person's name, but announce that the person belongs to him. AT: "I will announce that he belongs to me"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my Father** - This is an important title for God that describes the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **Let the one who has an ear listen  ... the churches** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:06|2:7]]
