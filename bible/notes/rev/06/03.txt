===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the second seal** - "the next seal" or "seal number two" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the second living creature** - "the next living creature" or "living creature number two" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **came out—fiery red** - This can the stated as a second sentence. AT: "came out. It was red like fire" or "came out. It was bright red" 
  * **To its rider was given permission** - This can be stated with an active verb. AT: "God gave permission to its rider" or "Its rider received person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **This rider was given a huge sword** - This can be stated with an active verb. AT: "This rider received a huge sword" or "God gave this rider a huge sword" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **huge** - "very large" or "great"
