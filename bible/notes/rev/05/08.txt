===== translationNotes: =====

  * **prostrated themselves** - "lay down on the ground." Their faces were toward the ground.
  * **Each of them** - Possible meanings are 1) "each of the elders and living creatures" or 2) "each of the elders."
  * **a golden bowl full of incense, which is the prayers of the believers** - The incense here is a symbol for the believers' prayers to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
