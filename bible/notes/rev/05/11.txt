===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ten thousands of ten thousands and thousands of thousands** - Use an expression in your language that shows that it is a huge number. AT: "millions" or "too many thousands to count" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Worthy is the Lamb** - "The Lamb is worthy"
  * **to receive power, wealth, wisdom, strength, honor, glory, and praise** - These are all things that the Lamb has. Being praised for having them is spoken of as receiving them. See how you translated a similar sentence in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:09|4:11]]. AT: "to be praised for his glory, wealth, wisdom, strength, honor, glory" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Worthy ... to receive ... praise** - This means that he deserves that everyone should praise him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
