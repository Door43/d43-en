===== translationNotes: =====

  * **those who were not killed by these plagues** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "those whom the plagues had not killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **things that cannot see, hear, or walk** - This phrase reminds us  that idols are not alive and do not deserve to be worshiped. But the people did not stop worshiping them.  AT: "even though idols cannot see, hear, or walk" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
