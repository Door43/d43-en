===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John continues to describe the horses and the plagues brought upon humanity.
 
  * **A third of the people** - "One third of the people." See how you translated "A third" in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:08:06|8:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
  * **For the power of the horses was in their mouths and in their tails** - The abstract noun "power" can be translated with an adjective. "For it was the horses' mouths and tails that were so powerful" or "for it was the horses' mouths and tails that were able to hurt people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
