===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The voice of the crowd from the previous verse continues speaking.

  * **Let us rejoice** - Here "us" refers to all of God's servants.
  * **give him the glory** - "give God the glory" or "honor God"
  * **wedding celebration of the Lamb ... his bride has made herself ready** - Here John speaks of the joining of Jesus and his people together forever as if it were a wedding celebration. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Lamb** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:05:06|5:6]].
  * **has come** - Existing in the present is spoken of as having come. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his bride has made herself ready** - John speaks of God's people as if they were a bride who is getting ready for her wedding. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **She was permitted to be dressed in bright and clean fine linen** - Here "she" refers to the people of God. John speaks of the righteous acts of God's people as if they were a bright and clean dress that a bride wears on her wedding day. You can state this in active form. AT: "God allowed her to wear a dress of bright and clean fine linen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
