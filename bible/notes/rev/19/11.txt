===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Then I saw heaven open** - This imagery is used to signify the beginning of a new vision.  See how you translated this idea in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:01|4:1]], [[:en:bible:notes:rev:11:19|11:19]], and [[:en:bible:notes:rev:15:05|15:5]].
    * **He wears a robe that was dipped in blood** - AT: "He wears a robe that was stained with blood" of "He wears a robe dripping with blood"
