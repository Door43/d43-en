===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **setting out in order to make war**  - "on their way to make war".
    * **The beast was captured**  - AT: "The one on the horse captured the beast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **those who had received the mark of the beast and who worshiped his image**  - This is the same group of people that have both received the mark and worshiped his image, not two separate groups of people.
    * **The two of them were thrown**  - AT: "The one on the horse threw the two of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
