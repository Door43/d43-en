===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John begins to describe the next part of his vision. This is the first of three angels who proclaim judgment on the earth.

  * **every nation, tribe, language, and people** - See how you translated a similar list in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:07:09|7:9]].
  * **the hour of his judgment has come** - The angel spoke of time as if it could come. The idea of "judgement" can be expressed with a verb. AT: "It is now the time for God to judge people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]))
