===== translationNotes: =====

  * **great wine vat ... winepress** - These refer to the same container.
  * **the great wine vat of God's wrath** - "the large wine vat where God will show his wrath"
  * **bridle** - a device made of leather straps that goes around a horse's head and is used for directing the horse
  * **1,600 stadia** - "300 kilometers" or "200 miles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
