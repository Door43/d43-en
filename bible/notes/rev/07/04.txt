===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I heard the number...sealed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God's angels marked them. I heard the number of people when they had marked them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **12,000** - There were twelve thousand in each tribe. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **144,000** - These are the twelve groups of 12,000 from each tribe. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **every tribe of the people of Israel** - There were twelve tribes in Israel, each named for one of Jacob's sons.
