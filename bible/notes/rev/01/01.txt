===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his servants**  - This phrase refers to Jesus' "willing servants;" these are the believers in Christ or all Christians.
  * **what must soon take place**  - AT: "The things that are necessary will soon occur."
  * **made it known**  - This phrase means, "communicated it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the one who reads aloud** - "all people who read aloud."
  * **the time is near**  - "Time" here refers to "what soon must take." AT: "The time of the things that must take place is near" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
 
  * **bold words**  - explanation
