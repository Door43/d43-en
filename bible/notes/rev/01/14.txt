===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **head and hair were as white as wool—as white as snow**  - These two phrases are used to describe and emphasize how white his head and hair were. (See: Doublet and Simile)
    * **eyes were like a fiery flame**  - This phrase describes that his eyes were shining like a bright fire. (See: Simile)
    * **feet were like burnished bronze**  - AT: "feet were like polished bronze" (See:Simile)
    * **like bronze that had been refined in a furnace**  - AT: "like bronze burned in fire to make it pure"
    * **He had in his right hand**  - AT: "He held in his right hand"
