===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - This refers to John.
  * **a loud voice** - The speaker is not identified, but would be a believer who is in heaven.
  * **our brothers** -  AT: "the brothers of us believers in heaven."
  * **day and night** - This means he is continually accusing the brothers.
