===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the bottomless pit** - See how you translated "bottomless pit" in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:11:06|11:7]].
  * **Then it will go on to destruction** - The noun "destruction" can be translated with a verb. AT: "Then he will be destroyed" or "Then God will destroy him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **it will go on to destruction** - The certainty of what will happen in the future is spoken of as if the beast were going to it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]], [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **those whose names have not been written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "those whose names God did not write" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **since the foundation of the world** - "before God created the world"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
