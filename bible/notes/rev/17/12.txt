===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ten horns that you saw**  - Here, "you" refers to the writer, John.
  * **who have not yet received a kingdom**  - "who have not yet started ruling".
  * **they will receive authority**  - AT: "God will give them authority" (See: Passive)
  * **for one hour**  - This does not mean one 60-minute time period, but symbolically represents a very short time. AT: "for a very short time".
  * **these are of one mind**  - "One mind" is an idiom meaning they are all in agreement. AT: "these are all in agreement" (See: Idiom)
  * **they give their power and authority to the beast**  - "power" and "authority" have very similar meanings here (See: Doublet).
  * **Lord of lords and King of kings**  - These two terms have very similar meanings.  AT: "lord an king of all" (See: Doublet).
  * **the called ones, the chosen**  - These two terms have the same meaning (See: Doublet).
  * **the called ones**  - those whom God has appointed and chosen to be His people
