===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Seven thousand people** - "7,000 people" (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the survivors** - "those who do not die" or "those who are still living"
  * **give glory to the God of heaven** - "say that the God of heaven is glorious"
  * **The second woe is past** - See how you translated "The first woe is past" in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:09:10|9:12]]. 
  * **The third woe is coming quickly** - Existing in the future is spoken of as coming. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]). AT: "The third woe will happen soon."
