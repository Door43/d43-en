===== translationNotes: =====

  * **twenty-four elders** - "24 elders." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:04|4:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **prostrated themselves on their faces** - They lay down facing the ground. See how you translated a similar expression in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:09|4:10]].
  * **you, Lord God Almighty, the one who is and who was** - These phrases can be stated as sentences. AT: "you, Lord God, the ruler over all. You are the one who is, and you are the one who was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
  * **the one who is** - "the one who exists" or "the one who lives"
  * **who was** - "who has always existed" or "who has always lived"
  * **you have taken your great power** - What God did with his great power can be stated clearly. AT: "you have defeated with your power everyone who has rebelled against you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
