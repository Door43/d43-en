===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the tent of witness -**  This term refers to the tent of the testimony. Translate this term the same way as you did in the Old Testament for the tent that the Israelites set up in the wilderness.
  * **after these things**  - This phrase refers to the other signs and wonders in the previous verses.
  * **with gold sashes around their chests**
 - The word "sash" is defined as a decorative piece of cloth worn on the upper body.
