===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then I saw...is completed** - Here 15:1 is a summary of what will happen in 15:2-16:21.
  * **Then I saw** - Here the word "I" refers to John.
  * **great and marvelous** - These words mean basically the same thing and are used for emphasis. AT: "something that greatly amazed me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **There were seven angels with seven plagues, which are the final plagues** - "There were seven angels and each one had one of the final plagues"
  * **for with them the wrath of God is completed** - AT: "for these plagues complete the wrath of God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
