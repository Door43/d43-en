===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **They were singing** - "Those who had been victorious over the beast were singing"
  * **Who will not fear you, Lord, and glorify your name?** - This question is used to show their amazement at how great and glorious the Lord is. It can be expressed as an exclamation. AT: "Lord, everyone will fear you and glorify your name!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **glorify your name** - The phrase "your name" refers to God. AT: "glorify you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your righteous deeds have been revealed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you have made everyone know about your righteous deeds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
