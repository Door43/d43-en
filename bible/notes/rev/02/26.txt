===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who conquers** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:06|2:7]]
  * **He will rule ... break them into pieces** - This is a prophecy from the Old Testament about a king of Israel, but Jesus applied it here to those to whom he gives authority over the nations.
  * **He will rule them with an iron rod** - Ruling harshly is spoken of as ruling with an iron rod. AT: "He will rule them harshly as if striking them with an iron stick." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **like clay jars he will break them into pieces** - Breaking them to pieces is an image that represents either 1) destroying evildoers or 2) defeating enemies. AT: "He will defeat his enemies completely like breaking clay jars into pieces"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Just as I have received from my Father** - Some languages may need to tell what he received. AT: "Just as I have received authority from my Father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **my Father** - This is an important title for God that describes the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **I will also give him** - Here "him" refers to the one who conquers.
  * **morning star** - This is a bright star that appears early in the morning just before dawn. It was a symbol of victory. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  *  **Let the one who has an ear listen** - Being willing to listen is spoken of as having an ear. AT: "Let the one who is willing to listen, listen" or "If you are willing, listen." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:06|2:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
