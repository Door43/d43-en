===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But I have this against you** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:03|2:4]].
  * **the woman Jezebel** - Jesus spoke of a certain woman in their church as if she were Queen Jezebel, because she did the same kinds of sinful things that Queen Jezebel had done long before that time. AT: "the woman who is just like Jezebel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I gave her time to repent** - "I gave her opportunity to repent" or "I waited for her to repent"
