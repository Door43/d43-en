===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the beginning of the Son of Man's message to the angel of the church in Ephesus.

  * **the angel** - See how you translated "angel" in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:01:19|1:20]]
  * **stars** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:01:14|1:16]]. The stars are symbols. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **lampstands** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:01:12|1:12]]. The lampstands are symbols. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **I know ... your hard labor and your patient endurance** - "Labor" and "endurance" are abstract nouns and can be translated with verbs "work" and "endure." AT: "I know ... that you work very hard and that you endure patiently" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **who claim themselves to be apostles** - "who say they are apostles"
  * **but are not** - "but are not apostles"
  * **and have found them to be false** - "and you have recognized that those people are false apostles"
