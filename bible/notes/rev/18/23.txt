===== Translation Notes: =====
The angel continues speaking to the people of Babylon.

  * **The voices of the bridegroom and the bride will not be heard in you anymore** - AT: "No one will ever again hear the happy voices of a bridegroom and a bride" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **your merchants were the princes of the earth** - "The people in other nations highly honored your merchants"
  * **and the nations were deceived by your sorcery** - AT: "and you deceived the people of the nations with your magic spells" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **In her the blood of prophets and saints was found, and the blood of all who have been killed on the earth** - AT: "God has declared you guilty for killing the prophets and saints and for all the other people in the world that you killed. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
