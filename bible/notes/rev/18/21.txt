===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **millstone** - large round stone used to crush grain
  * **Babylon, the great city, will be thrown down with violence and will not be seen anymore** - God will completely destroy the city. AT: "God will violently throw down Babylon, the great city, and it will no longer exist" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The sound of harpers, musicians, flute players, and trumpeters will not be heard anymore in you** - AT: "No one will ever again hear the sound of music in your city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **No craftsman of any kind will be found in you. No sound of a mill will be heard anymore in you** - AT: "No one will ever again see or hear workers in your city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
