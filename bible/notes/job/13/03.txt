===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I wish to reason with God - Job has found Zophar and other friends to be deceitful and not honest. Job would ather speak to God alone about his complaint. He finds his friends foolish and not trustworthy.**
  * **whitewash the truth - This is an idiom referring to painting over a wall to cover the dirt and means to not tell something that is true because it is not pleasant or is unpopular. (See: Idiom)**
  * **physicians of no value - These friends came to Job to mourn with him. The offered councel. They would probably better be called counselors that doctors, because this better conveys the sense. Their counsel was false deceitful and wrong.**
  * **hold your peace- This expression means "be silent". (See: Idiom) **
  * **be your wisdom**  - This means that by being silent they would not be saying more foolish talk and would appear wise in their silence.
