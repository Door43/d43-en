===== Translation Notes: =====
Job continues speaking.
  * **Why are times for judging wicked people not set by the Almighty?** - AT: "I do not understand why God does not set a time when he will judge wicked people" or "The Almighty should set a time when he will judge wicked people"
  * **Why do not those who are faithful to God see his days of judgment come?** - AT: "It seems that those who obey him never get to see when him judge the wicked" or "God should show the day he will judge the wicked to those who know him.
