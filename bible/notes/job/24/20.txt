===== Translation Notes: =====
Job continues speaking
  * **The womb** - AT: "The woman" or "The mother" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the worm will feed sweetly on him** - AT: "the worm will soon eat his dead body" or "he will soon be dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will be remembered no more** - AT: "no one will remember him anymore" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **wickedness will be broken like a tree** - AT: "God will destroy the wicked like a person cutting down a tree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **The wicked one devours** - AT: "Like a wild animal attacks its prey, the wicked person harms" or "The wicked person harms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **widow** - a woman whose husband has died
