===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Job continues speaking.

  * **by his power** - "by using his power" or "because he is powerful"
  * **he rises up and does not strengthen them in life** - Here "does not strengthen them in life" means God does not keep them alive. AT: "God rises up and does not give wicked people the strength to live" or "God rises up and causes them to die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **but his eyes are on their ways** - Here "eyes" refers to God. AT: "but he is always watching what they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
