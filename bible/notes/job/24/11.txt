===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They make oil** - "They" refers to the poor people mentioned in the previous chunk.
  * **within the walls of these wicked men** - AT: "in the houses of these evil men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **they themselves suffer thirst** - "they suffer from thirst" AT: "they are thirsty"
