===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I selected their ways** - Here "selected their ways" represents deciding what they should do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **sat as their chief** - Here "sat" represents ruling or leading. Chiefs sat down when they made important decisions. AT: "led them as their chief" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sat as their chief** - Job was their chief. AT: "led them because I was their chief" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I lived like a king with his army** - Job speaks of how he led the people and how they obeyed him as if he were a king and they were his army.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like one who comforts mourners** - This phrase means that Job actually was one who comforted people. AT: "and I comforted them when they mourned"
