===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my bones cling to my skin and my flesh - This saying means that Job is very thin, as from disease. (See: Idiom)**
  * **survive by the skin of my teeth - This saying means "I am barely surviving". (See: Idiom)**
  * **Why do you persecute me as if you were God - Job is saying, It is not your right to punish me like you are, since you are not God and do not understand my situation. (See: Euphemism)**
