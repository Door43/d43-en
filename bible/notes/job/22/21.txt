===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Eliphaz continues speaking to Job.

  * **Now** - Eliphaz uses this word to introduce something important he is about to say.
  * **instruction from his mouth** - Here "from his mouth" represents what God has spoken. AT: "the instruction that God has spoken" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **store up his words** - God's words are compared to treasures that Job could keep in a storeroom. AT: "treasure his commands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **your heart** - Here "heart" refers to Job's thoughts. AT: "your mind"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
