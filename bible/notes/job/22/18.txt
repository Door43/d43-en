===== Translation Notes: =====
Eliphaz continues speaking to Job.
  * **the plans of wicked people are far from me** - AT: "but I will not listen to their wicked plans"
  * **see their fate** - AT: "know what will happen to the wicked"
  * **laugh them to scorn** - AT: "mock the wicked people"
  * **those who rose up against us are cut off** - AT: "God has destroyed those who harmed us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
