===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who can bring something clean out of something unclean? No one** - This is a rhetorical question with the answer. AT: "No one can bring something clean out of something unclean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Man's days are determined** - AT: "You determined a man's days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **hired man** - a man who is hired to do a job and goes home afterwards
