===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement** - Job continues to tell about the fathers of the mockers.

  * **saltwort ... bushes' leaves ... the roots of the broom tree** - These are plants that people would eat only if they could find nothing better.
  * **the roots of the broom tree were their food** - Possible meanings are 1) the people ate the roots of the broom tree or 2) the people warmed themselves by burning the roots of broom trees.
  * **They were driven out from among people who shouted after them as ... a thief** - The phrase "were driven out" means "were forced to leave." These phrases can be reordered and stated in active form. AT: "The people shouted after them as ... a thief and forced them to leave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **shouted after them as one would shout after a thief** -"shouted at them as though they were thieves"
