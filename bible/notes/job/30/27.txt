===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My heart is troubled and does not rest** - Job speaks of his heart as if it were a person. AT: "I am troubled in my heart and the feeling does not end" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **days of affliction have come on me** - Days of affliction coming on Job represents Job experiencing affliction for many days. AT: "I experience affliction many days" or "I suffer every day" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I go about with darkened skin but not because of the sun** - Possible meanings are 1) Job's skin has become dark because of disease or 2) darkness is a metaphor that represents Job's deep sadness. AT: "I am terribly sad, and the sun does not cheer me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a brother to jackals, a companion of ostriches** - Being a brother to these animals is a metaphor for being like them. AT: "I am like jackals and ostriches that cry out in the wilderness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
