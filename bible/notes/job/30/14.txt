===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like an army through a wide hole in the city wall** - This phrase compares Job to a city under attack with a hole in its defensive wall. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **my honor is driven away as if by the wind** - AT: "the wind has blown away my honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my prosperity passes away as a cloud** - Job's belongings have disappeared just like a cloud that appears and then vanishes from the sky.
