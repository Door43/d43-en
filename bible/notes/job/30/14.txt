===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement** - Job continues to speak about the people who were mocking him.
  * **General Information** -  Job speaks about the mockers treating him cruelly as if they were an army attacking him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **They come against me like an army through a wide hole in a city wall** - This represents attacking Job forcefully. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **they roll themselves in on me** - This represents many coming to attack him at once, like giant ocean waves rolling in on him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **Terrors are turned upon me** - Possible meanings are 1) Job has become terrified or 2) things are happening to Job that make him afraid. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my honor is driven away as if by the wind** - Job speaks of suddenly having no honor as if the wind had blown it from him. AT: "Nobody honors me" or "I am now a person that people do not honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **my prosperity passes away as a cloud** - Job speaks of his prosperity ending as if it were a cloud that blew away. Job's belongings have disappeared like a cloud that suddenly appears and then vanishes from the sky. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
