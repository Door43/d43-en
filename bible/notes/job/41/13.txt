===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting statement:** - Yahweh continues to rebuke Job by asking him questions.

  * **Who can strip off his outer covering?** - This can be translated as a statement. AT: "No one can strip off his outer clothing." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Who can penetrate his double armor?** - The word "armor" is a metaphor for the hard scales or hide on his back. This can be translated as a statement. AT: "No one can penetrate his very thick hide." (See:  and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Who can open the doors of his face ... terror?** - This can be translated as a statement. AT: "No one can pry his jaws apart ... terror." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **tight together as with a close seal.** - This means the scales are very close to one another and nothing can get between them.
