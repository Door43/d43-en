===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verses 13-14 **- Both these verses contain rhetorical questions that are intended to be answered in the negative.(See:Rhetorical Question) These sentences could be translated positively like this, "No one can strip off his outer clothing. No one can penetrate his double armour. No one can open the double doors of his face".
  * **close together as with a close seal. **- This means the scales are very close to one another and tightly bound together.
