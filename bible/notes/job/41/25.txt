===== Translation Notes: ====

  * **even the gods become afraid;** - AT: "even very strong people become afraid;" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **He thinks of iron as if it were straw**  - AT: "He thinks of weapons made of iron as if they were weapon made of straw" This shows how ineffective these strong metals are against Leviathan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and of bronze as if it were rotten wood**  - AT: "He thinks of weapons made of bronze as if they were weapons made of rotten wood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
