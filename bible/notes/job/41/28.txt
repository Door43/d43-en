===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to him sling stones become chaff**- This expression means that sling stones are no more useful thaan chaff in the wind against Leviathan.(See: Metaphor)
  * **Verse 29-clubs as straw **- This expression means that a club is no more effective than a piece of straw for beating Leviathan.(See: Simile and Hyperbole)
  * **Verse 29-he laughs at the whirring flight **- This supposes that the Leviathan is as a human laughing at the futility of their battling against him by throwing spears.(See: Personification)
  * **Verse 30-like sharp pieces of pottery**- This expression says that his scales are sharp.(See: Simile)
  * **verse 30- as threshing sledge**- This compares his tail to threshing sledge that scrapes the ground and leaves a mark as it moves.(See: Simile)
