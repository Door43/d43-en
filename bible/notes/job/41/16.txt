===== translationNotes: =====


  * **One is so near to another** - "One row of shields is so near to another"
  * **them ... they** - The words "them" and "they" refer to the rows of shields. 
  * **they cannot be pulled apart** - This can be translated as a statement. AT: "no one can pull them apart" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
