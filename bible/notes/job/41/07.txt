===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Can you fill his hide with harpoons or his head with fishing spears?**  - AT: "You cannot pierce his skin with your hunting weapons, nor can you pierce his head with fishing spears." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **harpoon**  - a large spear with a barbed point used to hunt large fish or whales.
  * **will not anyone be thrown down to the ground just by the sight of him?**  - "Anyone will be thrown down to the ground just by the sight of him". (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
