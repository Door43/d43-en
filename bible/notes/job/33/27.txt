===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Elihu continues speaking to Job.

  * **but my sin was not punished** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "but God did not punish me for sinning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **rescued my soul from going down into the pit** - Here the person is referred to by his "soul." This speaks of the person dying and going to the place where dead people are. AT: "rescued me from dying and going to the grave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **my life will continue to see light** - Here the person is represented by his "life." Also, living is spoken of as seeing the light. AT: "I will contineu to live and see the daylight" or "I will continue to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
