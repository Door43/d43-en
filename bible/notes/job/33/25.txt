===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Elihu continues speaking to Job.

  * **then** - The word "then" is used here to mark what will happen if God grants the angel's request. AT: "then as a result" or "as a result of the angel's request to God"
  * **his flesh will become fresher than a child's** - This speaks of the man being healed and his body growing strong again as if his body became new like a child's body. AT: "the sick man's body will become new again like a young person's body" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **fresher than a child's** - In this comparison, the word "fresher" is an exaggeration. AT: "fresh like a child's" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **a child's** - The missing word may be added. AT: "a child's flesh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **he will return to the days of his youthful strength** - This speaks of the man being strong again when like when was young as if he was in fact actually young again. AT: "he will become strong again, like when he was young" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he sees God's face with joy** - This is an idiom. AT: "he joyfully worships God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **God's face** - Here God is represented by his "face." AT: "God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **God will give the person his triumph** - "God will save the person" or "God will make things right for the person again"
