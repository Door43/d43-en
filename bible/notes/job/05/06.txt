===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For difficulties do not come out from the soil; neither does trouble sprout from the ground** - Here difficulties and trouble are spoken of as if they were plants. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **but mankind is born for trouble, just as sparks fly upward** - It is as natural for people, once they are born, to have trouble as it is for sparks to fly up from a fire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
