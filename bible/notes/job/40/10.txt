===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **Scatter around the excess of your anger;** - AT: "Reveal how angry you are at the pride of people;" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **look at everyone who is proud**  - AT: "look at all the proud people"
  * **bring him down**  - AT: "lower him in importance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
