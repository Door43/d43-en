===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He** - This refers to the wicked man whom Eliphaz started to describe in the previous chunk.
  * **return out of darkness** - AT: "return from the dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the sword waits for him** - The sword is a metaphor for death. At the same time the sword is described as if it were a person that is waiting. AT: "the sword is like a person that is waiting for him to kill him" or "death waits for him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the day of darkness** - AT: "the day of disaster" or "the moment of his death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **is at hand** - AT: "is coming soon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **distress and anguish** - These two words mean the same thing and are used together to emphasize the emotion. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **they prevail against him** - "it will overpower him" or "they will defeat him"
  * **as a king ready for battle** - This compares the fears he has to a king with an army. AT: "just like a king, who is ready for a battle, would prevail against him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
