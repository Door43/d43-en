===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fire will consume their tents of bribery** - AT: "the tents they bought with their bribes will be burned by fire"
  * **They conceive mischief and give birth to iniquity; their womb conceives deceit** - The same thought is repeated three times to emphasize how much evil these people produce. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **They conceive mischief** - AT: "Like a woman conceives a child, so they conceive mischief" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **give birth to iniquity** - AT: "like a woman gives birth to a child, so they produce iniquity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **their womb conceives deceit** - AT: "they conceive deceit" or "like a woman conceives a child, so they conceive deceit" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
