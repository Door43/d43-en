===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he has reached out with his hand against God** - "he has shook his fist against God." This is a sign of aggression. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **runs at God** - This speaks of the wicked man acting aggressively against God as if he were running towards him to attack him. AT: "attacks God" or "acts violently against God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **with a thick shield** - "with his strong shield"
