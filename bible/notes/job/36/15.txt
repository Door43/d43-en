===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he opens their ears** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:job:36:10|v. 10]].
  * **your table would be set** - AT: "your servants would set your table" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **food full of fatness** - Meat that had plenty of fat on it was a sign of prosperity because the animals were healthy and well-fed. AT: "the very best food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
