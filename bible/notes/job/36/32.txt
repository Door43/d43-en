===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He covers his hands over the lightning bolts** - Possible meanings are 1) that God holds the lightning bolts in his hands in order to throw them, or 2) that God hides the lightning bolts in his hands until he is ready to use them.
  * **Their noise** - "The noise of the lightning bolts" or "The thunder"
