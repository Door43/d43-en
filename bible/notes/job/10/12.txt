===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **covenant faithfulness**  - "steadfast love"
  * **your help**  - AT: "your overseeing" or "your providence" or "your care"
  * **you hid in your heart **- AT: "you kept to yourself" or "you kept this a secret"
  * **that if I sinned, you would notice it**  - AT: "if I sinned, you would see it"
  * **you would not acquit me of my iniquity **- AT: "you would not allow me to go unpunished" or "you would not overlook my sin"
