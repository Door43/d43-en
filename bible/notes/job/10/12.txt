===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You have granted me life and covenant faithfulness** - Here the abstract noun "life" means physical life and "covenant faithfulness" refers to God being faithful to his promises. The abstract nouns "life" and "faithfulness" can be stated as "live" and "faithful." AT: "You have been faithful to your covenant and allowed me to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **your help** - "your care"
  * **guarded my spirit** - Here Job is represented by his "spirit." AT: "guarded me" or "watched carefully over me" or "kept me safe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **these things you hid in your heart** - Here "these things" refer to the things spoken of in the verses that follow.
  * **these things you hid in your heart** - Here "you hid in your heart" means God kept them secret or hidden. AT: "these things you kept secret" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you would notice it** - "you would watch me"
