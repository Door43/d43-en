===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You have granted me life and covenant faithfulness** - Here the abstract noun "life" means physical life and "covenant faithfulness" refers to God being faithful to his promises. They can be translated as verbs. AT: "You have allowed me to live and faithfully protected me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **your help** - "your care"
  * **guarded my spirit** - Here "spirit" represents Job's life. AT: "guarded me" or "watched carefully over me" or "kept me safe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **these things you hid in your heart** - Here "these things" refer to the things spoken of in the verses that follow, and "you hid in your heart" means God kept them secret or hidden. AT: "these things you kept secret" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you would notice it** - "you would watch me"
