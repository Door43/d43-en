===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Your hands** - Here "hands" represent God and his creative action. AT: "You" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  *  **Your hands have framed and fashioned me together round about** - Job is using a metaphor of a potter forming clay to describe how God carefully created him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **framed and fashioned me** - The words "framed" and "fashioned" are similar in meaning. AT: "shaped and formed me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Call to mind** - "Remember"
  * **bring me into dust again** - "turn me back into dust again"
