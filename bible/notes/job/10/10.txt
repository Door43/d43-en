===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Have you not poured me out like milk and curdled me like cheese? **  - This is a rhetorical question that expects a positive answer. This question also uses metaphorical language to describe a baby formed in the womb. AT: "You have poured me out like milk and curdled me like cheese." (See: Rhetorical Question, Metaphor)
  * **You -**  This pronoun refers to God.
  * **me -**  This pronoun refers to Job.
  * **You have clothed me with skin and flesh **- Job uses poetic language to further describe how God has created him. AT: "You created my physical body".
  * **knit me together**  - AT: "woven me together"
  * **sinews**  - This is the part of the body that holds the bones together.
