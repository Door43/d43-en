===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You bring new witnesses against me** - Job's troubles from God are spoken of as if they were people who were witnesses against him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **increase your anger against me** - The abstract noun "anger" can be translated as the adjective "angry." AT: "are more and more angry with me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **you attack me with fresh armies** - God sending troubles against Job is spoken of as if God was constantly sending new armies against him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
