===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A man lays his hand on the flinty rock** - AT: "A man digs into the flinty rock;" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **flinty rock** - "hard rock"
  * **he overturns mountains by their roots** - The man who digs for valuable rocks exposes the "roots" of the mountain by digging into it.
