===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hidden from the eyes ... hidden from the birds** - Wisdom is not a part of the natural experience of humans or animals. It can only come from God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **destruction and death** - Both "death" and "destruction" are represented as saying that they have "heard" about wisdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
