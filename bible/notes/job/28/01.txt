===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **mine** - a place where metal ore is dug out of the earth
  * **refine** - This is the process of heating a metal to remove all of the impurities.
  * **copper** - an important red-brown colored metal
  * **smelted** - This is a process where heat is used to melt metal ore out of stone.
