===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the earth, out of which comes bread** - Here "bread" is a metonym for food in general. Food coming out of the ground is a metaphor for food growing in the ground. AT: "the earth, where food grows," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonym]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **it is turned up below as if by fire.** - AT: "but below the earth it looks like everything has been heated by fire and crushed"
  * **it is turned ... Its stones ... its dust** - The word "it" refers to the "earth".
  * **sapphires** - This word refers to a type of rare and expensive blue gem stone.
