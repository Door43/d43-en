===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **out of which bread comes** - AT: "where food is produced," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **it is turned up below as if by fire.** - AT: "but below the earth it looks like everything has been heated by fire and crushed"
  * **it is turned ... its stones ... its dust** - The word "it" refers to the "earth".
  * **sapphires**  - This word refers to a type of rare and expensive blue gem stone.
