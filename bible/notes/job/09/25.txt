===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My days are swifter than a running messenger** - Job compares how quickly his days are passing to a fast runner. AT: "My days pass swiftly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **running messenger** - "runner" or "running man"
  * **my days flee away** - This pictures Job's days as being able to run away like a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **they see no good anywhere** - This pictures Job's days as being able to see like a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **no good** - "no good thing"
  * **They are as fast as papyrus reed boats** - Job compares how quickly his days are passing to the speed of fast boats. AT: "They pass quickly by like papyrus reed boats" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **papyrus reed boats** - "boats made out of reeds." Papyrus reed is a hollow grass that grows along the banks of rivers.  
  * **as fast as the eagle that swoops down on its victim** - Job compares how quickly his days are passing to a large bird diving toward its prey. AT: "as fast as the eagle that flies down quickly to catch its food"
  * **swoops down** - "rushes down"
