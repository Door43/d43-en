===== translationNotes: =====

  * **If I washed myself with snow water** - "If I bathed my body in pure, clean water"
  * **snow water** - the water that comes from melted snow
  * **snow** - white flakes of frozen water that fall from clouds in places where the air temperature is cold
  * **made my hands ever so clean** - "made my hands exceedingly clean." Some other versions of the Bible translate this with the meaning of "cleaned my hands with very strong soap."
  * **plunge me in a ditch** - "throw me into a pit"
  * **my own clothes would be disgusted with me** - Job's clothing is spoken of as if it would have a negative response to Job after God plunged him into a ditch. AT: "I would be too filthy for my own clothing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
