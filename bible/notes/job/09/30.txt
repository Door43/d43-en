===== translationNotes: =====

  * **If I washed myself with snow water** - "If I bathed in pure, clean water"
  * **and made my hands ever so clean** - AT: "and cleaned my hands with soap"
  * **would plunge me in a ditch** - "would throw me into a pit"
  * **and my own clothes would be disgusted with me** - Job's clothing is being described here with human-like characteristics. AT: "I would be too dirty for my clothing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
