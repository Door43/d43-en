===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I truly know that this is so** - Here the word "this" refers to Bildad's description.  
  * **But how can a person be in the right with God?** - "How can anyone win an argument with God?"  
  * **If he wants to argue with God, he cannot answer Him once in a thousand times** - "If anyone wants to argue with God, he would be unable to answer even one of God's questions."
  * **to argue** - "to dispute"
