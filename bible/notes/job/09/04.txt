===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wise in heart** - Here the heart represents the inner being or thoughts. AT: "wise in what he decides" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **mighty in strength** - The abstract noun "strength" can be expressed as the adjective "strong." AT: "mighty in how strong he is" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **who has ever hardened himself against him** - "who has ever challenged God"
  * **he who removes the mountains without warning anyone** - "he removes the mountains"
  * **when he overturns them** - "when he overthrows them"
  * **he who shakes the earth** - "he shakes the earth"
  * **its supports** - "its foundations"
