===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wise in heart** - Here the heart represents the inner being or thoughts. AT: "wise in what he decides" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **mighty in strength** - The abstract noun "strength" can be expressed as the adjective "strong." AT: "mighty in how strong he is" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **who has ever hardened himself against him and succeeded?** - This is a rhetorical question that expects an answer of "No one." It can be reworded as a statement. AT: "no one has ever hardened himself against him and succeeded." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **hardened himself against him** - To harden oneself means to be stubborn. AT: "resisted him" or "defied him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **he who removes the mountains** - "God removes the mountains"
  * **in his anger** - The abstract noun "anger" can be expressed as the adjective "angry." AT: "because he is angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **he who shakes the earth** - "God shakes the earth"
  * **its supports** - "its foundations"
