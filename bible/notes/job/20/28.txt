===== Translation Notes: =====
This concludes Zophar's speech to Job.
  * **vanish** - "disappear" or "go away"
  * **his goods will flow away** - This means the wicked person will lose his possessions as if a flood came and washed them away. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the heritage reserved for him by God** - AT: "this is what God will give to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
