===== Translation Notes: =====
Zophar continues speaking to Job.
  * **Although wickedness is sweet in his mouth...the poison of asps inside him** - Zophar means that doing evil things may seem good at first and may cause the person to prosper, but eventually it will become bad and cause the wicked person's death. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
