===== Translation Notes: =====
Zophar continues speaking to Job.
  * **enjoy flowing streams of honey and butter** - This means the wicked person will not be able to enjoy all the blessings that God gives his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he will not swallow it down** - AT: "he will not be able to keep it"
