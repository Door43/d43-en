===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Skin for skin, indeed** - "Skin" is a metonym for Job's life. AT: “A person will do anything to save his own life, even accept the loss of possessions and loved ones.” (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **put forth your hand** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:job:01:09|01:11]]
    * **renounce you to your face** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:job:01:09|01:11]]. “he will reject you.”
