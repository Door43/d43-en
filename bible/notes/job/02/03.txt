===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This verse is the same as [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:8]], except for the addition of "He still holds fast to his integrity, although you misled me against him, to destroy him without cause."

  * **Have you considered my servant Job?** - This rhetorical question actually makes a statement.See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:8]]. AT: "Consider my servant Job." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **a blameless and upright man** - See how you translated a similar phrasee in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **one who fears God and turns away from evil** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:01|1:1]].
  * **still holds fast to his integrity** - "remains completely dedicated to doing what is good and right"
  * **misled me against him** - "persuaded me without cause to attack him" 
  * **to destroy him** - Here "destroy" represents "make poor." AT: "to make him a poor man"
