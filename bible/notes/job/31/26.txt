===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues describing situations in which he would deserve God's punishment, but he knows they are not true. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])

  * **the moon walking** - AT: "the moon moving across the sky" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **if my heart has been secretly attracted** - AT: "if I have secretly desired to worship them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my mouth has kissed my hand** - This is a sign of love and devotion.
  * **to be punished by judges** - AT: "for which judges would be right to punish me"
