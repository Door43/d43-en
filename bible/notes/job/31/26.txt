===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues describing situations in which he would deserve God's punishment, but he knows they are not true. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])

  * **the moon walking** - Here "walking" represents moving slowly. AT: "the moon moving across the sky" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the moon walking in its brightness** - The abstract noun "brightness" can be expressed with the words "bright" or "brightly." AT: "the bright moon moving across the sky" or "the moon moving brightly across the sky"
  * **if my heart has been secretly attracted** - Here "my heart" represents Job. This phrase can be stated in active form. AT: "if I have been secretly attracted to them" or "if I have secretly desired to worship them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so that my mouth has kissed my hand** - Here "my mouth" represents Job. This is a sign of love and devotion. AT: "so that I have kissed my hand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **in worship of them** - "to worship the sun and moon"
  * **to be punished by judges** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "for which judges would be right to punish me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I would have denied the God who is above** - "I would have been unfaithful to the God who is above"
