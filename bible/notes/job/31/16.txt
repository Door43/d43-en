===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job begins describing situations in which he would deserve God's punishment (described in [[:en:bible:notes:job:31:22|31:22]]), but he knows they are not true. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])

  * **I have caused the eyes of the widow to grow dim from crying** - Here "to grow dim" refers to the widow having bad eyesight due to how much she cries. AT: "I have caused a widow to cry in great sadness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my morsel** - "my food"
  * **instead, from my youth the orphan grew up with me as with a father, and I have guided his mother, a widow, from my own mother's womb** - Job is describing how he truly treated the orphan and widow. AT: "actually, I have taken care of orphans and widows since I was very young"
