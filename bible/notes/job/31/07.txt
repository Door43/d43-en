===== Translation Notes: =====
Job continues describing situations in which he would deserve God's punishment, but he knows they are not true. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **if my steps have turned from the right way** - AT: "if I have stopped doing what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **if my heart has walked after my eyes** - AT: "if I have done any wicked things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **if any spot of impurity has clung to my hands** - AT: "if I am guilty of any sin at all"
  * **let the harvest be uprooted out of my field** - AT: "let someone else come and take the harvest from my field" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
