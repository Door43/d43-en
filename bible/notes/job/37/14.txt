===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do you know how God establishes the clouds and makes the lightning bolts to flash in them?** - Elihu asks this question to emphasize that Job cannot know this. AT: "You cannot understand how God establishes the clouds and makes the lightning bolts to flash in them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **establishes the clouds** - "controls the clouds" or "makes the clouds obey him"
