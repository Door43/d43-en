===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **can you spread out the heavens as he does** - This means, you do not have power like his. (See: Rhetorical question and Active or Passive)
  * **teach us** - This is sarcasm. He does not really want Job to teach him anything.
  * **Verse 20** - This is a rhetorical question asked two ways and is expected to be answered negatively. (See: Rhetorical Question and Active or Passive)
