===== Translation Notes: =====
Elihu continues speaking.
  * **He breaks mighty men into pieces** - AT: "He destroys rulers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for their ways that need no further investigation** - "because he already knows what they have done"
  * **he puts others in their places** - "and he chooses other people to rule in their places"
  * **in the night** - AT: "when they are not expecting it"
