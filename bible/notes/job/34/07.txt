===== Translation Notes: =====
Elihu continues speaking.
  * **What man is like Job** - Elihu uses a question to scold Job. AT: "There is no one else like Job" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **who drinks up mockery like water** - Elihu is accusing Job of enjoying mocking other people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **who walks with wicked men** - AT: "who behaves like wicked men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
