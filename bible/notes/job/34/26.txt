===== Translation Notes: =====
Elihu continues speaking.
  * **In the open sight of others** - "In a place where everyone can see"
  * **they turned away from following him** - AT: "they would not obey him"
  * **his ways** - This refers to God's instructions for how people should behave. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **they made the cry of the poor come to him** - "they made the poor people cry, and God heard them"
