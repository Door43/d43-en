===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **how you have helped, how you have save - These two statements in verse 2 are exageration. What Job really says is that they have not helped him.(See: Rhetorical question and Hyperbole)**
  * **how you have advised, announced sound wisdom - These two statements in verse three are also exageration, and Job is really saying the opposite. He says their counsel and wisdom is false. (See: Hyperbole and Rhetorical question)**
  * **With whose help have you spoken – - Verse 4 contains two rhetorical questions saying the same thing, "You are from Satan". (See: Euphemism and Rhetorical question)**
