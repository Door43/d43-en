===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the shades - This expression means those who have died and are in Sheol.**
  * **the inhabitants beneath the waters - this expression refers to those in Sheol, which is deeper in the earth than the deepest sea.**
  * **destruction**
 - This expression means the place of destruction. This is another way of saying Sheol.
