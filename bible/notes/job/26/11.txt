===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at his rebuke** - The shaking of the earth that comes from thunder or an earthquake are spoken of as if the earth were set on pillars they shake in fear at God's rebuke. AT: "The pillars of heaven shake(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])

  * **Rahab** - The word "Rahab" here refers to a monster of the sea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **pillars of heaven** - Here "pillars of heaven" refers to the force that holds up the sky. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
