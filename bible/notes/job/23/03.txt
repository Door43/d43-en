===== Translation Notes: =====
Job continues speaking.

    * **I might find him** - AT: "I might find God"
    * **and fill my mouth with arguments** - AT: "and I would speak all of my arguments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **the words with which he would answer me** - AT: "what he would answer me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
