===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]\\

Bildad the Shuhite is speaking to Job.

    * **Then Bildad the Shuhite answered and said**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:job:08:01|8:1]].
    * **Put an end to your talk**  - This can also be translated as a question: "How long will it be until you stop talking?" Either way this means that Job has been talking for too long. AT: "Stop talking!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Consider**  - "Be reasonable" or "Show that you have some basic wisdom"
