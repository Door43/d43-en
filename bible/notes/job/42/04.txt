===== translationNotes: =====

  * **but now my eye sees you** - The eyes represent seeing, and seeing represents understanding. AT: "but now I really understand you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **despise myself** - Job's self is a metonym for what he said. AT: "I despise the things I said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **despise** - "intensely dislike" or "am unhappy with"
  * **I repent in dust and ashes** - Sitting in dust and ashes is a symbolic act showing the person is sorry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
