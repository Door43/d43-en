===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but now my eyes see you**  - AT: "but now I really understand you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sit in dust and ashes**  - This was a cultural way of showing remorse and repentance.
