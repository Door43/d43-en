===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verse 11-sorrowed and comforted him **- These two activities of sorrowing and comforting work together in the process of restoration of Job. (See: Hendiadys)
  * **Verse 11- before–they came**- This expression in the ULB might better read, "before, came".
