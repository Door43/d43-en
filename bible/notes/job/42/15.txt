===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **no women were found as beautiful as Job’s daughters**  - AT: "Job's daughters were very, very beautiful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **being old and full of years **- These two short phrases have the same meaning. They are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
