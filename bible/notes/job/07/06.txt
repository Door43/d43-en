===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a weaver's shuttle**  - explanation. AT: "bobbin" (used in weaving)
  * **My days are swifter than a weaver's shuttle**  - My life goes by very quickly.
  * **they pass**  - AT: "they come to an end"
  * **without hope**  - AT: "with absolutely no hope"
  * **God, call to mind **  - The word God is not in the Hebrew text, but is understood from the singular imperative. One could translate "Call to mind." (See: Implicit / Explicit).
  * **my life is only a breath **- My life is very short like taking one breath (See: Metaphor).
  * **a breath**  - AT: "wind"
  * **my eye will no more see good **- "I will never again experience happiness" d
