===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **restrain my mouth **  - AT: I will not stop talking
  * **the anguish of my spirit **  - not only does his body hurt badly but in Job's spirit he does not understand why he is suffering is badly.
  * **the bitterness of my soul **  - because Job does not understand why he is suffering he is becoming bitter in his soul.
  * **I will speak . . .  I will complain . . . **- These two statements explain why Job will not restrain his mouth. They express the same idea in different words. (See <font inherit/inherit;;#FF0000;;inherit>Doublet</font> )
  * **sea monster **  - this is a Figure of Speech referring to the large creatures in the deep of the sea. (See: <font inherit/inherit;;#FF0000;;inherit>Metaphor</font> )
  * **Am I the sea or a sea monster, that you place a guard over me? **  - This is a question that implies a "no" answer. AT: I am not the sea or a sea monster, that you place a guard over me. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **place a guard over**  - explanation
