===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If you were pure and upright;**  - "If your heart and actions were right;"
  * **act**  - "arise", "awaken"
  * **then he surely would act for you and he will make the dwelling of your righteousness prosper.**- AT: "restore your righteous dwelling"
  * **Even though your beginning was small, still your end will be much greater.** - This is a figure of speech where 2 different phrases describe the same idea (see: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
