===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He reveals deep things from darkness** - "He shows us deep places from darkness"
  * **brings out to light the shadows where dead people are** - "makes the realm of death visible"
  * **He makes nations strong** - "he makes people groups strong"
  * **he also destroys them** - "he also wipes them out"
  * **He enlarges nations** - "He causes the extent of the land of people groups to become bigger"
  * **he also leads them along as prisoners** - AT: "he also leads them along"
