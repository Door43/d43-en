===== Translation Notes: =====
Elihu continues speaking to Job and his friends.

    * **a spirit...the breath of the Almighty** - Both phrases mean the same thing. Elihu is emphasizing that a man's wisdom comes from God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
