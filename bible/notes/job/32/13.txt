===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Elihu continues speaking to Job's friends.

  * **We have found wisdom** - "We have discovered what is wise." This means that they believe that they have figured out what is wise.  
  * **to defeat Job** - This speaks of God responding to Job and correcting him as if he were defeating him in battle. AT: "to refute Job" or "to answer Job" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **with your words** - "by saying what you have said"
