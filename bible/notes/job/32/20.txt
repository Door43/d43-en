===== Translation Notes: =====
Elihu continues speaking using parallelisms for emphasis.

  * **I may be refreshed** - AT: "I may feel better" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **open my lips** - AT: "open my mouth" or "speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **neither will I give honorific titles to any man** - "neither will I praise any man or give him titles of honor"
  * **my Maker** - "God who made me"
