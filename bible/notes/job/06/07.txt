===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues to use parallelisms in these verses as he speaks about his sufferings and his desire for death. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **I refuse to touch them** - "Them" refers to bad tasting foods. 
  * **Oh, that I might have my request; oh, that God would grant me the thing I long for** - AT: "Oh that God would do what I have been asking him to do"
  * **to crush me once** - This means for God to cause him to die. AT: "would crush me and let me die"
  * **that he would let loose his hand and cut me off from this life** - The phrases "let loose his hand" and "cut me off" are idioms. AT: "that he would act quickly and cut short my life" or "that he would act quickly and end my life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
