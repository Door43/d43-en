===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Your sons and your daughters were eating and drinking wine in their oldest brother's house** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:job:01:13|01:13]].
  * **A great wind** - "a tornado" or "a desert sandstorm"
  * **the four corners of the house** - "the structural supports of the house"
  * **and it fell upon the young men** - AT: "The house fell on the men and women" or "The house fell on your sons and daughters"
  * **I only have escaped to tell you** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:job:01:13|01:15]].
