===== translationNotes: =====

  * **tore his robe, shaved his head** - These were ritual mourning actions, symbolizing deep grief. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **I was naked when I came out of my mother's womb, and I will be naked when I will return there** - "At my birth, I brought nothing into the world, and at my death I will return to the earth with nothing"
  * **In all this matter** - "Regarding all this that happened" 
  * **accuse God of wrongdoing** - "say that God had done wrong"
