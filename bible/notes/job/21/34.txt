===== Translation Notes: =====
This concludes Job's speech to his friends.
  * **How then do you comfort me with nonsense, since in your answers there is nothing but falsehood?** - Job uses a question to scold his friends. AT: "You cannot comfort me with nonsense. All of your answers are false." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
