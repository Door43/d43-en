===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is it at your orders...nest in high places?** - The implicit answer to this question is "no." AT: "It is not at your orders...nest in high places." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Is it at your orders** - AT: "Is it because you tell it to do so" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **mounts up** - AT: "flies up into the sky" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a stronghold** - The high cliffs are strongholds for eagles because the animals that would want to eat them cannot reach them.
