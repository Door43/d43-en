===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **scoff** - "scorn" This word means to disrespect and to hate expressed in rudeness or crudeness.
  * **my eye pours out tears** - Job describes how strongly he feels his sorrow. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **as a person pleads for his neighbor** - Job describes how he wants that one in heaven to plead for him. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **this man** - Job refers to himself.
