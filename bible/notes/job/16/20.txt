===== translationNotes: =====

  * **scoff** - "scorn" or "ridicule" 
  * **my eye pours out tears** - Job describes how strongly he feels his sorrow. Here he exaggerates how he often cries by saying that tears pour from his eyes. AT: "my eyes are full of tears while I cry out" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **for this man** - "for me." Here Job refers to himself in the third person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **as a man does with his neighbor!** - "as a man does for his neighbor." Job describes how he wants that one in heaven to plead for him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **I will go to a place** - Here Job is referring to himself dying. AT: "I will die and go to a place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
