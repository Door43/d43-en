===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **have kept their hearts from understanding** - "have kept them from understanding." The word "their" refers to Job's three friends, Eliphaz, Bildad, and Zophar.
  * **you will not exalt them over me** - "you will not honor them for correcting me" or "you will not honor them for saying what his right"
  * **He who** - "Anyone who"
  * **denounces his friends for a reward** - "falsely accuses his friends in order to get a profit" or "betrays his friends in order to get a portion of his goods" 
  * **the eyes of his children will fail** - Compare how you translated "the eyes of the wicked will fail" in [[en:bible:notes:job:11:20|11:20]].
