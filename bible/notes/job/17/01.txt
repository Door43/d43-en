===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]\\

Job continues to speak.
  * **My spirit is consumed** - AT: "I have lost all of my strength." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **my days are over** - AT: "I am just about to die." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my eye must always see** - AT: "I always have to see." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **provocation** - the offensive, unpleasant, and irritating remarks of other people
  * **Give now a pledge** - Job is speaking to Yahweh.


  * **give a pledge or be a guarantee** - This means secure my release and is a doublet. (See: Doublet)
  * **who else is there to free me** - This means there is no one else to free me. (See: Rhetorical question)
