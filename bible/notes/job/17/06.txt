===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But he has made me a byword of the people** - AT: "But people all over use my name as an insult." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **But he has made me** - The word "he" possibly refers to 1) people in general or 2) God.
  * **a byword** - a short saying or proverb that people use to ridicule others
  * **the people** - "the peoples" or "the tribes" or "the nations"
  * **they spit in my face** - "people spit in my face." This was a very sever insult.
  * **My eye is also dim because of sorrow** - AT: "My eyes have become weak and can barely see because I am so sad." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **will be stunned** - "will be shocked" or "will be distraught"
  * **by this** - "by what has happened to me"
  * **will stir himself up against the godless** - "will be distress because of the godless" or "will be very angry with the godless"



  * **thin as shadows** - This expression means "very thin" (See: Simile)
