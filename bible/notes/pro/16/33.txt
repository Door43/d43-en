===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The lots are cast into the lap** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "A person throws the lots into his lap" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the decision is from Yahweh** - Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh decides how the lots will land or 2) it is not the lots, but Yahweh who determines what will happen.
