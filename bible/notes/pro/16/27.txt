===== translationNotes: =====

  * **worthless** - wicked, deceptive
  * **mischief** - evil, trouble
  * **his speech is like a scorching fire** - "his words injure people like a fire does" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a gossip** - This refers to a person who gossips a lot.
