===== translationNotes: =====

  * **has an argument with** - Another possible meaning is "goes to court against."
  * **he rages and laughs** - the fool becomes very angry and tries to keep the wise person from speaking or the court judge from judging
  * **rages** - This means to be loud and excited and to move with powerful movements like a strong storm. This is a negative word.
  * **there will be no rest** - "they will not be able to settle the problem"
  * **seek the life of** - This is an idiom.  "want to kill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
