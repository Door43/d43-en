===== Translation Notes: =====

The personification of wisdom continues to speak, describing the consequences of refusing her offer of wisdom.

  * **calamity**  - "event that causes great harm and suffering"
  * **terror**  - "strong feeling of fear"
  * **when your fearful dread comes like a storm** - The way great fear will come upon the person is compared to the way that a powerful storm strikes. (See: Simile)
