===== Translation Notes: =====

The wise woman wisdom continues to speak, describing what happens to those who refuse her offer of wisdom.

  * **calamity** - An event that causes great harm and suffering.
  * **your fearful dread** - "terror"
  * **whirlwind** - a damaging wind that blows things in circles
  * **distress** - "pain"
  * **anguish** - "extreme suffering"
