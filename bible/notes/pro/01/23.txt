===== Translation Notes: =====

The wise woman wisdom continues to speak. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
 
  * **Pay attention** - "Listen carefully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will pour out my thoughts to you** - The thoughts of wisdom are like liquid being poured out. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I reached out with my hand** - AT: "I offered my help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
