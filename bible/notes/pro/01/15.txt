===== translationNotes: =====

  * **do not walk down that road with them** - The word "walk" is a metaphor for being like the sinners. AT: "do not go with the sinners or do what they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **do not let your foot touch where they walk** - "let your foot touch" is a metaphor for not doing what they do. AT: "do not do anything like what they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **their feet run to evil** - "Feet run to evil" is a metaphor for their eager interest in doing evil things. AT: "they are eager to do wicked things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
