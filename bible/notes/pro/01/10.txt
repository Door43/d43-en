===== translationNotes: =====

  * **try to entice you into their sin** - "try to persuade you to sin as they do"
  * **refuse to follow them** - "refuse them" or "do not listen to them"
  * **If they say** - Here the speaker gives an example of what sinners may try to entice someone to do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **lie in wait** - "hide and wait for the right time"
