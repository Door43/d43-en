====== Proverbs 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The first chapter of Proverbs begins with a introduction of sorts in verses 1-7. It mentions Solomon son of David. Verse 7 contains a foundational verse for the whole book. It defines wisdom. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Parallelism ==

Proverbs are often written without any surrounding context and in two lines of text. Each line will have a certain relationship to the other line. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

Many of the proverbs are stated as promises but are intended to be advice.
