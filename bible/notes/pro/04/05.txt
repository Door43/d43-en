===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The father continues to teach his children what his father taught him.

  * **Acquire** - "Work hard to gain for yourself" or "Get"
  * **do not forget** - "remember"
  * **do not reject** - "accept"
  * **the words of my mouth** - Here the word "mouth" represents the person who speaks. AT: "what I am saying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **do not abandon wisdom and she will watch over you; love her and she will keep you safe** - The writer speaks of wisdom as if it were a woman who protects the person who is faithful to her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **do not abandon wisdom** - "hold tightly to wisdom" or "work hard to become wise" 
  * **love her** - "love wisdom"
