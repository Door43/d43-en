===== Translation Notes: =====

The father continues teaching his children

    * **acquire** - gain for oneself through one's actions or efforts or "get"
    * **do not forget** - "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **do not reject** - "accept" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **do not abandon** - "hold tightly to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **she will watch over you** - "wisdom will protect you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
