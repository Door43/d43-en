====== Proverbs 04:26-27 ======


===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 26 Plan carefully where you will go and what you will do
\q2 and then stay on that road.
\q1 Then what you do will be right.
\q1
\v 27 Do not leave the straight road by turning to the left or to the right.
\q1 \add Do only what is right\add*
\q2 and keep yourself from doing what is evil.
</usfm>


===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 26 Take care about where you will go, and take care to be sure that the way is safe.
\q2 then you will know what to expect along the way.
\q1
\v 27 Do not leave the straight road by turning to the left or to the right.
\q1 and keep your foot from walking in the way that leads to evil. Do not touch it.
</usfm>

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **secure**  - free from danger or harm; safe
    * **turn your foot away from evil** - keep away from evil
    * **your foot** = yourself (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
