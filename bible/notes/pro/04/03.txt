===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When I was a son of my father** - The refers to the time when the writer was a child still living under his father's care. AT: "When I was still a boy learning from my father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the tender and only child** - Here the word "tender" refers to a young age at which the child is still weak. It forms a hendiadys with the word "only." AT: "the tender only child" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
  * **Let your heart hold fast to my words** - Here the word "heart" represents the person's mind. The writer speaks of remembering words as if the heart were holding on tightly to them. AT: "Always remember what I am teaching you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
