===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **pay attention**  - "listen carefully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **know what understanding is** - "understand what is wise"
    * **I give you good precepts** - "What I am teaching you is good"
    * **precepts**  - commandments or directions given as a rule of action or conduct
