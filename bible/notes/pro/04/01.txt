===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer speaks as a father teaching his children.

  * **pay attention** - "listen carefully"
  * **you will know what understanding is** - "you will know how to understand" or "you will gain understanding"
  * **I am giving you good instructions** - "What I am teaching you is good"
