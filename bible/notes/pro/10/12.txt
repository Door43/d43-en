===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **love** - This is kind of love that comes from God that is focused on the good of others, even when it doesn't benefit oneself. This kind of love cares for others, no matter what they do. 
  * **covers over** - "provides payment for" or "forgives"
  * **on the lips of a discerning person** - "in what a sensible person says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
