===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Like vinegar on the teeth and smoke in the eyes, so is the sluggard to those who send him** - "Vinegar" and "smoke" represent things that hurt a person's teeth and eyes. AT: "Sending a lazy person to accomplish a task is irritating and unpleasant." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **vinegar** - a sour liquid used to flavor or preserve foods
  * **the years of the wicked** - "Years" represent the time a person lives. AT: "the lifetime of the evil person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
