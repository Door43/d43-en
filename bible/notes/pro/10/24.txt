===== translationNotes: =====

  * **overtake** - overcome someone
  * **The wicked are like the storm** - Just as storm comes and sweeps everything away so wicked people will disappear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **is a foundation that lasts forever** - "Foundation" represents the base or a beginning of something that people build over. AT: "is a start for something that lasts forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
