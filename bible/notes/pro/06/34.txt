===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **furious**  - "very angry"
  * **he will show no mercy**  - The "he" is the neighbor whose wife has committed adultery with another man. AT: "he will not limit the pain he will cause you" or "he will hurt you as much as he can" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person|First, Second or Third Person]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes|Litotes]])
  * **when he takes his revenge**  - "in the moment of his revenge" AT: "when the moment arrives when he can take his revenge"
  * **takes his revenge**  - If a person takes revenge, it is to cause hurt to the person who hurt him first.
  * **compensation**  - "payment"
  * **bought off**  - This happens when someone is paid money so he will not carry out a certain action
  * **though**  - "eventhough" AT: "despite the fact that"
  * **offer**  - "give" AT: "promise"
