===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In that case **- This is the result of the if-then construction that came before.
  * **save yourself**  - AT: "protect yourself" or "help yourself out of these problems"
  * **you are at the mercy of your neighbor**  - AT: "your neighbor can bring harm to you if he wants to" or "you neighbor has power over you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **neighbor**  - "friend"
