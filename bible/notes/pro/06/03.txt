===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Then** - This is the "then" of an if-then construction that started in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:06:01|6:1]].
    * **save yourself** - AT: "protect yourself" or "help yourself out of these problems"
    * **you are at the mercy of your neighbor** - AT: "your neighbor can bring harm to you if he wants to" or "you neighbor has power over you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **neighbor** - "friend"
