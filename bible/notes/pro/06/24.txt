===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It keeps you from**  - "It saves you from" or "It protects you from" The word "it" here refers to the lessons taught by the father and mother in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:06:20|06:20]].
  * **the immoral woman â€¦ the adulteress**  - These two words basically mean the same thing. See how you translated the word "adulteress" in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:05:03|5:3]].
  * **immoral**  - "morally evil"
  * **her beauty**  - AT: "what is beautiful about her" or "her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **capture**  - AT: "gain control over"
  * **her eyelashes**  - The eyelashes stand for the beautiful things about her body that she uses to catch a man's attention. AT: "her beautiful eyes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
