===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It keeps you from** - Here the word "it" refers to the lessons taught by the father and mother in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:06:20|6:20]]. AT: "It saves you from" or "It protects you from" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **the immoral woman ... an immoral woman** - These two words mean basically the same thing. See how you translated the word "adulteress" in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:05:03|5:3]].
  * **immoral** - "morally evil"
  * **in your heart** - Here "heart" represents the mind. AT: "in your thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **her beauty** - "what is beautiful about her." This can also be a metonym for the woman. AT: "her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **capture** - "gain control over"
  * **her eyelashes** - The "eyelashes" stand for the beautiful things about her body that she uses to catch a man's attention. AT: "her beautiful eyes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
