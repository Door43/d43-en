===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Can a man walk on hot coals and his feet not be scorched?** - Walking on hot coals will scorch a person's feet, so the implied answer is "no." AT: "Every man who walks on hot coals will have scorched feet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **walk** - That is to slowly walk a long distance, without using tricks or magic.
    * **and his feet not be scorched?** - AT: "without scorching his feet?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **be scorched** - "be burned" AT: "be hurt"
    * **has relations with her** - "has sex with her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
    * **will not go unpunished** - "will be punished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
