===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Can a man walk on hot coals without scorching his feet?**  - Walking on hot coals will scorch a person's feet, so the implied answer is "no." AT: "Every man who walks on hot coals will have scorched feet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **walk on hot coals**  - This stands for committing adultery. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **walk**  - That is to slowly walk a long distance, without using tricks or magic.
  * **scorching**  - AT: "burning"
  * **will not go unpunished**  - "will be punished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes|Litotes]])
