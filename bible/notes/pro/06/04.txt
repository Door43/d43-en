===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Give your eyes no sleep**  - "Do not allow yourself to sleep" or "Stay awake"
    * **sleep**  - The word "sleep" is used in the sense of natural sleep, not as a euphemism for death.
    * **Save yourself like a gazelle from the hand of the hunter**  - Escape from your neighbor like a gazelle flees from a hunter. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **gazelle** - a kind of antelope
    * **from the hand of** - "from the control of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **fowler**  - a hunter of birds
