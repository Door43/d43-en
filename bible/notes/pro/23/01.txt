===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the "thirty sayings" ([[:en:bible:notes:pro:22:20|22:20]]).

  * **put a knife to your throat** - Possible meanings of this exaggeration are 1) "be very careful not to eat too much" or 2) "do not eat anything at all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Do not crave** - "Do not strongly desire." See how you translated "craves" in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:21:09|21:9]].
  * **delicacies** - "special and expensive food" (UDB)
  * **it is the food of lies** - This is an idiom.  "he is giving it to you so he can deceive you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
