===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **drunkard** - a person who gets drunk often
    * **glutton** - a person who eats more than needed
    * **slumber will clothe them with rags** - This phrase describes how sleeping too much will cause the drunkard and the glutton to become poor and end up wearing rags. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
