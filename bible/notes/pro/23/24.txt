    * **The father of the one who does right will greatly rejoice ... he that begets a wise child will be glad in him **  - These two phrases are saying the same thing and are used together for emphasis.  AT: "The father of a righteous and wise son will be glad." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
