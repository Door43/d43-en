===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **ancient boundary stone** - very old and large stones to show the line that marks one area from another - See how you translated this in [[en/bible/notes/pro/22/28]]
    * **encroach** - slowly take or begin to use the land (or something) that belongs to someone else
    * **orphans** - children whose parents are dead
