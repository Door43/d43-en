===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **For a prostitute is a deep pit** - AT: "having sex with a prostitute is like falling into a dangerous" trap (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **another man's wife is a narrow pit** - AT: "having sex with another man's wife is like falling into another kind of dangerous trap" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **She lies in wait** - AT: "she stays hidden while ready to attack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **traitor** - a person who deceives or harms someone who was trusting him
