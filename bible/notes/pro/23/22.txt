===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **do not despise**  - "appreciate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **Buy the truth, but do not sell it; buy wisdom, discipline, and understanding**  - This phrase emphasizes the importance of having truth, wisdom, self-discipline and knowledge.  These virtues are to be treasured and not sold.
