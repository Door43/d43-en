===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Iron sharpens iron; in the same way, a man sharpens his friend.** - These two phrases are comparing how iron and a man can be improved. AT: "As iron can sharpen another piece of iron, so a man's character is improved by contact with his friend" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **tends** - "takes care of"
  * **the one who protects his master will be honored** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "a master will honor the one who protects him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
