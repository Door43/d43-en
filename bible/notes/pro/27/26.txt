===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The lambs will provide your clothing** - AT: "The lambs skin and wool will provide you with clothing"
  * **the goats will provide the price of the field** - AT: "selling your goats will provide the price of the field"
  * **and nourishment for your servant girls** - AT: "there will be goat's milk to nourish your servant girls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
