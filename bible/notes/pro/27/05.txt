===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Faithful are the wounds caused by a friend** - AT: "The wounds from a friend are trustworthy"
  * **the wounds caused by a friend** - The word "wounds" refers to the pain caused by rebuke or correction. AT: "the wounds caused by a friend's rebuke" or "the wounds of a friend's correction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **profusely** - "abundantly" or "too much"
