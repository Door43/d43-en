===== Translation Notes: =====

The father continues to teach his child how wisdom will protect him.

  * **immoral** - violating God's standards of conduct
  * **entice** - lead on by exciting hope or desire
  * **and her flattering words** - "when she lies to you by telling you how wonderful you are"
