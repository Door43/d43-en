===== translationNotes: =====

  * **her house leads down to death** - Possible meanings are 1) "going to her house leads to death" or 2) "the road to her house is the road to death."
  * **her tracks will lead you** - Possible meanings are 1) "the paths to her house will lead you." This refers to the tracks or path that lead to her house, or 2) this is a metaphor that speaks of her way of life as if it were a path on which she walks. AT: "her way of life will lead you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **to those in the grave** - This refers to the spirits of dead people and is a metonym for the place of the dead. AT: "to the grave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **go in to her** - This means to go into her house to sleep with her, as one would with a prostitute. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **they will not find the paths of life** - Possible meanings are 1) "they will not return to the land of the living" or 2) "they will never live a happy life again."
