===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **equity** - "fairness"
  * **every good path** - "ways to live that are pleasing to God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **wisdom will come into your heart** - "you will gain much wisdom" or "you will learn how to be truly wise"
  * **pleasant** - "pleasing" or "enjoyable"
