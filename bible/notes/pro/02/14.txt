===== translationNotes: =====


  * **They rejoice** - "They" refers to the same people as in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:02:11|2:12]].
  * **delight in the perversities of evil** - This means basically the same thing as the first part of the sentence. AT: "delight in doing what they know is evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **They follow crooked paths** - People who lie to others are spoken of as if they walk on crooked or twisted paths. AT: "They deceive other people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **using deception they hide their tracks** - People lying so that others will not find out what they did is spoken of as if they covered the tracks on a path so that no one could follow them. AT: "they lie so that no one will know what they have done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
