===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Whoever digs a pit will fall into it **- equivalent meaning can be found at Psalm 7:15
  * **A lying tongue hates the people he crushes **- A liar hates those that are hurt by his lies. (See: Metonymy)
  * **A flattering mouth brings about ruin**  - "Flattering" means to praise someone in a manner that is not sincere.
