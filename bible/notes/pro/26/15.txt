===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The lazy one puts his hand into the dish and yet he has no strength to lift it up to his mouth **- The extreme laziness of the person is shown by his putting his hand into the dish for food and then just leaving it there.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]]) The first part of the sentence is equivalent to Proverbs [[en/bible/notes/pro/19/23|19:24]]
  * **The lazy person is wiser in his own eyes than seven men with discernment **- AT: "The lazy person thinks he is wiser than 7 men who can give a reason"
