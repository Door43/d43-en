===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A whip is for the horse, a bridle is for the donkey, and a rod is for the back of fools** - Fools must be disciplined by force like a horse or a donkey. Please refer to [[en/bible/notes/pro/10/12|10:13]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Do not answer a fool and join in on his folly** - AT: "Answer not a fool according to his folly"
