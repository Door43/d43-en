===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A whip is for the horse, a bridle is for the donkey and a rod is for the back of fools** - The whip, bridle, and rod are things that people use to make the horse, donkey, and fool do what they want. 
  * **a bridle is for the donkey** - A bridle is made of straps. People put it on a donkey's head and hold one of the straps to make the donkey go the way they want it to go.
  * **rod is for the back of fools** - In the Bible, people would hit their children or their slaves with a wooden rod in order to discipline them.
