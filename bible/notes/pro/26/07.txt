===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **paralytic **- a person who is unable to move or feel all or part of his body
  * **Tying a stone in a sling is the same as giving honor to a fool**  - Tying a stone in a sling so that it cannot be thrown at a target is as foolish as honoring a foolish person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
