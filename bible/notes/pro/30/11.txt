===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A generation that curses their father and does not bless their mother** - "A generation of people who curse their fathers and do not bless their mothers"
    *  **are pure in their own eyes** - AT: "believes they are pure" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **they are not washed of their filth**  - AT: "they are not cleansed from their sins" or "they are still dirty with their sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
