===== translationNotes: =====

  * **is tested** - Words are spoken of as if they were metals that need someone to clean the bad parts out of them. AT: "is like a precious metal from which someone has removed all the useless material" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he is a shield to those who take refuge in him** - The word "shield" is a metaphor for something that protects a person. AT: "he protects those who come and ask him to protect them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **add to his words** - say more than he has said
  * **you will be proved to be** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "he will prove that you are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
