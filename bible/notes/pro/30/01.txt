    * **Agur ... Jakeh ...Ithiel ... Ucal**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

    * **to Ithiel, to Ithiel and Ucal**  - AT: "to Ithiel and Ucal" 
    * **Surely**  - "Certainly" or "There is no doubt that"
