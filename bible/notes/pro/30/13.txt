===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **how haughty are their eyes
and their eyelids are lifted up!** - These two phrases have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "their eyes show that they are haughty" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **jawbones** - the bones of the face where teeth grow
