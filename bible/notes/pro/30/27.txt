===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **locusts**  - a type of grasshopper
    * **march in rank**  - "walk with regular steps as a group" or "to walk in the regular and organized way of soldiers"
    * **lizard**  - a type of reptile that has four legs and a long body and tail
    * **kings' palaces**  - "the official homes of kings"
