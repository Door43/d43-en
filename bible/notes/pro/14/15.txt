===== translationNotes: =====

  * **naive** - "inexperienced, immature"
  * **prudent** - See how you translated this word in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:12:23|12:23]].
  * **his steps** - Here the idea of footsteps represents a person's behavior. AT: "his actions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **turns away from evil** - Here evil is spoken of as if it were a place. AT: "avoids doing evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **confidently dismisses** - "boldly ignores"
