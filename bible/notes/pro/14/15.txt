===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **believes** - This means to accept or trust that something is true.
  * **The one who has not been taught** - "The simple" or "The naive" AT: "The person who has not learned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **prudent** - See how you translated this word in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:12:23|12:23]].
  * **confidently dismisses** - "boldly ignores"
