===== translationNotes: =====

  * **bow down** - This means to bend over to humbly express respect and honor toward someone. Here this one action represents all yielding to the more influential person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **at the gates of the righteous** - "Gates" represents an entrance to meet with another. This means the wicked will have to wait for the righteous person and beg to enter his presence. AT: "to meet with the righteous person." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The poor person is hated even by his own companions** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Everyone hates the poor person even his own neighbors" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
