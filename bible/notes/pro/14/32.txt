===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **brought down**  - destroyed
    * **Wisdom rests in the heart**  - "Wisdom is found in the heart"
    * **the discerning** - a person who has understanding or good judgment
    * **she lets herself be known** - The word "she" refers to wisdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
