===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **From the mouth of a fool comes an offshoot of his pride** - The foolish man's words come out of his pride (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **offshoot**  - something that grows out of something else In this verse, the foolish 
  * **the lips of the wise**  - "the words of the wise"
  * **will preserve them**  - "will keep the wise from harm" or "will keep the wise from pride"
  * **abundant crop**  - a good harvest
