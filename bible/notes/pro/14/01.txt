===== Translation Notes: =====

Many verses in Chapter 14 are parallelisms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **tears it down with her own hands**  - This means that the foolish woman destroys her own family by the way she behaves.
  * **despises**  - hates or strongly dislikes.
  * **in his ways despises him**  - The word "his" refers to the dishonest man and "him" refers to Yahweh.
