===== translationNotes: =====

  * **but when there is only talk** - "but if you only talk" or "but when all a person does is talking"
  * **The crown of wise people** - The word "crown" represents the highest achievement possible and visible to all. AT: "The reward of wise people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the folly of fools** - Translate this phrase as in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:14:07|14:8]].
