===== translationNotes: =====


  * **on his lips** - AT: "on him" or "with him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the prudent** - a person who has good judgment or sense
  * **his own way** - AT: "himself" or "how he lives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the folly of fools is deception** - The foolishness of fools is that they think they are wise, when they are not.
