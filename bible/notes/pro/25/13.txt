===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Like the cold of snow at harvest time ... is a faithful messenger ...**  - Here a faithful messenger is being compared to the cold of snow, because both are pleasant. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **snow** - white flakes of ice that fall from the sky like rain
    * **the soul of his masters** - "his master's soul"
    * **Like clouds and wind ... is the one who boasts about a gift ...**  - Here the clouds and wind are being compared to the person who boasts, because both boast of something and give nothing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
