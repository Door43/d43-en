===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A man who bears false witness against his neighbor
is like a club used in war, or a sword, or a sharp arrow.**  - A false witness is compared to three weapons that can kill people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **Trusting in an unfaithful man in time of trouble
is like a bad tooth or a foot that slips.**  - Trusting in an unfaithful person is compared to a broken tooth or a foot out of joint, neither of which can work properly or be relied upon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
