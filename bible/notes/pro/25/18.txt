===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A man who bears false witness against his neighbor is like a club used in war, or a sword, or a sharp arrow.** - A false witness is compared to three weapons that can kill people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **An unfaithful man in whom you trust in a time of trouble is like a bad tooth or a foot that slips.** - A foolish man is compares to a part of the body that caused trouble for a person. AT: "Trusting in an unfaithful man in time of trouble will bring you pain like a bad tooth or a foot that slips." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
