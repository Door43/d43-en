===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your case** - "your disagreement"
  * **do not disclose another's secret** - "do not share your neighbor's secret with other people"
  * **an evil report about you that cannot be silenced** - Here "evil report" refers to harmful things that the person will tell others. The phrase "cannot be silenced" can be stated in active form. AT: "you will not be able to stop him from telling other people harmful things about you" or "he will tell people evil things about you and you will never have a good reputation again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
