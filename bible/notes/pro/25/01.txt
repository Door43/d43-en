    * **to conceal a matter** - "to keep something secret"

so the heart of kings is unsearchable.** - AT: "Just as no one can measure the height of the heavens or the depth of the earth, even so no one can understand the heart of kings." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
