===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Yahweh is far away from** - AT: "Yahweh does not listen to" or "Yahweh does not answer"
    * **hears** - AT: "responds to"
    * **light of the eyes** - AT: "look of happiness in the eyes"
    * **is health to the body** - AT: "brings health to the body" or "heals the body"
