===== translationNotes: =====


  * **The lips of wise people scatter knowledge about** - AT: "Wise people spread knowledge everywhere by what they say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **not so the hearts of fools** - AT: "but the hearts of fools do not scatter knowledge about" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the hearts of fools** - AT: "fools" or "foolish people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh hates the sacrifices of wicked people** - AT: "Yahweh hates it when wicked people sacrifice to him" or "Yahweh hates it when wicked people worship him"
  * **upright people** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:03:31|3:32]].
  * **is his delight** - AT: "pleases him" or "is Yahweh's delight"
