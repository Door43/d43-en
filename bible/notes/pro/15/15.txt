===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **All the days**  - This refers to what happens in those days. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **miserable**  - AT: "evil"
    * **a cheerful heart has an unending feast**  - The one with a cheerful heart enjoys life as if life were an unending feast (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **a cheerful heart**  - the one with a cheerful heart (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **treasure**  - wealth, money, riches, valuable possessions
    * **confusion**  - AT: "trouble"
