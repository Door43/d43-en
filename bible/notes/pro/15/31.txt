===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pay attention**  - AT: “listen carefully”
  * **you will remain among the wise**  - Possible meanings are 1) wise people will want you to stay with them or 2) people will continue to see you as a wise person.
  * **the one who**  - "the person who"
  * **despises**  - See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:01:07|1:7]].
  * **listens to correction**  - AT: "receives correction" (UDB) or "follows correcting instructions"
