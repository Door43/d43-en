===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **a pertinent reply** - "a good answer" (UDB) or "an answer"
    * **how good is a timely word** - This is a question and the implicit answer is "very good" AT: "a word at the right time is very good" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **leads upward** - This is a positive thing. AT: "makes life go better"
    * **prudent** - "wise"
