===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a pertinent reply** - "a good answer" (UDB) or "an answer"
  * **how good is a timely word** - This is an exclamation that functions to emphasize that a timely word is very good. AT: "a word at the right time is very good"
  * **leads upward** - This is a positive thing. AT: "makes life go better"
  * **prudent** - "wise"
