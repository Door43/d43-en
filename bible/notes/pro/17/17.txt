===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a brother is born for times of trouble** - One of the purposes of a brother is to be there to help his brother or sister in times of trouble. AT: "a brother is there for times of trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **no sense** - "no good judgement" 
  * **binding promises** - This refers to promises that must be kept and are often a burden on the person who made them.
