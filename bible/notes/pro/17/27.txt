===== translationNotes: =====

  * **uses few words** - This refers to the way he speaks. AT: "speaks with few words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **keeps his mouth shut** - This means that he does not speak. AT: "does not speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Even a fool is thought to be wise** - This can be written in active form. AT: "People even think a fool is wise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he is considered to be intelligent** - This can be written in active form. AT: "people consider him to be intelligent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
