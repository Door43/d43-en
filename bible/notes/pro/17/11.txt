===== translationNotes: =====

  * **seeks rebellion** - The word "rebellion" can be expressed as a verb. AT: "seeks to rebel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **a cruel messenger will be sent against him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "a cruel messenger will come against him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will be sent against him** - To be "sent against" someone means to be sent to harm them. AT: "will be sent to harm him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a bear robbed of her cubs** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "a bear who has just lost her cubs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in his foolishness** - The word "foolishness" can be expressed as an adjective. AT: "who is acting foolish" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
