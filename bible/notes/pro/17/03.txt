===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **crucible**  - a pot in which metals are melted at a very high temperature
  * **Yahweh refines hearts**  - AT: "Yahweh tests hearts" Just as heating gold and silver removes whatever is impure, so testing by Yahweh removes evil and foolishness from hearts.
