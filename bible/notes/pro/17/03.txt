===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The crucible is for silver and the furnace is for gold** - This refers to how gold and silver are refined. A metal is refined by heating it to a high temperature so that it melts and the impurities may be removed. AT: "The crucible is used to refine silver and the furnace is used to refine gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **crucible** - a pot in which metals are melted at a very high temperature
  * **Yahweh refines hearts** - This speaks of Yahweh testing people to help them stop being evil and foolish as if their hearts were a metal that Yahweh was refining to remove everything that is impure. AT: "Yahweh tests peoples' hearts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **hearts** - Here people are referred to by their hearts to emphasize their thoughts and desires. AT: "people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **wicked lips** - The word "lips" can be translated as either 1) a synecdoche for the person or 2) a metonym for the words that come out from those lips. AT:  "a wicked person" or "wicked talk." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **gives ear** - "listens" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **destructive tongue** - The word "tongue" can be translated as either 1) a synecdoche for the person or 2) a metonym for the words that the tongue produces. AT: "a destructive person" or "destructive talk." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
