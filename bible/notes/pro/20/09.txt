===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Who can say, "I have kept my heart clean; I am free from my sin"?** - The implicit answer to this question is, "No one can say that." This rhetorical question can be written as a statement. AT: "No one can say that his heart is clean and that he is free from sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **my heart** - Here a person is referred to by his "heart" to emphasize his thoughts and desires. AT: "myself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **clean** - A person who God considers spiritually acceptable is spoken of as if the person where physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I am free from my sin** - "I am without sin" or "I have not sinned"
