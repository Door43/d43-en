===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lips of knowledge are a precious jewel** - Lips of knowledge are compared to a valuable jewel. AT: "lips of knowledge are as valuable as an expensive jewel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **lips of knowledge** - AT: "wise words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * ** puts up money as security for a stranger's debt** - AT: "agrees to pay off the loan of a stranger if he does not pay"
  * **adulteresses** - See how you translated this word in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:05:03|5:3]].
