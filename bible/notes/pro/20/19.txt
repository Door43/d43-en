===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A gossip** - This means someone who gossips a lot.
  * **you should not associate with** - "you should not be friends with"
  * **curses** - This means to express a desire that bad things will happen to someone.
  * **his lamp will be snuffed out in the middle of darkness** - This speaks of t person dying suddenly and unexpectedly as if his life were a lamp snuffed out in the dark. AT: "his life will end as suddenly as the light of a lamp that is snuffed out in the dark" or "he will die as suddenly as when the light of a lamp disappears when someone snuffs it out in the dark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **his lamp will be snuffed out** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "his lamp will go out" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **his lamp** - This refers to a lamp with a burning flame. Here the lamp's flame is referred to as the lamp itself. AT: "the flame of his lamp" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
