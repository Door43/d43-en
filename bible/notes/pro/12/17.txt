===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The words of one who speaks rashly are like the thrusts of a sword** - The phrase, "thrusts of a sword" represents cruel words that hurt another. AT: "What a person says without thinking can hurt as much as if he stabbed with a sword." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **the tongue of the wise** - "Tongue" represents what a person says. AT: "what wise people say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **brings healing** - "comforts and heals"
