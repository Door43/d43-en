===== translationNotes: =====


  * **roast their own game** - "Game" means animals caught and killed while hunting and to roasting is to prepare it for eating. This phrase represents the work a person does who makes good use what he has gained. AT: "make what they gain useful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **precious wealth** - "valuable treasure"
