===== translationNotes: =====

  * **conceals his knowledge** - "does not tell everything he knows" 
  * **The hand of the diligent** - "Hand" represents what a person does -- his works. AT: "diligent people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will be put to forced labor** - "Forced labor" describes what a person must do who is not free to do what he wants. AT: "will become a slave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
