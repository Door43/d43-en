===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **prudent** - "wise" 
  * **conceals his knowledge** - "does not tell everything he knows" 
  * **the heart of fools** - "foolish people" (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **The hand of the diligent**  - This could be translated "A person who works hard." The hand represents the whole person. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the lazy**  - "the lazy person" or "the deceitful person"
  * **will be subject to forced labor**  - "will become a slave"
