===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The words of wicked people are an ambush waiting for a chance to kill** - "Ambush" represents a trap that kills by surprise." AT: "What a wicked person says can kill people by surprise." (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **words** - This represents what people say. AT: "What a person says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the upright** - "the righteous person" or "the honest person" or "the just person."
