===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Truthful lips last forever**  - AT: "A truthful person endures forever" A truthful person is consistent, and what he says will continue to be true. Truthful lips represent the truthful person because truth is expressed through words formed by the mouth, and lips are part of the mouth. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **a lying tongue is only for an moment**  - AT: "the one who lies lasts only for a moment" When a person lies, others will quickly realize that what he said is not true. The lying tongue represents the lying person because lies are expressed through words formed by the mouth, and the tongue is part of the mouth. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **advisors** - those who give recommendations as a guide to action
