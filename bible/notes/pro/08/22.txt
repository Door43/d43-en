===== Translation Notes: =====

   * **... works of old. From ages long ago...** - These phrases refer to the first acts of God in creating the earth and life and the universe during the beginning stages of creation.
   * **I was set up** - Wisdom was "set up" by being created by God, or God's wisdom was revealed to His new creation.
