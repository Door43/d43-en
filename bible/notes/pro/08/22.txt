===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the first of his deeds then** - "I was the first of the things he created then"
  * **In ages long ago** - "Very long ago"
  * **ages** - The word "age" refers to a general, extended period of time.
  * **I was made** - This can be put into active form. AT: "God made me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from the beginnings of the earth** - The idea of beginnings can be translated in a less abstract way. AT: "from when God created the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
