===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Acquire my instruction rather than silver** - "You should try much harder to understand my instructions than to get silver"
  * **For Wisdom is better than jewels; no treasure is equal to her** - Here Wisdom, personified as a woman, is not speaking. However, it is possible to maker Wisdom the speaker here as well. AT: "For I, Wisdom, am better than jewels; no treasure is equal to me"
