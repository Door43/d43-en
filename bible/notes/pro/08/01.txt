===== Translation Notes: =====

In chapter 8 wisdom is spoken of as a woman who teaches people how to be wise. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification|Personification]])\\
Many verses in chapter 8 have parallelisms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])

  * **Does not Wisdom call out?**  - This question is used to remind the readers of something they should already know. AT: "Wisdom calls out" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Does not Understanding raise her voice?**  - AT: "Understanding shouts loudly"
  * **the city gate**  - This is where people go in and out of a city that has a wall around it.
  * **she calls out **- "Wisdom calls out for the people to listen to her"
