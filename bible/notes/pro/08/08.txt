===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the words of my mouth** - Here "mouth" stands for the person who is speaking. AT: "The words that I say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **twisted** - "false"
  * **straight** - "honest and clear"
  * **upright** - "honest"
  * **my words are upright for those who find knowledge** - This probably means that those who find knowledge will easily understand that the speaker's words are upright. AT: "my words will be considered upright by those who find knowledge"
