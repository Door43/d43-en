===== translationNotes: =====

  * **love** - This refers to brotherly love or love for a friend or family member. This is natural human love between friends or relatives.
  * **diligently** - with careful and continued effort
  * **With me are riches and honor** - "I have riches and honor"
  * **lasting wealth and righteousness** - This explains what is meant by "riches and honor." AT: "I give lasting wealth and righteousness" or "People who find me find lasting wealth and righteousness"
  * **righteousness** - "the ability to live in a right way"
