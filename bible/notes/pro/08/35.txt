===== translationNotes: =====

  * **finds me ... hate me** - This is still wisdom talking about herself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **he who fails** - The complete thought is, "he who fails to find me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **his own life** - Here "life" represents the person's self. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
