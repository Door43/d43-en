===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **And now** - This is to focus the attention of the students to the conclusion of this section.
    * **my sons** - AT: "my children" or "my students"
    * **pay attention** - Translate as you did in [[en/bible/notes/pro/01/23|1:23]].
    * **Do not let your heart ... into her paths** - These two lines basically mean the same thing and are repeated to make the warning even stronger. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **Do not let your heart turn aside to** - AT: "Let your heart stay far away from" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **your heart** - AT: "yourself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **to her ways** - To the ways of the adulterous woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
    * **her ways ... her paths** - Possible meanings are 1) stay far away from her 2) do not live in a similar manner.
