===== translationNotes: =====

  * **she** - "She" refers to the woman that was introduced in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:07:10|7:10]].
  * **grabbed him** - "took hold of him firmly"
  * **I paid my vows** - Here "vows" represents what the person promised to sacrifice to God. AT: "I made the sacrifices I promised to God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **with a strong face** - Here "strong" represents "stubborn." A "strong face" means a stubborn expression on a person's face. This implies that the woman is acting in a stubborn way, that she is deliberately doing what she knows is wrong. AT: "with a shameless expression on her face" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **seek your face** - Here "face" represents the person and especially the person's presence. AT: "look for you" or "find out where you are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
