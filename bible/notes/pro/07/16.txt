===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **coverings on my bed**  - "blankets on my bed"
  * **perfumed my bed**  - "put sweet-smelling scents on my bed"

  * **cinnamon**  - This is a spice made from the bark of a tree that smells and tastes good.
