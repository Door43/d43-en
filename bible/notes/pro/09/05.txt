===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the message of Wisdom.

  * **Come ... eat ... drink ... Leave ... live ... walk** - All of these commands are plural; Wisdom is addressing many people at the same time. 
  * **the wine I have mixed** - In ancient Israel, people often mixed wine with water. AT: "prepared her wine by mixing water with it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Leave your naive actions** - Here naive actions are spoken of as if they were a place that a person could leave. AT: "Stop your naive behavior" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **naive** - "inexperienced, immature"
  * **the path of understanding** - Here the process of understanding wisdom is spoken of as if it were a path that a person could follow. AT: "the manner of living that a wise person has" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
