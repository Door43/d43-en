===== Translation Notes: =====
"Wisdom" is continuing her speech.


    * **fear of Yahweh**  - The word "fear" is used in the sense of deep awe and reverence.
    * **through me**  - The word "me" refers to the personification of wisdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
