===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses begin a parable in which wisdom is imagined to be a woman who is giving good advice to people.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  
  * **Wisdom** - The writer speaks about "wisdom" as if it were a woman who built her house. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **pillars** - This refers to large vertical structures that were used to hold up a roof or other part of a building.
  * **she has slaughtered her animals** - This refers to animals whose meat will be eaten in the dinner that Wisdom will give. AT: "she has killed the animals for meat at dinner" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **mixed her wine** - In ancient Israel, people often mixed wine with water. AT: "prepared her wine by mixing water with it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **she has set her table** - "she has prepared her table"
