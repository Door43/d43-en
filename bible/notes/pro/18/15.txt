===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The heart of the intelligent ... the hearing of the wise ...** - These two phrases are repeated for emphasis, because it is an important concept. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **The heart of the intelligent** - AT: "The intelligent person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **acquires** - "gains" or "obtains"
    * **the hearing of the wise** - "the ear of the wise" AT: "the wise person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **may open the way** - AT: "may create opportunities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **bring him before** - AT: "let him meet" or "let him be introduced to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
