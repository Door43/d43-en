===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The heart of the intelligent acquires** - Here the intelligent person is referred to by his heart to emphasize his desires. AT: "The intelligent desire to acquire" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the intelligent** - This refers to people who are intelligent. AT: "those who are intelligent" or "intelligent people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **acquires** - "gains" or "obtains"
  * **the wise** - This refers to people who are wise. AT: "those who are wise" or "wise people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **the hearing of the wise seeks it out** - Here the wise person is referred to by his hearing to emphasize what he desires to listen to. AT: "the wise seeks to learn about it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seeks it out** - Here the word "it" refers to "knowledge." 
  * **may open the way** - Here to "open the way" means to create an opportunity. AT: "may create an opportunity for him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **bring him before** - This means to be allowed to see someone. AT: "let him meet" or "let him be introduced to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
