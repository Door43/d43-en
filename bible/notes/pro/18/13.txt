===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A person's spirit will survive sickness**  - A person's desire to live will support him and help him endure when he is sick.
    * **but a broken spirit who can bear it?**  - This is a rhetorical question, expecting the answer that few can bear it. (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) AT:"but a broken spirit is hard to bear."
    * **a broken spirit**  - This suggests emotional pain and despair.
