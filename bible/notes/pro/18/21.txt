===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Death and life are in the power of the tongue**  - "What people say can lead to life or death" 
    * **those who love it will eat its fruit**  - Those who love to talk a lot must accept the consequences of their speech, whether good or bad. 
    * **love it**  - love the tongue, "love to talk"
