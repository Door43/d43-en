===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Death and life are controlled by the tongue** - This can be written in active form. AT: "The tongue can lead to life of death" or "What people say can lead to life or death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **by the tongue ... love the tongue** - Here the "tongue" refers to speech. AT: "by what people say ... love speaking" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will eat its fruit** - This speaks of a person receiving the consequence for what he says as if the consequences were fruit that he receives. AT: "will receive its consequences" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
