===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Death and life are in the power of the tongue** - AT: "What people say can lead to life or death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **those who love it will eat its fruit** - Eating the fruit of the power of the tongue means to experience the consequences, good or bad. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **love it** - "love the power of the tongue" or "love to talk"
