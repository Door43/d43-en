===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When a wicked person ... shame and reproach** - AT: "When an evil person comes, he has contempt for other people; they will feel ashamed and start reproaching each other" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **The words of ... a flowing stream** - What a person says is compared to water in three different ways over two parallel lines, this is to emphasize how good it is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
