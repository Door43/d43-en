===== translationNotes: =====


  * **It is not good to ... to those who do right** - "It is good to treat the wicked as he deserves, and to be just to those who do right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **A fool's lips bring ... invites a beating** - These two lines express the same idea to emphasize the danger of speaking foolishly. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **A fool's lips bring** - "what a fool says brings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **his mouth invites a beating** - "what he says calls for someone to give him a beating" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
