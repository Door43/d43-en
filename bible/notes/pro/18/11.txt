===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The wealth of the rich is his fortified city**  - A rich person finds protection in his wealth. This contrasts with the righteous person who finds his protection in Yahweh (verse 10).
    * **in his imagination it is like a high wall**  - The protection provided by wealth is not real but only imagined.
    * **Before his downfall a person's heart is proud,
but humility comes before honor.**  - Pride leads to ruin, but a humble person will be honored.
    * **humility comes before honor.**  - "humility leads to honor"
