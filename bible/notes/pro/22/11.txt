===== translationNotes: =====


  * **loves a pure heart** - It is his own heart that the person wants to be pure. The heart is a synecdoche for the person. AT: "loves having a pure heart" or "wants to be pure" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **gracious** - "kind"
  * **The eyes of Yahweh keep watch over** - The eyes are a synecdoche for the person. The writer speaks as if Yahweh had physical eyes like a person. AT: "Yahweh keeps watch over" or "Yahweh guards knowledge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **keep watch over knowledge** - Keeping watch is s metonym for protecting. AT: "protect knowledge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **overthrows** - "destroys"
  * **the treacherous** - The nominal adjective treacherous can be translated as a noun phrase. AT: "a treacherous person." Translate "treacherous" as in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:11:03|11:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
