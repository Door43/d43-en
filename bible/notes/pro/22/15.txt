===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Foolishness is bound up in the heart of a child** - "The heart of a child is full of foolish things"
  * **the rod of discipline** - The writer speaks of a parent using any form of discipline as if that parent were hitting the child with a wooden rod. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **drives it far away** - The writer speaks as if foolishness were a person that another person could use a physical rod to drive away. AT: "will make a child wise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **to increase his wealth** - "to become richer" or "to gain more money"
  * **gives to rich people** - "gives money to rich people"
  * **will come to poverty** - This is an idiom. AT: "will become poor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
