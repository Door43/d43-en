===== translationNotes: =====


  * **The one who has a generous eye will be blessed** - These words can be translated in active form. AT: "God will bless the one who has a generous eye" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **one who has a generous eye** - The eye is a metonym for seeing what other people need, and the "generous eye" not only sees but gives what the other people need. The eye is also a synecdoche for the whole person. AT: "generous person" or "person who is willing to give things to other people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **bread** - Since bread was the main food for many people in biblical times, it is often used to refer to food in general. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **disputes and insults will cease** - The abstract nouns "disputes" and "insults" can be translated as verbs. AT: "people will no longer argue with each other or say things to hurt each other" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
