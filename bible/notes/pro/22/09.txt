===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **The one who has a generous eye will be blessed** - AT: "God will bless the one who has a generous eye" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **bread** - Since bread was the main food for many people in biblical times, it is often used to refer to food in general.
  * **mocker** - This mean someone who mocks people a lot.
  * **has a generous eye** - AT: "has a generous attitude" or "is generous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **generous** - giving, unselfish
