===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Pay attention and hear** - These two verbs means the same thing and emphasize the importance of listening. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **the words of the wise** - AT: "what wise people say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **apply your heart** - AT: "do your best to remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **my knowledge** - The person speaking is probably the same as the father from the first chapter.
    * **ready on your lips** - AT: "available in your memory" or "in your mind, ready for you to recite them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **even to you** - This is repeated to emphasize the personal nature of this teaching.
