===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **crush the needy**  - "oppress people who are in need"
    * **Yahweh will plead their case**  - This means that Yahweh will defend the oppressed people in need.
    * **he will rob those of life who robbed them**  - The word "he" refers to Yahweh, and "them" refers to the poor.  This phrase means that Yahweh will destroy those who oppress poor people.
