===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the "thirty sayings" ([[:en:bible:notes:pro:22:20|22:20]]).

  * **someone who is ruled by anger** - someone who is unable to control his anger (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **rages** - shows violent anger
  * **you will take bait for your soul** - A person who wants to be like an angry person is like an animal taking the bait in a trap. AT: "you will be like an animal that eats the bait that closes a trap and is unable to escape" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **bait for your soul** - The soul is a metonym for the person's life. AT: "bait that someone has put out so he can kill you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
