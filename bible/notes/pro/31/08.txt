===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for the causes** - "speak for the causes" or "speak out for" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the poor and needy** - These two words have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "those who are poor and in need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
