===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Speak for those who cannot speak** - Speaking is a metonym for using words to defend innocent people. AT: "Defend those who cannot defend themselves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for the causes of all who are perishing** - "so that people will treat all who are perishing justly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for the causes** - The ellipsis can be filled in. AT: "speak for the causes" or "speak out for" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **poor and needy people** - These two words have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "people who are poor and cannot get the things that they need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
