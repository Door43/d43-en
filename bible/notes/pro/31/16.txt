===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the fruit of her hands** - The money she has earned from the work she did with wool and flax ([[:en:bible:notes:pro:31:13|31:13]]) is spoken of as if it were fruit growing off a tree. The hands are a synecdoche for the person. AT: "the money she has earned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **She dresses herself with strength** - Putting on clothes is a metonym for preparing for work. AT: "She prepares herself for hard physical work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **makes her arms strong** - "she strengthens her arms by doing her work"
