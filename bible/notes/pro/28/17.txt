===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he has shed someone's blood**  - "he has killed another" or "he has murdered another" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **fugitive**  - a person who is running away to avoid being captured
  * **will be kept safe**  - AT: "God will keep him safe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
