===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he has shed someone's blood** - Here "blood" represents a person's life. To "shed blood" means to murder someone. AT: "he has killed someone" or "he has murdered someone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **fugitive** - a person who is running away to avoid being captured
  * **until death** - "until he dies." This means for the rest of his life. AT: "all of his life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **will be kept safe** - AT: "God will keep him safe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
