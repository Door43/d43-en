===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Like a roaring lion or a charging bear is a wicked ruler over poor people** - Poor people who are helpless against an evil ruler are compared to a lion roaring at them or a bear attacking them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a charging bear** - A bear is a large, furry, dangerous animal that walks on four legs and has sharp claws and teeth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **The ruler who lacks understanding** - The abstract noun "understanding" can be translated as a verb. AT: "The ruler who does not understand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **oppressor** - a person who treats people harshly and makes their lives very difficult
