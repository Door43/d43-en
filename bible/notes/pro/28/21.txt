===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for a piece of bread a man will do wrong**  - AT: "a man will sin for very little gain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **A stingy man**  - a person who does not like to share his possessions
  * **hurries after riches**  - is greedy for riches.
