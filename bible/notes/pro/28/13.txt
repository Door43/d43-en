===== translationNotes: =====


  * **hides his sins** - "covers his sins." This is the opposite of confessing and forsaking sins, and is spoken of as covering sins rather than bringing them into the open (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **prosper** - "succeed" or "advance"
  * **the one who confesses them and forsakes them will be shown mercy** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will show mercy to the one who confesses and forsakes them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The one who always lives with reverence is blessed** - This can be expressed in active form. AT: "God will bless the one who always lives with reverence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **lives with reverence** - The abstract noun "reverence" can be expressed as an adjective. AT: "lives a reverent life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **reverence** - This refers to deeply respecting Yahweh and showing that respect by obeying him. 
  * **whoever hardens his heart** - "the one who hardens his heart"
  * **hardens his heart** - This is an idiom that means to be stubborn or unwilling to obey God. AT: "refuses to obey God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **will fall into trouble** - This represents ending up in misery and distress. AT: "will end up in trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
