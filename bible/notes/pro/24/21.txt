===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **who knows the extent of the destruction that will come from both of them?** The writer asks this question to emphasize the disaster.  - AT: "no one knows the extent of the destruction that will come from both of them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **both of them** - "Them" refers to both Yahweh and the king.
