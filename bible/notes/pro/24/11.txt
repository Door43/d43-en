===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **staggering** - walking unsteadily and almost falling
    * **slaughter** - violently killing
    * **Does not the One who weighs the heart understand what you are saying?**  - The writer assumes the readers know the answer and asks this for emphasis. AT: "The One who weighs the heart understands what you are saying." (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **the One who** - The writer uses this word to mean Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
    * **weighs the heart** - "tests or evaluates motives and desires"
    * **the One who guards your life, does he not know it?** - The writer assumes the readers know the answer and asks this for emphasis. AT: "the One who guards your life knows it." (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * ** will God not give to each one what he deserves?** - The writer assumes the readers know the answer and asks this for emphasis. AT: "God will give to each one what he deserves." (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
