===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A hot-tempered person** - AT: "A person who does not control his temper" or "A person who becomes angry quickly"
    * **if you rescue him** - "if you save him"
    * **Listen to advice and accept instruction** - These two phrases mean basically the same and are repeated to emphasize hoe important it is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **Listen to advice** - AT: "Pay attention to advice" or "Follow advice"
