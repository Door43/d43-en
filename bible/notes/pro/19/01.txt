===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Better is a poor person** - AT: "It is better to be a poor person"
    * **is perverse in speech** - AT: "speaks in an evil way"
    * **to have desire without knowledge** - AT: "for a person to be without knowledge" or "to work hard without knowing what you are doing"
    * **the one who runs too fast misses the path** - AT: "the one who acts too quickly makes mistakes" or "the one who acts too quickly makes poor choices" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
