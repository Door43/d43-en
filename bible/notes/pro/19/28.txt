===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the mouth of the wicked swallows iniquity** - AT: "wicked people enjoy doing evil as they enjoy eating food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Condemnation is ready for mockers** - AT: "God is ready to condemn mockers" or "people are ready to condemn mockers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
    * **flogging for the backs of fools** - AT: "flogging is ready for the backs of fools" or "fools will soon be flogged" or "people will soon punish fools" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **flogging** - beating with a whip or stick
