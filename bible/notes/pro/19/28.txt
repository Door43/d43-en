===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the mouth of the wicked swallows iniquity** - This speaks of how wicked people enjoy doing evil by saying that they swallow iniquity as easily as they swallow food. AT: "the wicked enjoy doing evil as much as they enjoy eating food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the mouth of the wicked swallows** - Here wicked people are referred to by their "mouths." AT: "the wicked swallow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the wicked** - This refers to wicked people. AT: "the wicked person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **Condemnation is ready for mockers and flogging for** - The words "condemnation" and "flogging" may be expressed as verbs. AT: "Yahweh is ready to condemn mockers and to flog" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **flogging for the backs** - The missing words may be added. AT: "flogging is ready for the backs" or "he is ready to flog the backs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **flogging** - beating with a whip or stick
