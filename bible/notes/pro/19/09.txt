===== translationNotes: =====


  * **A false witness will not go unpunished** - This can be written in positive and active form. Translate this phrase the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:19:05|19:5]]. AT: "They will certainly punish a false witness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **breathes out lies** - This figure of speech uses "breathes" to refer to lying constantly. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:06:17|6:19]]. AT: "constantly lies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **It is not fitting** - "It is not right"
  * **luxury** - condition of wealth and pleasure
  * **much less for a slave** - The missing words may be added. AT: "it is much less fitting for a slave" or "it is even worse for a slave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
