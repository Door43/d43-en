===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **his heart rages** - Here a person is referred to by his "heart" to emphasize his emotions. AT: "he rages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Wealth adds many friends** - This means that a person who is wealthy will have many friends because wealth attracts people. The full meaning of this can be made clear. AT: "Those who are wealthy easily find many friends" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **a poor person is separated from his friends** - A poor person is separated from many of his friends because of his poverty. The meaning of this can be made explicit. AT: "poverty causes a person to lose his friends" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
