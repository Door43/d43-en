===== translationNotes: =====

  * **how much more do his friends who go far away from him!** - This phrase is an exclamation to show that this is more likely than the previous phrase. AT: "therefore his friends will certainly hate him and go far away from him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **loves his own life** - Here the person is referred to by his "life" to emphasize himself being alive. AT: "loves himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **keeps understanding** - "has understanding"
