===== translationNotes: =====


  * **A false witness will not go unpunished** - "A false witness will certainly be punished" or "They will certainly punish a false witness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **breathes out lies** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:06:17|6:19]].
  * **will not escape** - AT: "will be captured" or "they will capture him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a generous person** - someone who often gives away things
