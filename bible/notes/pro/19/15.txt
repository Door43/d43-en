===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Laziness throws a person into a deep sleep** - This speaks of how laziness causes a person to sleep a lot as if laziness forcefully throws the person into sleep. AT: "Laziness makes a person sleep a lot" or "A lazy person sleeps a lot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **go hungry** - This is an idiom which means to not eat. AT: "not eat" or "be hungry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the command** - "the command that he was taught" 
  * **guards his life** - "protects his life"
  * **his ways** - This is an idiom which refers to how he lives. AT: "the way that he lives" or "how he lives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
