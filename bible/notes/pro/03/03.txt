===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not let covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness ever leave you** - "Always be faithful and trustworthy"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **write them on the tablet of your heart** - This phrase pictures the heart as a tablet, with lessons of faithfulness and trustworthiness written on it so they are remembered.(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **tablet** - a flat slab or surface to be used for writing or marking on.
  * **reputation** - the common opinion that people have about someone.
  * **in the sight of** - "in the judgement of"
