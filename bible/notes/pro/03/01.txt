===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer uses again the form of a father teaching his child using poetry.(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

    * **do not forget my commands** - remember my commands (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **length of days and years of life, and peace they will add to you** - AT: "they will give you a long, peaceful life."
