===== translationNotes: =====

  * **devastation caused by the wicked, when it comes** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "when the wicked cause devastation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh will be on your side** - "Yahweh will be by your side." A person standing by another person's side is an idiom that means that the one person will help and support the other. AT: "Yahweh will support and defend you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **will keep your foot from being caught in a trap** - The writer speaks of a person experiencing harm from "terror" and "devastation" as if the person were caught in a trap. The word "foot" represents the person. AT: "will protect you from those who want to harm you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
