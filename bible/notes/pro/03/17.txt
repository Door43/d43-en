===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues picturing wisdom as a woman, who is referred to with the pronouns "her" and "she."

    * **She is a tree of life to those who take hold of her** - "Wisdom gives life to those who have it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
