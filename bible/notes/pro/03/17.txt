===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues to picture wisdom as a woman.

  * **She is a tree of life to those who take hold of it** - "Wisdom gives life to those who have it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **tree of life** - "tree that gives life" as trees give fruit
