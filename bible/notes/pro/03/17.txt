===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Her ways are ways of kindness and all her paths are peace** - The writer speaks of wisdom as if it were a woman and of the benefits that wisdom gives a person as if wisdom were leading that person along a path. AT: "Wisdom will always treat you kindly and give you peace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **She is a tree of life to those who take hold of it** - The writer speaks of wisdom as if it were a tree that bears life-giving fruit and of a person who benefits from wisdom as if that person ate of the fruit. AT: "Wisdom is like a tree that sustains the life of those who eat of its fruit" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a tree of life** - "a tree that gives life" or "a tree whose fruit sustains life"
  * **those who hold on to it** - "those who hold on to its fruit"
