===== translationNotes: =====


  * **your foot will not stumble** - AT: "you will not do things that are wrong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **afraid** - This refers to the unpleasant feeling a person has when there is a threat of harm to himself or others.
  * **your sleep will be sweet** - "your sleep will be pleasant" or "you will sleep peacefully"
