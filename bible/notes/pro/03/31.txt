===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do not ... choose any of his ways** - It is implied that he is not to choose to imitate the actions of the violent person. AT: "Do not ... choose to imitate any of his ways" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the devious person is detestable to Yahweh** - "Yahweh detests the devious person"
  * **devious** - going away from the proper way; deceitful
  * **he brings the upright person into his confidence** - Yahweh shares his thoughts with the those who do right as with a close, trusted friend. AT: "Yahweh is a close friend to the upright" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
