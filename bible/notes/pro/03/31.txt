===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **devious** - going away from the proper way; lying
    * **detestable** - one who should be hated
    * **he brings the upright into his confidence** - Yahweh shares his thoughts with the those who do right as with a close, trusted friend.
    * **upright** - "righteous" or "honest" or "just"
