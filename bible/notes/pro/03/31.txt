===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **devious** - going away from the proper way; lying
    * **detestable** - a person who should be hated
    * **he brings the upright into his confidence** - Yahweh shares his thoughts with the those who do right as with a close, trusted friend.
    * **the upright** - "the righteous" or "the honest" or "the just" This word should be understood as a plural. AT: "the upright people"
