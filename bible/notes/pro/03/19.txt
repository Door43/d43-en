===== translationNotes: =====


  * **founded the earth** - "created the world"
  * **heavens** - This refers to everything we see above the earth, including the sun, moon, and stars. It also includes the heavenly bodies, such as far-off planets, that we cannot directly see from the earth.
  * **dew** - water that forms on the ground at night
