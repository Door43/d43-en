===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **To do what is right and just **- "right" and "just" mean basically the same thing, to reinforce the concept of righteousness. (See: Doublet)
  * **haughty eyes and a proud heart**  - "haughty" and "proud" together reinforce the concept of a person being prideful. "Eyes" and "heart" are all associated with being human. (See: Doublet; Metonymy)
  * **the lamp of the wicked **- their outward happiness, like a shining lamp, cause them to sin. (See: Metaphor)
