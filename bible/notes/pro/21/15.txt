===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When justice is done** - The abstract noun "justice" can be translated as a noun phrase. These words can be translated in active form. AT: "When rulers do what is just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **wanders from the way of understanding** - This is an idiom. "no longer lives wisely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will rest in the assembly of the dead** - "he will remain in the assembly of dead spirits"
