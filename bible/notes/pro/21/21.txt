===== translationNotes: =====

  * **scales the city** - "climbs up and over the wall that surrounds the city"
  * **the city of the mighty ones** - "a city in which mighty men are living" or "a city of mighty warriors"
  * **he brings down** - This is an idiom. AT: "he destroys" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the stronghold in which they trusted** - "the walls and towers around the city that they did not think anyone would be able to get past into the city, so they felt safe"
