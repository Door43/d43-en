===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There is no wisdom, understanding, or advice** - These three words refer to the same thing. AT: "Absolutely no human knowledge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **The horse is prepared for the day of battle** - AT: "Soldiers prepare horses for the day of battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the day of battle** - AT: "when there is a battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
