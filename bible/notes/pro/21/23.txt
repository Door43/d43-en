===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Whoever guards his mouth and tongue - **"guards" here means "observes" and "keeps watch", same as "keeps himself out of trouble"; "mouth and tongue" together refer to the speech organs and words. (See: Doublet; Metonymy; Synecdoche)
  * **The proud and haughty person**  - two words mean basically the same thing. (See: Doublet)
  * **the "mocker" is his name **- it makes an exclamation to someone - the proud and haughty person. (See: Parallelism; Apostrophe)
