===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who will not hear the cry of poor people** - "The one who will not listen to the outcry of the poor" AT: "The one who will not pay attention to how the poor suffer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he will not be heard** - AT: "no one will hear him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **A gift in secret ... a concealed gift takes** - These two phrases express the same idea to emphasize how effective this can be. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **appeases anger** - AT: "turns anger into peace"
