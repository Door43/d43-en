===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the diligent**  - "a diligent man" AT: "a man who works hard"
  * **comes only to poverty**  - "only becomes poor"
  * **Acquiring riches**  - "Obtaining riches" or "Gaining wealth"
  * **a lying tongue**  - AT: "speaking lies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **a fleeting vapor**  - These riches are compared to a mist that quickly goes away in the morning. "a disappearing mist" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **and a snare of death**  - Riches gotten dishonestly will kill the person who has them.
