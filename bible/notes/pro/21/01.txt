===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The king's heart is a stream of water in the hand of Yahweh** - Yahweh moves the king's thoughts and feelings freely, as if a man directs a small channel of water for irrigation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]; [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]; [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **right to him** - "morally and socially correct to him"
  * **it is Yahweh who weighs the hearts**  - "it is Yahweh who measures the motives." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]; [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
