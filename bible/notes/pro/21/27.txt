===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The sacrifice of the wicked is detestable** - The writer does not mention Yahweh here, as in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:15:07|15:8]], but the reader should understand that it is Yahweh who detests the sacrifice of the wicked.
  * **the wicked** - The nominal adjective "wicked" can be translated as a noun phrase. AT: "the wicked person," referring to any wicked person, or "wicked people," referring to all wicked people. [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]]
  * **it is even more detestable** - Yahweh detests the sacrifice even more
  * **will speak for all time** - because people will never forget what he said
