===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a corner of the housetop** - "the edge of the roof"
  * **craves** - "strongly desires" AT: "really wants to do something"
  * **no kindness in his eyes** - AT: "that he is not kind" or "that he is very mean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]], and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
