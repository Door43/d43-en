===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your flesh and your body waste away** - The words "flesh" and "body" mean basically the same thing and represent the whole person. AT: "your body wastes away" or "you waste away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **waste away** - "physically wear down" or "become weak and unhealthy"
  * **I hated discipline ... my heart despised correction** - These two phrases express the same idea and emphasize how much this person disliked what the teacher had said. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **How I hated discipline** - The word "How" is an exclamation that emphasizes the strength of his hatred. The word "discipline" can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "I hated so much when someone would discipline me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclamations]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **my heart despised correction** - Here the word "heart" represents the person and his emotions. The word "correction" can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "I despised people when they corrected me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
