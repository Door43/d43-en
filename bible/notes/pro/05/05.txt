===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Her feet go down to death ... her steps go all the way to sheol** - These two phrases express the same idea to emphasize how dangerous she is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Her feet go ... her steps go ... Her footsteps wander** - AT: "She goes ... she goes ... She wanders" This is the adulteress from [[:en:bible:notes:pro:05:03|5:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sheol** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:01:12|1:12]].
  * **She gives no thought to** - "She does not follow" or "She does not care about"
  * **life** - This refers to physical life on earth.
  * **Her footsteps wander** - AT: "She walks around randomly" or "She walks without a sense of direction"
