===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The iniquities of the wicked will seize him**  - This implies that wicked people will die as a punishment for their sin. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification|Personification]])
  * **the cords of his sin will hold him tight**  - Sin is pictured here as a small trap for birds that works with cords. AT: "he is no longer free from his desire to sin." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **he is led astray by his great foolishness**  - AT: "his great foolishness leads him astray" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **by his great foolishness**  - AT: "because he acted very stupidly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns|Abstract Nouns]])
