===== Translation Notes: =====

Verses 13 and 14 conclude the quote that started in verse 12.

    * **To some degree I was active in every evil**  - AT: "I was almost ruined"
    * **this was known to the assembly and to the congregation**  - "everyone knew about it"
    * **assembly and to the congregation** - Both assembly and congregation refer to gatherings of people for worship.
