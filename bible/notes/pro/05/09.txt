===== translationNotes: =====

  * **not give away your honor to others** - Possible meanings are 1) you will not lose the respect of other people (UDB) 2) what is honorable about you will not be lost because of what you gave to the adulteress.
  * **years of your life to a cruel person** - The cruel person is the adulteress.
  * **strangers will not feast on your wealth; what you have worked for will not go into the house of strangers** - Possible meanings are 1) strangers will not take your possessions (UDB) 2) the children you will have with the adulteress will not take your inheritance. AT: "your bastard children will not enjoy your wealth, and a family that is not your own will not claim your inheritance"
  * **feast on** - AT: "hold a feast because they took" or "enjoy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
