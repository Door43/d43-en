===== translationNotes: =====


  * **My son ... my wisdom ... my understanding** - That is the son, wisdom, and understanding of the wise person who started teaching in chapter 1.
  * **pay attention to my wisdom; listen carefully to my understanding** - These two phrases express the same idea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **discretion** - the quality of being careful with regard to one's actions and speech. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:01:04|1:4]].
  * **your lips** - "you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **may protect knowledge** - "will say the right thing"
