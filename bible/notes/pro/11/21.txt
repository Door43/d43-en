===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **will not go unpunished** - AT: "will be punished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
    * **Like a gold ring ... without discretion**  - A beautiful woman without discretion is compared to a useless and unsuitable golden ring in a pig's nose. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **discretion** - AT: "common sense" or "understanding"
