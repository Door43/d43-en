===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **will prosper** - AT: "will do well"
  * **the one who** - "the person who" or "anyone who"
  * **good gifts crown the head of him who sells it** - AT: "good gifts are given as a crown of honor to him who sells it" or "the person who sells it is honored with many blessings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
