===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Disaster runs after sinners** - Disaster is given human characteristics like the ability to run. AT: "Sinners have trouble wherever they go" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **those who do right are rewarded with good** - "God rewards those who do right with good" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **his grandchildren** - "the sons of his sons" or "the children of his children" AT: "his descendants"
    * **a sinner's wealth is stored up for the one who does right** - "the one who does right will receive the wealth that a sinner has stored up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
