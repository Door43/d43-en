===== translationNotes: =====


  * **the one who honors the command will be rewarded** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They will reward the one who honors the command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **fountain of life** - "Fountain" represents a good source of water and here a source of life. AT: "a bountiful source of life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **snares of death** - "Snares" represent dangers that will kill. AT: "traps that lead to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
