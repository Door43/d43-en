===== translationNotes: =====

  * **repugnant** - causing a strong feeling of disgust
  * **faultless in their path** - "Path" represents how a person directs his life. AT: "faultless in their way of living" or "lives a life of integrity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Righteousness protects those** - "Righteousness" represents a way of life approved by Yahweh. This quality acts like a person who protects. AT: "A way of life approved by Yahweh protects" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **wickedness turns away those who commit sin** - "Wickedness" represents an evil conduct of life. This quality acts like a person who turns away those who commit sin. AT: "wickedness will turn sinners away from a successful path" or "wickedness ruins sinners' lives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
