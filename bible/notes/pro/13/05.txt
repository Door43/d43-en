===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **repugnant** - causing a strong feeling of disgust
  * **faultless in their path** - AT: "faultless in their way of living" or "lives a life of integrity"
  * **wickedness turns away those who commit sin** - "wickedness will turn sinners away from a successful path" or "wickedness ruins sinners' lives"
