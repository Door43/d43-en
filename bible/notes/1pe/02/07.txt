===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter continues quoting from the Scriptures.

  * **the stone that was rejected by the builders** - Peter is telling what a prophet wrote in the Scriptures long ago. The "stone" is the cornerstone which is the most important stone in the building. This refers to Jesus whom many people rejected. AT: "the stone that the builders rejected." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a stone of stumbling and a rock of stumbling.** - Peter is again telling what a prophet wrote long ago. These two phrases share similar meanings. Together they emphasize that people will take offense at this "stone" which refers to Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **disobeying the word** - AT: "disobeying God's commands." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for which they were also appointed** - AT: "for which God appointed them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
