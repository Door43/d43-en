===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Come to him who is a living stone** - Peter compares Jesus to the most important stone in the foundation of a building. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **that has been rejected by people** - AT: "that many people rejected" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but that has been chosen by God** - AT: "but that God has chosen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **You also are like...spiritual house** - Just as stones are used to build a house, God is bringing us together to build his spiritual house or family. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **that are being built up to be a spiritual house** - AT: "that God is building into a spiritual house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
