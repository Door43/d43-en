===== Translation Notes: =====

Peter has been talking about Christ's resurrection to heaven and his authority and power.

  * **Therefore** - This word marks the conclusion of Peter's thoughts to his audience.
  * **in the flesh** - "in his body"
  * **arm yourselves with the same intention**  - The phrase "arm yourselves" refer to soldiers who get their weapons and prepare for battle. Here it means for believers to be determined in their mind to suffer as Jesus did. AT: "prepare yourselves to do the same thing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **yourselves**  - This refers to the believers in chapter one.
