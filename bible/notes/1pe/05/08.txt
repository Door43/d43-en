===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like a roaring lion** - Peter compares the devil to a lion to emphasize that the devil is cruel and fierce. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **stalking around** - "walking about" or "walking about and hunting"
  * **Stand against him** - "Resist him"
  * **in the world** - "in various places throughout the world"
