===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Be sober** - Here the word "sober" refers to mental clarity and alertness. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1pe:01:13|1:13]]. AT: "Control your thoughts" or "Be careful about what you think" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the devil, is stalking around like a roaring lion ... looking for someone to devour** - Peter compares the devil to a roaring lion. Just as a hungry lion completely devours its prey, the devil is seeking to completely destroy the faith of believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **stalking around** - "walking about" or "walking about and hunting"
  * **Stand against him** - Standing is a metonym for fighting. AT:"Fight against him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your community** - Peter speaks of fellow believers as members of the same community. AT: "your fellow believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in the world** - "in various places throughout the world"
