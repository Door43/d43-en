===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Peter begins to talk about the believers' salvation and faith.

  * **our Lord Jesus Christ...gave to us a new birth** - The words "our" and "us" refer to Peter and those to whom he is writing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **he gave to us a new birth** - "he caused us to be born again" 
  * **for the confidence of an inheritance** - You can translate this using a verb. AT: "so that we confidently expect to receive an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **inheritance** - Receiving what God has promised believers is spoken of as if it were inheriting property and wealth from a family member. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead** - Possible meanings are 1) that God has caused us the be born again "through the resurrection of Jesus Christ" or 2) that "through the resurrection of Jesus Christ" we confidently expect to receive an inheritance.
  * **will not perish, will not become stained, and will not fade away** - Peter uses three similar phrases to describe the inheritance as something that is perfect and eternal.
  * **It is reserved in heaven for you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God is reserving it in heaven for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **By God's power you are protected through faith** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Through your faith, God is protecting you by his power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that is ready to be revealed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that God is ready to reveal" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
