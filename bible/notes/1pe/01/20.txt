===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Christ was chosen** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God chose Christ" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **before the foundation of the world** - You can translate this with a verbal phrase. AT: "before God created the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]) 
  * **he has been revealed to you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God has revealed him to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **he has been revealed to you** - Peter does not mean that his readers actually saw Christ, but that they learned the truth about him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **and gave him glory** - "and glorified him" or "and showed that he is glorious" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
