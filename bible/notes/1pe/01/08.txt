===== translationNotes: =====

  * **unspeakable joy** - "joy that words cannot describe"
  * **the salvation of your souls** - Here the word "souls" refers to the whole person. You may translate this using a verb. AT: "your salvation" or "God is saving you" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **searched and inquired carefully** - The words "inquired carefully" mean basically the same thing as "searched." Together these words emphasize how diligently the prophets tried to understand this salvation. AT: "examined very carefully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
