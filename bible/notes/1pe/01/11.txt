===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter continues talking about the prophets' search for salvation.

  * **They searched to know** - "They tried to determine"
  * **the Spirit of Christ** - This is a reference to the Holy Spirit.
  * **It was revealed to the prophets** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God revealed to the prophets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they were serving these things, not for themselves, but for you** - "they were serving these things for you, not for themselves." In some languages it is more natural to put the positive before the negative. 
  * **they were serving these things** - "by doing these things, they were serving." The words "these things" refer to the prophecies that the prophets spoke and wrote down.
  * **the telling of these things through those bringing the gospel to you** - "these things have been told to you by those bringing the gospel to you." The things of which the prophets spoke and wanted to learn more are explained in the gospel. 
  * **angels desire to be revealed** - "angels desire to understand"
