===== translationNotes: =====

  * **twenty thousand, thousands upon thousands** - This is probably not meant to be an exact number, but to indicate a large number. AT: "many thousands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **ascended** - To "ascend" is to to move up, to go toward the sky.
