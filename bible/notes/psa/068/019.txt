===== Translation Notes: =====

David continues the story of God's protection of his people while they were in the desert fighting enemies.

  * **Yahweh the Lord comes to rescue from death** - Just as God rescued his people from physical death in their wars (See: definition 1 in Important terms) he continues to save his people from spiritual death.(See: Definition 2 in Important Terms)
