===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will bring them back** - The word "them" refers to God's enemies.
  * **so that you may crush your enemies, dipping your foot in blood,** - This describe the battle where their enemies are completely defeated. In a great battle like this, there is a lot of blood. Here it speaks of them stepping in that blood. AT: "so that you will totally defeat your enemies, stepping in their blood,"
