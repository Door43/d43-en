===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **formed me** - AT: "made me" or "created me"
  * **You formed my inner being; you formed me in my mother's womb.** - AT: "You made all of me before I was born. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
  * **awesome and wonderful** - These two words mean basically the same thing and emphasize how great Yahweh's deeds are. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
