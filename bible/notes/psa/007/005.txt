===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my life** - The life represents the person. AT: "me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **overtake me** - This represents capturing him. AT: "capture me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **let him trample my life on the ground** - Here "my life" represents the writer. AT: "allow him to destroy me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **lay my honor in the dust** - This refers to lying dead and unburied in disgrace.
