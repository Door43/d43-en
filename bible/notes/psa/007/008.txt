===== translationNotes: =====

  * **vindicate me** - "show them that I am not guilty"
  * **establish the righteous people** - "make the righteous people strong" or "make the righteous people prosper" 
  * **you who examine hearts and minds** - The hearts and minds represent people's desires and thoughts. AT: "you who know our inner thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
