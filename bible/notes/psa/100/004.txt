===== translationNotes: =====

  * **with thanksgiving** - "while thanking him" or "while giving thanks to him"
  * **endures forever** - AT: "is loyal to us forever" or "will never forsake us"
  * **bless his name** - The word "name" is a metonym for Yahweh himself. AT: "bless Yahweh" or "do what makes Yahweh happy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) See how "may his glorious name be blessed" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:072:018|72:19]].
  * **his truthfulness through all generations** - "his truthfulness endures through all generations"
  * **through all generations** - generation after generation. See how this is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:089:003|89:4]].
