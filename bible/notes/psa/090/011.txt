===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Who knows the intensity of your anger; your wrath matches the fear it inspires?** - AT: "Who knows the intensity of your anger, and your wrath that is equal to the fear it causes?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) or "no one knows the degree of your anger, and your rage that is equal to the fear it causes." 
  * **Turn back, Yahweh!** - AT: "Turn back from your anger, Yahweh!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **pity** - "sympathy"
