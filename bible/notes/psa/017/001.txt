===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A prayer of David** - "This is a prayer that David wrote."
  * **Give ear to my prayer from lips without deceit** - Here "lips" refers to the words a person speaks. AT: "Listen to my prayer which does not come from lying lips" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Let my vindication come from your presence** - Here "presence" refers to Yahweh's being. AT "Declare me innocent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **let your eyes see what is right!** - Here "your eyes" refers to that which Yahweh knows to be true. AT: "you know that I am telling the truth!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
