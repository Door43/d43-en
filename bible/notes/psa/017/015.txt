===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will see your face** - David is confident he will see Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I will be satisfied, when I awake**  - AT: "I will be happy to wake up in your presence,"  Possible meanings: 1) "when I wake up each day" or 2) "when I wake up after death"
  * **with a sight of you.**  - AT: "when I see you are still with me."
