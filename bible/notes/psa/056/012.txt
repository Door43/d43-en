===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The duty to fulfill my vows to you is on me** - Being obligated to do something is spoken of as if the duty to do something is on a person. AT: "I must fulfill my vows to you" or "I must do what I promised you I would do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you have kept my feet from falling** - The feet here represent the person.  Falling here probably represents being killed by his enemies. AT: "you have kept me from falling" or "you have kept me from being killed by my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **so that I may walk before God** - Living and being seen by God is spoken of as walking before God. AT: "so that I may live in God's presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in the light of the living** - Here "the light of the living" probably represents God's enabling people to live. AT: "with the life that you give" or "because you enable me to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
