===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Each verse consists of two lines that have very similar meanings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **like a heap** - "like behind a dam." The writer describes the creation of the sea as if God piles up all the waters together. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **he puts the oceans in storehouses** - The writer describes the creation of the oceans as if God put them in a storehouse. AT: "he puts the oceans in their place, just like a man puts grain in a storehouse" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Let the whole earth** - This refers to the people on the earth. AT: "Let everyone on earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **stand in awe of him** - Here "stand in awe" is an idiom that means "be in awe." AT: "honor him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **stood in place** - Here "stood in place" is an idiom that means "was created." AT: "started to exist" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
