===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Surely every man walks about like a shadow** - The lives of people are spoken of as if they are as insignificant as shadows. AT: "Everyone disappears like shadows do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **although they do not know who will receive them** - Here it is implied that they do not know what will happen to their wealth after they die. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Now, Lord, for what am I waiting?** - The writer asks this question to emphasize that people cannot help him. This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "So now, Yahweh, I can expect to receive nothing from anyone else." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
