===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh, make me know...the extent of my days** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Show me how transient I am** - "Show me how quick my life is" or "Show me how soon I will die"
  * **See, you have made my days only the width of my hand** - "It seems that you have caused me to live only a short time;" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Surely every man is a single breath.** - "The time that humans live is too short." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
