===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh is righteous in all his ways** - "People can see from everything that Yahweh does that he is righteous"
  * **gracious in all he does** - "and he is gracious in all he does" or "people can see from everything that Yahweh does that he is gracious"
  * **is near to all those who call to him** - "acts quickly to help those who pray" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **to all who call to him in trustworthiness** - The abstract noun "trustworthiness" can be stated as an action. AT: "to all who tell only the truth when they pray" or "to all whom he trusts when they pray" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
