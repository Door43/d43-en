===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My mouth will** - "I will" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **will speak out the praise of Yahweh** - "tell everyone how good Yahweh is"
  * **forever and ever** - see how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:021:003|21:4]]
  * **let all mankind bless his holy name** - The word "name" is a metonym for Yahweh himself. AT: "let all people bless him" or "do what makes him happy." See how "may his glorious name be blessed" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:072:018|72:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
