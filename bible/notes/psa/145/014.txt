===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the eyes of all wait**- AT: "everyone waits" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **You open your hand**- AT: "You provide"
  * **satisfy the desire of every living thing**- AT: "you go beyond just meeting the needs of everybody" or "give blessings beyond supplying the basic necessities for all"
