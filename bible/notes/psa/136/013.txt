===== translationNotes: =====

  * **overthrew Pharaoh** - Here defeat is spoken of as if it were tipping someone over. AT: "defeated Pharaoh" or "defeated the king of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Pharaoh** - That is, the army of Pharaoh (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in the Sea of Reeds** - The soldiers of Pharaoh drowned in the Sea of Reeds.
