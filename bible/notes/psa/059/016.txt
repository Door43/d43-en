===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you have been my high tower and a refuge** - High towers and refuges are places that people can go to for shelter from their enemies. The psalmist speaks of God protecting him as if God were a strong, safe shelter. AT: "you have protected me like a high tower and a refuge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in the day of my distress** - The word "day" here refers to a period of time. AT: "whenever I have had troubles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **To you, my strength, I will sing praises** - This can be expressed with two sentences. AT: "You are my strength, so I will sing praises to you"
  * **my strength** - God's being the psalmist's strength represents God protecting him. AT: "my protector" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for God is my high tower** - High towers are places that people can go to for shelter from their enemies. The psalmist speaks of God protecting him as if God were a strong, safe shelter. AT: "you have protected me like a high tower"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the God of covenant faithfulness** - AT: "he is the God of covenant faithfulness" or "You are the God of covenant faithfulness"
