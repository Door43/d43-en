===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My God will meet me** - God's rescuing the psalmist is spoken of as if God were to meet him. AT: "My God will rescue me"
  * **my desire on my enemies** - The abstract noun "desire" can be expressed with the verb "want." AT: "what I want to happen to my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Scatter them** - "Cause them to wander around" 
  * **our shield** - God's protecting the righteous is spoken of as if God were a shield. AT: "our protector" or "the one who protects us like a shield" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
