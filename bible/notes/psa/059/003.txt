===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they wait in ambush to take my life** - David's enemies are in hiding and are waiting quietly for the time when they can attack him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **awake** - Deciding to do something and beginning to do it is spoken of as waking up.  AT: "do something" or "take action" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and see** - What he wants God to see can be stated clearly. AT: "and see what is happening to me" or "and see what they are doing to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
