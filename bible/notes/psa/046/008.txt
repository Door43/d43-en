===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold** - AT: Pay attention to and understand.
  * **to the ends of the earth**- The entire earth is referred to by its ends. AT: "in all places" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **breaks the bow...cuts the spear...burns the chariots** - The destruction of things used for war is used to describe the end of war. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
