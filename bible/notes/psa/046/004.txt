===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There is a river, the streams of which make the city of God happy, the holy place of the tabernacles of the Most High** - AT: "The holy place of the tabernacles of the Most High is like a river because it causes the people of the city where God worshipped to be happy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **moved** - "shaken" When enemies try to destroy the walls of the city of God, they will fail and the walls will not even move.
