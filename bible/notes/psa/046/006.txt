===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the kingdoms were shaken** - AT: "the kingdoms were shaken with fear (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the earth melted** - AT: the earth melted in fear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **lifted up his voice** - To make a voice higher refers to making it louder.
  * **God of Jacob is our refuge** - AT: "God of Jacob is like a refuge because he protects us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
