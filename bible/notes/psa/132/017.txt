===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **make the horn ... to sprout**  - The horn is a metonymy for power. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **sprout** - grow up quickly
    * **my anointed one**  - AT: "my king"
    * **his crown will shine**  - a reference to the glory of the king (see:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
