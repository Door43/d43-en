===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **their...they** - He is talking about his enemies.
  * **their inward being is wicked** - "They want to destroy others"
  * **their throat is an open sepulchre** - "their talk threatens destruction." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **schemes** - "plans"
  * **downfall** - "destruction"
