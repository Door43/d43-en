===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information** - David talks about his enemies.

  * **For there is no truth in their mouth** - Truth being in the mouth represents speaking truthfully. AT: "For they never say what is true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **their inward being is wicked** - "They want to destroy others"
  * **their throat is an open sepulchre** - People who talk about destroying others are spoken of as if their throat were an open grave. An open tomb is ready for dead bodies to be put into it. AT:  "their talk threatens destruction." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sepulchre - tomb
  * **schemes** - plans
  * **downfall** - destruction
