===== Translation Notes: =====

(See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])

  * **For the chief musician;** - "This is for the director of music to use in worship;"
  * **A psalm of David**  - "This is a psalm that David wrote."
  * **with wind instruments**  - "This song should be accompanied by people who play wind instruments."
  * **groan**  - This means to make a low sound with the voice as a result of physical or emotional stress.
  * **Listen to my call to you, Yahweh; think about my groanings. Listen to the sound of my call, my King and my God,**  - AT: "Yahweh, you are my king and my God. Please pay attention to my desperate prayer." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **bring my petition**  - AT: "make my request" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **expectantly**  - "hopefully"
