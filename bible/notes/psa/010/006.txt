===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He says** - "The wicked man says"
  * **adversity** - This is a very difficult situation. 
  * **His mouth is full of cursing and deceptive, harmful words** - The "mouth" represents a place where evil speech comes from. AT: "He is lying and saying evil things about people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **his tongue injures and destroys** - Here the "tongue" represents his evil speech. AT: "he speaks words that threaten and hurt people."
