===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]].

  * **Why, Yahweh, do you stand far off? Why do you hide yourself in times of trouble?**  - AT: "Yahweh, it seems that you are very far away!" AT: "Yahweh, It seems as though you hide from me whenever I am in trouble." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **schemes** - "plans"
  * **his deepest desires** - AT: "the evil things that he wants to do." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **greedy** - This is a when someone desires more things selfishly.
