===== translationNotes: =====
  * **among the gods** - The writer is not acknowledging these other gods exist. He is referring to false gods that people from other nations worship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **All the nations** - Here "the nations" represent the people. AT: "The people from all nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They will honor your name** - Here "name" represents the whole person. AT: "They will honor you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
