===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For great is your covenant faithfulness toward me** - The abstract noun "faithfulness" can be stated as an adjective. AT: "For you are very faithful to me because of your covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **you have rescued my life from the depths of Sheol** - This does not mean God brought him back to life after he died. It means God saved him when he was about to die.
  * **the arrogant** - This nominal adjective can be stated as an adjective. AT: "arrogant people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **have risen up against me** - "are coming together to harm me"
  * **seek my life** - This is a way of saying they want to kill him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
