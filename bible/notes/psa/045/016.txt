===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will make your name to be remembered - **  The word "I" refers to the writer. A person's name represents the whole person and their character. (See: Metonymy**)**
  * **I will make your name to be remembered in all generations; therefore  the peoples will give you thanks forever and ever - **  AT: "Because of my song, (all) ** **people will always remember your name and they will always thank you."
