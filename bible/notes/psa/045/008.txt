===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **myrrh, aloes, and cassia - **  These three spices were used as beauty treatments and had a good smell.
  * **ivory palaces -**  This is a palace made of a hard white substance from the teeth of animals. An ivory palace would require much money to build.
  * **stringed instruments**  - Musical instruments that have strings (thin strips of material) and make sounds when a person touches or strikes the strings.
  * **honorable women **- These women are wives of the king who receive his approval.
  * **at your right hand stands the queen**  - The place at the right hand of the king is a place of honor. (See: right hand)
  * **Ophir - **  Ophir was a harbour talked about in the Bible. Ophir was famous for its wealth, such as gold and ivory.
