===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the chief musician -**
  * **set to Shoshannim**  -
  * **A psalm of the sons of Korah**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of the sons of Korah" or “This is a psalm that the sons of Korah wrote”
  * **A maschil - **  See how you translated this in 32:1.
  * **A song of loves **  -
  * **My heart overflows - **  The word "overflows" means to flow over the top edge of a container. The author compares the happiness that fills his heart to water that flows over the top of a container. (See: Metaphor)
  * **subject **-Here the term “subject” refers to the topic of the author's writing.
  * **composed**  - Here "composed" means to write or create a song.
  * **my tongue is the pen of a ready writer**  - This phrase compares the author's words (my tongue) to the work (the pen) of a quick and intellegent writer to say that he is skilled and eager to sing this song. (See: Synecdoche) (See: Metaphor)
  * **You are fairer than the children of men**  - The word "you" refers to the king. AT: "O King, you are more pleasant to look at than any other person"
  * **grace is poured onto your lips**  - AT: "you speak with mercy and you are kind"
