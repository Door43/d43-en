===== Translation Notes: =====
The author continues to speak about the queen.

  * **embroidered** - a design made by sewing together clothes of many colors.
  * **They will be led**  - The word "They" refers to the bride and her companions.
  * **They will be led by gladness and rejoicing **  -This phrase decribes "gladness and rejoicing" as a person who leads others to celebrate. AT: "The bride and her companions go forward, very glad and full of joy." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
