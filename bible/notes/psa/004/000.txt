====== Psalm 004 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 4 is a psalm of deliverance. It is an evening song, intended to be sung in the temple accompanied by musical instruments during evening worship. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]] and [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Trust ==
God protects all those who truly trust in him for protection. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:trust]])
