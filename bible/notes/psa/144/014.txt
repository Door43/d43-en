===== translationNotes: =====

  * **No one will break through our walls** - "No one will be able to invade our city"
  * **no outcry** - Here the outcry is associated with a severe problem that causes the person to cry out. AT: "no one crying in pain" or "no one crying for help" or "no one calling out for justice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
