===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Declare his glory among the nations** - "Tell all the people in every nation about his great glory"
  * **Yahweh is great and is to be praised greatly** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Yahweh is great. Praise him greatly" or "Yahweh is great, and people should praise him greatly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **He is to be feared above all other gods** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Fear him above all other gods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
