===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my sin is always before me** - Not being able to forget his sins is spoken of as if they were always in front of him where he can see them. AT: "I am always aware of my sins" or "I cannot forget my sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **done what is evil** - "I have done what is evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
