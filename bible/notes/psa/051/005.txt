===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See, ...** - "Indeed, ..."
  * **See, I was born in iniquity; as soon as my mother conceived me, I was in sin.** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **See, you desire trustworthiness ... you will make me know wisdom** - AT: "You desire honesty in my heart and you teach me wisdom"
