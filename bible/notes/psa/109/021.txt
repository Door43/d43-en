===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **deal kindly with me** - "do good things for me"
  * **with me** - The word "me" refers to David.
  * **for your name’s sake** - "for the sake of your reputation"
  * **oppressed and needy** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:109:014|109:16]].
  * **my heart is wounded within me** - "I am full of grief and dispair." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I am fading away like the shadow of the evening** - David feels like his is about to die just like a shadow that disappears in the evening. AT: "I am just about to die." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **I am shaken off like a locust** - AT: "My enemies drive me away as easily as the wind blows the locust away" or "My enemies chase me away like people chase away locust."
