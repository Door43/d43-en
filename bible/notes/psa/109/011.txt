===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the creditor** - someone who lends money to another person but expects that the person will pay the money back
  * **plunder** - "steal"
  * **May his children be cut off; may their name be blotted out** - These two phrases have similar meanings. AT: "May his children be destroyed; may he have no descendants in the next generation." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
