===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking directly to his people.

  * **forty years** - "40 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **that generation** - "all of those people" or "that entire generation of people"
  * **wander astray** - God speaks of the people as if they were sheep, which will go wherever they want to and not stay near their shepherd. AT: "move away from me" or "go their own way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they have not known my ways** - "they have not obeyed me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my ways** - "the way I want them to live"
  * **my resting place** - "the place where I would have enabled them to rest"
