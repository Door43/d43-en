===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In his hand** - This is an idiom which means "in his control" or "he is responsible for." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **depths** - deep places
  * **heights** - high places
  * **his hands formed** - The word "hands" is a metonym for Yahweh himself. AT: "he himself formed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
