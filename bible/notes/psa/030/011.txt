===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have**  - The word "You" refers to Yahweh throughout this section.
  * **clothed me with gladness**  - Biblical Hebrew often speaks of emotions as if they were clothing. To be clothed with gladness means to be happy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **So now my honored heart will sing praise**  - The speaker says that it is an honor for him to praise God.
