===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you established me as a strong mountain** - AT: "you have made my mountain to stand by your favor" 
  * **mountain** - The person does not become a "mountain," but strong like a mountain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * ** when you hid your face** - The writer feels as though Yahweh is not near him at times. He can't actually see Yahweh's face.
  * **I was troubled** - I was fearful" or "I was worried" 
  * **sought favor**  - "looked for favor"
