===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** A psalm of David. ** - AT: "A Psalm written by David."
  * **never let me be humiliated**  - AT: "do not let others humiliate me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **rock of refuge**  - AT: "like a huge rock on which I can be safe" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **be my rock of refuge, a stronghold to save me**  - The phrase "be my rock of refuge" is a request for protection. A second similar request is provided for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
