====== Psalms 031 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm is a psalm of deliverance. His enemies thought they had defeated him but God sustained him.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Even though everyone deserted and despised him, God did not abandon him.
