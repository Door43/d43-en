===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the faithful** - This refers to the faithful people. AT: "the people  who are faithful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **he pays back the arrogant in full** - Here "pay back" is an idiom that refers to punishment. AT: "he gives the proud people all of the punishment that they deserve" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
