===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as a dead man **- This phrase stresses that he has no part to play among people. He is ignored. (See: Simile)
  * **like a broken pot **- He feels he has no part to play among people. He is ignored.
  * **whispering of many **- AT: "people slandering me" (UDB) The people talk about him in secret. (See: Idiom)
  * **terrifying news from every side **- AT: "scary reports from many sources"
