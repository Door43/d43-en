===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My destiny is in your hand** - Here "your hand" refers to Yahweh's power. AT: "You have the power to decide my future" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **from those who pursue me** - "from people who try to capture me"
  * **Make your face shine on your servant** - The writer speaks of Yahweh acting favorably towards him as if Yahweh's face shone a light on him. AT: "Act favorably towards your servant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
