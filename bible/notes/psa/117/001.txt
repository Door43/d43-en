===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **For his** - "Because his"
  * **his covenant faithfulness is great toward us** - The abstract noun "faithfulness" can be translated with an adjective. AT: "he does great things for us because he is faithful to his covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the trustworthiness of Yahweh endures forever** - The abstract noun "trustworthiness" can be translated with an adjective or a verbal phrase. AT: "Yahweh remains trustworthy forever" or "Yahweh will forever be worthy of people trusting him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
