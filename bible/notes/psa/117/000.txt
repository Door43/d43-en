====== Psalm 117 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 117 is a psalm of praise.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Praise ==
Everyone should praise God for his love and unending faithfulness. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:love]] and [[:en:obe:kt:faithful]])
