===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who is the man who fears Yahweh?**  - This question introduces "the man who fears Yahweh" as a new topic.  AT: "I will tell you about the man who fears Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the man who fears Yahweh** - "any person who fears Yahweh"
  * **The Lord will instruct him in the way that he should choose**- "The Lord will teach him the way he should live"
