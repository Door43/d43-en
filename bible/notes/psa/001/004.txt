===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The wicked are not so** - "The wicked are not like the righteous" 
  * **instead like the chaff** - How they are like chaff can be stated clearly. AT: "worthless like the chaff" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **will not stand in the judgment** - Here "stand" represents "survive" or "continue to live." AT: "will not survive when God judges them" or "will not resist God's condemnation of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] 
  * **nor sinners in the assembly** - Translators can supply the verb "stand." AT: "neither will sinners stand in the assembly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **nor sinners in the assembly of the righteous** - Being accepted by God as righteous is spoken of as standing with the group of righteous people.  AT: "and God will not accepts sinners along with the righteous people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
