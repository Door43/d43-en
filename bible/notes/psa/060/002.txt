===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tremble** - "shake"
  * **you have torn it apart; heal its breaches** - AT: "you have caused the people to not trust each other; help us to be unified again"
  * **made us drink the wine of staggering** - Wine is used to refer to punishment.  A person staggers because they cannot balance. If a person cannot balance during battle they can be easily killed. AT: "punished us by making us easy to kill in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
