===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You have given a banner to those who honor you** - Calling his people to gather together because of the enemy army is spoken of as if God had given them a banner in order to call them. AT: "You have raised a battle flag for those who honor you" or "you have called those who honor you to gather" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **banner** - "battle flag." This is a cloth placed at the top of a stick used by kings or military to identify who they are in battle or elsewhere. 
  * **with your right hand** - God's right hand represents his power. AT: "by your power"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **answer me** - Answering here represents responding to his request. AT: "respond to my request" or "answer my prayer (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
