===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you have set up a banner** - God's guiding his people in battle is spoken of as if God were a human king or commander who had set up a banner for the army. AT: "You are like a king who sets up a banner" or "You command us in battle like a king who raises up a banner" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **banner** - "battle flag." This is a flag that a king or commander would raise up on a pole to show that the army should gather.

  * **to be displayed against those who carry the bow** - The phrase 
"those who carry the bow" refers to the enemy soldiers in battle.   AT: "to display when you take your army into battle against your enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **with your right hand** - God's right hand represents his power. AT: "by your power"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **answer me** - Answering here represents responding to his request. AT: "respond to my request" or "answer my prayer (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
