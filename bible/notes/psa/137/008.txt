===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **May the person be blessed, whoever pays you back**  - AT: "may God bless the one who pays you back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe|Apostrophe]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **pays you back for what you have done to us**  - "does to you what you did to us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe|Apostrophe]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **May the person be blessed**  - AT: "God will certainly bless him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **dashes your little ones against the rock**  - "smashes your babies' heads against rocks"
