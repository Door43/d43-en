===== translationNotes: =====

  * **If I ignore the memory of you, Jerusalem** - The writer speaks as if Jerusalem is listening to him. AT: "If I act as if I did not remember you, Jerusalem" or "If I try to forget you, Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **right hand** - the hand most people use most often
