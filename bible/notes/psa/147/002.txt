===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds** - The writer speaks of the people's sorrow and discouragement as if they were physical wounds, and of Yahweh encouraging them as if he were healing those wounds. AT: "He encourages those who are sad and helps them to heal from their emotional wounds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
