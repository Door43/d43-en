===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A michtam of David** - The meaning of the word "michtam" is uncertain. You may use the word "psalm" instead. This can be written as: "This is a psalm that David wrote."
  * **take refuge** - AT: "seek protection" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **As for the saints** - AT: "As for the believers"
