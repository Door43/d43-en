===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David continues to speak to God.

  * **Therefore my heart is glad; my glory is rejoicing** - The speaker is saying that it brings him honor to be able to praise God. Both of these clauses express very similar meanings. AT: "Therefore I am glad; I am honored to praise him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **my heart** - Here the "heart" represents the speaker's thoughts and emotions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my glory** - Many versions, including the UDB, interpret "my glory" to mean the heart of the writer, who is honored to rejoice because of God.
