===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **a new song** - a song no one has ever sung before. See how this is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:096:001|96:1]].
  * **his right hand and his holy arm have** - The words "right hand" and "holy arm" refer to Yahweh's strength. Together they emphasize how great his strength is. AT: "his very great power has" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **right hand** - the more powerful and skilled hand
  * **holy arm** - Here "arm" is a metonym for power. AT: "the power that is his alone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **given him victory** - Possible meanings are 1) "enabled him to defeat his enemies" or 2) "enabled him to save his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]) 
  * **made known his salvation** - The abstract noun "salvation" can be translated using the verb "save." AT: "showed people that he saves his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **showed his justice to all the nations** - The abstract noun "justice" can be translated using the adjective "just." The word "nations" is a metonym for "the people who live in all the nations." AT: "showed the people who live in all the nations that he is just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
