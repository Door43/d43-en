====== Psalms 098 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 98 is one of six worship psalms (Psalms 95-100)


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Everyone and every thing should sing praises to Yahweh because he is worth of all praise.
