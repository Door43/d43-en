===== translationNotes: =====

  * **His glory** - "The king's honor" or "The king's fame"
  * **you have bestowed on him** - "you have placed on him" or "you have put on him"
  * **you grant him** - "you allow him to have" or "you agree to give him"
  * **lasting blessing** - "a blessing that will last" or "a blessing that will stay"
  * **the joy of your presence** - AT: "the joy of being in your presence" or "the joy that comes from you being close"
