===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you bring him rich blessings** - The abstract noun "blessings" can be stated as a verb. AT: "you bless him greatly" or "you give him many good things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **you placed on his head a crown of purest gold** - To place a crown on a person's head is a symbol of making him a king. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **a crown of purest gold** - Here "purest gold" represents the great honor given to the king.
  * **He asked you for life; you gave it to him** - The abstract noun "life" can be stated as a verb. AT: "He asked that you cause him to live for a long time; you caused it to happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **long days forever and ever** - Possible meanings are 1) a very long life or 2) eternal life or 3) a long line of descendants.
