===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they intended** - "they planned." The word "they" refers to the enemies of God and the king.
  * **evil against you** - "to do evil things to you"
  * **they conceived a plot** - "they made a plan" or "they created a scheme"
  * **For you will turn them back; you will draw your bow before them** - Possible meanings for "you" and "your" are 1) they refer to the king or 2) they refer to God and speak about him as if he were a warrior with a bow and arrows. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will turn them back** - This is a way of saying that God and the king will defeat their enemies in battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you will draw your bow before them** - This implies that he actually shoots his arrows at his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
