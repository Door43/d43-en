===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May his name endure forever** - This saying is used to honor God by expressing the desire for people to always remember him. AT: "May people always know about him" or "May people never forget who he is" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **his name** - Here "his name" refers to God's reputation. AT: "the king's name" or "the king's reputation" or "the king's fame" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **as long as the sun** - The verb may be supplied from the previous phrase. AT: "as long as the sun endures" or "as long as the sun shines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **may people be blessed in him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "may God cause him to do good things for people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **call him blessed** - "recognize that God has blessed them"
