===== translationNotes: =====

  * **call to mind** - "remember." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:020:003|20:3]]
  * **your accomplishments** - The abstract noun "accomplishments" can be translated using the verb "accomplish." AT: "all that you have accomplished" or "all the great things you have done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **spread my hands out to you in prayer** - "pray to you with my hands lifted up at my sides"
  * **my soul thirsts for you in a parched land** - The psalmist speaks of wanting to be with God as if he were in a parched land and he also was about to die of thirst. AT: "I want to be with you the way a person in a parched land who is very thirsty wants water."
  * **my soul thirsts for you** - The soul is a metonym for the person. AT: "I am thirsty for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **parched land** - "a land where everything has died because there is no water"
  * **Selah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:003:001|3:2]].
