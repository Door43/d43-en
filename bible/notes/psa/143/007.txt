===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Answer me quickly**  - Urgency because of a circumstance of discouragement
  * **Do not hide your face from me - ** Desperation gives power to confidence
  * **Let me hear your covenant faithfulness**  - Just as David in another psalm pleaded "Restore unto me the joy of your salvation" he expresses confidence in God's mercy
  * **Show me the way where I should walk**  - looks for direction. Expresses surrender to God's perfect will.
  * **I lift up my soul**  - expresses his portion - there is active participation on David's part
