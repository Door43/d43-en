===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Rescue me from my enemies **- David knows that his salvation is in God
  * **I flee to you to hide**  - AT: "I run to you to hide".  David knows that God is his hiding place, as expressed in another psalm (You are my hiding place)
  * **land of uprightness**  - metaphor - indicating a right living before God
