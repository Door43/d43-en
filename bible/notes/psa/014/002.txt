===== translationNotes: =====

  * **sons of mankind** - This phrase refers to all humans.
  * **who seek after him** - This describes those who desire to know God as if they were actually following him on a path. AT: "who desire to know him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **has turned aside** - This describes a person who has rejected God as if he had stopped walking on the right path and went in another direction. AT: "has turned away from Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **filthy** - Here "filthy" refers to sinfulness, not physical filth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
