===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do they not know anything ... who do not call on Yahweh?** - This question is asked to add emphasis. AT: "They act as if they do not know anything ... who do not call on Yahweh. But they know what they are doing!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **those who commit iniquity** - Here "iniquity" refers to doing evil deeds. AT: "those who behave in evil ways" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]) 
  * **those who eat up my people** - This refers to those who do evil things and destroy God's people as if they were eating food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
