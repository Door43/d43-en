===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **to sing praises to your name** - The words "your name" are a metonym for "you." AT: "to sing praises to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **proclaim your covenant faithfulness** - The abstract noun "faithfulness" can be translated with the adjective "faithful." AT: "tell people that you are faithful to keep your covenant"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **your truthfulness** - The abstract noun "truthfulness" can be translated with the adjective "true." AT: "that everything you say is true"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
