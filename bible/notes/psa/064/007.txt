===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer continues to speak of the "evildoers" of [[:en:bible:notes:psa:064:001|64:01]].

  * **But God will shoot them ... with his arrows** - The writer speaks of God's punishment of the evildoers as if God were shooting arrows at them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **They will be made to stumble** - The writer speaks of God's causing the evildoers' plans to fail as if God were making them stumble in their paths. This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will make them stumble" or "God will cause their plans to fail" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **since their own tongues are against them** - Here "tongues" represents the words that they say. AT: "since the words that they say are against them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **what he has done** - "what God has done"
