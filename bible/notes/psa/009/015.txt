===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The nations have sunk down into the pit that they made; their feet are caught in the net that they hid** - The words "pit" and "net" represent the evil plans that wicked people made. AT: "The nations have failed and their own evil plans have destroyed them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ensnared** - "trapped" or "caught"
