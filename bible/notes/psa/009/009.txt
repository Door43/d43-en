===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Yahweh also will be a stronghold for the oppressed** - God is spoken of as if he were a place that people could go to for safety. AT: "Yahweh will also protect the oppressed" or "Yahweh will also provide safety for those who are oppressed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **stronghold** - "refuge" or "shelter"
  * **Those who know your name** - Here the words "your name" represent God. AT: "Those who know you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **do not abandon** - "do not forsake" or "do not leave"
