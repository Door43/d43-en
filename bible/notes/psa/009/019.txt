===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **man** - AT: "wicked people"
  * **may the nations be judged in your sight** - AT: "judge the nations in your presence." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Terrify them, Yahweh** - AT: "Put fear in them, Yahweh,"
  * **may the nations know** - AT: "and cause the nations to realize"
