===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Arise** - Getting up represents starting to do something. AT: "Do something" or "Take action" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **man** - "people"
  * **be judged** - Here judging represents punishing. AT: "be punished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in your sight** - Here sight represents presence. AT: "in your presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **may the nations be judged in your sight** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "judge the nations in your presence" or "take the nations into your presence and punish them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
