===== translationNotes: =====

  * **turn back** - "retreat" or "flee in fear"
  * **you sit on your throne, a righteous judge** - Kings had authority to judge people, and they would sit on their throne when they judged. David speaks as if God were an earthly king.  AT: "you judge like a king who sits on his throne, and you are righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
