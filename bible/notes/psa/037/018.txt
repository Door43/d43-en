===== translationNotes: =====

  * **watches over the blameless** - To "watch over" means to protect someone. Here "the blameless" refers to the blameless people. AT: "protects the blameless people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **day by day** - "every day" or "continually" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **when times are bad** - This phrase refers to disasters, such as famine. AT: "when calamities occur" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
