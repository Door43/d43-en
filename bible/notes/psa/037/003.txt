===== translationNotes: =====

  * **graze in faithfulness** - Faithfulness is spoken of as if it was an animal that would be strengthened by feeding in good pasture. AT: "nourish faithfulness" or "increase your faithfulness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the desires of your heart** - Here "heart" represents the person's inner being and thoughts. AT: "your deepest, inner desires" or "the things that you desire the most" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
