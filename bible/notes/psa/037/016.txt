===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Better is the little that the righteous has**  - AT: "It is better to be righteous, even if it means that you are poor..."
    * **than the abundance of many wicked people.**  - AT: "...than to be wicked with great wealth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **For the arms of the wicked people will be broken**  - AT: "For the power of the wicked people will be defeated." (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
