===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - "This is a psalm that David wrote."
  * **Do not be irritated because of evildoers**  - AT: "Do not let wicked people upset you."
  * **dry up as the grass**  - In the hot and dry climate of the Bible, plants die quickly. AT: "come to an end". (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and wither as the green plants**  - In the hot and dry climate of the Bible, plants die quickly. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
