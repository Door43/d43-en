===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Give your ways to Yahweh** - "Ask Yahweh to guide your actions in life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **like the daylight...like the day at noon** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **like the daylight** - "in full view of all." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like the day at noon** - "visible during the brightest time of day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
