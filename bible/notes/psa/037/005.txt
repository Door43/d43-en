===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Roll your ways onto Yahweh**  - AT: "Let your ways come from your belief that Yahweh guides you"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **like the daylight ... like the day at noon.**  - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **like the daylight**  - AT: "in full view of all." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **like the day at noon** - AT: "visible during the brightest time of day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
