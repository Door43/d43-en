===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh's enemies will be like the glory of the pastures**  - The writer compares Yahweh's enemies and the flowers blooming in the fields. (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and disappear in the smoke**  - The writer compares the fields that are burned off after the harvest with the wicked. AT:"his enemies will be defeated and cease to exist." (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the righteous person is generous and gives** - The phrase "is generous" describes how the righteous person gives. AT: "the righteous person gives generously" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
