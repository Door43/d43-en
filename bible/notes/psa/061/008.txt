===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will sing praise to your name forever** - Here "name" stands for God himself. AT: "I will always sing praise to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **my vows** - These refer to the promise to offer sacrifices to God every day.
