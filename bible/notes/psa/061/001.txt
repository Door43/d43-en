===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

This psalm is a song about God being faithful. 

  * **For the chief musician;** - "This is for the director of music to use in worship;"
  * **on a stringed instrument** - "People should play stringed instruments with this song."
  * **A Psalm of David**  - "This is a psalm that David wrote."
  * **overwhelmed** - "overcome"
  * **a strong tower from the enemy** - AT: "you have been like a strong tower safe from my enemy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
