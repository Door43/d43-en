===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **General Information:** - This is a song about God's faithfulness. 

  * **For the chief musician** - "This is for the director of music to use in worship"
  * **on a stringed instrument** - "people should play a stringed instrument with this song"
  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **Hear my cry, God; attend to my prayer** - These clauses have a similar meaning. AT: "God, listen to me and answer my prayer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) 
  * **overwhelmed** - "overcome"
  * **lead me to the rock that is higher than I** - Here the writer speaks of God as if he were a high rock that he could climb for protection. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **a strong tower from the enemy** - The implied words "that keeps me safe" can be added to the translation. AT: "you have been like a strong tower that keeps me safe from my enemy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **a strong tower** - Here the writer speaks of God as if he were a "strong tower" that provides protection from his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
