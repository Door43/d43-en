===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May you increase ... turn again and comfort** - "I want you to increase ... I want you to turn again and comfort." Some translations read, "You will increase ... you will turn again and comfort."
  * **turn again and comfort me** - This describes one action through two phrases. AT: "comfort me again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
  * **for your trustworthiness** - "because I can trust you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **to you I will sing praises with the harp, Holy One of Israel** - "to you, who is the Holy One of Israel, I will sing praises while I play the harp"
