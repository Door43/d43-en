===== translationNotes: =====

  * **watch for my life** - It is implied that they are watching for an opportunity to take his life. The euphemism "to take a life" means to kill. AT: "watch for an opportunity to take my life" or "wait for an opportunity to kill me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **They say** - This can be translated to make explicit that they are talking about him. AT: "They say about me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **pursue and take him** - "follow him when he runs away, and kill him"
