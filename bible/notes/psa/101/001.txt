  * **I will sing of covenant faithfulness and justice** - The abstract nouns "faithfulness" and "justice" can be translated with adjectives. AT: "I will sing that you are faithful to your covenant and that you are just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
