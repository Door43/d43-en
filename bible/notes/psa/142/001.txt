===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **A maschil** - This may refer to a style of music.  See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:032:001|32:1]].
  * **cave** - open place under the earth large enough for people to walk around in
  * **with my voice** - "using my voice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **tell him my troubles** - AT: "pray about my troubles" or "ask him for help with my troubles"
