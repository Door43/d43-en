===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The Mighty One, God, Yahweh** - The author refers to God in three different ways to add emphasis to his importance. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **called the earth** - The people of the earth are referred to by the planet they live on. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **from the rising of the sun to its setting** - The directions "east" and "west" are referred to by the fact that the sun rises in the east and goes down in the west. The reference to both the east and west uses the sides of the earth to refer to the entire earth and all the people in it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
    * **God has shone** - God is referred to as a light that has shone in Zion.  Light is used to refer to knowledge about God. AT: "God has taught about himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
