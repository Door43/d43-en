====== Psalms 079 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 79 is a deliverance psalm and a prayer for revenge against their enemies.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Jerusalem has  been destroyed and its people killed. God should destroy these enemy nations that do not pray to him.
