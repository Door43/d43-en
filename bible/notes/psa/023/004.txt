===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Even though I walk through a valley of darkest shadow **  - AT: "Even though I walk through a dark valley, as dark as death"
  * **I will not fear harm **  - AT: "I will not fear evil"
  * **your rod and your staff comfort me**
 - The shepherd used the rod to protect the sheep from danger and the staff to guide them along the path. (See: Metonymy and Parallelism)
