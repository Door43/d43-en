====== Psalms 066 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 66 is a psalm of praise.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Just like God brought Israel through the wilderness, he took the author through hard times but saved him. So the author will worship God by sacrificing animals to him.
