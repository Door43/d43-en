===== translationNotes: =====

  * **does acts of justice for** - "causes justice to happen to"
  * **all who are oppressed** - Who is doing the oppressing can be stated explicitly. AT: "​all ​who are oppressed by men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **his deeds to the descendants** - The missing words may be added. AT: "he made known his deeds to the descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **has great covenant loyalty** - "is full of mercy and love"
