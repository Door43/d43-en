====== Psalm 103 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 103 is the first in a series of five psalms praising to God. (Psalm 103-107)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's grace ==
God is good to us by forgiving sin and not punishing us as much as we deserve. His loving kindness goes on from generation to generation. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:grace]], [[:en:obe:kt:forgive]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
