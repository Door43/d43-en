===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **strength **- power
  * **who have great strength and obey his word,**  - AT: "He has given you great strength. You obey His word,"
  * **being obedient to his commands.**  - AT: "and His commands."
  * **Bless Yahweh, all you his armies, you servants of his who carry out his will.**  - AT: "Praise Yahweh, all you servants who carry out His will."
  * **Bless Yahweh, all his creatures, in all places of his kingdom;**  - AT: "Praise Yahweh all His creation."
