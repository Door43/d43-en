===== translationNotes: =====

  * **carry out his will** - This is an idiom meaning to "do his will." AT: "do his will" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in all the places where he reigns** - "praise him in all the places where he reigns"
  * **with all my life** - This phrase means that he will praise Yahweh wholeheartedly and is used to emphasize his devotion to him. AT: "with all of me" or "with all my soul" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
