===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh is from everlasting to everlasting on those who honor him.**  - AT: "But Yahweh keeps His promises forever to those who honor Him."
  * **They keep his covenant and remember to obey his instructions.**  - AT: "Those who honor God keeps His commands and obey His instructions." (See: Parallelism) (See: Doublet) (See: Ambiguity)
  * **Yahweh has established his throne in the heavens,**  - AT: "Yahweh's kingdom is in the heavens," (See: Metonymy)
  * **and his kingdom rules over everyone.**  - AT: "and He rules over everyone."
