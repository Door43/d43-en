===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We have sinned like our ancestors;**  - The author is saying they have done wrong just as their ancestors did. (See: Simile)
  * **we have done wrong, and we have done evil.**  - Repetition is made for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **did not appreciate your mar****velous deeds**  - AT: "took for granted the wonderful things you did"
  * **covenant faithfulness - ** God carrying out what he had promised in the covenant
