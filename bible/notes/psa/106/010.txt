===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He saved them from the hand of those who hated them**  - AT: "Yahweh saved his people from the authority of those who hated them" The author means the authority of their enemies. (See: Metonymy)
  * **and he rescued them from the power of the enemy.**  thought repeated for emphasis (See: Doublet)
  * **But the**  - The word 'but' is not necessary. AT: "The"
  * **they believed his**  - AT: "the Israelites believed God's"
