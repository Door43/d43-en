===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Meribah** - A place in the desert where Moses disobeyed God by hitting a rock trying to get water from it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **spoke rashly** - AT: "spoke without thinking" or "spoke quickly"
  * **but they mingled with the nations **-AT: "but they mixed in marriage with the other nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **learned their ways**  - AT: "they followed the gods of the nations"
