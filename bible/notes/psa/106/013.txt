===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they did not wait for his instructions** - It is implied that they did thing without waiting to find out what Yahweh wanted them to go. AT: "they did things without first waiting for Yahweh's instructions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **insatiable cravings** - "cravings that could not be satisfied" 
  * **they challenged God** - AT: "They rebelled against God"
  * **consumed** - "overtook" or "took over"
