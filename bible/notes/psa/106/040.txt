===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **despised**  - hated
  * **and he despised his own people.**  - (See: Doublet)
  * **He handed them over to the nations,**- God allowed them to be captured by the nations. (See: Metonomy)
  * **and those who hated them ruled over them.**  - AT: "and their enemies ruled over them." (See: Ambiguity)
