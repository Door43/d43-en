    * **the land of Ham** - AT: "the land where Ham's descendant live"
    * **mighty acts** - "awesome things"
    * **intervened with him in the breach to turn away his anger from destroying them **  - AT: "stood between Yahweh and the Israelites and begged Yahweh to not destroy them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
