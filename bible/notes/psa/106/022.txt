===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the land of Ham** - This refers to the land where Ham's descendants lived. AT: "the land where Ham's descendant live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_possession]])
  * **mighty acts** - "awesome things"
  * **intervened with him in the breach ... from destroying them** - Here David speaks about Moses persuading Yahweh not to destroy the Israelites as intervening with him in the breach. AT: "stood between Yahweh and the Israelites and begged Yahweh to not destroy them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
