===== translationNotes: =====

  * **torn by their teeth** - The enemies are spoken of as if they were wild animals that would destroy the Israelites by eating them. AT: "destroyed as if we were eaten by wild animals" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **escaped like a bird out of the snare of the fowlers** - The writer describes his escape from the enemies as if he was a bird that had escaped from the snare of the hunters. AT: "escaped from our enemies as a bird escapes from the trap that hunters have set" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **snare** - a small trap of string or wire made for catching small animals or birds
  * **the snare has been broken** - The plot of the enemies to catch the writer failed as if it was a snare that had broken. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
