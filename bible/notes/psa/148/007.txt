===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all ocean depths** - This phrase represents every creature that lives in the depths of the oceans. AT: "all creatures in the ocean depths" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **fire and hail, snow and clouds, stormy wind** - The writer speaks to these natural phenomena as if they are people and commands them to praise Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **stormy wind fulfilling his word** - The phrase "his word" represents what Yahweh has commanded. AT: "stormy wind that does what Yahweh commands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
