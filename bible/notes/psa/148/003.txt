===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **praise him, sun and moon** - Creation praises God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification|Personification]])
  * **praise him, all you shining stars** - Creation praises God.
  * **highest heaven ** - This is possibly a reference to the remotest parts of the universe.
  * **you waters that are above the sky** - This is likely a reference to the atmosphere, the place where rain comes from.
