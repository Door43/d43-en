===== translationNotes: =====

  * **His words are** - "What he says is" 
  * **do good** - "do things that are good"
  * **he sets out on an evil way** - The sinful actions of the man are spoken of as if he was walking along a path that was evil. AT: "he begins to do evil things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he does not reject evil** - Here "evil" can be stated in another form. AT: "he does not reject evil behavior" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
