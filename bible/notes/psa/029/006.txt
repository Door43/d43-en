===== Translation Notes: =====

The psalm writer continues to picture Yahweh's great power.

  * **skip like a calf and Sirion like a young ox**  - The word "skip" means to "jump lightly back and forth"
  * **Sirion**  - "Sirion" is a snow covered top of a mountain in Lebanon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
