===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **my deceitful enemies rejoice over me** - AT: "my enemies, who are deceitful, rejoice over me"
  * **their wicked schemes** - AT: "their evil plans"
  * **they do not speak peace** - AT: "they do not try to make peace"
  * **devise deceitful words** - AT: "plan to tell lies"
  * **those in our land who live in peace** - AT: "those who live peacefully in our land"
