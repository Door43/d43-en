===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **when I stumbled**  - AT: "when I had trouble", or "when I fell into trouble"
    * **and I did not know**  - AT: "behind my back"
    * **They tore at me without stopping**  - This may mean they ridiculed and scoffed at him without stopping.
    * **they gnashed at me with their teeth**  - (See metaphor)
