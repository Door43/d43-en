===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **who seek my life**  - AT: "who are trying to kill me" (UDB) (See:Idiom)
    * **confounded**  - This means to surprise and confuse.
    * **as chaff before the wind**  - AT: "weightless when facing God's strong judgments" 
    * **their way be dark and slippery**  - AT: "may their path be hidden with no sure footing"
