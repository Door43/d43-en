===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in his salvation** - AT: "in the salvation that he brings" or "because you save me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
    * **With all my strength** - AT: "With all my efford"
    * **Yahweh, who is like you ... those who try to rob them** - The implicit answer to this question is that no one is like Yahweh. AT: "Yahweh, there is no one like you ... those who try to rob them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **the poor and needy** - these two words basically mean the same thing and are repeated to empahsize that Yahweh saves many who needs his help. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
