===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **my vindication** - See how you translated this term in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:017:001|17:1]].
    * **may they say continually** - "may they always say"
    * **Yahweh be praised** - AT: "let us praise Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **who delights in** - AT: "who is happy whith" or "who enjoys"
    * **welfare** - well-being, happiness
