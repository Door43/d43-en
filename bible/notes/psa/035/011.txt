===== translationNotes: =====


  * **rise up** - This means they testify in a trial. AT: "volunteer to give a testimony" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They repay me evil for good** - This is a metaphor which means they have received good, and yet they give back evil in exchange for it. AT: "In return for my doing good things for them, they do evil things to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **evil** - This abstract noun can be stated other forms. AT: "evil things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **good** - This abstract noun can be stated other forms. AT: "good things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **I am sorrowful** - "I am extremely sad"
