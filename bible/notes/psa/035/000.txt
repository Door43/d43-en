====== Psalms 035 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 35 is a prayer for deliverance. His enemies are coming against him. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

When his enemies were sick, he tried to comfort them but when he was in trouble his enemies uses this as a time to attack him.
