===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness have met together;**  - AT: "God will show us both his faithfulness and his trustworthiness." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.**  - AT: God has blessed us with both righteousness and peace." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Trustworthiness will sprout up from the ground,**  - AT: "Trustworthiness will sprout forth like a plant," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **victory will look down from the sky -**  The writer describes victory as person looking down as God does. AT: "God will look upon us with favor and give us victory." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
