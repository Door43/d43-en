===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh, that my people ... walk in my paths** - The writer makes the one idea in two lines for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Oh, that my people would listen to me;** - AT: "Oh, how I long for my people to listen to me;"
  * **oh, that my people would walk in my paths.** - AT: "Oh, that they would obey my laws."
  * **Then I would quickly ... against their oppressors** - The writer makes one idea in two lines for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **turn my hand against** - "Hand" represents Yahweh's power. "I would overcome" or "defeat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
