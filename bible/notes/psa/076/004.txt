===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You shine brightly and reveal your glory**  - These two expressions emphasize God's majesty. (See: Doublet)
  * **descend from the mountains****, where you killed your victims**  - God returns from the place of battle where he destroyed his enemies.
  * **plundered**  - AT: "striped of everything they had"
  * **fell asleep**  - AT: "died" (See: Euphemism)
