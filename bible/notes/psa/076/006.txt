===== translationNotes: =====

  * **At your rebuke** - The abstract noun "rebuke" refers to saying something in an angry or critical way. AT: "When you rebuked them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **fell asleep** - This phrase is a polite way to say that they died. AT: "died" or "fell down dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **who can stand in your sight when you are angry?** - This can be translated as a statement. AT: "No one can stand in your sight when you are angry." or "You can destroy anyone at whom you are angry." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
