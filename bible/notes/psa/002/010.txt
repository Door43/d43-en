===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So now, you kings, be warned; be corrected, you rulers of the earth** - These two phrases have similar meanings. AT: "So now, you kings and rulers of the earth, be warned and corrected" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
  * **be warned** - This can be stated with an active form. AT: "listen to this warning" or "be wise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **be corrected** - This can be stated with an active form. AT: "listen to this correction" or "take this correction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
