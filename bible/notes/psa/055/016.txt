===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As for me** - This phrase shows that the writer has stopped talking about one thing and is now about to talk about himself. AT: "But I" 
  * **moan** - "cry out"
  * **he will hear my voice** - Here "voice" represents either 1) the psalmist or 2) the psalmist's complaints and moans. AT: "he will hear me" or "he will hear my moaning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my life** - Here "my life" represents the psalmist. AT: "me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for those who fought against me were many** - "for many people fought against me"
