===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I mention** - "I tell about"
  * **to my followers.** - "to those who worship God."
  * **Rahab** - This is a name that is sometimes used to refer to Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **This one was born there.** - "This one" refers to the nations the writer mentions. Although they were not physically born in Zion, those who follow God are natives of Jerusalem spiritually.
