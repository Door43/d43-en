====== Psalm 082 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 82 is a psalm of judgment against the the leaders of the other nations. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:judge]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Favoritism ==
The leaders of the nations need to protect the poor and needy and not to favor the evil rich people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:favor]] and [[:en:obe:kt:evil]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metonymy ==

The leaders of the nations are represented by their gods. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:obe:kt:falsegod]])
