===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **maintain the rights of the** - "do what is right for the "
  * **destitute** - "very poor people"
  * **take them out of the hand of the wicked** - Here the word "hand" refers to power or control. AT: "stop the wicked from harming them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
