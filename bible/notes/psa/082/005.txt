===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They neither** - Possible meanings are 1) "they" refers to the gods or 2) "they" refers to the wicked people.
  * **they wander around in the darkness** - Doing what is evil is spoken of as if they were walking in a very dark place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all the foundations of the earth crumble** - The gods corrupting the moral order that Yahweh established is spoken of as if the gods were shaking the earth and making it fall apart. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **crumble** - "fall apart"
