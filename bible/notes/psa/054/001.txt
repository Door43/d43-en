===== Translation Notes: =====

  * //**on stringed instruments. **- //  This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “It is to be accompanied by people playing stringed instruments” or “People should play stringed instruments with this song.”
  * **//A maschil of David // **//-//  See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:032:001|32:1-2]]
  * **//Ziphites// **//- //  people from the city of Ziph (Zif) in the Judean mountains, southeast of Hebron. (See: translating names)
  * **//Does David not hide himself with us? //**- AT: "David is hiding himself with us." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **by your name**  - AT: "by your power." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **judge me ****in your might**- "decide if I have done what is right or wrong"
  * **give ear **- AT: "listen." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **strangers**  - "people I don't know" (See: foreigner)
  * **pitiless men **- "men who have no mercy"
