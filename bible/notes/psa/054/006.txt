===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will give thanks to your name, Yahweh, for it is good** - Yahweh's name represents him. AT: "I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, for you are good" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **my eye has looked** - The eye represents the person. AT: "I have looked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **my eye has looked in triumph on my enemies** - Possible meanings are 1) seeing that his enemies have been defeated. AT: "I have seen that my enemies have been defeated" or 2) defeating his enemies. AT: "I have defeated my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
