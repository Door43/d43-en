===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he is robed in majesty; Yahweh is clothed with strength; he wears strength like a belt. **- in these phrases Yahweh's majesty and power are being represented as articles of clothing (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **strength**  - power and might
  * **belt** - a tie that goes around the middle of a person
