===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do good** - This is a request. The abstract noun "good" can be stated as an action. AT: "Please do good things" or "I beg you to do good things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **upright in their hearts** - Here "hearts" refers to their desires. AT: "desire to do the right things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **turn aside** - Refusing to obey Yahweh is spoken of as turning away from a good path. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **their crooked ways** - Here wicked ways are spoken of as if they were a path that is not straight. AT: "their wicked ways" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **lead them away** - People are lead away in order to punish them. AT: "lead them away to punish them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
