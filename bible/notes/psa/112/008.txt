===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The person who sings this song continues to describe a person who reveres Yahweh.

  * **His heart is tranquil** - "His heart is supported." Here the word "heart" refers to the person. Possible meanings are 1) "He is at peace" or 2) "He is confident" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **his righteousness endures forever** - "his righteous deeds will endure forever." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:112:003|112:3]].
  * **he will be exalted with honor** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will exalt him by giving him honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
