===== Translation Notes: =====

(See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

    * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: "This is a psalm of David" or "This is a psalm that David wrote"
    * **The heavens declare** - "The heavens show." The heavens are described as if they were a person. AT: "The heavens look like they declare"
    * **make his handiwork** - The word "handiwork" refers to God's creation.
    * **speech pours out** - The Psalm writer, David, speaks as though the creation is a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **their voice is not heard** - The words "their voice" refers to God's handiwork. Though creation isn't actually talking, it is pictured by creation itself.
