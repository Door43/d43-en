===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the soul ... the heart ... the eyes** - All three of these words refer to the whole person. AT: "a person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the simple** - "those who have no experience" or "those who have not learned"
  * **are right** - "are true" or "are correct"
  * **bringing light** - "bringing understanding"
