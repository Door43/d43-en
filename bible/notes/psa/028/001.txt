===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **I cry out** - "I call out loudly"
  * **my Rock** - A person could climb to the top of a large rock for protection from his enemies. AT: "You are like a rock that keeps me from my enemies" or "my Protector." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **do not ignore me** - "do not be silent to me" or "do not leave me alone"
  * **I will join those who go down to the grave** - People who die are spoken of as if they are going down into the grave. AT: "I will die like those who are in the grave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Hear the sound of my pleading** - Here "sound" refers to the content of his request. AT: "Hear my strong request" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I lift up my hands** - This is a symbol of worship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
