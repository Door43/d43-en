===== translationNotes: =====

  * **priests fell by the sword** - "The enemies killed many Israelite priests using swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **their widows could not weep** - Possible meanings are 1) someone forced the widows not to weep or 2) so many priests died that there was no time for proper funerals.
  * **widows** - women whose husbands have died
  * **the Lord awakened as one from sleep** - "it was as if the Lord awoke from sleeping" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **like a warrior who shouts because of wine** - Possible meanings are 1) like a warrior who had drunk too much wine and has become angry because he was awakened and so wants to fight or 2) like a warrior who drank much wine but is now able to think and fight well because he has slept.
