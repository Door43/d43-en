===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **priests fell by the sword **- "The enemy killed many Israelite priests using swords" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **their widows could not weep**  - Possible meanings are 1) someone forced the widows not to weep (see UDB) or 2) so many priests died that there was no time for proper funerals.
  * **like a warrior who shouts because of wine** - Possible meanings are 1) like a drunken warrior who has become angry because he was awakened and so wants to fight or 2) like a warrior who is now able to think and fight well because the effects of the wine have worn off as he slept.
  * **He drove his adversaries back**  - The original language uses a figure of speech that literally means "He struck his enemies in the back side."
  * **he put them to everlasting shame**  - See: Hyperbole
  * **he put them**  - The pronoun "them" here refers to God's adversaries.
