===== Translation Notes: =====

Verse 3 continues the sentence begun in verse 2.

    * **We will not keep them from their descendants** - AT: We will tell our descendants about them"
  * **the praiseworthy deeds of Yahweh**  - AT: "the deeds of Yahweh that are worthy of praise" or "the things we praise Yahweh for"
