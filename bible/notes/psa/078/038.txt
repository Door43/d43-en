===== translationNotes: =====

  * **forgave their iniquity** - "forgave them even though they had done evil deeds"
  * **held back his anger** - Asaph speaks of God's anger as if it were an angry person whom God held onto to keep him from attacking the one who had made him angry. AT: "did not punish them even though he was angry with them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **did not stir up all his wrath** - Asaph speaks of God's wrath as if it were a sleeping person whom God allowed to sleep and did not awaken. AT: "did not allow himself to become fully angry with them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
