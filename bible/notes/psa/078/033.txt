===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues telling of what God did to the Israelites.

  * **cut short their days**  - "made them die young"
  * **their days; their years**  - These two phrases are intended to both mean "their time of existence" and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **they would start to seek him, and they would return and look earnestly for him**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **look earnestly**  - "seek diligently" or "look urgently"
