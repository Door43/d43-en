===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They spoke**  - the Israelites spoke
  * **Can God really lay out a table for us in the wilderness?** - AT: "We don't believe that God can really lay out a table for us in the wilderness!" or "God, prove to us that you can really lay out a table for us in the wilderness!"
  * **lay out a table** - "provide us with food" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **waters gushed out** - much water came out quickly
  * **But can he give bread also? Will he provide meat for his people?** - "But we will not believe that he can give us bread also or provide meat for his people until we see him do it."
