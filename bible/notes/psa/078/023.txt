===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yet he commanded the skies**  - The pronoun "he" in these verses refers to God.
  * **opened the doors of the sky**  - See: Metaphor
  * **rained down manna for them to eat, and gave them the grain from heaven**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **the bread of angels**  - This refers to the manna. AT: "the grain of angels" or "the same kind of food that angels eat"
