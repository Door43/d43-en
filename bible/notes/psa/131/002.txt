===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I have stilled and quieted my soul** - The soul represents the person or his emotions. Being calm and peaceful is spoken of has having making one's soul still and quiet. AT: "I am calm and peaceful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my soul within me** - The soul represents the person or his emotions. AT: "I" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a weaned child with his mother** - The psalmist speaks of himself being content and resting as if he were a young child that no longer demands breast milk from his mother. AT: "content like a young child that no longer cries for breast milk but rests in his mother's arms" or "content and resting" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
