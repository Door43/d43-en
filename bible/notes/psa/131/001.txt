===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **song of ascents** - Possible meanings are 1) "song people sang while going up to Jerusalem for a celebration" or 2) "song people sang while going up the steps into the temple" or 3) "song whose words are like steps." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]]
  * **my heart is not proud or my eyes haughty** - The heart and the eyes represent the person. AT: "I am not proud or haughty" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I do not have great hopes for myself** - Possible meanings are "I do not expect to do great things" or "I do not think I am great."
  * **things that are beyond me** - Things that are too hard for a person to understand are spoken of as if they are beyond the person or too far away from the person to be able to reach them. AT: "things that are too hard to understand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
