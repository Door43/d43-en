===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the city of our God**- AT: "in Jerusalem where the people worship God"
  * **Beautiful in elevation** - The word "elevation" refers to how high something is. AT: "It is beautiful and elevated high on the mountain"
  * **joy of the whole earth** - AT: "it is loved by the people of the earth"
  * **on the sides of the north** - The author describes Jerusalem from the north side of the city.
  * **God has made himself known in her palaces as a refuge** - AT: "The people of Jerusalem's palaces know that God protects them"
