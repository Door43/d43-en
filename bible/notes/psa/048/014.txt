===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **forever and ever** - Both of these words have the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **he will be our guide**  - "he will lead us"
    * **to death** - AT: "for the rest of our lives" or "until we die"
