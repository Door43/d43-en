===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In this section, the writer speaks directly to the city of Jerusalem, as if the city was a person who could hear them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])

  * **For the sake** - "For the well-being"
  * **May there be peace within you** - "I pray that the people in you will live in peace"
  * **for your good** - The abstract noun "good" can be stated as an action. AT: "for good things to happen to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
