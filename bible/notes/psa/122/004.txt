===== translationNotes: =====

  * **as a law for Israel** - "as Yahweh commanded Israel to do"
  * **the name of Yahweh** - Here "name" refers to Yahweh himself. AT: "Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sit on thrones** - This refers to ruling over the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for judgment for the house of David** - The abstract noun "judgment" can be stated as an action. AT: "in order to rule fairly over the house of David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the house of David** - Here "house" refers to the descendants of David. AT: "the descendants of David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
