===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] 

  * **For the chief musician;** - "This is for the director of music to use in worship;"
  * **set to the Sheminith** - This may refer to a style of music. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:006:001|6:1]].
  * **A psalm of David** - “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **Help, Yahweh** - AT: "Yahweh, come to my aid,"
  * **faithful have vanished** - "faithful people have all vanished."
