===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like silver purified in a furnace on the earth, refined seven times. **- AT: "they are without any imperfection."
  * **You keep them. **- AT: "You keep the righteous people safe."
  * **the godly people**  - AT: "them"
  * **walk on every side **- AT: "surround us;"
  * **when evil is exalted among the sons of men.**  - AT: "evil is exalted everywhere."
