====== Psalm 012 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 12 is a judgment psalm, where the author asks God to judge and punish the wicked people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:judge]] and [[:en:obe:kt:evil]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh saves ==
There are so many wicked people now and they are proud of their evil. But God will save the poor who call out to him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:save]] and [[:en:obe:kt:call]])


== Words ==
This relatively short psalm focuses on the words people use.
