===== translationNotes: =====

  * **walk** - live, exist
  * **in the middle of danger** - Being in danger is spoken of as being in a physical place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will reach out with your hand against the anger of my enemies** - God is spoken of as if he will strike the enemies with his hand. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **anger of my enemies** - Anger is spoken of as if it were an object instead of an emotion. AT: "my enemies, who are angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **your hands have made** - God is spoken of as if he had hands. AT: "you have created" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the ones whom your hands have made** - This expression probably refers to the nation of Israel.
