===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **my whole heart** - AT: "completely"
  * **before the gods** - Possible meanings are 1) "in spite of the false idols that exist" or 2) "before the heavenly assembly," which means "in the knowledge of the angels in heaven." 
  * **I will bow down** - Bowing down is a symbolic action standing for worship and giving honor. AT: "I will worship you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **give thanks to your name** - "give thanks to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **You have exalted your word and your name above all** -  Possible meanings are 1) "You have made your word and your name to be more honored than anything else," 2) "You have made your word to be more honored than even your name," or 3) "you have made your promises to be more honored than even the heavens." 
  * **your word** - This refers to what God has said. AT: "what you have said" or "your commands and promises" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your name** - Possible meanings are 1) "yourself" or 2) "your fame." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
