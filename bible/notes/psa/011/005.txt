===== translationNotes: =====

  * **examines** - "watches carefully"
  * **do violence** - "hurt others"
  * **He rains burning coals and brimstone upon the wicked; a scorching wind will be their portion from his cup!** - God's punishment is described as if it were burning coals and brimstone from a volcano. AT: "He punishes the wicked; there will be no relief for them!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **brimstone** - This is another name for sulphur.
  * **see his face** - "be in his presence forever"
