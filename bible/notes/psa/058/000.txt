====== Psalm 058 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 58 is a deliverance psalm; asking God to deliver from the unjust rulers. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]] and [[:en:obe:kt:unjust]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Deliverance ==
Although the leaders are very evil and unfair, God can deliver his people from their oppression. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:evil]] and [[:en:obe:kt:oppress]])

== Superscription ==
The term "Michtam" is used in the superscription for this psalm. There is much discussion about this term but in the end no one is certain what it means. So it is easiest to transliterate this word into your language or you are welcome to translate it to psalm.
