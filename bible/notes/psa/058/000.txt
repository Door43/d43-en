====== Psalm 058 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 58 is a deliverance psalm. It asks God for deliverance from the unjust rulers. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]] and [[:en:obe:kt:unjust]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Deliverance ==
Although the leaders are very evil and unfair, God can deliver his people from their oppression. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:evil]] and [[:en:obe:kt:oppress]])

== Superscription ==
The word "Michtam" is used in the superscription for this psalm. There is much discussion about this word but, in the end, no one is certain what it means. It is easiest to transliterate this word into your language, or you are welcome to translate it to psalm.
