===== translationNotes: =====


  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **he hears my voice and my pleas for mercy** - Here the word "voice" represents the person who is speaking. The phrase "my pleas for mercy" explains what he is speaking and can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "he hears me when I plead with him for mercy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
