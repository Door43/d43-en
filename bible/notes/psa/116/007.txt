===== Translation Notes: =====

The person who made this song continues to speak.
  * **My soul, you can rest again** - AT: "I can rest in peace again." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **for Yahweh** - "because Yahweh"
  * **you rescued my life from death** - AT: "have saved me from death" or "have rescued me from death."
  * **my eyes from tears** - AT: "You have saved me from grief." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my feet from stumbling** - AT: "You have saved my feet from stumbling" or "You have kept the danger from destroying me."
