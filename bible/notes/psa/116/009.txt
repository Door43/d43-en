===== Translation Notes: =====

The person who made this song continues to speak.

  * **in the land of the living**  - AT: "while I am still alive." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I am greatly afflicted** - "I am greatly suffering"
  * **I rashly said** - "Without thinking, I said"
  * **All men are liars** - "Every person is a liar" or "All people are liars"
