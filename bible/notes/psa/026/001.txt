===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence: “This is a psalm of David" or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **without wavering** - "without slipping" or AT: "changing one's mind"
  * **Examine me** - "Test me" or "Try me" 
  * **my inner parts** - The words "inner parts" are defined within the definition for [[en:obe:other:heart]]: If a body part does not have this meaning, some languages may need to say “my thoughts” or “my emotions” instead of using a figure of speech.
