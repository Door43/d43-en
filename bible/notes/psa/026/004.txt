===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I do not associate with** - "I do not keep company with" or "I do not sit with"
  * **with deceitful people** - "with those who deceive others"
  * **nor do I mingle with dishonest people** - This means the same as the first part of the sentence. AT: "and I do not join with dishonest people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **dishonest people** - "hypocrites" or "those who lie to others"
  * **the assembly of evildoers** - "those who gather to do evil"
  * **the wicked** - This is a nominal adjective. AT: "wicked people" or "those who are wicked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
