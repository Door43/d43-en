===== translationNotes: =====

  * **listen to my cries** - This is a call for help. AT: "listen to me as I call to you now for help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you shield my head in the day of battle** - A person's head is in great danger during battle. Protecting the person's head represents protecting the whole person. AT: "you protect me when I go to war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **battle** - Here "battle" probably stands for any kind of severe trouble (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **do not grant the desires of the wicked** - "please do not allow the wicked to have what they desire"
  * **the wicked** - The adjective "wicked" can be translated as a noun phrase. AT: "wicked people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
