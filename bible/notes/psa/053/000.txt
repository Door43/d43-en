====== Psalm 053 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm is 53 is a wisdom psalm: warning people who do evil that God will judge them. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:wise]], [[:en:obe:kt:evil]] and [[:en:obe:kt:judge]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===
== God's watching mankind ==
God looks down from heaven and sees that all humans are evil and corrupt. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:heaven]])

== Superscription ==
This is called a "Maschil of David." The word in the original language has caused scholars to have various opinions about its meaning. Some say it means to instruct, while others say that the word is referring to the skill used in creating the psalm. Still others think it means that meditation should be used in reading this psalm.
