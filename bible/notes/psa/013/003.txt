===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Look at me and answer me** - "Give me your attention and listen to me"
  * **Enlighten my eyes** - This is a way of asking for strength. AT: "Make me strong again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **or I will sleep in death** - To "sleep in death" means to die. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **Do not let my enemy say ... so that my enemy may not say** - "Do not let my enemy say about me ... so that my enemy may not say about me"
  * **when I am brought down** - "when I fall" or "when they defeat me"
