===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look at me and answer me** - AT: "Give me your attention and listen to me,"
  * **Enlighten my eyes** - This is a way of asking for strength. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **or I will sleep in death** - To "sleep in death" means "to die." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **when I am brought down** - AT: "when I fall" or "when I lose."
  * **me, my, I, him** - David is referring to himself as he speaks to God.
