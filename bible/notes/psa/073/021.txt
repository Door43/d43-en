===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my heart was grieved** - The word "heart" represents the person emphasizing their thoughts and feelings. AT: "I was very sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I was deeply wounded** - The psalmist speaks of emotional pain as if it were physical pain by being pierced with a knife or arrow. AT: "I felt like someone had wounded me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ignorant and lacked insight** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize how little he knew. AT: "very ignorant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **lacked insight** - "understood nothing"
  * **you** - This "you" refers to God.
