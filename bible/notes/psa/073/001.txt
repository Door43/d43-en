===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of Asaph**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of Asaph" or “This is a psalm that Asaph wrote”
  * **my feet**  - This phrase represents the whole person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **my feet almost slipped; my feet almost slipped out from under me**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis.(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
