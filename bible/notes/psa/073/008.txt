===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Asaph continues to describe how he sometimes wants to complain to God about those who are "arrogant" and "wicked" ([[:en:bible:notes:psa:073:001|73:3]]).

  * **They mock** - Whom they mock can be stated clearly. AT: "They mock God and his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **They set their mouth against the heavens** - Here "their mouth" is a metonym for their speech, and "heavens" is a metonym for God, who lives in the heavens. AT: "They speak against God, who is in the heavens" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **their tongues march through the earth** - The word "tongues" represents the people themselves. Possible meanings are 1) "they go through the earth saying bad things about God" or "they go everywhere and boast about themselves." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
