===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **come quickly to me - ** sense of urgency
  * **listen to me when I call**  - the "demand" element in the prayer of faith to connote urgency
  * **like incense, like evening sacrifice**  - simile
  * **lifted hands**  - by reference, prayer (Eph. 2:8), by extension and parallel in this verse, prayer
