===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the destitute** - This refers to destitute people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_predadjectives]]) 
  * **he will not reject their prayer** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "Yahweh will accept their prayers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]]) 
  * **This will be written for future generations** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will write this for future generations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
