  * **groaning**  - a deep sound someone makes when they are grieving or in pain
  * **I am like a pelican of the wilderness** - AT: "I am lonely and despised like a pelican in the wilderness"
  * **a pelican**  - a large fish-eating bird
  * **an owl** - This a a bird that is awake at night. AT: "a night bird"
