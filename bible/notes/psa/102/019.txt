===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For he has looked down from the holy heights; from heaven Yahweh has viewed** - These two phrases have similar meaning and are used together for emphasize how God looks down from heaven. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the holy heights** - "his holy place high above the earth"
  * **those who were condemned to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "those whom the authorities had sentenced to die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
