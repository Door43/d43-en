===== translationNotes: =====

  * **have mercy on Zion** - Here "Zion" refers to the people who live in Zion. AT: "have mercy on the people of Zion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **upon her** - The word "her" refers to Zion.
  * **hold her stones dear** - The "stones" refer to the stones that were part of the city walls before they were destroyed. AT: "still love the stones that were formerly in the city walls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **your name** - Here "your name" refers to Yahweh. AT: "you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will honor your glory** - The people will honor Yahweh because of his glory. Here Yahweh is referred to by his glory. AT: "will honor you because you are glorious" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **will appear in his glory** - "will be seen as glorious" or "people will see his glory"
