===== translationNotes: =====

  * **taken away my strength** - David describes God causing him to become weak as if his strength were a physical item that could be taken away from him. AT: "has caused me to become weak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my days** - The word "days" here refers to his life. AT: "my life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **do not take me away** - David is asking God to not let him die. AT: "do not take me away from the earth" or "do not let me die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you are here throughout all generations** - "you are present throughout all generations"
