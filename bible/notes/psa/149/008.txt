===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **chains** - This is something used to restrict the movement of prisoners.
  * **shackles**  - This is something that limits the movement of a person's hands and feet
  * **This will be an honor** - AT: "This execution of vengeance and punishment will be an honor" or "The bringing about of God's justice will be an honor"
