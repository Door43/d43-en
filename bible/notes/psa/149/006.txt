===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May the praises of God be in their mouths** - The mouth is a metonym for the whole person. AT: "May they always be ready to praise God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a two-edged sword in their hand** - If swords are not known, use the name of a local weapon. The word "sword" is a metonym for being ready to fight in war. AT: "may they always be ready to go to war for him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the nations** - The phrase "the nations" is a metonym for the people who live in the nations. AT: "the people of the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
