===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Teach me your way** - AT: "Teach me to do what you want me to do" or "Teach me the way you want me to go"
  * **a level path** - "a plain path" or "a right path" 
  * **breathe out violence** - "puff out cruelty" AT: "send out cruelty"
  * **have risen up against me** - "have strengthened against me" AT: "have started against me"
