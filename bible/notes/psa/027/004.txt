===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have asked of Yahweh** - "I have asked Yahweh to let me do"
  * **to behold the beauty of Yahweh** - "to see how beautiful Yahweh is" or "to look at how wonderful Yahweh is"
  * **to meditate within his temple** - Possible meanings are 1) "to ask God what he wants me to do" or 2) "to think carefully about God in his temple"
