====== Psalm 027 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 27 is a psalm of praise to God because God keeps him safe from all his enemies. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's help ==
Because of God's help, he has nothing to fear no matter what happens. The psalmist shows his confidence in God as well as petitions him to help.
