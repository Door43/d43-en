===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The meek** - This refers to meek people in general. AT: "Meek people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **you who seek after God** - Seeking God represents either 1) asking God for help or 2) thinking about God and obeying him. AT: "you who ask God for help" or "you who think about God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **let your hearts live** - Here "hearts" refer to the people. Here "live" is an idiom that means to be encouraged. AT: "may you be encouraged" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **Yahweh hears** - Here "hears" means to respond. AT: "Yahweh answers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the needy** - This refers to needy people in general. AT: "needy people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **his prisoners** - "those who have suffered for him"
