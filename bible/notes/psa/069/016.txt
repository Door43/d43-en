===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your covenant faithfulness is good** - The abstract noun "faithfulness" can be translated as an adjective. AT: "you are good and faithful to your covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **your mercies for me are many** - "you are very merciful to me"
  * **turn to me** - The idea of turning towards someone means to pay attention to them or help them. AT: "help me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Do not hide your face from your servant** - To hide one's face means to refuse to hear or help someone. AT: "Please help your servant" or "Please help me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in distress** - "in great trouble"
