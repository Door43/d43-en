===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hide your face**  - AT: "when you do not look upon them" or "when you ignore them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **take away their breath** - AT: "make them stop breathing" or "end their life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **When you send out your Spirit, they are created,** - AT: "Your Holy Spirit gives life and creates them" 
  * **renew** - AT: "make something new" or to "make strong again"
