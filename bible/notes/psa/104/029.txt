===== translationNotes: =====

  * **hide your face** - This means that Yahweh does not look at them or pay attention to them. AT: "when you do not look upon them" or "when you ignore them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **return to dust** - This means that their bodies decay and are and become soil again. AT: "their bodies decay and return to the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When you send out your Spirit** - This refers to his Spirit being sent to give life to creatures. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * ** they are created** - It is Yahweh's spirit that created them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you renew the countryside** - "you cause the land to be full of new life"
