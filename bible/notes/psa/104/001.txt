===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]. This psalm is a song of praise.

  * **with all my life** - "with all of me" or "with all my soul." This phrase means that he will praise Yahweh wholeheartedly and is used to emphasize his devotion to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you are clothed with splendor and majesty** - The words "splendor" and "majesty" have similar meanings and emphasize the greatness of Yahweh's glory. They are described as clothing Yahweh like a garment. AT: "you have splendor and majesty all around you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **You cover yourself with light as with a garment** - Yahweh is described as being covered with light as if the light were a garment around him. AT: "You are covered in light" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **you spread out the heavens like a tent curtain** - Here God is described as spreading out the heavens like someone spreads out a tent when setting it up. AT: "you spread out the heavens like someone sets up a tent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **You lay the beams of your chambers on the clouds** - "You build your bedroom in the heavens"
  * **you make the clouds your chariot** - Here the clouds are described as carrying Yahweh as if they were a chariot. AT: "you make the clouds carry you like a chariot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you walk on the wings of the wind** - Here the blowing of the wind is described as wings on which Yahweh walks. AT: "you walk upon the wind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
