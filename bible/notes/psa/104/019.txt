===== translationNotes: =====

  * **seasons** - This word refers to different weather changes through the year. Some places have rainy season and dry season, while others have spring, summer, fall, and winter.
  * **the sun knows its time** - Here  David describes the sun as if it knows what time of day it is. AT: "he made the sun to set when it is time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  * **You make** - "Yahweh, you make." Here the authors switches from speaking about Yahweh to speaking to him.
