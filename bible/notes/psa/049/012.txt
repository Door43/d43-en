===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But man, having wealth, does not remain alive** - AT: "But even is a man has wealth, it does not keep him alive"
  * **This, their way, is their folly** - "This is the way of the foolish"
  * **yet after them** - AT: "yet after they die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **approve of their sayings** - AT: "agree with their opinions"
