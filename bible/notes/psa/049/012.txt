===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But man, having wealth** - "But man, even if he has wealth"
  * **This, their way, is their folly** - The writer speaks of the fate of fools as if it is the destination at the end of the path on which they walk. AT: "This is the fate of those who practice folly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **yet after them** - "yet after they die"
