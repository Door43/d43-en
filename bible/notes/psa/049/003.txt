===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My mouth will speak wisdom -**  AT: "I will say wise things" or "The words from my mouth will help you to be wise." (See: Synecdoche)
  * **I will incline my ear to a parable; **- The psalmist will "bend his ear" (idiom) to listen and hear what God wants to tell.
  * **iniquity surrounds me at my heels? **  - "iniquity surrounds me" (See: Metonymy) (See: Personification) Iniquity refers to evil.
  * **Why should I fear the days of evil, when iniquity surrounds me at my heels? **  - AT: "I am not afraid of trouble, when iniquity surrounds me?" (See: Rhetorical Question)
