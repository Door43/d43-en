===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** He blessed his soul while he lived—** - The rich man is referred to by referring to his inner self. AT: "he did good things for himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **live for yourself** - AT: "live to benefit yourself"
  * **and they will never see the light again** - "they" refers to all those who have died. AT: "and they will live no more"
