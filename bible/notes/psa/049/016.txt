===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the glory of his house increases** - The word "glory" here refers to wealth or riches. Possible meanings are 1) "when he gains more wealth in his house" or 2) "when his family becomes richer."
  * **he will take nothing away** - "he will take nothing with him to the grave"
  * **his glory will not go down after him** - The phrase "go down" refers to when the man dies. AT: "his glory will not go along with him when he dies" or "he will not keep his reputation when he dies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
