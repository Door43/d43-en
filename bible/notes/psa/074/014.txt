===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **leviathan**  - Leviathan is a sea monster often used to represent a cruel enemy. (See: Metaphor)
  * **you fed him**  - God fed the monster to the beasts of the dessert.
  * **You broke open springs**  - God provided water for his people in the dessert.
