===== Translation Notes: =====

These verses describe an actual event, a physical attack on the temple of Israel in Jerusalem.

  * **Come look**  - Asaph is addressing God, asking him to come look at the destruction.
  * **roared**  - The adversaries were shouting loudly with a great cry of victory.
  * **engravings**  - This refers to carvings on wood, metal, or stone in the temple.
