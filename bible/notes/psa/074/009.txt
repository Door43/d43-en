===== Translation Notes: =====

Asaph is addressing God about the destruction he sees.

  * **How long, God, will the enemy . . .?** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Will the enemy blaspheme your name forever?** - AT: "Will the enemy never stop speaking evil against you?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Why do you hold back your hand, your right hand** - AT: "Why do you withhold your power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **hand â€¦ your right hand** - Here the phrase "hand" represents God's power. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **take your right hand from your garment** - AT: "Stop hiding your power and take action." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
