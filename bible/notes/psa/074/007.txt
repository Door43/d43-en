===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Asaph continues to describe the destruction of the temple.

  * **They ... they ... They ... They** - Here "they" refers to the adversaries mentioned in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:074:003|74:4]].
  * **They said in their hearts** - This is an idiom. AT: "they said to themselves" or "they thought to themselves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
