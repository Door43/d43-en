====== Psalm 134 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 134 is a temple psalm. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Watchmen ==
The watchmen who work at night should praise God. Then he will bless them. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]])
