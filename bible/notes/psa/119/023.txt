===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Though rulers plot and slander me** - "Though rulers make plans to harm me and say bad things about me"
  * **Your covenant decrees are my delight**  - "Your covenant decrees make me very happy."
  * **and they are my counselors** - God's decrees are spoken of as if they were  people.  AT: "and they are like wise counselors to me" or "and they give me wise counsel" (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
