===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ZAYIN** - This is the name of the seventh letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 49-56 begins with this letter.
  * **Call to mind your promise** - "Remember your promise." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:020:003|20:3]]
  * **This is my comfort in my affliction: that your promise has kept me alive** - This clause can be restructured. AT: "The reason for my comfort is that your promise has kept me alive in my affliction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_sentences]])
