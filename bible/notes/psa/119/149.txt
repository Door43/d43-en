===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hear my voice in your covenant faithfulness** - The psalmist speaks of what he says as if it were the voice he uses to speak. AT: "hear what I say because of your covenant faithfulness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they are far from your law** - "they have strayed from your law" or "they do not pay any attention to your laws"
