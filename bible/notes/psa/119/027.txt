===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wondrous**  - "amazing"
  * **My heart drips**  - Possible meanings are 1) "My heart is very weak" or "My heart cries."
  * **with sadness**  - "because I am so sad"
  * **strengthen me**  - "help me stand." This means "make me strong." It may refer to physical strength or spiritual strength.
