===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Salvation is far from the wicked** - AT: "The wicked will certainly not be saved" or "You will certainly not save the wicked"
  * **Great are your merciful actions** - AT: "Your merciful acts are great" or "You have done many merciful things"
  * **as you always do** - Another possible meaning is "because you do what is just."
