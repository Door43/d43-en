===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They have almost made an end to me on earth** - This is a polite way of speaking about someone killing someone else. AT: "They have almost killed me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **By your steadfast love, keep me alive, so that I may obey your commands** - "according to your steadfast love." The phrase "steadfast love" "faithfulness" can be stated as an adverb. AT: "because you faithfully love me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
