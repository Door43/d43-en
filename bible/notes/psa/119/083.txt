===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I have become like a wineskin in the smoke** - A wineskin is ruined when it hangs for a long time in smoke. The writer compares himself to a wineskin in the smoke to emphasize that he feels useless. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **How long must your servant endure this; when will you judge those who persecute me?** - The writer uses question to request God to punish those who persecute him. AT: "Please do not make wait any longer. Punish those who persecute me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **your servant** - The writer refers to himself as "your servant." AT: "I, your servant" or "I" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
