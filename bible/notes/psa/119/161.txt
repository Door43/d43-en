===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **SHIN:**  This is the twenty-first letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 161-168 begins with this letter.
  * **my heart trembles**  - The writer is saying that he has such respect for God's word that he will continue to obey it even if he is persecuted.
  * **great plunder**  - AT: "great treasure"
