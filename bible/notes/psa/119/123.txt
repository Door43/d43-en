===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My eyes grow tired as I wait** - Here "eyes" represents the whole person. AT: "I grow tired because I wait and wait"
  * **for your salvation and for your righteous word** - The abstract noun "salvation" can be stated as a verb. Also, "word" represents what God communicates to people. AT: "for you to save me as you promised to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Show your servant** - The writer speaks about himself as "your servant." AT: "Show me" or "Show me, your servant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **your covenant faithfulness** - The abstract noun "faithfulness" can be stated as an adverb or adjective. AT: "that you faithfully love me" or "that you are faithful to me because of your covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
