===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **NUN:**  This is the fourteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 105-112 begins with this letter.
  * **is a lamp to my feet ****and light for my path **- These two phrases have the same meaning. AT: "shows me where to go" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
