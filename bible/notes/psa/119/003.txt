===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Most of this psalm is addressed to God, and the words "you" and "your" almost always refer to him.

  * **They do no wrong** - They do not disobey Yahweh.
  * **they walk in his ways** - "they walk in Yahweh's ways." Here their behavior is spoken of as "walking," and the way God wants them to behave is spoken of as "his ways." AT: "they behave as Yahweh wants them to" 
  * **keep your instructions** - "obey all the things that you have declared that we should do"
  * **carefully observe them** - This is to carefully know and understand the commandments and to obey those commandments.
