===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hot anger has taken hold of me** - Anger is expressed as if it is a person that can grab another person. AT: "I have become very angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Your statutes have been my songs** - "I have used your statutes as lyrics to my music" or "I have created songs from your statutes"
