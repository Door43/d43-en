===== translationNotes: =====

  * **PE** - This is the name of the seventeenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 129-136 begins with this letter.
  * **The unfolding of your words gives light** - The writer speaks of Yahweh's words as if they are material that is folded, and of the person who explains Yahweh's words as if they are unfolding them. AT: "The explanation of your words gives light" or "When someone explains your words, they give light" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The unfolding of your words gives light** - The writer speaks of Yahweh's words giving wisdom to a person as if his words were shining a light on them. AT: "The explanation of your words gives people wisdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
