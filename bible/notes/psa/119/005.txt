===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I would be firmly established** - The psalmist speaks of being established in God's commandments as if no one could physically make him move. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **observance of your statutes** - "in obeying your statutes"
  * ** I would not be put to shame** - He would not be ashamed when he is in God's presence.
  * **all your commandments** - "all that you have commanded us"
