===== translationNotes: =====

  * **YOD** - This is the name of the tenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 73-80 begins with this letter.
  * **Your hands have made and fashioned me** - God creating a person is spoken of as if God used his hands to shape the person the way someone may shape clay into an object. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Your hands** - Here "hands" represents God's power or action. AT: "You" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **because I find hope in your word** - Here "word" represents what God says. AT: "because I trust what you say" or "I confidently believe what you say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
