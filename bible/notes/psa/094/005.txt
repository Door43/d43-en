===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **They crush your people, Yahweh; they afflict the nation that belongs to you.**  - These phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. AT: "The wicked people oppress your people, Yahweh; they hurt your chosen nation." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **the widow** - "women whose husbands have died"
    * **the alien** - "strangers in the land"
    * **the fatherless** - "children without fathers"
    * **the God of Jacob does not take notice of it**  - AT: "the God of Israel does not see what we do"
