===== translationNotes: =====

  * **disputes** - "disagreements" or "arguments"
  * **have made me the head over nations** - Here "head" represents the ruler. AT: "appointed me to be the ruler over many nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **foreigners were forced to bow** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God forced foreigners to bow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **foreigners came trembling** - Here "trembling" shows that they were very afraid. This can be stated clearly in the translation. AT: "foreigners came shaking, showing that they were very afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
