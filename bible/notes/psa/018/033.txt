===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **makes my feet swift**  - The word "swift" means able to move very fast.
  * **He makes my feet swift like a deer** - God gives me the ability to make quick, certain progress. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and my arms to bend a bow of bronze** - AT: "and my arms able to break a metal bow"
  * **bow** - A "bow" is a weapon used with arrows against enemies or used for killing an animal.
