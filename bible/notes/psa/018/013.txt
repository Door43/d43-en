===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh thundered in the heavens** - "Yahweh's voice sounded similar to thunder." (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **Yahweh ... The Most High** - "Yahweh" and "the Most High" are both referring to God.  
  * **and sent hailstones** - The word "hailstone" refers to chunks of ice similar to small or large rocks falling from the sky. This is a picture of God's great power.
  * **He shot his arrows and scattered his enemies** - God's lighting strikes are being compared to arrows because they both destroy his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and dispersed them** - "and sent them in different directions."
