===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh thundered in the heavens** - Yahweh's voice sounded like thunder. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **the Most High** - "the Most High" refers to Yahweh.
  * **He shot his arrows and scattered his enemies ... lightning bolts dispersed them** - Both of these clauses have similar meanings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **He shot his arrows and scattered his enemies** - Here lighting strikes are being spoken of as if they were arrows (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **dispersed them** - "sent them in different directions"
