===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I smashed them** - "I crushed them" or "I broke them to pieces"
  * **unable to rise** - "unable to stand"
  * **they have fallen under my feet** - This idiom means the Psalmist has defeated his enemies. AT: "I have defeated all of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **put strength on me like a belt** - The Psalmist says that Yahweh has given him strength that surrounds and supports him like a belt. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:018:030|18:32]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]) 
  * **you put under me** - Here the Psalmist speaks of the defeat of his enemies as if he were standing on them. AT: "you defeat for me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **those who rise up against me** - This refers to those who oppose the Psalmist. AT: "those who are my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
