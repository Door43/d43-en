===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am set free** - AT: "God has set me free" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you lifted me above** - AT: "you made me more powerful than" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **who rose against me** - AT: "who attacked me" or "who rebelled against me"
  * **violent men** - "cruel men" or "savage men"
  * **among the nations** - AT: "where the nations will hear it"
  * **to your name** - AT: "in honor of your name" or "to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
