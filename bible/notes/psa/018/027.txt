===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you bring down** - "you humiliate"
  * **proud, uplifted eyes** - This idiom refers to those who are proud. AT: "who are proud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **For you give light to my lamp; Yahweh my God lights up my darkness** - The writer speaks of Yahweh's presence as if he were a light. These clauses have similar meanings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **For by you I can run over a barricade** - "For with your help I can cross any obstacle"
