====== Psalm 083 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 83 is a deliverance psalm. It is a prayer for deliverance from the many nations allied against Israel. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Destruction ==
God should destroy these enemy nations like he destroyed Israel's enemies during the time of the Judges.
