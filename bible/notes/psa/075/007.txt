===== Translation Notes: =====

Asaph speaks about God.

  * **Yahweh holds a cup in his hand**  - God is prepared to judge the wicked (see vs. 7). (See: Metaphor)
  * **a cup . . . of foaming wine**  - New wine foams as it ferments and ages. (See: Metaphor)
  * **mixed with spices**  - Spices can be added to wine to change or add to the flavor. (See: Metaphor)
  * **all . . . will drink**** to the last drop **- God's punishment upon the wicked will be complete. (See: Metaphor)
