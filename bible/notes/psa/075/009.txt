===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He says** - God says
  * **cut off all the horns of** - The horns of an animal are a metaphor for the power of a person. AT: "take away all power from" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the horns of the righteous will be raised up** - The horns of an animal are a metaphor for the power of a person. This can be translated as a statement in active form. AT: "I will raise up the horns of the righteous" or "I will make the righteous powerful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
