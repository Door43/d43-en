===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

    * **For the chief musician**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:004:001|4:1]].
  * **A psalm of David**  - This can be written as a sentence: “This is a psalm of David” or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **to bring to remembrance** - This can also be written as a sentence: "This psalm was written to cause people to remember". Another translation of this title is: "For the memorial offering" 
  * **confounded**  - "surprised and confused"
  * **those who ...** - "the people who ..."
  * **seek after my soul** - AT: "seek after my life" or "are trying to kill me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **be put to shame and confounded**  - AT: "may God put them to shame and confound them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **let them be turned back and brought to dishonor**  - AT: "Turn backward and bring to dishonor to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **​those who say, "Aha, aha"**  - This is an expression of mocking laughter. You may replace "Aha, aha" with whatever laughing sounds like in your language. AT: "those who mock and laugh at me"
