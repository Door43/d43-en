===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **our praise waits**  - This describes how the people are waiting silently and patiently to offer their praises to Yahweh (See: Personification)
    * **vows will be carried out to you**  - "we will bring our vows to you" (See: Explicit/Implicit)
    * **prevail**  - to defeat or be victorious over an enemy.
    * **Iniquities prevail over us**  - This phrase talks about iniquity as if it is a person that defeats or oppresses us. (See: Personification)
