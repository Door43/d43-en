===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In righteousness** - "Because you are righteous"
  * **you who are** - "you are"
  * **of all the ends of the earth ... across the sea** - These two phrases have similar meanings. AT: "of all the people who live throughout the earth and across the sea" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **of all the ends of the earth** - This refers to the people who live all over the earth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
