===== translationNotes: =====

  * **evidence** - proof or something that shows that another thing is true
  * **you make the east and the west rejoice** - The phrase "the east and the west" refers to the people who live all over the earth. AT: "you cause people everywhere to shout joyfully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]]) 
  * **help the earth** - This refers to the soil of the earth. 
  * **you greatly enrich it** - "you make the soil very good so that good things will grow in it"
  * **the river of God is full of water** - This refers to the supply of water in the sky that God sends to water the earth and fill the streams. AT: "you fill the streams with water" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
