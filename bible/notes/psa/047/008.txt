===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The princes of the peoples **- This phrase means "the rulers of the nations".
  * **The princes of the peoples have gathered together __to__  the people of the God of Abraham - **  AT: "The rulers of the nations have gathered together __with__  the people of the God of Abraham."
  * **shields**  - The author uses the word "shields" to represent the rulers of the world. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **shields of the earth**  - AT: "rulers of the world"
