===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **so many locusts** - "there were so many locusts"
    * **The locusts ate up all vegetation in their land; They ate up all the crops of the ground** -  AT: "The insects ate all the plants and all the crops in the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **He killed every firstborn in their land, the first fruits of all their strength** - Here "first fruits" means the same as firstborn. AT: "He killed every firstborn in their land, the strength of each family"
