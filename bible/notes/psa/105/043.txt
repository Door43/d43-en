===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **He led his people out ... his chosen with shouts ...** - These two phrases have basically the same meaning and are used together to empahsize that God's people were joyful when he led them out of Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **the lands of the nations**  - AT: "the lands of the people living in Canaan"
    * **so that they might**  - AT: "Yahweh did this for his people so that they would"
    * **keep his statutes and obey his laws** - These two phrase have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
