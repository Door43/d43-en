===== translationNotes: =====

  * **shackles** - metal restraints used to fasten a prisoner's wrists or ankles together
  * **His feet were bound by shackles; on his neck was put an iron collar** - These statements can be restated as active. AT: "The Egyptians bound his feet in shackles; they put an iron collar around his neck. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the word of Yahweh tested him** - "the message of Yahweh tested him"
