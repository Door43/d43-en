===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Seek Yahweh and his strength** - To "seek Yahweh's strength" means to ask him to strengthen you. AT: "(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Recall** - "Remember" 
  * **his miracles and** - It may be helpful to add the missing words. AT: "remember his miracles and" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **decrees from his mouth** - AT: "decrees that he has spoken" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you descendants of Abraham ... you people of Jacob** - The author is speaking to the Israelites, calling them these names.
  * **Abraham his servant** - "Abraham, Yahweh's servant"
