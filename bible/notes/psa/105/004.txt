===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **recall** - "remember" 
    * **decrees from his mouth** - "that he has spoken"
    * **his miracles and ...** - "Remember his miracles and ..."
    * **you descendants of Abraham ... you people of Jacob ...** - The author is speaking to the Israelites, calling them these names.
