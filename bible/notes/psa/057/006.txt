===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They have prepared** - "my enemies have prepared"
  * **a net for my feet**  - "a net to catch me" or "a trap for me to fall into" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I was distressed** - "my soul is bowed down in distress"
  * **have fallen into the middle of it.**  - AT: "fell into the pit they dug for me."
