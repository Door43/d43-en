====== Psalm 038 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 38 is a confession of sin and a prayer for deliverance. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:confess]], [[:en:obe:kt:sin]] and [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===
== Author's sickness ==
The author was very sick and his enemies were seeking ways to destroy him.
