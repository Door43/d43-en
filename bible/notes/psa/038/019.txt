===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But my enemies are numerous ... are many** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **They repay me evil for good** - The actions of the writer's enemies are spoken of as a financial transaction where they gave him evil things in exchange for good things. The abstract nouns "evil" and "good" can be stated as actions. AT: They give me bad actions in exchange for my good actions for them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **hurl accusations at me** - The way the writer's enemies accuse him is spoken of as if they were throwing accusations at him like rocks. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **pursued what is good** - The writers desire for what is good is spoken of as if he were running after good things. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
