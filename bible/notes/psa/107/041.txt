===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But he** - "But Yahweh"
  * **the needy** - This refers to needy people. AT: "needy people" or "poor people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **cares for his families like a flock** - Here David compares how Yahweh cares for his people to how a shepherd cares for his sheep. Possible meanings are 1) "makes the number of people in their families increase like flocks" or 2) "takes care of them like a shepherd cares for his sheep" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **The upright** - This refers to people who live in an upright way. AT: "Upright people" or "People who do what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **all wickedness** - Here wicked people are referred to as "wickedness." AT: "all wicked people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **shuts its mouth** - This means to not say anything in response. AT: "has nothing to say against Yahweh in reply" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **take note of these things** - This means to think about these things. AT: "think about these things" or "remember these things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
