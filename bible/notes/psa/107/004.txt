===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They wandered**  - "The redeemed of Yahweh wandered" or "Those whom Yahweh has saved wandered"
  * **on a desert road** - "on a road that was in the desert"
  * **in which to live** - "that they could live in"
  * **they fainted in exhaustion** - "they became weak" or "their became feeble"
  * **they cried to Yahweh** - It is implied that they are crying to Yahweh so that he will help them. AT: "they cried to Yahweh for help" or "they scream to Yahweh for help." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **distress **- "difficulties" or "afflictions"
