====== Psalm 107 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 107 is the last in a series of five psalms of praise to God (Psalm 103-107).

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===
== Praise ==
The psalmist praises Yahweh for saving people lost in the desert, in prison, sick, in a storm at sea, homeless and oppressed. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:save]] and [[:en:obe:kt:oppress]])
