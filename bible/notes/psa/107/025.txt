===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and aroused the windstorm** - "and made the wind of a storm appear"
  * **that stirs up the seas** - "and the wind caused the waves of the sea to become very high"
  * **They reached up to the sky, and then they dropped down to the depths** - the description is of sailors on the ocean (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **dropped down** - "went down"
  * **to the depths** - "deep into the sea between the high waves"
  * **The men's courage melted away** - AT: "They were terrified." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **like drunkards** - "like people who are drunk"
  * **and were at their wits's end** - "and the sailors did not know what to do." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
