===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh that people...for humanity**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:107:008|107:8]].
  * **Let them exalt** - AT: "They should praise"
  * **in the assembly of the people** - "when the people gather together"
  * **in the council of the elders** - "when the elders sit together." The elders sat together to discuss issues in the community and to make decisions for the community.
