===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they called out…their distress.**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:107:004|107:6]]
  * **Then they**  - The word "they" refers to the sailors.
  * **He calmed the storm**  - "He made the wind stop"
  * **the waves were stilled**  - AT: "he made the waves still." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **he brought them**  - "he guided them"
  * **their desired harbor**  - AT: "to where they wanted to go"
