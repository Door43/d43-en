===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **praise to our God** - AT: "a song of praise to our God" or "for us to praise our God" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
    * **Many will see it**  - AT: "Many people will hear about what he has done" 
    * **Blessed is the man who makes Yahweh his trust**  - AT: "Blessed is the man who trusts in Yahweh" or "Those who trust in Yahweh are blessed"
