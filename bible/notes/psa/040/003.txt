===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He has put a new song in my mouth** - Possible meanings are 1) "He has taught me the words to a new song" or 2) "He has given me a new reason to sing." 
  * **in my mouth** - Here "my mouth" refers to the writer. AT: "in me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **praise to our God** - The noun "praise" can be stated as a verb. AT: "a song to praise our God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Many will see it** - Here "see it" refers to hearing the writer sing his song about what God has done for him. AT: "Many people will hear me tell what Yahweh has done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Blessed is the man who makes Yahweh his trust** - "Blessed is the man who trusts in Yahweh" or "Those who trust in Yahweh are blessed"
  * **the proud** - This refers to the people who are proud. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **to lies** - The Hebrew word is unclear. Possible meanings are 1) "lies" or 2) "false gods" .
