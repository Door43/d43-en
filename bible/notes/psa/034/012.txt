===== Translation Notes =====

  * **What man desires life and desires to live many days and have a good life?** - The implicit answer to this question is "every man." AT: "Every man desires life and desires to live many days and have a good life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Then keep from speaking evil** - AT: "Therefore, keep from speaking evil"
  * **keep your lips from speaking lies** - AT: "keep yourself from speaking lies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
