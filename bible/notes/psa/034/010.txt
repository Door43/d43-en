===== translationNotes: =====

  * **will not lack anything good** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "will always have the good things they need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **sons** - Here this does not refer to literal sons of the writer, but to the people he is teaching about Yahweh. AT: "my students"
