===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I sought Yahweh** - Here "sought Yahweh" means David was asking Yahweh for help. AT: "I prayed to Yahweh" or "I asked Yahweh for help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Those who look to him** - Here "look to" represents seeking help from him. AT: "Those who look at him for help" or "those who expect help only from him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **are radiant** - This idiom refers to their appearance as being joyful. AT: "are joyful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **their faces are not ashamed** - Here "their faces" refers to the people who look to Yahweh. It can also be stated in positive form. AT: "they are not ashamed" or "they are proud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **This oppressed man** - David describes himself as an oppressed man. AT: "I was oppressed and" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **Yahweh heard him** - Here "heard" means that Yahweh helped him. AT: "Yahweh heard me" or "Yahweh helped him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
