===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Evil will kill the wicked** - Evil is described as if it were a man who can kill people. AT: "The evil deeds of wicked people will kill them" deeds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the wicked** - This refers to wicked people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **Those who hate the righteous will be condemned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will condemn those who hate the righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the righteous** - This refers to righteous people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **None of those who take refuge in him will be condemned** - This can be stated in active form. It can also be stated in positive form. AT: "Yahweh will forgive everyone who takes refuge in him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **take refuge in him** - Going to Yahweh for protection is spoken of as taking refuge in him. AT: "go to him for protection" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
