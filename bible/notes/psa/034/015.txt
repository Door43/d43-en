===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The eyes of Yahweh are on the righteous** - Here "the eyes of Yahweh" refer to his careful watching. "The righteous" is a reference to righteous people. AT: "Yahweh carefully watches over the righteous people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **his ears are directed toward their cry** - Here "his ears" refers to Yahweh's desire to respond to them. AT: "he pays attention to their cry" or "he answers their cry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to cut off the memory of them from the earth** - Yahweh will cause people to so completely forget them when they die that it is as if he used a knife to cut off any memory of them. AT: "so that when they are dead, people will forget them completely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Yahweh hears** - Here "hears" means that Yahweh desires to respond to them. AT: "Yahweh pays attention to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
