===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He gave us their land as an inheritance** - The land that the Israelites would possess forever is spoken of as if it they inherited it from God. AT: "He gave us their land for us to have forever" or "He gave us their land to be ours forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Your name** - His name represents his fame or reputation. AT: "Your fame" or "Your reputation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
