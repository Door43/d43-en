====== Psalm 080 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 80 is a deliverance psalm. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's help ==
Israel's enemies threaten them. The psalmist asks Yahweh to look down with joy on them then they will be saved. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:save]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

The author uses an extended metaphor of Israel as a vine planted by God which is being uprooted. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
