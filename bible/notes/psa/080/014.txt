===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and take notice** - "and look at"
  * **this vine** - The writer continues comparing the nation of Israel to the vine. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **This is the root that your right hand planted** - The right hand represents the Yahweh's power and control. AT: "This is the root that you, Yahweh, planted." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
