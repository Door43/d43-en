===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pay attention, - **  AT: "Listen to us, the Israelite people,"
  * **Shepherd of Israel- **  The writer is referring to God as the shepherd of Israel. (See: metaphor)
  * **Joseph **- Here Joseph means the whole tribe of Israel. (See: Metonymy)
  * **you who lead Joseph like a flock;**  - AT: "you who lead us as a shepherd leads his flock of sheep;" (UDB) (See: simile)
  * **you who sit above the cherubim,** - AT: "you who sit in authority over the cherubim,"
  * **shine over us!**  - AT: "act kindly toward us!"
