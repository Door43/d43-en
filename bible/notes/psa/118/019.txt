===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Open to me the gates of righteousness** - "Open to me the gates through which the righteous enter." This is a reference to the gates of the temple, and the writer is addressing the gatekeepers. AT: "Open for me the temple gates" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the gate of Yahweh** - "the gate that leads to Yahweh's presence" or "Yahweh's gate"
  * **I will give thanks to you** - Here the writer begins to speak to Yahweh.
  * **you have become my salvation** - The word "salvation" can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "you have saved me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
