===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A song, a psalm of the sons of Korah;**  - "This is a psalm that the sons of Korah wrote;"
  * **for the chief musician;** - "this is for the director of music to use in worship;"
  * **set to the Mahalath Leannoth style** - This may refer to a style of music.
  * **A maschil** - This may refer to a style of music. See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:032:001|32:1]].
