===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Your angry actions have passed over me** - This speaks of God punishing the writer as if God's actions are a large wave rising out of the sea that fall on and crush the writer. AT: "It is as if your angry actions crush me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your terrifying deeds have annihilated me** - This exaggeration speaks about God punishing the writer as if God has completely destroyed the writer. AT: "the terrifying things you do have destroyed me" or "the terrifying things you do have almost destroyed me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
