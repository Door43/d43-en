===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **A song of ascents** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]]
  * **they have attacked** - AT: "my enemies have attacked"
  * **The plowers have plowed on my back**- Here the cuts being inflicted on someone's back by whipping is being compared to a farmer plowing a field. AT: "My enemies have cut me deeply on my back" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they made their furrows long** - AT: "They made their cuts long and deep" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
