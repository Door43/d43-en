===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **dogs** - This word refers to his enemies or evil people. AT: "my enemies are like dogs who" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a company of evildoers** - "a group of evildoers" or "a gang of evildoers"
  * **they have pierced** - The word "They" refers to the enemies surrounding him.
  * **pierced my hands** - The word "pierced" refers to stabbing through the flesh with a sharp pointed tool.
  * **I can count all my bones** - AT: "I am so thin I can see every bone"
  * **look and stare at me** - This is repeated for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
