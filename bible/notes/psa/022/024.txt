===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he has not despised or abhorred** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "he has admired and cherished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **has not despised or abhorred the suffering of the afflicted one** - Here "the suffering" stands for the person who is suffering. AT: "has not despised or abhorred the one who is suffering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **despised or abhorred** - These two words mean basically the same thing and emphasize that God has not forgotten the writer. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **despised** - "detested" or "hated"
  * **abhorred** - "loathed" or "scorned"
  * **the suffering of the afflicted one ... from him ... the afflicted one cried** - This can be stated so that it refers to anyone who suffers. AT: "those who suffer ... from them ... those who are suffering cried" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **has not hidden his face** - This is an idiom. AT: "has not turned his attention away" or "has not ignored" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he heard** - "he listened." It is implied that he responded when he heard their cry. AT: "he answered" or "he helped" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **because of you** - Here "you" refers to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **I will fulfill my vows** - This refers to sacrifices the writer promised to offer to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **before those who fear him** - Here "him" refers to Yahweh. It can be stated as "you." AT: "in the presence of those who fear you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
