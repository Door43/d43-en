===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I am a worm and not a man** - The writer speaks of himself as if he were a worm. This emphasizes that he feels worthless or that the people treat him as if he were worthless. AT: "But it is like I am a worm and not a man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **disgrace to humanity and despised by the people** - Both of these phrase mean basically the same thing. The phrase "despised by the people" can be stated in active form. AT: "everyone thinks I am worthless and the people hate me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **taunt me; they mock me; they shake their heads at me** - These three phrases share similar meanings and emphasize how much people disrespect him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **they shake their heads at me** - This describes an action used to mock someone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **He trusts in Yahweh ... delights in him** - The people are saying this to mock the writer. They do not really believe Yahweh will rescue him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
  * **Let him rescue him** - "Let Yahweh rescue him"
  * **for he delights in him** - Possible meanings are 1) "for Yahweh delights in him" or 2) "for he delights in Yahweh"
