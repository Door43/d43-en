===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nadab ... Abihu** - These are men's names. See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:06:23|6:23]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Under his feet** - This speaks of God as if he had human feet. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  * **a pavement made of sapphire stone** - "a pavement made of blue stones called sapphires"
  * **pavement** - a hard surface for walking or riding
  * **sapphire stone** - This is a gemstone that is blue in color. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **as clear as the sky itself** - This is a simile. AT: "as clear as the sky is when there are no clouds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **God did not lay a hand in anger on the Israelite leaders** - This means that God did not harm the leaders. AT: "God did not harm the Israelite leaders" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
