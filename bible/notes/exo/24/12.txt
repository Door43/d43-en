===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **tablets of stone and the law and commandments** - God had written the law and commandments on the tablets of stone. This can be stated clearly. AT: "two stone slabs on which I have written all the laws" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **with his assistant Joshua** - "with Joshua who assisted him" or "with Joshua who helped him"
