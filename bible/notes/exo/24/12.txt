===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tables of stone**  - This refers to flat pieces of rock that were of a regular shape and suitable for Moses to carry and show to the people.
  * **set out**  - "left" or "went"
  * **assistant **- This refers to a person who is a close helper to the person in charge. In this case Joshua assists Moses.
