===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It came about** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **by their armed groups** - The term used for these groups is a military term referring to a large number of soldiers. AT: "divisions" or "regiments." See how you translated "armed group" in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:12:17|12:17]].
