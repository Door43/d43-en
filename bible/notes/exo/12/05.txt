===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **must be without blemish** - AT: "must be perfect, having no marks or scars"
  * **on the two doorposts and on top of the door frame** - AT: "all around the door" or "on the sides and top of the way into the house"
  * **roasting** - This means to cook over an open fire.
  * **bitter herbs** - These are small plants that have a strong and usually bad taste.
