===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **summoned**  - "called for"
    * **select**  - "choose" or "pick"
    * **bunch**  - "group" or "cluster"
    * **hyssop**  - "a plant of the mint family"
    * **dip it in the blood**  - "put it in the blood"
    * **doorframe**  - "the sides and top around the door"
