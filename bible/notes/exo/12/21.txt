===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **summoned**  - "officially called "
  * **select**  - "choose" or "pick"
  * **kids **- "young goats"
  * **bunch**  - " big handful" or "cluster"
  * **hyssop**  - This is a plant in the mint family, which is woody and used for thin twigs.
  * **door frame**  - This refers to the supporting sides and top around a door.
