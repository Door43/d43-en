===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **at midnight**  - AT: "in the middle of the night"
  * **cattle**  - This means sheep, cattle, goats, horses, and donkeys. (See: [[:en:obe:other:livestock|livestock]])
  * **there was a great cry in Egypt**  - "Everyone in Egypt cried loudly". (See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:active|Active or Passive]])
  * **for there was not a house where there was not someone dead**  - This means that there was someone dead in every house. AT: "because in every house, someone's son had died" (UDB). (See: [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:litotes|Litotes]])
