===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not eat it raw** - "Do not eat the lamb or goat uncooked"
  * **belt** - This refers to a strip of leather or fabric for tying around the waist.
  * **eat it hurriedly** - AT: "eat it quickly" or "eat it fast"
