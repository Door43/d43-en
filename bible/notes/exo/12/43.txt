===== translationNotes: =====

  * **no foreigner may share in eating it** - The pronoun "it" refers to the Passover meal.
  * **every Israelite's slave** - "any slave of an Israelite"
  * **bought with money** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom the Israelite has bought with money" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
