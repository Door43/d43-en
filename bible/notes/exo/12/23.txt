===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh will pass through**  - "Yahweh will pass through Egypt" (UDB); (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **pass over your door**  - This means that God will spare or have mercy on the Israelites in homes that are "covered" by blood.
