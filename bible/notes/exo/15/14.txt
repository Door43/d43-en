===== translationNotes: =====

  * **tremble** - This means to shake because you are afraid.
  * **terror will seize the inhabitants of Philistia** - Moses speaks of terror as if it were a person that could forcefully grab hold of someone and make them extremely afraid. AT: "the inhabitants of Philistia will be afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **will melt away** - Moses speaks of people becoming weak from their fear as melting away. AT: "will be weak from fear" or "will be afraid and faint" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
