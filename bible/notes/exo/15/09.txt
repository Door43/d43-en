===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **my desire will be satisfied on them;** - AT: I will satisfy my desire on them. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **sank like lead in the mighty waters.**  - Lead is a heavy metal, commonly used to make things sink in water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Who is like you, Yahweh, among the gods?** - AT: "O Yahweh, no one is like you among the gods!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
