===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **plunder** - This is things that are stolen, especially during a war.
  * **my desire will be satisfied on them;**  - AT: I will satisfy my desire on them. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **sank like lead in the mighty waters.**  - This means the army sank quickly in the water. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]) Lead is a heavy and soft metal, gray in color, that sinks very quickly in water. If people in your culture do not know what lead is, you can substitute the phrase "sank like a rock in the mighty waters."
  * **Who is like you, Yahweh, among the gods?**  - The implied answer is that there is no god like Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Who is like you, majestic in holiness, honored in praises, doing miracles? ** - The implied answer is that there is no god like Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
