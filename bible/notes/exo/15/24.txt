===== translationNotes: =====

  * **complained to Moses and said** - "were unhappy and told Moses" or "angrily told Moses"
  * **the voice of Yahweh your God** - Yahweh is speaking about his own voice.  AT: "my voice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **do what is right in his eyes** - The eyes represent seeing, and seeing represents thoughts or judgment. AT: "do what Yahweh considers to be right"(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will put on you none of the diseases** - God speaks of causing people to have diseases as putting diseases on them. AT: "I will not cause any of you to have the diseases" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
