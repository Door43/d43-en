===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a song about the events that happened in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:14:26|14:26-28]].

  * **he has triumphed gloriously** - It can be stated explicitly over whom Yahweh triumphed. AT: "he has achieved a glorious victory over the army of Egypt"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the horse and its rider he has thrown into the sea** - Moses sang about God causing the sea to cover and drown the horse and rider as if God had thrown them into the sea. AT: "he has made the sea cover over the horse and rider" or "he has made the horse and rider drown in the sea" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the horse and its rider** - This refers to all or many of the Egyptian horses and riders that were chasing the Israelites. AT: "the horses and their riders" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **rider** - This is a person who sits on a horse or travels in a chariot that a horse is pulling.
