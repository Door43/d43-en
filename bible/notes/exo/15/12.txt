===== translationNotes: =====

  * **with your right hand** - The phrase "right hand" represents the strong power of God. AT: "with your strong power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **You reached out with your right hand** - Moses speaks about God causing something to happen as if God reached out with his hand. AT: "With your strong power you made it happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the earth swallowed them** - Moses personifies the earth as if it could swallow or devour with it's mouth. AT: "the earth devoured them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
