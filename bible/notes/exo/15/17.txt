===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **plant them** - The word "plant" was a customary way of saying "set in place." AT: "put them" or "place them firmly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **mountain of your inheritance** - The word "mountain" was a customary way of say a "sturdy and abiding place." This refers to God's promise to live among them. AT: "your strong and lasting presence.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
