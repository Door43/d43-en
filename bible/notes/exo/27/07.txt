===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people should do.
  * **planks** - a long, flat piece of timber thicker than a board
  * **you were shown** - AT: "I showed you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
