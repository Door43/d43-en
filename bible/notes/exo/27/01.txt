===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do. (See: [[en:bible:notes:exo:25:01]])

  * **You must make extensions of its four corners shaped like ox horns**  - "You must make horns on its four corners"
  * **The horns will be made** - AT: "You must make the horns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **must cover them** - "must cover the altar and horns"
