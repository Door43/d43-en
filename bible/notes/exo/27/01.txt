===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:exo:25:01]])

  * **five cubits long and five cubits wide** - "2.2 meters long on each side" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **The altar must be square and three cubits high** - "The altar must be square and 1.3 meters high" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **cubits** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **You must make extensions of its four corners shaped like ox horns** - "You must make projections that look like ox horns on its four corners"
  * **The horns will be made** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must make the horns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **must cover them** - "must cover the altar and horns"
