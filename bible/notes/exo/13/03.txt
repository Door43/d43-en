===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Call this day to mind** - The words "Call ... to mind" were a customary way of telling someone to remember something. AT: "Remember and celebrate this day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the house of slavery** - Moses speaks of Egypt as if it were a house where people keep slaves. AT: "the place where you were slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Yahweh's strong hand** - Here the word "hand" refers to power. See how you translated "strong hand" in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:06:01|6:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **No bread with yeast may be eaten** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must not eat bread with yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the month of Aviv** - This is the name of the first month of the Hebrew calendar. Aviv is during the last part of March and the first part of April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **a land flowing with milk and honey** - Since milk comes from cows and goats, "milk" represents food produced by livestock. Because honey is produced from flowers, "honey" represents food from crops. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:03:07|3:8]]. AT: "food from livestock and from crops" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **you must observe this act of worship** - When the Israelites live in Canaan, they must celebrate the Passover on this day each year. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:12:24|12:25]].
