===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel

  * **For seven days**  - AT: "For 7 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|How to Translate Numbers]])
  * **on the seventh day**  - AT: "after six days"
  * **Bread without yeast must be eaten**  - AT: "You must eat bread without yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **no bread with yeast may be seen among you**  - AT: "You may not have bread with yeast among you"
  * **No yeast may be seen with you**  - AT: "You may not have yeast"
