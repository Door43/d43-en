===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My own presence** - "Yahweh will literally be present" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **give you** - give Moses (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you
]])
  * **how will it be known that I have found favor in your sight, I and your people** - This emphasizes the need for Yahweh to go with Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will it not only be if you go with us so that I and your people are different from all the other peoples that are on the surface of the earth** - This emphasizes the result of Yahweh going with Israel. That they will be "different from all the other peoples that are on the surface of the earth" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
