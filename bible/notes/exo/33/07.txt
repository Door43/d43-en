===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the pillar of cloud** - The cloud is spoken of as if it were a pillar because it had the shape of a pillar. AT: "the cloud shaped like a pillar" or "the tall cloud." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:13:19|13:22]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **would come down** - Where it came down from can be stated clearly. AT: "would come down from the sky" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
