===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I could have reached out with my hand** - This means that God could have used his power (See UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you would have been eradicated** - AT: "diseases that would have killed you all" (UDB); (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so that my name may be spread throughout all the earth** - AT: "so that all people will know how great I am"; (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
