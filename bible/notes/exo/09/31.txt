===== translationNotes: =====

  * **flax** - This is a plant that produces fibers that can be made into linen cloth.
  * **barley** - This is a type of grain used for making bread; also used for cattle feed.
  * **spelt** - This is a kind of wheat.
  * **spread out his hands to Yahweh** - This symbolic gesture accompanies prayer. AT: "lifted up his hands toward Yahweh and prayed" See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:09:29|9:29]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
