===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **menace** - A "menace" is someone who brings trouble or harm. 
  * **How long will this man be a menace to us?** - AT: "We cannot allow this man to continue to bring trouble to us!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Do you not yet realize that Egypt is destroyed?** - AT: "You should realize that Egypt is destroyed!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
