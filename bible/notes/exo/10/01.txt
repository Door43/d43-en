  * **for I have hardened **- AT: "for I have made his heart stubborn"
  * **I may show these signs among them,**  - "I may show them these signs,"
  * **the things I have done**  - "the stories of the things I have done,"
  * **harshly**  - "unpleasantly"
  * **with Egypt, and of my signs that **- "with Egypt by the miracles that"
  * **various **- "many different"
