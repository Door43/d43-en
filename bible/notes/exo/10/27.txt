===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Be careful about one thing** - AT: "Make sure"
  * **for on the day you see my face, you will die** - This is a threat made by Pharaoh that if Moses talked to him again, the king would have Moses killed.
  * **face** - "me"; Here "face" represents the whole person, not just his face. (See: Metonymy)
  * **You yourself have spoken** - "You are right" or "You have said it correctly"
