===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Be careful about one thing** - AT: "Make sure of one thing" or "Be certain of one thing"
  * **on the day you see my face** - Here "face" represents the whole person. AT: "The day you see me again"; (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I will not see your face** - AT: "I will never see you"; (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
