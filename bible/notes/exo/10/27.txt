===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh hardened Pharaoh's heart** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:09:11|9:12]].
  * **he would not let them go** - "Pharaoh would not let them go"
  * **Be careful about one thing** - "Make sure of one thing" or "Be certain of one thing"
  * **you see my face** - Here the word "face" refers to the whole person. AT: "you see me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **You yourself have spoken** - With these words, Moses emphasizes that Pharaoh has spoken the truth. AT: "What you have said is true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
