===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

  * **atonement lid** - This is the lid that sits on top of the ark where the atonement offering was made.
  * **two and a half cubits ... a cubit and a half** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "2.5 cubits ... 1.5 cubits" or "115 centimeters ... 69 centimeters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
  * **hammered gold** - "beaten gold"
