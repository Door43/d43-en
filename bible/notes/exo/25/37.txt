===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **for them to give light from it** - "so they shine light from it"
  * **The tongs and their trays must be made of pure gold** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Make the tongs and their trays of pure gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **one talent** - A talent weighs about thirty-three kilograms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **its accessories** - the tongs and the trays
  * **you are being shown on the mountain** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I am showing you on the mountain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
