===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **blue purple and scarlet wool**  - See how you translated this in 25:4.
  * **embroiderer**  - This is someone who embroiders a design into a piece of clothe.
  * **twined**  - This is a verbal noun meaning to spin tightly things such as wool or linen.
  * **cast**  - See how you translated this in 25:12.
