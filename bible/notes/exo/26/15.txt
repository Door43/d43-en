===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.
  * **vertical boards** - "upright boards"
  * **boards** - This may refer to wide, individual boards, or to frames (UDB) or panels that they made by joining together smaller pieces of wood.
  * **projections**  - This is a joint that sticks out.
