===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lord, why have you caused trouble for this people? Why did you send me in the first place?**  - When Moses asked God these questions, he also showed how disappointed he was that the Egyptians were treating the Israelites even more harshly now.
  * **to speak to him in your name**  - AT: "to give him your message"
