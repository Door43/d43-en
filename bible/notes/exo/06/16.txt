===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Gershon, Kohath, and Merari**  - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **years of the life of Levi were 137 years**  - These people lived longer than most people today. If your language does not have a way to express 137, translate "Levi lived a very long life" or "Levi lived a great number of years."
  * **Amran, Izhar, Hebron, Uzziel**  - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **clan ancestors of the Levi**  - "tribe of Levi"
  * **years of the life of Kohath were 133 years**  - These people lived longer than most people today. If your language does not have a way to express 133, translate "Kohath lived a very long life" or "Kohath lived a great number of years."
