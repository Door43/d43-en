  * **heads** - Often this term is used to refer to someone who is in authority over people.  
  * **Hanoch, Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi … Jemuel, Jamin, Ohad, Jachin, Zohar, and Shaul** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
