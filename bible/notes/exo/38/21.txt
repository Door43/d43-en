===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Bezalel's work crew continues to build the tabernacle and furniture.
  * **General Information:** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **as it was taken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "which Moses instructed the Levites to write down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Ithamar** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:06:23|6:23]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Bezalel son of Uri son of Hur** - "Bezalel" and "uri" are the name of men. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:01|31:1-2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Yahweh had commanded Moses** - "everything that Yahweh told Moses to do"
  * **Oholiab son of Ahisamach** - "Oholiab" and "Ahisamach" are names of men. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:06|31:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **an engraver, as a skillful workman, and as an embroiderer** - "as a skilled engraver and embroiderer"
