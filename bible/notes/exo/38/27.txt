===== translationNotes: =====

  * **One hundred talents of silver were cast** - A talent is about 33 kilograms. AT: "The workers cast 100 talents of silver" or "The workers cast 3,300 kilograms of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **One hundred talents** - "100 talents" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **1,775 shekels** - "one thousand seven hundred and seventy-five shekels" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **shekels** - A shekel is a unit of weight equal to about 11 grams. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **Bezalel** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:01|31:1-2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **seventy talents and 2,400 shekels** - "70 talents and two thousand four hundred shekels." This would be about 2,300 kilograms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
