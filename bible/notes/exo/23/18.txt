===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fat from the sacrifices**  - This passage discusses practices related to animal sacrifices, so different parts of the animal have specific rules.
  * **The fat...must not remain all night until the morning**
 - AT: "The fat...must be removed from the altar during the night" or "The priest must remove the fat...before dawn." This refers to a requirement that the fat of the sacrifice cannot stay all night on the altar.
  * **choicest**  - "best"
