===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to hold feast**  - AT: "hold sacred festivals"
  * **Abib**  - This is the name of a month. (See:[[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/translate/translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **which is fixed**  - Moses announces that this month will always be the time for this feast. AT: "which God has chosen to always be the time for this feast" (See: Passive.)
  * **appear before me empty-handed**  - AT: "come to me without a proper offering"
