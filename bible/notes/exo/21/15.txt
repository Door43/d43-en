===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Whoever hits his father or mother must surely be put to death** - AT: "If anyone hits his father or mother, you must surely put him to death" or "You must surely kill anyone who hits his father or mother."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **must surely** - "must certainly"
  * **in his possession** - "with him"
