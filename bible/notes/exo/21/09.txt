===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **designates**  - "chooses"
  * **diminish** - "take away" or "reduce" or "lessen" or "deprive her of"
  * **diminish her food** - The word "her" refers to the first wife.  
  * **marital rights** - This refers to the things the husband must do for the wife, including sleeping with her.
