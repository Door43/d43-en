===== translationNotes: =====

  * **designates** - "chooses"
  * **he must not diminish her food, clothing, or her marital rights** - This can be stated in a positive form. AT: "he must give the first wife the same food, clothing, and marital rights she had before" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **diminish** - "take away" or "reduce"
  * **or her marital rights** - This includes things that a husband must do for his wife, including sleeping with her. AT: "and he must continue to sleep with her as before" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
