===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **has gored** - "has injured with its horns"
  * **thirty shekels of silver**  - "330 grams of silver." A shekel weighed eleven grams. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalmoney|biblical money]])
  * **the ox must be stoned** - AT: "you must kill the ox by stoning it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
