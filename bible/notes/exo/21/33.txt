===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **opens a pit**  - This refers to re-opening a hole in the ground that had been dug earlier.
  * **repay the loss** - AT: "pay the owner of the dead animal an amount of money equal to the value of the dead animal"
  * **will become his** - "His" here refers to the other of the pit. AT: "will belong to the owner of the pit."
