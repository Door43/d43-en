===== translationNotes: =====

  * **as a result of the blow** - "because of the injury" or "because his master hit him"
  * **that man must surely be punished** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you must certainly punish that man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for he will have suffered the loss of the servant** - You can express clearly in the translation that the servant was valuable to his master. AT: "because he has already lost his servant who was valuable to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
