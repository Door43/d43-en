===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as a result of the blow** - "because of the injury" or "because he was hit"
  * **that man must surely be punished** - AT: "you must certainly punish that man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for he will have suffered the loss of the servant** - "because he has already lost his servant who was valuable to him"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
