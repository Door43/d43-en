===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
  * **five hundred shekels...250 shekels** - "500 shekels...250 shekels." A shekel is 11 grams. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **cinnamon...cane...cassia** - These are sweet spices. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **measured by the weight of the shekel of the sanctuary** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:exo:30:11|30:13]].
  * **one hin** - A hin is 3.7 liters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
  * **with these ingredients** - "with these items"
  * **the work of a perfumer** - This is a person who is skilled in mixing spices.
