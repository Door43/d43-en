===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh tells Moses how to build the worship equipment.

  * **You must make** - Here "you" refers to Moses and the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **Its horns must be made** - hese were projections that looked like ox horns attached to the four corners of the altar. AT: "You must make its horns." See how you translated "horns" in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:27:01|27:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
