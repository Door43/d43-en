===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must also make** - Here "you" refers to Moses and the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **basin** - "bowl" or "tub"
  * **a bronze stand** - This is what the basin would be put on.
  * **a basin for washing** - This phrase explains what the priests were to use the large bronze basin for. 
  * **the altar** - the altar of sacrifice
