===== Translation Notes: =====
Bezalel's work crew make the priestly garments.

  * **He made...one span wide** - For 39:8-9 see how you translated many of these words in [[en:bible:notes:exo:28:15|28:15-16]].
  * **He made** - "Bezalel made" or "Bezalel and the workers made"
