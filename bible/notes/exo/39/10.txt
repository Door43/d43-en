===== Translation Notes: =====

Bezalel's work crew make the priestly garments.

  * **They set...in gold settings** - For 39:10-13 see how you translated many of these words in [[en:bible:notes:exo:28:17|28:17-20]].
  * **They set in it** - "The workers set in the breastpiece"
  * **The stones were mounted in gold settings** - "They mounted the stones in gold settings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
