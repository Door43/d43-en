===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Pharaoh's heart is hard** - Here "heart" refers to Pharaoh. His stubborn attitude is spoken of as if his heart was hard. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:07:11|7:13]]. AT: "Pharaoh is stubborn" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **when he goes out to the water** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "when he goes down to the Nile River to bathe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]
