===== translationNotes: =====

  * **manna** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:16:26|16:26]]. 
  * **coriander seed** - Coriander is also known as cilantro. People dry the seeds and grind them into a powder and put it in food to give it flavor. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **wafer** - a very thin biscuit or cracker
  * **omer** - "2 liters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **bread** - God speaks of the food that he sent as if it were bread. The Israelites would eat this food every day, just as they had eaten bread every day before this. AT: "food" or "food like bread." See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:16:04|16:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
