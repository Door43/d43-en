===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **coriander seed** - Coriander is also known as cilantro. This seed is a spice when dried.
  * **wafer** - This is a very thin biscuit.
  * **omer** - This is an amount that is equal to 2 liters. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
