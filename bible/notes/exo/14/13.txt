===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Moses said to the people** - Moses responds to the Israelites' fears.
  * **provide for you** - The pronoun "you" refers to the Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **For you will never see again the Egyptians** - Moses used a polite way of saying God was going to destroy the Egyptian army. AT: "For God will kill the Egyptians" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
