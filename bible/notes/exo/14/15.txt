===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Why are you crying out to me?**  - God does not actually want to know why the Israelites are crying out to him. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **divide it in two** - "cut the sea into two pieces"
    * **Be aware**  - AT: "Know"
    * **...so they will go after them.**  - God will harden the hearts of the Egyptians so that the Egyptians will follow the Israelites into the sea.
