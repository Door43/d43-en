===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **a land flowing with milk and honey**  - This figurative description of the Promised Land uses the name of a part of the whole land to refer to the whole land. It is used repeatedly and should be translated consistently in all occurrences: Exodus 3:17, 13:5, 33:3, and many more throughout.
