===== translationNotes: =====

  * **midnight** - This is the time of 12 am (at night) or 2400 hours.
  * **All the first-born ... the first-born of Pharaoh ... the first-born of the slave girl ... the first-born of the cattle** - The "first-born" always refers to the oldest male offspring. 
  * **who sits on his throne** - This phrase refers to Pharaoh.
  * **who is behind the handmill grinding it** - "who is grinding at the handmill" or "who is behind the handmill grinding grain"
