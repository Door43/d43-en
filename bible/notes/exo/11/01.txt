===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **drive you out completely**  - Alternate Translation: "make all of you leave" or "make you all go all the way out"
  * **neighbor**  - a person who lives near you
  * **jewels**  - in the original language (Hebrew), this refers to objects of great value, including things you wear and things you use to eat (plates, cups) and to make your house pretty
  * **silver**  - a white metal which is very costly
  * **gold**  - a yellow metal which is very costly
