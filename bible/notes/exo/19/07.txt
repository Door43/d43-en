===== translationNotes: =====

  * **set before them all these words** - The author writes of Moses telling people things as if he were setting the words before them. AT "told them all these words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all these words that Yahweh had commanded him** - "all that Yahweh had commanded him" 
  * **Moses came to report** - Where Moses went can be stated explicitly. AT: "Moses went back up the mountain to report" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the people's words** - The word "words" refers to what the people said. AT: "what the people said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
