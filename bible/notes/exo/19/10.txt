===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you must dedicate them to me** - This probably means "tell them to dedicate themselves to me" or "tell them to purify themselves for me."
  * **garments** - "clothes"
  * **Be ready** - This was a command to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
