===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **trembled**  - "shook with fear"
  * **descended**  - "came down"
  * **like the smoke of a furnace**  - This shows that it was a very large amount of smoke. AT: "like the smoke from a very large fire"
  * **furnace** - an oven that can be made extremely hot
