===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bezalel** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:01|31:1-2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **two and a half cubits ... one cubit and a half** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "2.5 cubits ... 1.5 cubits" or "115 centimeters ... 69 centimeters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
  * **its four feet** - These four pieces of wood that supported the ark are spoken of as if they were human or animal feet. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
