===== Translation Notes: =====
Bezalel continues to build the tabernacle and furniture

  * **He made...and a half cubits** - For 37:4-6 see how you translated many of these words in [[en:bible:notes:exo:25:12|25:13-14]] and [[en:bible:notes:exo:25:15|25:17]].
  * **He made** - Though "he" refers to Bezalel, "he" may include all of the workers who assisted him.
