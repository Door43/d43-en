===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Moses agreed to stay with the man** - Moses agreed to live with Reuel.
  * **Zipporah** - This is Reuel's daughter. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gershom** - This is Moses' son's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **resident in a foreign land** - AT: "stranger in a foreign land"
