===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **widow** - "woman whose husband has died"
  * **fatherless child** - "orphan" or "child with no parents"
  * **my anger will burn** - AT: "I will become angry"; (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will kill you with the sword** - AT: "I will cause you to be killed in a war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
