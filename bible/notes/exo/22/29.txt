===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hold back offerings**  - This includes the idea of giving only a part of a full offering as well as giving no offering.  AT: "fail to bring all of your offering" or "refuse to bring offerings"
  * **give to me the firstborn of your sons**  - AT: "dedicate your firstborn sons to me"
  * **do the same with**  - AT: "dedicate to me the firstborn of"
  * **For seven days**  - AT: "for seven days after they are born"
  * **the eighth day**  - AT: "day number eight"
  * **give them to me**  - "dedicate them to me"
  * **a people consecrated to me, set apart to honor me**  - "a people dedicated to serving and honoring me"
  * **torn by animals**  - "killed by wild animals"
  * **throw it to the dogs**  - "throw it on the ground for dogs to eat"
