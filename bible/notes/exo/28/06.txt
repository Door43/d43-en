===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.
  * **fine twined linen** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:exo:26:36|26:36]].
  * **skillful craftsman** - "skillful workman"
  * **it must be made of one piece** - AT: "they must make it one piece" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **onyx stones** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:exo:25:03|25:7]].
