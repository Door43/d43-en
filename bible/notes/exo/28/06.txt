===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

  * **fine twined linen** - "finely twisted linen." This was cloth made from fine linen threads that someone twisted together to make a stronger thread. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:36|26:36]].
  * **skillful craftsman** - a person who can make beautiful objects by hand
  * **it must be made of one piece** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they must make it in one piece" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **onyx stones** - These are valuable stones that have layers of white and black, red or brown. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:25:03|25:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
