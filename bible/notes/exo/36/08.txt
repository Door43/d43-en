===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So all the craftsmen...joined to each other** - For 38:8-10 see how you translated many of these words in [[en:bible:notes:exo:26:01|26:1-3]].
  * **ten curtains made from fine linen** - These curtains are sheets of cloth woven and sewed together so that they can hang to form a wall or tent.
