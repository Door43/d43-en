===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **Bezalel made...and so on** - For 36:24-26 see how you translated many of these words in [[en:bible:notes:exo:26:19|26:19-21]].
  * **forty silver bases** - "40 silver bases" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **twenty boards** - "20 boards"
  * **and so on** - there will be two bases under each and every board.
