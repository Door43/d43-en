===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.

  * **coat** - This was a coat with a design woven into it. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:28:04|28:4]].
  * **finely-woven waistband** - This was a cloth belt made from narrow linen threads that someone twisted together to make a stronger thread. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:28:06|28:8]].
  * **turban** - This was a tall head covering made from cloth wrapped around the head several times. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:28:04|28:4]].
  * **holy crown** - This crown is described in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:29:05|29:6]] as being engraved with the words "dedicated to Yahweh" and made of pure gold.
