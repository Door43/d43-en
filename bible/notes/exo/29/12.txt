===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.
  * **the horns** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:exo:27:01|27:2]].
  * **the rest of the blood**  - "the remaining blood"
  * **covers the inner parts**  - "covers the organs"
  * **liver...kidneys** - These are organs in the body.
  * **But as for the bull's flesh, as well as its skin and dung** - "But as for the remaining parts of the bull"
