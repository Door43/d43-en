===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **This is the finger of God** - The words "finger of God" represent the almighty power of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Pharaoh’s heart was hardened** - This means Pharaoh broke his promise and became stubborn again. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
