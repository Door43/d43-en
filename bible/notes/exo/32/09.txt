===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I have seen this people** - God speaks of knowing the people and what they are like as if he saw them. AT: "I know this people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a stiff-necked people** - God speaks of the people being stubborn as if they had stiff necks. AT: "a stubborn people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Now then** - The word "now" is used here to mark a break in what God was telling Moses. Here God tells what he will do to the people.
  * **My anger will burn hot against them** - God speaks of his anger as if it were a fire that could burn hot. AT: "My anger towards them will be very strong" or "I am very angry with them" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **from you** - The word "you" refers to Moses. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **why does your anger burn against your people** - Moses used this question to to try to persuade God not to be so angry with his people. AT: "Do not let your anger burn against your people" or "Do not be so angry with your people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **great power ... mighty hand** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **a mighty hand** - Here the word "hand" refers to the things Yahweh did. AT: "and the powerful things you did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
