===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All the people** - This refers to all the people who rejected Moses as their leader and Moses' God as their God.
  * **fashioned it in a mold, and made it into a molded calf**  - Aaron melted the gold collected and poured the gold into a mold that contained the shape of a calf.
