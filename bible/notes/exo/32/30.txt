===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You have committed a very great sin** - They worshiped an idol.
  * **Perhaps I can make atonement for your sin** - Moses spoke of persuading God to forgive the people as if he could make atonement for their sin. AT: "Perhaps I can persuade Yahweh to forgive you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **blot me out of the book** - The word "me" here refers to the name of Moses. AT: "erase my name from the book"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the book that you have written** - What God had written in the book can be stated clearly. AT: "the book in which you have written the names of your people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
