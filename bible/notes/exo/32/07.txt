===== translationNotes: =====

  * **left the way that I commanded them** - God speaks of the people disobeying what he commanded them as if he had commanded them to walk on a certain road and they left that road.  AT: "stopped doing what I commanded them to do" or "have stopped obeying what I commanded them to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **They have molded for themselves a calf** - "They have made a gold statue for themselves shaped like a calf"
