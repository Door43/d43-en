  * **blue, purple, and scarlet wool**  - Possible meanings are 1) “wool that is dyed blue, purple and scarlet” or 2) “blue, purple and scarlet dye.” See notes on 25:3.
  * **scarlet**  - “red”
  * **dyed**  - to have changed a color.
  * **sea cow hides - **  the skin of a large animal that lives in the sea and eats plants
  * **spice**  - a plant used to improve the taste of food
  * **onyx - ** a valuable stone that has layers of white and black, red or brown. (See: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/translate/translate_unknown|How to Translate Unknowns]]) See how you translated this in 25:7
