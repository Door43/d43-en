===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people. 
  * **He has put it in his heart** - AT: "Yahweh has given Bezalel the desire"
  * **Oholiab, son of Ahisamach, from the tribe of Dan** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:exo:31:06|31:6]].
  * **engravers**  - a person who cuts designs into a hard material such as wood or stone
  * **embroiderer** - a person who sews designs into cloth
  * **craftsman**- a person who is skilled in making beautiful objects by hand
  * **weavers** - a person who creates cloth using thread
  * **artistic designers** - a person who creates beauty with materials
