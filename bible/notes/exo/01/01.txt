===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **household** - all the people who live in a house together, usually a large family with servants.
  * **seventy** - This number 70 is the same as 7 groups of 10. If your language has no way to express this number, you can translate it as "many" or "a large number." (see [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Joseph was in Egypt already** - AT: "Joseph was already living in Egypt" or "Joseph lived in Egypt before his brothers"
