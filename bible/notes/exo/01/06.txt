===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all his brothers** - This includes 10 older brothers and 1 younger brother. 
  * **were fruitful** - The birth of children to the Israelites is spoken of as if they were plants that were producing fruit. AT: "had many children" or "gave birth to many children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the land was filled with them** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They filled the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **with them** - The word "them" refers to the Israelites.
