===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God protected these midwives** - God kept pharaoh from killing these midwives.
  * **midwives** - These were women who helped a woman give birth to a baby. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:01:15|1:16]].
  * **The people increased in numbers** - "The Israelites increased in numbers"
  * **feared God** - "revered God" or "had reverence for God"
  * **You must throw every son ... into the river** - This order was given in order to drown the male children. AT: "You must ... into the river so they will drown" (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
