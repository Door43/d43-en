===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Go and show yourselves to the priests**  - This can also be translated as "Go and show yourselves to the priests so they can examine you."
    * **And it came about as they went** - This can also be translated as "And as they were on the way," or "And while they were on the way to the priests."
    * **they were cleansed** - This can also be translated as "they became clean when they were healed of their leprosy," or "they were cured of their leprosy."
    * **And when one of them** - This can also be translated as, "Then when one of the ten."
    * **saw that he was healed** - That is, "realized that he was healed of leprosy," or "became aware that he was healed of leprosy."
    * **he turned back** - That is, "he returned to Jesus," or "he went back to Jesus."
    * **with a loud voice glorifying God** - That is, "with a loud voice giving glory to God" or "with a loud voice praising God" or "and glorified God loudly."
    * **he bowed down at his feet** - This could also be translated as "he knelt down and put his face close to Jesus feet." He did this to honor Jesus.
