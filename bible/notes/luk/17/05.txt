===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Increase our faith**  - That is, "Please give us more faith" or "Please add more faith to our faith."
  * **If you had faith as a grain of mustard seed**  - That is, "If you have faith that is even as small as mustard seed," or "Assuming you have faith the same size as a small mustard seed."
  * **be uprooted, and be planted in the sea** - That is, "uproot yourself and plant yourself in the sea" or "take your roots out of the ground, and put your roots down into the ocean."
  * **it would obey you**  - That is, "the tree would obey you immediately," or "it would at an instant obey you."
