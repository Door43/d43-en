===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And it came about that as they were on the way to Jerusalem**  - This can be translated as "Now while on the way Jerusalem," or "Now as they were traveling towards Jerusalem."
  * **he was traveling through the middle of Samaria and Galilee**  - This can also be translated as, "he was passing between Samaria and Galilee."
  * **there he was met by ten met who were lepers**  - This can also be translated as "ten men who were lepers met him" or "ten men who had leprosy met him."
  * **they lifted up their voices, saying**  - This can also be translated as "they called with a loud voice, saying," or "they called aloud and said to him."
  * **Master**  - The Greek word that is translated here as “Master” is a not the normal word for “Master.” This one refers to someone who has authority, and not to  someone who owns someone else. You could also translate it as "Boss" or "Foreman" or with a word that is commonly used to address a person in authority, such as “Sir.”
  * **have mercy on us**  - This can also be translated as "please have mercy on us," or "please show us mercy by healing us of leprosy."
