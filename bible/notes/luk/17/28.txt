===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Likewise even-**  Or, "another example is"
    * **in the days of Lot**  - that is, "In the time of Lot" or "when Lot was alive."
    * **but in the day that Lot went out from Sodom**  - or, "the very day that Lot went out of Sodom" or "the moment Lot left Sodom."
    * **they ate**  - That is, "The people of Sodom ate."
    * **it rained fire and brimstone from heaven**  - That is, "fire and burning sulfur fell from the sky like rain."
    * **destroyed**- See explanation in notes for Luke 17:26-27.
    * **bold words**  - explanation
