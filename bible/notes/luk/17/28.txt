===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Likewise even as it happened in the days of Lot** - The "days of Lot" refers to the time just before God punished the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah. AT: "Another example is how it happened in the days of Lot" or "As people were doing when Lot lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they were eating and drinking** - "the people of Sodom were eating and drinking"
  * **it rained fire and sulfur from heaven** - "fire and burning sulfur fell from the sky like rain"
  * **destroyed them all** - This does not include Lot and his family. AT: "destroyed all those who stayed in the city"
