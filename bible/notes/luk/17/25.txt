===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking)
    * **But first he must suffer - **  that is, "But first the Son of Man must suffer". Jesus is speaking of himself in the 3rd person.
    * **the days of Noah…the days of the Son of Man**  - that is, "the time of Noah…the time of the Son of Man"
    * **the ark**  - The ark was a large ship.
    * **They ate, they drank, they married, they were given in marriage **- The people alive in Noah's time lived ordinary lives, not caring about the coming Judgment of God.
    * **destroyed them**- that is, "killed them" or "put them to death".
