===== translationNotes: =====

  * **After the same manner it will be** - "It will be like that." AT: "In the same way people will not be ready." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the day that the Son of Man is revealed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "when the Son of Man appears" or "when the Son of man comes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the Son of Man** - Jesus is speaking about himself. AT: "I, the Son of Man"
  * **do not let him who is on the housetop go down** - "whoever is on the housetop must not go down" or "if anyone is on his housetop, he must not go down" 
  * **on the housetop** - Their housetops were flat and people could walk or sit on them.
  * **his goods** - "his possessions" or "his things"
  * **return** - They were not to go back to the house to get anything. They were to flee quickly. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
