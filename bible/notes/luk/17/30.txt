===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking.)
    * **After the same manner it will be** - This can also be translated as, "It will be like that" or "It will be the same."​ Many people were not ready when God judged them in the past, and many will not be ready in the future.
    * **the Son of Man is revealed**- That is, "the Son of Man will be made fully known" or "the Son of Man appears."
    * **do not let him that is on the housetop go down**  - That is, “He that is on the housetop must not go down” or “If anyone is on his housetop, he must not go down” or “If you are on your housetop, do not go down.”
    * **return back**  - That is, "go back to the house."
