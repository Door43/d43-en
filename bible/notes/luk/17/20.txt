===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. We do not know where this take places; it is simply one day when Jesus is talking with the Pharisees.

  * **Being asked by the Pharisees when the kingdom of God would come, Jesus answered them and said,** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Pharisees asked Jesus 'When will the kingdom of God come?'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **Being asked by the Pharisees** - This is the beginning of a new part of the story. Some translations start it with "One day" (UDB) or "Once."
  * **The kingdom of God is not something that can be observed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The kingdom of God is not something that you can see with your eyes" or "Although you look for the kingdom of God, you will not see it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the kingdom of God is among you** - "the kingdom of God is here" or "God has already begun to rule among you"
