===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking)
    * **that night**  - This refers to when the Son of Man, Jesus, will come.
    * **there will be two men in one bed**  - This could also mean, "there will be two persons on one couch." Jesus is describing a hypothetical situation. It could be translated as, "there may be two men on one couch." (See talink Hypothetical Situation)
    * **the one will be taken and the other left**  - That is, "one man will be taken and the other man will be left behind." You could also translate it in the active voice as "God will take one of the men and leave the other," or "angels will take one and leave behind the other." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active]])
    * **there will be two women grinding together**  - This can also be translated as or "two women will be grinding grain at the same place" or "there may be two women grinding grain together." (See talink Hypothetical Situation)
    * **Where lord**-That is, "where will this happen", Lord (Master) is one who has absolute authority such as the homeowner versus the servant (Agent)
    * **Where there is a body**  - This can also be translated as, "Where there is a dead body," or "Where there is a corpse."
    * **there will the vultures also be gathered together**  - This can also be translated as "the vultures will gather together there."
    * **vultures**  - Vultures are large birds that eat the flesh of dead animals that they find. You could translate this as "carrion birds" or "birds that eat dead animals," or you could use the word for a local bird that does this.
