===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Therefore I say to you** - "So I tell you" or "For that reason I tell you" or "Because of what this story teaches us"
    * **Do not worry**- That is, "Do not be thinking too much about." This has the idea of "do not make it your habit to worry," or "do not make it part of your life to worry."
    * **about your life - what you will eat** - This can be translated as "about your life and what you will eat" or "about having enough food in order to live"
    * **about your body - what you will wear** - This can be translated as "about your body and what you will wear" or "about having enough clothes to put on your body."
