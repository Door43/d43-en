===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues teaching his disciples in front of the crowd.

  * **Therefore** - "For that reason" or "Because of what this story teaches"
  * **I say to you** - "I want to tell you something important" or "you need to listen carefully to this"
  * **about your life—what you will eat** - "about your life and what you will eat" or "about having enough food in order to live"
  * **about your body—what you will wear** - "about your body and what you will wear" or "about having enough clothes to put on your body"
  * **For life is more than food** - This is a general statement of value. AT: "life is more important than the food you eat" 
  * **the body is more than clothes** - This is a general statement of value. AT: "your body is more important than the clothes you put on it"
