===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues speaking to his disciples. Now he warns them against fearing people rather than God.)
    * **they have no more that they can do**  - "they cannot do anything else" or "they cannot cause any more harm" or "they cannot hurt you anymore."
    * **Fear the one who** - This could also be translated as "Fear God who" or "Fear God, because."
    * **after he has killed** - "after he kills someone"
    * **has power to cast into hell**  - "has authority to throw the person into hell"
