===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to his disciples. He assures them that God cares for them, so they don't need to be afraid of people.)
    * **Are not five sparrows sold for two small coins?**  - This is a rhetorical question. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]]). It can also be translated as "Think about sparrows. They have so little value that you can buy five of them for only two small coins" (UDB).
    * **sparrows**  - Sparrows are very small, seed eating birds.
    * **not one of the is forgotten in the sight of God**  - "God never forgets any of them! (UDB) or "God never neglects to care for any sparrow!"
    * **Even the hairs of your head are numbered**  - "God knows even how many hairs are on your head" 
    * **Do not fear**  - "Do not be afraid of people" or "So do not be afraid of people who could hurt you"
    * **You are more valuable that many sparrows**  - "God values you more than many sparrows"
