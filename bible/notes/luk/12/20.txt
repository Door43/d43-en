===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Foolish man **- That is "You fool, or "You foolish man."
    * **tonight your soul is required of you**- That is, "tonight you will die," or "tonight your life is to be taken back from you."
    * **the things you have prepared**- That is, "the things you have stored up," or the things you have kept."
    * **whose they will be**- That is, "who will own what you have stored up," or "who will have what you prepared."
    * **So is he**- That is, "This is what will happen to the person," or "It will be the same way for he."
    * **stores up treasures**- That is, "saves up treasures," or "saves up things."
    * **not rich towards God**- That is, "not truly rich towards God," or "lacking in the eyes of God" or "poor in the view of God."
