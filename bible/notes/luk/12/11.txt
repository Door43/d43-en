===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When they bring you** - It is not stated who brings them into judgment.
  * **before the synagogues** - "into the synagogues to question you before the religious leaders" 
  * **rulers ... authorities** - It may be necessary to combine these into one statement. AT: "other people who have power in the country" 
  * **in that hour** - "at that time" or "then"
