===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues speaking to his disciples.

  * **before the synagogues**  - "into the synagogues to question you before the religious leaders" (UDB)
  * **authorities**  - "other people who have power in the country" (UDB)
  * **in that hour**  - "at that time" or "then"
