===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Moreover know this** - "In addition, also know" or "But you must also remember" 
  * **had known the hour** - "had known when"
  * **he would not have let his house be broken into** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he would not have let the thief break into his house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **because you do not know the hour when the Son of Man comes** - The only similarity between a thief and the Son of Man is that people do not know when either one will come, so they need to be ready.
  * **do not know the hour when** - "do not know at what time"
  * **when the Son of Man comes** - Jesus was talking about himself. AT: "when I, the Son of Man, will come"
