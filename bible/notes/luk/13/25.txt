===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues to talk about entering into God's kingdom.

  * **Once the owner** "When the owner"
  * **the owner of the house** - This refers to the owner of the house with the narrow door in the previous verses. This is a metaphor for God as the ruler of the kingdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will stand outside** - Jesus was talking to a crowd. The form of "you" is plural. He is addressing them as if they will not enter through the narrow door into the kingdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **pound the door** - "hit on the door." This is an attempt to gain the owner's attention.
  * **Get away from me** - "Go away from me"
  * **evildoers** - "people who do evil"
