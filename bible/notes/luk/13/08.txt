===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus finishes telling his parable. This is the end of the part of the story that began in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:12:01|12:1]]. 

  * **Leave it alone** - "Do not do anything to the tree" or "Do not cut it down" 
  * **put manure on it** - "put manure in the soil." Manure is animal dung. People put it in the ground to make the soil good for plants and trees. AT: "put fertilizer on it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **If it bears fruit next year, good** - It may be helpful to state what will happen. AT: "If it has figs on it next year, we can allow it to keep growing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **cut it down** - The servant was making a suggestion; he was not giving a command to the owner. AT: "Tell me to cut it down" or "I will cut it down"
