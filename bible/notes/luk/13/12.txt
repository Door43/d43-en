===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus heals a woman in the synagogue.)
    * **he placed his hands on her**  - or, "he touched her"
    * **Be free from your weakness**- That is, "be cured of your sickness"
    * **Synagogue ruler was indignant**- That is, the ruler was angry, furious, offended and unhappy with what is happening on the Sabbath day. (He was uptight with the keeping of the rules of Sabbath rather than be upright with the release or healing of one freed from bondage).
    * **indignant**  - outraged
    * **answered and said**  - or, "responded by saying", or "responded and said"
    * **Immediately**- That is, on the very moment, not a gradual healing or freedom gained.
    * **come and be healed**  - or, "come for healing"
