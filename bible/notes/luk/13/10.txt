===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the next part of the story. These verses give background information about the setting of this part of the story and about a crippled woman who is introduced into the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Now** - This word marks a new part of the story.
  * **during the Sabbath** - "on a Sabbath day." Some languages would say "a Sabbath" because we do not know which particular Sabbath day it was.
  * **Behold, a woman was there** - The word "behold" here alerts us to a new person in the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]])
  * **eighteen years** - "18 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **an evil spirit of weakness** - "an evil spirit that made her weak"
