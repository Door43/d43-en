===== translationNotes: =====

  * **His winnowing fork is in his hand** -  John speaks of the Christ coming to judge as if he were a farmer who is ready to separate wheat grain from chaff. AT: "He is holding a winnowing fork because he is ready" or "He is ready to judge like a farmer who is ready to winnow grain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **winnowing fork** - This is a tool for tossing wheat into the air to separate the wheat grain from the chaff. The heavier grain falls back down and the unwanted chaff is blown away by the wind. It is similar to a pitchfork.
  * **to thoroughly clear off his threshing floor** - The threshing floor is the place where wheat was stacked in preparation for threshing. To "clear off" the floor is an idiom that means to finish threshing the grain. AT: "to finish threshing his grain." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **to gather the wheat** - The wheat is the acceptable harvest that is kept and stored.
  * **burn up the chaff** - The chaff is not useful for anything, so people burn it up.
