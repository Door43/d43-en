===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Now it came about** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here. 
    * **while all the people were being baptized by John** - The phrase "all the people" refers to tho people present with John. This could be translated as "while John was baptizing all the people." (See:  [[en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
    * **Jesus also was baptized by John** - This could be translated as "John baptized Jesus, too." (See:  [[en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
    * **heaven opened up** - "the sky opened up" This is more than a simple clearing of clouds.
    * **the Holy Spirit came down on him** - "the Holy Spirit came down onto Jesus"
    * **dove** - A  dove is a small, gentle bird that people often used in the temple sacrifices and for food. It is similar to a pigeon.
    * **in bodily form like a dove** - "in physical form looking like a dove"
    * **a voice came out of heaven** - "someone spoke from heaven" This could be translated as "God spoke from heaven."
    * **You are my beloved Son**  - "You are my Son whom I love"
