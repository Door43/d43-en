===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **to be baptized**  - "for John to baptize them"
    * **Do not collect more money**  - "Do not ask for more money" or "Stop collecting more money" The tax collectors were collecting more money than they should have been. They should stop doing that as proof of their repentance.
    * **than you are supposed to** - "than you have been given authority to take"
