===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **to be baptized** - "for John to baptize them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Do not collect more money** - "Do not ask for more money." The tax collectors were collecting more money than they should have been. They should stop doing that.
  * **than you are supposed to** - "than you have been given authority to take"
