===== Translation Notes: =====

John responds to questions that people in the crowd ask him.

  * **asked him, saying**  - "asked him and said" or "asked John"
  * **answered and said to them**  - "answered them, saying" or "answered them" or "said"
  * **do likewise**  - "do the same thing." AT: "give food to someone who does not have any."
