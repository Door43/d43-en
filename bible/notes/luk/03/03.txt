===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And he **- "John"
  * **preaching a baptism of repentance**  - This could be translated as "preached that people should be baptized to show that they were turning away from their sins."
  * **for the forgiveness of sins**  - "so that their sins would be forgiven" or "so that God would forgive their sins." The repentance was for the forgiveness of sins.
