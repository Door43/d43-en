===== Translation Notes: =====

John finishes speaking to people who have come to him to be baptized.

  * **the ax has been placed against the root of the trees **- This word picture means that the punishment is about to start. AT: "It is as though the ax has already been placed against the root of the trees" or "God is like a man who has placed his ax against the root of the trees." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **every tree that does not produce good fruit is chopped down ** - AT: "He chops down every tree that does not produce good fruit." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **thrown into the fire **- AT: "throws it into the fire" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
