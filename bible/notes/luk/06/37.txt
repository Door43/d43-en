===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Do not judge **- That is, "do not judge people" or "do not make it your habit to judge people." Judge here has a negative meaning, that is to criticize or find fault. 
    * **you will not be judged**  - That is "you will certainly not be judged by God," or "God will surely not judge you."
    * **do not condemn **- That is, "do not condemn people," or 'do not make it your habit to condemn people." To condemn is declare someone as guilty and should be punished.**.**
    * **you will not be condemned**  - That is, "you will certainly not be condemned by God," or "God will surely not condemn you."
    * **judge**-That is, "to make a choice" about another's actions.
    * **Forgive**- That is, "forgive others," or "forgive people as a habit."
    * **you will be forgiven**- That is, "you will be forgiven by God," or "God will forgive you."
