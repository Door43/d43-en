===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to them** - "to the Pharisees"
  * **is it lawful ... save a life ... destroy it?** - Jesus wanted to correct the Pharisees' understanding that it was wrong to heal on the Sabbath. He asked the question as a contrast between doing good and doing evil in order to make it obvious which one is lawful and which one is wrong.   * **Stretch out your hand** - "Hold out your hand" or "Extend your hand"
  * **restored** - "healed"
