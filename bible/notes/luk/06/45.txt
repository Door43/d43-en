===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The good man**- That is, "the morally good person." Man refers to both male and female. 
    * **from the good treasure of his heart**- That is, "out of what is morally good in his heart" or "from the goodness that person keeps in his heart."
    * **brings forth that which is good**- That is, "produces life that is morally good, or "lives his life in a morally good way."
    * **the evil man**  - That is "the morally evil person." man refers to both male and female.
    * **from the evil treasure of his heart**- That is, "out of what is morally evil in his heart," or "from the evil that person keeps inn his heart."
    * **brings forth that which is evil**- That is, "produces life that is morally evil," or "lives his life in a morally evil way."
    * **For from the abundance of his heart  his mouth speaks**  - That is, "What a person keeps in his heart is what a person will talk about," or "what a person highly value in his heart will show itself in how he speaks."
