===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues to teach his disciples.)
    * **love your enemies**- This can also be rendered "care about your enemies." It does not mean to have affection for your enemies, or to be sexually attracted to your enemies. The word in the language of origin here refers to putting the other person's interests before your own and caring about what happens to them regardless of how they make you feel.
    * **do good to those who hate you**  - That is, "begin and continue on doing good to the people who are continually hating you" or "continue doing good to the people who continue to hate you." 
    * **bless those who curse**  - "Bless" here does not mean "to be happy with", but rather "to speak well of."
    * **Love…do good….bless…and pray-**  these are all commands to do now
    * **those who curse you**  - That is, "those who are cursing you," or "those who continue to curse you."
    * **mistreat**-Or, "say threats or false accusations"
    * **those who mistreat you**- That is, "those who keep on mistreating you" or "the people who continue to mistreat you" or "those who are mistreating you."
