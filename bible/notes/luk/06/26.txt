===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues to teach his disciples.)
    * **woe to you when** - “how terrible it will be for you ” or “trouble will come to you” or “how sad you will be” or “what sorrow you will have”
    * **when all men** - That is "when all people," or "when everyone."
    * **treated the false prophets in the same way**  - "spoke well of the false prophets"
