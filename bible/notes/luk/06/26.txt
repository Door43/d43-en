===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues to teach his disciples.

  * **Woe to you** - "How terrible it is for you " or "Trouble will come to you" or "How sad you should be" or "What sorrow you will have"
  * **when all men** - "when all people" or "when everyone"
  * **treated the false prophets in the same way** - "spoke well of the false prophets"
