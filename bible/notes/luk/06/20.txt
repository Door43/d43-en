===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **blessed are you who ...** - "You who ... receive God's favor" or "you who ... benefit" or "how good it is for you who ..."
    * **yours is the kingdom of God**  - "the kingdom of God belongs to you" or "you belong to God's kingdom" or "you have the right to be citizens of God's kingdom." Languages that don't have a word for kingdom might say "God is your king" or "God will be your ruler."
    * **you will laugh**  - "you will laugh with joy" or "you will be joyful"
