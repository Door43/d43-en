===== Translation Notes: =====



    * (Jesus continues to teach his disciples.)
    * **To him who strikes you** - "If anyone hits you."
    * **on the one cheek**  - "on one side of your face.
    * **offer him also the other**  - This can also be translated as, "turn your face so that he can hit the other side, too."
    * **Give to everyone who asks you** - This can also be translated as "If anyone asks you for something, give it to him." 
    * **do not ask him** - "do not require him" or "do not demand"
