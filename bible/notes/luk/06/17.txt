===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **with them** - "with the twelve he had chosen" or "with his twelve apostles"
    * **and to be healed** - AT: "and for Jesus to heal them." or "and Jesus healed them." (See [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]], [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **troubled with unclean spirits** - "bothered by unclean spirits." AT: "controlled by unclean spirits" or "held captive by unclean spirits." See the UDB for a translation using the active voice. 
    * **were also healed** - "Jesus also healed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **power to heal was coming out from him** - AT: "he had power to heal people."
