===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Though Jesus especially addresses his disciples, there were many people around who listen.

  * **with them** - "with the twelve he had chosen" or "with his twelve apostles"
  * **to be healed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "for Jesus to heal them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **People who were troubled with unclean spirits were also healed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Jesus also healed people who were troubled with unclean spirits" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **troubled with unclean spirits** - "bothered by unclean spirits" or "controlled by evil spirits"
  * **power to heal was coming out from him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he had power to heal people" or "he used his power to heal people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
