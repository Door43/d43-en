===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **No man tears** - "No one tears" or "People never rip" (UDB)
    * **a new garment** - "a new garment that has not shrunk yet"
    * **mend** - "repair"
    * **would tear the new cloth**  - "would tear the new garment" or "would ruin the new garment by tearing it" (UDB).
    * **would not fit with** - "would not match" or "would not be the same as"
