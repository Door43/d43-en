===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold a man full of leprosy was there** - The word "behold" alerts us to a new person in the story. This could also be translated as "There was a man there who was full of leprosy."
 * **he fell on his face** - "he bowed down to the ground" (UDB) or "he knelt and touched the ground with his face" 
 * **implored him** - "begged him" or "pleaded with him" (UDB)
 * **if you are willing** - "if you want to"
