===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **word of God**  - This phrase usually refers to the Scriptures, but here it means that Jesus was giving the people a message from God.
  * **standing by the lake of Genessaret**- Or, "standing by the lake of Genessaret for a while."
  * **lake of Genessaret**  - This refers to the Sea of Galilee.
  * **lying**  - Or, "having been laid."
  * **washing their nets**  - That is, they were cleaning them to be used again to catch fish.
  * **asked him to put it out into the water**  - This could also be translated as "asked Peter to move the boat farther into the water." Peter probably got into the boat with Jesus to do this.
  * **Jesus sat down and taught the people**  - Sitting was the normal position for a teacher. When people taught or spoke with authority, they usually sat down.
  * **taught the people out of the boat**  - Or, "was teaching the people from the boat for a while." Jesus was in the boat a short distance from the shore and he was speaking to the people who were on the shore.
