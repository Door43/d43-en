===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Now it happened** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **lying by the edge of the lake**  - 
  * **washing their nets**  - They were cleaning their fishing nets in order to use them again them again to catch fish.
  * **asked him to put it out into the water**  - This could also be translated as "asked Peter to move the boat farther into the water." Peter probably got into the boat with Jesus to do this.
  * **Jesus sat down and taught the people**  - Sitting was the normal position for a teacher. 
  * **taught the people out of the boat**  - That is, "taught the people while he sat in the boat." Jesus was in the boat a short distance from the shore and he was speaking to the people who were on the shore.
