===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then**  - This refers to after John baptized Jesus. It could also be translated as "Then after Jesus was baptized."
  * **being full of the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "being fully guided by the Holy Spirit" or "in obedience to the Holy Spirit." The phrases "full of the Holy Spirit" and "guided by the Holy Spirit" are metaphors for obeying what the Spirit tells a person to do. (See [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **returned from the Jordan River**  - That is, "left the Jordan River."
  * **was led by the Spirit**  - That is, "was continuously guided by the Spirit." The Holy Spirit led, or guided, Jesus to walk in a particular place, and He guided Jesus the entire time that Jesus was in the wilderness.
  * **in the wilderness**  - This can also be translated as, "into the desert."
  * **for forty days**  - That is, "for a period of forty days" or "for a length of forty days."
  * **and was tempted there by the devil**  - This can be translated as "was continuously tempted by the devil to disobey God" or "was being directed by the devil to disobey God" or "and the devil kept tempting him."
  * **he did not eat**  - "He" refers to Jesus.
  * **at the end of that time**  - That is, "at the end of those forty days" or "after those forty days."
