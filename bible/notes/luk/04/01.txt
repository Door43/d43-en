===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then**  - This refers to after John baptized Jesus. It could also be translated as "Then after Jesus was baptized."
  * **returned from the Jordan River**  - That is, "left the Jordan River."
  * **was led by the Spirit**  - This can also be translated in active voice as "the Spirit led him." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active]])
  * **for forty days**  - That is, "for a period of forty days" or "for a length of forty days."
  * **was tempted there by the devil**  - It may be helpful to add "was tempted by the devil to disobey God." It is not clear if he was tempted throughout the time, or at the end of the time. This can also be translated in active voice as "the devil tempted him there." 
  * **he did not eat**  - "He" refers to Jesus.
  * **at the end of that time**  - That is, "at the end of those forty days" or "after those forty days."
