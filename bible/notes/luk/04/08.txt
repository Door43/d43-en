===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **answered and said to him**  - That is, "responded to him" or "replied to him."
    * **It is written** - That is, “it is written in the scriptures” or “the scriptures say” or “in the scriptures God said.”
    * **you** - This refers to the people in the OT who received God's Law. You could use the singular form of 'you' because each person was to obey it, or you could use the plural form of 'you' because all of the people were to obey it.  (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
    * **serve**  - This literally means "offer sacrifices and prayers" like a priest. In this context it also means "do the will of" or "obey" like a servant.
    * **him**  - This refers to the Lord.
