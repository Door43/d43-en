===== Translation Notes: =====

People follow Jesus and he tells them why he will leave Capernaum.

  * **When daybreak came** - "At sunrise" or "At dawn"
  * **a solitary place** - "a deserted place" or "a place where no people lived" or "a place where there were no people"
  * **to many other cities** - AT: "to the people in many other cities."
  * **this is the reason I was sent here** - AT: "this is the reason God sent me here" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
