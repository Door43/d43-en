===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **When daybreak came**  - That is, "At sunrise" or "At dawn" or "Early the next morning." (UDB)
    * **a solitary place** - That is, "a deserted place" or "a place where no people lived" or "a place where there were no people."
    * **They kept urging him**  - They repeatedly said to him, or several people in the crowd said to him. Or, "they were restraining" him.
    * **to many other cities** - That is, "to the people in many other cities."
    * **this is the reason I was sent here** - That is, "this is what I was sent to do" (UDB). It could also be translated with an active clause as in "this is the reason God sent me here." (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
