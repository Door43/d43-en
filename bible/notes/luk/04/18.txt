===== Translation Notes: =====

This is what Jesus read in the synagogue in Nazareth. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:61:01|Isaiah 61:1-2]])

  * **The Spirit of the Lord is upon me** - By someone's saying this, he is claiming to speak the words of God. AT: "God is with me in a special way." 
  * **proclaim freedom to the captives** - AT: "tell people who are being held captive that they can go free" or "set free the prisoners of war"
  * **recovery of sight to the blind** - "give sight to the blind" or "make the blind be able to see again"
  * **set free those who are oppressed** - "set free those who are treated harshly"
  * **proclaim the favorable year of the Lord** - AT: "announce that this is the year that the Lord will show his kindness" or "tell everyone that the Lord is ready to bless his people"
