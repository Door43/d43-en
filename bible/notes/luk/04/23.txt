===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in your hometown** - This was in Nazareth, the town in which Jesus had grown up. 
  * **no prophet is accepted in his own country** - Jesus was rebuking the people for refusing to believe him just because they thought they knew him.
  * **own country** - AT: "own town" or "hometown"
