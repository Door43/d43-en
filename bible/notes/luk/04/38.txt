===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus heals Simon's mother-in-law in Capernaum.

  * **was suffering with a high fever** - AT: "was sick and her skin was very hot"
  * **Simon's mother-in-law** - "the mother of Simon's wife"
  * **rebuked the fever** - "spoke sternly to the fever" or "commanded the fever to leave her" (UDB). AT: "commanded that her skin should become cool" or "commanded the sickness to leave her."
