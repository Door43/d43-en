===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **she was suffering with a high fever**  - In some languages people would say "she was sick and her skin was very hot."
    * **Simon's mother-in-law**  - That is, "the mother of Simon's wife."
    * **they pleaded to him on her behalf**  - That is, "they pleaded with Jesus to heal her."
