===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **high fever**  - That is, "big fever" or "bad fever."
    * **mother-in-law**  - This is the spouse's mother. In this case, it refers to the mother of Simon's wife.
    * **they entreated him on her behalf**  - That is, they asked Jesus about her.
