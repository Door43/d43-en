===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus answers the devil a third time.

  * **It is said**  - Jesus quotes Deuteronomy. AT: "Moses has said" or "Moses has said in the scriptures"  [[:en:bible:notes:deu:06:16|6:16]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **You must not test the Lord your God** - Jesus quoted this Scripture to say why he would not put God to the test by jumping down from the temple. This command is for God's people.  AT: "Do not test the Lord your God." 
  * **until another time**  - "until another occasion"
