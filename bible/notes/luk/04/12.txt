===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jesus answering said to him **- That is, "Jesus responded to the devil" or "Jesus replied to the devil."
    * **it is said**  - This could be translated as "it has been said" or "someone said" or "a prophet said."
    * **You must not test ****the Lord your God **- This can also be translated as "do not test the Lord your God." Jesus quoted this Scripture as a response to the devil that he (Jesus) should not put God to the test by jumping down from the temple. This command is for God's people.
    * **when the devil had finished tempting**  - That is, "when every temptation had been brought to an end."
    * **another time**  - Or, "an appropriate time."
