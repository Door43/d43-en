===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jesus answering said to him** - That is, "Jesus said to the devil" or "Jesus replied."
    * **it is said**  - This could also be translated as "The scriptures say" or "It is written." 
    * **You must not test the Lord your God** - This can also be translated as "Do not test the Lord your God." Jesus quoted this Scripture to say why he would not put God to the test by jumping down from the temple. This command is for God's people.
    * **until another time**  - This could also be translated as "until another occasion."
