===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he went down - ** That is, he went downhill. There was an elevation change.
    * **was teaching -**  One could say Jesus "was continually teaching."
    * **astonished**  - That is, "overwhelmed."
    * **were astonished**  - One could say "were continually astonished."
    * **he spoke with authority **- This literally means, "his word was with authority" or "his message was continually having authority."
