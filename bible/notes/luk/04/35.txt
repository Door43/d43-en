===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Jesus rebuked the demon, saying**  - "Jesus scolded the demon, saying" or "Jesus sternly said to the demon" 
  * **come out of him**  - AT: "leave him alone" or "stop bothering him"
  * **What kind of words are these** - The people were expressing how amazed they were that Jesus had the authority to command demons to leave a person. AT: "These are amazing words" or "His words are amazing!"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **he commands the unclean spirits with authority and power** - "he has authority and power to command the unclean spirits"
  * **news about him began to spread** - "reports about Jesus began to spread" or "people spread the news about Jesus"
