===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jesus rebuked the demon, saying**  - That is, "Jesus scolded the demon, saying" or "Jesus sternly said to the demon." 
    * **come out of him**  - If being "in" a person is a metaphor for controlling a person, then this could also be translated as "leave him alone."
    * **What kind of words are these** - This is a rhetorical question. The people were expressing how amazed they were that Jesus had the authority to command demons to leave a person. This could also be translated as "These are amazing words" or "His words are amazing!"
    * **he commands the unclean spirits with power and authority** - That is, "with power and authority Jesus commands the unclean spirits" or "he has power and authority to command the unclean spirits."
    * **news about him began to spread** - That is, "reports about Jesus began to spread" or "people spread the news about Jesus."
