===== Important Terms: =====


===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **his goods are in peace** - That is, "his possessions are safe" or "no one can steal this things."
    * **armed, armor-**  Refers to body armor, defensive and offensive weapons
    * **not with me**- That is, "not with me in purpose or mission"
