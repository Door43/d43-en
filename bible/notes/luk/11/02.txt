===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them** - "Jesus said to his disciples"
  * **When you pray say, 'Father'** - Jesus is commanding the disciples to honor the name of God the Father by addressing him as "Father" when praying to him.
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **may your name be sanctified** - "cause everyone to honor your name." "Name" often refers to the entire person. AT: "may all people honor you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **May your kingdom come** - The action of God ruling over everyone is spoken of as if it were God himself. AT: "May you come and rule over everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
