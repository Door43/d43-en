===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **tested**-Word can mean to test or to tempt, depending on the context. Here it is tempt.
    * **sought from him **- That is, "tried to have him do" or "demanded from him."
    * **a sign from heaven**  - That is, "a miracle that is from heaven."
    * **But he knew their thoughts **- That is, "he knew what they were thinking."
    * **Every kingdom divided against itself **- That is, "every kingdom that has fights within itself," or "every kingdom that has people fighting among themselves.""
    * **is made desolate**  - That is, it is turned into a wasteland.
    * **a house divided against itself falls **- That is, " a family with members opposing each other falls."
