===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **beelzebul**  â€‹-Or, "lord of flies or filth." Another name for Satan. Another idea here would be for the translator to pick the name of a tribal god, who approximates what the Jews meant by Beelzebul.
    * **if Satan is divided against itself**- That is, "if Satan and members of his kingdom are fighting among themselves" or "if Satan and members of his kingdom are fighting each other."
    * **how will his kingdom stand**- The implied answer to this question is a no. That is, "Satan's kingdom will not stand," or "Satan's kingdom will not continue to exist."
    * **since you say **- That is, "because you say, " or "for you say."
    * **I cast out demons**- That is, "I expel demons out of people," or "I drive out demons out of people."
