===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **insulted him**
- "insulted Jesus"

  * **Are not you the Christ?**
- This question is intended not to gather information but to express doubt that Jesus really was the Christ

  * **the other answered**
- "the other criminal answered"

  * **and rebuking him said**
 - "rebuking Jesus by saying"
