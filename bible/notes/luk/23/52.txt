===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This man** - "Joseph"
  * **approaching Pilate, asked for** - "went to Pilate and requested"
  * **for the body of Jesus** - "to take the body of Jesus and bury it"
  * **He took it down** - "Joseph took Jesus' body from the cross"
  * **wrapped it in fine linen** - "wrapped the body in a fine linen cloth." This was the normal burial custom at that time.
  * **that was cut in stone** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "which someone had cut in a rock cliff" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **where no one had ever been laid** - This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "No one had ever before put a body in that tomb"
