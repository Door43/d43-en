===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he was very glad** - "Herod was very glad"
  * **he had wanted to see him** - "Herod had wanted to see Jesus"
  * **He had heard about him** - "Herod had heard about Jesus"
  * **he hoped** - "Herod hoped"
  * **to see some miracle done by him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "to see him perform some kind of miracle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Herod questioned Jesus in many words** - "Herod asked Jesus a lot of questions"
  * **answered him nothing** - "did not reply" or "did not give Herod an answer"
  * **stood** - "were standing there"
  * **violently accusing him** - "severely accusing Jesus" or "accusing him of all kinds of crimes"
