===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now**  - Can be omitted
  * **he was very glad**  - "Herod was very glad"
  * **he had wanted to see him**  - "Herod had wanted to see Jesus"
  * **he had heard about him**  - "Herod had heard about Jesus"
  * **he hoped**  - "Herod hoped"
  * **see some miracle done by him**  - This could be stated in active voice: "he wanted to see him perform a miracle" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
  * **stood** - "were standing there"
  * **accusing him** - "accusing Jesus"
