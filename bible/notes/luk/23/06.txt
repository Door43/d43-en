===== translationNotes: =====

  * **heard this** - "heard that Jesus began to teach in Galilee"
  * **whether** - "if"
  * **the man** - This refers to Jesus.
  * **he discovered** - "Pilate found out"
  * **he was under Herod's authority** - The passage does not state the implied fact that Herod was the ruler of Galilee. AT: "Jesus was under the authority of Herod because Herod ruled over Galilee" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he sent** - "Pilate sent"
  * **who himself** - This refers to Herod.
  * **in those days** - "at that time"
