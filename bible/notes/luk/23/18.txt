===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verse 19 tells us background information about who Barrabas is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **they cried out all together** - "All the people in the crowd shouted"
  * **Away with this man** - "Take this man away!" They are asking for Jesus to be killed. AT: "Take this man away and execute him!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **release to us** - "us" refers to the crowd only, and not to Pilate and his soldiers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **Barabbas was a man ... for murder** - This is background information that Luke gives about who Barrabas was. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **who had been put into prison** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom the Romans had put in prison" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for** - "because of his involvement in" or "because of his crime of"
  * **a certain rebellion in the city** - "trying to persuade the people of the city to rebel against the Roman government"
