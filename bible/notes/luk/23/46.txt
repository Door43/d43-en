===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Crying** - "Shouting"
  * **into your hands I commit my spirit** - "I entrust my spirit to your care." This could be translated as "I give my spirit to you, knowing you will care for it."
  * **Having said this** - "After Jesus said this"
  * **he died** - "Jesus died"
  * **what was done** - "what had happened"
