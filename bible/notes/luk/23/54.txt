===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the day of preparation**
- "the day when people got ready for the Jewish day of rest called the Sabbath" (UDB)

  * **the Sabbath was dawning - "**It was soon going to be sunset, the start of the Sabbath." (UDB)
- explanation

  * **who had come with him out of Galilee**
- who had traveled with Jesus from the region of Galilee"

  * **followed after**
- "followed behind Joseph and the men who were with him"

  * **and saw the tomb**  - "and the women saw the tomb"
  * **and how his body was laid**  - "and saw how the men laid Jesus' body inside the tomb"
  * **They returned **- Could be translated as, "The women went to the houses where they were staying" (UDB)
  * **and prepared spices and ointments **- Could be translated as "and prepared spices and ointments to prepare Jesus' body for burial."
  * **they did no work **- "the women did no work"
  * **just as the law required **- according to the law
