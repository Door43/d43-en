===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pilate asked him**  - "Pilate asked Jesus"
  * **You say so**  - This idiom means "what you said is correct." It could also be translated as "It is just as you have asked me" (UDB). (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **multitudes**  - "the crowd of people"
  * **I find no fault in this man **- "I don't find this man guilty of anything"
  * **stirs up**  - "caused trouble among"
  * **beginning from Galilee and even to this place**  - "He began causing trouble in Galilee and now is causing trouble here"
