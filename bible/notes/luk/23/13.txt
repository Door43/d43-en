===== translationNotes: =====

  * **called together the chief priests and the rulers and the crowd of people** - "called the chief priests and the rulers and the crowd of people to come meet together"
  * **the crowd of people** - It is not likely that Pilate asked a crowd to come. The crowd was probably still there waiting to see what would happen to Jesus. AT: "the crowd that was still there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **this man** - This refers to Jesus.
  * **like a man who** - "saying that he"
  * **I, having questioned him before you** - "I have questioned Jesus in your presence, and." It is implied that they were witnesses to the proceedings. AT: "I have questioned Jesus with you here as witnesses, and" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **find no fault in this man** - "do not think that he is guilty"
