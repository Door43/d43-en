====== Luke Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Jesus say the poor widow put more in the treasury than all of them?

A. Because she gave out of her poverty and the others gave out of their abundance [21:4].

Q? What did Jesus say would happen to the temple in Jerusalem?

A. It would be torn down and not one stone would be left on another [21:6].

Q? What two questions did they ask Jesus about the temple?

A. When will these things happen, and what will be the sign that they are about to happen [21:7]?

Q? Jesus warned that many deceivers will come saying what?

A. "I am he," and "The time is near. [21:8]"

Q? What terrible events did Jesus say would happen first?

A. Wars, earthquakes, famines, plagues, and great signs from heaven [21:11].

Q? What opportunity will the persecution of believers create?

A. An opportunity for their testimony [21:13].

Q? Who will hate Jesus' followers?

A. Parents, brothers, relatives, friends, and "everyone" [21:16-17].

Q? What event would indicate that Jerusalem's destruction was near?

A. When Jerusalem was surrounded by armies [21:20].

Q? What did Jesus tell people to do who saw that the destruction of Jerusalem was near?

A. Flee to the mountains, leave the city, and do not enter the city [21:21].

Q? Jesus called the days of the destruction of Jerusalem the days of what?

A. The days of justice, fulfilling all the things written [21:22].

Q? How long will Jerusalem be trampled by the Gentiles?

A. Until the times of the Gentiles are fulfilled [21:24].

Q? What signs did Jesus say would precede his coming with power and great glory?

A. Signs in the sun, moon, and stars, and distress of the nations on the earth [21:25].

Q? What example did Jesus give of how his listeners knew when a season had come?

A. The fig tree - when it sprouts buds they knew that summer was near [21:30].

Q? What will pass away?

A. Heaven and earth [21:33].

Q? What will never pass away?

A. Jesus' words [21:33].

Q? What did Jesus warn his listeners //not// to do since that day will come suddenly?

A. Do not be troubled with debauchery, drunkenness, and the worries of life [21:34].

Q? What did Jesus warn his listeners to do since that day will come suddenly?

A. Be alert and praying [21:36].
