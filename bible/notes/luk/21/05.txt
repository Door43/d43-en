===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus switches from talking about the widow to teaching about the temple.

  * **offerings** - "things people had given to God"
  * **these things that you see** - This refers to the beautiful temple and its decorations.
  * **the days will come when** - "there will be a time when" or "someday" 
  * **not one stone will be left on another** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "enemies will not leave any stone on top of another" (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]).
  * **not one stone will be left ... not be torn down** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "every stone will be removed from its place and they will all be torn down"
  * **which will not be torn down** - This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "They will all be torn down" or "Enemies will tear down every stone" (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
