===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues speaking to his disciples about the future.)
  * **these things** - This refers to the terrible things that Jesus had said would happen in verses 8-11.
  * **they will lay their hands on you** - "they will arrest you" or "they will grab you." Some languages will need to say who will do these things: "people will lay their hands on you" or "enemies will lay their hands on you." Other languages might use passive verbs: "You will be arrested."  (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
  * **you** - Jesus was talking to his disciples. The word "you" is plural. (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou|Forms of "You"]])
  * **delivering you over to the synagogues**  - "handing you over to the leaders of the synagogues
  * **and prisons**  - "and putting you in prison"
  * **for your testimony**  - "for you to tell them your testimony about me"
