===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Son of Man** - Jesus is refers to himself. AT: "me, the Son Man"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **coming in a cloud** - "coming down in a cloud"
  * **with power and great glory** - Here "power" probably refers to his authority to judge the world. Here "glory" may refer to a bright light. God sometimes shows his greatness with a very bright light. AT: "powerfully and gloriously" or "and he will be powerful and very glorious"
  * **stand up** - Sometimes when people are afraid, they crouch down in order to avoid being seen or hurt. When they are no longer afraid, they get up. AT: "stand up with confidence"
  * **lift up your heads** - By lifting their heads to look up, they would be able to see their rescuer coming to them. AT: "look up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **because your deliverance is coming near** - God, who delivers, is spoken of as if he were the deliverance that he causes. AT: "because your deliverer is coming to you" or "because God will soon save you"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
