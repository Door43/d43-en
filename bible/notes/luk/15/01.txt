===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **were coming to Jesus**  - That is, "were drawing near to Jesus," or "kept gathering around near Jesus." This was something they did many times.
    * **to listen to him **- That is, "in order to hear him teach" or "to listen to what he would say."
    * **grumbled to each other**  - That is, "complained to each other" or "began to complain to each other."
    * **This man welcomes sinners**  - This can also be translated as, "This man admits sinners into his presence" or "This man accepts sinners" or "This man associates with sinners."
    * **even eats with them** - The word 'even' shows that they thought it was bad enough that Jesus allowed sinners to come to him, but it was worse that he would eat with them.
