===== Important Terms: =====




===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **packed up**  - Some languages may need to say "packed his things" or "put his things in his bag."
    * **he wasted his money**  - This could also be translated as, "he spent his money foolishly." Some languages might translate it as, "he threw away his money" or "he ate his money."
    * **with wildly extravagant living**  - This could also be translated as, "by spending his money and not saving any" or "by spending his money on things he did not really need."
    * **And not many days later**  - That is, "Not long after the youngest son asked for his portion of the inheritance,"
    * **There a great famine happened**  - This can also be translated as, "There was a severe famine" or "there was an extreme lack of food" or there was not enough food."
    * **famine**  - A famine is a time when there is little food. Often it is the result of little rain and the failure of crops to grow well.
    * **to be in need**  - That is, "to lack what he needed" or "to not have enough."
