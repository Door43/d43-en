===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues telling his parable.

    * **He went** - The word "he" refers to the younger son.
    * **hired himself out to**  - This can be translated as  "took a job with" or "began to work for."
    * **one of the citizens of that country** - "a man of that country"
    * **to feed pigs**  - "to give food to his pigs"
    * **would gladly have eaten** - "wished very much that he could eat"
    * **bean pods** - These are the husks of beans that grow on the carob tree. This can be translated as "carob pods" or "pods" or "bean husks."
