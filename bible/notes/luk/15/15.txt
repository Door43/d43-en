===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **And he** - The word "he" refers to the younger son.
    * **hired himself out to**  - This could also be translated as  "took a job with" or "began to work for."
    * **one of the citizens of that country**  - That is, "a man of that country" or "a native resident of the country."
    * **to feed pigs**  - That is, "to give food to pigs" or "to give pigs their food."
    * **would gladly have eaten** - That is, "was longing to eat" or "wished very much that he could eat."
    * **bean pods** - These are the husks of beans that grow on the carob tree. This could also be translated as 'carob pods' or 'pods' or 'bean husks.'
