===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **his elder son**  - That is, "the father's oldest son." This can also be translated as "his first son."
    * **came and drew near** - This could also be translated as "came back and approached" or "came near."
    * **servant**  - Thew word "servant" here refers to a young male servant. Apparently he was one who worked in or around the house.
