===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **servant**  - The word that is translated here as “servant” is normally translated as “boy.” It may indicate that the servant was very young.
    * **what these things might be** - "what was happening" (UDB)
    * **the fattened calf** - This can be translated as “the best calf” or “the young bull we have been been giving lots of food to” or “the young animal we have been making fat.”
