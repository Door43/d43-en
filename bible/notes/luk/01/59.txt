===== Translation Notes: =====

Zechariah and Elizabeth obey God by naming their son John like the angel told them.  

    * **Then, on the eighth day** - "When the baby was eight days old" or AT: "Then, on the eighth day after the baby was born" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeday]])
    * **they** - AT: "Zechariah and Elizabeth's friends and relatives" or  "people"
    * **to circumcise the baby** - One person circumcised the baby, and the others were there to celebrate with the family. AT:  "for the baby's circumcision ceremony."
    * **they would have called him** - "they were going to name him" or "they wanted to give him the name"
    * **after the name of his father** - "his father's name"
    * **by this name** - "by that name" or "by the same name"
