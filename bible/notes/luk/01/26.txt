===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the angel Gabriel was sent by God** - That is, "God told the angel Gabriel to go" or, "God sent the angel Gabriel" as in the UDB. (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:active]])
    * **engaged to** - That is,  "pledged to" or, "promised to be married to." This meant that Mary's parents had made a commitment for her to marry Joseph.
    * **came to her** - That is, "came to where Mary was" or, "went to where Mary was."
    * **Greetings** - The original text says literally, “Rejoice” or, "Be glad." This was a common greeting.
    * **highly favored one** - This could also be translated as, “you who are highly favored" or, "you who have received grace" or, "you who have received kindness.”
    * **very troubled** - This is literally, "stirred up". It can be also be translated as "perplexed" or, "confused".
    * **what kind of greeting this might be** - That is, "why the angel greeted her this way" or, "what the angel meant by these words" as in the UDB. Mary understood the meaning of the individual words, but she did not understand why the angel said them to her.
