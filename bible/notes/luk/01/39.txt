===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** - This word introduces a new part of the story. Consider how your language does this. Some translations might not use an introductory word here.
  * **got up** -  AT: "started out" or AT: "got ready"
  * **the hill country** - "the hilly area" or "the mountainous part of Israel" 
  * **where she went** -  "and she went" or AT: "when she arrived, she went"
  * **leaped** - "moved suddenly"
