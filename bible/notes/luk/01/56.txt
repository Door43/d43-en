===== Translation Notes: =====

Mary returns home and Elizabeth gives birth to a son.

  * **three months** - See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]].
  * **returned to her house** - "Mary returned to her (Mary's) house" or "Mary returned to her own house"
  * **deliver her baby** - "give birth to her baby" or "bear her child"
  * **Her neighbors and relatives** - "Elizabeth's neighbors and relatives"
