===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Elizabeth delivers her baby and then Zechariah names their baby. 

  * **returned to her house** - "Mary returned to her (Mary's) house" or "Mary returned to her own house"
  * **Now** - This word marks the beginning of the next event in the story.
  * **Her neighbors and her relatives** - "Elizabeth's neighbors and relatives"
  * **shown his great mercy to her** - "been so kind to her"
