===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **spoke this prophecy** - Consider natural ways of introducing direct quotes in your language.  AT: "prophesied and said" or "and prophesied, and this is what he said"   (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations|Speech Quotations]])
  * **the God of Israel** - Israel here refers to the nation of Israel. Zechariah, as well as the people to whom he was talking, all belonged to the nation of Israel.  AT: "the God who reigns over Israel" or "the God whom Israel worships" 
  * **has come to us** - AT: "has come to help us" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **his people**  - "God's people"
