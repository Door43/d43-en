===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Zechariah tells what will happen with his son John.

  * **His father Zechariah was filled with the Holy Spirit** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Holy Spirit filled his father Zechariah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **His father** - John's father
  * **prophesied, saying** - Consider natural ways of introducing direct quotes in your language. AT: "prophesied and said" or "prophesied, and this is what he said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **the God of Israel** - "Israel" here refers to the nation of Israel. The relationship between God and Israel could be stated more directly. AT: "the God who reigns over Israel" or "the God whom Israel worships" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **his people** - "God's people"
