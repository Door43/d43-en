===== Translation Notes: =====

Zechariah continues to prophesy.

  * **to show mercy towards** - "to be merciful to" or "to act according to his mercy toward"
  * **remember** - Here the word "remember" means to keep a commitment or fulfill something.
  * **from the hand of our enemies** -  "Hand" refers to the harmful control or power a person has over another person. AT: "from the control of our enemies" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **without fear** - AT: "without being afraid of our enemies"
  * **in holiness and righteousness** - AT: "while we live in a holy and righteous way" or "to live holy lives and do what is right"
  * **before him** - "in his presence." AT:  "according to his will."
  * **all our days** - "our entire life"
