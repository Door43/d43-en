===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lowly** - "humble" or AT: "poor." Mary did not have high social standing.
  * **paid attention** - "thought about" or "remembered." This is not an issue of God's ability to remember, but his choosing to remember. AT: "did not forget." 
  * **From now on** - "Now and in the future"
  * **the Mighty One** - AT: "God, the Powerful One"
  * **his name** - Here "name" refers to the entire person of God. AT: "he." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
