===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Zechariah finishes prophesying.

  * **our God** - Throughout these verses "us" and "our" are inclusive. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **because of the tender mercy of our God** - AT: "because God is compassionate and merciful to us"
  * **to shine on** - AT: "to give knowledge to" or "to give spiritual light to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **them who sit in darkness** - AT: "people who do not know the truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the shadow of death** - AT: "who are about to die" or "who are afraid that they will soon die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **guide** - "direct." or AT: "teach." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **our feet** - AT: "us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **into the path of peace** - "into the way of peace." Make sure your translation of this fits with how you translate "our feet."  AT: "into a peaceful life" or "to walk along a path that leads to peace"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
