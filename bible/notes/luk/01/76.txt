===== Translation Notes: =====

Zechariah directs his prophesies to his newborn son.

  * **will be called the prophet** - This means he actually will be the prophet, not just called a prophet, and people will know him as such. AT: "will be the prophet." 
  * **of the Most High** - AT: "who serves the Most High" or "who speaks for God Most High"
  * **how they can be saved through the forgiveness of their sins**  - AT: "how God will save them by forgiving their sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
