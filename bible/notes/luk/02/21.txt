===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The laws God gave the Jewish believers told them when to circumcise a boy baby and what sacrifice the parents had to bring.

  * **When it was the end of the eighth day** - This phrase shows the passing of time before this new event. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **the end of the eighth day** - "it was eight days after his birth" or "he was eight days old"
  * **he was named** - Joseph and Mary gave him his name.
  * **the name he had been given by the angel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the name the angel had called him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
