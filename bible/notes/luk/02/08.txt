===== translationNotes: =====

  * **An angel of the Lord** - "An angel from the Lord" or "An angel who served the Lord"
  * **appeared to them** - "came to the shepherds"
  * **the glory of the Lord** - The source of the bright light was the glory of the Lord, which appeared at the same time as the angel.
