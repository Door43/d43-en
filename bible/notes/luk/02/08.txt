===== Translation Notes: =====

Shepherds hear the news about Jesus' birth.

  * **In that same region** - "In that area" or AT: "Near Bethlehem"
  * **keeping watch over their flocks** - "taking care of their flocks" or "guarding their flocks in order to keep them safe"
  * **flocks of sheep** - "groups of sheep"
  * **at night** - AT: "after the sun had set and it was dark"
  * **an angel of the Lord** - AT: "an angel from the Lord" or "an angel who served the Lord"
  * **appeared to them** - "came to them"
  * **the glory of the Lord** - This means the bright light that is all around the Lord was also around the angel.
