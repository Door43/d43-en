===== Translation Notes: =====

Simeon continues to praise God for baby Jesus. 

  * **your salvation** - This refers to Jesus, the one through whom God would save people. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **have prepared** - "have planned" or "caused to happen"
  * **in the sight of all peoples** - "for all people groups to see"
  * **He will be a light for revealing truth** - AT: "this child will enable people to perceive accurately the truth of God, as light allows people to see accurately" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He will be** - "He" refers to the one who will provide salvation.
  * **will be the glory of your people Israel** - AT: "he will be the reason that glory will come to your people Israel"
