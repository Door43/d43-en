===== Translation Notes: =====

Simeon meets baby Jesus at the temple.

  * **devout** - "devoted to God" or "faithful to God"
  * **comforter of Israel** - It is another name for the "Messiah" or "Christ."  AT: "the one who would comfort Israel." 
  * **the Holy Spirit was upon him** - "the Holy Spirit was with him." God was with him in a special way and gave him wisdom and direction in his life. 
  * **It had been revealed to him by the Holy Spirit** - AT: "The Holy Spirit had shown him" or "The Holy Spirit had told him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he would not die before he had seen the Lord's Christ** - The word "Lord" here refers to God.  AT: "he would see the Lord's Christ before dying" or "he would see the Lord's Messiah and die only after that."
