===== Translation Notes: =====

Anna meets baby Jesus at the temple.

  * **Anna** - "Anna" is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Phanuel** - "Phanuel" is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **very advanced in years** - AT: "very old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **seven years** - "7 years" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **after her marriage**  - "after she married him"
  * **a widow for eighty-four years** - Possible meanings are 1) she had been a widow for 84 years or 2) she was a widow and was now 84 years old. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **never left the temple** - This is probably an exaggeration meaning that she spent so much time in the temple that it seemed as though she never left it. AT: "was always at the temple" or "was often at the temple." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **with fasting and prayers** - "by abstaining from food and by praying"
  * **came up to them** - "approached them" or "went to Mary and Joseph"
  * **the redemption of Jerusalem** - Here the word "redemption" is used to refer to the the person who would do it. AT: "the one who would redeem Jerusalem" or "the person who would bring God's blessings and favor back to Jerusalem." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
