===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **his parents** - That is, "Jesus' parents."
    * **they went up **- Jerusalem was on a hill, so people had to physically climb to reach the gates.
    * **the full number of days of**** the feast **- That is, "when the days of celebrating the feast were over" or "after celebrating the feast for the required number of days."
    * **Jesus stayed behind** - Greek shows Jesus made the immediate decision himself to stay behind. He purposely intended to interact with the Jewish religious leaders.
    * **they assumed** - That is, "they thought."
    * **started looking** - Perhaps better as started to continuously look for him. Greek shows they went into a continuous search mode until they found him. As His parents, they were worried. Their child is missing.
