===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Do not be afraid** - AT: "Stop being afraid"
    * **because I am about to announce to you good news** - "for I bring good news to you" or "I will tell you some good news"
    * **that will bring great joy to all the people** - AT: "that will make all the people very happy."
    * **all the people** - Some understand this to refer to the Jewish people. Others understand it to refer to all people.
    * **the city of David** - AT: "in Bethlehem, the city of David"
    * **This is the sign that will be given to you** - AT: "God will give you this sign" or "You will see this sign from God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **sign** - This could either be a sign to prove that what the angel was saying was true, or it could be a sign that would help the shepherds recognize the baby. AT: "proof" or "distinguishing mark"
    * **wrapped in strips of cloth** - "wrapped in pieces of cloth." AT: "who was wrapped comfortably in a blanket."
