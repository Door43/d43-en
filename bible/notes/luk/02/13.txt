===== translationNotes: ===== 

  * **a great, heavenly army** - The word "heavenly army" here could refer to a literal army of angels, or it could be a metaphor for an organized group of angels. AT: "a large group of angels from heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **praising God** - "giving praise to God"
  * **Glory to God in the highest** - Possible meanings are 1) "Give honor to God in the highest place" or 2) "Give the highest honor to God."
  * **may there be peace on earth among people with whom he is pleased** - "may those people on earth with whom God is pleased have peace"
