===== Translation Notes: =====

After the shepherds see baby Jesus, they tell people what the angels said about him.

  * **what had been said to them**  - AT: "what the angels had told the shepherds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **this child** - "the baby"
  * **what was spoken to them by the shepherds** - "what the shepherds told them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **treasuring them in her heart**  - A treasure is something that is very valuable or precious. Mary considered the things she was told about her son to be very precious. AT: "carefully remembering them" or "joyfully remembering them." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **returned** - AT: "went back to the sheep fields."
  * **glorifying and praising God** - AT: "talking about and praising God's greatness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
