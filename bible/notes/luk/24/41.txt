===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **incredulous with joy**  - "could not believe that it was really true!" They were so excited, but at the same time, it was difficult for them to believe that it had really happened.
  * **and wondered**  - "and were amazed" or "were shocked" (UDB)
  * **before them**  - "in front of them" or "while they were watching" (UDB)
