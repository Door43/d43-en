===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he who would free Israel**  - The Jews were ruled by the Romans. The disciples meant "The one who would free Israel from their Roman enemies." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **since these things came to pass**  - "since he was killed" (UDB)
