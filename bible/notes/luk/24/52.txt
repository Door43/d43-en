===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **they worshipped him** - This means to bow down, kneel or lie face down at the feet of another in reverence, humility, or awe.
    * **continually in the temple** - They went into the temple courtyard every day.
    * **Blessing God** - "Praising God" (UDB)
