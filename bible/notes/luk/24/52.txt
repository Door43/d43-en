===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses tell us about the ongoing actions of the disciples as the story ends. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_endofstory]])

  * **they worshiped him** - This means to bow down, kneel or lie face down at the feet of another in reverence, humility, or awe.
  * **continually in the temple** - They went into the temple courtyard every day.
  * **blessing God** - "praising God" (UDB)
