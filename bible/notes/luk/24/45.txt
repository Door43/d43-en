===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then he opened their minds, that they might understand the scriptures** - To "open the mind" is an idiom that means to enable someone to understand. AT: "Then he enabled them to understand the scriptures" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Thus it is written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "This is what people long ago wrote" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from the dead ones** - "from among the dead people"
  * **the third day** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:24:06|24:7]].
  * **repentance and forgiveness of sins should be preached in his name to all the nations** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the Messiah's followers should preach to everyone everywhere that they need to repent and have God forgive their sins through Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in his name** - His "name" here refers to this authority. AT: "as his representative" or "with his authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all the nations** - "all the ethnic communities" or "all the people groups" (UDB)
  * **beginning from Jerusalem** - "starting in Jerusalem"
