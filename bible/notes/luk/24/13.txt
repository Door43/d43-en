===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  - This marks the beginning of another event discrete from the one involving the women and Peter.
  * **that very day**  - "that same day" (UDB).
  * **…had happened**  - "…had happened to Jesus" (UBD) makes clear what "all the things" the two men were talking about.
