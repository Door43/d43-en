===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **remembered ... returned ... told**  - The main verbs here are **remembered** and **told**.
    * **returned from the tomb**  - The narrator is still, as it were, standing at the tomb, noting that the women have left, but more concerned that they reported to the apostles.
