===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus now directs his attention to his disciples and speaks to them.

  * **Beware of** - "Be on guard against"
  * **who desire to walk in long robes** - Long robes would show that they were important. AT: "who like to walk around wearing their important robes." 
  * **They also devour widows' houses** - "They also eat up widows' houses." AT: "They also take away from widows all their possessions." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **pretentiously make long prayers** - "they pretend to be righteous and make long prayers" or "they make long prayers so that people will see them"
  * **pretentiously** - This means the scribes do things to make themselves seem more important and righteous than what they really are.
  * **These will receive greater condemnation** - AT: "They will receive greater condemnation than others" or "God will punish them more severely than others"
