===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rented it out to vine dressers**  - That is, "allowed some vine dressers to use it in exchange for money" or "allowed some vine dressers to use it and pay him later."
  * **vine dressers**  - These are people who tend to grape vines and grow grapes. It could also be translated as "grape farmers."
  * **of the fruit of the vineyard**  - That is, "grapes." It could also refer to things made from grapes or the money earned from the sale of the grapes.
  * **sent him away empty-handed**  - That is, "sent him away without the grapes" or "sent him away without paying him."
