===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rent**  - That is, allow someone to use property in exchange for money.
  * **vine dressers**  - People who tend to grape vines; people who grow grapes
  * **fruit of the vineyard**  - That is, grapes and grape products
  * **empty-handed**  - That is, without the fee for use of the land.
