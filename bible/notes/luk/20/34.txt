===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sons of this world**  - That is, "People in this world."
  * **marry and are given in marriage**  - Jesus includes both men and women here: in that culture men married women, and women were given to men in marriage by their male relative, usually their father.
  * **they that are judged to be worthy** - That is "the people whom God has judged to be worthy."
  * **to receive the resurrection** - That is, "to be resurrected from the dead" or "to be raised from the dead" or "of being in heaven after they rise from the dead" (UDB).
  * **sons of God**  - That is, "children of God" or "God's adopted children."
  * **sons of the resurrection**  - God's children who will one day be raised from the dead
