===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But Jesus understood their craftiness** - "But Jesus understood how tricky they were" or "But Jesus saw that they were trying to trap him." The word "their" refers to the spies.
  * **a denarius** - This is a Roman silver coin worth a day's wages. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **Whose image and name is on it?** - Jesus uses a question to respond to those who were trying to trick him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **image and name** - "picture and name"
