===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But he understood their craftiness**  - That is, "But Jesus understood those who were trying to trap him"
  * **craftiness**  - This may also be translated as, "Skill" or "manipulation" or "trap".  Craftiness can be positive, for someone who is skilled in an art, or negative, for someone who is sly and evil.
  * **image and superscription**  - That is, "Picture and signature".
