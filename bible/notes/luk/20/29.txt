===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (The Sadducees continue their story.)
  * **the first took a wife**  - This may also be translated as, "One brother married a woman."
  * **and died childless** - That is, "and died without having any children" or "and died, but did not have any children."
  * **and the second as well** - That is "and the second brother married her and died without having any children."
  * **The third took her**  - This may also be translated as, "the third married her."
  * **likewise**  - That is, "in the same way."
  * **in the resurrection**  - That is, "when people are raised from the dead" or "when dead people will come alive again" (UDB).
