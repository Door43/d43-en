===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And they answered**  - "The chief priests, scribes and elders answered"
  * **they answered that they did not know…**  - Some languages may prefer a direct quote. "They said, 'We do not know…'" (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:quotation]])
  * **where he came from** - Another meaning is "where John's baptism came from" or "where John's authority to baptize came from." It can also be translated as "who authorized John to baptize people."
  * **Neither will I tell you**  - That is, "And I will not tell you" or "Just as you will not tell me, I will not tell you."
