===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **became angry**  - "became angry with the people he had invited"
    * **The servant said** - It may be necessary to state clearly the implied information that the servant did what the master commanded him as  in the UDB: "After the servant went out and did that, he came back and said."  (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
