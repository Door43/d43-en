===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues speaking to the Pharisee who had invited him to his home.
    * **they cannot repay you**  - AT: "they cannot invite you to a banquet in return"
    * **in the resurrection of the just**  - "when God brings the righteous people back to life"
