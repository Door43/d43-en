===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to the crowd following him)
    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **how can it be made salty again**  - This is a rhetorical question. It can also be translated as, "It cannot be made salty again" or, "No one can make it salty again."
    * **He who has ears to hear**- That is, the person who has the ability to make sense out of this story, do so.
    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **he who has ears to hear, let him hear**  - or, "if you can hear me, listen!", or, "if you are listening, pay attention!"
