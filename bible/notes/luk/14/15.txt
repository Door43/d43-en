===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - One of the men at the table speaks to Jesus and Jesus responds to him by telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **one of them who sat at the table** - This introduces a new person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]])
  * **Blessed is he** - The man was not talking about a specific person. AT: "Blessed is anyone who" or "How good it is for everyone."
  * **he who will eat bread** - The word "bread" is used to refer to the whole meal. AT: "who will eat at the meal." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **But Jesus said to him** - Jesus begins telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])
  * **When the dinner was prepared** - AT: "At the time for the dinner" or "When the dinner was about to begin"
  * **those who were invited** - AT: "those he had invited" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
