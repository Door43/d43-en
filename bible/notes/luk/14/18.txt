===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling his parable. 
  * **General Information:** - All of the people who were invited gave the servant excuses about why they they could not come to the banquet.

  * **to make excuses** - "to say why they could not come to the dinner"
  * **Please excuse me** - "Please forgive me" or "Please accept my apology"
  * **five pairs of oxen** - Oxen were used in pairs to pull farming tools. AT: "10 oxen to work in my fields." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **married a wife** - Use an expression that is natural in your language. Some languages may say "gotten married" or "taken a wife."
