===== Important Terms: =====



===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **And they all**  - that is, the invited guests all
    * **began to make excuses**  - gave reasons why they could not come
    * **excuse me**  - do not be offended
    * **five pairs of oxen** -  An ox is a male cow used to pull heavy loads or do other hard work. Oxen usually worked in pairs. 
    * **bold words**  - explanation
