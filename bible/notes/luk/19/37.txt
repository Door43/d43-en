===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As he was now approaching** - "As Jesus was going near." Jesus' disciples were traveling with him.
  * **where the Mount of Olives descends** - "where the road goes down from the Mount of Olives"
  * **mighty works which they had seen** - "great things they had seen Jesus do"
  * **Blessed is the king** - They were saying this about Jesus.
  * **in the name of the Lord** - Here "name" refers to power and authority. Also, "Lord" refers to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Peace in heaven** - "May there be peace in heaven." They are expressing what they want to happen.
  * **glory in the highest** - "highest" refers to heaven, where God lives. AT: "let everyone give glory to God in the highest heaven" or "let everyone praise God the Highest One"
