===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
  * **I say to you** - This was the king speaking. Some translators may want to start this verse with "But the king replied, 'I say to you'"or "But the king said 'I tell you this'" (UDB).
  * **everyone who has**- That is, "everyone who uses well what they have been given."
  * **from him that has not**  - That is, "from the person who does not use well what he has been given."
