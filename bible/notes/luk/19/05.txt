===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the place** - "the tree" or "where Zacchaeus was"
  * **So he hurried** - "So Zacchaeus hurried"
  * **they all complained** - The Jews hated the tax collectors and did not think any good person should associate with them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **He has gone in to visit a man who is a sinner** - "Jesus has gone into the house of a sinner to visit him"
  * **a sinner** - "an obvious sinner" or "a real sinner"
