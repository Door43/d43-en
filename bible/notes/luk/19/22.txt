===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
  * **wicked servant**  - A wicked servant could be a disgusting servant or a lazy servant.
  * **severe man**  - A severe man is used to getting what he wants, and when he does not he is not happy.
  * **Why did you not put my money** - This starts a rhetorical question. This could also be translated as "You should have put my money." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion|rhetoricalquestions]])
  * **put my money in the bank** - This could also be translated as  "lent my money to a bank." Cultures that do not have banks might translate it as "let someone borrow my money."
  * **bank**  - A bank is a business that borrows people's money and lends it to others. It pays people who lend to it, and it charges a fee to people who borrow from it.
  * **I would have collected it with interest** - This could also be translated as "I would collect it with interested" or "I could have collected that amount plus the interest it would have earned" (UDB) or "I would gain a profit from it."
  * **interest**  - Interest is money that a bank pays people who put their money in the bank.
