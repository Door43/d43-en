===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues telling his parable.
  * **your mina, lord, has made five minas**  - This can be translated as "Lord, with the money you gave me, I have have earned five times the amount." 
  * **mina** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:19:13|19:13]].
  * **You take charge over five cities.**  - This can be translated as "You will have authority over five cities."
