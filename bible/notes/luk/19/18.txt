===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
  * **you mina, lord, has made five minas**  - This could also be translated as "With the money you gave me, I have have earned five times the amount." Or better, the money you gave me, I have made another five, meaning, I doubled the amount you gave me.
  * **And he said to him,**  - That is, "And the nobleman said to the servant."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **You take charge over five cities.**  - This is a command. Jesus had just given a command to the first servant. The word "you" is here to focus on the second man. The word you is singular. (See [[:en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
