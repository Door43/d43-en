===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he was trying to see who Jesus was**  - That is, "Zacchaeus was trying to see who Jesus was."
  * **a sycamore tree**  - That is, "a sycamore fig tree." It produces small round fruit about 2.5 centimeters across. It could also be translated simply as "a fig tree" or "a tree."
  * **because he was small in height - ** That is, "because he was a short man."
