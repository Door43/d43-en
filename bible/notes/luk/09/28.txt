===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Eight days after Jesus tells his disciples that some would not die before they saw the kingdom of God, Jesus goes up the mountain to pray with Peter, James, and John who all fall asleep while Jesus is changed to a dazzling appearance. 

  * **It came about** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **these words** - This refers to what Jesus said to his disciples in the preceding verses.
  * **into the mountain** - It is unclear how far up the mountain they went. AT: "on the side of a mountain" 
  * **white and dazzling** - Possible meanings are 1) "shining white and brilliant" or 2) "dazzling white and shining like lightning" (UDB).
