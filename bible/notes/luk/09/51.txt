===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the days were approaching**  - Or, "the time was coming."
    * **resolutely - **  or, "determinedly", or "deliberately"
    * **set his face to go**  - that is, "he was heading to" or "he was traveling towards"
    * **to prepare for him**  - That is, to make arrangements for his arrival in the area, possibly including a place to speak, a place to stay, and food.
    * **did not receive him**  - That is, "did not welcome him," or "did not want him to stay."
