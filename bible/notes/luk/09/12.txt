===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The day began to come to an end** - "The sun set" or "The day ended"
  * **Dismiss the crowds** - "Tell the crowds to go away"
  * **unless we went and bought food** - If "unless" is difficult to understand in your language, you could make this a new sentence. AT: "That is not enough, so in order to feed them all, we would have to go and buy food" 
  * **unless we** - "we" here refers only to the disciples, and not to Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **about five thousand men** - "about 5,000 men." This number does not include the women and children who might have been present. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Have them sit down** - "Tell them to sit down"
  * **fifty each** - "50 each" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
