===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus is praying, alone with his disciples, and they begin to talk about who Jesus is. Jesus tells them of his death and resurrection that will follow and urges them to follow him no matter what happens.

  * **It came about that** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new event. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **as he was praying** - "as Jesus was praying"
  * **praying alone** - The disciples were with Jesus, but he was praying personally and privately by himself. 
  * **Answering, they said** - "They answered him by saying"
  * **John the Baptist** - It may be helpful to restate part of the question here. AT: "Some say you are John the Baptist" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **one of the prophets from ancient times has risen again** - It may be helpful to restate part of the question here. AT: "you are one of the prophets from ancient times who has risen again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **from ancient times** - "who lived a very long time ago"
  * **has risen again** - "has come back to life"
