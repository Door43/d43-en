===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the ones who had been sent out**  - That is, "the twelve apostles that Jesus had sent out."
    * **returned** - That is, "came back to where Jesus was."
    * **they told him**  - That is, "the apostles told Jesus."
    * **everything they had done** - This refers to the teaching and healing that they did when they went to the other cities.
    *  **And taking them with him, he went away by himself** - This could also be translated as "He took them with him and they went by themselves." Jesus and his apostles went away by themselves.
    * **those who had need of healing** - This could also be translated as, "the people who were sick" or "the sick people" (UDB).
