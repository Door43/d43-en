===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So they did that** - The disciples told the crowd to sit down in groups of fifty people.
  * **He took** - "Jesus took"
  * **loaves** - These are specific amounts of baked bread. AT: "whole breads"
  * **and looking** - "while looking" or "after looking"
  * **up to heaven** - This refers to looking up, toward the sky. The Jews believed that heaven was located above the sky. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he blessed them** - This refers to the loaves of bread and the fish.
  * **to set before** - "to pass out to" or "to give to"
  * **were satisfied** - This idiom means they ate enough food so they were not hungry. AT: "they had as much as they wanted to eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the leftover pieces of food were picked up and filled twelve baskets** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the people picked up enough of the leftover pieces of food to fill twelve baskets with it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
