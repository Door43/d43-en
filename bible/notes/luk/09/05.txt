===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues to give directions to his twelve apostles.
    * **as for those who do not receive you**  - This can be translated as "here is what you should do about the people who do not receive you."
    * **they departed** - "they left the place were Jesus was" 
    * **everywhere** - "everywhere they went"
