===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues to give directions to his twelve apostles.)
    * **as for those who do not receive you**  - This can also be translated as, "here is what you should do about the people who do not receive you."
    * **for a testimony**  - This can also be translated as "as a statement" or "as a warning" as in the UDB.
    * **they departed** - That is, "they left the place were Jesus was." 
    * **everywhere** - That is, "everywhere they went."
