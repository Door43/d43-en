===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **after supper** - This refers to the completion of the Passover meal. 
    * **that you do not enter into temptation** - This can be translated as "that you are not tempted" or "that you do not sin when you are tempted" or "that you are not tempted so severely that that you sin."
