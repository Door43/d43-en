===== translationNotes: =====

  * **those who were around Jesus** - This refers to Jesus' disciples.
  * **what was happening** - This refers to the priests and soldiers coming to arrest Jesus.
  * **strike with the sword** - "fight against them with a sword"
  * **one of them** - "one of the disciples"
  * **struck the servant of the high priest** - "struck the servant of the high priest with a sword"
  * **That is enough** - This expression means, "Do not do any more of that" 
  * **touched his ear** - "touched the servant where his ear had been cut off"
