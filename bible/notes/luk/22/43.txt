===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **appeared to him**  - "appeared to Jesus"
  * **strengthening him**  - "encouraging him"
  * **Being in agony, he prayed**  - "He was in agony, and he prayed"
  * **his sweat became like great drops of blood falling down on the ground**  - Drops of sweat that were as large as drops of blood fell from his skin onto the ground. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]])
