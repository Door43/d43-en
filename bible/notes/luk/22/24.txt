===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then there arose also a quarrel among them** - "Then the apostles began to argue among themselves" 
  * **was considered to be greatest** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the people would think was most important" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **He said to them** - "Jesus said to the apostles"
  * **are masters over them** - "rule harshly over the Gentiles" or "like to exercise power over them"
  * **are referred to as** - The people probably did not think of those rulers as people who do good to their people. AT: "like to be called" or "call themselves"
