===== translationNotes: =====

  * **bread** - This bread did not have yeast in it, so it was flat.
  * **he broke it** - "he ripped it" or "he tore it." He may have divided it into many pieces or he may have divided it into two pieces and given them to the apostles to divide among themselves. If possible, use an expression that would apply to either situation.
  * **This is my body** - Possible meanings are 1) "This bread is my body" and 2) "This bread represents my body."
  * **my body which is given for you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "my body, which I will give for you" or "my body, which I will sacrifice for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Do this** - "Eat this bread"
  * **in remembrance of me** - "in order to remember me"
  * **This cup** - The word "cup" refers to the wine in the cup. AT: "The wine in this cup" or "This cup of wine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the new covenant in my blood** - His "blood" here  "represents the new covenant, which God will establish when my blood is poured out."
  * **is poured out for you** - Jesus spoke of his death by referring to his blood being poured out. AT: "is poured out in death for you" or "will flow out of my wounds for you when I die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
