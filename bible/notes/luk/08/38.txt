===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **But the man** - Some may need to start this with "But before Jesus and his disciples left, the man" or "Before Jesus and his disciples set sail, the man"
    * **he sent him away**  - "Jesus sent the man away."
    * **your house** - "your household" or "your family."
