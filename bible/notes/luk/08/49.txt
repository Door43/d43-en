===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus has just finished speaking to the woman that he had healed of her bleeding.

  * **While he was still speaking** - AT: "While Jesus was still speaking to the woman"
  * **synagogue leader** - This refers to Jairus. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:luk:08:40|8:41]])
  * **he answered him** - "Jesus answered Jairus." Jesus was speaking to the synagogue ruler, and not to the messenger.
  * **she will be saved** - "she will be well." or  AT: "I will heal her" or "she will live again" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
