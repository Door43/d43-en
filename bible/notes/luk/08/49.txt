===== translationNotes: =====

  * **While he was still speaking** - "While Jesus was still speaking to the woman"
  * **synagogue leader** - This refers to Jairus. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:luk:08:40|8:41]])
  * **Do not trouble the teacher** - This statement implies that Jesus will not be able to do anything to help now that the girl is dead. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the teacher** - This refers to Jesus.
  * **she will be healed** - "she will be well" or "she will live again" .
