===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus and his disciples come ashore at Gerasa where Jesus removes many demons from a man. 

  * **region of the Gerasenes** - Gerasenes were people from the city called Gerasa. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **opposite Galilee** - "on the other side of the lake from Galilee"
  * **Jesus stepped out on the land** - "Jesus stepped out of the boat onto the land" (UDB)
  * **a certain man from the city** - "a man from the city of Gerasa" 
  * **this man had demons** - "this man was controlled by demons" or "demons controlled this man"
  * **For a long time he had worn no clothes ... but rather in the tombs** - This is background information about the man who had demons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **he had worn no clothes** - "he had not worn clothes"
  * **tombs** - These are places where people put dead bodies, possibly caves. The fact that the man was living in them indicates that these were not holes dug down in the ground.
