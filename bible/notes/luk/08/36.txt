===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the ones who had seen what happened** - These were the people who had been with the man when Jesus made the demons leave him.
  * **the man who was controlled by demons had been saved** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Jesus had saved the man that demons had controlled" or Jesus had healed the man that demons had controlled" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the region of the Gerasenes** - "that area of the Gerasenes" or "the area where the Gerasene people lived"
  * **they were gripped with great fear** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they became very afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to return** - The destination can be stated. AT: "to go back across the lake" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
