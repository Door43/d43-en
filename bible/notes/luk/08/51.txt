===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then when he came to the house** - "Then when they came to the house"
  * **he did not allow anyone ... except Peter, John and James, the father of the girl, and her mother** - This could be stated positively. AT: "he only allowed Peter, John, and James, the girl's father, and her mother to go inside"
  * **he did not allow** - "Jesus did not permit"
  * **the father of the girl** - This refers to Jairus.
  * **all the people there were mourning and wailing for her** - This was the normal way of showing grief in that culture. AT: "all the people were showing how sad they were and crying loudly because the girl had died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **knowing that she was dead** - "because they knew the girl was dead" (UDB)
