===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **What is your name?** - It is unclear if Jesus was addressing the man or the demon, but the demon answered.
    * **Army**  - This can be translated as "Battalion" or "Brigade" in languages that have words for large groups of soldiers in an army.
    * **they kept imploring him**  - "the demons kept begging Jesus"
