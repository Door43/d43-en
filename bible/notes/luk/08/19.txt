===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **it was told him**  - That is, "people told him."
    * **are standing**  - Or, "have been standing." It seems Jesus' family had been waiting for a while to see him.
    * **desiring to see you**  - That is, "and they want to see you" or "waiting to see you."
    * **answered as said**  - This could also be translated as "replied them" or "said to them in response."
    * **My mother and my brothers are those…**  - Another way of translating this as … (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]]).
