===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brothers** - These were Jesus' younger brothersâ€”the other sons of Mary and Joseph who were born after Jesus. Since the Father of Jesus was God, and their father was Joseph, they were technically his half-brothers. This detail is not normally translated.
  * **it was told him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "people told him" or "someone told him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **desiring to see you** - "waiting to see you" or "and they want to see you"
  * **My mother and my brothers are those who hear the word of God, and obey it** - This metaphor expresses that the people who were coming to listen to Jesus were as important to him as his own family was. AT: "Those who hear the word of God and obey it are like a mother and brothers to me" or "Those who hear the word of God and obey it are as important to me as my mother and brothers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
