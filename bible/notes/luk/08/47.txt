===== Translation Notes: =====



    * **in the presence**  - That is, "in the sight of" or "in the hearing of" or "before."
    * **falling down before Jesus** - The woman purposely got down on the ground before Jesus. She did not stumble and fall.
    * **Daughter**  - This was a kind way of speaking to a woman. Your language may have another way of showing this kindness.
    * **your faith has healed you**  - "because of your faith you have become well."
    * **go in peace**  - "as  you go, be at peace"
