===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he, taking the girl by the hand** - "Jesus took hold of the girl's hand"
  * **Child, arise** - "Little girl, get up"
  * **Her spirit returned** - "Her spirit returned to her body." The Jews understood that life was the result of the spirit coming into a person. AT: "She came back to life" or "She became alive again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **spirit** - "breath" or "life"
  * **that something be given to her to eat** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that they give her something to eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to tell no one** - This could be stated differently. AT: "not to tell anyone"
