===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **But he**  - Or, "But Jesus."
    * **called out, saying,**  - That is, "called out and said" or "said loudly."
    * **commanded that something be given to her**  - That is, "told them to bring her" or "told them to give her."
    * **astonished**  - That is, "very surprised" or "extremely surprised."
