===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues to explain the meaning of the parable.)
    * **they become choked with the cares** - "they are overwhelmed with the caresâ€¦" This can be translated with an active verb: "the cares and riches and pleasures of this life choke them." (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:active]])
    * **so they bring no fruit to maturity** - "they do not bear ripe fruit." This metaphor can be translated as a simile: "so like a plant that does not become mature and bear fruit, they do not become mature." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor]])
    * **produce fruit with perseverance**  - This could be translated as "produce fruit by persevering." This metaphor can be translated as a simile "like healthy plants that produce good fruit, they produce good works by persevering."
    * **perseverance**  - That is, by means of "endurance" or "being stubborn."
