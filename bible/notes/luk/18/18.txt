===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **good teacher-** Or, "worthy to be listened to and obeyed"
    * **what must I do**  - That is, "what do I need to do" or "what is required of me"
    * **No one is good, **-That is, "No person is good," or "No human is good,"
    * **do not kill,**  - That is, "do not murder,"
    * **All these things - **  This could be translated, "All of these commandments"
