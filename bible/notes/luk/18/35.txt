===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;;;#ffffff>Jesus of Nazareth</font> **- Jesus came from the town of Nazareth that was located in Galilee.
  * **a certain blind man**  - This can also be translated, "Some blind man" or "a blind man"
  * **and hearing a crowd going by**  - This can also be translated, "Because he heard a crowd going by"
  * **and they told him**  - that is, people in the crowd told the blind man
  * **drew near to - **  That is, "was approaching"
  * **begging; and hearing**  - That is, "begging. Hearing"
  * **Nazareth was passing by**. - That is, "Nazareth was walking past him."
