===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the prophets**- means the Old Testament prophets like Isaiah and Jeremiah.****
    * **will be accomplished - ** This can also be translated, "will happen" or "will occur".
    * **the Gentiles**- means "the non Jews," like the Roman Pontius Pilate.
    * **Son of Man**  - Jesus speaks of himself as "the Son of Man" and uses "he" to refer to himself.
