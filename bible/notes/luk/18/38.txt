===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cried out**  - "called out" or "shouted"
  * **Son of David**  - Jesus was a descendant of David, Israel's most important king.
  * **have mercy on me**  - "show me pity" or "show me compassion"
  * **The ones**  - "The people"
  * **to be quiet**  - "to be silent" or "not to shout"
  * **cried out all the more** - This could mean that he cried out louder or that he cried out more persistently.
