===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to his disciples.)
    * **Then he**- That is, "Then Jesus."
    * **saying**- This could also be translated as "He said" (UDB) or the the new sentence could simply begin with what is in the quote, without the words "saying" or "he said."
    * **and not become discouraged**  - This could also be translated as, "and not grow tired of praying" or "and not quit."
    * **There was in a certain city a certain judge**  - This can be translated as, "In a certain city there was a judge," or "There was a certain judge in a certain city." Or it could also be translated "in some city some judge."
    * **fear**  - That is, "revere" (UDB)
    * **did not respect people**  - Also, "paid no regard to a person."
