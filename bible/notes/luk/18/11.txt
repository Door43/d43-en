===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
    * **The Pharisee stood**  - The Pharisee stood off by himself so that other people could see him. He wanted to be prominent so that others could hear him and praise him for his righteousness.
    * **robbers**- A robber is someone who steals things by forcing people to give them to him, or by threatening to force them.
    * **fast**  - "To fast" means not to eat a meal. The Pharisees did this twice a week.
    * **get**  - That is, "earn."
