===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And when Jesus heard that**  - "And when Jesus heard the man say that"
  * **he said to him** - "he answered him"
  * **One thing you still lack** - "You still need to do one more thing" or "There is one thing that you have not yet done"
  * **Sell all that you have**  - "Sell all your possessions" or "Sell everything that you own"
  * **distribute**  - "give"
  * **distribute it to the poor**- "give away the money to the poor people"
