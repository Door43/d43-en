===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And when Jesus heard that**  - That is, "And when Jesus heard him say that."
  * **he said to him **- That is, "he answered him."
  * **One thing you still lack **- That is, "One thing you still need to do" or "One thing you still need."
  * **Sell all that you have**  - That is, "Sell all that you possess" or "Sell all that you own."
  * **distribute**  - give
  * **distribute it to the poor**- That is, "give the money to the poor people," or "distribute the money to the poor people."
