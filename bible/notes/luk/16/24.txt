===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his story.)
    * **and he cried out and said**  - That is, "and the rich man called out to say" or "he shouted saying" or "he yelled out to Abraham."
    * **have mercy on me** - This can also be translated as "please have pity on me," or "please be merciful to me."
    * **and send Lazarus** - This can also be translated as "by sending Lazarus" or "Please send Lazarus to me" or "and tell Lazarus to come to me." This is how the rich man wanted Abraham to have mercy on him. 
    * **he may dip the tip of his finger** - This indicates the smallness of the amount requested. This can be translated as "he may wet the tip of his finger."
    * **I am in anguish in this flame** - That is "I am in terrible pain in this flame" or "I am suffering terribly in this fire" or "this fire is giving me terrible pain."
