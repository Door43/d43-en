===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues to teach the people.)
    * **No servant can**  - This can also be translated as, "A servant cannot."
    * **devoted**  - That is, "dedicated" or "loyal."
    * **despise the other**  - That is, "hold the other in contempt" or "sneer at at other" or "hate the other."
    * **You cannot serve**  - Jesus was talking to a group of people, so languages that have a plural form of "you" would use that. (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou]])
    * **serve**  - That is, "be a slave to."
    * **You cannot serve God and wealth**  This can also be translated,"Similarly, you cannot serve God and wealth." (UDB) or "In the same way, you cannot serve God and wealth."
    * **he will hate** - That is, "the servant will hate"
    * **You cannot serve God and wealth** - That is, "You cannot devote your life to serving God if you are devoting your life to acquiring money and other material possessions." (UDB)
