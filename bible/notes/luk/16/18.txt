===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to the people.)
    * Since this is an example of a law that has not changed, this verse could also start with "For example."
    * **Everyone who divorces his wife**  - That is, "Anyone who divorces his wife," or "Any man who divorces his wife" or "If a man divorces his wife."
    * **commits adultery**  - That is, "Is guilty of adultery."
    * **he who marries one**  - That is, "any man who marries a woman" or "if a man marries a woman."
