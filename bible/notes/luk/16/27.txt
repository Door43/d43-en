===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues telling his story.)
  * **that you would send him to my father's house**  - That could also be translated as "that you would tell Lazarus to go to my father's house" or "Please, send him to my father's house."
  * **in order that he may warn them**  - This could also be translated as, 'Tell Lazarus to warn them."
  * **for fear that they also come**  - This could also be translated as, "so that they do not also come" (UDB) or "Otherwise they might come" or "If they are not warned, they might come." Since it is implied that the way they would avoid going there is by repenting, this implied information could be made clear, as in "so that they might repent and not come." (See talink Implicit Information)
  * **this place of torment**  - This could also be translated as "this place where we suffer torment" or "this place where we suffer terrible terrible pain" or "this place where we are tortured."
