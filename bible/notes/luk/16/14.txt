===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a break in Jesus teachings, as verse 14 tells us background information about how the Pharisees ridiculed Jesus. In verse 15, Jesus continues teaching and responds to the Pharisees. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Now** - This word marks a shift to the background information.
  * **who were lovers of money** - "who loved having money" or "who where very greedy for money"
  * **they ridiculed him** - "the Pharisees ridiculed Jesus"
  * **And he said to them** - "And Jesus said to the Pharisees"
  * **You justify yourselves in the sight of men** - AT: "You try to make yourselves look good to people"
  * **God knows your hearts** - AT: "God understands your true desires" or "God knows your motives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **That which is exalted among men** - AT: "Those things that men think are very important"
  * **is detestable in the sight of God** - AT: "God hates" or "are things that God hates"
