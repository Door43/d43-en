===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues telling his story.)
    * **his master's debtors**  - That is, "the people who were in debt to his master" or "the people who owed his mater something." In this story the debtors owed olive oil and wheat.
    * **He called… he said… ** - There are a lot of pronouns here. They refer either to the rich man's manager, or the various people who had borrowed from the rich man, and owned him a repayment.
    * **a hundred measures of olive oil ** - That is, "around 3400 liters of olive oil".
    * **a hundred measures of wheat**  - That is, "around 22,000 dry liters of wheat grain."
    * **he called… he asked…**  - In verse 5, the pronoun "he" refers to the manager. That is, "the manager called ... the manager asked."
    * **he said… he said to him**  - In verse 6, the first "he" refers to the debtor, and the second "he" refers to the manager. That is, "the debtor said ... the manager said to the debtor."
    * **Then he said… he said… he said to him **- In verse 7, the first "he" refers to the manager, the second "he" refers to the second debtor, and the third "he" refers to the manager. That is "Then the manager said .... The debtor said.... The manager said to the debtor."
