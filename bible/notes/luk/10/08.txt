===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues to give directions to the 72 people he was sending out.)
    * **and they receive you** - This could also be translated as, "if they welcome you."
    * **eat what is set before you**  - That is, "eat whatever your host gives you to eat."
    * **The kingdom of God has come near to you.**  -
