===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues to give directions to the 70 people he was sending out.

  * **and they receive you** - "if they welcome you"
  * **eat what is set before you**  - "eat whatever food they give you"
  * **The kingdom of God has drawn near to you**  - This refers to the fact that the activity of the kingdom of God was happening all around them in the healing of the disciples and the teaching of Jesus. AT: "You can see the kingdom of God all around you right now."
