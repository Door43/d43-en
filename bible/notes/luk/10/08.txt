===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and they receive you** - "if they welcome you"
  * **eat what is set before you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "eat whatever food they give you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the sick** - "the sick people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **The kingdom of God has come close to you** - The abstract noun  "kingdom" can be expressed with the verbs "reign" or "rule." Possible meanings are 1) the kingdom of God will begin soon. AT: "God will soon rule everywhere as king" or 2) the activities of kingdom of God are happening all around you. AT: "The proof that God is reigning is all around you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
