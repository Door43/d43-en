===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But the teacher, desiring to justify himself, said** - "But the teacher wanted to find a way to justify himself, so he said" or "But wanting to appear righteous, the teacher said"
  * **Jesus answered him and said** - Jesus answers the man by telling a parable. AT: "In response, Jesus told him this story" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])
  * **Who is my neighbor?** - It may be helpful to add additional information. AT: "Who should I consider to be my neighbor and love as I love myself?" of "Which people are my neighbors that I should love?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **A certain man** - This introduces a new character in the parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]])
  * **He fell among robbers** - "He was surrounded by robbers" or "Some robbers attacked him"
  * **stripped him of his belongings** - "took everything he had" or "stole all his things"
  * **half dead** - This idiom means "almost dead." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
