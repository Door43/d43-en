===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **seventy others**  - Some versions say "seventy-two others."
  * **sent them out by pairs**  - "sent them out in groups of two" or "sent them out with two people in each group"
  * **He said to them**  - This was before the men actually went out. This can be translated as "This is what he said to them" or "Before they went out he told them."
  * **The harvest is plentiful but the laborers are few**  - "There is a big crop ready to pick, but there aren't enough workers to do it." This metaphor means that there are many people who are ready to be brought into the [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:kingdomofgod|kingdom of God]], but there are not enough workers to bring them in. To show that Jesus was really talking God's kingdom, it can be translated as "There is a big crop of people ready to be harvested and brought into God's kingdom, but there are few workers to bring them in." (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor]])
