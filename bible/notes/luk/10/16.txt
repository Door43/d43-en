===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus finishes teaching the 70 people he was sending out.

  * **The one who listens to you listens to me** - AT: "When someone listens to you, it is as if they are listening to me."
  * **the one who rejects you rejects me** - AT: "When someone rejects you, it is as if they are rejecting me."
  * **the one who rejects me rejects the one who sent me** - AT: "When someone rejects me, it is as if they are rejecting the one who sent me."
  * **the one who sent me** - This refers to God the Father who appointed Jesus for this special task. AT: "God, who sent me" (UDB).
