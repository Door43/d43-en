===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **when he saw him**  - "when the Samaritan saw the injured man"
    * **he was moved with compassion**  - "he felt sorry for him."
    * **bound up his wounds, pouring oil and wine on them**  - Since he would have put the oil and wine on the wounds first, it can be translated as "he put wine and oil on the wounds and wrapped them with cloth." Wine was used to clean the wound, and oil was probably used to prevent further infection.
    * **on his own beast**  - "on his own pack animal." This was an animal that he used to carry heavy loads. It may have been a donkey.

    * **the host**  - "the innkeeper" or "the person who took care of the inn."
