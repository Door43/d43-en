===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **came to where he was**  - That is, "came to where the injured man was."
    * **when he saw him**  - That is, "when the Samaritan saw the injured man."
    * **moved with compassion**  - or, "he felt sorry for him."
    * **bound up his wounds**  - That is, "wrapped his wounds" or "covered his wounds."
    * **pouring oil and wine on them**  - This could also be translated as, "after putting oil and wine on the wounds to help them heal." These were used as medicines. Wine was used to clean the wound, and oil was probably used to prevent further infection.
