===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your sins are forgiven**  - "You are forgiven." It can also be expressed with an active verb: "I forgive your sins." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active]])
  * **Your faith has saved you**  - "Because of your faith, you are saved." The idea of "faith" can be translated with a verb: "Because you believe, you are saved."
  * **go in peace**  - This is a way of saying "good-bye" and giving a blessing at the same time. It can be translated as "As you go, be peaceful" or "May God give you peace as you go" (UDB).
