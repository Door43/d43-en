===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then he said to her** - "Then he said to the woman" 
  * **Your sins are forgiven** - "You are forgiven." This can be stated in active form. AT: "I forgive your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **reclining together** - "reclining together around the table" or "eating together"
  * **Who is this that even forgives sins?** - The religious leaders knew that only God could forgive sin, and did not believe that Jesus was God. This question was probably intended to be an accusation. AT: "Who does this man think he is? Only God can forgive sin!" or "Why is this man pretending to be God who can forgive sin?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Your faith has saved you** - "Because of your faith, you are saved." "faith" could be stated as an action. AT: "Because you believe, you are saved" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Go in peace** - This is a way of saying good-bye while giving a blessing at the same time. AT: "As you go, do not worry anymore" or "May God give you peace as you go"
