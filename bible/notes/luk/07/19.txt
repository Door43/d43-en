===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **sent us to you, saying, 'Are you the Coming One** - This is a direct quote. This could also be translated as "sent us to you asking if you the person who is coming." (See [[en:ta:vocabulary:quotation|Direct and Indirect Quotes]])
    * **come near to him** - That is, "come near to the Lord" or "come near to Jesus."
    * **we should be looking for? **- Or, "we are anticipating?"
