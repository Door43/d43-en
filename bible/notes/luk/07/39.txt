===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he thought to himself, saying**  - "he said to himself"
  * **If this man were a prophet, then he would know**  - The Pharisee thought that Jesus was not a prophet because he allowed the sinful woman to touch him. AT: "Apparently Jesus is not a prophet. If he were, he would know."
  * **Simon** - This was the name of the Pharisee who invited Jesus into his home. This was not Simon Peter.
