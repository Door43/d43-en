===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **told him** - That is, "told John."
    * **these things** - "the things Jesus was doing"
    * **saying, 'Are you the Coming One** - This is a direct quote. This could also be translated as "asking if you the person who is coming or if there is another person" (See [[en:ta:vocabulary:quotation|Direct and Indirect Quotes]])

    * **we should be looking for** - "we are anticipating"
