===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **told him** - "told John"
  * **all these things** - "all the things Jesus was doing"
  * **sent them to the Lord to say** - (John) "sent them to the Lord to ask" (Jesus)
  * **we should be looking for** - "for whom we should be waiting" or "we should be expecting to come"
