===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **told him** - "told John"
    * **all these things** - "all the things Jesus was doing"
    * **sent them to the Lord, saying** - "sent them to the Lord, telling them to ask him"
    * **has sent us to you saying, 'Are you the Coming One** - "has sent us to you. He asks 'Are you the Coming One'" or "has sent us to to ask you, 'Are you the Coming One'" (UDB)
    * **we should be looking for** - "we should be waiting for" or "we should be expecting to come"
