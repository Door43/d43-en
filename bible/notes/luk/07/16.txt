===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fear overcame all of them** - "fear filled all of them." AT: "fear took everyone."
  * **A great prophet** - They were referring to Jesus, not to some unidentified prophet. AT: "This great prophet."
  * **has been raised among us** - "has come to be with us" or "has appeared to us." AT: "God has raised a great prophet among us." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **looked upon** - AT: "cared for"
  * **This news about Jesus spread** - "This news" refers to the things people were saying in verse 16.  AT: "People spread this report about Jesus" or "People told others this report about Jesus." 
  * **This news** - "This word" or "This message"
