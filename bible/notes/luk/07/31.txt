===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues speaking to the people about John the Baptist.

  * **To what, then, can I compare ... they like?** - Jesus used these questions to introduce a comparison. They can be written as a statement. AT: "This is what I compare this generation to, and what they are like" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **I compare ... What are they like** - These are two ways of saying that this is a comparison. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the people of this generation** - The people living when Jesus spoke.
  * **They are like** - These words are the beginning of Jesus' comparison. Jesus is saying that the people were like children who were never satisfied with the way other children acted. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **marketplace** - a large, open-air area where people came to sell their goods
  * **and you did not dance** - "but you did not dance to the music"
  * **and you did not cry** - "but you did not cry with us"
