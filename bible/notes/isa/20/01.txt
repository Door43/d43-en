===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Tartan** - the name of the chief commander of the armies of Assyria (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sargon** - the name of the king of Assyria 
  * **he fought against Ashdod and took it** - Ashdod refers to the army of Ashdod. AT: "he fought against the army of Ashdod" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **walking naked and barefoot** - Here the word "naked" probably refers to wearing only his undergarments. AT: "walking around without clothes and without sandals"
