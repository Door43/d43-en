===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Tartan** - This is the name of the chief commander of the armies of Assyria. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sargon** - This is the name of the king of Assyria. 
  * **he fought against Ashdod and took it** - Here "Ashdod" refers to the army of Ashdod. Alternate translation: "he fought against the army of Ashdod and captured their city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **son of Amoz** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:01|1:1]].
