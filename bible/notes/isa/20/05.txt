===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They**  - “They” refers to the <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>people of other countries who trusted that the armies of Egypt and Ethiopia would be able to help them.</font>
  * **dismayed and ashamed **- “confused and disappointed”
  * **because of Ethiopia their hope and of Egypt their glory **- “because they had trusted in the armies of Ethiopia and Egypt”
  * **and now, how can we escape? **- This is a rhetorical question. ([[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
