===== translationNotes: =====

  * **dismayed and ashamed** - "afraid and put to shame"
  * **because of Ethiopia their hope and of Egypt their glory** - Hope and glory refer to their trust in the military power of these countries. AT: "because they had trusted in the power of the armies of Ethiopia and Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The inhabitants of these coasts** - the people who lived in the lands bordering the Mediterranean Sea
  * **where we fled for help to be rescued** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "where we fled to so they would rescue us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **now, how can we escape?** - The author uses this question to emphasize how hopeless their situation was. AT: "now there is no way for us to escape!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
