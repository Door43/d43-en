===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh of hosts** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **the word** - "the message"
  * **Look** - This word is used here to draw Hezekiah's attention to what is said next. AT: "Listen" 
  * **when everything in your palace ... will be carried to Babylon** - This can be expressed positively. AT: "when the enemy army will take everything in your palace ... back to Babylon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
