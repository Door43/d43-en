===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ah, the land of the rustling of wings, which is beyond the rivers of Ethiopia** - "Listen, Ethiopia, the land of many boats on the upper part of the Nile River"
  * **by the sea** - This refers to the river.
  * **vessels of papyrus** - Papyrus is a tall, aquatic plant that grows in the area of the Nile valley. Sailing ships were built by binding together papyrus bundles.
  * **to a nation tall and smooth** - "to a nation whose people are tall and have smooth skin" The Ethiopians were considered to be grand people.
  * **whose land the rivers divide** - Ethiopia is divided by two large branches of the Nile River, the White Nile and the Blue Nile.
