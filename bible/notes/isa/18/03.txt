===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All you inhabitants of the world and you dwellers on the earth, when a signal is lifted up on the mountains, look; and when the trumpet is blown, listen.** - All people of the earth should look and listen for signals that Yahweh is at work in the world.
  * **a signal is lifted up** - A banner is used to get someone's attention. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:13:01|13:1]])
  * **when the trumpet is blown** - A trumpet is a musical instrument with a powerful, penetrating tone.  It is often used to get people's attention.
