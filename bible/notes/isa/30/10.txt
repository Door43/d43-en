===== Translation Notes: =====
Isaiah continues speaking about the people of Judah.
  * **Do not prophesy straightforward truth to us; speak to us smooth things; prophesy deceptions;** - “Do not tell us the truth but instead lie to us and tell us things that are nice to hear”
  * **Holy One of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:04|1:4]].
