===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **show the motion of his arm** - AT: "show his power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in storming anger and flames of fire** - AT: "in anger that is like a storm and flames of fire" or "in great anger" (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **with windstorm, rainstorm, and hailstones** - "with storms full of wind, rain, and hail"
  * **hailstones** - hard pieces of ice that fall from the sky like rain
