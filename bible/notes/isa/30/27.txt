===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the name of Yahweh** - AT: "Yahweh" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **burning with his anger and in a heavy burden** - Yahweh's anger is compared to fire to emphasize its power to destroy. The "heavy burden" refers to the smoke that comes from the fire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **His lips are full of fury, and his tongue is like a devouring fire** - AT: "When he speaks his words are full of fury and they destroy like a fire" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **His breath is like an overflowing torrent** - This compares the air coming out of Yahweh's mouth to a flood to emphasize its power to destroy. 
  * **to sift the nations with the sieve of destruction** – AT: “Yahweh will separate and destroy the nations"
  * **a bridle** – a device that goes over a horse's head and in it mouth and it is used to guide the horse.
