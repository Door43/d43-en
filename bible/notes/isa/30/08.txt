===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** - This word marks a new section in the book.
  * **in their presence** - "in the presence of the people of Judah"
  * **lying children, children who will not hear the instruction of Yahweh.**" - This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "They behave like children who lie and will not listen to what Yahweh commands." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
