===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Judah.

  * **You will have a song** - This can be reworded so the abstract noun "song" is stated as a verb. AT: "You will sing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **as in the night when a holy feast is observed** - This simile emphasizes how happy the people will be. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **when a holy feast is observed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "when you observe a holy feast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **gladness of heart** - Here "heart" represents a person's inner being. AT: "you will be glad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **as when one goes ... Rock of Israel** - This simile emphasizes how happy the people will be. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **to the Rock of Israel** - Yahweh having the power to protect his people is spoken of as if he were a rock on which the people could climb and escape from enemies. AT: "to Israel's protective rock" or "which is like a protective rock for Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
