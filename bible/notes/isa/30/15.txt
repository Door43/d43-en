===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Holy One**  - (See: Standard Notes)
  * **In returning and resting you will be saved; in quietness and in trust will be your strength. - “**Return to me in repentance and quietly trust in me and I will be your power and strength” See: (Active/Passive)
  * **for we will flee on horses," so you will flee; and, "We will ride upon swift horses," so those who pursue you will be swift -**  This compares their frenetic activity of fleeing on horses to their own futile and anxious attempts to deal with the situation independent of God. (See: Metaphor)
