===== translationNotes: =====
  * **For at the voice of Yahweh, Assyria will be shattered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "For Yahweh will shatter the people of Assyria with his voice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Assyria will be shattered** - Isaiah speaks of Assyria's fear as if Assyria is an object that Yahweh's voice shatters. AT: "Assyria will be terrified" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the appointed rod** - This is likely a reference to an enemy army that Yahweh will use to conquer Assyria.
  * **will be accompanied** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the people of Judah will accompany it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **tambourines and harps** - See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:11|5:12]].
