===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Judah.

  * **You will throw them away like a menstrual rag** - This simile means they will throw away their idols like they were garbage. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **You will say to them, "Get out of here."** - This speaks of the idols as if they could hear and get up and leave a place. Yahweh means that the people will no longer need or want the idols. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
