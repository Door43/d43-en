===== translationNotes: =====

  * **See** - "Listen to this"
  * **I will lay in Zion a foundation stone ... sure foundation** - Yahweh sending a strong person to help the people of Israel is spoken of as if Yahweh is constructing a strong foundation for a building. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a tried stone** - "a stone that is solid"
  * **a sure foundation** - "a firm support"
  * **He who believes will not be ashamed** - "Anyone who trusts in this foundation stone will not be sorry"
