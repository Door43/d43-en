===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See** - AT: "Listen to this"
  * **I will lay in Zion a foundation stone** - AT: "I will prepare a sure start for building in Zion" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a tried stone** - AT: "a stone that is proven to be solid"
  * **a sure foundation** - AT: "a firm support"
  * **He who believes will not be ashamed** - AT: "Anyone who believes in the foundation stone placed by Yahweh will not be sorry."
