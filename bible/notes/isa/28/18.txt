===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Jerusalem.

  * **Your covenant with death will be dissolved, and your agreement with Sheol will not stand** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will end the deal you think you have with death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **covenant with death ... agreement with Sheol** - 
  * **When the raging flood passes through** - The invasion is compared to a flood of water. AT: "When the enemy army comes like a flood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **it will overwhelm you** - "it will destroy you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
