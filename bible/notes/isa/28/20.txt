===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the bed is too short for a man to stretch out on**  - the bed is too short to get a good night's sleep lying on it.
  * **and the blanket too narrow for him to wrap himself in**  - the blanket is not wide enough to cover a man.
  * **Yahweh will rise up as on Mount Perazim**  - Yahweh will cause you to be defeated like he did to the army of philistia at Perizim Mountain.
  * **He will rouse himself as in the Valley of Gibeon**  - he will do like he did to the Amor people-group at Bigeon Valley.
  * **to do his work, his strange work, and perform his strange deed**  - what he will do will be very strange and unusual.
