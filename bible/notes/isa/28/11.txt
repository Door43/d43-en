===== translationNotes: =====

  * **with mocking lips and a foreign tongue he will speak to this people** - Here "lips" and "tongue" represent the foreigners who speak a different language than the Israelites do. It is implied that this refers to the Assyrian army that will attack Israel. AT: "Yahweh will speak to this people through enemy soldiers who will speak a foreign language" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **mocking lips** - "stammering lips"
  * **This is the rest** - The abstract noun "rest" can be stated as an adjective. AT: "This is the resting place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **give rest to him who is weary** - The abstract noun "rest" can be stated as a verb. AT: "Let whoever is tired come and rest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **this is the refreshing** - The abstract noun "refreshing" can be stated as a verb. AT: "this is the place where you can be refreshed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
