===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But even these**  - "But even the priests and prophets"
  * **reel with wine****, and stagger with strong drink**  - "walk unsteadily because they drink too much wine, and stumble after drinking strong drink"
  * **The priest and the prophet reel with strong drink, and they are swallowed up by wine **- The leaders and the people made a practice of drinking.
  * **staggering in vision and reeling in decision**  - Because they are drunk. they are not able to understand the meanings of visions correctly or make good decisions.
  * **Truly, all tables are covered with vomit, so that there is no clean place **- This compares the filth that comes out of a drunk persons mouht with the evil words and actions of the priests and prophets.  alternate translation "The priests and prophets have defiled all the holy places with filth"
