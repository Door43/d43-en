===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On this mountain** - "Here in Jerusalem"
  * **a feast of fat things** - "a wonderful feast"
  * **choice wines** - "fine, well-aged wine" (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **a feast on the lees** - "a feast of lavish food" or "a feast of rich food"
  * **the covering over all peoples** - "like a dark cloud that hangs over them"
  * **the web woven over all the nations** - This could refer to a covering of death over the people.
  * **He will swallow up death forever** - "he will get rid of death forever"
  * **Lord Yahweh will wipe away tears from off all faces** - "Yahweh our God will cause people to no longer mourn because someone has died"
  * **the disgrace of his people he will take away** - "he will stop other people insulting and making fun of his land and us his people"
  * **for Yahweh has spoken it** - "this will surely happen because Yahweh has said it"
