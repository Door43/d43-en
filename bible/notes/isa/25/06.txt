===== translationNotes: =====

  * **On this mountain** - This refers to Jerusalem or Mount Zion.
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **a feast of fat things** - Here "fat things" means the best food.
  * **a feast on the lees** - "the oldest wine." This means the best wine.
  * **the covering over all peoples, the web woven over all the nations** - Death, suffering, and sadness are spoken of as if they were a dark cloud or web that covers everyone on the earth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **He will swallow up death forever** - Yahweh causing people to live forever is spoken of as if he would swallow death. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the disgrace of his people he will take away from all the earth** - Yahweh causing the people to never be ashamed again is spoken of as if disgrace were an object that Yahweh would take away. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
