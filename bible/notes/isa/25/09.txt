===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It will be said** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "People will say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **For on this mountain the hand of Yahweh will rest** - The "hand" refers to God's power. AT: "The power of Yahweh will be on this mountain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
