===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They will spread their hands ... his hands to swim** - This simile emphasizes how badly Yahweh will humiliate the people of Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **They will spread their hands in the midst of it** - "The people of Moab will push their hands through the dung"
  * **as a swimmer spreads his hands to swim** - "as if they were swimming"
  * **will bring down their pride** - Yahweh humiliating a proud person is spoken of as if pride were something high and Yahweh would cause it to be low. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in spite of the skill of their hands** - Here "hands" represents the power to do or make something. AT: "in spite of the great things you have built" or "in spite of the great things you have done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he will bring down** - Here "he" refers to Yahweh. This represents Yahweh sending an army to tear down the walls of Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
