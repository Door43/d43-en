===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Prepare your slaughter for his children, for the iniquity of their ancestors** - "People will say, 'Slaughter this man's children because of the sins that their ancestors committed"
  * **this is the declaration of Yahweh of Hosts** - "this is what Yahweh, Commander of Angel armies says"
  * **I will cut off from Babylon name, descendant, and posterity** - "I" refers to Yahweh. He will completely destroy Babylon and its people and descendants.
  * **her** - This refers to Babylon.
  * **I will also make her into a possession of owls** - "Yahweh will make Babylon into a wilderness where owls live"
  * **sweep her with the broom of destruction** - This compares the way a broom removes all the dirt from an area to the way that Yahweh will completely remove all the people and buildings from Babylon. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
