===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The whole earth is at rest and is quiet; they break forth into singing** - The description of the earth singing is referring to the people of the earth singing again. (See: Personification)
    * **no woodcutter comes up to cut us down** - This is comparing cutting down a tree to getting rid of people. This can be translated as "no one comes to get rid of us". (See: Metaphor)
    * **Sheol below is eager to meet you when you go there** - The description of Sheol eager to meet you when you get there is referring to the dead people who are eagerly waiting for you to the place where they are. (See: Personification)
    * **the dead** - This refers to the kings of the nations who are dead.
    * **making them rise up from their thrones, all the kings of the nations** - This is referring to the dead kings who will stand up to welcome you.
