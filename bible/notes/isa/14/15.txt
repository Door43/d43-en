===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement** - This continues what the people of Israel will say to the king of Babylon. 

  * **Yet you are now brought down to Sheol** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "But now God has sent you down to Sheol" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Is this the man** - The people will use a question to mock the king of Babylon. AT: "Surely, this is not the man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **who made the earth tremble** - "The earth" refers to the people of the world. AT: "who made the people of the earth tremble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who shook kingdoms** -  "who terrified the people in all the kingdoms"
  * **who made the world a wilderness** - "who made the places where people lived into a wilderness"
