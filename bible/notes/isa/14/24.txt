===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh of Hosts**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **as I have intended, so it will come about; and as I have purposed, so it will be**  - AT: "the things that I have planned will surely happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **the Assyrian**  - AT: "the king of Assyria and his army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Then his yoke will be lifted from off them and his burden from off their shoulder**  - AT: "Then I will free the people of Israel from the king of Assyria like taking the yoke off of an ox." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
