===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is the plan that is intended for the whole earth, and this is the hand that is stretched out over all the nations** - This compares a giant hand that stretches over the world and controls the leaders like a puppet.  It can be translated "Yahweh has a plan for the whole earth and he controls what is done in all the nations." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **For the Lord of Hosts has planned this, and who will thwart him?** - This question is intended to warn about Yahweh's impending punishment on Assyria and that no one will be able to stop Him. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **His hand is stretched out, and who will turn it back?** - This question is also intended to warn about Yahweh's impending punishment of Assyria. No one will be able to stop their punishment.
