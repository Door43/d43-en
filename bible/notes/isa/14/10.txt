===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Your pomp has been brought down to Sheol** - This can be translated as, "You were very proud and powerful, but all that ended when you died" (UDB)
    * **Maggots  are spread under you, and worms cover you**  - This is a figure of speech referring to maggots in the grave spread like a sheet under them and worms that cover them like a blanket. (See: Metaphor)
