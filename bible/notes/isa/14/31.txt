  * **you will melt away** - "you will grow weak with fear"
  * **How then will one answer the messengers of that nation?** - The author uses a question to show how to decide to answer the messengers. AT: "This is how we will answer the messengers of Philistia." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
