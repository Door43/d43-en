===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **For out of the north comes a cloud of smoke** - This is a figure of speech used to describe a very powerful army that will come from the north to attack them and their chariots stir up the dust like a cloud of smoke. (See: Metaphor)
    * **and there is no straggler in his ranks** - "Each of their soldiers is ready to fight" (UDB)
    * **How then will one answer the messengers of that nation?** - This question is used to prepare them to give an answer to the messengers from Philistia who come to the Israelite people. This is what they must tell them. (See: Rhetorical question)
    * **Yahweh has founded Zion** - "Yahweh has created Jerusalem"
    * **her** - Refers to Jerusalem.
    * **find refuge** - "will be safe"
