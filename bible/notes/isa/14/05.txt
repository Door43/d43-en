===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Yahweh has broken the staff of the wicked, the sceptre of those rulers** - This is saying the same thing in two ways for emphasis and can be translated as, "Yahweh has destroyed the power of the wicked". (See: Doublet)
    * **with unceasing blows** - "many times"
    * **that ruled the nations** - "that conquered other nations"
