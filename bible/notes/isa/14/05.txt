===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh has broken the staff of the wicked, the scepter of those rulers** - This means that Yahewh has destroyed the power and the rule of the Babylonians. It can be translated "Yahweh has broken the power of wicked Babylon and crushed their strong rule. (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **with unceasing blows** - " again and again"
  * **that ruled the nations** - "that conquered other nations"
