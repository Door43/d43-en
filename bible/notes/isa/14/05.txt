===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh has broken the staff of the wicked** -  The staff of the wicked probably refers to a stick that wicked people would hit other people with. Breaking that stick represents destroying their power to treat people cruelly. AT: "Yahweh has destroyed the power of the wicked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the scepter of those rulers** - A scepter represents a ruler's power to rule. Breaking the scepter represents destroying the ruler's power. AT: "Yahweh has destroyed the power of the wicked rulers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **that struck the peoples** - Wicked people struck the peoples with their staff. AT: "who struck the peoples"
  * **with unceasing blows** - "without stopping" or "again and again"
  * **that ruled the nations** - "who conquered other nations"
  * **with an attack that was unrestrained** - "attacking them without stopping"
