===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He will set up a banner for the nations** - The banner is the symbol of power. AT: "Yahweh will set up the Messiah like a flag for the nations to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He will turn aside the envy of Ephraim** - That is, God will stop Ephraim, the northern kingdom, from envying the southern kingdom of Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **hostilities of Judah will be cut off** - In this context, this expression refers to the hatred that the people of Judah, the southern kingdom, often displayed against the people of the northern kingdom. This phrase can be stated in active form. AT: "he will stop the people of Judah from being enemies of the people of Ephraim" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
