===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaiah describes what the world will be like when the Messiah comes.

  * **leopard** - a large cat with yellow fur and black spots that kills and eats other animals
  * **bear** - a very large animal with thick hair and sharp claws that kills and eats other animals
