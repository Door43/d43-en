===== Translation Notes: =====
Isaiah continues to describe the complete peace on earth when the Messiah comes.

  * **weaned** - a child who no longer drinks mother's milk
  * **my holy mountain** - This refers to Mount Zion which is Jerusalem. It is unclear who is speaking here. AT: "his holy mountain."
