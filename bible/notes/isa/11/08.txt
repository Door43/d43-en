===== Translation Notes: =====
Isaiah continues to describe the complete peace on earth when the Messiah comes.

  * **weaned** - a child who no longer drinks mother's milk
  * **They will not hurt nor destroy** - "Nothing will hurt or destroy"
  * **my holy mountain** - This refers to Mount Zion which is Jerusalem. It is unclear who is speaking here. Alternate translation: "his holy mountain."
  * **for the earth will be full knowing Yahweh, as the waters cover the sea** - This compares the amount of those who know Yahweh to the amount of water in the sea. This emphasizes that the entire world will know Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
