===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues to describe the complete peace in the world when the king rules.

  * **the snake ... the serpent** - These phrases refer to snakes in general. AT: "snakes ... serpents" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **A baby will play over the hole of the snake** - It can be stated clearly that the baby will be safe because the snake will not bite it. AT: Babies will play safely over the hole of the snake
  * **the weaned child** - a child who no longer drinks mother's milk
  * **my holy mountain** - This refers to Mount Zion, which is Jerusalem. It is unclear who is speaking here. AT: "his holy mountain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
