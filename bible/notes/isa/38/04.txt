===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - This means that Yahweh gave his message to Yahweh. Yahweh did not give him a piece of paper, rather he imparted the message to Isaiah's inner being. AT: "Yahweh spoke his word" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **See** - This phrase is used to ask the listener to pay attention to what is said next. AT: "Listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **fifteen years** - "15 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the hand of the king of Assyria** - Here the king's "hand" refers to his power. AT: "the power of the king of Assyria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
