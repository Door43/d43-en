===== Translation Notes: =====
Hezekiah continues his written prayer.

    * **save me** - "save me from dying"
    * **we will celebrate** - Here "we" refers to Hezekiah and the people of Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
