===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking about his servant.

  * **startle **- to surprise suddenly, possibly from fear, making someone move without thinking about it
  * **That which they had not been told**  - AT: "That which no one had told them" or "Something no one had told them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
