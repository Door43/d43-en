===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he will be high and elevated; he will be very exalted**  - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are repeated to emphasize the his greatness. AT for one single expression could be: "I will give my servant the greatest honor" (See the way you translated something similar in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:06:01|52:13]]; [[:en:bible:notes:isa:33:10|33:10]] and [[:en:bible:notes:isa:57:14|57:15]].) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **high and elevated**  - These words mean basically the same thing.  AT: "highly elevated" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **horrified at you**  - Here "you" refers to Yahweh's servant. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you|Forms of You]])
  * **his appearance was so disfigured out of human appearance, so that the sight of him was far from anything human**  - AT: "His enemies beat his body so badly that he did not even look like a human anymore"
