===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Awake, awake**  - This word is repeated for emphasis and shows urgency. Isaiah is speaking to the people of Israel to arouse them, not to //literally//  wake them from sleep. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe|Apostrophe]])
  * **Zion…Jerusalem**  - AT: "people of Zion…people of Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **put on your strength**  - AT: "show your strength" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
