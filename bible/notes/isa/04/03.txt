===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will be called holy**  - This can be translated with an active clause: "Yahweh will call them holy" or "Yahweh will be pleased with them" or "will belong to God" (UDB) (See: Active)
  * **will have washed away the filth of the daughters of Zion, and will have cleansed the blood stains from Jerusalem's midst **- This compares the stains of sin with physical dirt that needs to be scrubbed away. It can be translated as "will have removed the sins of impure living from the women of Jerusalem, and will have purged the shedding of innocent blood from the people of Jerusalem" (See: Metaphor)
  * **by the spirit of justice, and by the spirit of burning fire **- This can be translated as "the Lord will use deserved consequences and a blast of fiery judgment to cleanse the people" (See: Active)
