===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he who is left in Zion, and he who remains in Jerusalem**  - The Hebrews often repeated things in different words as a way of emphasis. Alternate translation: "whoever remains in Jerusalem." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
  * **will be called holy**  - This can be translated with an active clause: "Yahweh will call them holy" or "will belong to God" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **every one who is recorded as living in Jerusalem**  - "every one whose name is written in the list of the people who live in Jerusalem"
  * **will have washed away the filth of the daughters of Zion, and will have cleansed the blood stains from Jerusalem's midst**  - This compares moral sins with physical dirt that one washes away with water. It can be translated as "will have removed the sins of the women of Jerusalem, and will have taken away those in Jerusalem who harm innocent people." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **by the spirit of justice, and by the spirit of burning fire**  - This can be translated as a new sentence. "And the Lord will do this with his spirit that judges fairly and like a fire will purify the people."
  * **a canopy over all the glory**  - "a glorious cover over the entire city"
