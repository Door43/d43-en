===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The ox knows his owner, and the donkey his master’s feeding trough, but Israel does not know, Israel does not understand - **  This metaphor compares an animal who knows its master to the people of Israel who do not. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **ox...donkey** - These are animals. They can be translated with plural nouns: oxen...donkeys. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
  * **but Israel does not know, Israel does not understand**  - This is saying the same thing in two ways. It can be translated "Israel certainly certainly does not know that God is her master" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
  * **Ah, nation, sinners, a people weighed down with iniquity **  - This is saying the same thing in two ways. It can be translated "a nation of people loaded with much sin" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
  * **They have abandoned Yahweh, they have despised the Holy One of Israel **  - This is saying the same thing in two ways. It can be translated "they have turned their backs on Yahweh, the Holy one of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
  * **they have estranged themselves from him**  - "they have separated themselves from him"
