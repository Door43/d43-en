===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **faithful city** - "city that in the past honored me" The faithful city refers to Jerusalem.
  * **prostitute **- Isaiah compares Jerusalem to a prostitute, a woman who performs sexual acts for money. In the Bible, God sometimes uses the word “prostitute” to figuratively refer to a person who worships false gods or who practices witchcraft. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **she** - This pronoun refers to Jerusalem and its people. In the Bible, cities are often referred to as women.
  * **your silver has become impure, your wine mixed with water** - These two phrases say the same thing for emphasis to show how sinful the people have become. (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]]) and (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
