===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For you will be ashamed of the sacred oak trees that you desired** - The people were worshiping false gods.
  * **For you will be as an oak whose leaf fades, and as a garden that has no water.- ** The people who are worshipping false gods are being compared to a tree or garden that does not get nourishment. (see: Simile)
