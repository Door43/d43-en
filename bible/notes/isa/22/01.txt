===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A declaration about the valley of vision** - "Yahweh's warning about Jerusalem". Jerusalem was surrounded by mountains. Many people came to this valley to hear a message from Yahweh about what would happen in the future
  * **What is the reason that you all have gone up to the housetops? A noisy city, a town full of revelry** - The city was in disorder as people watched and prepared for the coming attack of the enemy
