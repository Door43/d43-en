===== translationNotes: =====

  * **but they were caught without a bow** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "but the enemy caught your rulers who were not even carrying a bow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **all of them were caught and captured together** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the enemy caught and captured all of them together" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Therefore I said** - Here "I" refers to Isaiah.
  * **of the daughter of my people** - Here "daughter" represents the people and may imply Isaiah's feeling of love for them. AT: "of my people whom I love" or "of my people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
