===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For there is a day of tumult, treading down, and confusion for the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts** - Yahweh has warned the people of this day when the enemy will destroy the city, but when it happened, the leaders are uncertain about what to do.
  * **in the valley of vision** - See how you translated this in 22:1
  * **a breaking down of the walls, and people crying out to the mountains** - "The strong cities will be destroyed, and people will make such a loud wail that it will resound off the mountains surrounding Jerusalem"
  * **Elam and Kir** - These are two nations that joined with the Assyrian army against Judah. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **Elam takes up the quiver, with chariots of men and horsemen, and Kir lays the shield bare** - They prepare for battle with bows and arrows, some rode in chariots, some fought on foot,and they have taken the shield out of the case to prepare to fight.
  * **will take their positions at the gate** - “will set themselves in battle array at your gates”
