===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues to speak God's message to Shebna.

  * **He will surely wind you round and round, and toss you like a ball into a vast country** - The enemy soldiers coming and taking Shebna as a captive to a foreign land is spoken of as if Yahweh were throwing him like a ball into another land. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will be the shame of your master's house** - Here "house" represents the people who work in the king's palace. AT: "you will cause shame for all those in your master's palace." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will thrust you from your office and from your station. You will be pulled down** - Yahweh causing Shebna to no longer work in the king's palace is spoken of as if Yahweh will throw him to the ground. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **You will be pulled down** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will bring you down from your honored position" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
