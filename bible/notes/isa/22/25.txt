===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Shebna is like a peg that is firmly fastened to the wall. But there will be a time when I will remove him from his position; he will lose his power, and everything that he promoted will fail**. – Shebna will be like a peg forcibly removed from the wall. It can be translated - -“ I will forcibly remove Shebna from his position and his influence and power will be cut off and fall.”
