===== translationNotes: =====


  * **It is as if he were eating ashes** - Yahweh compares worshiping idols to eating ashes. This means the idols are worthless and cannot help anyone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **his deceived heart misleads him** - The heart represents the inner person. AT: "he has deceived himself and gone astray" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He cannot rescue himself** - "The person who worships idols cannot save himself"
