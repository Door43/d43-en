===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **Jacob my servant** - This refers to Jacob's descendants. AT: "descendants of Jacob, my servants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he who made you and formed you in the womb** - Yahweh speaks of creating the nation of Israel as if it were forming the nation as a baby in its mother's womb. AT: "he who made you, as I form a baby in the womb" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you, Jeshurun, whom I have chosen** - The verb may be supplied from the previous phrase. AT: "you, Jeshurun, whom I have chosen, do not fear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Jeshurun** - This also refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
