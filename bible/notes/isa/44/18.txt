===== translationNotes: =====


  * **for their eyes are blind and cannot see** - Yahweh speaks of those who cannot understand the foolishness of worshiping idols as if they were blind. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for their eyes are blind** - Here "their eyes" represents the whole person. AT: "for they are blind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **their hearts cannot perceive** - Here the word "hearts" represent the minds and thoughts of the people. AT: "they cannot understand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
