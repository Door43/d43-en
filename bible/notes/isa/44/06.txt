===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his Redeemer** - "Israel's Redeemer"
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **I am the first, and I am the last** - This phrase emphasizes Yahweh's eternal nature. Possible meanings are 1) "I am the one who began all things, and I am the one who ends all things" or 2) "I am the one who has always lived, and I am the one who always will live." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
