===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking.
  * **all his associates will be put to shame** - AT: "I will put to shame all who help him make his idol" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Let them take their stand together** - "Let them all come together before me"
  * **they will cower and be put to shame** - AT: "they will be afraid and I will put them to shame"
