===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **with a line** - A string was used to outline the shape of the idol in the wood.
    * **stylus** - This is a sharp tool to scratch the wood so the craftsman can see here to cut,
    * **a compass** - This is a tool with two points that spread out used to help mark the wood to make the idol.
