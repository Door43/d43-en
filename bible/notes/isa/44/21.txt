===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **Jacob, and Israel** - This refers to people descended from Jacob, Israel. AT: "you descendants of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **you will not be forgotten by me** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will not forget you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I have blotted out, like a thick cloud, your rebellious deeds, and like a cloud, your sins** - Both of these statements mean the same thing. God has removed their sins as quickly and easily as a wind can blow away a cloud. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like a cloud, your sins** - The verb may be supplied from the previous phrase. AT: "like a cloud, I have blotted out your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
