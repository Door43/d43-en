===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel in exile through Isaiah.

  * **everyone who is thirsty, come to the water** - This metaphor points to the spiritual thirst of the people for Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **come** - There is an urgency shown through three uses of "come."
  * **wine and milk** - The land of Judah produced wine and milk. These are symbols of God's mercy.
