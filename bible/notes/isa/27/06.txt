===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah is speaking. He continues to describe the people of Israel as a vineyard. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:27:01|27:2]])

  * **In the coming day** - This speaks about a day as if it travels and arrives somewhere. AT: "In the future" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Jacob will take root; Israel will blossom and bud** - Yahweh blessing the people of Israel and causing them to prosper is spoken of as if they were a vine that grows roots and blossoms. AT: "the descendants of Israel will prosper like a vine that has taken root and blossomed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Jacob ... Israel** - Here "Jacob" and "Israel" are metonyms that represent the descendants of Jacob. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they will fill the surface of the ground with fruit** - Yahweh causing the people of Israel prosper greatly so that they can help other people is spoken of as if they were a vine that grows so much fruit that it would cover the earth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
