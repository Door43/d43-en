===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the coming day**- "There is a time in the future"
  * **Jacob will take root; Israel will blossom and bud**- "Jacob" and "Israel" here refer to the descendants of Jacob, the people of Israel. The people are being again compared to a vine or vineyard; taking root they will be well established in relationship with Yahweh; blossom and bud they will be prosperous.  Alternate translation "The people of Israel will be well established in a relationship with Yahweh and prosperous in all they do"
  * **they will fill the surface of the ground with fruit**- "they will bless other nations with their good works"
