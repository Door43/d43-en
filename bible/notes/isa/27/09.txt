===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So in this way** - "By sending them away into exile"
  * **the iniquity of Jacob will be atoned for** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will atone for the sins of the people of Israel" or "Yahweh will provide a payment for the sins of the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for this will be the full fruit of turning away from his sin** - AT: "for this is what will happen when Yahweh removes their sin"
  * **He will make all the altar stones as chalk and pulverized, and no Asherah poles or incense altars will remain standing** - "They will completely destroy all the altars to false gods and remove all the Asherah idols"
