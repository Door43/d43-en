===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The horde of your invaders will be like small dust, and the multitude of the ruthless ones as chaff that passes away.** - This emphasizes how weak and insignificant the invading army is before God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **horde of your invaders** - "the many soldiers that will attack you"
  * **the ruthless ones as chaff** - The translator can supply the verb "will be". AT: "the soldiers who show you no mercy will be as chaff" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Yahweh of hosts will come to you** - The word "you" refers to the people of Jerusalem. Possible meanings are 1) "Yahweh of hosts will come to help you" or 2) "Yahweh of hosts will come to punish you"
