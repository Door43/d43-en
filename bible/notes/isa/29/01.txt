===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ariel **  - Refers to Jerusalem- Ariel refers to an altar - a place of bloodshed and sacrifice. Jerusalem was a place of sacrifices.
  * **Woe to Ariel**  –This is a message of bad news to Jerusalem
  * **Add year to year; let the festivals come round**. –“ Year after year you go through your ritual of sacrifices”
  * **Besiege Ariel**  – “bring great distress to Ariel”
  * **Like Ariel - ** A play on the word Ariel. Jerusalem will become a place of human blood shed -" you yourselves will experience bloodshed"
