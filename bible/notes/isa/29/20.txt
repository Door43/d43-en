===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the ruthless will cease, and the scoffer will vanish**  –“The cruel and proud persons will come to an end”
  * **All those who love evil will be eliminated**– “All who plan evil will be removed”
  * **by a word make a man out to be an offender**  –“ by their false testimony condemn a person”
  * **They lay a snare for him who seeks justice at the gate **-" They trick those who expect to receive justice in the courts"
  * **put the the righteous down with empty lies**  –“who condemn the righteous based on false testimony”
