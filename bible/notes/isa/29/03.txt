===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I (Yahweh) will encamp against you (Jerusalem) in a circle and will lay siege against you with a palisade, and I will raise siege works against you. - "**Yahweh will be as a army against you by surrounding and laying siege to Jerusalem"
  * **You will be brought down and will speak from the ground; your speech will be low from the dust.–**This expresses their great humiliation that instead of their speech being proud and haughty, they have few words (See: Metaphor)
  * **Your voice will be like a ghost out of the ground, and your speech will be very weak from the dust**. -Their voice will be like a dying man – weak and quivering (See: Simile)
