===== Translation Notes: =====

• **This people draws near to me with their mouth and honor me with their lips** –“ These people profess with their mouths to have a relationship with me”

• **but their heart is far from me** – “but their thoughts and actions deny that they have that relationship”

• **is a commandment taught by men** –“consist of teachings that come from men”

• **Therefore, see, I will proceed to do a marvelous thing among this people, wonder after wonder **- "Therefore, look and see! I am going to do something among you that you cannot explain".

• • **The wisdom of their wise men will perish**–“Their wise men will no longer be capable of making wise decisions”

• **will disappear** – "will be hidden from them"
