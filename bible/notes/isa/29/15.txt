===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This may be Isaiah speaking or it may continue Yahweh's speech in 29:13-14.

  * **who deeply hide their plans from Yahweh** - People trying to make plans without Yahweh knowing about it is spoken of as if they hide their plans in a deep place where Yahweh cannot see. AT: "who try to hide their plans" or "who try to keep Yahweh from finding out what they are planning to do" [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]
  * **whose deeds are in darkness** - It is implied that they are secretly doing evil things. AT: "who do evil things in the dark so no one can see them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Who sees us, and who knows us?** - They use a question to emphasize that they believe no one knows what they are doing. AT: "No one, not even Yahweh, sees us or knows what we are doing!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
