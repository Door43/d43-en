===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking.
  * **I will smooth out all his paths** - This means God will cause Cyrus to accomplish everything God has planned. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He will build my city** - AT: "He will command the people to build my city, Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
