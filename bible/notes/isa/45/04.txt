===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Cyrus.
  * **Jacob...Israel** - These both refer to the descendants of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **I will arm you** - AT: "I will strengthen you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **from the rising of the sun, and from the west** - AT: "from every place on earth" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
