===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **An earthen potsherd among all the earthen potsherds in the ground** - Yahweh speaks of himself as if he were a potter, and of the one who would argue with him as if that person and the rest of humanity were all clay pots. AT: "You are like one piece of broken pottery among many other pieces of broken pottery scattered on the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **An earthen potsherd** - Possible meanings are 1) "A piece of broken clay pottery" or 2) "A clay pot."
  * **Should the clay say to the potter ... did it?'** - God uses a question to rebuke those who argue with him about what he does. (See: UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
