  * **the mind that is stayed on you** - "You" refers to Yahweh. AT: "those whose thoughts are continually on you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yah, Yahweh** - Yah is another name for Yahweh.
