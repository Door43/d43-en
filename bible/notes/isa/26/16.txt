===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they looked to you** - Here "they" refers to the people of Israel. This would include Isaiah. AT: "we looked to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **looked to you** - This idiom means they asked Yahweh for help. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **when your discipline was on them** - The abstract noun "discipline" can be stated as a verb. AT: "when you disciplined them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **As a pregnant woman ... cries out in her labor pains** - This compares the people to a woman giving birth. This emphasizes their suffering and crying when Yahweh disciplined them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
