===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The path of the righteous is level** – “The way of the righteous is flat”
  * **O Righteous One, the path of the righteous you make straight** – “Yahweh makes the way straight for those who do what is right”  
  * **in the way of your judgments, O Yahweh, we wait for you** - "the Israelites wait for Yahweh to finish this time of judgement"  
  * **your name and your memory are the desire of our soul** - The Israelites desire to once again know and be known by Yahweh.
  * **My soul desires you in the night** - "The Israelites long for Yahweh in the time of their sorrow"
  * **learn righteousness** - "learn to do what is right"
