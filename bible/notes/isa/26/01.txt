===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In that day**  - "Someday in the future"
  * **We have a strong city**  - This is referring to the city of Jerusalem.
  * **God has made salvation its walls and ramparts**  - "Yahweh has protected our city like a wall that surrounds a city" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **Open the gates, that the righteous nation that keeps faith**  - "Open the gates of Jerusalem for the faithful people who obey Yahweh"
