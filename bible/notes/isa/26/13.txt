===== translationNotes: =====

  * **but we praise your name alone** - The "name" refers to the person of God. AT: "but we praise you alone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they will not arise** - "they will not come back to life"
  * **made every memory of them to perish** - Yahweh causing people to no longer remember those he destroyed is spoken of as if Yahweh made their memory perish or die. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
