===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **our God** - Here "our" refers to Isaiah and includes all the righteous people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **but we praise your name alone** - AT: "but we praise you alone" or "but we praise your reputation alone" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
