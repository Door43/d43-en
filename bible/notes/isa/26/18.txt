===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **We have been pregnant, we have been in labor** - This is comparing the pain the pregnant woman goes through to the suffering the people have endured. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
    * **but it is as if we have brought forth only wind** - All the pain and suffering they went through has not brought about any good in their life. This can be translated as, "but it has resulted in no good."
    * **We have not brought any deliverance to the earth** - "our people have not been rescued from being conquered by our enemies"
    * **and the inhabitants of the world do not fall** - "and our enemies did not die in battle"
