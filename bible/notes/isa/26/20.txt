===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the earth will uncover her bloodshed** - Here earth is personified as if it can uncover something. The blood refers to the blood of the people who have been murdered. (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **and will no longer conceal her slain** - "and their dead bodies will be shown"
