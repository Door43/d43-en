===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **like smoke … like a garment … like flies**  - All of these refer to things that quickly and easily disappear or become useless. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
