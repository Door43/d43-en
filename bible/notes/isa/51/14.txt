===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **The one who is bent down** - This refers to the people of Israel who slaves of the Babylonians. This phrase describes their work. AT: "The slave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the pit** - This refers to Sheol. AT: "the pit of Sheol" or "the grave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **nor will he lack bread** - This can be written in positive form. AT: "and he will have bread" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **bread** - This refers to food in general. AT: "food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **who churns up the sea** - This speaks of Yahweh causing the sea to move and the waves to rise and fall as if he stirred the sea as one stirs the contents of a bowl with a large spoon. AT: "who cause the sea to churn" or "who makes the sea move up and down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **its waves roar** - A roar is a powerful loud sound made by something that is alive, such as a bear or lion. Here the crashing of the waves are described as making this powerful sound. AT: "its waves crash loudly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - Translate this name for Yahweh the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
