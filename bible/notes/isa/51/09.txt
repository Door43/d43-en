===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Is it not you who crushed the sea monster, you who pierced the dragon?** - This is a rhetorical question meant to emphasize Yahweh's power and ability to do might things just like he crushed the sea monster and pierced the dragon. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **and make the depths of the sea into a way for the redeemed to pass through?** - This rhetorical question is also used to emphasize Yahweh's power to do great things like he dried the Red Sea and made a path for the Israelites to pass through. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
