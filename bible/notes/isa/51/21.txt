===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **See, I have taken the cup of staggering from your hand** - This means God will stop being angry with them and stop causing them to suffer. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
