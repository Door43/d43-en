===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **Abraham, your father** - God speaks of their ancestor as if he were their father. AT: "Abraham, your forefather" or "Abraham, your ancestor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Sarah, who bore you** - God speaks of Abraham's wife as if she were their mother and had given give birth to them. AT: "Sarah, your mother" or "Sarah, Abraham's wife"
  * **when he was a lone individual** - This means that he did not have any children yet. This can be stated clearly. AT: "when he had no children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **made him many** - God speaks of Abraham's descendants being many as if Abraham were many. AT: "made his descendants many" or "made him have many descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
