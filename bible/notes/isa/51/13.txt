===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **Why have you forgotten Yahweh**  - Yahweh uses a question to emphasize that they should not forget Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Maker**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:17:06|17:7]].
  * **who stretched out the heavens**  - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:40:21|40:22]].
  * **the foundations of the earth**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:24:17|24:18]].
  * **Where is the fury of the oppressor?**  - Yahweh uses a question to emphasize that the people should not fear those who want to oppress them. They are no longer a threat.
