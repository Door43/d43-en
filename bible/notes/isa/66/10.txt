===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking about Jerusalem as if it were a mother and the inhabitants of Jerusalem as newly born children. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:66:07|66:7-8]])

  * **For you will nurse and be satisfied; with her breasts you will be comforted**  - This means Jerusalem will be a place of safety and comfort for God's people. AT: "For she will satisfy you with her milk; she will comfort you with her breasts." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
