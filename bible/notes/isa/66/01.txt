===== translationNotes: =====

  * **heaven is my throne, and the earth is my footstool** - Yahweh compares heaven to a throne and the earth to a footstool to emphasize how great he is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Where then is the house you will build for me? Where is the place where I may rest?** - Yahweh uses questions to emphasize that humans cannot build a place for him to dwell. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
