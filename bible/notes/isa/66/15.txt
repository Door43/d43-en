===== Translation Notes: =====
Isaiah continues speaking to God's faithful people.
  * **coming with fire** - Here "fire" refers to God's holiness and his terrible anger about the people's sin.
  * **like the windstorm** - This means the chariots will come quickly and with great power. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **with his sword** - This refers to God's power to judge and to kill his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Those slain by Yahweh will be many** - AT: "Yahweh will kill many people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
