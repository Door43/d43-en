===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaiah continues speaking to God's faithful people.

  * **coming with fire**  - God's appearances in the Old Testament are often accompanied by fire.
  * **like the windstorm**  - Clouds are often called the chariots of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **with his sword**  - This refers to God's power to judge and to kill his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **Those killed by Yahweh will be many**  - AT: "Yahweh will kill many people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
