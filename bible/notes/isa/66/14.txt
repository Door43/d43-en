===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah is speaking to God's faithful people.

  * **your bones will sprout** - "Bones" refers to the whole body as a part of it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **will sprout like the tender grass** - "Tender grass" grows fast and strong and compares to the health and vigor of God's faithful people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **The hand of Yahweh will be made known to his servants** - Here "hand" refers to his power. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will reveal his power to his servants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
