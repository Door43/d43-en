===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **They will go out** - Here "they" refers to all the people, the faithful Israelites and foreigners, who come to worship Yahweh.
  * **will not be quenched** - This can be expressed positively. AT: "will burn forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
