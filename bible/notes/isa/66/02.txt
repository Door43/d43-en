===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **My hand has made all these things** - "Hand" refers to the power and authority of Yahweh, not his physical body. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:14:21|14:22]].
  * **the broken and contrite in spirit** - Here "broken" and "contrite" refers to a person who is truly humble and who suffers for his faith.
