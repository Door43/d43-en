===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:14:21|14:22]].
  * **the broken and contrite in spirit** - Here "broken" and "contrite" means basically the same thing. It refers to a person who is truly humble. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
