===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **I will uphold you with the right hand of my deserved victory** - Here "right hand" refers to God's power. AT: "I will support you with my powerful and righteous hand." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
