===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **I will uphold you with the right hand of my deserved victory** - Yahweh strengthening his people is spoken of as if he were holding them with his hand. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the right hand of my deserved victory** - Here "right hand" represents Yahweh's power. Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh's right hand is righteous in that he will always do the right thing or 2) Yahweh's right hand is victorious in that he will always succeed in what he does. AT: "my righteous power" or "my victorious power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
