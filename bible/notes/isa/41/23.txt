===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues mocking the idols and the people who worship them. (See: [[en:bible:notes:isa:41:21|41:21-22]])

  * **Do something good or evil** - AT: "Do anything at all" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **he who chooses you** - Here "you" is plural and refers to the idols. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
