===== translationNotes: =====


  * **it will again be destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "armies will again destroy the land of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **as a terebinth or an oak is cut down and whose trunk remains, the holy seed is in its stump** - This simile means that even after Yahweh destroys Israel, he will still set apart people from among the Israelites to serve him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **terebinth** - a kind of oak tree (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **trunk ... stump** - The "trunk" is the thick main stem of a tree. The "stump" is the part of a tree that remains in the ground after the tree is cut down. 
  * **the holy seed** - The people who will serve Yahweh after destroys Israel is spoken of as if they were a holy seed. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
