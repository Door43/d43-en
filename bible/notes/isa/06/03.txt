===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues to describe his vision.

  * **Each one called to another and said** - "The seraphim called out to one another" or "The creatures proclaimed to one another"
  * **Holy, holy, holy, is Yahweh of hosts** - Repeating the word "holy" three times indicates God is completely holy. AT: "Yahweh of hosts is holy beyond all else" or "Yahweh of hosts is completely holy"
  * **The whole earth is full of his glory** - "The glory of Yahweh fills all the earth"
