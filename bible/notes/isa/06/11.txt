===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Lord, how long?** - This can be stated with the understood information. AT: "Lord, how long should I do this?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Until cities crash into ruins and are without inhabitants, and the houses are without people** - "Until all the cities and houses are ruined and no one lives there" 
  * **the land falls into a desolate waste** - Here "falls into" is an idiom that means to become something worse. AT: "until the land becomes a desolate waste" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **until Yahweh has sent the people far away, and the solitude of the land is great** - Here Yahweh speaks about himself in the third person. AT: "until I, Yahweh, have sent all the people far away from their land, so that no one is left" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
