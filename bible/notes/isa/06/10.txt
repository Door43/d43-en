===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Make the heart of this people calloused, and make their ears deaf, and blind their eyes**  - Yahweh will make the people unwilling to accept Isaiah's message.
  * **so that they will not see with their eyes or hear with their ears, and understand with their heart, and then turn again and be healed**  - " so the people will refuse to turn back to Yahweh to be be healed"
