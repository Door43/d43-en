===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you** - Here this refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
  * **draw water from the wells of salvation** - This compares salvation to a deep well that won't run dry.  The people are promised an unending source of blessing. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **and call upon his name** - This can be translated as "and praise the name of Yahweh." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **proclaim that his name is exalted** - "Tell everyone that Yahweh's name is great"
