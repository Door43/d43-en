===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **With joy**  - Joy can describe how a person feels and can be translated as "happiness." See list of Important terms concerning how to translate this.
  * **you**  - This is a plural for of "you" and is referring to more then one person. (See: Forms of 'you' - Dual/Plural)
  * **draw water from the wells of salvation - **  This is a metaphor that refers to God as the source of spiritual power and can be translated as "be rescued by God's power." (See: Metaphor)
  * **Yahweh - **  This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the Important Term page concerning how to translate this.
