===== translationNotes: =====

  * **With joy you will draw water from the wells of salvation** - Isaiah speaks of people being saved as if they were getting salvation the way people get water out of well. AT: "you will rejoice when he saves you, as people rejoice when they draw water from a well" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **call upon his name** - Here "his name" refers to Yahweh. Calling on him represents either praising him or asking him for help. AT: "praise him loudly" or "call to him to help you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **declare his deeds among the peoples** - The noun "deeds" can be expressed with the phrase "what he has done". AT: "Tell the peoples about the great things he has done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  *  **proclaim that his name is exalted** - Here "his name" refers to Yahweh. AT: "proclaim that he is exalted" or "proclaim that he is great" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
