===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On that day**  - "After the judgment and restoration of the remnant of Israel takes place"
  * **you will say - **"the people of Jerusalem will say"
  * **Yahweh - **  This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the Important Term page concerning how to translate this.
  * **your wrath has turned away**  - "your anger has become less"
  * **I will trust in you - **"I will put my hope in you"
  * **will not fear - **"will not be afraid"
