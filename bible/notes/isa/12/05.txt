===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let this be known throughout the earth** - "let people all over the earth know what Yahweh has done"
  * **Cry aloud and shout** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **you inhabitants of Zion** - The word "you" refers to all the people living in Jerusalem, but Isaiah includes himself as part of these people. Alternate translation: "you people of Jerusalem" (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **for great in your midst is the Holy One of Israel** - See how you translated "Holy One" in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:04|1:4]]. Alternate translation: "because the Holy One of Israel lives mightily among us"
