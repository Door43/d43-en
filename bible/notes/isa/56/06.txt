===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who join themselves to Yahweh **  - This refers to non-Hebrews who have joined Yahweh's people.
  * **who love the name of Yahweh**  - AT: "who love Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **holy mountain**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:11:08|11:9]].
  * **will be accepted on my altar**  - AT: "I will accept on my altar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **my house will be called a house of prayer**  - AT: "my house will be a house of prayer"
