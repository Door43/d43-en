===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in my house and within my walls** - These two phrases mean the same thing. AT: "inside the walls of my temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **that will never be cut off** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that will never end" or "that will never be forgotten" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
