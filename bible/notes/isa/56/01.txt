===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for my salvation is near, and my vindication is about to be revealed.** - AT: "I will soon save you and show you that I am righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **and whoever who holds it tightly** - AT: "and whoever is careful to always do this" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and keeps his hand from doing any evil** - Here "hand" refers to the person's actions or behaviors. AT: "and does not do evil things" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
