===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The city of chaos**  - This does not refer to one city, but to many cities. 
  * **The city of chaos has been broken down; every house is closed up and empty.** - The cities have been so completely destroyed that homes cannot be entered or inhabited.
  * **because of the wine**  - "because there is no wine"
  * **all joy is darkened, the gladness of the land has disappeared**  - Both of these phrases say that happiness is gone from the earth. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
