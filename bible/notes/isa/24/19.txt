===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The earth will stagger like a drunken man** - Many earthquakes with shake the earth so that it appears like a drunken man. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **like a hammock** - A hammock is a swinging bed usually made of netting or canvas and held by cords from supports at each end.
  * **will sway to and fro like a hammock.** - The violent earthquakes will cause the earth to sway back and forth like a hammock moves in the wind. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **The transgression of it will be heavy on it, and it will fall and not rise again.** - "The sins of the people will be heavy on all the earth, and it will fall and not rise again"  Yahweh will judge and punish the earth because of the people's rebellion against Yahweh.
