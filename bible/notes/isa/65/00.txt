====== Isaiah 65 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of this chapter, which are extended quotations.

This chapter is Yahweh's response to the repentance of the people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:repent]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Judgment and hope ==
This chapter records that Yahweh's judgment on the people must come. Despite this, there is hope that in the end, Yahweh will make all things new and restore them to himself. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:judge]] and [[:en:obe:kt:restore]])
