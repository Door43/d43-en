===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking about his faithful people.

  * **one hundred years** - "100 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Anyone who does not reach one hundred years old** - Some modern versions have "A sinner of one hundred years."
  * **one hundred years old will be considered a young person** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "one hundred years old, people will consider him a young person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
