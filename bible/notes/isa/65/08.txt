===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As when juice is found in a cluster of grapes** - Yahweh compares the people of Israel to a cluster of grapes that still has some good juice in it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **when juice is found in a cluster** - This can be stated in an active way. AT: "when you find juice in a cluster" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I will not ruin them all** - This can be expressed positively. AT: "I will spare some of those of them who are righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
