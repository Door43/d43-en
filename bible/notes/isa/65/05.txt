===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking about the people of Israel.

  * **These things are smoke in my nose, a fire that burns all day long**  - This means these people continually irritate Yahweh and make him angry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
