===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **is upon me** - Here "me" refers to Yahweh's unknown servant. It may refer to Isaiah or to promised Messiah.
  * **to proclaim liberty to the captives, and the opening of the prison to those who are bound** - This may not just mean freeing the exiles from captivity. It may also mean the servant will remove their sins.
