====== Isaiah 23 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of this chapter, which is an extended quotation.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Phoenicia ==
This chapter prophecies destruction against several cities in Phoenicia as well as in Tarshish. The exact location of Tarshish is unknown. Because of the locations in this chapter, it appears that the Assyrians will conquer many of the nations trading on the Mediterranean Sea.
