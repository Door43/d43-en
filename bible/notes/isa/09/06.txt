===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the rule will be on his shoulder**  - "The child will carry the load of leadership.
  * **as he rules on the throne of David, and over his kingdom, to establish it and sustain it**  - This can be translated as, "He will rule fairly and justly, like his ancestor King David did." (See: metonymy)
