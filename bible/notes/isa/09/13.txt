===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yet the people will not turn to him who struck them**  - "But even though Yahweh will punish his people like that they still will not return to him and worship him." (UDB)
  * **nor will they seek Yahweh of Hosts**  - "They still will not ask Yahweh, Lord of the angel armies, to help them."
  * **head and tail, palm branch and reed**  - This includes those at the top and at the bottom.
  * **in one day**  - "suddenly"
  * **The leader and the noble man are the head **- This is stated in two ways for emphasize. (See: Doublet)
  * **tail**  - This is referring to the prophet that teaches lies. Both the leaders and the prophet are giving them bad advice.
