===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Therefore Yahweh will raise up against him, Rezin, his adversary** - See how you translated Rezin in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:07:01|7:1]]. AT: "Therefore, Yahweh will send the enemy of Rezin against the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and will stir up his enemies** - This means to push them into action. AT: "and Yahweh will make the enemy armies attack the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They will devour Israel with open mouth** - "Devour" is how wild animals eat their prey. AT: "Like a wild beast eating its prey, the army of the enemy will destroy the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his hand will still be raised to strike** - "Hand" refers to punishing people. AT: "he is ready to punish them more with his powerful hand" or "he will punish them again with his power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
