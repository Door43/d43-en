===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the yoke of his burden, the beam across his should, the rod of his oppressor, you have shattered**
- these words show that the condition of the people have been completely changed from slaves to free. (See: Metaphor)

  * **as on the day of Midian **- a past battle when the army of the Midian people-group were destroyed
  * **For every boot treading in the tumult and the garments rolled in blood **- vivid description of soldiers of war
  * **fuel for the fire **- the gear of war becomes nothing, just something to be burned up in a big fire
