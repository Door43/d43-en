===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The gloom will be dispelled **- there will be no more gloom​
  * **the way to the sea, beyond the Jordan,Galilee of the nations **- this is the area between the Jordan and the Mediterranean Sea
  * **have seen a great light **- the opposite of darkness, the heart of the verse is the two opposites. see: metaphor
  * **the land of the shadow of death**- this is the land of great troubles
