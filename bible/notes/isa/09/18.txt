===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Wickedness burns like a fire** - "When people do wicked things, it is like a brush fire that spreads rapidly" (UBD) (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **overflowing anger of Yahweh of Hosts** - Yahweh is very angry.
  * **and the people are like fuel for the fire** - "the people become worthless like fuel for the fire" (See: Simile)
  * **No man spares his brother** - "Not even his brother will be rescued from the fire."
