===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Those who lead this people lead them astray** - "Those who lead lead this people lead them in the wrong way"
  * **those who are led by them are swallowed up** - "those who follow these leaders are destroyed"
  * **nor will he have compassion on their fatherless and widows** - The fatherless and widows were often the objects of special mercy, but the punishment will extend to even these.
  * **Because of all this his anger does not subside** - "Because of all this Yahweh's anger continues against them"
  * **but his hand will still be stretched out to strike** - "but his punishment is not finished yet"
