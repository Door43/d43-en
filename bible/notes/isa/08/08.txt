===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and it will sweep onward into Judah, flooding and passing on, until it reaches to the your neck **- This is a metaphor and can be translated as, "and the soldiers will go all over Judah, like a river whose waters rises as high as a person's neck." (UDB) (See: Metaphor)
  * **Its outstretched wings will fill your entire land ** - This is also a metaphor and can be translated as, "Their army will spread over the land quickly, like an eagle" (UDB)
  * **O Immanuel**  - "But Yahweh will be with you"
