===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will be a sanctuary **  - This is a singular form of 'you' and can be translated as, "He will protect you." (See: Forms of 'you' - Singular)
  * **but he will be a stone that makes one trip and a rock that makes one stumble ** - This is a metaphor that can be translated as " but Yahweh will cause his enemies to stumble and fall." This is also a doublet where the same thought is repeated in two ways for emphasis. (See: Metaphor and Doublet)
  * **to both the houses of Israel **- This is a metonym that refers to the "other people in Judah and Jerusalem." (See: Metonymy)
  * **as a trap and as a snare **- This is a doublet where the same thought is repeated in two ways for emphasis. (See Doublet)
  * **inhabitants of Jerusalem **- "people living in Jerusalem"
  * **fall and be broken**  - "fall down and never get up again" (UDB)
