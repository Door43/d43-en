===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Bind up my testimony, seal the official record</font>    **- This is a doublet, where the same thought is stated in two ways for emphasis. It can be translated as, "Seal up the scroll in which the messages of God are written." (See: Doublet)
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px; text-indent: -42px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19px; text-indent: -42px;;;inherit>I will wait for Yahweh, who hides his face from the house of Jacob</font> **- This is a metonym<font 14px line-height: 19px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19px;;;inherit>where a word or phrase is used as a substitute for something it is associated with. This can be translated as, " I will wait to see what Yahweh does, He has rejected the descendants of Jacob." (UDB) (See: Metonymy)</font>
