===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because this people have refused the gentle waters of Shiloah **- This is a metaphor and can be translated as "Because they have rejected my help" (See: Metaphor)
  * and are happy over Rezin and Remaliah’s son    
  * **- "and have been happy to request help from King Resin and King Pekah"
  * ** therefore the Lord is about to bring up on them the waters of the River, mighty and many, the king of Assyria and all his glory  ** - This is a metaphor and can be translated as, "therefore, I will cause the people of Judah to be attacked by the powerful army of the king of Assyria, which will be like a great flood from the Euphrates River."
  * ** It will come up over all its channels and overflow its banks ** - This is a metaphor that can be translated as, "Their soldiers will be everywhere in the country, like a river that overflows all its banks."
