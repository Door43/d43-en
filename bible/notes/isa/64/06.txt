===== Translation Notes: =====
The people of Israel continue speaking to Yahweh.

    * **and all our righteous deeds are like a menstrual rag** - Here all their good deeds are being compared to the blood stained rags of a woman having her periods. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]) 
    * **We have all withered like leaves** - Because of their sins, they are being compared to leaves that wither and dry up. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
