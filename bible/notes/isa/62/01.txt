===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will not be silent** - Possible meanings are 1) "I" refers to Isaiah, 2) "I" refers to Yahweh, or 3) "I" refers to Yahweh's servant.
  * **until her righteousness proceeds brightly, and her salvation as a burning torch** - He will not stop praying for them until people can see clearly as they can see a brightly burning torch in the night. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **You will be called by a new name that Yahweh will choose** - "Yahweh will give your city a new name"
