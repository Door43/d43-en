===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He saw that there was no man, and wondered that there was no one to intervene**-“ Yahweh looked for someone to intervene but was greatly disturbed when He found none”
  * **Therefore his own arm brought salvation for him, and his righteousness sustained him -** he used his own power to rescue them; it is because he is always righteous that he did that. (UDB)(See: Metonymy)
