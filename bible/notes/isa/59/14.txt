===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking.

  * **Justice is driven back** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The people drive justice back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Justice ... righteousness ... truth ... right ... Trustworthiness** - These are ideas that Isaiah describes as acting like persons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
