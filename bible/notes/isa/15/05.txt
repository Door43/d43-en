===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fugitives** - This refers to the people of Moab who have escaped from the battle.
  * **Zoar, Eglath, SShelishiyah, Luhith, Horonaim, Nimrim** – These are names of cities and towns. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])  
  * **The abundance they have grown and stored** – All their preparations will not help them to survive.
