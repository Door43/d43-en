===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaiah continues speaking to the people in exile in Babylonia, describing Yahweh's servant. (See: [[https://door43.org/en/bible/notes/isa/53/01|53:1-2]])

  * **All we like sheep have gone astray**  - Sheep often leave the path on which the shepherd leads them. Isaiah means that every person has done what God commands not to do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
