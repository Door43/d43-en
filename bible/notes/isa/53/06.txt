===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues describing Yahweh's servant. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:53:01|53:1-2]])

  * **We all like sheep have gone astray** - Sheep often leave the path on which the shepherd leads them. Isaiah means that we do what we want instead of what God commands. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **the iniquity of us all** - Our "iniquity" here represents the guilt for our sin. AT: "the guilt for the sin of every one of us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
