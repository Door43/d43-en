===== Translation Notes: =====

The disciples of Isaiah (or the servant) continue speaking to the people in exile in Babylonia. They continue describing Yahweh's servant. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:53:01|53:1-2]])

  * **But he was pierced because of our rebellious deeds, he was crushed because of our sins**  - The words "rebellious deeds" and "sins" mean the same thing. AT: "God allowed the enemy to stab and beat him because of our sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **the punishment for our peace was on him**  - AT: "he accepted this punishment so we could have peace with God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **and with his wounds we are healed**  - AT: "and he healed us by allowing the enemy to wound him"
