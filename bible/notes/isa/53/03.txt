===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaiah continues speaking to the people in exile in Babylonia. He is describing Yahweh's servant. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:53:01|53:1-2]])

  * **He was despised and rejected by people**  - AT: "People considered him as nothing and rejected him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **a man of sorrows**  - "a man knowing all kinds of sadness"
  * **from whom men hide their faces**  - "from whom people turn away"
  * **he was despised**  - AT: "they considered him to be worthless"
