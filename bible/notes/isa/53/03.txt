===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues describing Yahweh's servant. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:53:01|53:1-2]])

  * **He was despised and rejected by people** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "People considered him as nothing and rejected him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a man of sorrows** - "a man knowing all kinds of sadness"
  * **from whom men hide their faces** - "Face" represents a person's attention or fellowship. Hiding one's face means to turn away from someone. AT: "from whom people turn away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he was despised** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they considered him to be worthless" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
