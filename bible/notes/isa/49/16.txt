===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **I have inscribed your name on my palms** - This means that God is always thinking about his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * 
  * **declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:14:21]].
  * **you will surely wear them like jewelry; you will put them on, like a bride** - Here Yahweh compared the return of the people to their city to a bride who wears her jewelry to show them. The people will be around to be shown. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
