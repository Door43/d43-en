===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh's servant continues speaking.
  * **He said** - "Yahweh said"
  * **You are my servant, Israel** - "You are my servant, whom I call Israel"
  * **Though I thought** - Here the servant begins to speak. "I" refers to Yahweh's servant.
