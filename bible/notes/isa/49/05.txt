===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh's servant continues speaking.
  * **that Israel be gathered to him** - "to bring the people of Israel back to himself." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I am honored in the eyes of Yahweh** - Here "eyes" means God's thoughts or opinion. AT: "Yahweh considers me honored" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will make you a light to the Gentiles** - This means he will cause even those who are not Israelites to worship Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
