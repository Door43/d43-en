===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Zion as if it were a person.

    * **Though you were a waste and desolate** - "Though the enemy left you alone and empty"
    * **The children born during the time of your bereavement will say in your hearing** - This refers to the time when the people were in exile in Babylon. AT: "The children born during the time you mourned over the loss of your people" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
