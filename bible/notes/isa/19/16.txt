===== Translation Notes: =====

  *

**Egyptians will be like women **– A simile that likens the characteristic of the fearfulness of women to the whole nation being fearful – See:Simile
  *

**raises over them **– “raises against them”
  *

**her **– “Judah”
  *

**because of the plan of Yahweh, that he is planning against them**.–Change to active voice - “Because Yahweh decided  His armies will be against them”  See: Active
