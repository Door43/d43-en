===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Israel will be the third with Egypt and Assyria** - The names of the three nations represent the people of those nations. AT: "the Israelites will be the third with the Egyptians and Assyrians" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **be the third with** - Possible meanings are 1) "join together with" or 2) "be the third blessing with" or 3) "be equal to." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Blessed be Egypt, my people; Assyria, the work of my hands; and Israel, my inheritance** - The names of the three nations refer to the people of those nations. This can be stated in active form. AT: "I have blessed you, people of Egypt, because you are my people; and I have blessed you, people of Assyria, because I created you; and I have blessed you, people of Israel, because I securely possess you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the work of my hands** - Here "hands" refers to God's power and action. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
