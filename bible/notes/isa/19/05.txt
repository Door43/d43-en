===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The waters of the sea will dry up, and the river will dry up and become empty** - Egyptians referred to the Nile River as "the sea." AT: "The Nile River will completely dry up." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **the reeds and flags will wither away** - "The plants along the river will die"
