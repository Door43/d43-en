===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **princes of Zoan** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:19:11|19:11]].
  * **Memphis** - This is a city in the northern part of Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **they have made Egypt go astray, who are the cornerstones of her tribes** – Here Isaiah compares leaders to a cornerstone that supports a building. This emphasizes that leaders are supposed to guide the people with faithfulness and truth. The leaders of Egypt have failed to do this. Alternate translation: "the leaders, who are like the cornerstone of a building, have led the people of Egypt astray." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Yahweh has mixed a spirit of distortion into her midst** - This compares Yahweh who sent a spirit to confuse the leaders to a person who mixes alcoholic drinks that cause people to be drunk. Alternate translation: "Yahweh sent a spirit that distorts their minds as they make plans"  
  * **like a drunk staggering in his vomit** – This emphasizes that the leaders of Egypt are so confused they can no longer make good decisions. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]])
  * **whether head or tail, palm branch or reed**  - See how you translated similar phrases in [[en:bible:notes:isa:09:13|9:14]].
