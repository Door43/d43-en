===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **there will be a highway** - "there will be a large road"
  * **and the Assyrian** - This refers to people from Assyria. AT: "and the Assyrian people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and the Egyptian to Assyria** - The words "will come" are understood: "and the Egyptian people will come to Assyria." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
