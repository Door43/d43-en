===== translationNotes: =====

  * **there will be a highway** - A highway is a large road on which many people can travel.
  * **the Assyrian will come** - "the Assyrian" refers to a person from Assyria, but represents anyone from Assyria who comes to Egypt. AT: "Assyrians will come" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **the Egyptian to Assyria** - The words "will come" are understood. AT: "the Egyptian will come to Assyria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the Egyptian** - This refers to a person from Egypt, but represents anyone from Egypt who comes to Assyria. AT: "Egyptians" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **the Egyptians will worship with the Assyrians** - The object of their worship can be made explicit. AT: "the Egyptians and Assyrians will worship Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
