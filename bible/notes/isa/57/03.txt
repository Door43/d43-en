===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **children of the adulterer and the woman who has prostituted herself** - This is comparing the people who worship idols to children of adulterers and women who prostitute themselves. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **Whom are you merrily mocking?** - Yahweh is using this rhetorical question to emphasize that it is Yahweh that they are ridiculing and insulting. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Against whom are you opening the mouth and sticking out the tongue?** - This rhetorical question is also used by Yahweh to point out to them that it is Yahweh that they are mocking by sticking out their tongue. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Are you not children of rebellion, children of deceit?** - With this rhetorical question, Yahweh says that they are always rebelling against him and lying to him. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
