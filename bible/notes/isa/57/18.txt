===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **his ways**  - Here "his" refers to the people of Israel. AT: "their ways"
  * **comfort and console those who mourn for him**  - "I will soothe and calm those who feel sad for the people's sinful behavior"
  * **and I create the fruit of the lips**  - AT: "I cause them to praise and thank me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Peace, peace to him**  - The word "peace" is repeated for emphasis. AT: "I have made peace with him"
