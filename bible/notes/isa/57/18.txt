===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **comfort and console those who mourn for him** - "I, Yahweh will take care of and calm those who feel sad for the sins that were done"
  * **and I create the fruit of the lips** - "I cause them to use their mouth to praise and thank Yahaweh"
  * **Peace, peace** - "perfect peace"
