===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But it will hail when the forest is destroyed**  - even if a severe hailstorm knocks down the trees in the forest
  * **and the city will be completely annihilated**  - and all the buildings in the city are blown down
  * **Blessed are you**  - Yahweh will greatly bless you;
  * **who sow beside all the streams**  - you will plant seeds in fields alongside the streams and there will be abundant crops
  * **and who send out your ox and the ass to graze**  - your donkeys and cattle will easily find grass to eat when you send them out to pasture.
