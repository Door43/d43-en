===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **look**  - listen to this
  * **princes will rule in justice**  - his officials will rule fairly
  * **like a shelter**** from the wind**- they will provide protection (See: Metaphor)
  * **a refuge from the storm **- a place to be protected
  * **like streams of water in a dry place**  - like streams of water in the desert (See: Simile)
  * **like the shade of a great rock in a land of weariness**  - like the shade under a huge rock in a very hot and dry land
  * **the eyes of those that see will not be dim**  - those leaders will enable people who have not been able to understand God's truth to understand it (See: Metaphor)
  * **the ears of those that hear will hear attentively**  - they will enable those who have not paid attention to God's truth to pay attention to it (See: Metaphor)
