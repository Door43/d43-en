===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **they will not be shut day nor night**  - AT: "no one will ever shut them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism|Merism]])
  * **so that the wealth of the nations may be brought, with their kings being led**  - AT: "so that the people of the nations may bring their wealth, along with their kings"
