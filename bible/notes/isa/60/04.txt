===== Translation Notes: =====
Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **They all gather themselves** - "They" refers to the people of Israel returning to Jerusalem.
    * **your daughters will be carried in their arms** - AT: "People from other nations will carry your daughters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **Then you will look and be radiant, and your heart shall rejoice and overflow** - This is saying the same thing in two different and emphasizes that they will be very happy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **the abundance of the sea** - This refers to all of the riches and goods that people earned by trading with other people across the Mediterranean Sea.
