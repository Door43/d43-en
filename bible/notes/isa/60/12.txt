===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **those nations will be completely destroyed**  - AT: "I will completely destroy the people of those nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **The glory of Lebanon will come to you**  - This refers to Lebanon being known for its beautiful trees, especially cypress and cedar. The exact identification of all the trees is not known.
  * **the place of my feet**  - This refers to Yahweh's temple. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
