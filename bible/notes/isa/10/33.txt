===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold** - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **will lop off the boughs...and Lebanon in his majesty will fall** - In 10:33-34 Isaiah compares the army of Assyria to the tall trees in Lebanon. God will destroy the army like cutting down these mighty trees. This emphasizes that though the army is strong God has the power to destroy them. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
