===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ah, Assyrian, **- God is addressing one warrior but actually talking to the entire the army of Assyria. (See: Personification)
  * **the club of my anger**, **the rod by whom I wield my fury!**- God is referring to the army as a "club" and a "rod"; instruments that He will use to punish other nations.describing the Assyrians as a "rod". (See: Personification, Doublet)
  * **I am sending him- **  God is referring to the Assyrian army when he says, "him". Can be translated as "I am sending them"
  * **I ordered him- **  The word "him" here also refers to the people of Assyria. "I ordered them"
