===== Important Terms: =====

  * judgement
  * destruction
  * Yahweh

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **What will you do on judgement day when the destruction comes from far?**- "There is no one to help you on the day that the judgement of Yahweh comes!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **To whom will you flee for help, and where will you leave your wealth?**- "There is nowhere to run, and your possessions are not safe!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **For all this Yahweh's anger does not subside**,- "Even after that has happened, Yahweh will continue to be angry,"
  * **but his hand is still stretched out to strike.**-  The phrase "his hand" refers to Yahweh and can be translated as "and Yahweh is still ready to destroy you." (See: Metonymy)
