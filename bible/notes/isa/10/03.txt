===== translationNotes: =====

  * **What will you do on judgment day ... away?** - Isaiah uses a question to scold those in Judah who harm the poor and weak people. AT: "You will be able to do nothing on judgment day ... away!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **judgment day** - "the day when Yahweh comes to judge you" or "the day when Yahweh punishes you"
  * **To whom will you flee for help, and where will you leave your wealth?** - Isaiah uses a question to scold those in Judah who harm the poor and weak people. AT: "You have nowhere to run for help, and you will have nowhere to hide your riches!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Nothing remains** - "Nothing remains for you to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you crouch among the prisoners or fall among the killed** - "your enemies will either take you as a prisoner or will kill you"
  * **In all these things, his anger does not subside** - "Even though all these things have happened, he is still angry." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:25|5:25]].
  * **his hand is still stretched out** - Isaiah speaks as if Yahweh were a person about to hit another person with his fist. This is a metaphor for Yahweh punishing Israel. AT: "he will still be ready to punish them" See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:25|5:25]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
