===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to those who enact unjust laws and write unfair decrees** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. AT: "to those who make laws and decrees that are not fair to everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **They deprive the needy of justice, rob the poor of my people of their rights** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. AT: "They are unjust to the poor and needy among my people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the needy** - "poor people" 
  * **plunder widows** - "take everything from women whose husbands have died"
  * **make the fatherless their prey** - Isaiah compares orphans to animals that other animals hunt and eat. This emphasizes that orphans are powerless and judges can easily harm them. AT: "harm children who do not have parents like an animal that goes after its prey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **prey** - Translated "prey" as in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:29|5:29]].
