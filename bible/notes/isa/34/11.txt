===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will stretch over it the measuring line of ruin and the plumbline of destruction **- Here "measuring line" and "plumbline" are very precise tools for taking measurements. Yahweh is "measuring" or "judging" Edom to determine his wrath on them.
