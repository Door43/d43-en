===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my sword **- Here "my sword" is the judgement of Yahweh. (See: Metonymy)
  * **will have drunk its fill in heaven **- "will have destroyed all that is in the sky."
  * **it will now come down on Edom, on the people whom I am consigning to annihilation **- "my judgement will come upon Edom and all the people that I have said I would destroy."
  * **The sword of Yahweh is dripping with blood and covered with fat, dripping with the blood of lambs and goats, covered with the fat of the kidneys of rams **- These phrases are emphasizing that it will be a complete slaughter. Lambs, goats and rams are animals that would normally be used as a sacrifice. "Yahweh will completely destroy all the people of Edom"
  * **Bozrah **- "Bozrah" is a chief city in Edom. (See: How to Translate Names)
  * **For Yahweh has a sacrifice in Bozrah and a great slaughter in the land of Edom**  - "Yahweh will offer up the city of Bozrah as a sacrifice and kill many people in all the land of Edom."
