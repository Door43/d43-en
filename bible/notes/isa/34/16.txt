===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Search through the scroll of Yahweh** - The phrase "the scroll of Yahweh" means that it contains the messages spoken by Yahweh. AT: "Read carefully what is written in this scroll that contains the messages of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_possession]])
  * **not one of these** - "not one of the animals"
  * **None will lack for a mate** - This can be written as a positive statement. AT: "Each animal will have a mate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **for his mouth has commanded it** - "Mouth" means that Yahweh has spoken. AT: "for Yahweh has commanded it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **He has cast lots for their places** - This refers to how people divided things in antiquity. AT: "He has determined where they will live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and his hand has measured it out for them by a cord** - This refers to how people divided things in antiquity. AT: "and he has used a cord to measure it and give it to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
