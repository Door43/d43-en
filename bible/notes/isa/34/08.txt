===== translationNotes: =====

  * **it will be a day of vengeance** - Here "day" is an idiom for a point in time; it is not a literal "day." AT: "it will be the time when Yahweh gets revenge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will pay them back for the cause of Zion** - This means that he will take revenge on them for how they had previously waged war against the people of Jerusalem. AT: "he will give them the punishment they deserve for what they had done to the people of Zion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The streams of Edom will be turned into pitch** - Here Yahweh emphasizes how full of pitch the streams will be by saying that they will actually turn into pitch. AT: "The streams in Edom will be full of pitch" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **her dust ... her land** - "Edom's dust ... Edom's land"
  * **her dust into sulfur** - Here Yahweh emphasizes how much the ground will be covered with sulfur by saying that the ground will actually turn into sulfur. AT: "her ground will be covered in burning sulfur" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **and her land will become burning pitch** - Here Yahweh emphasizes how everything in Edom will be burned and covered with pitch by saying that the land will actually turn into pitch. AT: "and the land will be covered in burning pitch" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **pitch** - a thick, black substance that burns for a long time
  * **day and night** - This means all of the time. AT: "It will burn throughout the day and the night" or "It will burn constantly, all day and all night" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **from generation to generation** - "for generation after generation" or "for many, many generations"
