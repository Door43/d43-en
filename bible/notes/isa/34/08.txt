===== Translation Notes =====

  * **The streams of Edom will be turned into pitch, ****her dust into brimstone and her land will become burning pitch **- The word "her" is referring to the land of Edom. (See: Metonymy) The words "pitch", "brimstone" and "burning pitch" can all be translated as a resin-like or lava-like substance that can continue to burn for a long time.
