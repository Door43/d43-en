===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh's servant continues speaking.

  * **therefore I am not disgraced** - AT: "therefore my enemies cannot humiliate me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so I have made my face like flint** - Here "my face" refers to the servant. Also "flint" is a hard rock. This means he will endure anything his enemies will do to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **for I know that I will not be put to shame** - AT: "for I know that my enemies will not put me to shame"
