===== translationNotes: =====

  * **let this ruin be in your hands** - Here "hands" represents authority. AT: "take charge of this ruin" or "rule over this ruin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **this ruin** - Possible meanings are 1) many or most of the buildings in the city of Jerusalem were destroyed or 2) the people in Jerusalem have no more prosperity or leadership. AT: "this city, which is now ruined" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will not be a healer** - Solving the people's troubles is spoken of as if it were healing them. AT: "No, I cannot fix this problem" or "No, I cannot help you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
