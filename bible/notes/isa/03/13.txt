===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh stands up to judge in court; he stands up to judge his people**." Yahweh has taken his place in a courtroom and is ready to judge his people."
  * **You**  - the elders and rulers
  * **Vineyard** - The vineyard represents Judah - (See: Metaphor)
  * **Why do you crush my people and grind the faces of the poor? - **  grind the faces of the poor is an idiom denoting great cruelty. This is a doublet which says -" Why are you cruel to my people especially the poor" (See: idiom, doublet)
