===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues to describe how Yahweh will judge the women of Judah.

  * **perfumeâ€¦sash**  - See how these were translated in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:03:18|3:20]]
  * **a rope**  - This may refer to enemies capturing the people of Judah.
  * **well-coiffed hair, baldness**  - "pretty hair, their heads will be bald"
  * **Your men will fall by the sword, and your strong men in war**  - "Enemies will kill your husbands and soldiers in battle."
  * **Jerusalem's gates will lament and mourn**  - Here "Jerusalem's gates" are described as a woman that is crying because of God's punishment. Alternate translation: "The people of Jerusalem will sit at the city gates and cry and mourn." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **and she will be alone and sit upon the ground**  - "and they will sit on the ground with no one to help them."
