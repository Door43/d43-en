====== Isaiah Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? On that day why will they give thanks to Yahweh?

A. They will give thanks because although Yahweh was angry with them, his wrath turned away from them and he comforted them [12:1].

Q? On that day what will they command others to do?

A. They will command others to give thanks to Yahweh and call upon his name; declare his deeds among the peoples, proclaim that his name is exalted. Sing to Yahweh. Cry aloud and shout for joy [12:4-6].
