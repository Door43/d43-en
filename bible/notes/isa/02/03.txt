===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he will teach us some of his ways, and we may walk in his paths** - "he will teach us his will so that we may obey him"
  * **For out of Zion will go the law, and the word of Yahweh from Jerusalem** - These phrases mean the same thing. Isaiah was emphasizing that all nations will understand that the truth is found in Jerusalem. "People in Zion will teach God's law, and people in Jerusalem will teach the word of Yahweh." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **For out of Zion will go the law** - "For the law will go out from Zion." AT: "They will teach the law in Zion" or "They will hear the law in Zion"
  * **the word of Yahweh from Jerusalem** - "the word of Yahweh will go out from Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
