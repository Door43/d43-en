===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The pride of man will be brought low and the haughtiness of men will fall**  - T "Yahweh will cause people to no longer be arrogant." (UDB) (See: Doublet and Active)
  * **Yahweh alone will be exalted **- This can be translated "people will praise and honor only Yahweh." (UDB)
  * **on that day**  -  This can be translated as "on the day that Yahweh comes to judge everyone." (See: metonymy)
  * **The idols will completely pass away - ** This can be translated** **"God will destroy all idols"
  * **from before the terror of Yahweh **- This can be translated "to get away from Yahweh's terrifying presence"
  * **the splendor of his majesty- ** This can be translated** **as** **"his awesome power" or "his great power"
  * **when he rises to terrify the earth**  - This can be translated as "when Yahweh comes to bring judgment for man's sin"
