===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hide in the dust**  - "hide in the holes in the ground"
  * **The lofty gaze of man will be brought low, and the pride of men will be brought down, **- This emphasizes that God will cause people to no longer be proud. This could be translated: “He will cause people to no longer be arrogant.” (UDB) (See: Doublet and Active)
  * **on that Day** - This refers to the time when Yahweh will judge all people. This can be translated as “on the day that Yahweh comes to judge everyone.” (See: Metonymy)
