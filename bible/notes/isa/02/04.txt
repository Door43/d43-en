===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they will hammer their swords into plowshares, and their spears into pruning-hooks; **- This is an idiom and a doublet that refers to the day when there will be no need for weapons because there will be no more war. It can be translated as: “they will make their swords and their spears into farm tools;”  (See: Idiom and Doublet)
