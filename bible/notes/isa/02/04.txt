===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He**  - Here this word refers to Yahweh
  * **They...their**  - Here these words refer to the people of many nations.
  * **they will hammer their swords into plowshares, and their spears into pruning-hooks; **- This is an idiom that refers to the day when Yahweh will bring peace to all the world. There will be no need for weapons because there will be no war. It can be translated as: “they will make their swords into plow blades and their spears into pruning knives;” (UDB) (See: Idiom)
