===== Translation Notes: =====

The people of Israel continue speaking.

  * **Where are your zeal and your mighty acts?**  - They use a question to express deep emotion and concern because it seems God is not helping them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Your pity and your compassionate actions are kept from us**  - AT: "You keep your pity and compassion from us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
