===== Translation Notes: =====

The people of Israel continue speaking.

  * **Yahweh, why do you make us wander from your ways and harden our hearts, so we do not obey you?**  - They use a question to express their complaint to God. AT: "Yahweh, you have made us wander from your ways and become stubborn so that we do not obey you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **why do you make us wander from your ways**  - This people lament, wondering if God has misled them so they wander off the correct path. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
