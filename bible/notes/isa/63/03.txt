===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have trod grapes**  - Here "I" refers to Yahweh. This imagery refers to Yahweh destroying his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **For I looked forward to the day of vengeance**  - Here "day" refers to a period of time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **vengeance**  - Yahweh will punish in an appropriate way to bring about justice. His kind of vengeance differs from man's.
