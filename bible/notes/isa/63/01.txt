===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Who is this who comes from Edom** - Isaiah speaks as a watchman using the question and answer format to present this information about Yahweh's judgment on Edom, an enemy of Israel. AT: "I, Yahweh, am coming from Edom, ..." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Bozrah** - This is the capital city of Edom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **It is I** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh.
