===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are your clothes red, and why do they look like you have been treading grapes in a wine press?** - In this simile, the red stains on Yahweh's clothes represent him defeating his enemies and is being compared to the stains on a person who has been tramping grapes in a wine press.
