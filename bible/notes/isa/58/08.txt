===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Then your light would be broken open like the sunrise** - Here Yahweh is making a comparison. If they do all that he has asked them to do for others, then their deeds will be like the early morning sunshine. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **and your healing would quickly sprout up** - "and the troubles that have been caused by your sins will end quickly"
    * **and the glory of Yahweh would be your rearguard** - "and with my glorious presence I will protect you from behind like I protected the Israelite people when they left Egypt"
