===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **and I will make you ride on the high places of the earth** - This emphasizes how much God will honor and bless the people. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **for the mouth of Yahweh has spoken** - AT: "for Yahweh has spoken it"  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
