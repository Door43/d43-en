===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **A crushed reed he will not break, and a dimly burning wick he will not quench** - Yahweh speaks of weak and helpless people as if they were crushed reeds and dimly burning wicks. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **crushed reed** - A reed is the long, thin stem of a plant like tall grass. If it is crushed, it cannot carry any weight. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:06|36:6]].
  * **he will not break** - "My servant will not break"
  * **the coastlands** - This refers to the people who live on the islands and in the lands bordering or beyond the Mediterranean Sea. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:41:01|41:1]]. AT: "the people who live on the coastlands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
