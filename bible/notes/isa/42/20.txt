===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **You see many things, but do not comprehend** - "You" refers to Yahweh's people of Judah. AT: "Although you see many things, you do not know understand what they mean."
    * **It pleased Yahweh to praise his justice and make glorious the Law** - "Yahweh is pleased to honor his justice and his law"
