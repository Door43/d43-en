===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I have...I will** - Yahweh is speaking.
    * **I will cry out like a woman in labor** - The process of a woman giving birth is also referred to as “labor.”  Yahweh has been quiet and restrained, but now he will cry out against his enemies to deliver his people.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **dry up the marshes** - A marsh is an area of soft, wet land, with many tall grasses and pools of water.
