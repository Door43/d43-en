===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **But this is a people robbed and plundered; they are all trapped in pits, held captive in prisons; ** - "A people" mentioned here is the nation Israel which was defeated and carried in to captivity by Assyria several years earlier.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **they have become a plunder with no one to rescue them, and no one says, "Bring them back** - There was no one to protect them or rescue them because they had disobeyed Yahweh.
