===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking.

  * **Therefore he** - "Therefore Yahweh"
  * **he poured out his fierce anger against them** - Isaiah speaks of Yahweh's anger as if it were a liquid that could be poured out. AT: "he demonstrated his fierce anger towards them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **against them** - Here "them" refers to the people of Israel, but Isaiah still included himself as part of the people. AT: "against us"
  * **with the devastation of war** - The word "devastation" can be translated with a verb. AT: "by devastating them with war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **It blazed around them ... it burned them** - Isaiah speaks of Yahweh's fierce anger as if it were a fire that burned the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they did not take it to heart** - Paying attention to something and learning from it is spoken of as if it were placing that thing on one's heart. AT: "they did not pay attention" or "they did not learn from it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
