===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Therefore he poured out his fierce anger against them, with the devastation of war** - "Yahweh was angry at the disobedience of his people, so he allowed their soldiers to be defeated in war"
    * **It blazed around them, yet they did not realize it** - Yahweh's anger was like fire all around the people, but they did not understand that it was Yahweh punishing them. 
    * **it burned them, but they did not take it to heart** - Even when the people were defeated, they did not return to worship Yahweh.
