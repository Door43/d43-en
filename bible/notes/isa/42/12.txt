===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **Let them give glory** - The pronoun "them" refers to the people along the coast lands.
  * **Yahweh will go out as a warrior; he will go forth as a man of war** - Yahweh will show his strength and power. (See:  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  *  **He will stir up his zeal.** - Yahweh will show his enthusiasm. The “zeal of Yahweh” refers to God's strong, persistent actions to bless his people or to see justice done.
