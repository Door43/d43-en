===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **Let them give glory** - Here "them" refers to the people along the coast lands.
  * **Yahweh will go out as a warrior; he will proceed as a man of war** - This means Yahweh will show his strength and power. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
