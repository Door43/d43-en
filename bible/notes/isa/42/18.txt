===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Listen, you deaf...you blind...my servant...my messenger...my covenant partner... Yahweh's servant** - These terms are referring to Yahweh's people, the people of Judah.
    * **Who is blind...as blind as my covenant partner, or blind as Yahweh's servant?** - Yahweh asks a series of questions to emphasize that his people, Judah, have been blind and deaf to Yahweh's instructions to them.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
    * **my covenant partner** - In this covenant, God promised to fulfill his part only if his people obeyed him and fulfilled their part of the covenant.
