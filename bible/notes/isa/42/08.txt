===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **See, the previous things came to pass,​** ​- Yahweh reminds the people that things he said would happen in the past have come true.  ​	 
    * **now I am about to declare new events** ​- Yahweh tells the people that he is going to share with them things that will happen in the future.
