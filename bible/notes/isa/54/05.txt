===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Jerusalem.

  * **For your Maker is your husband** - Yahweh loving and taking care of his people is spoken of as if he were their husband. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Maker** - Translate this the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:17:06|17:7]].
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - Translate this the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:24|1:24]].
  * **The Holy One of Israel** - Translate this the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:15|5:16]].
  * **Redeemer** - Translate this the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:41:14|41:14]].
  * **he is called the God of the whole earth** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he is the God of the whole earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh has called you back as a wife ... like a woman married young and rejected** - Yahweh sending his people away into exile then bringing them back is spoken of as if Yahweh were a husband who had rejected his wife but now accepts her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **grieved in spirit** - Here "spirit" represents a person's inner being. AT: "grieved" or "made sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
