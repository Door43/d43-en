===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel. 

    * **For your Maker is your husband...like a woman married young and rejected** - Yahweh continues describing the people of Israel as a woman without a husband. Yahweh says he will be their husband. This means he will take care of them and he will increase the people. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **Maker** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:17:06|17:7]].
    * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:24|1:24]].
    * **the Holy One of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:05:15|5:16]].
    * **Redeemer** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:41:14|41:14]].
    * **the God of the whole earth he is called** - AT: "He is the God of the whole earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **and grieved in spirit** - Here "spirit" refers to the whole person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
