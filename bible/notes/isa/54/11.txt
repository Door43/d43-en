===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Jerusalem.

  * **storm-driven** - Yahweh speaks of the people as if they were blown around and damaged by the winds and storms. This means the people were hurt and without stability. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **uncomforted one** - "without comfort"
  * **turquoise ... sapphires ... rubies** - These are precious gemstones. Turquoise is light blue-to-green, sapphires are typically dark blue, and rubies are red-to-pink. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
