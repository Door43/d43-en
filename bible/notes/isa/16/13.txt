===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is the word** - "This is the message." This refers to all he said in 15:1-16:12.
  * **concerning Moab** - The word "Moab" refers to the people of Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the glory of Moab will disappear** - "The country of Moab will no longer be glorious"
