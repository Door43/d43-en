===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jazer…Sibmah…Heshbon…Elealeh**- These are names of cities. (See: How to Translate Names)
  * **Indeed I will weep along with Jazer for the vineyard of Sibmah**- Here "I" refers to Yahweh who is weeping and mourning over the destruction brought on Moab.
