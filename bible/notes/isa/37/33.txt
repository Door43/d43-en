===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He will not come ... He will not come** - Here the Assyrian king refers to both him and his army. AT: "His army will not come ... They will not come" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **with shield** - "with shields"
  * **siege ramp** - a large mound of dirt built against the wall of a city that better enables an army to attach the city
  * **he came ... he will not enter** - Here the Assyrian king refers to both him and his army. AT: "they came ... they will not enter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:30:01|30:1]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
