===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Lachish** - See how you translated the name of this city in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sennacherib** - See how you translated this man's name in  [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Libnah** - This is a city in southern Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Tirhakah king of Ethiopia and Egypt had mobilized to fight against him** - "Tirhakah" is the name of a man. He had mobilized his army so that they were ready to fight. The full meaning of this statement can be made clear. AT: "Tirhakah king of Ethiopia and Egypt had mobilized his army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to fight against him** - The word "him" represents Sennacherib. This phrase refers to fighting against Sennacherib's army. AT: "to fight against the army of Sennacherib" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Jerusalem will not be given into the hand of the king of Assyria** - This can be stated in active form. The word "hand" refers to the king's military power. AT: "The king of Assyria and his army will not conquer you in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
