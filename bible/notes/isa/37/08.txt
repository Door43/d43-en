===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the chief commander ... Lachish ... Sennacherib** - See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:1-2]].
  * **Libnah** - A city in southern Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Tirhakah** - This is the name of a man.
  * **Jerusalem will not be given over into the hand of the king of Assyria** - This can be stated in active form. "Hand" refers to the king's military power. AT: "The king of Assyria and his army will not conquer you in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
