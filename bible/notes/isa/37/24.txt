===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **By your servants have you have defied the **  Lord and have said - “ You sent your servants to defy the Lord by saying” (See: Active/Passive)
  * **I will cut down its tall cedars and the choice fir trees, and I will enter into its farthest height, its most fruitful forest****. –**This refers to laying waste Lebanon which is known for its trees – (See: Metaphor)
  * **I dug wells and drank water; I dried up all the rivers of Egypt under the soles of my feet.**' – This refers to the boastful arrogance of Assyria by dominating the lands which he had conquered (See: Metaphor)
