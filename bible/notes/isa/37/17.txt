===== Translation Notes: =====
Hezekiah continues praying to Yahweh.

  * **Turn your ear, Yahweh, and listen. Open your eyes, Yahweh, and see, and hear the words** - Hezekiah pleads for Yahweh to see and hear what the king of Assyria is saying against Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
