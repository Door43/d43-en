===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hezekiah continues praying to Yahweh.

  * **which he has sent** - "the message he has sent" 
  * **Turn your ear, Yahweh, and listen. Open your eyes, Yahweh, and see, and hear the words** - Hezekiah pleads for Yahweh to see and hear what the king of Assyria is saying against Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Sennacherib** - Translate this man's name the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
