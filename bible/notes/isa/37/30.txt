===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues Yahweh's message to Hezekiah. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:37:21|37:21]])

  * **sign for you** - "sign for you, Hezekiah." Here "you" is singular and refers to Hezekiah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **you will eat ... you must plant** - Here "you" is plural and refers to the people of Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **second year** - "2nd year" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the second year what grows** - This describes what the people will eat. AT: "in the second year you will eat what grows" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **what grows from that** - "what grows wild from that" or "what grows wild"
