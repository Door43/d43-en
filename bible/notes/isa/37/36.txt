===== translationNotes: =====

  * **putting to death** - This idiom means to kill. AT: "killing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Sennacherib king of Assyria left Israel and went home and stayed in Nineveh** - Here Sennacherib refers to both him and his army. They all left Israel and returned home to Assryia. Sennacherib returned to the city Nineveh. AT: "Sennacherib and his army left Israel and went home, and Sennacherib stayed in Nineveh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Sennacherib** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
