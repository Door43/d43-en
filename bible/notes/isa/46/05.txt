===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **To whom will you compare me? Who do you think I resemble, so that we may be compared?** - Yahweh uses questions to emphasize that there is no one like him. AT: "There is no one to whom you can compare me. I resemble no one, so that we might be compared." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **so that we may be compared** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "so that you may compare us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
