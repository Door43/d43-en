===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **I, yes, I** - The word "I" is repeated for emphasis.
  * **for my own sake** - "for my own honor" or "for my own reputation"
  * **call to mind** - AT: "remember"
