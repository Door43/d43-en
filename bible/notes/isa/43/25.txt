===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **blots out your offenses**  - forgive you for all your sinsexplanation
  * **for my own sake**  - because you are mine and I am the only one who is able to do this
  * **I will not call to mind your sins any longer**  - I will never think about them again
  * **Remind me of what happened**  - Tell me what I have done that you do not like
  * **Let us debate together, present your cause **- state your case
  * **that you may be proved innocent**  - you think you will be shown to be innocent (See: Irony)
