===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Bring out the blind people, who have eyes**  - Summon the people who have eyes but act as though they were blind
  * **and the deaf, though they have ears**  - Summon those who have ears but act as though they were deaf.
  * **all the peoples**  - ones from all people-groups
  * **who among them could have declared this and announced to us earlier events?**- that are happening has any of their idols foretold the things now and can any of them predict what will happen in the future?
  * **their witnesses to prove themselves right**  - people who will testify and say "I heard them predict things and what they predicted is what happened.
  * **It is true**  - but they will be lying
