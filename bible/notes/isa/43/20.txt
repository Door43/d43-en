===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wild animals of the field will honor me, the jackals and the ostriches** - Here animals honor Yahweh as if they were people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the jackals and the ostriches** - These are examples of the kinds of animals that will honor Yahweh. See how you translated the names of these animals in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:13:21|13:21-22]]. The understood information can be supplied to make the meaning clear. AT: "the jackals and the ostriches will honor me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
