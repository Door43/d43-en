===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your first father sinned**  - the first ancestor of you Israelites sinned against me
  * **your spokesmen have transgressed against me**  - and since then all your leaders have rebelled against me.
  * **therefore I will defile the officials of the sanctuary**  - that is why I have caused your priests to be disgraced
  * **I will hand Jacob over to the destructive band**  - I have allowed others to destroy you people of Israel
  * **to abusive humiliation**  - caused you to be despised
