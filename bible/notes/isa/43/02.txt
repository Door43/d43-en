===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **when you pass through the waters**  - when you experience dangerous situations
  * **and through the rivers**  - and you feel like you are crossing deep rivers (See:Metaphor)
  * **they will not overwhelm you**  - you will be able to endure them, and they will not hurt you
  * **when you walk through the fire**  - when people try to kill you like they tried to kill Daniel's three friends by throwing them in the fire
  * **you will not be burned, nor will the flames harm you**  - you will not die, just like Daniel's three friends did not die (See: Litotes)
  * **your Savior**  - the one who rescues you
  * **I have given Egypt as your ransom**  - I will sacrifice Egypt in your place to the army of the Emperor of Persia
  * **Ethiopia and Seba in exchange for you **- I will enable them to conquer Ethiopia and Seba in Arabia in order that you can be saved
