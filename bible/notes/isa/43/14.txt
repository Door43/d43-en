===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **Holy One of Israel** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:04|1:4]].
  * **I send to Babylon and lead them all down** - The object of the verb "send" may be supplied in translation. AT: "I send an army to Babylon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **lead them all down as fugitives** - "lead all of the Babylonians down as fugitives"
  * **fugitives** - A fugitive is a person who runs away so that his enemy will not capture him.
