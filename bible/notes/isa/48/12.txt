===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **Jacob, and Israel, whom I called** - Both of these refer to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **my hand...my right hand** - Here "hand" refers to Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **the foundations of the earth** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:24:17|24:18]].
    * **spread out the heavens** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:isa:42:05|42:5]].
