===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jacob, and Israel, whom I called** - "Descendants of Jacob" and "People of Israel."
    * **Yes, my hand laid the foundation of the earth** - When Yahweh describes how he is the creator, the one who laid the foundation of the earth, he referred to himself as the hand since, it is with his hand that he created all things. (See: Synecdoche)
    * **and my right hand spread out the heavens** - Same concept as above.
    * **when I call to them, they stand up together** - "Them" and "they" refers to the stars in the heavens.
