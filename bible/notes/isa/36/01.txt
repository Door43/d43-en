===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the fourteenth year** - "the 14th year" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **King Hezekiah** - Translate the name of this king the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:01|1:1]].
  * **Sennacherib** - This is the name of the king of Assyria. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sennacherib ... attacked all the fortified cities** - Sennacherib's army attached the cities. AT: "Sennacherib and his army ... attacked all the fortified cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the chief commander** - Some versions of the Bible translate this as "the Rabshakeh." This is the Assyrian word for one of the highest ranking military leaders in Assyria.
  * **Lachish** - This is a city southwest of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the conduit of the upper pool, on the highway to the launderers' field** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:07:03|7:3]].
  * **Hilkiah ... Eliakim** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:22:20|22:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shebna** - Translate this man's name the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:22:15|22:15]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Asaph ... Joah** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
