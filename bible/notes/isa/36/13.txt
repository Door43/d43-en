===== translationNotes: =====


  * **the chief commander** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:2]].
  * **this city will not be given into the hand of the king of Assyria** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will not give Jerusalem into the hand of the king of Assyria" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the hand of the king** - The king's "hand" refers to his "control." AT: "the control of the king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
