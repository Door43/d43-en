===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A declaration** - "This is what Yahweh declares" or "This is a message from Yahweh"
  * **about Dumah** - This is another name for Edom. Here "Dumah" represents the people who live there. AT: "about the people of Dumah" or "about the people of Edom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **One calls to me** - Here "me" refers to Isaiah.
  * **Seir** - This is the name of mountains west of Edom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Watchman, what is left of the night? Watchman, what is left of the night?** - This is repeated to emphasize that the person asking the question is worried and nervous. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Watchman** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:21:06|21:6]].
  * **if you will ask, ask, and then come back again** - "if you want to ask, then come back later and ask"
