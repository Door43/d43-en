===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they will behold** - "your eyes will see" or "you will see" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Your heart will recall the terror** - AT: "You will remember the terror that the Assyrians caused you" when they attacked. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **weighed the money** - Much "money" was lumps of metal that were weighed in markets and for taxes.
