===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look** - This marks a new section in the book. It also adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **their envoys cry** - UDB understands that Isaiah includes himself among the people of Jerusalem, whose envoys cry.
  * **treaties** - agreements between governments
