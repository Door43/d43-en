===== Translation Notes: =====

Lebanon, Sharon, Carmel, and Bashan are the four most beautiful regions of the Holy Land, taking the word in its widest extent.

  * **The land mourns and withers away**  - "The land of Judah is dry and barren"
  * **Lebanon is confounded and withers away**  - "The great cedar trees in Lebanon are becoming dry and weak"
  * **Sharon is like a desert plain**  - "The plain of Sharon where flowers and trees once grew is now dry like a desert"
  * **and Bashan and Carmel shake off their leaves**  - "The trees in Bashan and Carmel loose all their leaves"
