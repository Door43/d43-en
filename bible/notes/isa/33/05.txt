===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh is exalted** - "Yahweh is greater than all else"
  * **for he dwells on high** - "for he lives in heaven"
  * **he will fill Zion with justice and righteousness** - "Yahweh will be known throughout Jerusalem, so the rulers will be fair and good"
  * **He will be the stability in your times** - "The knowledge of Yahweh will be the strength of the land"
  * **the fear of Yahweh is his treasure** - This is comparing reverence of Yahweh to valuable treasure. If the people revere Yahweh, he will be like valuable treasure to them. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
