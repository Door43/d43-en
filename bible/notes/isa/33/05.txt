===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh is exalted**  - "Yahweh is greater than all else"
  * **for he dwells on high**  - "for he lives in heaven"
  * **he will fill Zion**  - "he will rule Jerusalem"
  * **the fear of Yahweh is his treasure**  - This is comparing reverence of Yahweh to valuable treasure. If the people revere Yahweh, he will be like valuable treasure to them. (See: Metaphor)
