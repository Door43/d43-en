===== translationNotes: =====

  * **At the loud noise the peoples flee** - Possible meanings of "the loud noise" are 1) it refers to Yahweh's voice. AT: "The peoples flee at the sound of your loud voice" or 2) it refers the loud sounds of Yahweh's army. AT: "The people flee at the sound of your army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **arise** - This means to begin doing something. AT: "begin acting" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the nations are scattered** - This can be written in active form. AT: "the nations scatter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Your spoil is gathered as the locusts gather** - This can be stated in active form. This can mean 1) God's people will take plunder from their enemies, specifically the Assyrians, or 2) God's people will take the plunder that Assyrians have taken from others. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **gather** - "gather food"
  * **as locusts leap** - The locusts see food and jump to get it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
