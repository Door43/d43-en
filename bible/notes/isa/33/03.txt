===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At the loud noise the peoples flee**  - "the people of the nations run away when they hear the loud noise of the Assyrian army attacking"
  * **when you arise**  - "when you show your power"
  * **Your spoil is gathered as the locusts gather**  - In this Simile, the plunder of the enemies' possessions is compared to when the locusts strip off all the leaves of the plants. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
