===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You who are far away, hear what I have done; and, you who are near, acknowledge my might** - "All people near and far will hear and agree that Yahweh is mighty"
  * **The sinners in Zion are afraid; trembling has seized the godless ones** - "The sinners in Israel and the godless people will be afraid" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Who among us can dwell with the devouring fire? Who among us can dwell with everlasting burnings?** - These questions are used to warn the godless people of their coming destruction. This can also refer to eternal punishment and hell fire. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
