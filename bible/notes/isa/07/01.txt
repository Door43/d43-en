===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **During the day of**  - This is an idiom that refers to when a king ruled. it can be translated as "During the rule of." (See: Idiom)
  * **Ahaz **  - This is a name of a king of Judah.  (See:  How to Translate Names)
  * **Jotham**  - This is a name of a king of Judah.  (See:  How to Translate Names)
  * **Uzziah **- This is a name of a king of Judah.  (See:  How to Translate Names)
  * **Rezin**  - This is a name of a king of Syria.  (See:  How to Translate Names)
  * **Pekah**  - This is a name of a king of Israel.  (See:  How to Translate Names)
  * **Remaliah **- This is a name of a king of Israel.  (See:  How to Translate Names)
  * **house of David **- This refers to the descendants of David who are now ruling Israel.
  * **His - ** Ahaz
