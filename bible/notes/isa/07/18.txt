===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At that time** - This refers to before the child knows to refuse the evil and choose the good, mentioned in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:07:13|7:15]].
  * **Yahweh will whistle** - Yahweh will "call" or "summon" two armies.
  * **a fly from the distant streams of Egypt** - This compares the army of Egypt to a fly that buzzes all around and annoys people.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **a bee from the land of Assyria** - This compares the army of Assyria to a bee that flies around and stings people. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **They will all come and settle down into all the gorges, into the clefts of the rocks, on all the thorn bushes, and onto all the pasture.** - The armies of Egypt and Assyria will cover all of the land in Judah, leaving the people of Judah no place to hide.
