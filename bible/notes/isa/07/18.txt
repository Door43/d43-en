===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At that time **- This refers to before the child knows to refuse the evil and choose the good, mentioned in verse
  * **whistle - ** Yahweh will "call" or "summon" two armies.
  * **a fly from the distant streams of Egypt**  - This refers to the army of Egypt.
  * **a bee from the land of Syria**  - This refers to the army of Syria.
  * **They will all come and settle down into all the gorges, into the clefts of the rocks, on all the thorn bushes, and onto all the pasture. **  - The armies of Egypt and Syria will cover all of the land in Judah, leaving the people of Judah no place to hide.
