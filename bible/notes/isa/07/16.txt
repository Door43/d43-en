===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **before the child know to refuse the evil and choose the good - ** In Hebrew culture, the child would be about 12 years old.
  * **the land whose two kings **- This refers to the nation of Israel, called Ephraim, and the nation of Syria. Ephraim was ruled by Pekah and Syria was ruled by Rezin.
  * **you**  - Ahaz
  * **your people**  - This refers to the people of Judah.
  * **father's house**  - This refers to the descendants of David who are now ruling Israel.
  * **Ephraim**  - This refers to the northern kingdom of Israel.
  * **he**  - Yahweh
  * **you**  - This refers to Ahaz, his family, and the entire nation of Judah.
