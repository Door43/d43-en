===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues to describe the sign that God will give the house of David.
 
  * **refuse the evil and choose the good** - Here "the evil" and "the good" refer to evil and good things in general. AT: "refuse to do evil deeds and choose to do good deeds." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:07:13|7:15]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **you dread** - "you fear." Here "you" is singular and refers to Ahaz. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **your people** - This refers to the people of Judah.
  * **Ephraim seceded from Judah** - "the people of Ephraim separated from the people of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
