===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lord**  - Yahweh
  * **Ahaz**  - This is a name of a man. (See: How to Translate Names) See how you translated this name in 1:1, 7:1
  * **Ask a sign of Yahweh your God**  - "Ask Yahweh to prove He will help you by giving you some evidence of His faithfulness"
  * **ask for it in the depths or in the height above**  - Yahweh did not limit what Ahaz could request.
