===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Comfort, comfort** - The word "comfort" is repeated for emphasis. 
  * **says your God** - Here "your" is plural and refers those whom the prophet tells to comfort God's people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
  * **Speak tenderly to Jerusalem** - The prophet speaks of Jerusalem as if it were a woman whom Yahweh has forgiven. As such, Jerusalem represents the people who live in that city. AT: Speak tenderly to the people of Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **proclaim to her ... her warfare ... her iniquity ... she has received ... her sins** - These pronouns refer to Jerusalem but may be changed if "the people of Jerusalem" is adopted as a translation. AT: "proclaim to them ... their warfare ... their iniquity ... they have received ... their sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **her warfare** - Possible meanings are that the word "warfare" refers 1) to military battle or 2) to forced labor.
  * **her iniquity is pardoned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh has pardoned her iniquity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from Yahweh's hand** - Here the word "hand" represents Yahweh himself. AT: "from Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
