===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Go up on a high mountain** - Mountains provide a convenient place to speak to many people. AT: "Get into a position where everyone around can hear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Zion** - This refers to the people who live in Zion. AT: "you people of Zion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **lift up your voice with strength** - "shout loudly"
  * **and his strong arm rules for him** - "Arm" refers to God's power. AT: "and he rules with power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **his reward is with him, and his recompense goes before him** - Both of these clauses mean the same thing. AT: "he is bringing his reward with him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **his recompense goes before him** - "the payment for his efforts goes ahead of him"
