===== translationNotes: =====

  * **To whom then will you compare me, whom do I resemble?** - Here "you" is plural and refers to God's people. Yahweh uses a question for emphasis. The two phrases mean basically the same thing. AT: "There is no one who is like me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Who has created all these stars?** - This question emphasizes that it is Yahweh who has created the stars. AT: "Yahweh has created all the stars!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **By the greatness of his might and by the strength of his power** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize how extremely great Yahweh's power is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
