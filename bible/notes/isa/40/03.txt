===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A voice cries out, "In the wilderness prepare the way of Yahweh** - "Someone shouts out, "clear a path in the wilderness for Yahweh to come'"
  * **make straight in the Arabah a highway for our God** - "Arabah" is a deep, sunken valley. This basically means the same as the previous statement. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **and the glory of Yahweh will be revealed, and all people will see it together; for the mouth of Yahweh has spoken it.** - Yahweh has said, "I will reveal my glory for all the people to see. "mouth of Yahweh"  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
