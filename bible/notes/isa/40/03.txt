===== translationNotes: =====

  * **make straight in the Arabah a highway for our God** - Prepare yourselves to receive Yahweh when he comes, like people make a straight road for an important official. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in the Arabah** - See how you translated "Arabah" in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:35:01|35:1]]. This phrase means basically the same as the previous statement. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **and the glory of Yahweh will be revealed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and Yahweh will reveal his glory" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for the mouth of Yahweh has spoken it** - "Mouth" refers to what Yahweh says. AT: "for Yahweh has spoken it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
