===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Look ... see** - These words add emphasis to what follows.
  * **the nations are like a drop in a bucket, and are regarded like the dust on the scales** - The prophet compares the nations to a drop of water and to dust in order to emphasize how small and insignificant they are to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like a drop in a bucket** - Possible meanings are 1) a drop of water that falls into a bucket or 2) a drop of water that drops out of a bucket.
  * **are regarded like the dust on the scales** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh regards them as dust on the scales" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they are regarded by him as nothing** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he regards them as nothing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
