===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **judge between me and my vineyard **  - Yahweh compares himself to the farmer and the people of Jerusalem and Judah to the vineyard. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Yahweh is asking the people to decide who has acted in the right way. It could be translated as a direct quote, “You judge between me and my vineyard(see: Metaphor, Direct Quote)</font>
  * **What more could have been done for my vineyard, that I have not done for it**  – This could be stated “I have done all that I could do to help my vineyard make good grapes.” (see: Rhetorical Question)
  * **When I looked for it to bring forth good grapes, why it produced wild grapes** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>– This could be stated</font>    “Yahweh <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>waited</font>    <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>for the vineyard to make good grapes, but it only produced sour grapes.”</font>
