===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **the most admired of kingdoms** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the kingdom that people most admire" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the splendor of Chaldean pride** - The noun "pride" can be expressed with the adjective "proud." AT: "the beautiful city that the Chaldeans are so proud of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Then Babylon ... will be overthrown by God** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Then God will destroy Babylon, the most admired of kingdoms, the splendor of Chaldean pride, as he destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **It will not be inhabited or lived in** - The two verbs mean basically the same thing. This can be stated in active form. AT: "No one will live in it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from generation to generation** - The phrase "generation to generation" refers to all generations of people who will live in the future. AT: "for ever" "ever again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **The Arab** - This refers to Arab people in general, not one person. AT: "Arabs" or "Arab people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
