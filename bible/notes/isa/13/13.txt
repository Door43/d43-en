===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **Like a hunted gazelle or like a sheep with no shepherd** - Yahweh speaks of the defenseless people of Israel as if they were a gazelle or a sheep that was being chased by hunters. AT: "Like gazelles that run away swiftly when people hunt them, and like sheep that have no shepherd run away from wild animals" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **gazelle** - an animal that is similar to a deer. People hunt them, and wild animals sometimes attack and kill them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **like a sheep with no shepherd** - Sheep that have no shepherd have no one to protect them from wild animals that attack and kill them.
