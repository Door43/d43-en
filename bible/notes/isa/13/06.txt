===== Translation Notes: =====
This continues the prophecy about the people of Babylon. Isaiah is speaking.

    * **Howl** - "Cry out loud"
    * **it comes with destruction from the Almighty** - "the Almighty God will come to destroy you"
    * **their faces will be aflame** - "They will have the look of terror on their faces"
