===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I was afraid of the people and obeyed their voice**  -   Here "the people" refers to the "nation of Israel." The phrase "obeyed their voice" means "did as the the nation of Israel said." AT: "I was afraid of the nation of Israel and did as they told me to." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]
