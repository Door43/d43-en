===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But please honor me now before the elders** - Saul may have been more interested in being honored by the people than in actually worshipping God.
  * **before the elders of my people and before Israel** - Here "Israel" refers to the people of Israel. AT: "before the people of Israel and the elders who lead them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Turn again with me** - "Come back with me" or "Return with me"
  * **So Samuel turned again after Saul** - This implies that Samuel changed his mind, and that they went together to where the people were. AT: "So Samuel finally agreed to do that, and they went together back to where the people were"
