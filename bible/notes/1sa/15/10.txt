===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came to Samuel, saying,** - The idiom "the word of Yahweh came to" is used to introduce a special message from God. AT: "Yahweh gave a message to Samuel. He said," or "Yahweh spoke this message to Samuel:" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **It grieves me** - "I am sorry" (UBD)
  * **he has turned back from following me** - Saul not obeying Yahweh is spoken of as if Saul had physically turned away from walking behind God. AT: "he has stopped following me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **has not performed my commandments** - "has not obeyed what I commanded him to do." Saul was to completely destroy everything and everyone. God had placed a ban on the Amalekites. But Saul permitted some livestock to live.
  * **Samuel was angry** - Possible meanings are 1) Samuel was angry with Saul for his disobedience or 2) Samuel was disturbed .
