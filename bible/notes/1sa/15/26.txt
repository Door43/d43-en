===== translationNotes: =====

  * **for you have rejected the word of Yahweh** - Samuel made it clear that Saul understood that he was disobeying God at the time when he spared the best animals and did not kill Agag.
  * **rejected the word of Yahweh** - "rejected Yahweh's command" or "rejected Yahweh's message." This means that he refused to obey Yahweh's command. AT: "refused to obey Yahweh's command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Saul took hold of the hem of his robe** - Saul did this to try to stop Samuel from leaving. This can be stated explicitly. AT: "Saul tried to stop him by grabbing the edge of Samuel's robe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the hem of his robe** - "the edge of his robe" or "the fringe of his robe"
