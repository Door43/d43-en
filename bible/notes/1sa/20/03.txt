===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I have found favor in your eyes** - Here "found favor" is an idiom that refers to being friendly to someone. AT: "I have become your friend" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will be grieved** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "this will grieve him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **there is but a step between me and death** - Here "step between me and death" is an idiom that refers to being very close to death. AT: "I am very close to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
