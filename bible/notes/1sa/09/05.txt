===== translationNotes: =====

  * **land of Zuph** - This is an area in Israel just north of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **man of God** - This phrase usually means a prophet of Yahweh. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:02:27|2:27]]. AT: "a man who hears and tells words from God"  
  * **which way we should go on our journey** - "which way we should go to find the donkeys"
