===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he had eaten no food all that day, neither that whole night** - Saul had not eaten during the night before he traveled from Gilboa ([[:en:bible:notes:1sa:28:03|28:4]]) to Endor ([[:en:bible:notes:1sa:28:05|28:7]]), nor during the day as he journeyed, nor during the night on which he visited the woman.
  * **I have put my life in my hand and have listened** - "I could die because I listened" or "Someone could kill me because I listened" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
