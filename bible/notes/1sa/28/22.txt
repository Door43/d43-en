===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the voice of your woman servant** - The woman speaks as if she is another person to show that she respects Saul. She refers to herself by her "voice" to emphasize what she says. AT: "my voice" or "me speak to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]] or [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **refused** - "did not accept" or "would not do what they asked"
  * **compelled** - "forced"
