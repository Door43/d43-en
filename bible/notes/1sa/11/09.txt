===== Translation Notes: =====
 
    * **They said to the messengers**  - "They" refers to Samuel and Saul. 
    * ** by the time the sun is hot** - AT: "before the hottest part of the day" or "before noon."
    * **Jabesh Gilead...Jabesh**  - See how you translated these places in [[en:bible:notes:1sa:11:01|11:01]].
    * **Nahash**  - This is the name of a king. See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:1sa:11:01|11:01]].
