  * **Now David had also taken Ahinoam ... Saul had given Michal** - These events took place before David married Abigail. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Palti ... Laish** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
