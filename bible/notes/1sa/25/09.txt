===== translationNotes: =====

  * **David's young men** - "David's army"
  * **said all of this to Nabal on David's behalf** - "gave Nabal David's complete message"
  * **Who is David? And who is the son of Jesse?** - Nabal probably knew who David was, but he did not want to help David. (See [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:18:06|18:6-7]]; the words "servants ... breaking away from their masters" in verse 10 probably refer to David breaking away from Saul.) These rhetorical questions can be translated as statements. AT: "This David son of Jesse you speak ofâ€”I do not know him." or "I do not know who this David is, so I will not do as he says. I do not know who Jesse is, so I do not care what happens to his son." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **breaking away from** - "running away from" or "rebelling against"
  * **my bread** - Here "bread" is an idiom for any kind of food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **men who come from I do not know where** - "men when I do not know where they have come from" or "men whom I do not know"
