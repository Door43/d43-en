===== translationNotes: =====

  * **morning light** - This is a metonymy the first appearance of the sun's light in the morning. AT: "dawn" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **like the feast of a king** - This is a simile that the feast in his house is described in this manner to make the reader understand the majesty and magnificence of the feast in his house. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Nabal's heart was merry within him** - This is a metonymy that means Nabal was drunk (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
