===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Abigail continues to reason with David.

  * **my master ... my master ... my master ... your servant** - Abigail speaks of herself and David as if they are two different people to show that she respects David. "you ... you ... you ... me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **this matter will not be a staggering burden for you** - Abigail is stating that if David chooses not to take revenge he will have a clear conscience when Yahweh makes him king of Israel. This can be stated in positive form. AT: "you will always be glad you acted as you did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **when Yahweh will do good for my master** - That is, when Yahweh actually makes him king after Saul's reign is over.
