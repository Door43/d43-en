===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pursue** - "chase" or "hunt"
  * **bundle** - a collection of a longer, narrow item of similar lengths (sticks, wheat, wooden rods, or similar shapes) gathered together and wrapped around with strings, small ropes, or twine. The sticks in the center are protected by the sticks on the outside of the bundle.  Used here as a metaphor. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **sling** - In this usage, a sling is a weapon created from a small carrying pouch with two long strips of leather or string attached. A rock placed in the pouch could be whirled around the head, then released toward a target.
