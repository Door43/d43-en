===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David offers to protect Abigail by marrying her.

  * **May Yahweh be blessed** - This can be translated in active form. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:25:32|25:32]]. Possible meanings are 1) AT: "I praise Yahweh" or 2) AT: "May all people praise Yahweh"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **has taken up the cause of my insult from the hand of Nabal** - This is an idiom.  AT: "has defended me after Nabal insulted me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **has taken up the cause of my insult** - The abstract nouns "cause" and "insult" can be translated as verbs. Possible meanings are 1) AT: "has shown that what I was doing was right by punishing Nabal for insulting me" or 2) AT: "has acted, when Nabal insulted me, as if Nabal had insulted him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **has kept back his servant from evil** - David speaks as if he were another person to show his respect for Yahweh. AT: "has kept me back from doing evil deeds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **has turned Nabal's evil action back on his own head** - The abstract noun "action" can be translated as a verb. AT: "has done to Nabal what Nabal planned to do to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **on his own head** - The head is a synecdoche for the person. AT: "on him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **sent and spoke to Abigail, to take her to himself as wife** - You may make clear the understood information. AT: "sent men to speak to Abigail and tell her that David wanted to take her to be his wife" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
