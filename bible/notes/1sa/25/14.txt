===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But one of the young men told Abigail, Nabal's wife** - You may need to make explicit what the young man did before he spoke with Abigail. AT: "One of Nabal's servants found out what David and his men were planning to do, so he went to Nabal's wife Abigail" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **We were not harmed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Nobody harmed us" or "We were safe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **did not miss anything as long as we went with them** - "we did not lose anything when we were with them." David's men kept wild animals and other people from stealing Nabal's flocks.
