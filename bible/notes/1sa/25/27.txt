===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Abigail continues pacify David's anger.

  * **make my master a sure house** - AT: "make David's family a leading or ruling family in Israel" or "reward you by allowing many of your descendants to become kings of Israel" (UDB)
  * **my master is fighting the battles of Yahweh** - Abigail knew and understood that David's raiding parties were against Israel's enemies.
