===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Ophrah, to the land of Shual...Bethhoron...valley of Zeboim** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]].)
    * **Ophrah** - The meaning of the word "Ophrah" is "young deer."
    * **Bethhoron** - The meaning of the word "Bethhoron" is "house of hollowness" or "house of emptiness" or "house of waste"
    * **Zeboim** - The meaning of the word "Zeboim" is "hyena," a type of mean wild dog.
