===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **thirty thousand chariots, six thousand men**  - 30,000 chariots, 6,000 men (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **troops as numerous as the sand on the seashore** - This is a deliberate exaggeration made for emphasis. There cannot possibly be as many soldiers as the "sand on the seashore." AT: "a very large, almost uncountable groups of soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
    * **Michmash** - This is the name of a place. "Michmash" is a place meaning "hidden." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Bethaven**  - This is the name of a place. "Bethaven" means "house of pride" or "house of self-love."
