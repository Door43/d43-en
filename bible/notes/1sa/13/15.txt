===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Samuel arose and went up** - This is an idiom for "Samuel left and went up." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **went up from Gilgal** - Gilgal is a city. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:07:15|7:15]].
  * **Gibeah of Benjamin** - Gibeah is a town. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:10:26|10:26]].
  * **six hundred men** - "600 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Geba of Benjamin** - Geba is a town. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:13:03|13:03]].
  * **Philistines camped at Michmash** - Michmash is the name of a place. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:13:01|13:02]].
