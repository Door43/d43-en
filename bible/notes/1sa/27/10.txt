===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Achish**  - This is a man's name.  See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:19:10|19:10]]
  * **Jerahmeelites**  - The Jerahmeelites were a clan from the tribe of Judah (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Kenites**  - people group who lived in the land of Midian during the time of Moses (Jethro, Moses' father in law was a Kenite), came up out of Egypt and journeyed with Israelites to Canaan. During the time of king Saul, they lived the southeast part of Palestine, near the border with Moab.  See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:15:06|15:6]].
