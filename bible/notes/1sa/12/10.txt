===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They cried out to Yahweh and said** - "Then our ancestors pleaded with Yahweh again to help them" (UDB) or, "The people prayed with great feeling that Yahweh would come to their aid"
  * **Jerub Baal**  - Also translated Jerubbaal. This is a name of godly honor and strength to fight the false god.
  * **have served the Baals and the Ashtoreths**  - "We have worshiped idols that represent the god Baal and the goddess Ashtoreth" (UDB)
