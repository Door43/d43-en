===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from any man's hand ... in my hand** - These phrases mean what a person possesses or what they have done to obtain favor from others. This is a polite way of saying he has not stolen nor has he given or taken bribes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
