===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **up from the land of Egypt **- "out of Egypt" (UDB)
  * **I may plead with you before Yahweh about all of the righteous deeds of Yahweh **- This is a Hebrew figure of speech to say that Samuel will now show that their ways in God's eyes have not been as pleasing as his own. (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])
  * **all of the righteous deeds of Yahweh **- "all the great miracles that Yahweh performed" (UDB) Samuel is calling their attention to the entire history of mankind, back to Adam and Eve, has been filled with goodness and purpose. (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
