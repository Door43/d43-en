===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the guard that stood around him**  - "the men that stood around him"
    * **their hand also is with David**  - This means the priests are loyal to David. AT: "they plot with David against me" or "they are on David's side" (See: Synecdoche)
    * **would not put out their hand to kill the priests**  - This means they stayed still and refused to obey Saul. AT: "did not move to kill the priests"  (See: Metonymy)
