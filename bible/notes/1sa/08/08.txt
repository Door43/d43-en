===== translationNotes: =====


  * **I brought them out of Egypt** - This refers to Yahweh freeing the Israelites from slavery in Egypt many years before. 
  * **Now listen to them** - "Now do what they are asking you to do" 
  * **warn them solemnly** - "be very serious as you warn them"
