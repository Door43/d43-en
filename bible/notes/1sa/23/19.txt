===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ziphites** - This is the name of the people group from Ziph. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Is not David hiding among us in ... Jeshimon?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as an exclamation. AT: "David is hiding among us in ... Jeshimon!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the hill of Hachilah** - This is the name of a hill in the wilderness of Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jeshimon** - This is the name of a desert area near the Dead Sea. It can also be translated as "the Judean Wilderness" or "the wasteland." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **surrender him into the king's hand** - The word "hand" is a metonym for power. The Ziphites speak to Saul as if he were another man to show him that they respect him. AT: "give David to you so you can do whatever you desire with him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
