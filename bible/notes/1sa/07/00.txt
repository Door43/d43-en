====== 1 Samuel 07 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter completes the section on Samuel leading Israel. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Israel begin to worshiping Yahweh again ==

Samuel told the people to get rid of all their idols and worship Yahweh alone. The people were obedient. When the Philistines heard the Israelites were all gathered together, the Philistines attacked, but God defeat them. There was a time of peace and Samuel settled disputes between the people as the judges had before.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idioms ==

Whenever Samuel spoke to a group of people, he used many  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|idioms]]: "to turn to" means "to worship," "with your whole heart" means "completely," "rescue from the hand" means "rescued from the power."
