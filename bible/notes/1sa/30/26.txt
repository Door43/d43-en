===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The writer begins a list of cities whose elders received gifts from David.

  * **Ziklag ... Bethuel ... Jattir ... Aroer ... Siphmoth ... Eshtemoa** - names of towns (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **booty** - goods that David had taken from the Amalekites after the battle. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:30:16|30:16]].
  * **a present** - "a gift"
