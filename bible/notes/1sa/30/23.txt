===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **goes into battle** - This means the one who actually fights the enemy in war.
  * **Who will listen to you in this matter?** - This is a rhetorical question that means , "No one will listen to you in this matter." (See: Rhetorical questions)
  * **share** - This word means the portion of reclaimed goods one earned.
  * **baggage** - This word refers to the belongings of the soldiers left behind when they went into battle.
