====== 1 Samuel 30 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David fought the Amalekites ==
When David and his men arrived home, they found the Amalekites had raided Zaglig and taken everything. They caught and destroyed them and brought back all of their goods and families. Then, David sent gifts to all the cities in Judah which they had travelled through.
