===== translationNotes: =====

  * **six cubits and a span** - A cubit is a unit of measurement equal to about 46 centimeters. A span is a unit of measurement equal to about 23 centimeters. AT: "about 3 meters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **he was armed with a coat of chainmail** - A "coat of chainmail" is a flexible piece of body armor covered with protective scales or small plates. This can be stated in active form. AT: "he wore a coat of chainmail" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **five thousand shekels** - A shekel is a unit of weight equal to about 11 grams. AT: "about 55 kilograms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
