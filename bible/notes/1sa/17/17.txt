===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ephah** - An ephah is a unit of measurement equal to about 22 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **the captain of their thousand** - "the captain of your brothers' thousand." Possible meanings are 1) the word "thousand" represents the exact amount of soldiers that this captain led. AT: "the captain of your brothers' unit of 1,000 soldiers" or 2) the word translated as "thousand" does not represent exact number, but is the name of a large military division. AT: "the captain of your brothers' military division" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **See how your brothers are doing** - "Check and find out how your brothers are doing"
