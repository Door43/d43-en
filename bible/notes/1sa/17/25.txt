===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Have you seen this man who has come up?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "Apparently you have not really looked at this man who has come up!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **will give him his daughter ... will make his father's house** - Here "him" and "his father's house" refer to the man who kills Goliath.
