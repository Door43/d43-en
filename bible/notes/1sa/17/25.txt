===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Have you seen this man who has come up?** - The soldiers were saying this to draw one another's attention to the man.  AT: "Look at this man who has come up!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the king** - This refers to the king of Israel. AT: "our king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **his daughter** - This refers to the king's daughter. 
  * **will give him ... his father's house** - The words "him" and "his" refer to the man who kills Goliath.
  * **will make his father's house free from taxation in Israel** - The word "house" refers to the household or family. AT: "will no longer require his family to pay taxes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
