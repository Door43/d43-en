===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is not this David the king of the land?**  - In this usage, the word "king" means commander or king of the army. AT: "This is David, the great man of Israel." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ambiguity|Ambiguity]])
  * **Did they not sing to one another about him in dances?**  - AT: "When they were dancing, they sang to each other about him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
