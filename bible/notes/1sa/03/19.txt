===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he let none of his prophetic words fall to the ground** - Here messages that do not come true are spoken of as if they fell to the ground. This can be stated positively. AT: "he made all the things he prophesied happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **All Israel** - "All the people in Israel"
  * **from Dan to Beersheba** - This is a merism for "in every part of the land." AT: "from one end of the land to the other" or "from Dan in the very north to Beersheba in the very south" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]]) 
  * **Samuel was appointed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh had appointed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
