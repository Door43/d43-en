===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word he spoke** - "the message Yahweh gave"
  * **May God do so to you, and even more** - This is an idiom to emphasize how serious Eli is. AT: "May God punish you the same way he said he will punish me, and even more" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
