===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **After whom has the king of Israel come out?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]].   The idea is that the King does not need to come out after an insignificant someone who is not a threat.
    * **After whom do you pursue?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]].   The idea is that there is no need for the king to pursue someone who is not a threat.   See also: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]
    * **After a dead dog!**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] implying after someone who has no power over the king.
    * **After a flea!**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] implying after someone who is small and insignificant compared to the king.
    * **permit me to escape from your hand**  - Here "your hand" implies Saul and his army.   See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]
