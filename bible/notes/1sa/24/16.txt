  * **my son David** - Saul speaks as if David were his son to show David that he loves David. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **lifted up his voice and wept** - The writer writes as if a voice were something a person could lift up with his hands. AT: "wept loudly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
