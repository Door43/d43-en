===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ichabod** - The name is actually a phrase that means "no glory." The name of a person sometimes reveals information about the person, place, or thing it refers to. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **for the ark of God had been captured ... the ark of God has been captured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "for the Philistines had captured the ark of God ... because the Philistines have captured the ark of God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
