====== 1 Samuel 04 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== The Ark of the Covenant in Philistine country ==

This chapter begins a new section on the Ark and the Philistines. It recounts two battles between Israel and the Philistines. In both battles, Israel was badly defeated and in the second the Ark was captured.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The Ark is captured ==

The Philistine Army invaded Israel and the Israeli army went to fight them, but was defeated. Israeli decided; if they took the Ark into the next battle, then God would cause them to win. When Eli's sons took the Ark into the next battle and the Philistines won the battle, the killed Eli's two sons and captured the Ark. When Eli heard the Ark was captured, he fell over, broke his neck and died. Hearing this news, his daughter-in-law named her baby "The Glory has Departed." 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Rhetorical Questions ==

The Philistines expressed their fear using a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|rhetorical question]]: "Who will protect us from the strength of this mighty God?"
