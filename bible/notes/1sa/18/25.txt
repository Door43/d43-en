===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The king does not desire any dowry, only one hundred foreskins**  - AT: "The king does not desire a dowry; he desires that you bring him one hundred foreskins" (See: Ellipsis)
    * **dowry**  - money, property or services that families exchange as part of an arranged marriage
    * **one hundred**  - "100" (See: Translate Numbers)
    * **foreskin**  - the fold of skin that covers the end of the penis
    * **to be avenged from the king’s enemies**  - AT: "then the king will get revenge on his enemies" (See: Active - Passive)
    * **to make David fall by the hand of the Philistines**  - Here "fall" means to die. See how you translated "hand of the Philistines" in 18:17. AT: "the Philistines would kill David" (See: Euphemism and Active - Passive)
    * **son-in-law** - See how you translated this in 18:18.
