===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from all the cities of Israel** - This is a hyperbole and it does not mean all, but many. AT: "from many of the cities of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **with tambourines** - A "tambourine" is a small handheld drum.
  * **And David his ten thousands** - This is an ellipsis that means David killed many people. AT: "And David has killed his ten thousands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
