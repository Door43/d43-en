  * **Is it a small matter to you to be the king's son-in-law, since I am a poor man, and lightly esteemed?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "It is no small matter to be the king's son-in-law, and I am too poor and unimportant for that." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
