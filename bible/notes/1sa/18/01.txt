===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **When he had finished speaking to Saul** - "When David had finished speaking to Saul"
  * **the soul of Jonathan was closely bound to the soul of David**  - Here "soul" refers to the person and is used to emphasize how "closely bound," or tightly connected, they were. AT: "Jonathan admired David and they became close friends" (See: Synecdoche and Active - Passive)
  * **and Jonathan loved him as his own soul** - Here "loved" refers to the love between friends, not romantic love. AT: "and Jonathan loved David as much as he loved himself" or "and Jonathan cared for David as though they shared the same soul"
