===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jonathan loved him as his own soul** - Here "loved" refers to the love between friends, not romantic love. The word "soul" represents the person or the person's life. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:18:01|18:1]]. AT: "Jonathan loved David as much as he loved himself" or "Jonathan loved David as he loved his own life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
