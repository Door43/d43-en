===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **David went out wherever Saul sent him**  - David went on military missions with the army. (See: Explicit - Implicit)
    * **and he succeeded**  - "and he prospered"
    * **set him over**  - AT: "gave him command over" or "appointed him a leader of"
    * **This was pleasing in the eyes ... in the sight**  - This means the people were pleased with Saul's decision. (See: Metonymy)
