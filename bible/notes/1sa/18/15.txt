===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he stood in awe of him** - AT: "Saul was in awe of David" or "Saul was amazed and feared David" (See: Idiom)
    * **all Israel and Judah loved David** - AT: "many people in Israel and Judah admired David" (See: Hyperbole)
    * **he went out and came in before them**  - See how you translated this in 18:13
