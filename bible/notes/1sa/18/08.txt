===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **What more can he have but the monarchy?**  - Saul says this to express his anger and fear of losing his role as king. AT: "The only thing left for him to take is the kingdom" (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **monarchy** - a country ruled by a king or queen
    * **Saul watched David with suspicion**  - AT: "Saul suspected that David would try to become king and began watching him" (See: Abstract Nouns)
    * **from that day and forward**  - AT: "that day and every day after"
