===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then Eli answered** - Eli was the head priest residing over the tabernacle.
  * **your servant** - Hannah speaks of herself as if she were speaking of someone else to show respect for Eli, the head priest. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **and ate; her face** - Here "her face" is a metonym for Hannah herself. You can make this a separate sentence, if needed. AT: "and ate. She" or "and ate. People could see that she" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
