===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then Eli answered** - Eli was the head priest residing over the tabernacle.
  * **Let your servant find** - Hannah speaks of herself in the second person to show respect for Eli, the head priest. This can be stated in the first person. AT: "Let me, your servant, find" or "Let me find" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **in your sight** - Here seeing represents judging or deciding the value of something. AT: "in your judgment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ate; her face was** - Here "her face" represents Hannah herself. You can make this a separate sentence, if needed. AT: "ate. She was" or "ate. People could see that she was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
