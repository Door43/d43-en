===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Saul seeks to continue the fight against the Philistines.

  * **Do whatever seems good to you** - Saul had the support of his army to continue the fight.
  * **But God did not answer him that day** - This implies that God was not willing to help Saul.
