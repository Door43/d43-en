===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The lots had just showed that Jonathan had sinned.
 
  * **Tell me what you have done** - "Tell me how you have sinned" or "Tell me what you have done that was wrong" 
  * **I will die** - Possible meanings are 1) "I am willing to die" or 2) "do I deserve to be executed because of doing that?"
  * **God do so and more also to me, if you do not die, Jonathan** - Saul makes a second foolish oath in less than one day. AT: "May God kill me if I do not kill you, Jonathan"
