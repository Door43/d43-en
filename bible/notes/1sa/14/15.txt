===== Translation Notes: =====

Jonathan and his armor bearer have killed about 20 men.

    * **There was a panic** - "There was extreme fear"
    * **the raiders panicked** - "the thieves greatly feared" or "the looters greatly feared." After the soldiers killed an enemy, they and those with them would steal from the bodies and property. 
    * **raiders** - The word "raiders" here means "ruin" or "cast off"
    * **earth quaked** - "earth shook violently" or "the land shook violently"
