===== translationNotes: =====

  * **will not cross over to them** - will not go over to the other side of the valley where the Philistines are 
  * **given them into our hand** - Here "into our hand" is an idiom that means to defeat them. AT: "The Lord will enable us to defeat them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **This will be the sign to us** - "This will be confirmation that the Lord will be with us"
