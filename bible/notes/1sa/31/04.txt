===== translationNotes: =====

  * **armor bearer** - The armor bearer carried the large shield of his commanding officer and other weapons. He protected the commanding officer during a battle. 
  * **these uncircumcised** - The nominal adjective "uncircumcised" can be translated as an adjective. Not being circumcised is a metonym for being foreigners. AT: "these men who have not been circumcised" or "these non-Israelite men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **would not** - The ellipsis can be filled in. AT:  "would not do what Saul had told him to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **took his own sword and fell on it** - "killed himself with his own sword" 
  * **that same day** - "on that one day"
