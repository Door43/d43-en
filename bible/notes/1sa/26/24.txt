===== translationNotes: =====

  * **precious in my eyes** - Here "eyes" is a metonymy refers to me. AT: "precious to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **valued in the eyes of Yahweh** - Here "eyes of Yahweh" is a metonymy that refers to Yahweh. AT: "valuable to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
