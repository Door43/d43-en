===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my son** - Saul was not David's true father. Saul speaks as if he were David's father to show David that he wants David to trust and respect him as David would trust and respect his own father. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Why does my master pursue his servant?** - Possible meanings are 1) David really does want Saul to answer the question or 2) this rhetorical question can be translated as a statement, "My master has no reason to pursue his servant." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **What have I done?** - Possible meanings are 1) David really does want Saul to answer the question or 2) this rhetorical question can be translated as a statement, "You know that I have done nothing to harm you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **What evil is in my hand?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: Possible meanings are 1) "You know that I have done nothing evil to you" or 2) "You can see that I am not planning to do anything evil to you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
