===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Is not David hiding in the hill of Hachilah, which is before the wilderness?"**  - AT: "David certainly must be hiding in the hill of Hachilah, which is near the desert."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Then Saul arose** - an idiomatic expression for "taking action." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **three thousand** - 3,000 (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
