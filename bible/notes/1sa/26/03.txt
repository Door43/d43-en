===== translationNotes: =====

  * **before Jeshimon** - "from which they could see Jeshimon"
  * **Jeshimon** - This is the name of a desert area near the Dead Sea. It can also be translated as "the Judean Wilderness" or "the wasteland." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:23:19|23:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
