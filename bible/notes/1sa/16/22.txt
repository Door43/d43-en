===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Let David stand before me** - Here "stand before me" is an idiom that means to continue in Saul's service. AT: "Let David stay in my service" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **harmful spirit from God** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:16:14|16:14-16]].
  * **Saul would be refreshed and well** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the music would refresh Saul and make him well" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
