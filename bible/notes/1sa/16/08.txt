===== translationNotes: =====
  * **Abinadab ... Shammah** - These are names of Jesse's sons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **made him pass before Samuel** - "told him to go to Samuel"
  * **Then Jesse made Shammah pass by** - It is understood that Shammah passed by Samuel. AT: "Then Jesse made Shammah pass by Samuel" or "Then Jesse told Shammah to go to Samuel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
