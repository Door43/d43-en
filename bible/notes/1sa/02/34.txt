===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will raise up ... a faithful priest** - "I will cause a man to become priest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for myself** - "to serve me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **what is in my heart and in my soul** - "what I want him to do and what I tell him to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **I will build him a sure house** - Here "house" is an idiom used for "descendants." AT: "I will ensure that he always has a descendant who serves as high priest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
