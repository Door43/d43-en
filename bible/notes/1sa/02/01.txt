===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **My heart exults in Yahweh; my horn is exalted in Yahweh** - She is saying the same thought in two ways, that she is happy and encouraged or strengthened by what Yahweh has done for her.  (See: [[: en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
    * **horn** - This is another way of saying strength.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
