===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Your** - The word "your" refers to Yahweh.
    * **Your eyes are too pure to gaze upon evil**  - Yahweh's eyes are so holy that he cannot be profaned by evil, and so all of Yahweh is holy. (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
    * **those who betreay**  - The Babylonian/Chaldeans. 
    * **You make men like fish in the sea, like creeping things without a ruler over them.**  - Israelites have been made to be like lowly animals. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
