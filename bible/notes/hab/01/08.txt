===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **their...they** - The word "their" refers to the Babylonian/Chaldean soldiers. 
    * ** swifter than leopards** - A leopard is a kind of large cat. It is yellow and covered in spots.
    * **they fly like an eagle**  - The word "like" is used to compare the speed of horses to an eagle.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
    * **their multitudes go like a desert wind, and they gather captives like sand** - The Chaldeans are compared to a wind in the desert that moves the sand to show how many people they have and how powerful they are. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
