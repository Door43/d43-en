===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I have heard your report**  - This can mean 1) "I have heard people talk about what you have done" (see UDB) or 2) "I heard what you just said."
    * **revive your work**  - "bring your work back to life" or "do again what you did before"
    * **make it known**  - " make your work known"
