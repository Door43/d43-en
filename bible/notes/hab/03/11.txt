===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Habakkuk continues to describe his vision of Yahweh.

  * **lofty** - very high
  * **at the light of your arrows** - because the light of Yahweh's arrows was so bright
  * **gleaming** - bright
  * **You have marched over the earth with indignation. In wrath you have threshed the nations.** - These two sentences share similar meanings. Together they refer to Yahweh punishing the nations for their wickedness. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **indignation** - anger at something that is wrong
