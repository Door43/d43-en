===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the elder**  - Refers to John, the apostle and elder.
  * **to the elect lady and her children **- This could refer to the church and its believers.
  * **all those**  - This is a pronoun referring to fellow believers. (See: Pronoun)
  * **the truth**  - "The true message that Jesus taught." (UDB)
