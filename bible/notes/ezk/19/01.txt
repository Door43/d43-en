===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh tells Ezekiel to speak to the people of Israel and compared them to a lion and their kings to young lions.

  * **lift up a lamentation**- In this phrase, singing a funeral song is compared to lifting a sad song up to heaven. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Who was your mother?** - This question was asked as an impossible riddle.  The answer is given to teach a point.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **raised up** - This phrase refers to a parent influencing their children's development. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
