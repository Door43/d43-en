===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your** - The word "your" is masculine singular and refers to the prince of Israel as though they were one person. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])
  * **mother** - Yahweh describes the nation of Israel as though it was the mother of the princes of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in your blood** - It is implied that this is the blood of grapes. AT: "in the blood of your grapes" or "in your wine." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **because of the abundance of water** - "because there was a lot of water"
  * **for ruler’s scepters** - "that rulers could use as scepters"
  * **her heights were exalted** - "she grew tall"
  * **among the branches of the thicket** - Possible meanings are 1) "up above the branches of other plants" or 2) "up to the clouds."
