===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But**  - This word could also be translated "And," "So," or "Then".
  * **set against him**  - "prepared to attack (the lion)"
  * **nets**  - Nets can be used as a type of trap.
  * **They put him in a cage with hooks - ** Alternate translation: "They pulled him into a cage using hooks."
  * **mountain fortresses**  - a place of secure and remote confinement
