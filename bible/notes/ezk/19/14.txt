===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For fire went out from rods of her branches; it has consumed her fruit - **  The vine is on fire; the fire has destroyed its fruit.
  * **Her**  - A Pronoun referring to the tree.
  * **This is a lamentation and will be a lamentation.**  - Alternate translation: "This is a sad song and will be sung sadly."
