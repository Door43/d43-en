===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **I will certainly make things worse -  ** Alternate translation: "I promise to make things worse" or " I will cause four things to happen to the people" (UDB).
  * **from her - ** The word "her" refers to Jerusalem.
