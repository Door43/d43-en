===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But say this to them**  - The word "them" refers to the "men from the elders of Israel"
  * **Every man of the house of Israel - **"any Israelite person" (UDB)
  * **Before his face - **  The idiom “before his face” means “ahead of him” or “in front of him.”
  * **I, Yahweh, will answer him according to the number of their idols**  - alternate translation: "I, Yahweh will tell him worshipping many idols**
  * **house of Israel**  - Alternate translation: <font 13.888888359069824px line-height: 13.194443702697754px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>“people of Israel."</font>
  * **Therefore declare to them ****- ** Alternate translation:** **"Therefore say to them"
  * **seize the house -** Alternate translation: "grab the house" or "take the house"
  * **because ( should be "become") estranged **- alternate translation: "become estranged" or "become separated from"
