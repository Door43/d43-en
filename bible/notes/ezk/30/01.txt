===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **prophesy and say** - Alternate translation: "prophesy by saying" or "say this when you tell the people my message." (See: [[en:ta:translate:hendiadys]]) 
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **howl**  - "Wail" or "Cry out with grief"
  * **Woe is this day** – "This day is very frightening" or "Very bad things will happen today"
  * **The day is near** - "Soon, it will be this day" or "This day will come soon"
  * **a day of clouds**  - "a time of destruction". This is a reference to a time of great violence and destruction. (See: Metaphor).
  * **a time for nations**  - This is in reference to this day being a time of judgment for the nations.
