===== Translation Notes:- explanation =====

  * **behold -**  This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **arms **- "power" or "control"
  * **make the sword fall from his hand **- "make him lose power". (See:Metaphor)
  * **lands**  - This use of the word is a reference to a nation.
  * **groan**  - This is a noise that is made when one is in pain or dying.
