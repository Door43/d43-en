===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **shapeless idols** - You could also translated simply as "idols."
  * **bring an end to worthless idols** - "destroy the useless idols"
  * **Memphis** - Memphis was a very important city in Egypt. It was little be south of where Cairo is today. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **a prince in the land of Egypt** - "an important ruler in the land of Egypt"
  * **I will set terror on the land of Egypt** - "I will make Egypt very afraid"
  * **Patros** - This was another important city in Egypt. It was in the southern part of Egypt. 
  * **set fire in Zoan** - "I will burn Zoan with fire"
  * **Zoan** - Zoan was another important city in Egypt.
  * **No** - No was the capital city of southern Egypt.  it was also called Thebes.
