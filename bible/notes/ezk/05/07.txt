===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh **- "the Lord says" or "this is what I, Yahweh the Lord, say" (UDB)
  * **Because you are more tumultuous than**  - "because your sinfulness is worse than" or "have been more rebellious" (UDB)
  * **or even acted according to the judgments of the nations that surround you**  - "not even obeyed the laws of the nations around you" (UDB)
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - "the Lord says" or "this is what I, Yahweh the Lord, say" (UDB)
  * **I am against you - **"I am opposed to you" (UDB)
  * **I will execute judgments within your midst - **"I will punish you"(UDB)
