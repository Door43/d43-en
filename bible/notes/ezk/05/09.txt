===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel and Jerusalem.

  * **what I have not done and the like of which I will not do again** - "as I have not done before and will not do in a similar way again" or "like I have never done before and will never do again" .
  * **because of all your disgusting actions** - "because of all the disgusting things you do." God was angry because the people were worshiping idols and false gods.
  * **fathers will eat the children in your midst, and sons will eat their fathers** - Ezekiel is probably telling what will really happen when the people have no food.
  * **I will execute judgment on you** - "I will judge you" or "I will punish you severely" 
  * **scatter to every direction all of you who are left** - "I will force all of you who are left to go to different places."
