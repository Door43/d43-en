===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **breaks**  - a crack, hole, etc., that is caused by damage, injury, or pressure
  * **wall**  - (see: Talink Temple)
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in 3:1. 
  * **repair** - to put (something that is broken or damaged) back into good condition
  * **resist** - to fight against (something): to try to stop or prevent (something)
  * **battle** - a military fight between groups of soldiers, sailors, etc., often supported by ships, airplanes, etc. 
  * **false** - not correct
  * **predictions**  - a statement about what will happen or might happen in the future
  * **Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in 5:11.
  * **messages**  - a piece of information that is sent or given to someone
  * **spoken**  - using speech and not writing
