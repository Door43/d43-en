===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Son of man**  - See how you translated this in 2:3.
  * **set your face against**  - See how you translated this in 4:3.
  * **daughters**  - female children
  * **prophesy out of their own minds**  - prophesy with only their own thoughts, not inspired by God. (See: Metaphor)
  * **prophesy against**  - See how you translated this in 4:7.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **sew**  - "make" or "fasten" (UDB)
  * **charms**  - objects that are believed to have magic powers, especially to prevent bad luck, but also to tell the future
  * **veils**  - pieces of cloth worn by women over the head and shoulders and sometimes over the face
  * **trap**  - a situation in which someone is tricked into doing or saying something that they don't want to do or say.
