===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **discourage**  - to try to make people not want to do something
  * **lies**  - things that are not true and said to make someone believe they are true
  * **desire**  - "want" or "wish for"
  * **discourage / encourage**  - to make someone less / more likely to do something
  * **instead**  - in place of
  * **actions**  - things that a person or group does
  * **turn from his way**  - stop doing the things he has been doing
  * **no longer**  - not any more
  * **false**  - not true
  * **continue**  - to keep doing something in the same way as before
  * **predictions**  - a statement about what will happen or might happen in the future
  * **make predictions**  - to say statements about what will happen or might happen in the future
  * **And you will know that I am Yahweh!**  - This is a summary statement which emphasizes God's power.
