===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I**  - refers to Yahweh
  * **I will annihilate in my fury**  - "when I act on my anger I will bring to an end"
  * **whitewashed it**  - "painted the wall white" (See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:13:10|13:10]])
  * **The wall exists no more, nor do the people** - "The wall is no longer there, nor are the people"
  * **visions of peace for her**  - The word "her" refers to Jerusalem.
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
