===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh **- See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **against**  - not in agreement with
  * **charms**  - objects that are believed to have magic powers, especially to prevent bad luck, but also to tell the future
  * **ensnare **- to catch (an animal or person) in a place from which there is no escape
  * **tear **- to remove (something) quickly or violently
  * **people whom you have trapped like birds**  - people you have controlled or surrounded (see Metaphor/simile)
  * **trapped **- tricked into doing or saying something that you don't want to do or say; this is usually bad
  * **veils **- pieces of cloth worn by women over the head and shoulders and sometimes over the face
  * **they will no longer be trapped in your hands **- Alternate translation: "they will not be trapped by you for any more time"
  * **And you will know that I am Yahweh!** -
