===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **Are you the one I spoke of in former days**  - "You" in this phrase refers to Gog, "I" is God.
  * **my fury will arise in my nostrils**  - "my anger will boil over"
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in 5:11.
