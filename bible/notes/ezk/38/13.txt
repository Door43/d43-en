===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sheba and Dedan -** see Translate Names
  * **young lions -**  The warriors of a country.
  * **spoils -**  Valuable treasures gained by conquoring another nation
  * **plunder - **  Other,useful but less valuable, items taken from the conquored people.
  * **property -**  Could be actual land gained. More likely animals, furniture, clothing, etc.
