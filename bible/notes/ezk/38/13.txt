===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues speaking to Gog.

  * **Sheba** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:27:22|27:22]].
  * **Dedan** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:25:12|25:13]].
  * **Have you come to plunder? Have you assembled your armies ... to haul away much plunder?** - These two questions are used to accuse Gog of attacking Israel without cause. These can be translated as statements. AT: "It is not right for you to assemble your army to plunder, to carry off silver and gold, to take their livestock and property, and to haul away much plunder!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **to take their livestock and property ... to haul away much plunder** - These phrases have similar meaning and can be combined into a single phrase. AT: "to take away their livestock and all their valuable possessions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
