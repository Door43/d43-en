===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message about Gog.
  * **I will summon a sword against him** - This phrase means "I will destroy him with swords." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **Each man’s sword will be against his brother** - "the people of Gog's army will kill each other with swords"
  * **will judge them** - "will judge him." It is implied that Yahweh will judge Gog by destroying his army. AT: "will judge Gog by destroying his army." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])


  * **each man's sword will be against his brother **- Alternate translation: "soldiers in Gog's armies will attack one another"
  * **I will judge him with plague … blood **- "I will punish him with disease and bloodshed"
  * **brimstone - **  burning sulphur
  * **hailstones - f**rozen chunks of water
  * **I will magnify myself and sanctify myself - **  These words stress Yahweh's divine power in relation to man. Alternate translation: "I will exalt myself and show my holiness"
