===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **Set your face toward** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:06|4:7]].
  * **Gog** - This is the name of a leader or king who ruled in the land of Magog. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the land of Magog** - It is implied that this is the land that Gog rules over. AT: "who rules over the land of Magog." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Magog** - the name of an ancient nation that probably lived in the land that is now Turkey. Magog might be the ancient nation of Lydia.
  * **Meshech and Tubal** - See how you translated "Tubal, and Meshech" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:27:12|27:13]].
  * **prophesy against** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:06|4:7]].
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Behold**  - AT: “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **I am against you** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:21:01|21:3]].
