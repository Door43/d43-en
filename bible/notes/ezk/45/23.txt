===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the princes of Israel.
 
  * **food offering** - This is also called a "grain offering."
  * **seven bulls and seven unblemished rams** - "seven bulls and seven rams that are completely healthy"
