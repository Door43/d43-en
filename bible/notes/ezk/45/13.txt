====== Ezekiel 45:13-15 ======


===== TFT: =====

\v 14 You must give him one quart\f + \ft Or: liter \f* of olive oil for every 100 quarts\f + \ft Or: liters \f* that you make.
\v 15 Also, I, Yahweh, declare that you must take one sheep or goat from each 200 in your flocks in the pastures of Israel.
