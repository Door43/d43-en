===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **first month **  - This is the Lunar calendar month.
  * **unblemished **  - The animal is not sick or injured.
  * **bull **  - The animal is a male cow.
  * **perform **  - The priest does his duty.
  * **door posts **  - This is a part of the wall that holds the opening secure for access.
  * **house **  - This is the Temple.
  * **altar **  - This is the place for burning the sacrifice.
  * **gate **  - This is the opening in a wall that people go through.
  * **inner court **  - This is the place where the Temple is found.
  * **seventh of the month **  - This is the seventh day following the new moon.
  * **accidental sin or ignorance **  - This is not an intended sin but a failure to keep the law unintentionally.
