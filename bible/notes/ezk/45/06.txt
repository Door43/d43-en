===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **five thousand wide and twenty five thousand long**  - These units are in cubits (See: Biblical distances).
  * **property**  - This is a piece of land.
  * **chief**  - A chief is a leader of a group of people.
  * **and the property of the city, on the side of the sea to the sea and from the east side towards the east**  - Alternate translation: "The property of the city will extend on the side of the sea to the sea and from the east side towards the east."
  * **corresponding to**  - equal to.
