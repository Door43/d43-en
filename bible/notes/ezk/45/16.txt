====== Ezekiel 45:16-17 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 16 All the people of the land must join in bringing these offerings to the king of Israel.
\v 17 And the king must provide the animals to be completely burned on the altar, grain for the offerings made from grain, and wine for the sacred festivals that I have appointed for the people Israel—including the festivals to celebrate the new moons, and the Sabbaths. He must provide the animals for the offerings for the people to become acceptable to God, offerings of things made from grain, offerings to be completely burned, and offerings to maintain fellowship with Yahweh, to purify the people of Israel.

</usfm>

He will make the sin offering, the food offering, the burnt offering, and the peace offering for atonement on behalf of the house of Israel.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **chief - **  the main leader of the group.
  * **and it will be the chief’s responsibility: the burnt offering, the food offering, the drink offering, the feasts and the new moons, and the Sabbaths will all the feasts of the house of Israel- **  the chief is to use these contributions in performing all the feasts and offerings of the house of Israel, including the: burnt offerings, the food offerings, the drink offerings, new moons and Sabbaths feasts.
  * **on behalf-- ** for
