====== Ezekiel 45:16-17 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 16 All the people of the land must join in bringing these offerings to the king of Israel.
\v 17 And the king must provide the animals to be completely burned on the altar, grain for the offerings made from grain, and wine for the sacred festivals that I have appointed for the people Israel—including the festivals to celebrate the new moons, and the Sabbaths. He must provide the animals for the offerings for the people to become acceptable to God, offerings of things made from grain, offerings to be completely burned, and offerings to maintain fellowship with Yahweh, to purify the people of Israel.
\p
</usfm>

===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 16 All the people in the land must join in bringing these offerings to the ruler of Israel.
\v 17 The ruler must provide the animals to be completely burned on the altar, flour for the offerings made from grain, and wine for the sacred festivals that I have appointed for the people Israel—including the festivals to celebrate the new moons, and the offerings for the Sabbath days. He must provide the animals for the offerings for the people to become acceptable to God, offerings of flour made from grain, offerings to be completely burned, and offerings to promise friendship with Yahweh, to atone for the sins of the people of Israel."


\p
</usfm>

===== ULB: =====

<usfm>
\v 16 All the people of the land will give this contribution to the prince in Israel.
\v 17 It will be the prince's responsibility to furnish animals for the burnt offerings, the grain offerings, and the drink offerings at the festivals and the new moon celebrations, and on the Sabbath days—all the fixed festivals of the house of Israel. He will provide for the sin offerings, the grain offerings, the burnt offerings, and the peace offerings for atonement on behalf of the house of Israel.
</usfm>
