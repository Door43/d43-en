===== Important Terms: =====

  * burnt offering
  * cubits (see: Biblical distances page)
  * span (see: Biblical distance page)

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cut stone**  - This was a large, smooth stone used for preparing the meat.
  * **instruments**  - alternate translation: Tools used in the preparation of the meat.
  * **two-pronged hooks **- These were long hooks that split into two sharp points at one end.
