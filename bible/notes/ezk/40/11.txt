===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cubits** - The cubit referred to the length of a man's forearm, from the elbow to the tip of the longest finger.  (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **border in front of the niches **  - This is the edge in front of the recesses in the wall.
