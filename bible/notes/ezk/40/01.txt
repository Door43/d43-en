===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in that same day**  - This can be left out because its meaning is repeated from the previous sentence.
  * **the city**  - This city here is Jerusalem.
  * **the hand of Yahweh**  - The term "hand" is often used to refer to God's power and action.  Alternate translation: "the power of God." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]], [[en:ta:translate:anthro]])
  * **God brought me in visions**  - Ezekiel was not physically transported. He was shown the vision by God.
  * **the city was struck**- Alternate translation: After the Babylonians had destroyed the city (See: [[:en:ta:translate:grammar|Active/passive]])
  * **structure of a city**  - alternate translation: ruins
