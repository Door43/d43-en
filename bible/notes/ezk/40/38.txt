===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **by each of the inner gateways** - "in each of the inner gates"
  * **they rinsed the burnt offerings** - The word "they" refers to people or priests who were bringing an offering.
  * **either side** - "on both sides"
  * **the burnt offering was slaughtered** - AT: "they slaughtered the burnt offering" or "they killed the animals that they would burn as an offering." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
