===== Translation Notes: =====

Ezekiel continues to describe the vision of the temple and of the man who looked like bronze.
  * **rinsed**  - This means to wash with clean water.
  * **slaughter**  - This means to kill an animal for its meat. In this case, the meat was to be offered to God.
