===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He brought me**  - The word "here" refers to the Lord
  * **He brought me there** - The word "there" refers to the city mentioned in 40:2.
  * **behold! **- The word “behold” here shows that Ezekiel was surprised by what he saw. This can also be translated as “suddenly”.
  * **set your heart upon all that I am revealing to you**  - Alternate translation: "consider what I tell you seriously and remember it."
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]]
