===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **porch of the gate** - The gate of this city was large and contained rooms within the gate.
  * **cubits** - The cubit referred to the length of a man's forearm, from the elbow to the tip of the longest finger. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **the gate towards the house** - This is the gate that faces the house.
