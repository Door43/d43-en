===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the portico of the gate facing the temple** - Here "facing the temple" means that the portico that was attached to the gate was in the temple courtyard. AT: "the portico of the gate on the inner side of the wall that surrounded the temple"
  * **portico** - This was a covering in front of an entrance with columns or posts for support. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **one stick** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]].
  * **two cubits** - about one meter (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **cubits** - Translate these "long" cubits as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]].
  * **had the same measurement** - "were the same size"
