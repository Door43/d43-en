===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **portico** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **its doorposts** - "the doorposts of the house"
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].

  * **fourteen cubits** - about 7.5 meters
  * **three cubits** - about 1.6 meters
  * **twenty cubits** - About eleven meters
  * **eleven cubits** - About six meters
  * **columns** - pieces of stone that are tall and narrow and support buildings
