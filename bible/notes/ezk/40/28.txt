====== Ezekiel 40:28-31 ======


===== TFT: =====

\v 29 Its alcoves, its projecting walls, and its entry room measured the same as the ones on the other sides. The entrance and the entry room had windows. The entrance was 87.5 ft.\f + \ft Or: 36.5 meters \f* long and 43.75 ft.\f + \ft Or: 13.3 meters \f* wide.

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **inner courtyard** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **by way of its southern gate** - "through its southern gate"
  * **chambers** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:7]].
  * **porticos** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **measured the same** - "had the same measurements"
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **fifty cubits** - about 27 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **twenty-five cubits** - about 13.5 meters
  * **five cubits** - about 2.7 meters
  * **This portico faced the outer courtyard** - Here "faced" is an idiom that means it was oriented in that direction. People could go in and out of it from the courtyard. AT: "the portico was toward the outer courtyard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **with carved palm trees** - "and it had carvings of palm trees"
