===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Its/It **- These pronouns refer to the north gate.
   * **niches**  - A niche is a recess in a wall.
  * **corresponded to **- The measurements were the same as for the east side (verse 6). An alternate translation "measured the same as." (UDB)
  * **the gate that faced the way to the east**  - Alternate translations are "the gate to the east." or "the gate on the east side" (UDB)
  * **they/them **- These pronouns are a general reference to those who entered the temple from this direction.
  * **inner**  - There was an inner and outer courtyard in the temple.
  * **There was a gate to the inner courtyard in front of ****the gate to the north and to the east, and he measured from one gate to the other gate - 100 cubits**  - An alternate translation could read "There was a gate to the inner courtyard in line with the north gate, just as there was on the east side. He measured the distance between the north gate and the gate to the inner courtyard as 100 cubits." The outer courtyard of the temple was between the gate entry area and the inner courtyard gate.
