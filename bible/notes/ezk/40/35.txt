===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **measured the same** - "had the same measurements"
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **fifty cubits** - about 27 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **twenty-five cubits** - about 13.5 meters
  * **Its portico faced the outer courtyard** - Here "faced" is an idiom that means it was oriented in that direction. AT: "Its portico opened toward the outer courtyard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **on either side of it** - "on both sides of it"
