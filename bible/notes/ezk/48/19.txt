===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Israel.
 
  * **twenty-five thousand cubits** - Each long cubit was about 54 centimeters. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]. AT: "25,000 cubits" or "about 13.5 kilometers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **In this way you will make the holy offering of land, together with the land for the city.** - "You will offer the holy offering and also the property of the city"
  * **you** - This is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **the holy offering** - the land that the people of Israel gave to Yahweh for the Levites, the priests, and the temple
