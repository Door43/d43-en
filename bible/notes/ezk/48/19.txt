===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and the ones who work in the city - - ** This means the Israelites who work in Jerusalem.
  * **will work in it-** The same people will work in the farmland
  * **All the contribution - ** This is the land that the Israelites will farm.
  * **holy contribution-**- This is special sacred land that will be reserved for the temple.
