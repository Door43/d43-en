===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **twenty five thousand long and wide**  - This means twenty five thousand cubits.
  * **that you offer up to Yahweh**  - alternate translation: that you will set aside for Yahweh's use.
  * **will be in its midst**  - alternate translation: will be in the middle of it.
