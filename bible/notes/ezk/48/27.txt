===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Israel.

  * **Tamar ... Meribah Kadesh** - names of towns. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:47:18|47:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the brook of Egypt** - a very large ravine in the northeast part of the Sinai. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:47:18|47:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **you** - This is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **cast lots** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:45:01|45:1]].
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]]. AT: "This is what the Lord Yahweh has declared" or "This is what I, the Lord Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
