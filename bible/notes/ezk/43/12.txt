===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the peak** - This is the highest point of a mountain or hill.
  * **all of its** - The word "its" refers to the mountain.
  * **its surrounding borders** - The area around the mountain.
  * **Behold!** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
