===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let them remove their promiscuity â€¦ from before me**  - This means "they must stop doing wrong in front of God".
  * **their promiscuity**  - The word "promiscuity" refers to Israel's unfaithfulness to God when they worshiped false gods.
  * **the corpses of their kings**  - Alternate translation: "the bodies of their dead kings".
  * **I will dwell in their midst**  - Alternate translation: "I will live in the middle of them" or "I will live among them"(UDB).
