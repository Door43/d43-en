===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cubits** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **the groove** - A long, narrow cut in a surface or "gutter" (UDB).
  * **border** - The line or part where an object begins or ends.
  * **edge** - The line or part where an object begins or ends.
  * **handspan** -The width of a human hand measured from the tip of the thumb to the tip of the little finger; also referred to as **span** (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **And its border around its surrounding edge will be one handspan** - Alternate translation: "The border will be one handspan".
  * **the base of the altar** - "the bottom supporting the altar" or "the foundation of the altar".
  * **edging** - something that forms an edge or a border.
