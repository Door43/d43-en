===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you will offer** - The word "you" refers to the priests.
  * **he-goat** - This is a male goat.
  * **blemish** - A mark that makes something imperfect or a defect.
  * **bull** - This is a male cow or ox.
  * **unblemished** - This is a sacrifice that is not sick our injured.
  * **ram**  - This is a male sheep.
  * **flock** - This is a large group of birds or animals.
  * **cast out salt onto them**  - This means to sprinkle salt on the sacrifice.
