===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **statutes** - The laws or regulations for worship that God gave Israel.
  * **sprinkle blood**  - This means to drop or spread small amounts of blood.
  * **bull** - This is a male cow.
  * **Zadok**  - This is a family name of Levitical priests (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]]).
  * **declaration**  - This is  a public statement usually about something of importance.
  * **this the Lord Yahweh’s declaration**  - See how you translated this in [[:en/bible/notes/ezk/05/11|5:11]].
