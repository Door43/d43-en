===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And it was like**- The word "it" refers to the coming of the glory of God (43:2)
  * **like the appearance of the vision that I saw**  - This phrase suggests that the vision described in this verse came to the prophet in the same way as the other visions reported in the earlier portions of this book.
  * **River Chebar** - See the definition of this river in 1:1
  * **fell on my face** - prostrate, a sign of honor and worship (See [[:en:obe:kt:worship|worship]])
  * **Glory of Yahweh**  - the “splendor” or “majesty” of God
  * **Behold** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **lifted me up**  - "carried me"
