===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It was** - "The glory of God coming from the east was"
  * **according to the appearance of the vision** - The abstract noun "appearance" can be translated as a verb. AT: "just like what appeared in the vision" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **when he had come to destroy the city** - Possible meanings are 1) "when the God of Israel had come to destroy the city" or 2) "when I had come to see the God of Israel destroy the city."
  * **Behold** - Your language may have a different word to show that Ezekiel was surprised by what he saw.
