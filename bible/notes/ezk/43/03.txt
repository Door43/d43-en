===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And it was like** - The word "it" refers to the coming of the glory of God (43:2).
  * **like the appearance of the vision that I saw**  - This phrase suggests that the vision described in this verse came to the prophet in the same way as the other visions reported in the earlier portions of this book. This can be rephrased as: "This vision of the coming of the glory of God was like my previous visions at the river Chebar (See: 1:28) and when God came to destroy Jerusalem" (See: 14:12-21).
  * **River Chebar** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:1]].
  * **fell on my face**  - This means "to prostrate" or "bow down"; a sign of honor and worship (See [[:en:obe:kt:worship]])
  * **Glory of Yahweh**  - This means the “splendor” or “majesty” of God
  * **behold** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **lifted me up**  - Alternate translation: "carried me"
