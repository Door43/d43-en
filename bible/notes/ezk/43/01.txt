===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Behold!** - The word "Behold" here alerts the reader to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **like the sound of many waters** - This simply means "a lot of water." It could refer to a loud river or a large waterfall or the waves crashing at the ocean. All of these are very loud.Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:24|1:24]].
  * **the earth shone** - "the earth was full of bright light"
