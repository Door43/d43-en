===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In order for me**  - The word "me" refers to God.
  * **from you**  - "You" refers to the people of Israel
  * **my sword**  - 'My' refers to God.
  * **against all flesh from the south to the north - ** This means "against everyone in the entire country."
  * **It will no longer relent **- Alternate translation: "It will not go back in its sheath."
