===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Ammon.

  * **You will become fuel for the fire** - "The fire will burn up your bodies"
  * **Your blood will be in the midst of the land** - "Your blood will soak into the ground of your land" 
  * **You will not be remembered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "No one will remember you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
