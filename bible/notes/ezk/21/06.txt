===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **groan as your loins break**  - Ezekiel is being told to make sounds as if he were in great pain. (See: Simile)
  * **groan**  - This means to make a sound in response to pain or despair.
  * **For what reason are you groaning?**  - Ezekiel's groaning will cause them to question the emotion he is expressing.
  * **report**  - This word means spoken or written news.
  * **every heart will faint away**  - This means they will feel very weak or fearful.
  * **every hand will falter **- This means to lose strength or move unsteadily.
  * **every knee will flow like water**  - "every knee will be as weak as water" (See: Simile) Alternate translation: "every knee will be wet with urine."
  * **Behold! **- This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **declaration **- This means a formal statement or announcement.
