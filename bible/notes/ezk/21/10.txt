===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is sharpened**  - A sword is sharpened.
  * **slaughter**  - This word means to kill in a bloody or violent way.
  * **flash like lightning**  - "flashing in a brief, sudden or intermitten way" (See: Simile).
  * **Should we rejoice?**  - This rhetorical question is to emphasize the point that there will be no rejoicing in this horrible act. Alternatively; "We should not rejoice!" (See: Rhetorical question).
  * **The rod of my son rejects every tree**  - Alternate translation: "My son rejects every rod of discipline."
  * **rod **- This word describes a piece of wood used for discipline.
  * **my son**  - This means God's people.
  * **So it is given to be polished to be seized in the hand!** - The rod is fashioned so it can be held in the hand (as a sword).
