===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **is drawn** - "is out of its sheath" 
  * **empty visions** - "visions that are not true"
  * **for you** - The word "you" is feminine singular and refers to the nation of Ammon. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])
  * **to be killed** - AT: "to die." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the wicked** - the wicked people of Ammon
