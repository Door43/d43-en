===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues to describe the sword of Yahweh which is a metaphor for the enemy army that Yahweh will use to attack Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **hit your two hands together** - This means for him to clap his hands as a sign of his grief. AT: "clap your hands to show that you are very sad" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **even a third time** - "even a 3rd time." This is an idiom that means multiple times. AT: "again and again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **A sword for the ones to be slaughtered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "It is a sword for slaughtering people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **It is a sword for the many to be slaughtered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "It is a sword for slaughtering many people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **piercing them everywhere** - "attacking them from every side." Some Bibles translate this as "which enters into their rooms." This means no matter where they hide, the sword will kill them.
  * **piercing them** - The word "them" refers to the people of Jerusalem and of the land of Israel.
