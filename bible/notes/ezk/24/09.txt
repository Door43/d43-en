===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **Woe to the city of blood **- See how you translated this in Ezekiel 24:6.
  * **I will enlarge the pile of wood **  - This means that Yahweh will make the stack or pile of wood bigger and taller.
  * **Kindle the fire **- This means 'start the fire.'.
  * **let the bones be charred **  - This means to allow the bones to be burnt.
