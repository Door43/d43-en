===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling a parable to Ezekiel about a cooking pot that represents Jerusalem. This parable continues through [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:24:14|24:14]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **the city of blood** - Here "blood" is a reference to "murder." AT: "the city of murderers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will also enlarge the pile of wood** - It is implied that the pile of wood is under the cooking pot that represents Jerusalem. "I will make the pile of wood on the fire under you even bigger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **kindle the fire** - "light the fire" 
  * **let the bones be charred** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "burn the bones" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
