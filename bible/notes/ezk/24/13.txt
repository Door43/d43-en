===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your, you - **  Refers to the Israelites (A Possessive Pronoun)
  * **shameful behavior is in your uncleanliness**  - This means Israel's bad behaviour or 'immoral' behavior that is unfit in God's eyes
  * **I have cleansed you but you were not clean**-(Alternate Translation); "<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;;;inherit>I tried to cleanse you from your wicked behavior, but you did not allow me to do that."</font>
  * **I, my**  - This is a Pronoun which refers to Yahweh
  * **until my fury rests from you **- this means that until God withdraws his wrath from Israel
