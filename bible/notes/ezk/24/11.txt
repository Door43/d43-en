===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **scorch** - to burn the surface of something.
  * **corrosion** - when something causes metal to break apart and rust.
  * **consumed** - "burnt away"
  * **She has become weary** - AT: "Jerusalem has become tired"
  * **toil** - difficult labor.
