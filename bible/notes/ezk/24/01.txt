===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **the word of Yahweh came to me**  - "God spoke to Ezekiel"
  * **in the ninth year, the tenth month, on the tenth day of the month**  - It is almost nine years after Ezekiel's group has been taken to live in Babylon.
  * **the king of Babylon set against Jerusalem** - The words "set against" refer to surrounding an enemy.  Alternate translation: "the king of Babylon sent his army to surround the city of Jerusalem" or "the army of the king of Babylon has surrounded Jerusalem"(UDB). 
  * **the king of Babylon**  - The words "king of Babylon" refer to the army of Babylon. (See: Metonymy)
