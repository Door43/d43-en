===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh speaks to Ezekiel.

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the ninth year** - AT: "in the ninth year of the exile of King Jehoiachin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the tenth month, on the tenth day of the month** - "the tenth day of the tenth month." This is the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the beginning of January on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the king of Babylon has besieged Jerusalem** - The army of Babylon is referred to by its leader. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
