===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);/ ;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: 'Noto Sans', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);>- See how you translated this in 3:16.</font>
  * **in the ninth year, the tenth month, on the tenth day of the month**  - It is almost nine years after Ezekiel's group has been taken to live in Babylon.
  * **the king of Babylon set against Jerusalem**  - The words "set against" refer to facing or surrounding an enemy. Alternate translation: "the king of Babylon sent his army to attack or surround the city of Jerusalem" or "the army of the king of Babylon has attacked or  surrounded Jerusalem"(UDB).
  * **the king of Babylon**  - The words "king of Babylon" refer to the army of Babylon. (See: Metonymy)
