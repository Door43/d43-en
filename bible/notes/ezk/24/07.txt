===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Jerusalem.
  * **her** - The word "her" refers to Jerusalem, which is represented by the cooking pot.
  * **has set it on the smooth rock** - "has put the blood on bare rocks"
  * **to cover it with dust** - "and cover it with dirt"
