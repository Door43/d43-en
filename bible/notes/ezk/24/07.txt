===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Her**  - This is a pronoun referring to Jerusalem.
  * **For her blood ... in the midst of her** - The people who were murdered in Jerusalem (See: Metonymy).
  * **She has set it on the smooth cliff; she has not poured it out on the ground to cover it with dust** -   Alternate Translation: "they were murdered on the bare rocks, not on the soil, where their blood could be covered".(UDB) with earth or dust.
  * **I**  - This is a pronoun referring to Yahweh.
  * **I will set her blood on the smooth cliff so it cannot be covered**  - Alternate Translation : "I did that in order that I could see it and then be angry and get revenge." (UDB).
