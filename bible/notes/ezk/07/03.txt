===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues God's prophecy of judgment on Israel through the prophet Ezekiel.

  * **the end is upon you** - "your life is finished" or "your end of your time has arrived"
  * **your ways** - This phrase compares the way people act to a road that they are walking on. Alternate translation: "the way you act" or the things you do." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will bring all your abominations upon you** - "I will make you experience the effects of your disgusting behavior"
  * **For my eyes will not look compassionately at you** - "For I will not look at you with compassion" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **I will bring your ways upon you** - "I will make you experience the effects of your bad behavior"
  * **your abominations will be in your midst** - "your wicked actions will surround you."
