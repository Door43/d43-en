===== Translation Notes: =====

This starts God's prophecy of judgment on Israel.

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]]. 
  * **to the land of Israel** - "to the people of Israel". (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the four borders of the land** - This refers to the entire land of Israel.
