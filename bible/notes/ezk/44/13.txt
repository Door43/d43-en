===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel.
  * **They** - "These Levites"
  * **come near** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:40:46|40:46]].
  * **draw near** - "come near"
  * **to any of my holy things, to the most holy things** - AT: "To any of my holy things, including the most holy things."
  * **will bear their reproach and their guilt** - "will be ashamed and suffer the consequences for"
  * **disgusting actions** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:09|5:9]].
  * **keepers**  - a person whose job is to guard or take care of something or someone
  * **that is done in it** - AT: "that they need to do in it." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
