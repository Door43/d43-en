===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **draw near**  - come closer.
  * **keepers**  - a person whose job is to guard or take care of something or someone.
  * **workings**  - the activities required for something to work.
  * **to any of my holy things, to the most holy things** - Alternate translation: "To any of my holy things, including the most holy things."
  * **they will lift up their reproach and their abominations that they have done** - Alternate translation: "they will exalt their sinfulness and their sin".
  * **for all of its workings and for everything that is done in it** - to maintain all the activities of the temple.
