===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel.
  * **outer courtyard** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:10:03|10:5]].
  * **they** - the descendants of Zadok who serve Yahweh in his temple
  * **in order to go to the people** -  "to approach others" or "to go where other people are" (UDB).
  * **lay them down** - "leave them"
