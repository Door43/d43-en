===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel.
  * **So it will be that ** - "So"
  * **they will have** - The word "they" refers to the descendants of Zadok who were faithful to Yahweh.
  * **the inner courtyard** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **dress** - "to put on"
  * **linen** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:09:01|9:2]].
  * **wool** - cloth or clothing made of the soft hair of sheep
  * **and its house** - "or inside the temple" (UDB)
  * **turban** - a head covering that is that is made of a long cloth wrapped around the head
  * **underclothes** - "pants"
  * **sweat** - when a person produces small drops of water on their skin when they are hot
