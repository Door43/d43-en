===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Next he** - Possible meanings are 1) "Next the man" or 2) "Next Yahweh".
  * **behold** -  The word "behold" here shows that Ezekiel was surprised by what he saw.
  * **the glory of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:03:12|3:12]].
  * **I fell on my face** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:01:27|1:28]].
  * **set your heart** - See how you translated "fix your mind on" in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:03|40:4]].
  * **all its regulations** - "all the instructions about the house of Yahweh"
