===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Next he** - Possible meanings are 1) "Next the man" or 2) "Next Yahweh".
  * **behold** -  The word “behold” here shows that (the person) was surprised by what he saw. 
  * **I fell upon my face** - This means to bow down.
  * **set your heart** - This means to prepare yourself (See: Metonymy).
  * **look with your eyes**  - The phrase "with your eyes" emphasizes the command to look.
  * **listen with your ears**  -  The phrase "with your ears" emphasizes the command to hear.
  * **And set your heart to enter the house with all the exits of the sanctuary** - In this phrase, Yahweh asks Ezekiel to prepare himself to enter the house, and to pay attention to the exits also.
  * **statutes** - "regulations".
