===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel about the descendants of Zadok who serve as priests.
  * ** The best of the first-fruits of all things** - This is the best part of the harvest.
  * **anything from all of your contributions** - alternate translation: anything that you give
  * **The priests will not eat any carcass or animal rent by a beast** - The priests are not allowed to eat an animal that has been injured or killed by another animal.
  * **carcass** - This is the body of a dead animal.
  * **by a beast, whether bird or beast** - alternate translation: "by any bird or beast"
