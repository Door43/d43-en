===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my holy office**  - This is God's position.
  * **have established some to keep my office**  - This means to put other people in God's place.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **No foreigner of all the foreigners** - the repetition emphasizes the ban of foreigners.
  * **sons** - male children.
