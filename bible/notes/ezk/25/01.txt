===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:3]].
  * **came to me** - The word "me" refers to Ezekiel.
  * **set your face against** - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:04:01|4:3]].
  * **the sons of Ammon** - "the descendants of Ammon" or "those who live in the land of Ammon"
  * **and prophesy against** - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:04:06|4:7]].
