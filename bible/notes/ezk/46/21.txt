===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **outer courtyard** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:10:03|10:5]].
  * **Behold** - The word “behold” here shows that Ezekiel saw something interesting.
  * **there were four corner courtyards** - "there were courtyards that had four corners"
  * **There the same dimensions for all four four of the corner courtyards** - "All four of the courtyards in the corners were the same size"
