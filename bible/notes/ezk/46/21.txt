===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A court was in the each corner of the court** - There was a small area that was walled in.
  * **cubits** - (See: Biblical distances).
  * **there were four corner courts, forty cubits long and thirty wide** - Each of the four small courts were forty cubits long and thirty cubits wide.
  * **cooking hearths were made under the surrounding row of stones** - The place where the sacrifices were cooked was below a ledge of stones.
