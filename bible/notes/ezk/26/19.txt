===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.

  * **make you** - The word "you" here refers to the city of Tyre. In the original language "you" here is feminine singular.
  * **When I make you a desolate city, like the other cities that are not inhabited** - Here Tyre is compared to other cities that no longer had people living in them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **the deeps** - Here "the deeps" means the deep waters of the sea. AT: "the deep waters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **then I will bring you down to the people of ancient times, like the others who have gone down into the pit; for I will make you live in the lowest realms of the earth as in ruins of ancient times** - What Yahweh would do to Tyre is compared to people going to the grave and cities falling into ruin. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **the pit** - This refers to the grave; because the grave was thought to be the entrance to the world of the dead, the pit also represents that world.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **stand in the land of the living** - This represents being restored to the city's former existence. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will place disaster on you** - The abstract noun "disaster" can be translated using the adjective "terrible" or the verb "destroy." AT: "I will bring you to a terrible end" or "I will destroy you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Then you will be sought, but you will never be found ever again** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Then people will seek you but not be able to find you ever again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - Translate this phrase as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
