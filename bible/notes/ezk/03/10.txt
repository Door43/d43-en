===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **take them into your heart and hear them with your ears** - "remember them and listen carefully" 
  * **Then go to the captives** - The word "captives" refers to the people of Israel who were living in Babylon.
  * **your people** - The phrase "your people" refers to the people of Israel who were taken by force to Babylon.
  * **the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:04|2:4]].
