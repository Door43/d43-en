===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **with bitterness in my spirit's rage**  - The word "bitterness" means a taste that is strong and bad. Many fruits have a thick outer skin that is bitter so animals won't want to eat it. Here it is used to refer to his emotions as strong and negative. (See: [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **for the hand of Yahweh was deeply impressed upon me**  - The "impressed" means to press or push in and create the form of what has been pressed. It is like pressing or pushing a hand into mud and creating the form or impression of a hand. God impressed his ideas on to Ezekiel and they became a part of him. "because the desire of Yahweh had become a part of me"
  * **captives at Tel-abib**- "Tel-abib" is the name of a place in Babylon, about 80 kilometers southeast of the main city also called Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **along the Chebar canal**  - The "Chebar canal" was a waterway that was dug to bring water from the main river to where the people live and their crops grow. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **overwhelmed in amazement**  - The word "overwhelmed" refers to having a powerful emotion that often comes by surprise and takes all of a person's focus.
