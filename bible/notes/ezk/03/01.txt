===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Ezekiel continues to tell about the vision that he saw.

  * **He said to me** - The word "He" refers to the Spirit of God. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:2]]
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:03]].
  * **what you have found** - This refers to the scroll that God was giving him. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:09|2:9]])
  * **that scroll** - Many versions have "the scroll" or "this scroll."
  * **feed your stomach and fill your belly with this scroll** - These two phrases mean the same thing. AT: "eat this scroll." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **it was as sweet as honey** - "it tasted sweet like honey"
