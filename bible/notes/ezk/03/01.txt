===== Important Terms: =====

  * Son of Man
  * Scroll

===== Translation Notes: =====

  
-This an Old Testament phrase explaining man or human being.

  * **He said to me-** -"the spirit said to me"
  * **What you have found**-- This refers to the scroll.

  * **Eat the scroll, then go speak**  - This phrase suggests that Ezekiel must have the scroll message within himself in order to share.
  * **House of Israel**  - This refer to the entire nation of Israel.and could mean as the people of Israel
  * **Honey**- a sweet liquid.
