===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **at dawn** - "Early in the morning"
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in 3:16.
  * **house of Israel** - See how you translated this in 3:1.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **this oracle** - "this lament" or "this sad song" or "this sad chant".
  * **in whose midst they are** - "all the people who are there."
