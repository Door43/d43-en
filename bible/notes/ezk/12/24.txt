===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **The matter will no longer be delayed** - "What I predict will happen soon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **speak this word** - Possible meanings 1) "everything that I say will happen" (UDB) or 2) "speak this message" or 3) "speak this prophecy"
  * **in your days** - "while you are alive"
