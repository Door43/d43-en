===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **false visions**  - This refers to dreams in the daytime but it was not from God and was invented to deceive or give untrue information.
  * **deceitful oracles** - "dishonest" or "insincere" prophecies
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
