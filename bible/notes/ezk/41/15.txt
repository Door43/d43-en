===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cubits** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **porches** - an entrance to a building that has a separate roof; it may or may not have walls. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-temple|porch]]).
  * **galleries** - passageways or veranda or balcony.  (see: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-temple|gallery]]).
  * **wooden enclosure** - this describes an area contained or closed-in by a wooden wall.
