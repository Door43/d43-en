===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **raised part** - This is the platform.
  * **chamber** -  This refers to a small room.
  * **cubits** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **joint**  -  This refers to a corner.
  * **And that is what was left of the house: side chambers that were for the house** - Alternate translation: All around the temple there was an open area between those side rooms".
