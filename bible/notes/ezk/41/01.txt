===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The man in Ezekiel's vision ([[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:03|40:3]]) continues to show Ezekiel around the temple.

  * **the temple's holy place** - the room in the temple in front of the "most holy place"
  * **six cubits in width on either side** - These cubits were the "long" cubits ([[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]), 54 centimeters. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]. "they were six cubits in width on both sides"
  * **six cubits** - about 3.2 meters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **cubits** - Each long cubit was about 54 centimeters. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **ten cubits** - about 5.4 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **five cubits** - about 2.7 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **the dimensions** - "the size"
  * **forty cubits** - about 22 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **twenty cubits** - about 11 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
