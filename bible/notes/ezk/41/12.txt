===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **faced **- "across from" or "in front of"
  * **the separated side by the way to the west **- This could mean a separate area or yard on the the west side
  * **The building that faced the separated side by the way to the west — seventy cubits wide. And the building wall – five cubits wide all around, and its length was ninety cubits**  - This can be rephrased as "The buliding across from the separate area was seventy cubits wide, five cubits thick, and ninety cubits long"
  * **He measured **- The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance was like bronze (40:3)
  * **house **- "temple"
  * **The width of the front of the house and the separated building east of it – one hundred cubits**
 - This can be rephrased as: "The width of the front of the temple and the  separate area on the east side was one hundred cubits"
