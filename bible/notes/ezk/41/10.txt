===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the priests' outer side rooms** - "the side rooms of the priests that were further out from the sanctuary"
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **twenty cubits** - about 11 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **five cubits** - about 2.7 meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **There were doors into the side rooms from another open space—one doorway was on the north side, and the other on the south side** - "And the two doorways of the side chambers faced the temple, with one facing north and one facing south"
  * **The width of this open area was five cubits all around** - "The entire space between the chambers and the temple was five cubits wide, all around the temple"
