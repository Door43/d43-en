===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to Ezekiel.
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **the wind** - The same word is used for "wind," "spirit" and "breath."
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **from the four winds** - AT: "from the four different directions that the wind can blow" or "from every direction"
  * **these killed ones** - AT: "these people that enemies and disasters had killed." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **as I was commanded** - AT: "as Yahweh had commanded me" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
