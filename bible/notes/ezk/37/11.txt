===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to Ezekiel
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. Alternate translation: “Indeed.”
  * **prophesy and say** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:21:08]].
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
