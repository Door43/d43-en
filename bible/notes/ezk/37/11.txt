===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **son of man - (**  See Standard Notes for Ezekiel) See how you translated this in 2:3.
  * **Behold - (**  See Standard Notes for Ezekiel) Adds emphasis to what is to follow. Could be translated "indeed".
  * **prophesy and say** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:21:08]].
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5
  * **they, we, them, you, your, our **- All these words refers to the people of Israel
  * **me**  -This refers to Ezekiel
  * **I and my -** This****  refers to Yahweh
