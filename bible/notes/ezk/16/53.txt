===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to refer to other nations as relatives of Jerusalem.
  * **the fortunes of - ** The word "fortune" means their "wealth."
  * **and her daughters- ** The word "daughters" refers to the people that are related to those to whom this is addressed.
  * **you will be humiliated because of everything that you have done**- alternate translation: "you will be sorry for what you have done" or "You will be very ashamed of the wicked things that you have done" (UDB).
  * **comfort to them**- Israel will be a help them.
  * **So your sisters ** - The word "So" may also mean "when" as in this context.
