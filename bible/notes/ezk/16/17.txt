===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **made for yourself male figures** - Alternate translation: "made for yourself masculine images to worship" or "made male idols." (UDB).
  * **You took your embroidered garments and covered them** - The word "them" refers to the male figures.
  * **and you set my oils and perfumes before them** - This means that the woman put before the male figures the oils and the perfumes given to her by Yahweh.  The word "perfumes" (ULB) is the word "incense" (UDB).
  * **a sweet savor** - Alternate translation: "a wonderful taste" or "fragrant sacrifice" (UDB).
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in [[:en/bible/notes/ezk/05/11|5:11]].
