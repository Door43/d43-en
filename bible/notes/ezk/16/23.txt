===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Woe! Woe to you! **- Alternate translation: "Sadness! Sadness to each of you!" or "declare that terrible things will happen to you" (UDB).
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in 5:11.
  * **Therefore, in addition to all this wickedness**  - Yahweh is explaining that in with all of the previous wickendess the people committed more wickedness.
  * **a ritual place - ** Alternate translation: "a place for sacrifice" or "a shrine to worship idols" (UDB).
