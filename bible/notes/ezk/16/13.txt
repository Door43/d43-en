===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So you were adorned with gold and silver, and you were dressed in fine linen, silk, and embroidered clothes** - Here Yahweh is clothing the young woman in preparation for her marriage with appropriate clothing.
  * **royal estate** - Alternate translation: "royalty" or "queen."
  * **Your fame** - The word "fame" may be translated to "reputation" or "popularity."
  * **was perfect in the majesty that I had given to you** - The word "perfect" may be translated as "complete." The word "majesty" is a word used for persons of royalty (a king or a queen).
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in [[:en/bible/notes/ezk/05/11|
5:11]].
