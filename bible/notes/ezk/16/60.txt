===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **call to mind** - "remember"
  * **your older sisters and your younger sisters** - This doesn't refer to family members but to non-Jewish people.
  * **I will give them to you as daughters** - This means Israel must take care of them.
  * **but not because of your covenant** - God will not have the same relationship with them that God has with Israel.
