===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to refer to Jerusalem as an unfaithful wife and other nations as relatives of Jerusalem.
  * **call to mind** - "remember"
  * ** in the days of your youth** - "when you were young".
  * **I will give them to you as daughters** - This means Israel must take care of the other nations.
  * **but not because of your covenant** - They are not being brought back because of Yahweh's promise to Israel but because Yahweh has chosen to show kindness to them.
