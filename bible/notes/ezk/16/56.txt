===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to refer to other nations as relatives of Jerusalem.
  * **Sodom your sister was not even mentioned by your mouth** - Jerusalem's mouth speaking refers to the people of Jerusalem speaking. AT: "You would not even say the name of Sodom your sister" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **you are an object of scorn** - This phrase refers to Jerusalem as something people talk badly about.
  * **despise**  - "hate"
  * **all about** - "all around" or "on every side"
  * **You will show your shame and your disgusting actions** - "You will be humiliated and everyone will understand how sinful you have been"
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
