===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you devour men and your nation has become bereaved of children**  -The word "you" refers to the mountains of Israel. The mountains of Israel were spoken of as if they were a wild animal devouring men and children. God is saying that will not happen anymore.(See: Personification)
