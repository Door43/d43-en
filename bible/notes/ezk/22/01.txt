===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Son of man, will you judge? Will you judge the city of blood?**  - This emphasizes the point that Ezekiel is to be ready to judge Jerusalem. God is saying to Ezekiel to get ready to speak to Jerusalem. (See Rhetorical question.)
  * **her/herself**  - This pronoun refers to Jerusalem.  It is considered feminine.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - “This is what the Lord Yahweh says!"
  * **makes idols against herself to make herself unclean! **- Jerusalem makes idols to her own harm, making herself unclean!
