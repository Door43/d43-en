===== Important Terms: =====

Yahweh continues to speak through Ezekiel to the people of Jerusalem.

  * **guilty**
  * **blood**
  * **unclean**
  * **idols**
  * **reproach**
  * **nations**
  * **mock**
  * **name**

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You are guilty by your blood that you have poured out, and are made unclean by your idols which you have made!**  - This is a summary of verse 3. The people of Jerusalem are guilty of two things: Shedding blood and worshiping idols.
  * **For you drawn your days close and approached your years.**  - The days of the lives of the people of Jerusalem are coming to an end.
  * **derision**  - This word means the act of ridiculing or laughing at someone or something.
  * **your famous name is full of turmoil**  - Jerusalem's once famous name is now full of confusion.
