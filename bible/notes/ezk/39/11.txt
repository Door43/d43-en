===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message about Gog.
  * **it will happen...that** - an important event marker. If your language has a way for doing this, you could use it here.
  * **It will block** - Possible meanings are 1) "the grave will block" or 2) "the dead army will block"
  * **There they** - "There the house of Israel"
  * **They will call it** - "People will call it"
  * **Valley of Hamon Gog** - "Valley of the Great Army of Gog"
