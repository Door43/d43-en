===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **that I myself am sacrificing for you**  - "I" here is God speaking, "you" means the birds and all the living things.
  * **fatlings**  - The meaning is stall-fed or fattened livestock.
  * **Bahan**  - is a place (See How to Translate Names.)
