===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message about Gog.

  * **I will set my glory among the nations** - "I will make the nations see my glory"
  * **my judgment that I perform and my hand that I have set against them** - Both of these phrases refer to the punishment that Yahweh will inflict upon Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **my hand** - Here the word "hand" refers to Yahweh's power that he exercises to bring punishment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **against them** - "against the people of Gog's great army"
