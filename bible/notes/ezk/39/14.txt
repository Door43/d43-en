===== Important Terms: =====

  * purify
  * name
  * cleanse

===== Translation -refers the people (or leaders) of Israel. =====

  * **They **- refers to "men set apart."
  * **set apart men with the vocation **-****  This means men designated for a special job/function (to bury the dead).
  * **crossed over**  - this is a way of saying those who had died in battle
  * **it **- refers to the land.
  * **Valley of Hamon-Gog **  –This is the name of a place. (See How to Translate Names)
  * **Hamonah -  ** This is the name of a city which means "huge army".
