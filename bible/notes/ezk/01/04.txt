===== Translation Notes: =====

Ezekiel continues to describe his vision.

  * **a wind storm** - This is a storm that has a lot of wind.
  * **coming from the north** - North is the directions to your left when you face the rising sun.
  * **a great cloud with fire flashing within it and brightness surrounding it and inside of it, and the fire was like amber within the cloud** - This could be translated as a new sentence: "The storm had a very large cloud with constant lightning and a very bright light all around the cloud, and inside of the storm there was something like amber inside of a fire."
  * **fire flashing** - Possible meanings are 1) "flashing lightning" or 2) "constant lightning."
  * **a brightness** - "a very bright light"
  * **In the middle** – "Inside of the storm"
  * **was the likeness of four living creatures** - there was something that looked like four creatures that were alive"
  * **And this was their appearance** - "This is what the four creatures looked like"
  * **they had the likeness of a man, but they had four faces each, and each of the creatures had four wings** - "the four creatures looked like people, but each of the creatures had four different faces and four wings"
