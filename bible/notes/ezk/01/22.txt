===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **an expansive dome**  - A dome looks like a hollow ball that is cut in half and is placed over something else. "Expansive" means that the dome is very large like the mountains or the sky. At night time, the stars in the sky look like they are in "an expansive dome."
  * **it looked like** - The word "it" refers to the "expansive dome"
  * **an awe-inspiring crystal** - "awe-inspiring ice" or "ice that makes people marvel when they look at it"
  * **stretched out over their heads above** - "and the expansive dome was spread out above the heads of the creatures" or "and the expansive dome took up a lot of space over the heads of the creatures"
  * **Beneath the dome** - "under the dome"
  * **Each living creature also had a pair to cover themselves–each had a pair to cover their own bodies** - "Each of the living creatures also had two wings, which they used to cover their bodies"
