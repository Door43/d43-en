===== translationNotes: =====

  * **looked like burning coals of fire, or like torches** - AT: "were bright like coals in a hot fire, or like torches" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and there were flashes of lightning** - AT: "and lightning came out from the fire."
  * **The living creatures were moving swiftly back and forth, and they looked like lightning!** - Ezekiel compares the creatures with lightning because they move very fast like lightning. AT: "The living creatures were going forward and coming back so quickly that they looked like lightning!"
