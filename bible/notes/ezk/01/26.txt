===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **over their heads** - "over the heads of the living creatures"
  * **was the appearance of a throne** - "was something that looked like a throne"
  * **sapphire**  - a very valuable stone that is clear blue and very shiny
  * **on the throne was** - "on the throne there was"
  * **the appearance of one who looked like a man** - "someone who looked like a man" or "someone who looked like person"
