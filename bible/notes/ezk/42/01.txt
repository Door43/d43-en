===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the outer courtyard** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:10:03|10:5]].
  * **one hundred cubits ... fifty cubits ... twenty cubits** - Each long cubit was about 54 centimeters. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]. AT: "100 cubits ... 50 cubits ... 20 cubits" or "about 54 meters ... about 27 meters ... about 11 meters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **inner courtyard** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **were open to them** - "looked out onto the inner courtyard"
  * **having a walkway** - "because there was a place where one could walk along the rooms"
