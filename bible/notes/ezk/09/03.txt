===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from the cherubim where it had been** - Possible meanings are 1) "from above the four winged creatures" ([[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:04|1:5]]) or 2) from between the two cherubim in the most holy place in the temple. Try to translate this literally. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **threshold of the house** - The "house" refers to God's temple.
  * **linen** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:09:01|9:2]].
  * **scribe's equipment** - tools that scribes write with. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:09:01|9:2]].
  * **groan and sigh** - These are sounds people make when they feel very sad or grieved about something. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **the abominations being performed in the midst of the city** - "the horrible things being done in the city" or "the detestable things that people are doing in the city
