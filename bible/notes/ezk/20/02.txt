===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **me -**  This pronoun refers to Ezekiel
  * **declare - **"speak" or "state."
  * **inquire**  - See how you translated this command in 20:1.
  * **elders **- These are spiritually matured men who in the Old Testament helped lead the people of Israel in social justices and  religious matters such as maintaining the Law of Moses.
  * **As I live**  - God is swearing on His eternal nature. “I” refers to Lord Yahweh.
  * **I - **  This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **you - **  This pronoun refers to the Israeli elders.
  * **declaration**  - Synonyms for declaration include “announcement” or “proclamation”. An alternate translation could read “the Lord Yahweh strongly stated.”
