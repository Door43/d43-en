===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the forest of the Negev.
  * **will see** - "will understand"
  * **Then I said** - The word "I" refers to Ezekiel.
  * **Alas** - a word that expresses distress or grief
  * **they are saying** - The word "they" refers to people who heard what Ezekiel said.
  * **Is he not a mere teller of parables** - The people use this rhetorical question to say that Ezekiel is making up stories and that he is not delivering messages from Yahweh. AT: "He is just telling stories." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
