===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as I live . . . surely - **  God is swearing by His eternal nature. Another translation could be" I, as Yahweh..."
  * **I**  - Ths pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **declaration - ** See how you translated these commands in 20:3.
  * **outstretched arm and fury poured out - ** This phrase means the Jews will be ruled by a powerful force and feel the wrath flowing from Yahweh.
  * **bring you out of the wilderness and into the peoples - ** This phrase means that Yahweh will move the people of Israel to another area surrounded by other nations.
  * **you**  - This pronoun refers to Israel.
