===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **me/my**  - These pronouns refer to Yahweh.
  * **them/they**  - These pronouns refer to "the children" which is the house (or people) of Israel.
  * **statutes**  - the law of God.
  * **keep**  - do or perform.
  * **determined**  - decided.
  * **pour our**  - to flow forth in great quantity.
  * **fury**  - violent anger or rage, wrath.
  * **brought them out**  - This phrase refers to bringing "the children" of Israel out of Egypt in the book of Exodus.
