===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rebelled against me **- They refused to submit to me.
  * **me**  - "Me" is a pronoun referring to God in this sentence.
  * **not willing to listen**  - "not willing to hear"
  * **did not throw away**  - This means "did not get rid of."
  * **detestable things of his eyes **- See how you translated this phrase in Ezekiel 20:7.
  * **forsake**  - This means "to give something up."
  * **pour out my fury**- God's anger will flow out.
  * **wrath**  - This means 'intense anger.'
  * **acted for my name's sake**  - A phrase that God is acting with all power and authority.
  * **profaned in the eyes of the nations**  - This means disrespected among the nations.
