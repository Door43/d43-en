===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I/myself/my**  - These pronouns refer to Yahweh.
  * **them/they/their**  - These pronouns refer to “the children” which is the house (or people) of Israel.
  * **lifted my hand**  - God is giving a solemn promise. It was a common practice to take an oath by raising a hand to heaven.
  * **to scatter them among the nations and disperse them among the lands**  - Literally this means to dash into pieces and scatter to the wind. The word "scatter" means to separate and drive off in various directions. The word disperse is very similar and the two words are often used interchangeably. This is a doublet, emphasizing what God will do.  (See: Doublet)
  * **nations…lands**  - These two words refer to other places or countries.
  * **perform**  - "obey" or "do" (that is, "execute")
  * **their eyes were after their fathers' idols**  - The phrase "eyes were after" refers to worshipping other idols. The place where a person sets their eye is where they are giving their attention.
