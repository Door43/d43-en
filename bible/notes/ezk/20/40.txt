===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my **- This pronound refers to Yahweh.
  * **holy mountain . . . mountain peak of Israel **- The location of the holy mountain is considered to be Mount Zion in Israel.
  * **declaration **- See how these commands were translated in 20:3
  * **all of the House of Israel will serve **- explanation
  * **me **- This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **I will be pleased - **  This phrase means that God will happily receive the offering and sacrafices stated in 20:40. An alternate translation could be, "I will gladly receive" or "I will accept." (UDB)
  * **soothing fragrance - **  This phrase is a metonym for the way in which Yahweh receives the offerings from Israel. An alternate translation could be, "I will accept your offerings as a joyful scent." (See: Metonymy).
  * **in the sight of the nations - **  This phrase means that the surrounding nations will be aware of the interactions between Yahweh and Israel. An alternate translation could be, "The neighboring nations will know." (See: Personification).
