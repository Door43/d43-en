===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I/my/me **- These pronouns refer to Yahweh.
  * **their/your/yourselves/you**  - These pronouns refer to the House of Israel.
  * **the statutes of their fathers **- These statutes (or laws) refer to man-made statutes and not those of God.
  * **do not keep their judgments**  - "Keep" means "to do" or "follow."
  * **Walk in my statutes - **  These statutes refer to the laws of God. "To walk in" means "to obey."
