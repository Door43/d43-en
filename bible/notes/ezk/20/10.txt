===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wilderness **- This means a dry barren place where very few trees and plants grow.
  * **made my judgements known to them**  - God decided and stated whether a certain behavior was morally right.
  * **Sabbath**  - This means "The Sabbath day was a special day each week that God commanded the Israelites to set apart as a day of rest. It was established as the seventh (last day) of every week.
  * **makes them holy**  - "sets them apart for the purpose of serving me and bringing me glory"
