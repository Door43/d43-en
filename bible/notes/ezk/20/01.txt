===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about** - This phrase is used to mark the beginning of a new part of the story.
  * **in the seventh year** - AT: "in the seventh year of the exile of King Jehoiachin" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the tenth day of the fifth month** - This is the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the beginning of August on western calendars. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **before me** - "in front of me"
