===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I - **  This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **your, you -**  These pronouns refers to Israel.
  * **declaration - ** A formal statement, either spoken or in writing. (See how you translated these commands in 20:3.)
  * **pass under the rod - **  This phrase is a metonym for punishment or discipline. (See Metonymy)
  * **bring into the bond of the covenant - **  This phrase means to restore an important agreement.
  * **purge**  - This phrase means "remove" or "cause them to leave."
