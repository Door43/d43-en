===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the elders of Israel.
  * **to them** - "to the descendants of the house of Jacob"
  * **from before his eyes** - This phrase means "that he loves" or "that he worships." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
