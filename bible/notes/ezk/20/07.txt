===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the detestable things of his eyes** - This is a phrase meaning "things that God dislikes strongly but that humans desire," including idols and money, among others.
  * **idols of Egypt** - Idols in Egypt are made from gold, silver and expensive wood to represent gods to worship.
  * **unclean** - 'spiritually sinful'.
