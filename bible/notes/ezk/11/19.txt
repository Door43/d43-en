===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues the prophecy of what will happen to the scattered Israelites.

  * **the stone heart from their flesh** - This is a way of saying the people will not change their way of thinking. Alternate translation: "their stubbornness" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **a heart of flesh** - This is a way to say the people are willing to obey God. Alternate translation: "a willingness to obey" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **affection** - "love"
  * **detestable things** - "things I hate"
  * **conduct** - "actions"
  * **declaration** - "statement"
