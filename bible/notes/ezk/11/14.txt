===== Translation Notes: =====

After Pelatiah dies, Yahweh God continues his message to Israel through Ezekiel.

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **your brothers! Your brothers!**  - This is stated twice for emphasis.
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **dwelling in Jerusalem** - Possible meanings: 1) "who are still in Jerusalem"(See: UDB) or 2) "who live in Jerusalem"
