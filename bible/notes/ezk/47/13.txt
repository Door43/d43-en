===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **border- ** The place where two pieces of land meet.
  * **divide the land**
 - Give a piece of land to each tribe.
  * **and you will inherit it**
 - You will not have to pay for it.
  * **each man and his brother**
 - This refers to each family of the tribe.
  * **as I have lifted my hand to give it to your fathers- **  God refers to  a process that was started with your fathers.
  * **the land has fallen to you-**  Now it is your turn to receive the land.
