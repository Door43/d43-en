===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will happen** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:21:06|21:7]].
  * **En Gedi** - a very large spring on the western side of the Salt Sea
  * **to dry out** - "for people to dry out"
  * **En Eglaim** - a large spring on the eastern side of the Salt Sea
