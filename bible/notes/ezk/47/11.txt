===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **swamps and marshes- **  This is land that is very wet.
  * **will not be healed**-The salt made the soil unable to grow plants. This land was not to be for growing plants.
  * **given over to salt-**  alternate translation: The marshes are left for producjng salt.
  * **each new moon-**  alternate translation: monthly.
