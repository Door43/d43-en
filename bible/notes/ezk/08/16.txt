===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold**  - The word “behold” here shows that Ezekiel was surprised by what he saw.
  * **portico **- This word refers to a large covered porch area.
  * **Shamesh**  - This is the name of a god in Babylon.  He was known as the sun god and the god of law and justice. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
