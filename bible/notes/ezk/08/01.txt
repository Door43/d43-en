===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So it came about**  - This is a way to introduce a new time period in the story. Alternate translation: "And it happened"
  * **as I sat…before me…from his hips**  - The words “I” and "me" refer to Ezekiel. The word "his" refers to the figure who looked like a man.
  * **the hand of the Lord Yahweh**  - The term "hand" is often used to refer to God's power and action. Alternate translation: "the power of God. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]], [[:en:ta:translate:anthro|Anthropomorphism]])
  * **the hand of the Lord Yahweh again fell on me**  - The phrase "fell on me" refers to a feeling or event that suddenly happened without your control. Someone may say a feeling of cold fell on me to say they suddenly got cold. Or my job just fell on me to say they were offered their job without looking for it. It's "the power of God suddenly began to control me"
  * **figure like a man**  - This is the shape or form of a person's body. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]]).
  * **glowing metal**  - The words "glowing metal", refer to metal which is so hot it produces a yellow/orange light.
