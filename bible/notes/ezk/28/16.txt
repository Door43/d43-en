===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the king of Tyre.
  * **I have thrown you as defiled from the mountain of God** - "I made you leave the mountain of God because you were unholy"
  * **destroyed you, you, cherub guard** - "destroyed you, the guarding cherub"
  * **from among the fiery stones** - "so that you were no longer among the fiery stones"
  * **the fiery stones** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:28:14|28:14]].
  * **with your beauty** - "because you were beautiful"
  * **before** - "in front of"
