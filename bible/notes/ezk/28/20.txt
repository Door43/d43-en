  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **set your face against** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:01|4:3]].
  * **prophesy against** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:06|4:7]].
  * **Behold**  - AT: “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you”
  * **so your people** - "so the peoples" or "so the nations"

  * **set your face**  - "turn toward"

  * **Behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **execute**- "to do or perform"
  * **I will be sanctified in her **- "I will show my holiness"
