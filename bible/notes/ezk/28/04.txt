===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the ruler of Tyre.
  * **with wisdom and skill** - "by your wisdom and your understanding"
  * **and obtained** - "and stored up"
  * **treasuries** - places to store valuable objects and keep them safe
