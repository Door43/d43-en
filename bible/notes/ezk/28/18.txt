===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the king of Tyre.
  * **dishonest trade**  - "cheating in deals and sales"
  * **sent out fire from your midst**  - "burn up your places to live"
  * **it will consume you**  - "it will destroy you completely"
  * **I will set you in the ashes of the earth - **"I will put you ashes in the ground."
  * **in the sight of **- This explains that all will be able to see.
  * **shudder-** "shake with fear"
  * **horrified- ** This expresses great shock.
  * **you will never more exist for all time- "**at no time in the future will you live."  (See:Doublet)
