  * **dishonest trade**  - cheating in deals and sales
  * **sanctuaries**  - safe, holy places
  * **sent out fire from your midst**  - burn up your places to live
  * **it will consume you**  - it will eat you up completely
  * **set - **  put
  * **in the sight of**
- in front of, facing

  * **shudder-**  shake with fear
  * **horrified- **  greatly upset, shocked
  * **you will never more exist- **  at no time in the future will you live
