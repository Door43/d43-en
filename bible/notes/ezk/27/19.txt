===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.

  * **cinnamon** - the type of spice that comes from the bark of a tree. "Cassia spice" is another name for this spice.
  * **calamus** - a type of grass that people used as perfume and for medicine
  * **saddle blankets** - a piece of cloth that people placed on horses beneath a saddle
