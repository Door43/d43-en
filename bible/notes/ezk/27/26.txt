===== Translation Notes: =====

Continuation of metaphor in which Tyre is compared to a great ship in verses 3-11 but is now being destroyed in verses 26-36.

  * **Your rowers have brought you to many waters** - This may refer to the leaders of Tyre who endangered the people.
  * **The eastern wind has broken you in the middle of them** - "Eastern wind" may refer to a destructive power or a symbol of God's power and indicates that Tyre is being destroyed in the middle of the seas.
  * **Men of war** - "soldiers" or "army" or "fighting men"
  * **They will fall into the depths of the sea** - "The city of Tyre will be destroyed."
