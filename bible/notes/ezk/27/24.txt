===== Important Terms: =====

  * robes
  * Tarshish
  * glorified
  * heart

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ornate robes of violet cloths with woven colors** - "beautiful, expensive garments".
  * **blankets of multi-colored and embroidered, strong cloth** - "beautiful and durable blankets".
  * **the ships of Tarshish were caravans** - The ships for trade were repeatedly entering and leaving Tyre.
  * **So you were filled up** - Tyre was full of merchandise. This is a picture of wealth.
