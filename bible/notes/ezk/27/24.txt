===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **ornate robes of violet cloths with woven colors** - "very beautiful purple robes with many different colors"
  * **blankets of multicolored, embroidered, and well-woven cloth** - "blankets with many colors that had designs on them and were of very high quality"
  * **were the transporters of your merchandise** â€“ "carried your merchandise" or "took your merchandise from place to place"
  * **were filled up** - "were full of merchandise." Yahweh continues to compare Tyre with a ship.
  * **heavily laden with cargo** - Possible meanings are 1) "you had a very large amount of merchandise" or 2) "and people honored you greatly."
  * **in the heart of the seas** - This phrase means "in the middle of the seas." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
