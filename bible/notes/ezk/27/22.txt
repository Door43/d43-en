===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **Sheba** -  the name of a tribe in the southern part of Arabia. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Raamah** -  the name of a tribe in the south-western pat of Arabia
  * **precious gems** - "valuable stones"
  * **Canneh** - the name of a place somewhere north and east of Tyre
  * **Eden** - the name of a nation in northern Syria that lived by the Euphrates
  * **Asshur** – a tribe that lived in the northern part of Iraq. This is another way of referring to the Assyrians
  * **Kilmad** – the name of the place
