===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  
  * **pilots** - people who control where the ship goes
  * **filled your seams** - "repaired you cracks" or "repaired you leaks"
  * **sailors** - people who sail ships on the sea
