===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
 
  * **iron** - a hard metal that was good for making weapons and building things
  * **tin** - the metal that is left after refining gold, silver or iron
  * **lead** - a valuable metal that is very heavy and softer than other metals
  * **Javan** - a nation on the western shore of Asia minor. It was also known as Ionia. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
