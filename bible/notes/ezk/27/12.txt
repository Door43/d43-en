===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **was your client** - "was your trader." People from Tarshish bought and sold the goods of Tyre.
  * **iron** - a hard metal that was good for making weapons and building things
  * **tin** - the metal that is left after refining gold, silver or iron
  * **lead** - a valuable metal that is very heavy and softer than other metals
  * **They bought** - "The people of Tarshish
  * **wares** - "products" or "goods"
  * **Javan** - a nation on the western shore of Asia minor. It was also known as Ionia. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **dealers with you in men's lives and in items of bronze** - "your traders, selling your goods in exchange for slaves and things made of bronze"
  * 
  * **Javan, Tubal, Meshech**  - These cities paid for Tyre's goods with slaves and bronze.
  * **traders in the souls of men **- "They bought and sold slaves"
