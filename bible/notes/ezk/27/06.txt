===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.

  * **oars** - long pieces of wood that people use to make the boat move
  * **decks** - the parts of the boat that people can walk on
  * **ivory** - a white, beautiful, and hard material that is made from the teeth of animals
  * **sails** - large pieces of cloth that move a ship when the wind blows on them
