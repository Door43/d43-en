===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Again the word of Yahweh</font>    **- Yahweh is speaking again, but this time directed at Tyre.
  * **came to me**  - "Me" is referring to Ezekiel.
  * **Now you, Son of man **- "You, Son of man" refers to Ezekiel.
  * **You who dwell - ** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The word of Yahweh is directed to "You who dwell" or "the people</font>    <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>who live in Tyre."</font>
  * **gates of the sea -**  Tyre was a gateway or important city or seaport or <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"gate of the sea."</font>
  * **thus says**  - This phrase was used by prophets to show they had authority to speak God's word <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>meaning "so Yahweh says.</font>
