===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **begin a lamentation concerning Tyre** - "sing a sad song about Tyre" or "mourn for Tyre by singing a sad song"
  * **You who live** - The word "you" is feminine singular and refers to the city of Tyre. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
  * **who live within the gates of the sea** - "who live at the gates of the sea." This phrase means 1) "who live by the ports of the sea" or 2) "who rule over the ports of the sea."
  * 



  * **You who dwell - **  The word of Yahweh is directed to "You who dwell" or "the people who live in Tyre."
  * **gates of the sea -** "ports" or "edge of the sea." Tyre was a gateway or important city or seaport or "gate of the sea." (See:Metaphor)
  * **thus says**  - This phrase was used by prophets to show they had authority to speak God's word meaning "so Yahweh says.
  * **Tyre! You have said “I am perfect in beauty!" **  - This portion begins a metaphor comparing the city of Tyre to a beautiful ship which continues for most of the chapter. (See:Metaphor)
