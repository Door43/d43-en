===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **Beth Torgamah** - the name  of a nation in Asia Minor, north of Syria. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **stallions** - strong male horses
  * **Rhodes**  - These exact identity of these people is uncertain.
  * **in your hand **- This indicates that the mechandise was "owned" or "recieved."
  * **horns, ivory and ebony **- Likely, these were goods that would have been available in Rhodes.
