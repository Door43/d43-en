===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **cities at the sea will tremble**  - "cities near the sea will be afraid"
  * **those who handle oars** - "those who row with oars"
  * **will come down from their ships** - "will get off of their ships"
  * **mariners and every sailor of the sea** - "sailors and all the pilots that work in the sea"
  * **they will make you listen to their voice** - "they will make their voices heard because of you"



  * **will ****wail bitterly. ****They will cast dust upon their heads. They will roll about in ashes. - ** These show mourning or great sadness.
