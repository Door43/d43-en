===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.

  * **purple** - "purple cloth" or "purple yarn"
  * **pearls** - beautiful pieces of hard white material that come from creatures in the sea
  * **rubies** - red stones that are very valuable
  * **balsam** - a sweet smelling gum or resin that comes from a tree
