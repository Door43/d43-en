===== Important Terms: =====

  * I am Yahweh 
  * contempt
  * desolate
  * devour
 

===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **mountain of Israel** - Refers to the rebellious exiled people of Israel.
    * **they are desolate** -  Without hope.
    * **they have been given to devour** - Given themselves over to be killed.
    * **gone against with their mouth** - Speak evil against the Lord.    
    * **multiplied your words against me** - Used all of the evil terms they knew to downplay the Lord.
    * **magnified yourself against me -**  "The people of Edom were  telling  others  they were more important than the Lord Jehovah."
  * **multiplied your words against me -**"The people of Edom told many lies against the Lord Jehovah"
