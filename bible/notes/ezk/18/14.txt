===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  - The word "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **if he bears a son **- This means that if he is the father of a son
  * **commits **- This word means ' to do '
  * **who sees all the sins of his father that he committed, …. and does not do such things **- Alternate Translation: “and the father does these evil things that his son would never do.”
  * ** nor eats upon the mountains -  ** Shrines to idols were often on mountain or hill tops.
  * ** and does not lift his eyes to the idols -  ** Another translation could be “nor look to idols for help” or “nor worshiped idols.”
  * **defiles**  - This refers to sexual sins which make them unacceptable to God.
