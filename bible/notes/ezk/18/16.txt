===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak about the son who does not commit the same sins as his father.

  * **interest** - This word refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money.
  * **interest** - Some modern versions interpret the Hebrew expression as "too much interest."
