===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the poor**  - This term means having little or no money, goods or other means of support
  * **who seizes and plunders - **  This means robbing valuables or things by force
  * **and does not return a pledge**  - A pledge is something given to guarantee a loan. Often, a cloak was used as a pledge for a debt and would be needed for warmth.
  * **who lifts up his eyes to the idols - **  Another translation could be "nor look to idols for help" or "nor worshipped idols."
  * **He/His**  - These pronouns refers to the violent son in 18:10
  * **who gives interest **- The word 'interest' refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money.
  * **and takes profit**  - The word 'profit' in this case refers to an unfair rate of interest.
  * **should he live? **- The purpose of the rhetorical quesiton is to emphasis a point. The meaning of the rhetorical question follows after the question. (see: Rhetorical question)
  * **his blood is upon him**  - This phrase refers to his guilt. Alternate translation: he takes full blame for his death.
