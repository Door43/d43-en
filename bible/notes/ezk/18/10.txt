===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who sheds blood** - This phrase represents murder in any form. AT: "who murders" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **any of these things** - This refers to the sinful actions that Yahweh had mentioned beginning in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:18:05|18:5]].
  * **to a brother** - Here the word "brother" refers to a fellow Israelite. AT: "to a fellow Israelite" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **eats on the mountains** - People often worshiped and offered sacrifices to pagan gods on the mountains. It is implied that this person has participated in these kinds of pagan rituals. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:18:05|18:5]]. AT: "eats meat sacrificed to idols on the mountains" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
