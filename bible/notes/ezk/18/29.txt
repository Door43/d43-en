===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The way of the Lord - **  This phrase refers to the actions of the Lord.
  * **How is my way not fair, house of Israel? **- This phrase is a rhetorical question used to emphasize a the point that Israel is not in a position to question the Lord. An alternate meaning could be, "My way is fair." (See Rhetorical Question).
  * **my **- This pronoun refers to the Lord.
  * **How is your way not fair? - **  the Lord used this question as a rebuke towards Israel, to say that the Lord's actions were a response to Israel's actions. An alternate meaning could be, "Your ways are not fair, Israel." (See Rhetorical Question)
  * **your **- This pronoun refers to the house of Israel.
  * **Each man among you according to his own way **- An alternate translation could be, "the actions of each man."
  * **you -**  This pronoun refers to the house of Israel.
  * **his - **  this pronoun refers to the House of Israel.
  * **they - **  this pronoun refers to the transgressions.
  * **declaration**  - "statement"
