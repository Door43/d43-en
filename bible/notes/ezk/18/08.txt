===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to describe the actions of the righteous man.

  * **interest** - This word refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money. However, some modern versions interpret "any interest" in this passage as "too much interest."
  * **excessive** - "too much"
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
