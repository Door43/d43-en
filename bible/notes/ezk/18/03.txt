===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As I live**  - God is swearing on His eternal nature. "I" refers to Yahweh.
  * **surely there will no longer be an occasion for you to use this proverb in Israel**. - "You" refers to the people of Israel. This could be translated as "This proverb will no longer be used by the people of Israel."
  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “indeed”. This can also be translated as “look” or “listen” or “pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
