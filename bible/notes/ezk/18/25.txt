===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The way of the Lord is not fairâ€‹**  - Alternate translation: "The way of the Lord is not correct."
  * **Listen, house of Israel!** - This phrase is a command or order and deserves exclamation. Alternate translation: "Hear, house of Israel!"
  * **Are my ways unfair?**  - The purpose of this rhetorical question is to rebuke the listeners. Another way of stating this is, "My ways are not unfair!" (See: [[en:ta:translate:RhetoricalQuestion]]).
  * **Is it not your ways that are unfair?**  - This second rhetorical question continues the rebuke to the listeners. Another way of stating this is, "Your ways are unfair!" (See: [[en:ta:translate:RhetoricalQuestion]]).
  * **When the righteous man turns away from his righteousness** - The phrase "turns away" has the sense of "to stop doing something."
  * **commits iniquity** - The word "commit" means "does" or "performs." This could be stated as "does evil."
  * **and dies because of them** - "Them" refers to the sins or iniquity he committed.
  * **he dies in the iniquity that he has done**- This repeats the same thought expressed in "and dies because of them." (See: Doublet).
