===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The Spirit continues speaking to Ezekiel.

  * **wheat, barley ... millet, and spelt** - These are different kinds of grains.
  * **beans** - vines whose seeds, which grow in a single row inside its otherwise empty fruit, can be eaten
  * **lentils** - These are like beans, but their seeds are very small, round, and somewhat flat. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **twenty shekels per day** - "20 shekels per day." AT: 200 grams of bread each day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **a sixth of a hin** - "1/6 hin" or "a sixth part of a hin" or "about one-half liter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
