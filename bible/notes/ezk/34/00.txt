====== Ezekiel 34 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== God protects the people ==

God is against those who exploit others under them. He will take care of his people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:peopleofgod]]) 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

The people of Israel are compared to sheep and God and the son of David as good shepherds. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
