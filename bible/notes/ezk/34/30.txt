===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I, Yahweh their God, am with them** - Here "I ... am with them" is an idiom that means Yahweh helps them. AT: "I, Yahweh their God, am helping them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **with them. They are my people** - This can be stated as one sentence. AT: "with them, and that they are my people"
  * **the house of Israel** - Here "house" represents people. AT: "the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **For you are my sheep, the flock of my pasture** - This speaks of the people of Israel as if they were a flock of sheep and Yahweh is their shepherd. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
