===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me: **  An alternate translaiton is "Yahweh spoke to me".
  * **me– **  This word referrs to Ezekiel.
  * **shepherd of Israel: **  This refers to the leaders of Israel (see Metaphor)
  * **prophesy and say** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:21:08]].
  * **Shouldn't shepherds shepherd the flock? (**  See: Rhetorical Question)**. **  An alternate translation is "Shepherds take good care of the flock. But you…"
  * **you eat the fat–**  The word "you" refers to the shepherds or leaders of Israel.
  * **The fatty portion**… **dress in wool…the fatlings of the flock**  all refer to the leaders/priests taking the best of the sacrifices for themselves.
