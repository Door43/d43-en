===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **34:28–"be plunder"**  –to be the thing that is robbed. Alternate translation: "The soldiers of other nations will stop attacking and robbing them of their valuable possessions." or "Soldiers from other nations will no long take away their valuable possessions…" (UDB)
  * **34:28--"devour"--** to eat with great hunger or with greed.
  * **34:28--"dwell securely"--** live peacefully or live in peace and safety**.**
  * **34:28, 29--**"They" and "them" is the flock/people of Israel. "I" is Yahweh.
  * **34:29--"I will raise up a renowned plantation..."** --Alternated translation: "I will make their homeland famous (renown) and fertile so they will no longer be hungry." or "I will cause their land to be famous-known for its good crops." (UDB)
