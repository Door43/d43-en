===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fugitive**  - a person who is running away so that he will not be captured.
  * **The city is struck down**  - Alternate translation: "Jerusalem is destroyed".
  * **hand of Yahweh**  - The term "hand" is often used to refer to God's power and action.  Alternate translation: "the power of God." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]], [[en:ta:translate:anthro]])
  * **The hand of Yahweh … I was no longer mute**  - On the previous evening, Yahweh took hold of me and gave me back my voice, so I was able to speak when this man arrived the next morning.
  * **dawn**  - early hours of the morning when sunlight appears.
