===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to Ezekiel.
  * **to them** - "to the people living in the ruins in the land of Israel"
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:10|3:11]].
  * **eat the blood** - It is implied that they eat blood by eating flesh that still has blood in it. Alternate translation: "eat flesh with blood in it." Yahweh had commanded people to not eat flesh with blood in it. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[en:bible:notes:gen:09:03|Genesis 9:4]] and [[en:bible:notes:lev:17:14|Leviticus 17:14]])
  * **lift up your eyes toward your idols**  - This phrase means "worship your idols" (UDB). Yahweh had commanded his people to not worship idols. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[en:bible:notes:exo:20:04|Exodus 20:5]]. 
  * **then you pour out peoples blood from them** - This can be translated as "and you pour out blood." This phrase means "you murder people" or "you kill people."
  * **Should you really possess the land?** - Yahweh used this question to rebuke the people. Alternate translation: "I will certainly not give you the land as an inheritance." (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **you have depended on your swords**  - This can be translated as "you have used your swords to get what you want."
  * **done disgusting things**  - This can be translated as "done things that I hate very much."
  * **each man defiles his neighbor's wife** - It is implied that "each man defiles his neighbor's wife by have sex with her." Yahweh commanded his people not to commit adultery. (See: [[en:bible:notes:exo:20:12|Exodus 20:14]])
