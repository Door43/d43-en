===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **eat the blood**  - "eat meat which still has blood in it"   Yahweh had commanded Israelites to drain the blood from aminals which they had killed for food.
  * **lift up your eyes toward**  - "honor" or "worship" (UDB)
  * **pour out blood**  - "murder people"
  * **Should you inherit the land?**  - Yahweh is s scolding and criticizing the people. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **stood on your swords**  - "relied upon your swords to get what you want"
  * **committed abominations**  - done acts that were evil and disgusting
  * **but you should inherit the land? **- Yahweh is scolding and criticizing the people. (See [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
