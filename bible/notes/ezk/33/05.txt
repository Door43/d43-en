===== Important Terms: =====

  * **blood**
  * **sword**
  * **iniquity**

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sound of the horn ... take warning**
 - See how your translated these phrases in Ez 33:3-4.
  * **If he hears the sound ... his blood will be upon him**  - See how you translated this in Ez 33:4.
  * **deliver his life**
 - "save himself"
  * **if the watchman sees the sword as it comes**  - See how you translated these phrases in Ez 33:2-3.  (See: Personification)
  * **so the people are not warned**  - Alternate translation: "to warn the people"  (See: Active or Passive)
  * **and the sword comes and takes their life** - See how you translated this in Ez 33:4.  Alternate translation: "and the sword comes and takes any one of them"
  * **he is taken in his own iniquity **- Alternate translation: "he will die because of his sin"
