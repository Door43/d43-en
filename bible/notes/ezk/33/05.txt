===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hears sound of the horn and pays no attention** - See how you translated "hear the sound of the horn but do not pay attention" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:33:01|33:4]].
  * **his blood is on him** - "it will be his own fault that he dies"
  * **pays attention** - "listens to the warning"
  * **save his own life** - "will keep himself from dying"
  * **the sword as it comes**  - See how you translated "the sword as it comes on the land" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:33:01|33:3]]. 
  * **so the people are not warned**  - Alternate translation: "he does not warn the people." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **and the sword comes and takes anyone's life**  - This can be translated as "and the enemy comes and kills with a sword any one of the people." See how you translated "the sword comes and kills" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:33:01|33:4]].
  * **that person dies in his own sin** - "that person dies because of his own sin"
  * **will require his blood from the watchman** - "will hold the watchman responsible for his death" or "will consider it the watchman's fault that he died.
