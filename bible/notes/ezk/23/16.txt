===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling the story about his unfaithful wife Oholibah to Ezekiel.
 *
  * **her soul turned away from them**- The word "soul" refers to thoughts and emotions. Changing from wanting something to hating it is described as turning away from it. AT: "her emotions regarding them changed from desire to disgust"
