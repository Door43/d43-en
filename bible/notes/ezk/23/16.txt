===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling the story about his unfaithful wife Oholibah to Ezekiel.
  * **As soon as her eyes saw them** - Oholibah's eyes are used to describe her seeing something. AT: "As soon as she saw them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **to her bed of lust** - Oholibah having sexual relations with her is referred to by referring to her bed. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **her soul turned away from them**- The word "soul" refers to thoughts and emotions. Changing from wanting something to hating it is described as turning away from it. AT: "her emotions regarding them changed from desire to hate"
