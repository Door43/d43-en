===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold **- See: key terms page
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh- **  See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **I **- This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **I will set you into the hand of the ones you hate **  - God will give Israel to its enemies.
  * **Your soul turned away from **- See how this command was translated in 23:17
  * **acquisitions **- These are things that people buy, gain, or save.
  * **You **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **They **- This pronoun refers to the Chaldeans, Babylonians and Assyrians listed in 23:23
