===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold!** - The word "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows."Indeed!"
  * **unclothed and bare.** - Both of these words have the same meaning.  Both are used to emphasize that she will have nothing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **prostitution...shameful behavior...promiscuousness** - These all describe the same actions in different ways.  This is done to emphasize what Oholibah did was wrong. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
