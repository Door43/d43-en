===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then I said**  - Ezekiel is prophesying in the voice of the Lord.
  * **the one - ** The next verse reveals that this is a different woman than the sisters.
  * **worn out**  - "very tired"
  * **penetrated**  - Alternate translation: "entered" or ""pushed through" or "had sex with." Your language may have a word or expression that is clear but more polite and acceptable. (See: Euphemism.)
  * **one who pours out blood...blood on their hands**  - Adultery is seen as an act of violence.
