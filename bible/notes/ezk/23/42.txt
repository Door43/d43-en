===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** the sound of a crowd who were with worries was with you** - The sound of a crowd is used to refer to the crowd of people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **drunkards**  - people who often get drunk. (The meaning of "Sheba" is "drunkard".)
  * **ornate** - decorated or carved to make more beautiful.
