===== Important Terms: =====

  * worthless (worthy)
  * crowns
  * heads

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a crowd who were with worries**  - A large group of people were excited because of their fear.
  * **with you**  - This pronoun refers to the two sisters, Oholah and Oholibah.  (See 23:36 and 23:40)
  * **drunkards**  - People who drink too much alcohol.  This meaning might also be translated with a word for "fools."
  * **brought from the wilderness**  - The men came from outside the local society and were perhaps simple as well as foolish.
