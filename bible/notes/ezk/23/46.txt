===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a company**  - A "company" refers to a group that has a purpose, such as a part of an army, but here it is more general in meaning.
  * I - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **give them over**  - "allow"
  * **them - **  This refers to both the evil sisters and the people of Jerusalem (See UDB.)
  * **to be terrorized and plundered**  - God will alow the company to terrorize and plunder them. (See: Passive)
  * Their - This pronoun refers to Oholah and Oholibah.
