===== Important Terms: =====

  * sanctuary
  * unclean
  * Sabbaths
  * idols
  * behold (see: key terms page)
  * house

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Still they are doing this to me **  - This continues to happen.
  * **then they came to my Sanctuary on the same day to profane it **  - They came into God's sanctuary after making sacrifices to idols. This made the sanctuary unclean.
  * **Thus they have done in the midst of my house **  - This line shows the anger of God.
