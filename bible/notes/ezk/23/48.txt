===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So they will** - "So the company will." The word "they" refers to the "company" of people from [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:23:46|23:47]].
  * **set your shameful behavior against you** - This is an idiom. AT: "cause you to accept responsibility for your shameful behavior" or "will punish you for your shameful behavior" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **bear the guilt of your sins with your idols** - This implies that they will be punished for their sins. AT: "accept responsibility for the guilt of your sins with your idols" or "receive the punishment for sinning by worshiping your idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
