===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they**  - This pronoun refers to Oholibah's “lovers”: the Babylonians, Pekod, Shoa, Koa, and the Assyrians.
  * **your**  - This pronoun refers to Oholibah (a symbol of Jerusalem/Judah).
  * **jewelry**  - This word refers to decorative objects that people wear on their body (necklaces, rings, earrings).
  * **behavior**  - This phrase refers to their "actions."
  * **lift up your eyes**  - alternate translation: “wish for" or "want".
