===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **set large shields, small shields, and helmets against you** - These defensive items are used to refer to different kinds of soldiers in an army.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will set my anger on you** - Anger is referred to as something that can be placed on someone.  This means Oholibah will feel the results of Yahweh being angry with her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **They will cut off your nose and your ears** - This describes the punishment in Babylon for married women who have sexual relations with men that are not their husbands. AT: "They will punish you as an adulterous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **your descendants will be devoured by fire** - Something that is devoured by fire is something that has been burned up and destroyed. AT: "your descendants will be destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
