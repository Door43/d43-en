===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **stationed his army to do hard work against Tyre** - "made his army do very hard work against Tyre." Nebuchadnezzar lay siege to Tyre for thirteen years. He probably made his men build siege mounds and ramps and tried to build a bridge of dirt out to the island of Tyre.
  * **Every head was shaved and every shoulder was bared** - AT: "Every head was bald and every shoulder was rubbed because because each man worked so hard carrying loads of dirt." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
