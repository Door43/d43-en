===== Important Terms: =====

  * Yahweh
  * Egypt
  * Nebuchadnezzar
  * Babylon
  * service (serve)

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **Behold** - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **lift up** - "get" or "win" or "to raise up in victory".
  * **its abundance and plunder its spoils and despoil its booty, for that will be the wages of his army!** - Everything would be taken from Egypt and given the soldiers in Babylon's army.
  * **I have given him the land of Egypt as the wages for its service which they did for me** - God will give the land of Egypt to Babylon for bringing about his will.
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in 5:11.
