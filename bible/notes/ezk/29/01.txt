===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **set your face against Pharaoh, **- "turn firmly toward Pharoah,"
  * **and prophesy against him and against all of Egypt! - **"tell the terrible things that will happen to him and everyone in Egypt!"
  * **Declare and say**, - "Tell the king and his people,"
  * **‘Thus says the Lord Yahweh: Behold! I am against you, - **"The Lord Yahweh says, 'I am your enemy!'"
  * **The great sea creature that lies in the midst of the River, that says to me, “I have made the River my own!” - **"You have made yourself like a great sea monster that lies in the middle of the Nile River. You say to me, 'The Nile Rive is my own; I made it for myself.' ' In the Egyptian religion, a crocodile god from the Nile River was responsibe for the protection of Egypt. (See:Irony)
