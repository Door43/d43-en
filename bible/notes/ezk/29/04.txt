===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For I will set hooks in your jaw - "I**t will be as though I put hooks in your jaws"  This refers to one of the gods of Egypt, a crocodile, and God is telling Pharoh he has made himself like a crocodile.
  * **and the fish of your Nile - **  refers to fish that normally live in the Nile river. The fish may also refer to the people of Egypt that worshipped this false god.
  * **will cleave onto your scales; - **"will stick to you;"
  * **and I will lift you up from the midst of your River along with all the fish of the River that cleave to your scales. - **"and I will pull you out of the river along with the fish." This refers to Pharoah and the people of Egypt.
  * **So I will throw you down into the wilderness,you and all the fish of your river - "**You and the fish will be left on dry land."
  * **You will fall upon the face of the field: - **"You will fall down in the desert"
  * **you will not be gathered nor will you assemble. -**  " and your dead body and the fish will not be moved or buried."
  * **I have given you to the living things of the earth, to the birds of the heavens for food! - **'I' refers to the Lord Yahweh. "I have given you to the wild beasts and birds as food."
