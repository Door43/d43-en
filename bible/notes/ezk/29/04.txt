===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Pharaoh, comparing him with a monster in the water.
  * **hooks** - sharp and bent pieces of metal or wood that people used to catch fish and other animals in the water 
  * **scales** - hard pieces of skin that are on fish, crocodiles, and other animals
