===== Translation Notes: =====

The parable about the cedar continues.

  * **Therefore** - "So"
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:10|3:11]].
  * **Because it was so tall** - "because the cedar was so tall"
  * **he raised the top of his tree above the branches** - This can be translated as: "the cedar tree put his top in the clouds."
  * **and his heart was lifted** - This phrase means that the cedar tree became very proud or arrogant. Alternate translation: "and the cedar became very proud." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **to that height** - "because the it was very tall" or "because it was so tall" (UDB)
  * **I therefore grasped him by the hand of the mightiest ruler** - This can also be translated as "I put him in the hand of the strongest nation." This phrase means that Yahweh gave the strongest nation power over the cedar. Alternate translation: "I gave the strongest nation power over the cedar."
  * **This ruler acted against him** - "This nation did bad things to the cedar"
  * **and drove him away because of his wickedness** - "and sent the cedar away from the cedar's land because the cedar was wicked"
