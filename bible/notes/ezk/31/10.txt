===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **you**  - This refers to God talking to Assyria.
  * **he**  - God is doing the action.
  * **tall in height, and he set his tree top in between the branches**  - This is a reference to the great power of Assyria.
  * **his heart was lifted up**  - "Assyria's heart was proud".
  * **hand of the mighty one of nations**  - Because of Assyria's pride, God used Babylon to destroy the nations.
  * **He did his works upon him**  - "God did God's work upon Pharaoh".
  * **drove him away**  - "cast out".
