===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **shuddering** - "fear".
  * **with the sound of his downfall** - "after hearing of her destruction".
  * **cast him down to Sheol with those who descend into the pit!** - "destroyed the nation".
  * **I comforted all the trees of Eden within the lowest parts of the earth** - "the nations destroyed by Assyria were comforted by the destruction of Assyria".
