===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The princes of the north are there! -**  The exact nations in the north are not defined.
  * **All of them and all the Sidonians who went down with the slain! -**  "They and all the Sidonians who were killed!"
  * **In the shame of the terror of their strength, they lied down - **"They once were strong, but now they lay in their graves in shame."
  * **they lied down uncircumcised with those slain by the sword, - **"They are with other heathen who died in battle"
  * **and they lift up their insult with the ones going down to the pit! - **"and they share the punishment and insult with all those dead beside them."
