===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Assyria is there with all her assembly** – Alternate translation: "The corpses of the people of Syria and their army will also be there." (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Her graves surround her** - This can be translated as "The graves of the people of Assyria are all around the graves of other people of Assyria."
  * **All of them were killed by the sword** This can be translated as "Enemies had killed all of them with swords." See how you translated "who had been killed by the sword" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:31:17|31:17]].
  * **Those whose graves** - This can be translated as "The people of Assyria whose graves."
  * **are set in the recesses of the pit** - This can be translated as "are in the deepest part of the grave."
  * **with all her assembly. Her graves surround** - It is implied that the assembly is already in graves. Alternate translation: "The army that the people of Assyria gathered is in graves surrounding the graves of the people of Assyria." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **all those who were killed, who fell by the sword** - Alternate translation: "enemies injured all the people of that army and killed them with swords." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **brought terror on the land of the living** – This can be translated as "when they were still alive, caused people to be extremely afraid."
