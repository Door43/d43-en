===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **her graves surround her**  - "Assyria is surrounded by dead bodies".
  * **Those whose graves are set in the recesses of the pit, and her assembly is there**  - "Their graves are put in a deep pit and the corpses of an army lie around their graves".
  * **Her graves surround all of them who are slain, the ones who have fallen by the sword**  - "Assyria's graves are surrounded by the others who were killed by the sword".
  * **Who brought terror on the land of the living**  - "who caused terror and fear when they were still living".
