===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pharaoh will see and be comforted concerning all their entrouage who are slain by the sword!** - "Pharaoh, king of Egypt, will see all the other armies who were slain by his enemies and will be comforted!".
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration!** - "God makes this very clear".
  * **I will set his terrors in the land of the living** - "God will make him fear the other nations".
