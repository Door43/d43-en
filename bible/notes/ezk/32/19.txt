===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to Ezekiel. 

  * **Are you really more beautiful than anyone else? Go down and lie down with the uncircumcised.** - This is a question and command that the prophet is to ask the Egyptians. AT: "Go down and lie down with the uncircumcised. Ask them all: 'Are you really more beautiful than anyone else?'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Go down** - It is implied that they must go down to Sheol. AT: "Go down to Sheol." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Are you really more beautiful than anyone else?** This question really makes a statement. AT: "You are not more beautiful than anyone else." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **They** - "Egypt and her people"
  * **who were killed by the sword** - This can be put into active form. AT: "whom enemies killed with swords" or "who died in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **She has been given to the sword** - This can be put in active form. AT: "I have given Egypt to the sword" 
  * **The sword has been drawn!** - This represents the beginning of war. AT: "War has begun" [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the sword** - This represents defeat in battle. AT: "Egypt has been handed over to be defeated in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **her enemies will seize her and her multitudes** - "Her enemies will seize Egypt and her people" 
  * **about Egypt and her allies** - "about the Egyptians and those who join them"
