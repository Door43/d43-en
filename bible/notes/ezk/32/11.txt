===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For thus says the Lord Yahweh** - "The Lord Yahweh says".
  * **The sword of the king of Babylon will come upon you!** - "The king of Babylon will attack you!".
  * **I will cause your entourage to fall by warriors’ swords—all of them terrors of nations** - "the people around you will be killed by the swords of mighty Babylonian soldiers, who are the feared by all the nations".
  * **And they will devastate the excellence of Egypt** - "They will crush the pride of Egypt".
  * **and destroy all of the multitudes!** - "and kill all Egyptians!".
