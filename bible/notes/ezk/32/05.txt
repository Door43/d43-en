===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to speak to Pharaoh and to compare him to a monster that lives in the water.
  * **your worm-filled corpse** – "your rotting body"
  * **Then I will pour your blood over the mountains** – This can be translated as "I will soak the the land that the river usually floods with your blood instead, all the way to the mountains."
  * **And the stream beds will be filled with you blood** - Alternate translation: "and I will fill the streams with your blood." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
