===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.
  * **a land that was full** - "a land that was full of living creatures" or "a land that was full of life"
  * **When I strike all of her inhabitants** - "when I destroy all who live in Egypt"
  * **then they** - Possible translations are 1) "then the Egyptians" or 2) "then people" or "the nations"
  * **know that I am Yahweh** – See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:06|6:7]].

Verse sixteen shows that the song of mourning for Pharaoh is finished and describes who will sing the song.


  * **There will be a lament** - This can be translated as "this is the song of mourning that people will sing" or "this is the sad song about the bad things that will happen to Pharaoh that people will sing" 
  * **For the women of the nations will lament over her** - This can be translated as "and the daughters of people in other nations will sing this song of mourning."
  * **they will lament over Egypt. They will lament over all her entourage** - This can be translated as"the women will sing the song while mourning for Egypt and all the people of Egypt"
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
