===== Translation Notes: =====

These verses are a continuation of Ezekiel's lament to the King of Egypt, meaning the Egypt nation.

  * **Then when I extinguish you** - "When there is nothing left of you" or "When I destroy you" (See: Metaphor).
  * **I will cover the heavens** - Cover here may mean "blot out". "Heavens" refers to the expanse of the sky.
  * **the moon will not shine its light!**  - "the moon will not reflect light!" or "The moon will not show light!" or "The moon will be dark!".
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration** - "I, the Lord God, have said it."
