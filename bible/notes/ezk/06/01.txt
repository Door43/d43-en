===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **set your face against** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:01|4:3]].
  * **the mountains of Israel** - "the mountains in the land of Israel." This refers to the people living in the mountains of Israel. 
  * **Behold!** - AT: "Look!" or "Listen!" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you!"
  * **I am bringing a sword against you** - The word "sword" here refers to war. AT: "I am bringing war against you" or "I will enemy soldiers against you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
