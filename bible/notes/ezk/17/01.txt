===== Translation Notes: =====

Ezekiel continues the narrative of his vision.

  * **Son of man **- This refers to Ezekiel himself.
  * **put forth a riddle and speak a parable **- "give them a puzzle to think about" or "tell this story as an illustration" (UDB)
  * **long pinions full of feathers - ** The word "pinion" means the outer end of the wings. Alternate translation: "fully feathered wingtips for flying."
  * **he set it in a city of merchants**  - "The eagle brought the treetop to a city that was known for its markets."
