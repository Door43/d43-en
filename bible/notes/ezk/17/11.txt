===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me **- Ezekiel heard from God.
  * **Don't you know what these things are saying?**  - This is a rebuke. He is asking, "Why don't you understand?" or "You should understand." (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **Behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **The king of Babylon came to Jerusalem and he took her king and her princes and brought them to him in Babylon**  - Alternate translation: "The king of Babylon came to Jerusalem and took the king and princes of Judah and brought them to Babylon".
  * **her king - **  This refers to the king of Judah in Jerusalem**.  ** The feminine pronoun is used for Jerusalem in the Bible.
  * **her princes**  - This refers to the princes of Judah in Jerusalem.  The feminine pronoun is used for Jerusalem in the Bible.
