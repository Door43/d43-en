===== Important Terms: =====

  * **royal**
  * **seed**
  * **mighty**
  * **oath**
  * **kingdom**
  * **lowly**

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he took -**  "He" refers to the king of Babylon.
  * **royal seed**  - This refers to a descendant of the king.
  * **covenant with him and brought him**  - "Him" refers to the descendant of the king.
  * **mighty ones - **  This could be translated as "strong ones" or "important ones" (UDB) or "leaders."
  * **might stand**  - "might continue to exist" (UDB) or "might last a long time."
