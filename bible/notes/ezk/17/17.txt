===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **war assembly**  - "gathering of soldiers and weapons in preparation for battle"
  * **heap up seige-mounds and build seige-walls**  - These are defensive preparations for war.
  * **heap**  - To put in a pile.
  * **to cut off many lives**  - "and kill many of its people" (UDB)
  * **for he**  - This refers to the King of Judah.
  * **behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
  * **"He gave his hand but did all these things."**  - "Gave his hand" means that he held out his hand (as for a handshake) as part of the process of making a covenant. Alternate translation: "The king of Judah agreed to the covenant, but broke it."
  * **He will not escape **  - The king of Judah will not escape.
