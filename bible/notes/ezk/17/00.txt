====== Ezekiel 17 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Breaking allegiance ==

The king of Judah had vowed to obey Babylon but instead he followed Egypt; so God said he cannot prosper. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:vow]])


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

Judah is compared to a vine that grows one direction and then another. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
