===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - God continues to speak to Ezekiel.

  * **are most rebellious** - "are very rebellious" or "totally rebel against me"
  * **house** - This is a metonym for the family that lives in the house, in this case the Israelites, the descendants of Jacob over many years. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:04|2:5]]. AT: "people group" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
