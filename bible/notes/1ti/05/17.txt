===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul again talks of how elders (overseers) should be treated and then gives Timothy some personal instructions.

  * **Let...be considered worthy** - AT: "All of you believers should think of...as worthy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **double honor** - Possible meanings are 1) "both kinds of honor: respect and payment" or 2) "more respect than others receive."
  * **those who work with the word and in teaching** - "those who preach and teach God's Word"
  * **put a muzzle on an ox** - a muzzle is a sleeve that goes over an animal's snout and mouth to prevent it from eating while it is doing work. 
  * **ox** - a large, strong animal like a cow
  * **treads the grain** - Walking on or drawing a heavy object over the cut grain to separate the grain to be eaten from the stalks. The oxen were allowed to eat some of the grain as they worked.
  * **is worthy of** - "deserves"
