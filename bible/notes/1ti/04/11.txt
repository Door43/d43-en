===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Proclaim and teach these things** - "Command and teach these things" or "Command and teach these things I just mentioned"
  * **Let no one despise your youth** - "Do not let anyone consider you less important because you are young"
  * **attend to the reading** - "continue to read the scriptures in public"
  * **attend to the reading, to the exhortation, and to the teaching** - "continue reading, explaining, and teaching the scriptures to believers"
