===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Women in the same way** - Possible meanings are 1) "women" refers to the wives of deacons or 2) "women" refers to female deacons.
  * **be dignified** - "act properly" or "be worthy of respect"
  * **They should not be slanderers** - "they must not speak evil about other people"
  * **moderate** - "not do anything to excess." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ti:03:01|3:2]].
  * **husbands of one wife** - A man must have only one wife. It is unclear if this excludes men who have been previously widowed, divorced, or never married. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ti:03:01|3:2]].
  * **manage well their children and household** - "properly take care of and lead their children and others who live in their homes"
  * **For those** - "For those deacons" or "For these church leaders"
  * **acquire for themselves** - "receive for themselves" or "gain for themselves"
  * **a good standing** - The implied meaning may be stated explicitly. AT: "a good reputation among other believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **great confidence in the faith that is in Christ Jesus** - Possible meanings are 1) they will trust in Jesus with even more confidence or 2) they will speak confidently to other people about their faith in Jesus.
