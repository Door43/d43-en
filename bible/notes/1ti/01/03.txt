===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I urged you **- "As I pleaded with you," or "As I asked you with authority," or "I told you."
  * **you**  - singular (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou|]])
  * **to wait in Ephesus **- "to remain there in the city of Ephesus," or "to stay put in Ephesus"
  * **while I traveled to Macedonia**  â€‹ - "when I was going to Macedonia" or "when I was leaving for Macedonia"
  * **you would command certain men**  - "you would give orders to some men"
  * **They should not pay attention **- "Nor to give close attention" or "Command them not to pay attention."
  * **myths**  - In this case, the original word refers to fictional stories.
  * **genealogies**  - A genealogy is the written or verbal record of a person's parentage and ancestors. These were very important in Jewish culture as they established one's tribal affiliation within the people of Israel. Matthew 1 and Luke 3 are good biblical examples.
  * **which cause arguments **- "which produce conclusions without purpose and evidence" or "which brings about aimless arguments"
  * **rather than helping the plan of God**  - "rather than advancing the plan of God" or "rather than managing what is from God"
  * **which is by faith**  - "which is received by faith or "which is accomplished by faith"
