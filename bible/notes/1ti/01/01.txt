===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul** - "From Paul" or "I, Paul"
  * **according to the commandment of** - "by the command of" or "by the authority of"
  * **our...our...our** - Paul is talking about himself, Timothy, and probably other people as well. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **God our Savior** - "God who saves us"
  * **Christ Jesus our hope** - "Christ Jesus, who is our hope" or "Christ Jesus in whom we put our confidence"
  * **to Timothy** - "This letter is for Timothy"
  * **true son** - Paul speaks of his close relationship to Timothy as if they were father and son. Timothy was not Paul's actual son, but he gave Paul the same level of honor, obedience, and service that any son should give his father. AT: "who is like a real son to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Grace, mercy, and peace from** - "May grace, mercy, and peace be yours from," or "May you experience kindness, mercy, and peace from"
  * **God the Father** - "God, who is our Father"
  * **Christ Jesus our Lord** - "Christ Jesus, who is our Lord"
