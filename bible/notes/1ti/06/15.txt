===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God will reveal his appearing** - It is implied that God will reveal Jesus. AT: "God will reveal Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **at the right time** - "at the proper time" 
  * **the Blessed One** - "the One worthy of praise." This refers to God the Father.
  * **Only he has immortality** - "Only he has the power to live forever"
  * **dwells in inapproachable light** - "and he dwells in a light so bright that no one can approach him"
