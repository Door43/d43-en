===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because as for us**  - Paul is including his audience in the use of "us". (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive|Inclusive]])
  * **our citizenship is in heaven**  - "our homeland is in heaven" or "our true home is in heaven"
  * **from where we also wait for a savior, the Lord Jesus Christ**  - "and we are waiting for a savior, the Lord Jesus Christ, to return to earth from heaven"
  * **He will transform our lowly bodies**  - "He will change our weak, earthly bodies"
  * **into bodies formed like His glorious body**  - "into bodies like his glorious body"
  * **formed by His power to control all things**  - This can be translated as a new sentence and with an active clause: "He will change our bodies with the same power He uses to control all things." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
