===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul advises the believers to have unity and humility, and reminds them of Christ's example.

  * **that there is encouragement in Christ** - "that you have received encouragement from Christ"
  * **that there is comfort from his love** - "that you have been comforted by his love"
  * **that there is fellowship of the Spirit** - "that you have fellowship with the Spirit"
  * **that there are tender mercies and compassions** - "that you have experienced many of God's acts of tender mercy and compassion"
