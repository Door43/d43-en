===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If there is therefore any…if any…if any…if any**  - These "if" statements do not mean Paul thinks these things may not exist. He is using them here to emphasize that all of these certainly do exist. They may be translated as "since there is…since there is…since there is…since there are"
  * **fellowship of the Spirit**  - "fellowship with the Spirit"
