===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is right for me to feel this way about all of you because I have you in my heart **  . - Paul is telling the Philippians how very much he loves them because they have helped him ever since they believed/trusted/put their faith in Christ (Verse 5).
  * **You all have been my partners in grace**  - The Philippians, along with Paul, have experienced God's love and kindness in <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>hard</font>    times (when he was in prison) and in <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>good</font>    times (when he was defending <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>the Gospel</font>    and telling others about Christ).
  * **how I long after you all**  - This could be stated: "how much I love and miss you all"
  * **you all**  - all of the Philippian believers in Christ
