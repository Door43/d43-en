===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do not be frightened by anything** - This is a command to the Philippian believers. If your language has a plural command form, use it here. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you|Forms of You]])
  * **This is for them a sign of their destruction. But for you it is a sign of your salvation, and this from God** - "Your courage will show them that God will destroy them. It will also show you that God will save you"
  * **For you have the same conflict such as you saw with me, and that you hear is with me now** - "That is why you are suffering in the same way that you saw me suffer, and that you hear I am still suffering"
