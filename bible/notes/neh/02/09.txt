  * **Sanballat the Horonite** - Sanballat is the name of a man, and the Horonites were a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Tobiah the Ammonite servant** - This man was likely a freed slave now serving as an officer in Ammon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **heard this** - "heard that I had arrived"
