===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the River **- AT: "the Euphrates"
  * **Sanballat the Horonite**  - Sanballat was a Samaritan leader in the area of Iran.
  * **Tobiah the Ammonite**
 - Tobiah was a descendent of the Ammonites who were enemies of the Israelites.
  * **they were greatly displeased**  - AT: "they were very upset"
  * **someone had come who was seeking to help the people of Israel**  - AT "a man had come to help the children of Israel."
