===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Sanballat, Tobiah and Geshem"**  - (see verses 2:9 and 10} Geshem is part of their group opposing Nehemiah and the people as they start to rebuild the Walls and Gates.
  * **We are his servants and we will arise and build"**  - AT: "We his servants will begin rebuilding."
  * **"But you have no share, no right and no historic claim in Jerusalem"**  - AT: "But you have no share or ancient right in Jerusalem." This is a type of Hebrew constuction in which the first and second word in this triplet retain their normal meaning but the third word is descriptive.
