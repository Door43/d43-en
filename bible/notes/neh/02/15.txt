===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So we went up** - The "we" can also be "I." AT: "So I went up" understanding that the other men with Nehemiah also followed him.
  * **The rulers did not know** - The rulers were the chief administrators or leaders.
  * **the rest who did the work** - AT: "the others who would later do the work of rebuilding the walls"
