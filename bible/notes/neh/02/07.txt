===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **give me letters**  - these letters would show that Nehemiah had the permission of the king to do what he was doing. Consider clarifying this when translating. AT: "Give me letters of permission"
  * **governors **- these were Persian government adminstrators. Persia controlled all the territory from Persia to Egypt, that included Judah.
  * **the River**  - refers to the Euphrates river, which is a large river that marked the boundary leading to the west from the perspective of the Persians.
  * **the fortress next to the temple**  - AT: "the fortress belonging to the temple"
  * **the good hand of God was on me **- the hand of God is used to refer to God's guidance and favor. AT: "God's favor was upon me." (See: Metonymy).
