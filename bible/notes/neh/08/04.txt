===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Mattithiah, Shema, Anaiah, Uriah, Hilkiah, and Maaseiah ... Pedaiah, Mishael, Malchijah, Hashum, Hashbaddanah, Zechariah, and Meshullam** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **when he opened it all the people stood up** - The text is very clear that the opening of the book was the cue to stand.
