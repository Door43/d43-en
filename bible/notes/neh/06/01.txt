===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sanballat, Tobiah, and Geshem**  - These are all male names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **rebuilt the wall and that**  - "rebuilt the wall so that"
  * **no longer any sections left broken open**  - "no more breaks in the city wall"
  * **Ono**  - This is the name of a valley. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
