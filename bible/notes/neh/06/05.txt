===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **a fifth message**  - meaning a message distinct in some way(s) from the previous group 4
    * **an open letter**  - it was an unsealed diplomatic communication, an insult to the recipient
    * **in his hand**  - in the hand of the courier, an insult to the recipient that made it plain that the contents were public because the courier was free to read it.
