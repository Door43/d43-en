===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **So the wall was finished** - This passive clause can be translated as an active clause. AT: "We finished the wall" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the twenty-fifth day of the month Elul** - "Elul" is the sixth month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-fifth day is near the middle of September on western calendars. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **fifty-two days** - "52 days" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeday]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]))
  * **they fell greatly in their own esteem** - "they thought much less of themselves" or "they lost confidence."
