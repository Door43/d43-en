===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the nobles of Judah sent many letters to Tobiah, and Tobiah's letters came to them.**  - meaning, there was constant communication between the wealthy of Judah and the enemy of the Jews, Tobiah.
    * **There were many in Judah who were bound by an oath to him**  - many in Judah who were in an alliance with Tobiah
    * **he was the son-in-law of Shecaniah** - This means that Tobiah was married to the daughter of Shecaniah
    * **Shecaniah the son of Arah, Jehohanan, Meshullam the son of Berechiah** - these are the names of males (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **the son of Arah** - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **the son of Berechiah** - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **They also spoke to me about his good deeds and reported my words back to him.**  - "The Jewish nobles told me about Tobiah's good deeds and then told him about my responses."
    * **Letters were sent to me from Tobiah to frighten me**  - This passive clause can be translated as an active clause. AT: "Tobiah sent letters to me to scare me" 
    * **to frighten me**  - "to scare me."The continuing goal: subjugate Nehemiah using fear
