===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **and I cannot**  - "and I am unable", "and I am not able"
    * **Why should I let the work come to a stop while I leave it and come down to you?"**  - This is a figure of speech. It is a statement posed as a question that does not expect (and does not get) an answer. (see: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **They sent me four times the same message, **  - "Four times they sent me the same message," also see UDB
    * **they, them**  - meaning Sanballat, Tobiah, and Geshem
    * **bold words**  - explanation
