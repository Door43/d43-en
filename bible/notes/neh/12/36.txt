===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There also were** - "Along with them were"
  * **Zechariah ... Shemaiah, Azarel, Milalai, Gilalai, Maai, Nethanel, Judah, Hanani ... David ... Ezra** - These are the names of males. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ezra the scribe was in front of them** - "Ezra the scribe was leading them"
  * **Fountain Gate ... Water Gate** - These are the names of openings in the wall.
