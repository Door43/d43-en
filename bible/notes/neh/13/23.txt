===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** These verses introduce the action that follows.

  * **Jews that had married women of Ashdod, Ammon, and Moab** - "Jews that had married foreign women" God had forbidden intermarriage.
  * **Ashdod** - city name (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ammon ... Moab** - names of nations (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Half of their children** - "As a result, half of their children"
