===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Palal...repaired** - The implied information here is that the wall was being repaired. AT: "Palal...fixed the wall" (See Implicit/Explicit)
    * **Palal**  - This is the name of a man (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **the son of Uzai**  - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **buttress**  - See how you wrote this in 3:19
    * **the tower that extends upward**  - "the tower that rises up"
    * **upper house of the king**  - "higher palace of the leader of Israel"
    * **the court of the guard**  - This is the place where the guards stayed
    * **Pedaiah...repaired** - See above note for implied information. AT: "Pedaiah...fixed the wall"
    * **Pedaiah** - This is the name of a man
    * **the son of Parosh** - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **Ophel** - This is the name of an area or region
    * **Water Gate** - This is the name of an entranceway in the wall
    * **projecting tower** - "tall tower" (UDB)
    * **the Tekoites** - This is the name of people who came from the town of Tekoa
    * **Ophel** - See previous note
