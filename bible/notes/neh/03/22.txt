===== translationNotes: =====

  * **around Jerusalem, repaired ... Benjamin and Hasshub repaired ... Azariah repaired ... Henadad repaired** - These phrases refer to repairing the wall. AT: "around Jerusalem, repaired the wall ... Hasshub repaired the wall ... Azariah repaired the wall ... Henadad repaired the wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Benjamin ... Hasshub ... Ananiah ... Maaseiah ... Ananiah ... Binnui ... Henadad ... Azariah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **opposite their own house** - "in front of their own house" 
  * **After them ... After him** - "Next to them ... Next to him"
