===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Malchijah ... Harim ... Hasshub ... Pahath-Moab ... Shallum ... Hallohesh** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **repaired another section ... repaired, along with his daughters** - These phrase refer to repairing the wall. AT: "repaired another section of the wall ...  repaired the wall, along with his daughters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Shallum son of Hallohesh, the ruler** - Shallum was the ruler, not Hallohesh. 
  * **ruler** - "chief administrator" or "leader"
  * **half the district** - "Half" means one part out of two equal parts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
