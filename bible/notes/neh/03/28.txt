===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The priests repaired** - The implied information here is that the wall was being repaired. AT: "The priests repaired the wall" (UDB) (See: Implicit/Explicit)
    * **Horse Gate**  - This is the name of an entranceway in the wall. (See: How to Translate Names)
    * **Zadok the son of Immer**  - This is the name of a man
    * **the son of Immer**  - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **Shemaiah the son of Shecaniah, the keeper of the East Gate** - Shemaiah was the keeper of the East Gate, not Shecaniah
    * **Shemaiah...repaired** - See above note for implied information. AT: "Shemaiah repaired the wall"
    * **Shemaiah the son of Shecaniah**  - This is the name of a man
    * **the son of Shecaniah**  - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **the keeper of the East Gate** - "the person who looked after the East Gate" or "the person who opened and closed the East Gate"
    * **East Gate**  - This is the name of an entranceway in the wall
    * **Hananiah the son of Shelemiah** - This is the name of a man
    * **the son of Shelemiah** - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **Hanun** - This is the name of a man
    * **the sixth son of Zalaph** - This means that Hanun was the number six son born to Zalaph, that is, Zalaph already had five sons before Hanun was born.
    * **Meshullam...repaired** - See above note for implied information. AT: "Meshullam repaired the wall"
    * **Meshullam the son of Berechiah** - This is the name of a man
    * **the son of Berechiah** - See how you wrote something similar in 3:18
    * **his living chambers** - "the rooms where Meshullam stayed"
