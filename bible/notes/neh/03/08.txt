===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Uzziel ... Harhaiah ... Hananiah ... Rephaiah ... Hur ... Jedaiah ... Harumaph ... Hattush ... Hashabneiah** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **goldsmiths** - A goldsmith is a person who makes gold jewelry and other gold objects.
  * **goldsmiths, repaired ... Hur repaired ... Harumaph repaired ... Hashabneiah repaired** - These phrases refer to repairing the wall. AT: "goldsmiths, repaired the wall ... Hur repaired the wall ... Harumaph repaired the wall ... Hashabneiah repaired the wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **next to him was Hananiah, a maker of perfumes** - Hananiah repaired the wall as well. AT: "next to him Hananiah, a maker of perfumes, repaired the wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **perfumes** - liquid substances that people put on their body in small amounts to smell pleasant
  * **ruler** - "chief administrator" or "leader"
  * **half the district** - "Half" means one part out of two equal parts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
