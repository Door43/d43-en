===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Baruch...Zabbai...Eliashib...Meremoth...Uriah...Hakkoz**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **buttress** - "supporting corner" or "turning of the wall." The corner of a building where a supporting structure is.
