===== Translation Notes: =====

In these verses, Nehemiah continues to list the names of the people who signed the sealed document. 

    * **Bunni, Azgad, Bebai, Adonijah, Bigvai, Adin, Ater, Hezekiah, Azzur, Hodiah, Hashum, Bezai, Hariph, Anathoth, Nobai, Magpiash, Meshullam, Hezir, Meshezabel, Zadok, Jaddua** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
