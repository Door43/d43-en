===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - In these verses, the Levites continue to praise Yahweh in the presence of the people of Israel. 

  * **Because of all this** - because the people had disobeyed and Yahweh had punished them
  * **On the sealed document are the names** - The reader should understand that the men wrote their names on the document before it was sealed.
