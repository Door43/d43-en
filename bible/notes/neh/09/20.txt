===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - In these verses, the Levites continue to praise Yahweh in the presence of the people of Israel.

  * **Your good Spirit ... your manna ... water** - The writer changes the usual word order to emphasize the good things Yahweh gave his people. Your language may have another way of emphasizing these items.
  * **instruct** - "teach"
  * **your manna you did not withhold from their mouths** - This litotes can be expressed positively. AT: "you generously gave them manna" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **from their mouths** - The mouth is a synecdoche for the whole person. AT: "from them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
