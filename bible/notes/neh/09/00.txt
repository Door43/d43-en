====== Nehemiah 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter and the next one form a single section.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Prayer to God ==

The people prayed and thanked God for his care for them and the blessings he gave to them. They also confessed their sin of disobeying him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]], [[:en:obe:kt:confess]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])

== Learning from their ancestor's mistakes ==
This chapter teaches that the Jews learned from the mistakes of their ancestors. They became determined to worship Yahweh alone, to not intermarry with other peoples, and to worship Yahweh as the law of Moses instructed them. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses]])

== Recalling the great power of God ==
It was common to recall the great things God did for Israel. This is a reminder to Israel of God's power. It is intended to bring the people to repentance and proper worship of Yahweh. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:repent]])
