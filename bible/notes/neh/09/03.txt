===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They stood up** - All the Israelites stood up
  * **they were confessing** - "they were admitting the wrong things they had done"
  * **bowing down before** - "worshiping" or "praising"
  * **The Levites, Jeshua, Bani ... stood on the stairs** - Some versions translate, "Jeshua, Bani ... stood on the stairs built for the Levites"
  * **Jeshua, Bani, Kadmiel, Shebaniah, Bunni, Sherebiah, Bani, and Kenani** - men's names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
