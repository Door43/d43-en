===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - In these verses, Nehemiah continues to list the provincial officers who lived in Jerusalem. 

  * **Maaseiah ... Baruch ... Kol-Hozeh ... Hazaiah ... Adaiah ... Joiarib ... Zechariah ... Perez** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shilonite** - This refers to a person who comes from the town of Shiloh.
  * **All the sons of Perez who lived in Jerusalem were 468** - "Perez had 468 descendants who lived in Jerusalem"
  * **They were outstanding men** - "They were courageous men" or "They were valiant men"
