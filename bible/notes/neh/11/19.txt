===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gatekeepers** - people assigned to each gate, responsible to control access to the city or temple, as well as to open and close the gates at times and for reasons set by the administrator. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:01|7:1]].
  * **Akkub ... Talmon ... Ziha ... Gishpa** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ophel** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
