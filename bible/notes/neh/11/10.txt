===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jedaiah...Joiarib...Jachin...Seraiah...Hilkiah...Meshullam...Zadok...Meraioth...Ahitub...Adaiah...Jeroham...Pelaliah...Amzi...Zechariah...Pashhur...Malchijah** - These are the names of men.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **ruler** - "official" or "overseer"
  * **who did the work of the clan** - "who did the work of the temple"
  * **their associates** - "their brothers" or "their kinsmen"
  * **822 men** - See how you translated numbers in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:11|07:11-14]].
