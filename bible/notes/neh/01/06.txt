===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **listen**  - AT: "give your attention to," or "please consider," or "give ear to".
  * **open your eyes **- Nehemiah is speaking with God as if he has human attributes. AT: "see and respond," or "look at what is happening". (See: Personification)
  * **so you may hear **- AT: "with the result that you hear," or "so that you grant my request".
  * **your servant**  - This was how a person would address their superior. Refering to yourself in this way showed humility and respect.
  * **day and night**  - AT: "without stopping," or "all of the time". (See: Idiom).
  * **confessing**  - AT: "pleading guilty," or "admitting guilt for".
  * **We have acted very wickedly**  - AT: "we have lived very sinful lives."
