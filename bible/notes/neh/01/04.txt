===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then I said** - Nehemiah tells what he prayed. AT: "Then I said to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **who love him and keep his commandments** - Since Nehemiah is speaking to Yahweh, the pronouns "him" and "his" can be translated as "you" and "your." AT: "who love you and keep your commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
