===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jeshua, Kadmiel, Hodevah, Asaph, Shallum, Ater, Tlamon, Akkub, Hatita, Shobai **- These are all individuals.
  * **singers**  - AT: "musicians"
  * **gatekeepers**  - These were individuals that guarded the gate. If your language can translate "gatekeepers" then use that otherwise use a term that signifies those that guard the gates of a village or city.
