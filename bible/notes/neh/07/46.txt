===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the names of people whose descendants returned from the exile.

  * **Ziha ... Hasupha ... Tabbaoth ... Keros ... Sia ... Padon ... Lebana, ... Hagaba ... Shalmai ... Hanan ... Giddel ... Gahar** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]).
  * **Sia** - This is the same man known by the name Siaha in Ezra 2:44.
