===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gatekeepers** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:01|7:1]].
  * **singers** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:01|7:1]].
  * **some of the people** - The implied information is that this refers to some of the Israelites who were not priests or other temple workers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **and all Israel** - Possible meanings are: 1) all the groups of Israelites that are listed in this verse or 2) the rest of the Israelites who did not work in the temple.
  * **the seventh month** - "month 7." This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of September and the first part of October on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]])
  * **were settled in their cities** - "lived in their own cities"
