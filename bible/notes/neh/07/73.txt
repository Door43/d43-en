===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gatekeepers** - See how you translated this in [[en/bible/notes/neh/07/01|7:1]]
  * **the seventh month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. This is during the early rain season which would soften the land for sowing. It is during the last part of September and the first part of October on western calendars. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
