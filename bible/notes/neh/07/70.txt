===== Translation Notes: =====

(See how you translated numbers in [[en:bible:notes:neh:07:11|07:11-14]].)

  * **the heads of ancestors' families** - AT "heads of the clans" or "heads of the families"
  * **darics of gold** - A "daric" was a small gold coin that people in the Persian Empire used. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
  * **basins** - "large bowls"
  * **garments** - "items of clothing"
  * **minas of silver** - A mina is 600 grams. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalweight]])
