===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the heads of ancestors' families** - "the chief patriarchs" or "the leaders of the clans" 
  * **gave to the treasury** - "put into the treasury"
  * **one thousand darics** - "1,000 darics"
  * **darics of gold** - A daric was a small gold coin that people in the Persian Empire used. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **basins** - large bowls
  * **garments** - items of clothing
  * **twenty thousand darics** - "20,000 darics"
  * **minas of silver** - A mina is about one half of a kilogram in weight. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **silver** - a shiny, gray precious metal used to make coins, jewelry, containers, and ornaments
  * **two thousand minas** - "2,000 minas"
  * **sixty-seven priestly garments** - "67 priestly garments"
