===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Nehemiah is continuing to recount the number of people who returned from exile. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **went up** - This is an idiom that refers to traveling toward Jerusalem, which was on higher ground than the surrounding area. AT: "returned" or "came back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Tel Melah ... Tel Harsha ... Kerub ... Addon ... Immer** - These are names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Delaiah ... Tobiah ... Nekoda ... Habaiah ... Hakkoz ... Barzillai** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
