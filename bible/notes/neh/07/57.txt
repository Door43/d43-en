===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Nehemiah is continuing to recount the number of people who returned from exile.

  * **Solomon ... Sotai ... Sophereth ... Perida ... Jaala ... Darkon ... Giddel ... Shephatiah ... Hattil ... Pochereth Hazzebaim ... Amon.** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sophereth** - This is the name of a man who is called Hassophereth in Ez. 2:55.
  * **Perida** - This is the name of a man who is also called Peruda in Ez. 2:55.
