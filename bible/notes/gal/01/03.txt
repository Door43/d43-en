===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for our sins**  - AT: "to take the punishment we deserved because of our sins"
  * **that he might deliver us from this present evil age **- AT: "that he might set us free from the evil powers at work in the world today"
  * **our God and Father** - This refers to "God our Father." He is our God and our Father.
