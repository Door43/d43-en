===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul gives his reason for writing this letterâ€”he reminds them to continue to understand the gospel.

  * **I am amazed that you are turning so quickly** - Another possible meaning is "I am amazed at how quickly you are changing"
  * **amazed that** - "amazed to see that"
  * **you are turning...to a different gospel** - Possible meanings are 1) "you are changing your mind...and believing a different gospel" or 2) "you are no longer loyal to the gospel we told you and have...become loyal to a different gospel."
  * **him who called you** - "God, who called you"
  * **called** - Here this means God has appointed or chosen people to be his children, to serve him and proclaim his message of salvation through Jesus.
  * **by the grace of Christ** - "because of Christ's grace" or "because of Christ's gracious sacrifice"
  * **men** - "people"
