===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **carefully observe**  - "keep" or "celebrate." Paul is speaking of feasts and fasts.
  * **labored over you** - "worked hard to teach you about Jesus." This word is never used for childbirth in the New Testament.
  * **in vain**  - "with no effect" or "to no purpose"
  * **you**  - plural
