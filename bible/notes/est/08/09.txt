===== translationNotes: =====

  * **called** - "summoned"
  * **the third month, which is the month of Sivan, on the twenty-third day of the month** - "Sivan" is the name of the third month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-third day is near the middle of June on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **A decree was written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They wrote a decree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **provinces** - A "province" is a large area into which some countries are divided for the purposes of government. See how you translated this in in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]].
  * **written in their own writing** - "written in their own script." There are different writing systems around the world.
