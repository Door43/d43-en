===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Harbona** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **A gallows fifty cubits tall** - You may convert "fifty cubits" to a modern measure. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:est:05:14|5:14]]. AT: "A gallows twenty-three meters high" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]]) 
  * **Then the king's rage died down** - The king's rage is a spoken of as if it were a large fire that became smaller. AT: "Then the king's rage lessened" or "Then the king was not so angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
