===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **let royal robes be brought** - "let royal robes be brought by servants" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **ridden** - This is the past tense of "ride".
    * **royal crest** - A "royal crest" is a special symbol that represents the king's family.
    * **Then let the robes and the horse be handed over** - "Hand over the robes and the horse" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **Let them** - "Them" refers to the servants and the noble official.
