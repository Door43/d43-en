===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Haman** - Use the same name as used in [[en:bible:notes:est:03:01|Esther 3:1]]
    * **outer courtyard** - "first courtyard from the outside"
    * **hanging Mordecai** - "putting Mordecai to death"
    * **gallows** - Use the same word or phrase as used in [[en:bible:notes:est:02:22|Esther 2:23]].
    * **set up** - "built"
    * **said in his heart** - This is a metaphor for something that he thought that he truly desired to be the truth.  AT: "thought to himself" or "said to himself" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **"Whom would the king take pleasure in honoring more than me?"** - "Surely the king wants to honor me!"  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
