===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Haman** - Translate as in [[en:bible:notes:est:03:01|Esther 3:1]]
    * **outer courtyard** - "first courtyard from the outside"
    * **hanging Mordecai** - "putting Mordecai to death by hanging"
    * **gallows** - Translate as in [[en:bible:notes:est:02:22|Esther 2:23]].
    * **set up** - "built"
    * **said in his heart** - AT: "thought to himself" or "said to himself" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **"Whom would the king take pleasure in honoring more than me?"** - "Surely there is no one whom the king would take greater pleasure in honoring more than me!"  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
