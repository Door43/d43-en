===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Abihail** - "Abihail" was Esther's father and Mordecai's uncle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **Hegai** - Use the same name used in [[:en:bible:notes:est:02:03|Esther 2:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the tenth month, which is the month of Tebeth** - "Tebeth" is the name of the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part December and the first part January on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **seventh year** - AT: "year number 7" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
