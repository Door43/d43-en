====== Esther 02:15-16 ======

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Abihail** - "Abihail" was Esther's father and Mordecai's uncle.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
    * **Hegai** - Use the same name used in [[en:bible:notes:est:02:03|Esther 2:3]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **tenth month** - "month number 10" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **Tebeth** - "Tebeth" was the Babylonian name for the month December-January.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **seventh year** - "year number 7" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
