===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verses 12-14 are background information about the customs for the women who became the king's wives. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **complying with the regulations for the women** - "acting in accordance with the requirements for the women"
  * **beauty treatments** - Things done to make the girls look more beautiful and smell good.
  * **cosmetics** - Use the same word or phrase used in [[:en:bible:notes:est:02:03|2:3]].
  * **whatever she desired was given to her** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "she could take whatever she desired" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **palace** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:05|1:5]].
