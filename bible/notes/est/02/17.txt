===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The king loved** - This is the romantic use of the word "love."
  * **received acceptance and favor before him** - These idioms mean the same thing and emphasize how much the Esther pleased the king. AT: "greatly pleased him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **set the royal crown on her head** - The king did this to show that he was making her his queen. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **"Esther's feast,"** - It may be helpful to state that this is the name of the feast. AT: "he called it, "Esther's feast," (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **taxation** - "the collection of taxes"
  * **provinces** - A "province" is a large area into which some countries are divided for the purposes of government. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]].
  * **royal generosity** - "generosity that only a king can give"
