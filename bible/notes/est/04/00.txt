====== Esther 04 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Mordecai warns Esther to act ==

Mordecai tells Esther she must beg the king for the Jews life even if she risks death. 


=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== Implicit information ==

There is implicit information that translators may not understand. "Who knows whether you have come to this royal position for such a time as this?"  Meaning maybe God made you the queen so you could save the Jews. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
