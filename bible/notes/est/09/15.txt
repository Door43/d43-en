===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the fourteenth day of the month of Adar** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:est:03:12|3:13]].
  * **laid no hands on the plunder** - Laying their hands on things represents taking them. AT: "took none of the plunder" or "did not take any of the plunder" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seventy-five thousand** - "75,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **they did not lay their hands on the valuables of those they killed** - Laying their hands on things represents taking them. AT: "they did not take any of the valuables of the people they killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **valuables** - "valuable things" or "possessions"
