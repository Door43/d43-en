===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the days of Ahasuerus** - "In the time of Ahasuerus" or "When Ahasuerus was ruling as king"
  * **this is Ahasuerus who reigned from India as far as Ethiopia, over 127 provinces** - This is background information to help the reader identify Ahasuerus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **127** - "one hundred and twenty-seven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **provinces** - A "province" is a large area into which some countries are divided for the purposes of government.
  * **sat on his royal throne** - Here "royal throne" may refer to his rule over the kingdom. AT: "ruled the empire" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **fortress** - This refers to a castle, stronghold or fortified city.
  * **Susa** - This was a royal city of Persian kings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
