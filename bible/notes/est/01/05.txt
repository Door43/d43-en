He invited to the feast all the men who worked in the palace, including both those who did important work and those who did less important work. This celebration lasted for seven days. ===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When these days were completed** - "At the end of that feast"
  * **a feast lasting seven days** - This was a second feast that was only for the officials in Susa. AT: "another feast that lasted seven days"
  * **seven** - "7" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **fortress** - This refers to a castle, stronghold or fortified city.See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]]. 
  * **Susa** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **a mosaic pavement** - A "mosaic" consists of colored stones arranged in an attractive pattern.
  * **porphyry** - This is a kind of red and purple rock that contained pieces of crystal.
