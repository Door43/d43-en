===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Drinks were served in golden cups** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The guests drank wine from gold cups" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **there was much royal wine that came because of the king's generosity** - "the king was very generous with the royal wine"
  * **generosity** - "great willingness to give"
  * **There must be no compulsion** - "No one must be forced to drink"
  * **The king had given orders to all the staff of his palace to do for them whatever each guest desired** - This statement means that the king told his workers to give all the guests as much wine as they wanted.
