===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the first month (which is the month of Nisan)** - "Nisan" is the name of the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the twelfth year** - "in year number 12" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **threw Pur** - "cast lots" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the twelfth month (the month of Adar)** - "Adar" is the name of the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of February and the first part of March on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
