  * **Haman son of Hammedatha the Agagite** - This is the name and description of Haman, one of the king's officials.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **placed his seat of authority** - "promoted him"
  * **prostrated themselves to Haman** - "humbled themselves and lay flat on the ground before Haman"
