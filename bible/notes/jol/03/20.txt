===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to speak about the day of the Lord.

    * **Judah will live forever... Jerusalem will live from generation to generation** - The victory for people of Israel is compared to the enemies who were destroyed in [[en:bible:notes:jol:03:18|3:19]]. AT: "Jerusalem will remain and never be destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **I will avenge their blood that I have not yet avenged**  - AT: "I will punish the enemies who killed the people of Israel and still haven't been punished"
