===== Translation Notes: =====
God continues to speak of future events.

  * **in those days, and at that time**  - These two phrases are both pointing to a future event (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
  * **my people, and my inheritance Israel**  - These two phrases emphasize how Yahweh views Israel as his personal and precious people (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
