===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - God speaks to the people of the nations that surround Israel.

  * **why are you angry at me** - God uses this question to scold the people of Tyre, Sidon and Philistia. AT: "You have no right to be angry at me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **Will you repay me?** - "Will you get revenge on me?" God uses this question to make the people think about what they are doing. AT: "You think you can get revenge on me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]). 
  * **I will immediately return your retribution on your own head** - Here the word "head" refers to the person. The revenge that they wanted to exact towards Yahweh, he will make happen to them. AT: "I will cause you to suffer the revenge that you tried to put on me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **retribution** - "revenge" or "payback"
