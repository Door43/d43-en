===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now, are you angry at me, …Will you repay me?**  - There are rhetorical questions. Yahweh is challenging the people of Tyre, Sidon, and Philitia to answer for themselves if they feel they have a case.
  * **Retribution**  - This term means to receive punishment.
  * **You**  - This pronoun refers to Judah and Jerusalem.
  * **Me, I**  - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
