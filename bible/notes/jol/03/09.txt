===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Rouse the mighty men**  - "wake up the mighty men" 
    * **plowshares**  - a plow is a farm equipment that is used to dig and turn over soil; the plowshare is the part of the plow that digs into the soil.
    * **pruning knives**  - these are small knives that are used to cut off small branches of trees and bushes.  They are often very sharp. 
    * **Beat your plowshares into swords ... your knives into spears**  - God's people are being told to turn whatever tools they have into fighting weapons, and prepare for war against their enemies.
