===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a tumult, a tumult** - A tumult is noise caused by a large crowd. This is repeated to show that it will be very noisy from all the people.
  * **valley of Judgment...valley of Judgment** - This phrase is repeated to show that the judgment will certainly happen.
