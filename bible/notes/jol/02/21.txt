===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Do not fear, land,** - "Do not be afraid, you people of the land," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **the wilderness will sprout** - plants good for food will grow on the land
  * **autumn rain and the spring rain** - the first rains of the rainy season in early December and the last rains in April and May
