===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fast**  - Sometimes people fast in order to show that they are very sad or grieved about something.
  * **the people**  - This refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **Consecrate the assembly… Assemble the elders… Gather the children and the nursing infants… Let the bridegrooms come out their rooms… the brides from out of their bridal chambers**  - This emphasizes the importance of the entire nation be sorry before Yahweh. Every kind of person should prepare, regardless of their status in life. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
