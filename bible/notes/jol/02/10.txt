===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The earth shakes in front of them, The heavens tremble, The sun and the moon are darkened, And the stars stop shining**  - There are so many locust that the earth and sky shakes, and the all the objects in the sky cannot be seen. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
  * **Yahweh raises his voice...for his warriors are very numerousâ€¦for they are strong** - The phrase "raises his voice" is a showing of power and command to gain control of a large and powerful army. (See: Anthropomorphism)
  * **great and very terrible** - In this phrase both descriptions mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Who can survive it?**  - AT: "No one will be strong enough to survive the Yahweh's judgement." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
