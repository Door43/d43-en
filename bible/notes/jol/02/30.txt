===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking of things he will do in the future.

  * **blood, fire and pillars of smoke** - "blood" is symbolizing the death of people. AT: "death, fire and pillars of smoke" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and the moon into blood** - Here the word "blood" refers to the color red. You can supply the verb for this phrase. AT: "and the moon will turn red like blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **great and terrible day** - Here the word "great" modifies the word "terrible." AT: "greatly terrifying day." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
