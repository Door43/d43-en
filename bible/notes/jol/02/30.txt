===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues the speech he began in [[:en:bible:notes:jol:02:24|2:25]], promising good things for the people of Israel.

  * **blood, fire, and pillars of smoke** - "blood" is symbolizing the death of people. AT: "death, fire and pillars of smoke" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The sun will turn into darkness** - "The sun will no longer give light" 
  * **the moon into blood** - Here the word "blood" refers to the color red. You can supply the verb for this phrase. AT: "the moon will turn red like blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
