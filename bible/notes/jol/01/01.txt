===== Translation Notes: =====
God speaking through Joel to the people of Israel.
  * **Pethuel** - This is the name of Joel's father. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * ** this... it** - The proclamation that the prophet is making. 
  * **Has this ever before happened in your days Or in the days of your ancestors** - The prophet is saying something is going to happen that has never happened before. Alternate translation: "This has never happened before either to you or your ancestors" (See:[[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **Tell your children about it, And let your children tell their children, And their children the next generation.** - These phrases are all used to say this story should be told to every generation of children and never forgotten by the people of Israel. Alternate translation: "Tell this to all your people forever."(See:[[en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
