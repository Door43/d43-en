===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your** - This pronoun refers to the nation of Israel.
  * **Hear this... And listen** - The words "hear" and "listen" mean basically the same thing and are used for emphasis. Alternate translation: Do not just hear these words without meaning, but understand what they mean. (See:[[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **you elders... all you inhabitants of the land** - These are referring to the nation of Israel. Alternate translation: The leaders and every person in the nation of Israel.  (See:[[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * ** this... it** - The proclamation that the prophet is making. 
  * **Has this ever before happened in your days
Or in the days of your ancestors** - The prophet is warning the people of how bad what is coming will be. Alternate translation: "In all of history nothing this bad has ever happened to the nation of Israel." (See:[[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **Tell your children about it,
And let your children tell their children,
And their children the next generation.** - This phrase use a structure like a ladder to show that what the prophets says should be passed on with each coming generation. Alternate translation: "Tell this to all your people forever."(See:[[en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
