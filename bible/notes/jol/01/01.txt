===== Translation Notes: =====

God speaks through Joel to the people of Israel using poetry. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Pethuel** - Joel's father (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Has this ever before happened in your days Or in the days of your ancestors?** - AT: "This has never happened before either to you or your ancestors" (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
