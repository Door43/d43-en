===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their masters** - "their own masters"
  * **in all things** - "in every situation" or "always"
  * **please them** - "make their masters happy" or "satisfy their masters"
  * **pilfer** - steal small things from their master
  * **show all good faith** - "demonstrate they are worthy of their masters' trust"
  * **in every way** - "in everything they do"
  * **adorn our teaching** - Paul speaks of Christian teaching as if it were an object that could be decorated. AT: "make our teaching attractive to other people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **God our savior** - "our God who saves us"
