===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **their masters** - "their own masters"
  * **in everything** - "in every situation" or "always"
  * **please them** - AT: "make their masters happy" or "satisfy their masters"
  * **show all good faith** - AT: "be faithful to their masters in everything" or "demonstrate they are worthy of their masters' trust"
  * **God our Savior** - "our God who saves us"
  * **attractive** - This means drawing people to it, as when a gift is decorated to make it desirable.
  * **in every way** - "in everything they do"
