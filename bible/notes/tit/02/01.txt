===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But you **- That is, "But you Titus in contrast with the false teachers."
  * **say what agrees **- That is, "keep speaking what fits," or "teach what agrees."
  * **with healthy doctrine **- That is, "with sound doctrine," or 'with correct teachings."
  * **Older men **- That is, "men of old age," or "elderly men," or "teach older men."
  * **must be temperate**  - That is, "to be temperate," or "to be sober minder," or "to be free from any habit of excess." Could be translated as "must be self-controlled" or "must rule themselves"
  * **dignified**  - That is, "to be dignified,"to be worthy of respect," or "to be of proper behavior," or "to be well behaved."
  * **sensible**  - That is, "to be self controlled," or "to be in control of one's desire." Could be translated as "must be prudent," "must be wise" or "must be reasonable."
  * **sound in faith **- That is, "to be well grounded in the faith," or "to be correct in their beliefs." To be "sound" means to be "healthy;" so then "sound in faith" means that one's faith is healthy.
  * **in love **- That is, "sound in love," or "well grounded in love," or "to be loving." That is, one's love is healthy.
  * **in perseverance**  - That is, "sound in perseverance," or "well grounded in perseverance," or "to be persevering." Could be translated as "must be steadfast," "must be constant" or "must be untiring." That is, one's endurance is healthy.
