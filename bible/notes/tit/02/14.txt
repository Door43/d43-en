===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gave himself for us**  - "gave himself to die for us"
  * **to set us free from lawlessness**  - "to liberate us from our sinful condition." This is a metaphor that compares being released from sin's control of a the freedom of a slave being purchased by someone. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **make pure**  - "cleanse from all impurity"
  * **a treasured people**  - This could be translated as "a group of people that God treasures."
  * **are eager**  - "have a strong desire
  * **bold words**  - explanation
