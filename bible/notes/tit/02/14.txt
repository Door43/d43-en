===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gave himself for us**  - "gave himself to die for us"
  * **to set us free from lawlessness**  - "to release us from our sinful condition." This is a metaphor that compares being released from sin's control to the freedom of a slave being purchased by someone. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **make pure**  - "cleanse"
  * **a treasured people**  - AT: "a group of people that he treasures."
  * **are eager**  - "have a strong desire"
