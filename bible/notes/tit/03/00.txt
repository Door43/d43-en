====== Titus 03 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter appears to shift the letter's focus to give Timothy personal instructions.

[[:en:bible:notes:tit:03:12|3:12-15]] forms a formal conclusion to this letter. This is a common type of conclusion for a letter in the ancient Near East.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Genealogies ==

This is probably a reference to some group's speculation over the identification of the Messiah. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:christ]])
