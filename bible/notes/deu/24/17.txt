===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **You must not force away the justice that is due the foreigner or the fatherless**  - AT: "You must make sure that you treat foreigners and orphans justly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **take the widow’s cloak as a pledge**  - AT: "take as a pledge the clothing a widow needs to stay warm" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **call to mind** - AT: "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
