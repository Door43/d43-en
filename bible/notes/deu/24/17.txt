===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **You must not force away the justice that is due the foreigner or the fatherless**  - AT: "You must make sure that you treat foreigners and orphans justly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
    * **fatherless** - children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them
    * **nor take the widow's cloak as a pledge** - A lender would take something from the borrower to ensure that she would paid him back. He was not allowed to take her cloak because she needed it to stay warm.
    * **call to mind** - AT: "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
