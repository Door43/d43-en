===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **you must not to back to get it** - AT: "You must not walk back to the field in order to recover the forgotten sheaf."
    * **it must be for the foreigner** - "It" refers to the forgotten sheaf.
    * **all the work of your hands**  - That is, all the work that you do with your hands. (See: Metonymy)
    * **when you shake your olive trees** - That is, when you shake its branches, causing the olives to fall to the ground to be picked up.
    * **you must not go over the branches again**  - That is, do not pick every single olive from the tree.
    * **it will be for the foreigner**  - "It" refers to the remaining olives.
