===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **What is left over will be for the foreigner, for the fatherless, and for the widow** - "The grapes that you do not pick will be for the foreigner, the fatherless, and the widow to pick"
  * **for the foreigner, for the fatherless, and for the widow** - These refer to groups of people. AT: "for foreigners, for those who are fatherless, and for the widows" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **call to mind** - This is an idiom. AT: "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
