===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **it will be for the foreigner** - "the grapes that you do not pick will be for the foreigner"
  * **call to mind** - This is an idiom. AT: "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
