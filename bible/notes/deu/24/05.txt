===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **when a man takes a new wife**  - AT: "when a man is newly married to a woman"
    * **neither may he be commanded to go on any forced duty** - AT: "and the government may not command him to live away from his home in order to do any kind of work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **he will be free to be at home** - "he will be free to live at home"
