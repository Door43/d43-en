===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **kidnapping**  - taking someone away, using physical force
    * **any of his brothers of the people of Israel**  - "any of his fellow Israelites"
    * **that thief must die**  - AT: "then other Israelites should kill that thief as a punishment for what he did"
    * **In this manner you must eradicate the evil from among you**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:deu:21:20|21:21]].
