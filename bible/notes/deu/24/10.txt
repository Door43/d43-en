===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **When you make your neighbor any kind of loan**  - AT: "When you loan something to your neighbor"
    * **to fetch his pledge**  - "to take his pledge"
    * **You will stand outside**  - AT: "You should wait outside his house"
