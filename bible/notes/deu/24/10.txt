===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **When you make your neighbor any kind of loan** - "When you loan something to your neighbor"
  * **to fetch his pledge** - "to take his pledge"
  * **You will stand outside** - "You should wait outside his house"
