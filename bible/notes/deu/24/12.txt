===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **you must not sleep with his pledge in your possession** - "you must not keep his coat overnight"
  * **You must surely restore to him the pledge** - "You must return his coat"
  * **so that he may sleep in his cloak and bless you** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "so that he will have his coat to keep himself warm when he sleeps, and he will be grateful to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **cloak** - a coat or other clothing that keeps a person warm at night
  * **it will be righteousness for you before Yahweh your God** - "Yahweh your God will approve of the way you handled this matter"
