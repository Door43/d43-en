===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what the previous generation of Israelites did.

  * **Yahweh!—who went before you...** - Moses reminds them of all the ways Yahweh had gone ahead of Israel in all their travels and that they should have continued to trust him in battle.
