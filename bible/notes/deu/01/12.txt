===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **But how can I myself alone carry your loads, your burdens, and your disputes?** - Moses uses a question to emphasize that he cannot solve all of their problems by himself. This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "I cannot carry your loads, your burdens and your disputes by myself." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **your loads, your burdens** - This means their problems and complaints are like a heavy load. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your disputes** - "your arguments" or "your disagreements"
  * **and men of good repute, tribe by tribe** - "and respected men from every tribe of Israel"
