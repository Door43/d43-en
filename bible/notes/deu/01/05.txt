===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Beyond the Jordan**  - "Beyond the Jordan River"
  * **spoke to us**  - The word "us" refers to Moses and the other people of Israel.
  * **You have lived long enough in this hill country**  - This phrase means "You do not need to stay at this mountain any longer." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
