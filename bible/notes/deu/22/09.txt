===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so that the whole harvest is not confiscated by the sanctuary** - Possible ways to translate are: 1) "so that the priests in Yahweh's sanctuary do not take away the whole harvest" 2) "so that the whole harvest is not defiled so that you are not allowed to use it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **and the yield of the vineyard**  - "and the fruit that grows in the vineyard"
  * **wool**  - soft, curly hair that grows on a sheep 
  * **linen**  - thread made from a flax plant
