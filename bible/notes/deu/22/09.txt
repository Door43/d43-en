===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **two kinds of seeds -**  it also means diverse or exclusive nature, note that "any crop in the area" (UDB) translates differently than "two kinds" (ULB)
  * **whole harvest is not confiscated** - AT: "whole harvest is not defiled" or "whole harvest is not naturally corrupted"
