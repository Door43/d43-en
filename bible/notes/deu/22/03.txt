===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you must do the same…you must do the same…you must do the same **- this structure appears three times, which means 'likewise shall you do…' It gives readers a sense of repetiton and urgency. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **or his ox fallen down in the road **- Possible meanings of the action are 1) fallen down, 2) collapsed to near death or 3) lying on the road
