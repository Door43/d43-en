===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **You must do the same with his donkey**  - AT: "You must return his donkey in the same way"
  * **you must do the same with his garment**  - AT: "you must return his clothing in the same way"
  * **you may not hide yourself**  - AT: "you may not go away without doing anything"
  * **you must surely help him to lift it up again**  - AT: "you must help your fellow Israelite lift the animal back up onto its feet"
