===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **at that time** - Moses is referring to an event that happened about 40 years earlier.
  * **out of the house of slavery** - AT: "out of the place where you were slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
