===== translationNotes: =====

  * **within your city gates** - Here the metonym "gates" refers to the gates at the entrance of a city. AT: "within your towns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **fatherless** - These are children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them.
  * **widow** - This means a woman whose husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age.
  * **call to mind** - "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
