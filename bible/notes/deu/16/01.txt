===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **month of Abib** - This is the name of the first month in the Jewish calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]])
  * **keep the Passover** - "celebrate the Passover" or “eat the Passover meal”
  * **sacrifice the Passover** - "offer the Passover sacrifice"
