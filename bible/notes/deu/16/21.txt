===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must not** - Here "You" refers to all of the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **any sacred stone pillar, which Yahweh your God hates** - The phrase "which Yahweh you God hate" is giving more information about the sacred stone pillar. AT: "any sacred stone pillar because Yahweh you God hates them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
  * **sacred stone pillar** - This refers to sacred pillars for worshiping false gods.
