===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **When you march out to battle against your enemies** - "When you go out to fight in a war against your enemies"
  * **see horses, chariots** - People considered an army with many horses and chariots to be very strong. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he who brought you up out of the land of Egypt** - Yahweh brought the people from Egypt to Canaan. It was common to use the word "up" to refer to traveling from Egypt to Canaan. AT: "Yahweh who led you out from the land of Egypt"
