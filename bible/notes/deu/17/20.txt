===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so that his heart is not lifted up above his brothers** - AT: "so that he does not become arrogant" or "so that he does not think he is better than his fellow Israelites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **so that he does not turn away from the commandments, to the right hand or to the left** - This compares a king disobeying the commandments to a person who walks off the correct path. AT: "so that he does not disobey any of the commandments" or "so that he obeys all of the commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **prolong his days** - AT: "lengthen his time"
