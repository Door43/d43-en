===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **At the mouth of two witnesses, or three witnesses,  will he who must die be put to death** - Here "mouth" refers to a testimony. AT: "If 2 or 3 witnesses speak against the person, then you must execute him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **The hand of the witnesses must be the first to put him to death and afterward the hand of all the people** - Here "hand" refers to the entire person. AT: "The witnesses themselves must be the first ones to throw stones. Then all the people will execute the person by throwing stones at him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **and you will remove the evil from among you** - "you must remove from among the Israelites this person who does this evil thing" or "you must execute this evil person"
