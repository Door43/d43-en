===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **At the mouth of two witnesses, or three witnesses, will he who must die be put to death** - AT: "If two or three witnesses speak against the person, then you must put him to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **The hand of the witnesses must be the first to put him to death and afterward the hand of all the people** - AT: "The witnesses should be the first ones who throw stones. Then all the people will throw stones and put the person to death"
