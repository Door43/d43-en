===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will not do like all the things that we do here today**  - "You will not do as we are doing here today" This means that they would worship in the promissed land different than the way they were doing today. They would have to follow the instructions of how Yahweh wanted them to worship.
  * **every man whatever is right in his own eyes**
 - “every man doing what seems best to him” (See: Idiom )
  * **come to the rest**  - "come to the place of rest"
  * **to the inheritance**  - This refers to the land Yahweh would give them as a gift.
