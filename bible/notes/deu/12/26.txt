===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **offerings for your vows** - "offerings to fulfill vows" or "vow offerings"
  * **the blood of your sacrifices will be poured out** - AT: "the priest will pour out the blood of the sacrifice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you will eat the flesh** - "you will eat the meat." God's law specifies what parts of the animal are for the burnt offering, what parts are for the priest and what parts are for the offerer to eat. AT: "you will eat some of the meat." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
