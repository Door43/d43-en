===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **enlarges your borders** - AT: "enlarges your territory" or "increases the size of your land"
  * **I will eat flesh** - AT: "I will eat the meat of an animal"
  * **as your soul desires**  - AT: "as you want" or "as you crave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
