===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **rejoice before Yahweh** - "rejoice in the presence of Yahweh"
  * **the Levites who are within your gate** - Here "gate" refers to the towns or cities where the Levites lived. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **because he has no portion or inheritance among you** - The tribe of Levi did not receive a portion of land as their inheritance. Their share of the inheritance was the honor of serving Yahweh as his priests.
