===== translationNotes: =====

  * **cuts off the nations from before you** - "destroys the nations before you." Here "nations" refers to the people already living in Canaan.
  * **when you go in to dispossess them, and you dispossess them** - "when you take everything from them"
  * **pay attention to yourself** - "be careful"
  * **that you are not trapped into following them ... trapped into investigating their gods, into asking** - Here those who worship the gods of other people groups are compared to wild animals being caught in a trap, unable to escape. This statement can be translated in active form. AT: "that no one causes you to act like them ... causes you to ask about their gods, saying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **after they are destroyed from before you** - "after Yahweh destroys them before you"
