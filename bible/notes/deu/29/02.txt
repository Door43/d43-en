===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have seen everything that Yahweh did before your eyes** - AT: "You have seen all that Yahweh did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **the great sufferings that your eyes saw** - AT: "the great sufferings that you saw"
  * **the signs, and those great wonders** - AT: "and all the works of God's power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **Yahweh has not given you a heart to know, eyes to see, or ears to hear** - The people do have hearts, eyes, and ears. This means God has not let them understand what all of these things really mean.
  * **a heart to know** - AT: "a mind to know" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
