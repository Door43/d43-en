===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You have seen everything that Yahweh did before your eyes** - Here "eyes" means the whole person. AT: "You have seen all that Yahweh did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the great sufferings that your eyes saw** - Here "eyes" means the whole person. AT: "you saw God cause the people to suffer terribly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **your eyes** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the word "your" here is singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **the signs, and those great wonders** - The words "signs" and "wonders" both refer to the plagues that Yahweh had sent upon Egypt. AT: "and all of the powerful things that Yahweh did." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Yahweh has not given you a heart to know, eyes to see, or ears to hear** - The people do have hearts, eyes, and ears. This means God has not let them understand what all of these things really mean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a heart to know** - This is an idiom. AT: "a mind to know" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
