===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sihon, the king of Heshbon, and Og, the king of Bashan** - See how you translated these names in [[en:bible:notes:deu:01:03|1:4]].
  * **came out against us** - Here "us" refers to Moses and the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **keep the words of this covenant and do them** - Here "keep" and "do" mean basically the same thing. AT: "obey all the words of this covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
