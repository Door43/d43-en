===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues blessing each tribe of Israel.

    * **Dan is a lion cub that leaps out from Bashan** - This means the people of Dan are strong, and they attack the enemies of the Israelites who live in Bashan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
