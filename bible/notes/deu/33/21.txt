===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues blessing the tribe of Gad.

    * **the leader’s portion** - This means a larger piece of land which a leader normally took.
    * **He came with the heads of the people** - AT: "They met with all the leaders of the Israelites"
    * **He carried out the justice of Yahweh and his decrees with Israel** - AT: "They obeyed all that Yahweh commanded the Israelites"
