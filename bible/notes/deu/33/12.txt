===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues the blessings to the individual tribes of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

    * **The one loved by Yahweh lives** - AT: "The ones Yahweh loves live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **he lives between Yahweh’s arms**  - Hebrew: "He shall dwell between his shoulders (mountain slopes)." This may mean Yahweh carries him as a child on his shoulders, or that he leans on Yahweh, or it may mean "he lives between his borders" or "he lives between his mountain slopes."
