Moses continues his blessings to the tribe of Levi.

    * **He teaches Jacob your decrees, Israel your law**  - This is the same thought stated in two ways. AT: "The Levites teach your law to all the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
