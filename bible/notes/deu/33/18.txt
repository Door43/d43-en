===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Rejoice, Zebulun, in your going out,and you, Issachar, in your tents.**  - Zebulun went out to war and also traveled by sea, since their land was near the sea. Issachar preferred peaceful living and working the land and raising cattle. They may have been mentioned together because their lands were next to each other.
    * **sacrifices of righteousness**  - acceptable or proper sacrifices
