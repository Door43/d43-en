===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to bless the tribes of Joseph.

  * **him who was in the bush** - This refers to Yahweh who first appeared to Moses in a burning bush. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **come on the head of Joseph, and on the forehead of the head of him** - Here "head" and "forehead" refer to the whole person and are a reference to Joseph's descendants. AT: "be upon the descendants of Joseph" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
