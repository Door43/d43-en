===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues blessing the tribe of Levi.

    * **Shatter the loins of those** - The loins were considered the center of strength. AT: "Destroy those" or "Strike those" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
