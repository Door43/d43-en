===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The firstborn of an ox**  - This refers to Ephraim, Joseph's younger son. Jacob (Joseph's father) had prophesied that Ephraim's descendants would be more numerous than Manasseh's.  
    * **firstborn**  - This is sometimes used figuratively to indicate something excels above others or should be given special honor.   
    * **ox**  - The ox was considered a symbol of strength and power.
