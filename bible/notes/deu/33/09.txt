===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues blessing the tribe of Levi.

  * **The man who said…kept your covenant**  - Moses is explaining that the tribe of Levi was more dedicated to Yahweh's law than to their own family.
  * **For he guarded your word**  - AT: "for they obeyed your commands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
