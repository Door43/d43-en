===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues his blessings to the individual tribes of Israel.

    * **The man who said...kept your covenant** - Moses is explaining that the tribe of Levi were more dedicated to Yahweh's law than they were to even their own family.
    * **For he guarded your word** - AT: "for they observed your commands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
