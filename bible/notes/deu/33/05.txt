===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to bless the tribes of Israel, which he began to do in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:33:01|33:2]]. The blessings are short poems. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **General Information:** - Verse 5 is difficult to understand.

  * **there was a king** - "Yahweh became king" (UDB)
  * **Jeshurun** - This is another name for Israel. Translated as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:32:15|32:15]].
  * **Let Reuben live** - This begins Moses' blessings to the individual tribes of Israel.
  * **but may his men be few** - Other possible meanings are 1) "and may his men not be few" or 2) "even though his men are few."
