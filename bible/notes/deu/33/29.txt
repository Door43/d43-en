===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Who is like you, a people saved by Yahweh,the shield of your help,
and the sword of your majesty?**  - This is a rhetorical question with the expected answer "No one." (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) AT: "No one is like you, a people saved by Yahweh, the shield of your help,
and the sword of your majesty?"
    * **the shield of your help,
and the sword of your majesty**  - Yahweh protects like a shield and defends like a sword. (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
