===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **for a thousand generations** - "for 1,000 generations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **repays those who hate him to their face** - This is an idiom. It means God will repay them quickly and openly so that they know God has punished them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate==== : ====
figs_idiom]])
  * **he will not be lenient on whoever hates him** - This means that Yahweh will severely punish everyone who hates him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **I command you** - Moses is speaking to the people of Israel.
