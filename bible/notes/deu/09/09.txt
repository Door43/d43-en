===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses is reminding the people of Israel what happened in the past.

  * **the tablets of stone, the tablets of the covenant that Yahweh made with you** - Here the second phrase clarifies that "the tablets of stone" are the ones on which God wrote the Ten Commandments. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **forty days and forty nights** - "40 days and 40 nights" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **on them was written everything just like all the words that Yahweh announced to you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh wrote on them the same words he had said to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
