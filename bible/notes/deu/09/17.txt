===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.

  * **I broke them before your eyes** - Here "your eyes" means the people of Israel. AT: "I broke them right in front of you" or "I broke them where you could see them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **lay facedown before Yahweh** - This is a sign of Moses humbling himself before God.
  * **lay facedown** - "lay with my face on the ground"
  * **forty days and forty nights** - "40 days and 40 nights" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
