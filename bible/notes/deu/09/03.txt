===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the Israelites as if they are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **today** - "now"
  * **like a devouring fire** - Yahweh is powerful and able to destroy the armies of the other nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **subdue them before you** - "put them under your control"
