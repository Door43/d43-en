===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the Israelites as if they are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **Do not say in your heart** - Here "in your heart" means "in your thoughts." AT: "Do not think to yourselves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **has thrust them** - "has driven the other peoples out"
