===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.

    * **I took your sin** - Here "your sin" refers to the golden calf. AT: "I took your sinful idol" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
