===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.

  * **I took ... burned ... beat ... ground ... threw** - Moses probably commanded other men to do the actual work. AT: "I had people take ... burn ... beat ... grind ... throw"
  * **your sin** - the golden calf. AT: "the idol you sinned by making" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
