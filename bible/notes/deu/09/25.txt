===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.

  * **I lay facedown before Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:deu:09:17|9:18]].
  * **forty days and forty nights** - "40 days and 40 nights" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **that you have rescued through your greatness** - God revealed His greatness to Egypt and Israel by leading Israel out of Egypt.
  * **which you have brought out of Egypt with a mighty hand** - God revealed his mighty power. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
