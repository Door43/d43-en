===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.

  * **I lay facedown before Yahweh** - "lay with my face on the ground." Translate this as it is in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:09:17|9:18]].
  * **forty days and forty nights** - "40 days and 40 nights" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **you have rescued** - ""
  * **through your greatness** - Yahweh showed how great he was to the Egyptians and the Israelites by leading the Israelites out of Egypt.
  * **with a mighty hand** - Here "a mighty hand" refers to Yahweh's power. AT: "with your mighty power" Similar words also appear in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:04:34|4:34]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
