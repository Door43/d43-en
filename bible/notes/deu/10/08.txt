===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer continues to tell where the Israelites had traveled. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]]) 
 
  * **in his name** - "with the authority of Yahweh" or "as a representative of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **as today** - "as they are doing today"
  * **no portion nor inheritance of land** - The tribe of Levi did not receive a portion of the promised land when they arrived there. The full meaning of this statement may be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Yahweh is his inheritance** - "Yahweh is their possession" or "their share of the inheritance is the honor of being Yahweh's priests"
  * **spoke to him** - "spoke to the tribe of Levi"
