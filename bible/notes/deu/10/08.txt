===== Translation Notes: =====
This continues the background information. 
 
  * **to bless people in his name, as today** - AT: "to bless people with the authority of Yahweh, as they are doing today"
  * **no portion nor inheritance of land** - The tribe of Levi did not receive a portion of the promised land when they arrived there.
  * **spoke to him** - "spoke to the tribe of Levi" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
