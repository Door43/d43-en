===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **circumcise the foreskin of your heart** - The word "foreskin" refers to the fold of skin on a man's private parts that is removed during circumcision. Here Moses is referring to a spiritual circumcision. This means the people must remove the sin from their lives. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **God of gods** - AT: "the supreme God" or "the only true God"
  * **Lord of lords** - AT: "the supreme Lord" or "the greatest Lord"
  * **the fearsome one** - AT: "the one who causes people to fear"
