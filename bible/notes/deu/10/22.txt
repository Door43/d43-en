===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **went down into Egypt** - AT: "traveled south to Egypt" or "went to Egypt"
  * **seventy persons** - "70 persons" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **as many as the stars of the heavens** - This emphasizes the large number of Israelites that were with Moses. AT: “beyond what you can count” (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
