===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.

  * **At that time** - "After I finished praying"
  * **like the first** - This "first" is the  ordinal number for one. It is referring to the first set of tablets that Moses had broken. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **on the mountain** - Here "the mountain" is referring to Mount Sinai. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **chest of wood** - "wooden box"
