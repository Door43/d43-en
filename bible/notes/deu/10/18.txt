===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **He executes justice for the fatherless** - AT: "Yahweh is just to the fatherless"
  * **fatherless** - children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them
  * **widow** - A true widow is a woman whose husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age.
