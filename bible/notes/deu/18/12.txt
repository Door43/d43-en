===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **driving them out**  - The pronoum "them" refers to the nations or people groups that were inhabiting the land at the moment, but would lose it, when the people of Israel arrived.
  * **dispossess**  - "displace"
  * **but as for you**  - still addressing the people of Israel.
