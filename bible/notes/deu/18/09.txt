===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When you have come** - Here "come" can be translated as "gone" or "entered." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **you must not learn to observe the abominations of those nations** - God hates the religious activities of the people in the surrounding nations. He considers them to be terribly evil.
  * **There must not be found among you any one who** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "None of you must" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **puts his son or his daughter into the fire** - "sacrifice his children by fire on an altar" 
  * **augury** - the use of magic to predict the future
  * **any enchanter, or any sorcerer, any charmer** - These are different people who practice magic. God has forbidden every kind of magic.
