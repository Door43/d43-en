====== Deuteronomy 18 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Levites ==
As Yahweh's chosen priests, the priest were to trust in Yahweh to provide for them. He provided for them through the rest of the Israelites. Yahweh was their inheritance. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:elect]], [[:en:obe:kt:priest]], [[:en:obe:kt:trust]] and [[:en:obe:kt:inherit]])

== Messiah ==
This chapter is one of the first prophecies about the promised, coming messiah. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]], [[:en:obe:kt:promise]] and [[:en:obe:kt:christ]])
