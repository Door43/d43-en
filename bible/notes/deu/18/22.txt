===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **a prophet speaks in the name of Yahweh** - Here "name of Yahweh" refers to Yahweh himself and his authority. AT: "a prophet claims to speak for me" or "a prophet claims to speak with my authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the prophet has spoken it arrogantly** - "the prophet has spoken this message without my authority"
