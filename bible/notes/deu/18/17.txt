===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **said to me**  - Moses, the author of the book, is refering to himself.
  * **from among their brothers**  - the prophet would be an Israelite, from the people of Israel.
  * **like you**  - means, like Moses.
  * **put my words in his mouth - ** this expression emphasizes that the prophet would speak from God.
  * **speak to them**  - the prophet would speak to the Israelites that would be alive during that future time.
  * **in my name**  - Yahweh is refering to himself.
  * **require it of him**  - "I will hold him accountable."
