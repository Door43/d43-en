===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking.
  * **who speaks a word arrogantly** - "who dares to speak" or "who presumes to speak"
  * **in my name** - AT: "for me" or "with my authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **If say in your heart** - AT: "If you ask yourself"
  * **How will we recognize a message that Yahweh has not spoken?** - "How will we know if the message that the prophet speaks is from Yahweh?" Here "we" refers to the people of Israel.
