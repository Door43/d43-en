===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **arrogantly**  - "
corrupted with pride"


  * **in my name**  - in the name of Yahweh
  * **say in your heart**  - this expression refers to talking to yourself. AT: "if you ask yourself" "See: Metaphor)
  * **we recognize**  - it refers to the people of Israel
  * **Yahweh has not spoken**  - This expression is not literal as they would not hear the voice of Yahweh, but it refers to Yahweh speaking through a human person.
