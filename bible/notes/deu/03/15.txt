===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **I gave** - Here "I" refers to Moses.
  * **to Machir** - This is the son of Manasseh. He had died before Moses gave this land. AT: "to the descendants of Machir" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the valley of the Arnon** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:02:24|2:24]].
