===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all his people were put under our control** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh put all of Og's people under our control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **not one of his people remained** - This is a litotes that is used to emphasize that the Israelites did not let anyone live. AT: "all of his people were dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **there was not one city that we did not take** - This is a double negative that emphasizes that they took all of the 60 cities. AT: "we took every one of the cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **sixty cities** - "60 cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the region of Argob** - This is the name of a region within Bashan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
