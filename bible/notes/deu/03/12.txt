Moses continues reminding the people what happened in the past.

    * **Aroer** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:deu:02:36|2:36]].
    * **the valley of the Arnon** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:deu:02:24|2:24]].
    * **the region of Argob** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:deu:03:03|3:4]].

If your language has a way of showing that what follows is background information, you should use it here. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
