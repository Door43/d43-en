===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that parts the hoof** - "that have split hooves." This is a hoof that is split into two parts instead of being one whole. 
  * **chews the cud** - This means the animal brings its food up from its stomach and chews it again. 
  * **rabbit** - This is a small animal with long ears that usually lives in holes in the ground. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **rock badger** - This is a small animal that lives in rocky places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **they are unclean for you** - Something Yahweh says is unfit for his people to eat is spoken of as if it were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
