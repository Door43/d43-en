===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **chews the cud **- This means to regurgitate what was eaten in haste to chew it again, it is known as a ruminant animal.
  * **rabbit**  - a small animal with long ears that usually lives in holes in the ground
  * **rock badger**  - a small animal that lives in rocky places (See: Translate Unknowns)
  * **they are unclean for you **- This means that these foods are not acceptable to Yahweh. See the list of unclean animals in Leviticus 11.
