===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must not eat any disgusting thing**  - This means that the people of Israel were not supposed to eat unclean things (See: List of Important Terms "unclean") See how you translated the name of unclean animals in Leviticus 11. AT: "You must not eat any abominable thing"
  * **These are the animals that you may eat**  - Yahweh identified in the law the animals that were acceptable for food. See Leviticus 11.
