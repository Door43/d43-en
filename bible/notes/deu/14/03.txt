===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must not eat any disgusting thing** - This means that the people of Israel were not supposed to eat anything that God said was ritually unclean.
  * **the deer, the gazelle** - The deer is the same as "the hart." See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:deu:12:15|12:15]].
