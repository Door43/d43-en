===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **at the end of every three years **- See 26:12, it refers to "at the end of three years", the year of the tithing for the poor. It is exacted only in each third year for charity purpose (See: Translate Numbers)
  * **store it up within your gates **- store it in your towns (UDB)
  * **because he has no portion nor inheritance with you **- about landless Levites, see how you translated this in 14:27.
