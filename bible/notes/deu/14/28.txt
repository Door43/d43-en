===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **every three years you will present all the tithe of your produce** - Once every 3 years the Jews were to give their tithe to the town so that it would be used to provide for the Levites, orphans, widows and foreigners.
  * **fatherless** - children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them
  * **widow** - a woman whose husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age
