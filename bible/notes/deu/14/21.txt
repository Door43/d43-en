===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **anything that dies of itself** - This means an animal that dies a natural death. 
  * **the foreigner who is within your gate** - "the foreigner who is in your town" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
