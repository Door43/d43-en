===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **anything that dies of itself** - This means an animal that dies a natural death.
  * **the foreigner who is within your gate** - Here "gate" represents the entire city or town. AT: "the foreigner who is in your town" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **For you are a nation that is set apart to Yahweh your God** - Yahweh choosing the people of Israel to belong to him in a special way is spoken of as if Yahweh set them apart from all other nations. This can be stated in active form. AT: "For Yahweh your God has set you apart from other nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
