===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **Of these things that are in water you may eat** - "You may eat these kinds of animals that live in the water"
  * **fins**  - the thin, flat part which the fish uses to move through the water
  * **scales**  - the small plates that cover the body of the fish
