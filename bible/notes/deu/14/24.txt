===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **carry it** - Here "it" refers to the tithe of crops and herds the people were to bring to Yahweh's sanctuary.
  * **you will convert the offering into money** - "you will sell your offering for money"
  * ** tie up the money in your hand, and go** - "put the money in a bag and take it with you"
