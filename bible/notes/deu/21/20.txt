===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **They must say**  - "The father and mother must say"
  * **This son of ours**  - "Our son"
  * **he will not obey our voice**  - AT: "he will not obey us." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **In this manner**  - "In this way"
  * **you must eradicate the evil**  - AT: "you must get rid of the evil" or "you must destroy the evil"
  * **All Israel**  - AT: "All the people of Israel" 
  * **will hear of it and fear**  - AT: "will hear about what happened to the son and they will be afraid of that happening to them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
