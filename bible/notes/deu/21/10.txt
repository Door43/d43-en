===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **you have a desire to her**  - AT: "you love her"
  * **wish to take her for yourself as a wife**  - AT: "want to take her to be your wife"
  * **she will shave her head**  - "she will shave the hair off of her head"
  * **cut her nails**  - "cut her fingernails"
