===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **must come near** - "must come to the valley"
  * **for them Yahweh your God has chosen to serve him** - "because Yahweh your God has chosen the priests to serve him"
  * **Yahweh your God** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the word "your" is singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **to bless the people** - "to bless the people of Israel"
  * **in the name of Yahweh** - Here the metonym "in the name of" refers to Yahweh and his authority. AT: "as a representative of Yahweh" or "with the authority of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **listen to their advice** - "listen to what the priests say"
  * **for their word will be the verdict** - Here the metonym "their word" refers to what the priests say. AT: "because whatever the priests say, that will be the decision" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in every dispute and case of assault** - "every time people are in the court of law because they disagree with one another or somebody hurts another person"
