===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the priests and the elders.
  * **your foreigner who is within your city gates** - AT: "your foreigners who live in your cites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **keep all the words of this Law**  - AT: "carefully obey all the commands in this law"
