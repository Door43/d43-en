===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he did to Sihon and to Og, the kings of the Amorites** - See how you translated these names in [[en:bible:notes:deu:01:03|1:4]]. AT: "he did to Sihon and Og, the kings of the Amorites, and to their armies." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Be strong and of good courage** - "Be strong and courageous"
  * **fear not, and do not be afraid of them**  - Moses repeats this for emphasis. AT: "and do not fear them at all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **he will not fail you nor forsake you** - AT: "he will always fulfill his promise to you and will always be with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
