===== translationNotes: =====

  * **lay up these words of mine in your heart and soul** - "be very careful to remember what I am saying to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **bind them as a sign on your hand, and let them be as frontlets between your eyes** - See how you translated similar phrases in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:06:08|6:8]].
  * **when you sit in your house, when you walk on the road, when you lie down, and when you get up** - The people of Israel were to discuss God's commandments and teach them to their children anytime and anywhere. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
