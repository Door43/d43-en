===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what the previous generation of Israelites did.

    * **Then we turned** - This happens about 40 years after the events in Deuteronomy 1.
    * **we went around Mount Seir for many days** - Possible meanings are 1) the Israelites journeyed around the mountain called Seir for a very long time or 2) Mount Seir refers to a section or region of land that the Israelites wandered in for a very long time.
    * **Mount Seir** - This is an area south of the Dead Sea.
