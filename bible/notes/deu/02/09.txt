===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of a part of Moses' speech reminding the Israelites of how Yahweh had guided them in the wilderness. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Do not trouble Moab** - The word "Moab" is a synecdoche for the people of Moab. AT: "Do not trouble the people of Moab" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Ar** - This is the name of a city in Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the descendants of Lot** - The people of Israel were related to the descendants of Moab. Moab was the son of Lot. Lot was the nephew of Abraham. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
