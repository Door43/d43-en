===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still talking to the people of Israel.

  * **You will think about in your heart** - The heart is a symbol of the thoughts and understanding of a person. AT: "You will understand" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **corrects** - "disciplines"
