===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

  * **You must - **  The word "You" refers to the entire assembly of the people. (See: Forms of 'You')
  * **call to mind**  - "remember"
  * **all the way - **"the whole way" or "all the distance"
  * **in order that**  - "so"
  * **to know**  - "to reveal"
  * **what was in your heart**  - "your intentions" or "what you were intending"
  * **as to**  - "regarding" or "about"
