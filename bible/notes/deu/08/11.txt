===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses begins stating a warning to not forget Yahweh.

  * **do not neglect his commandments** - AT: "do not stop obeying his commandments" or "continue to obey his commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **Otherwise** - AT: "If you are not careful"
  * **you will eat and be full** - AT: "you will have enough food to eat"
