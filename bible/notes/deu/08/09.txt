===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

  * **eat bread without lack** - "have enough food"
  * **where you will not go without anything** - This is a negative phrase to make the positive meaning stronger. AT: "where you will have everything you need." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **You will eat and be full** - "you will have enough food so you will not be hungry" or "you will have enough food to eat until you are full"
  * **you will bless** - "you will praise" or "you will give thanks to"
