===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **against your enemies**  - "to fight against your enemies"
  * **keep yourselves from every evil thing**  - "keep yourselves away from all bad things"
  * **any man who is unclean because of what happened to him at night **  - AT: "any man who is unclean because he had an emission of semen while he was asleep" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
