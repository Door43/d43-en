===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **a slave who has escaped from his master** - AT: "a slave from another country who escaped from his master and came to Israel." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] )
  * **Let him live with you**  - "let the slave live among your people"
