===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **…no prostitute among…daughters of Israel…sodomite among the sons of Israel **- Prostitution was habitual religious observance among idolatrous nations, in particular the worship of Ashtoreth or Astarte in temples. (See: Parallelism)
  * **sodomite**  - male prostitute
  * **sacred female prostitute…sacred male prostitute **- Hebrew words mean whore and dog. Wage or hiring refers to their earnings (See: Euphemism).
