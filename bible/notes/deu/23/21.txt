===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * ** If you will refrain from making a vow, it will be no sin in you ** - No one from the children of Israel was under any obligation to vow anything; it was a purely voluntary act.
  * **you must observe and do **- AT: "you must keep and perform, even a freewill offering"
