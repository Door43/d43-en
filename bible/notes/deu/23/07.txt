===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must not detest an Edomite**  - "Do not hate an Edomite"
  * **for he is your fellow Israelite**  - "because he is your relative"
  * **you must not abhor an Egyptian**  - "do not hate an Egyptian"
  * **The descendants of the third generation that are born to them**  - AT: "If an Edomite or an Egyptian joins the Israelite community, his grandchildren may become full members of that community." (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
