===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **may eat your fill of its grapes at your own pleasure**  - AT: "may eat its grapes as you wish until you are fully satisfied"
  * **container **- vessel, utensil and apparatus of any materials as a bag or basket.
  * **then you may pluck the ears with your hand **- 'Ears' here means the head of the grain, as it is scraped or rubbed. AT: "then you may pick the  kernels with your hands"
  * ** sickle ** - a tool with a curved bladed used for cutting grass, grain, and vines
