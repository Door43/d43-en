===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **then you may eat your fill of its grapes at your own pleasure**  - "then you may eat as many of the grapes as you want"
  * **but you must not put any in your container**  - AT: "but you may not put any grapes in something to take them away with you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When you come into your neighbor’s standing grain**  - "When you go into your neighbor's wheat field"
  * **then you may pluck the ears with your hand **  - AT: "then you may take as many kernels of wheat as you can pick with your hands."
  * **but you must not put a sickle to your neighbor’s standing grain**  - AT: "but you must not use a sickle to cut down lots of wheat to take from your neighbor's field." 
  * **sickle** - a sharp tool that farmers use to harvest wheat
