===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **must not enter into the assembly of Yahweh**
  * **illegitimate child** - Possible meanings are 1) a child born to a mother and father who are not married or 2) a child born to a prostitute or 3) a child born to parents who committed incest or adultery. 
  * **as far as to the tenth generation of his decendants**  - to the many descendants coming after - The number ten marks completeness. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **none of them**
