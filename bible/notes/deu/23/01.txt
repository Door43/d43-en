===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **must not enter into the assembly of Yahweh**  - "may not be a full member of the Israelite community"
  * **illegitimate child** - Possible meanings are 1) a child born to parents who committed incest or adultery 2) a child born to a prostitute or 3) a child born to a prostitute serving a false god. 
  * **as far as to the tenth generation of his descendants**  - "even after ten generations of the illegitimate child's descendants"
  * **none of them**  - "none of these descendants"
