===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **may not belong to the assembly of Yahweh** - AT: "may not be a full member of the Israelite community"
  * **illegitimate child** - Possible meanings are 1) a child born to parents who committed incest or adultery, 2) a child born to a prostitute, or 3) a child born to a prostitute serving a false god
  * **as far as to the tenth generation of his descendants** - AT: "even after 10 generations of the illegitimate child's descendants"
  * **none of them** - "none of these descendants"
