===== translationNotes: =====

  * **would not listen** - This is an idiom. AT: "did not pay attention" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **turned the curse into a blessing for you** - "had him bless you and not curse you"
  * **You must never seek their peace or prosperity** - Possible meanings are 1) "You should never make a peace treaty with the Ammonites and the Moabites" or 2) "You must not do anything to cause things to go well for those 2 people groups to enable them to prosper" (UDB).
  * **during all your days** - This is an idiom. AT: "as long as you are a nation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
