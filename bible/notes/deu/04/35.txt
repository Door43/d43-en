===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **To you these things were shown** - AT: "Yahweh showed you these things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **he made you...you heard** - Here "you" refers to those who were at Mount Sinai when God spoke to them.
