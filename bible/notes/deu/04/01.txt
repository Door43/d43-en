===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am about to teach you** - Moses is telling the people of Israel what God wants them to do.
  * **to do them** - AT: "and obey them"
  * **You will not add to the words...neither will you diminish them** - They are not to create more laws nor are they to ignore laws that God specified.
