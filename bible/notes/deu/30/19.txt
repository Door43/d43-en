===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I call heaven and earth to witness** - Possible meanings are 1) Moses is calling on all those who dwell in heaven and earth to be witnesses to what he says (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) or 2) Moses is speaking to heaven and earth as if they are people, and he is calling them to be witness to what he says (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]).
  * **obey his voice**  - AT: "obey what he says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and to cling to him** - "and to rely on him"
  * **For he is your life and the length of your days** - This means that Yahweh is the one who can give the Israelites a long life.
