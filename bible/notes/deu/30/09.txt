===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **the work of your hand** - Here "hand" refers to the whole person. AT: "in all the work you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **in the fruit of your body** - This is an idiom. AT: "in children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the fruit of your cattle** - This is an idiom. AT: "in calves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the fruit of your ground** - This is an idiom. AT: "in crops" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that are written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that I have written" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **with all your heart and all your soul** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:04:29|4:29]].
