===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **He should do this**  - he person who accidentally killed someone should run to one of these cities.
  * **so that the deceased’s relative responsible**  - If somebody killed a person, it was the responsibility of the dead person's closest relative to kill the person who killed his family member.
  * **does not pursue him out of anger** - "does not get angry and run after him"
