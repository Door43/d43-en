===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **enlarges your borders** - "gives you more land to possess" 
  * **as he has sworn to your ancestors to do** - "like he promised your ancestors that he would do"
  * **if you keep all these commandments to do them** - "if you keep all of these commandments by obeying them"
  * **to always walk in his ways** - How God wants a person to live or behave is spoken of as if it were Yahweh's way or road. A person who obeys Yahweh is spoken of as if he were walking on Yahweh's way or road. AT: "to always obey him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **then you must add three more cities for yourself** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "then you must choose three more cities for a person to escape to if he accidentally kills someone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **besides these three** - "in addition to the three cities you already established" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Do this so that innocent blood is not shed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Do this so that family members do not kill an innocent person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **in the midst of the land** - "in the land" or "in the territory"
  * **innocent blood is not shed** - Here "blood" represents a person's life. To shed blood means to kill a person. This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one kills an innocent person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh your God is giving you for as an inheritance** - The land that Yahweh is giving the people of Israel is spoken of as if it were an inheritance. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **so that no bloodguilt may be on you** - The people of Israel being guilty for a person dying because they did not build a city where he would be safe from the "blood avenger" is spoken of as if the guilt of his death is on them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **bloodguilt** - Here "blood" represents a life. And "bloodguilt" refers to the guilt someone has for killing an innocent person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **may be on you** - This means that if a family member kills an innocent person, then all the people of Israel will be guilty for letting that happen.
