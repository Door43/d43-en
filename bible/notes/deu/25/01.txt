===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **If there is a dispute between men**  - AT: "If there is a disagreement between 2 men"
  * **If the guilty man deserves to be beaten**  - AT: "If the guilty man deserves a beating as a punishment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **and be beaten in his presence**  - AT: "and watch him get a beating" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **with the ordered number of blows, as was his crime**  - AT: "with the number of hits appropriate for his crime"
