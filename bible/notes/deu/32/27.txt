===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. Moses continues to use parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])

  * **Were it not that I feared the provocation of the enemy**  - AT: "If it were not for their enemies' taunts" or  "My concern is that their enemies would wrongly boast"
  * **judge mistakenly**  - AT: "misunderstand"
  * **Our hand is exalted**  - AT: "Our strength has triumphed" or "Our power has won" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
