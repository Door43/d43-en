===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. He continues to quote Yahweh's words. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Were it not that I feared the provocation of the enemy** - "I was afraid of the provocation of the enemy"
  * **the provocation of the enemy** - This abstract noun can be translated as a verb. AT: "that the enemy would provoke me" or "that the enemy would cause me to be angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the enemy** - Yahweh speaks of his enemies as if they are one man. AT: "my enemy" or "my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **judge mistakenly** - "misunderstand"
  * **Our hand is exalted** - Here "hand" represents the strength or power of a person. To be exalted is an idiom for defeating an enemy. AT: "We have defeated them because we are more powerful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
