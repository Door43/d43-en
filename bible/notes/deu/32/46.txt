===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said to them**  - Moses said to the people of Israel
  * **Fix your mind on**  - AT: “Pay attention to” or “Think about” (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **your children **- your descendants
  * **this is no trivial matter - **"these are not mere empty words"
  * **because it is your life**  - This phrase emphasizes the importance of following the commandments of Yahweh because their entire lives would be affected by whether they followed the commandments or not.
