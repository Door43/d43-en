===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **proclaim the name of Yahweh** - "tell how good Yahweh is" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **ascribe greatness to our God** - "make sure people know that our God is great"
  * **The Rock** - This a name that Moses gives to Yahweh, who, like a rock, is strong and able to protect his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his work** - "everything he does"
  * **all his paths are just** - "he does everything in a just way"
  * **He is just and upright** - These two words mean basically the same thing and emphasize that Yahweh is fair and does what is right. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
