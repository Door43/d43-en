===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **I am commanding you** - Moses is speaking God's commandments to the people of Israel.
  * **will be in your heart** - This is an idiom. AT: "you must always remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you will diligently teach ... you will talk** - The reader should understand these as commands. AT: "I command you to diligently teach ... I command you to talk"
