===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am commanding you** - Moses is speaking God's commandments to the people of Israel.
  * **will be in your heart** - AT: "you must always remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will diligently teach them ** - God's commandments are to be discussed anytime, anywhere. They are not just for the priests and Levites to speak.
