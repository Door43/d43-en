===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **You will do what is right and good in the sight of Yahweh** - "Do what Yahweh says is right and good." This idiom is a command and blessing. If the Israelites obey Yahweh, they will receive blessings from Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
