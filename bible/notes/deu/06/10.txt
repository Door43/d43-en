===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **large and very good cities that you did not build** - All these cities will belong to the people of Israel when they conquer the people in Canaan.
  * **out of the house of bondage** - Here the metonym "house of bondage" refers to Egypt, the place where the people of Israel had been slaves. AT: "out of the place where you were slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
