===== translationNotes: =====
  * **May the man be cursed** - This is the statement the Levites must shout to all the people of Israel. It may be stated in active form. AT: "May Yahweh curse the person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the work of the hands of a craftsman** - This is an idiom. AT: "something a man made himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
