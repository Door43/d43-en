===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **May the man be cursed** - This is the statement the Levites must shout to all the people of Israel. AT: "May Yahweh curse the person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the work of the hands of a craftsman** - AT: "something a man made himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
