===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues telling the Levites and the people what they must say. 

  * **May the man be cursed** - AT: "May Yahweh curse the man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **fatherless** - children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them
  * **widow** - is a woman whose husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age
