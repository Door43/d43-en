===== translationNotes: =====

  * **one of your brothers** - "one of your fellow Israelites"
  * **within any of your gates** - Here the metonym "gates" refers to the entrance of the city. AT: "within any of your towns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **you must not harden your heart nor shut your hand from your poor brother** - "do not be uncaring and refuse to help him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **shut your hand from your poor brother** - The idiom "shut your hand from him" means to refuse to help. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **but you must surely open your hand to him** - Here "open your hand" means to be generous. "but you must surely help him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
