====== Deuteronomy 15 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Forgiveness of Debt ==
This chapter gives instructions about the necessity to regularly forgive debt among their fellow countryman. This prevented people from being trapped by their poverty.

== Justice ==
As Yahweh's chosen nation, there must be justice in Israel. This chapter gives commands that encouraged justice by protecting the poor, servants and slaves. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:elect]] and [[:en:obe:kt:justice]])
