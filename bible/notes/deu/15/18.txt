===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It must not seem difficult for you to let** - "Do not resent it when you let." AT: "Be pleased when you let" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **a hired person** - This is not a slave, but a free person who works for regular pay.
