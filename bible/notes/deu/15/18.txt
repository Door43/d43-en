===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It must not seem difficult for you to let him go free from you** - This means that they are not resent it when they let someone go free. AT: "Be pleased when you let him go free" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **a hired person** - This is a person who works for pay.
