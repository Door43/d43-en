===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **within your gates - **  “within your community” or “inside your city” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a gazelle or a deer** - A "deer" is the same as a "hart." See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:deu:12:15|12:15]].
  * **you must not eat its blood** - "you must not eat the meat with blood in it" or "you must not consume its blood"
