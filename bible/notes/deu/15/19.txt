===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **You must sanctify** - "You must set apart" or "You must dedicate"
  * **shear** - to cut off wool or hair
  * **year by year** - AT: "each year" or "every year" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]])
  * **lame** - crippled or physically disabled
