===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"I wish it were evening…morning"**  - the expressions of 'you shall say,' which implies terror increases. (See: Indirect Quotation; Parallelism)
  * **I had said to you, 'You will see Egypt no more again.**’ - this was a prophecy at that time, refers to Jewish bondage, some were carried back to Egypt again for slavery. (See: Indirect Quotation; Pronouns)
