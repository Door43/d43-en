  * **sleep with her** - AT: "violate her" or "rape her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])

  * **your donkey will be forcibly taken away from before you, and will not be restored to you** - AT: "someone will take your donkey by force and will not give it back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Your sheep will be given to your enemies** - AT: "Your enemies will take your sheep" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
