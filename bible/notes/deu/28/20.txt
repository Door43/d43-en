===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **curses, confusion, and rebukes** - "disasters, fear, and frustration"
  * **in everything that you put your hand to do** - This is an idiom. AT: "in everything you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **until you are destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "until your enemies destroy you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **forsaken me** - Here "me" refers to Yahweh.
  * **cling to you** - "remain on you"
