===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **curses, confusion, rebuke - **  the group of words presents a mixture of distress, pain of the spirit and correction (See: Doublet)
  * **by which you have forsaken me **- "me" may refer to Yahweh, rather than Moses (See: Pronouns)
  * **cling to you - **  remain on you
