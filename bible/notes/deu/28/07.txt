===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but will flee before you seven ways **- AT: "but will scatter before you in various directions" 
  * **the blessing to come on you in your barns - **  AT: "blessings on you in your barns." See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:pro:03:09|Proverbs 3:10]].
  * **and in all that you put your hand to **- AT: "and in everything you do" The blessing includes both income and expenditure. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
