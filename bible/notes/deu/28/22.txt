===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **infectious diseases, with fever, with inflammation** - These all refer to diseases that cause people to grow weak and die. AT: "diseases and burning fevers that will make you weak"
  * **with drought** - "with lack of rain"
  * **mildew** - mold that grows on crops and causes them to rot
  * **These will pursue you** - AT: "Yahweh will cause these things"
