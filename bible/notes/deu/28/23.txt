===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **skies that are over your head will be bronze **- "like brass, hard, unresponsive" (See: Idiom)
  * **the earth that is under you will be iron **- the ground will be as hard as iron (UDB). These two clauses together contrast those of Leviticus 26:19. (See: Parallelism)
  * **make the rain of your land into powder and dust **- this possibly means showers and clouds of dust (See: Doublet)
