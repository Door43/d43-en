===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There he found a certain man**  - "There Peter met a man". Peter was not intentionally searching for a paralyzed person but happened upon him.
  * **paralyzed**  - unable to walk, probably unable to move below the waist
  * **make your bed**  - "roll up you mat" (UDB)
  * **everyone who lived…**  - This is a hyperbole for "many people who lived. . . ." (see: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
