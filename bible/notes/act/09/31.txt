===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **was built up**  - God caused them to grow
  * **walking in the fear of the lord**  - "continuing to honor the Lord"
  * **in the comfort of the Holy Spirit**  - "the Holy Spirit was strengthening and encouraging them"
  * **Throughout the whole region**  - This is hyperbole for Peter visiting many of the believers throughout Judea, Galilee and Samaria. (See: [[en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **God's people**  - This is metonymy for the people who believed in the gospel message of Jesus Christ. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Lydda** Lydda is a city located about 18 km southeast of Joppa. This city was called Lod in the OT, and in modern Israel.
