===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but they were all afraid of him**  - "They were all" is an a hyperbole for many or most. It can be restated as "almost all of them". (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **But Barnabas took him**  - "But Barnabas took Saul"
  * **Saul had boldly preached in the name of Jesus**  - This is metonymy for Saul preaching or teaching the gospel message of Jesus Christ. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
