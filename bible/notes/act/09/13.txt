===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your holy people** - Here "holy people" refers to Christians. AT: "the people in Jerusalem who believe in you" 
  * **authority ... to arrest everyone here** - It is implied that the extent of the power and authority granted Saul was limited to the Jewish people at this point in time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **calls upon your name** - Here "your name" refers to Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he is a chosen instrument of mine** - "chosen instrument" refers to something that is set apart for service. AT: "I have chosen him to serve me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to carry my name** - This is an expression for identifying or speaking out for Jesus. AT: "in order that he might speak about me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for the cause of my name** - This is an expression meaning "for telling people about me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
