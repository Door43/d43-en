===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they sent two men to him**  - "the disciples sent two men to Peter"
  * **disciple** - Anyone who obeys and follows Jesus is a disciple of Jesus.
  * **widows**  - Women whose husbands have died
  * **while she had been with them**  - "while she was still alive with the disciples" (UDB)
