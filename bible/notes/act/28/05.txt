===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **shook the animal into the fire** - "shook his hand so that the snake fell from his hand into the fire"
  * **become inflamed with fever** - Possible meanings are 1) "get a high fever" or 2) "become swollen."
  * **said he was a god** - There was a belief that someone who lived after a poisonous snake bite was divine or a god.
