===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a viper came out** - "a poisonous snake came out of the bundle of sticks"
  * **fastened onto his hand** - "bit Paul's hand and did not let go"
  * **This man certainly is a murderer** - "For sure, this man is a murderer" or "This man is truly a murderer"
  * **yet justice** - The word "justice" refers to the name of a god that they worshiped. AT: "the god called Justice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
