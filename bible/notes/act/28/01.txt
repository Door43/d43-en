===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When we were brought safely through** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "When we arrived safely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **we learned** - The word "we" refers to Paul and Luke, the author of Acts, who traveled with Paul on this journey. AT: "we learned from the people" or "we found out from the residents" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **the island was called Malta** - Malta is an island located south of the modern-day island of Sicily. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **The native people** - "The people who lived there"
  * **offered to us not just ordinary kindness** - Being kind to someone is spoken of as if it were an object that someone offers. AT: "were not only very kind to us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **not just ordinary kindness** - This phrase is used to emphasize the opposite of what is said. AT: "a great deal of kindness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **they lit a fire** - "they put together twigs and branches and burned them"
  * **welcomed us all** - Possible meanings are 1) "welcomed all of the people from the ship" or 2) "welcomed Paul and all his companions."
