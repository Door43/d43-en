===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul's journey to Rome continues.
  * **General Information:** - The Twin Brothers refers to Castor and Pollux, the twin sons of Zeus, a Greek god. They were thought to be  protectors of ships. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])

  * **that had spent the winter at the island** - "that the crew left at the island for the cold season"
  * **a ship of Alexandria** - Possible meanings are this refers to 1) a ship that came from Alexandria, or 2) a ship that was registered or licensed in Alexandria.
  * **the twin gods** - On the bow of the ship, there was a carving of the two idols called "the twin gods." Their names were Castor and Pollux.
  * **city of Syracuse** - Syracuse is a city on the southeast coast of the modern-day island of Sicily, just southwest of Italy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
