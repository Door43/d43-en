Then Paul and Barnabas entrusted the leaders and other believers to the Lord Jesus, in whom they had believed, in order that he would care for them.

\v 25 In that district, they arrived at Perga town and preached God's message about the Lord Jesus to the people there. Then they went down to the seacoast at Attalia town.
