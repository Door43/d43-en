====== Acts 25 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Favor ==
It was highly unusual for the Jewish leaders to try to get a favor from the Roman officials. They often viewed the Gentile leaders as [[:en:obe:kt:evil|evil]]. The governor though wanted peace with the Jews, so he sought to gain favor with the Jewish leaders. 

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== Roman citizenship ==

Men who had Roman citizenship were given many privileges. They could appeal to Caesar if they were accused of a crime. Having Roman citizen was very valuable and people would go to great lengths to attain citizenship. Roman officials could have gotten into a lot of trouble for treating a Roman citizen in this way.
