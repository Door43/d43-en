===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After he stayed**  - "After Festus stayed"
  * **sat in the judgment seat**  - "sat upon the seat where he acted as judge"
  * **Paul to be brought to him**  - "that they bring Paul to him". (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **When he arrived**  - "When Paul came and stood before Festus"
  * **the name of the Jews**  - "the law of the Jews" (UDB)
  * **not against the temple**  - This means he did not break any rules about who could enter the Jerusalem Temple. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
