===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Festus finishes explaining Paul's case to King Agrippa.

  * **But when Paul appealed to be kept in custody while awaiting the decision of the emperor** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "But when Paul insisted that he stay under Roman guard until the time when the emperor could decide his case" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I ordered him to be held in custody** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I ordered the soldiers to keep him in custody" or "I told the soldiers to guard him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **"Tomorrow," Festus said, "you will hear him."** - The phrase "Festus said" can be moved to the beginning of the sentence. AT: "Festus said, 'I will arrange for you to listen to Paul tomorrow.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_quotations]])
