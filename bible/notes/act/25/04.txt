===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that Paul was a prisoner in Caesarea**- This is an indirect speech quote. Alternated translation: "Paul is a prisoner in Caesarea, and I myself will soon return there." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations|Speech Quotations]])
  * **If there is something wrong with the man**  - "If Paul has done something wrong"
  * **you should accuse him**  - "you shoud bring charges" or "you should accuse him of violating laws"
