===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul finishes his speech to the philosophers in the Areopagus, which he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:17:22|17:22]].

  * **Therefore** - This word marks a statement that is said because of what was said previously. 
  * **ignorance** - Possible meanings are 1) "simply not knowing about something" or 2) "willful rejection."
  * **world** - Here, the word "world" refers to the people who live in the world.
  * **by the man he has chosen** - "by the man God has chosen"
  * **God has given proof of this man** - "God has demonstrated His choice of this man"
