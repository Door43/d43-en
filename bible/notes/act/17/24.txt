===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues to speak.

  * **served** - "cared for" The word has the sense of a doctor treating a patient to make him/her well again.
  * **since he is Lord* ** - "because he is the Lord"
  * **built with hands** - "through the action of people"
  * **men's hands** - "human hands"
  * **since he himself ** - "because he himself"
