===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues to speak.

  * **world** - In the most general sense, the "world" refers to the heavens and the earth and everything in them.
  * **since he is Lord** - "He" is referring to the unknown god that Paul is explaining is the Lord God. AT: "because he is the Lord."
  * **built with hands** - "through the action of people"
  * **is he served** - "Served" has the sense of a doctor treating a patient to make the patient well again. AT: "cared for."
  * **by men's hands** - "by human hands"
  * **since he himself** - "because he himself"
