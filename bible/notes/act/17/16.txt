===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his spirit was provoked**  - "he was disturbed" or "he was irritated" or "he became very distressed" (UDB)
  * **reasoned**  - "debated" or "discussed". This verb implies that there is more interaction from the listeners than in preaching.
  * **marketplace**  - This is a public space of business, where buying and selling of goods, cattle, or services take place; “public square” (UDB).
