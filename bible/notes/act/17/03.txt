===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He** - This pronoun refers to Paul.
  * **opening the Scriptures** - "opening the book or scroll of Scriptures"
  * **it was necessary** - "it was part of the plan" or "it had to happen".
  * **rise again** - "come back to life"
  * **persuaded ** - "convinced" or "won over"
  * **joined ** - "became associated with"
  * **devout ** - those who worship God but have not converted to Judaism through circumcision
  * **great** - "large"
