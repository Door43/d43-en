===== translationWords: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they were troubled** - "worried" or "distressed mentally"
  * **had taken financial security** - "bond" or "deposit" or "fine (UDB)". This money was a promise of good behavior, which might be returned if all went well, or used to repair damages brought on by bad behavior.
  * **and the rest** - "the believers other than Jason"
  * **they let them go** - "the officials released Jason and the other believers arrested"
