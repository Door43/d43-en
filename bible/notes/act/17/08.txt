===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **troubled** - "worried" or "distressed mentally"
  * **financial security** - "bond" or "deposit" or "fine (UDB)". This money was a promise of good behavior, which might be returned if all went well, or used to repair damages brought on by bad behavior.
  * **the rest** - "the believers other than Jason"
  * **let them go** - "released them"
