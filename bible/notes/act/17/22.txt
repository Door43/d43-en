===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Religious** - This does not refers to inward personal devotion but to public honor to the gods through prayers, building altars and offering sacrifices.
  * **For** - "seeing that" or "because"
  * **passed along** - "walked past"
  * **UNKNOWN GOD ** - This could include willful rejection of what is known of God. This could also be translated as “to AN unknown god” or “to any god we do not know about."
