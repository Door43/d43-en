===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Julius treated Paul kindly**  - "Julius treated Paul with a friendly concern". See how you translated "Julius" in [[:en:bible:notes:act:27:01|27:1]].
  * **go to his friends to receive their care**  - "go to his friends to receive the care he needed" or "go to his friends to get whatever help he needed"
  * **sailed around the island of Cyprus that was sheltered from the wind**  - "went along the coast of Cyprus, on the side that kept the wind from blowing us the wrong way" (UDB)
  * **Pamphylia**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:act:02:08|2:10]].
  * **came to Myra, a city of Lycia**  - Myra was a city in Lycia province which is on the south western coast of modern day Turkey. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **a city of Lycia**  - Lycia was a roman province which is on the south western coast of modern day Turkey. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Alexandria**  - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Italy**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:act:27:01|27:1]].
