===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When they had gone long without food** - Here "they" refers to the sailors. It is implied that Luke, Paul, and those with them had not eaten either. AT: "When we had gone a long time without food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **among the sailors** - "among the men"
  * **so as to get this injury and loss** - "and as a result suffer this harm and loss"
  * **there shall be no loss of life among you** - Paul is speaking to the sailors. It is implied that Paul also means that he and those with him will not die either. AT: "none of us will die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **but only the loss of the ship** - Here "loss" is used in the sense of destroy. AT: "but the storm will destroy only the ship"
