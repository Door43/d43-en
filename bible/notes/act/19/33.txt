===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jews brought Alexander** - Paul warns Timothy of Alexander the coppersmith (2 Timothy [[en:bible:notes:2ti:04:14|4:14]]). It is not clear if they are the same person.
  * **motioned with his hand ** - "gestured to the audience"
  * **make an explanation** - He wanted to "offer a defense," but it is not clear what he was going to say.
