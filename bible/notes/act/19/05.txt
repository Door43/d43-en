===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul continues staying in Ephesus.
 
  * **When the people** - Here "people" refers to the disciples in Ephesus who were talking with Paul. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:act:19:03|19:1]])
  * **they were baptized** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they received baptism" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in the name of the Lord Jesus** - Here "name" refers to Jesus' power and authority. AT: "as believers in the Lord Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **laid his hands on them** - "placed his hands on them." He probably placed his hands on their shoulders or heads. AT: "placed his hands on their heads as he prayed" 
  * **they spoke in other languages and prophesied** - Unlike in [[:en:bible:notes:act:02:01|2:3-4]], there are no details of who  understood their messages.
  * **In all they were about twelve men** - This tells how many men were baptized. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **twelve men** - "12 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
