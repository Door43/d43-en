===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about**  - "it happened"
  * **passed through**  - "traveled through"
  * **upper country**  - This was an area of Asia (modern Turkey) which was to the north of Ephesus and Alexandria Troas and eastward inland.
  * **receive the Holy Spirit** - To obtain or get the Holy Spirit.
  * **we did not even hear about the Holy Spirit**  - "we had not heard about the Holy Spirit."
