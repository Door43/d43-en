===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and Paul I know **- This could also be translated as "and Paul I have heard about;"
  * **who are you **- This is a rhetorical question that asks the sons of Sceva what authority they have over evil spirits. This can also be translated as "what authority do you have?" or "you have no authority." (See: rhetorical question)
  * **exorcists **- These were people who tried to cast out evil spirits from people by using magic words.
  * **naked**  - "with some or all of their clothes ripped off them"
