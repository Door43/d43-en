===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the word "you" is plural and refers to the captain of the temple and the chief priests. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **they were much perplexed** - "they were very puzzled" or "they were very confused"
  * **concerning them** - "concerning the words they had just heard" or "concerning these things"
  * **what would come of it** - "and what would happen as a result"
  * **standing in the temple** - They did not go into the part of the temple building where only the priests were allowed. AT: "standing in the temple courtyard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
