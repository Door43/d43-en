===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Gallio said** - The Roman governor of the Province.
  * **your own law ** - These were the Law of Moses and other Jewish customs of Paul's time.
  * **I do not wish to be a judge of these matters ** - "I refuse to make a judgment about these matters."
