===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the word "he" refers to Paul. 
  * **General Information:** - The first word "his" refers to Titius Justus. The second word "his" refers to Crispus.

  * **Titius Justus ... Crispus** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **worshiped God** - A worshiper of God is a Gentile who gives praise to God and follows him but does not necessarily obey all of the Jewish laws.
  * **â€‹leader of the synagogue** - a layperson who sponsored and administered the synagogue, not necessarily the teacher
  * **all those who lived in his house** - Here "house" refers to the people who lived together. AT: "the people who lived with him in his house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **were baptized** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "received baptism" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
