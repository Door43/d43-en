\v 24 While Paul was going through Galatia and Phrygia, a Jewish man named Apollos came to Ephesus. He was from Alexandria city and spoke very well about the Scriptures.
\v 25 Other believers had taught Apollos how the Lord Jesus wanted people to live, and he taught those things to the people. However, he was not teaching everything about Jesus, because he only knew about the baptism of John the Baptizer.
