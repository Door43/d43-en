===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they all took** - "everyone took" or "all of them grabbed". This is a hyperbole because every single member of the crowd could not take him. (See: [[en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
  * **beat him** - "hit him", "punched him". Sosthenes was physically beaten.
