===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After these things** - "After the events that took place in Athens"
  * **found ** - "happened to find by chance" or "found after seeking with intent." The text is not clear which is the case.
  * **recently** - This is probably sometime in the past year.
  * **commanded ** - "ordered" or "instructed". This indicates a detailed instruction was given.
