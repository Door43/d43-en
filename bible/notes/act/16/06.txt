===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they, them ** - "They" and "them" throughout this passage refer to Paul and his companions.
  * **they had been forbidden by the Holy Spirit** - "the Holy Spirit had forbidden them" (See: [[en:ta:translate:Active]])
  * **the Spirit of Jesus** - This refers to the Holy Spirit.
