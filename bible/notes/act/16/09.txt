===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **calling him **  - "imploring him" or "pleading with him".
  * **help us **  - "Help me and the other people of Macedonia."
  * **we and us **  - The pronouns "we" and "us" refer to Paul and his companions. It is "we" because Luke, the author of Acts, was one of Paul's companions at this time.
  * **preach the gospel to them**  - "preach the gospel to the people of macedonia".
