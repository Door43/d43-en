===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They spoke…to him - **"Paul and Silas spoke..to the jailer"
  * **in his house -**  "in the jailer's house"
  * **took them…washed their wounds - **"took Paul and Silas… washed the wounds of Paul and Silas"
  * 
  * **he and all his household were baptized** - This can also be translated as, "Paul and Silas baptized the jailer and all members of his household" (See: Active or Passive) 
  * **he,his**  - Refer to the jailer
  * **them**  - "Paul and Silas"
  * **because they had all believed**  - "because all members of his household had believed"
