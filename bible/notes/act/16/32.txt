===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul and Silas continue their involvement with the Jailer and his household.

  * **in his house**  - The Jailer brought Paul and Silas to his home. AT: "in his home."
  * **he and all his household were baptized**  - AT: "Paul and Silas baptized the jailer and all members of his household" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **him…he…his**  - These pronouns refer to the jailer.
  * **because they had all believed**  - "because all members of his household had believed"
