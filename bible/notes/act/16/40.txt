===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of the story about the Paul and Silas being in jail. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_endofstory]])

  * **came to the house** - Here "came" can be translated as "went." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **the house of Lydia** - "the home of Lydia"
  * **saw the brothers** - Here "brothers" refers to believers whether male or female. AT: "saw the believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **they encouraged them** - "Paul and Silas encouraged the brothers" or "Paul and Silas encouraged the believers"
