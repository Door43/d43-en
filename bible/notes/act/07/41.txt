  * **to serve the host of heaven** - "to serve the lights in the sky"
  * **Did you offer to me slain beasts and sacrificesâ€¦?** - God asked this question to show Israel they did not worship Him with sacrifices. AT: "You did not offer to me slain beasts and sacrifices." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
