===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This ends the story of Stephen.

  * **receive my spirit** - "take my spirit." It may be helpful to add "please" to show that this was a request. AT: "please receive my spirit"
  * **He knelt down** - This is an act of submission to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **do not hold this sin against them** - This can be stated in a positive way. AT: "forgive them for this sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **fell asleep** - This is a polite expression that means he "died." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
