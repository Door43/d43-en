===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The patriarchs** - "Jacob's older sons" or "Joseph's older brothers"
  * **were motivated by jealousy against** - "became jealous of." This can be stated in the active form. At: "jealousy motivated the patriarchs against" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **sold him into Egypt** - The Jews knew their ancestors sold Joseph to be a slave in Egypt. AT: "sold him as a slave in Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **was with him** - This is an idiom for helping someone. AT: "helped him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **over Egypt** - This refers to the people of Egypt. AT: "over all the people of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all his household** - This refers to all his possessions. AT: "everything he owned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
