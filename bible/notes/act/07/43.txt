===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Stephen continues his response to the high priest and the council which he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:07:01|7:2]]. 
  * **General Information:** - The quotation from the prophet Amos continues here. 

  * **You accepted** - It is implied that they took these idols with them as they traveled in the wilderness. AT: "you carried with you from place to place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **tabernacle of Molech** - the tent that housed the false god Molech
  * **the star of the god Rephan** - the star that is identified with the false god Rephan
  * **the images that you made** - They made statues or images of the gods Molech and Rephan in order to worship them.
  * **I will carry you away beyond Babylon** - "I will remove you to places even farther than Babylon." This would be God's act of judgment.
