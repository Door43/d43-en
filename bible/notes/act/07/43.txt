===== Translation Notes: =====

Stephen continues his defense before the Jewish council that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:07:01|7:2]]. Stephen here continues a quotation of Amos [[:en:bible:notes:amo:05:25|5:25-27]] that he started in [[:en:bible:notes:act:07:41|7:42]].

  * **tabernacle of Molech**  - The tabernacle or tent that housed the false god Molech
  * **the star of the god Rephan**  - the star that is identified to the false god Rephan
  * **Images that you made**  - They made statues or images of the gods Molech and Rephan for the purpose to worship them.
  * **I will carry you away beyond Babylon**  - "I will remove you to Babylon"
