===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stopped their ears**  - They covered their ears so that they would not hear any more of what Stephen was saying.
  * **They threw him out of the city**  - "The council members seized Stephen and forcefully took him out of the city"
  * **outer clothes**  - these are cloaks or robes one would wear outside to stay warm, similar in function to a jacket or coat.
  * **at the feet**  - "in front of" for the purpose of guarding them.
