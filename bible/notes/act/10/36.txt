===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter continues to talk to Cornelius and his guests.
  * **General Information:** - The word "him" here refers to Jesus.

  * **You know the message ... and with power** - This long sentence can be shortened into several sentences as in the UDB.
  * **who is Lord of all** - Here "all" means "all people."
  * **throughout all Judea** - This is a hyperbole. AT: "in many places in Judea" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **after the baptism that John announced** - "after John preached to the people to repent and then baptized them"
  * **God anointed him with the Holy Spirit and with power** - The Holy Spirit and God's power are spoken of as if they are something that can be poured out onto a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all who were oppressed by the devil** - The word "all" is a generalization. AT: "those who were oppressed by the devil" or "many people who were oppressed by the devil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **God was with him** - The idiom "was with him" means "was helping him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
