===== Translation Notes: =====

Peter continues to speak.

  * **You know the message**  - "You know the word"
  * **who is Lord of all**  - This is all inclusive, both Hebrew and Greek.
  * **you yourselves**  - Refers to Cornelius and his guests (plural)
