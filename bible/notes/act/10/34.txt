===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter begins speaking to everyone in the house of Cornelius.

  * **Then Peter opened his mouth and said** - "Peter began to speak to them" 
  * **Truly** - This means that what he is about to say is especially important to know.
  * **God does not take anyone's side** - "God does not favor certain people"
  * **anyone who worships and does righteous deeds is acceptable to him** - "he accepts anyone who worships him and does righteous deeds"
  * **worships** - The word "worships" here has the sense of deep respect and awe.
