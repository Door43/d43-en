===== Translation Notes: =====

The believers together continue the prayer that they began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:04:23|4:24]].

  * **look upon their warnings**- This is the reason for granting the disciples boldness to speak God's word.
  * **they spoke the word of God with boldness** - this is result of the disciples being filled with the Holy Spirit.
