===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Speaking together, the people quote a Psalm of David from the Old Testament.
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "they" refers to the rest of the believers, but not to Peter and John.

  * **came to their own people** - The phrase "their own people" refers to the rest of the believers. AT: "went to the other believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **You spoke by the Holy Spirit through the mouth of your servant, our father David** - This means that the Holy Spirit caused David to speak or write down what God said.
  * **through the mouth of your servant, our father David** - Here the word "mouth" refers to the words that David spoke or wrote down. AT: "by the words of your servant, our father David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **our father David** - Here "father" refers to "ancestor/"
  * **Why did the Gentile nations rage, and the peoples imagine useless things?** - This is a rhetorical question that emphasizes the futility of opposing God. AT: "The Gentile nations should not have raged, and the peoples should not have imagined useless things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the peoples imagine useless things** - These "useless things" consist of plans to oppose God. AT: "the peoples imagine useless things against God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **peoples** - "people groups" or "tribes"
