===== Translation Notes: =====

The believers together continue the prayer that they began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:04:23|4:24]].

    * **Herod and Pontius Pilate, together with the Gentiles and the people of Israel** - In the Psalm, David included only the Gentile nations, but Peter included Israel and their rulers among those who opposed the Messiah.
    * **in this city** - in Jerusalem
    * **your hand and your desire decided** - "you decided" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
