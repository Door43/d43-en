===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all who owned title to lands or houses** - The word "all" here is a generalization. AT: "Many people who owned title to lands or houses" or "People who owned title to lands or houses" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **owned title to lands or houses** - "owned land or houses"
  * **the money of the things that were sold** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the money that they received from the things that they sold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **laid it at the apostles' feet** - This means that they presented the money to the apostles. AT: "presented it to the apostles" or "gave it to the apostles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **it was distributed to each one according to their need** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they distributed the money to each believer who needed it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
