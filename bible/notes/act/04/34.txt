===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the money of the things that were sold** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the money that they received from the things that they sold." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **laid it at the apostles' feet** - This means that they presented to money to the apostles. AT: "presented it to the apostles." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **distribution was made to each believer** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they distributed the money to each believer." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
