===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they were unable to find any excuse to punish them** - The leaders could not figure out how to punish Peter and John without causing a riot among the people who saw the man healed.
  * **after further warning** - The leaders threatened them with further punishment.
  * **The man...was more than forty years old** - It was a well established fact that the man had been crippled and was recently healed.
