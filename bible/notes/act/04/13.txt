===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **when they saw the boldness**  - "they" refers to the group leaders.
  * **realized that they**  - "perceiving" or "knowing"
  * **they were ordinary**  - "they" refers to two, Peter and John.
  * **uneducated men**  - "untrained" or "no formal schooling"
