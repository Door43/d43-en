===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The believers complete their quotation from King David in the Psalms that they began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:04:23|4:25]].

  * **set themselves together** - "joined their armies together" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Christ** - This is a quotation from Psalm 2:1-2. The Hebrew "Messiah" is translated "Christ" and means the "anointed one."
