===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stretched out his hand** - "raised his hand toward the crowd to get their attention"
  * **made his defense** - "began to defend himself against the accusations"
  * **I regard myself as happy** - Paul was happy because he considered his appearance before Agrippa to be an opportunity to speak about the Gospel.
