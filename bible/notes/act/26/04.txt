===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all the Jews** - This is a generalization. Possible meanings are 1) this refers to Jews in general who knew about Paul. AT: "the Jews" or 2) this refers to Pharisees who knew Paul. AT: "the Jewish leaders" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **in my own nation** - Possible meanings are 1) among his own people, not necessarily in the geographical land of Israel or 2) in the land of Israel.
  * **the strictest party of our religion** - "a group within Judaism who live by very strict rules"
