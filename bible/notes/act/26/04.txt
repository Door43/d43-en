===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues to speak.

  * **all the Jews** - Possible meanings: 1) Pharisees who grew up with Paul and knew him as a Pharisee or 2) "Paul was well known amongst the Jews due to his zeal as a pharisee and now as a believer"
  * **in my nation** - Possible meanings: 1) among his own people, not necessarily in the geographical land of Israel or 2) in the land of Israel.
