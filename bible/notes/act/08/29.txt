===== translationNotes: =====

  * **stay close to this chariot** - Philip understood that this meant he was to stay close to the person riding in the chariot. AT: "accompany the man in this chariot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **reading Isaiah the prophet** - This is the Old Testament book Isaiah. AT: "reading from the book of the prophet Isaiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Do you understand what you are reading?** - The Ethiopian was intelligent and could read, but he lacked spiritual discernment. AT: "Do you understand the meaning of what you are reading?"
  * **How can I, unless someone guides me?** - This question was asked to state emphatically that he could not understand without help. AT: "I cannot understand unless someone guides me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **He begged Philip to ... sit with him** - It is implied here that Philip agreed to travel down the road with him to explain the scriptures. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
