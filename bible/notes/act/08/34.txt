===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I beg you** - "Please tell me"
  * **himself, or someone else** - It may be helpful to your audience to add in the missing information. AT: Is the prophet speaking of himself, or is he speaking of some other person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **this scripture** - This refers to Isaiah's writings in the Old Testament. AT: "in the writings of Isaiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to preach about Jesus to him** - "to teach the good news about Jesus to the eunuch"
