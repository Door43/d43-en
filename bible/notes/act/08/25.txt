===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **born witness**  - Peter and John told what they personally knew about Jesus to the Samaritans.
  * **spoken the word of the Lord**  - Peter & John explained how the scriptures spoke of Jesus to the Samaritan.
  * **…to many villages of the Samaritans **  - "to the people in many Samaritan villages" . (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
