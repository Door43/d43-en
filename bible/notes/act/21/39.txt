===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul begins to defend what he did.

  * **I ask you** - "I beg you" or "I plead with you"
  * **allow me** - "please allow me" or please permit me"
  * **the captain had given him permission** - The abstract noun "permission" can be stated as a verb. AT: "the captain permitted Paul to speak" or "the captain allowed Paul to speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Paul stood on the steps** - The word "steps" here refers to the steps on a stairway to the fortress.
  * **motioned with the hand to the people** - It can be stated explicitly why Paul motioned with the hand. AT: "motioned with his hand for the people to be quiet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When there was a deep silence** - "When the people were completely silent"
