===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When we had finished the voyage**  - The word "we" refers to Luke, Paul and those traveling with them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **we arrived at Ptolemais**  - Ptolemaic was a city south of Tyre, Lebanon. Ptolemaic is modern day Acre, Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **one of the seven**  - One of the seven men chosen to distribute food and aid to the widows earlier in Acts [[:en:bible:notes:act:06:05|6:5]]
  * **this man**  - "Philip"
  * **four virgin daughters who prophesied**  - "four virgin daughters who were known as people who regularly received and passed along messages from God"
