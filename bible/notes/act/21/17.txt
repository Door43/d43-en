===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the brothers welcomed us** - These were the remaining Jewish believers, both men and women, who remained in Jerusalem after the great persecution began.
  * **When he had greeted them, he reported** - "After Paul greeted the elders, he reported" or "after greeting the elders, Paul reported"
  * **his ministry** - "Paul's ministry"
  * **he reported one by one the things that God** - AT: "Paul gave a detailed account of all the things that God."
