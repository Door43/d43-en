===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul and his companions arrive in Jerusalem. 

  * **the brothers welcomed us** - Here "brothers" refers to the believers in Jerusalem whether male or female. AT: "the fellow believers welcomed us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **When he had greeted them, he reported** - "After Paul greeted the elders, he reported" or "after greeting the elders, Paul reported"
  * **he reported one by one the things that God** - "Paul gave a detailed account of all the things that God"
  * **his ministry** - "Paul's ministry"
