===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we picked up our bags**  - The word "we" refers to Luke, Paul and those traveling with them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **They brought with them**  - The word "They" refers to some of the disciples from Caesarea.
  * **disciples** - Anyone who obeys and follows Jesus is a disciple of Jesus.
  * **Mnason, a man from Cyprus** - Mnason is a man from the island of Cyprus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
