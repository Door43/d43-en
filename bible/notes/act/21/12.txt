===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we **- "Luke and the others who were traveling with Paul"
  * **What are you doing, weeping and breaking my heart? **- He asks a rhetorical question because he wants them to stop trying to persuade him to not go to Jersualem, by making him sad with their tears. (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **"May the will of the Lord be done."**  - That is, they are now accepting whatever is God's plan for Paul in Jerusalem.
