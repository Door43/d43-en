===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **godly men**  - people who wanted to honor and or worship God
  * **every nation under heaven**  - "every nation in the world"
  * **when this sound was heard**  - This refers to the sound that was like a strong wind. This can be translated with an active verb: "when they heard this sound." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **the multitude**  - "the large crowd of people"
  * **Galileans**  - This can be translated as "from Galilee."
