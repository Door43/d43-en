===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Fear came upon every soul** - AT: "All of the people were very afraid," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **had all things in common** - "shared all of their possessions"
  * **distributed them to** - "gave them to" or "gave money to"
  * **fear** - "reverent awe"
  * **were together** - "believed the same thing"
  * **according as anyone had need** - When a need was expressed or observed, other believers gave to meet that need.
