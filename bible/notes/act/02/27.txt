===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter finishes quoting David.
  * **General Information:** - Since Peter says that David said these words about Jesus, the words "my," "Holy One," and "me" refer to Jesus and the words "you" and "your" refer to God.

  * **neither will you allow your Holy One to see decay** - The Messiah, Jesus, refers to himself with the words "your Holy One." AT: "neither will you allow me, your Holy One, to see decay" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **to see decay** - Here the word "see" means to experience something. The word "decay" refers to the decomposition of his body after death. AT: "to decay" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the ways of life** - "the ways that lead to life" 
  * **full of gladness with your face** - Here the word "face" refers to the presence of God. AT: "very glad when I see you" or "very glad when I am in your presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **gladness** - "joy" or "happiness"
