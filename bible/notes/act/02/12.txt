===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **amazed and perplexed** - The people did not know what to think about what was happening (UDB). AT: "surprised and confused."
  * **What does this mean** - Some people were amazed at what was happening.
  * **But others mocked** - "But others insulted" or "put down"
  * **They are full of new wine** - AT: "They are drunk." Some chose to not believe the miracle, but to mock the apostles.
  * **new wine** - a stronger drink than normal wine
