===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** - This begins a new part of the story.
  * **about that time** - This refers to the time the disciples in Antioch sent money to help the brothers in Judea.
  * **laid hands on** - This means Herod oppressed the believers. AT: "sent soldiers to arrest." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **some of those from the assembly** - It is implied from context that this refers to leaders of the assembly. AT: "the leaders of the assembly." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to mistreat them** - "to cause the believers to suffer"
  * **He killed James...with the sword** - This was specifying the manner in which James was killed.
  * **He killed** - "Herod the king killed" or "Herod the king gave orders to kill"
