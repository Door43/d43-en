===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But Peter continued knocking**-The word here for continued means that Peter kept knocking the entire time those inside were talking.
  * **when they had opened the door, they saw him and were amazed**  - "after opening the door, the people inside the house saw Peter and they were amazed"
  * **motioned to them… and told them**  - "motioned to the people inside the house…and told the people"
  * **report these things**  - "tell these things"
  * **he left**  - "Peter left"
