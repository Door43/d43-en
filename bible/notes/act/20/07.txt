===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the beginning of the part of the part of the story about Paul preaching in Troas and about Eutychus.

  * **when we were gathered together** - "when we came together." Here "we" refers to Luke, Paul, and Paul's traveling companions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **to break bread** - Bread was a common food during meals. Here "break break" probably means they shared a meal with more kinds of food than just bread. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **he kept speaking** - "he continued to speak"
  * **upper room** - This may have been the third floor of an apartment complex.
