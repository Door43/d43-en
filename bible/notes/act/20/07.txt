===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On the first day of the week **- "on Sunday"
  * **to break bread **- Bread was broken and eaten during the Lord's Supper (see UDB)
  * **kept **- "continued"
  * **upper room**  - This is probably on the third floor of an apartment complex.
