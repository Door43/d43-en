===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Paul continues to speak.)
  * **I coveted no man's silver, **- "I did not greatly desire someone's silver," or "I did not want for myself anyone's silver,"
  * **man's silver, gold, or clothing. **- At that time, having many clothes was thought of as part of being rich. Clothing was considered a treasure; the more you have, the richer you are.
  * **these hands served my own needs**  - "I worked with my own hands to earn money" to pay for our own expenses.
  * **help the weak by working, **- "Work so as to have money to help people who cannot earn it for themselves,"
  * **"It is more blessed **- "A person receives the favor of God and experiences his joy more when he gives"
