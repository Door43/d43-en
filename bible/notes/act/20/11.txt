===== Important Terms: ===== 

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he went upstairs** - "Paul went upstairs"
  * **broke bread** - "shared a meal." This included dividing up loaves of bread between all the people.
  * **he left** - "he went away"
  * **the boy** - Possible meanings are 1) young man over 14 (UDB), 2) servant or slave, 3) or a boy between 9 and 14 years old.
