===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we** - Paul, Luke, and those travelling with them
  * **touched** - "arrived"
  * **Miletus**  - Miletus was a port city in western Asia Minor near the mouth of the Meander River.
  * **For Paul had decided to sail past Ephesus, **- That is he sailed south from Assos, past the port city of Ephesus, further south in order to land at Miletus.
