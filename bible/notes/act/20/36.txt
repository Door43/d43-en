===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the end of the part of the story about Paul praying with the elders of the church of Ephesus. 

  * **fell on Paul's neck** - "hugged him closely" or "put their arms around him"
  * **kissed him** - Kissing someone on the cheek is an expression of brotherly or friendly love in the Middle East.
  * **they would never see his face again** - The word "face" here represents Paul's physical body. AT: "will not see me in the flesh anymore on this earth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
