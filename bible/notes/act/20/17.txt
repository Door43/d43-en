===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **set foot in Asia,**- "entered this region of Asia,"
  * **lowliness of mind**  - "humility" or "humbleness"
  * **with tears**  - "I sometimes cried when I was teaching you,"
  * **keep back**  - "hold back" or "withhold"
  * **going from house to house.**  - That is, he taught people in various private homes.
  * **repentance toward God**  - "That they should turn from their sins toward God."
