===== translationNotes: =====

  * **be on guard. Remember** - AT: "be on guard and remember" or "be on guard as you remember" or "be on guard by remembering."
  * **be on guard** - "be awake and alert" or "watch out" (UDB) or "stay alert"
  * **Remember** - "Continue to remember" or "Do not forget"
  * **for three years I did not stop instructing** - Paul did not teach them continuously for three years, but over the space of three years. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **I did not stop instructing** - Possible meanings are 1) "I did not stop warning" or 2) "I did not stop encouraging with corrections."
