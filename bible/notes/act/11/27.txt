===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now in these days** - This introduces a new part of the matter. (see: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **came down from Jerusalem to Antioch** - There was an elevation change from Jerusalem to Antioch. Many also recognized the great importance of the Temple in Jerusalem. Regardless of elevation, people go up to Jerusalem and the Temple, and down when going away.
  * **a great famine would occur** - "a great shortage of food would occur"
  * **over all the world** - This was an exaggeration refering to the Roman Empire. AT: "throughout the Roman Empire." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
