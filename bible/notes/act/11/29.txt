===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **disciple**  - Anyone who obeys and follows Jesus is a disciple of Jesus.
  * **as each one was able**  - The richer people sent more; the poorer people sent less.
  * **They did this; they sent money**  - "the believers in Antioch donated money and they sent the money…."
  * **by the hand of Barnabas and Saul **- This is an expression for "Barnabas and Saul personally brought the money to the church elders in Jerusalem." (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
