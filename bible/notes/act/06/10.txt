===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The background information that began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:06:08|6:8]] continues through verse 10. 
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "We" refers only to the men they persuaded to lie.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **General Information:** - The word "they" refers back to the people from the synagogue of the freemen in [[:en:bible:notes:act:06:08|6:9]].

  * **not able to stand against** - This phrase means they could not prove false what he said. AT: "could not argue against" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Spirit** - "Holy Spirit"
  * **some men to say** - They were given money to give false testimony. AT: "some men to lie and say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **blasphemous words against** - "bad things about"
