===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Peter, fastening his eyes upon him, with John, said** - Both Peter and John looked at the man, but only Peter spoke.
  * **fastening his eyes upon him** - Possible meanings are 1) "looking directly at him" or 2) "looking intently at him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **The lame man looked at them** - Here the word "looked" means to pay attention to something. AT: "The lame man paid close attention to them"
  * **Silver and gold** - These words refer to money. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **what I do have** - It is understood that Peter has the ability to heal the man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **In the name of Jesus Christ** - Here the word "name" refers to power and authority. AT: "With the authority of Jesus Christ" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
