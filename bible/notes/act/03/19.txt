===== Translation Notes: =====

Peter continues his speech to the Jews that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:03:11|3:13]].

  * **turn again**  - "turn back to God"
  * **so that there may come periods of refreshing from the presence of the Lord**  - "so that the Lord may strengthen you"
  * **blotted out**  - "erased" or "crossed out"
