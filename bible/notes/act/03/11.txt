===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the porch that is called Solomon's** - "Solomon's Porch." This was a covered walkway that consisted of rows of pillars that supported a roof, and which people named after king Solomon.
  * **greatly marveling** - "extremely surprised"
  * **When Peter saw this** - "When Peter saw the people running to them"
  * **You men of Israel** - "Fellow Israelites" (UDB). Peter was addressing the crowd. The word "men" in this context includes everyone present.
  * **why do you marvel?** - Peter asks this question to emphasize that they should not be surprised by what had happened. AT: "you should not be surprised" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Why do you  fix your eyes on us, as if we had made him to walk by our own power or godliness?** - Peter asks this question to emphasize that the people should not think that he and John had healed the man by their own abilities. This can be stated as two sentences. AT: "Do not fix your eyes on us. We did not make him walk by our own power or godliness." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **fix your eyes on us** - This means that they looked intently at them without stopping. AT: "stare at us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **as if we had made him to walk** - Here the word "we" refers to Peter and John but not to the crowd that Peter is addressing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
