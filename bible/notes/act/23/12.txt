===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - While Paul was in the prison at the fortress, the disbelieving religious Jews vow to kill him.

  * **formed a conspiracy** - "organized a group with a shared purpose" here, to kill Paul.
  * **called a curse down upon themselves with an oath** - The word"curse" can be re-stated. It can also be made explicit what would cause them to be cursed. AT: "asked God to curse them if they did not do what they promised" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **forty men** - "40 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **who formed this conspiracy** - "who made this plan" or "who planned to kill Paul"
