===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul the prisoner called me to him** - "Paul the prisoner asked me to come and talk with him"
  * **this young man** - Since the chief captain took the young man by the hand suggests that Paul's nephew is younger than older. Possibly 12 to 15 years old.
