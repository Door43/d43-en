===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - James begins to speak to the apostles and elders.

  * **After they stopped speaking** - "After Barnabas and Paul finished speaking"
  * **Brothers** - This refers to "fellow believers."
  * **in order to take from them a people** - "so that he might choose from among them a people"
  * **for his name** - Here "name" refers to God. AT: "for himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
