===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **after they stopped speaking** - "After Barnabas and Paul finished speaking"
  * **God first visited** - "God concerned himself" or "God looked favorably"
  * **a people - **"a group of people"
  * **take from them** - "choose from among them"
  * **for his name** - This is an expression for himself. This can be translated as "for himself." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
