===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then there arose a sharp disagreement** - The abstract noun "disagreement" can be stated as the verb "disagree." AT: "They strongly disagreed with each other" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **that they separated** - "that Barnabas and Paul separated"
  * **and sailed away** - This means they both sailed away. AT: "and they sailed away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis|Ellipsis]])
  * **after he was entrusted by the brothers to the grace of the Lord** - To entrust to someone means to place the care and responsibility for someone or something to another person. This can be stated in active form. AT: "after the believers in Antioch prayed for the Lord to take care of Paul and show kindness to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **And he went** - It is implied that Silas was with Paul. AT: "And they went" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **went through Syria and Cilicia** - These are provinces in Asia Minor, north of the island of Cyprus.
  * **strengthening the churches** - Helping someone to trust in Jesus even more is spoken of as if a person were making someone physically stronger. Also, "churches" stands for the group of believers. AT: "helping the community of believers to trust even more in Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
