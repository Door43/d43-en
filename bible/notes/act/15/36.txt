===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let us**  - "I suggest we"
  * **visit the brothers**  - "visit" means to "care for" or "see how one can help."
  * **see how they are**  - "learn about the current condition of the brothers and how they are holding on to the truth given to them"
  * **take with them John who was called Mark**  - "take John, who was also called Mark, along with Paul and himself (Barnabas)"
