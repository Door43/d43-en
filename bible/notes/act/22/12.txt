===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues to speak.

  * **devout man according to the law** - Ananias was very serious about following God's law.
  * **well spoken of** - "with a good reputation among others"
  * **In that very hour** - “at that instant” or “instantly.” This is an idiom which means something happens immediately. (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
