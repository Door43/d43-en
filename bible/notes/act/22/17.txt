===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues to speak.

  * **I fell into a trance** - "A vision appeared to me" or "God gave men a vision"
  * **I saw him say to me** - "I saw Jesus as he said to me"
  * **they will not accept** - "those who live in Jerusalem will not accept"
