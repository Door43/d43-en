===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As Paul and Barnabas left** - "When Paul and Barnabas were leaving"
  * **begged them that they might** - "begged them to"
  * **these same words** - Here "words" refers to the message that Paul had spoken. AT: "this same message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **When the synagogue meeting ended** - Possible meanings are 1) this restates "As Paul and Barnabas left" in verse 42 or 2) Paul and Barnabas left the meeting before it ended and this occurs later.
  * **proselytes** - These were non-Jewish people who converted to Judaism.
  * **who spoke to them and urged them** - "and Paul and Barnabas spoke to those people and urged them"
  * **to continue in the grace of God** - It is implied that they believed Paul's message that Jesus was the Messiah. AT: "to continue to trust that God kindly forgives people's sins because of what Jesus did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
