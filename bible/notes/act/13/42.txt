===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As Paul and Barnabas left** - "When Paul and Barnabas were leaving"
  * **begged them that they might** - "begged them to"
  * **proselytes** - these were non-Jewish people who converted to Judaism.
  * **who spoke to them and urged them** - "and Paul and Barnabas spoke to those people and urged them"
  * **to continue in the grace of God** - "to continue trusting in the grace of God"
