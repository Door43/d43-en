===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When he…**  - "he" is referring to Jesus.
  * **with them…**  - "them" is referring to the 11 apostles.
  * **he commanded them not to leave Jerusalem**  - "he ordered them to remain in Jerusalem". This can also be translated with a direct quote as in the UDB. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **promise of the Father**  - This is a reference to the Holy Spirit.
  * **baptized with water...baptized in the Holy Spirit**  - Jesus contrasted John's baptism with water, to God's baptism which will be with the Holy Spirit.
  * **John indeed baptized with water**  - For languages that require an object with "baptize," this can be translated as "John baptized people with water" or "John baptized them with water." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_verbs]])
  * **you shall be baptized**  - AT:: "God will baptize you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
