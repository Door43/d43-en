===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They prayed and said** - Here the word "They" refers to all of the believers, but it was probably one of the apostles who spoke these words. AT: "The believers prayed together and one of the apostles said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **You, Lord, know the hearts of all people** - Here the word "hearts" refers to the thoughts and motives. AT: "You, Lord, know the thoughts and motives of everyone." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to take the place in this ministry and apostleship** - Here the word "apostleship" defines what kind of "ministry" this is. AT: "to take Judas' place in this apostolic ministry" or "to take Judas' place in serving as an apostle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **from which Judas transgressed** - Here the word "transgressed" means that Judas turned aside from, or stopped performing, this ministry. AT: "from which Judas turned aside"
  * **to go to his own place** - This phrase refers to Judas' death and likely to his judgment after death. AT: "to go where he belongs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **They cast lots for them** - The apostles cast lots to decide between Joseph and Matthias.
  * **the lot fell to Matthias** - The lot indicated that Matthias was the one to replace Judas.
  * **he was numbered with the eleven apostles** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the believers considered him to be an apostle like the other eleven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
