===== Translation Notes: =====

Peter continues his speech to the believers that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:01:15|1:16]].

  * **It is necessary, therefore** - Peter is about to express why he quoted the verses from Psalms and what they should do about it.
  * **That the men who accompanied us ...one of these must be a witness with us of his resurrection**  - Peter defined the qualifications for the man who is to replace Judas as an apostle.
  * **They put forward two men**  - As they looked to replace Judas, they found two men who qualified.
  * **Joseph called Barsabbas who was also named Justus** - Joseph was also known by the names Barsabbas and Justus.
