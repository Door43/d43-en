===== Translation Notes: =====

Peter continues his speech to the believers that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:01:15|1:16]].

  * **his evil act**  - "his evil act of guiding Jesus' enemies to him." This makes explicit what "evil act" refers to. (See [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]])
  * **Then falling head first his body burst wide open and all his bowels poured out.**  - On this plot of land, Judas had a severe head first fall that caused his body to burst open. Other scriptures mention he hung himself.
