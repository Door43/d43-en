===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the list of leaders of the tabernacle work started in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:01|1 Chronicles 25:2]].

  * **Bukkiah ... Mahazioth** - These are names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
