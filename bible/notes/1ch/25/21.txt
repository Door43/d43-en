===== translationNotes: =====
  * **General Information:** - This continues the list begun in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:09|25:9]].

This shows the order in which the families were chosen. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
