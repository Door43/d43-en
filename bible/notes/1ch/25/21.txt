===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the list begun in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:09|1 Chronicles 25:9]].

  * **fourtenth ... seventeenth** - This shows the order in which the families were chosen.  AT: "number 14...number 15...number 16...number 17" (See: Ordinal Numbers and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Mattithiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:01|1 Chronicles 25:3]].
  * **twelve persons** - "12 people" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Jerimoth** - This is another spelling for "Uzziel" in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|1 Chronicles 25:4]].
  * **Hananiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|1 Chronicles 25:4]].
  * **Joshbekashah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|1 Chronicles 25:4]].
