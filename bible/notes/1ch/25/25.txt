===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the list begun in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:09|1 Chronicles 25:9]].

  * **eighteenth ... twenty-first** - This shows the order in which the families were chosen.  AT: "number 18...number 19...number 20...number 21" (See: Ordinal Numbers and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Hanani** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|1 Chronicles 25:4]].
