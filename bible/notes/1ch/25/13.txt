===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the list begun in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:09|1 Chronicles 25:9]].

  * **sixth ... ninth** - This shows the order in which the families were chosen.  AT: "number 6...number 7...number 8...number 9" (See: Ordinal Numbers and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Bukkiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|1 Chronicles 25:4]].
  * **twelve persons** - "12 people" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Jesharelah** - This is another spelling for "Asharelah" in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:01|1 Chronicles 25:2]].
  * **Jeshaiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:01|1 Chronicles 25:3]].
  * **Mattaniah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|1 Chronicles 25:4]].
