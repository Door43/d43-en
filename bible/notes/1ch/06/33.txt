===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were those** - "These were the musicians"
  * **Kohathites** - This is the name of a people group, the descendants of Kohath ([[:en:bible:notes:1ch:06:01|6:1]]). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **going back in time** - This means the list is going in order from the most recent to the oldest.
  * **Heman ... Jeroham ... Eliel ... Toah ... Zuph ... Mahath** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Elkanah ... Amasai** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:06:25|6:25]].
