===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **To the rest of Kohath's descendants were given by lot ten cities** - AT: "The rest of Kohath's descendants gained control of 10 cities when the lots were cast" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Gershom's descendants in their various clans were given thirteen cities** - AT: "The clans of Gershom's descendants gained 13 cities" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Kohath ... Gorsham** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **from the half the tribe of Manasseh** - The tribe had been divided into two equal groups.
