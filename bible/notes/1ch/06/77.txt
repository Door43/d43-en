===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]

  * **The rest ... descendants received from the tribe of Zebulun** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "The tribe of Zebulun gave the rest ... descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Merari's** - Translate "Merari" as in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:06:01|6:1]].
  * **Rimmono ... and Tabor ... Bezer ... Jahzah ... Kedemoth ... Mephaath** - These are all names of towns. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **pasturelands** - grassy land where animals eat. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:05:16|5:16]].
  * **and from the tribe of Reuben** - The information in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:06:77|6:78-79]] has been rearranged so its meaning can be more easily understood. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_versebridge]])
  * **from the tribe of Reuben** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the tribe of Reuben gave these" or "they also received these from the tribe of Reuben" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
