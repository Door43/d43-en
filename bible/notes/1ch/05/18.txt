===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Reubenites** - This refers to the people of the tribe of Rueben. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gadites** - This refers to the people of the tribe of Gad.
  * **forty-four thousand soldiers**  - "44,000 soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Hagrites** - This refers to the descendants of Ishmael.
  * **Jetur, Naphish, Nodab** - These are the names of the regions of Gilead.
