===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Reubenites**  - This refers to the people from the tribe of Rueben. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Gadites**  - This refers to the people from the tribe of Gad.
  * **forty-four thousand soldiers**  - "44,000 soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **who carried shield and sword, and who drew the bow**  - The soldiers are described as skilled in warfare by the weapons they carried. AT: "They were all trained to fight well in battles." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]][[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis|Ellipsis]] )
  * **Hagrites…Jetur…Naphish…Nodab**  - These are the names people groups.
