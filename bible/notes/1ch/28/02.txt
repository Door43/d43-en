===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my brothers and my people**  - words used to begin a formal talk to a group of people (in this case, David was talking to all of his officials and leaders, and to all of the nation of Israel). (see: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a footstool for our God**  - Symbolically, this is a picture of David wanting to build a temple that would have a place for the ark of the covenant - and that God would always be present in that place in the temple. (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a man of war and have shed blood **- David was not only the king, but he was the commander of the army and had killed many enemies. (see: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
