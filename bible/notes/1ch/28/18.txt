===== translationNotes: =====

  * **refined gold** - "purified gold" or "extra valuable gold"
  * **of the gold for the design** - The refers to the weight of the gold. AT: "the weight of the gold for the design" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **I have put all this in writing** - This is an idiom. AT: "I wrote all of this down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **gave me to understand regarding the design** - This refers to the detail for the design of the temple. AT: "helped me to understand the details for his temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
