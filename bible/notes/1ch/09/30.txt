===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Mattithiah ... Shallum** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Korahites ... Kohathites** - These are names of people groups.
  * **bread of the presence**  - See the Important Term "bread" for the specific definition of "bread of the presence." The "bread for the offering on the altar" was different than the "bread of the presence."
