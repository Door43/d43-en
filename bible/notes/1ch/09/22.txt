===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All those who were chosen as gatekeepers at the entrances numbered 212** - AT: "Two hundred twelve men guarded the entrances" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Their names were recorded in the people's records in their villages** - AT: "The people's records in their villages included the names of these men."
  * **gatekeepers were posted** - AT: "men guarded the entrances" 
  * **seer - **  seer was another word for prophet
  * **tabernacle**  - in this verse, "tabernacle" refers to the tent form of the house of Yahweh (God) during the time that David was king (prior to the construction of the permanent temple by Solomon)
