===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they were free from work**  - AT: "they did not have to do other work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **had to carry out their assigned tasks**  - AT: "had to complete their assigned task"
  * **day and night**  - This means "at all times" and can be translated using a phrase or word from your language or culture that gives the same meaning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism|Merism]])
  * **These were family leaders among the Levites, as listed in their genealogical record**  - AT: "The family history lists included the names of these Levite family leaders"
