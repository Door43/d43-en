===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Some of them** - "Some of the guards"
  * **they counted the articles when they were brought in and when they were taken out** - AT: "they counted the articles that people took out to use, and they counted the articles when people brought them back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **utensil** - a simple device used for doing kitchen tasks or eating
  * **flour** - powder made from grain that is used for cooking
  * **spice** - usually a powder or seed from a dried plant that is used to add flavor to food
