===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in charge of them** - "in charge of the guards"
  * **temple, the "tent of meeting"**  - Yahweh was present with His people in the "tent of meeting" just as He was present with His people in the temple.  These were both places that Yahweh met with His people.
