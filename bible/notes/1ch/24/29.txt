===== translationNotes: =====

  * **From Kish: The son of Kish** - This is written in the text as a title, but could be stated in a more complete form. AT:  "These are the sons who were descended from Kish"
  * **Kish ... Mushi** - Translate the names of these men as in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:21|23:21]].
  * **Jerahmeel ... Mahli ... Eder ... Jerimoth** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ahimelech** - Translate the name of this man as in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:18:14|18:16]].
