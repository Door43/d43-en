===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins the list of names of the other sons of Levi. This list will end in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:24:29|24:30]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])


  * **Amram** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:06:01|6:3]].
  * **Shebuel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:15|23:16]].
  * **Amram's descendants included Isshiah** - Amram was the father of Aaron, who was the father of Eleazar. Eleazar was the father of Rehabiah who was the father of Isshiah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Izhar's descendants**  - Izhar was a son of Kohath.
  * **Izhar ... Shelomith** - See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:15|23:18]].
