===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins the list of names of the other sons of Levi. This list will end in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:24:29|1 Chronicles 24:30]].


  * **Amram** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:06:01|1 Chronicles 6:3]].
  * **Shubael** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:15|1 Chronicles 23:16]].
  * **Jehdeiah** - This is the name of a man.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Amram's descendants** - Amram was the father of Aaron, who fathered Eleazar. Eleazar was the father of Rehabiah, who fathered Isshiah. (UDB 24:21)
  * **Isshiah** - This is the name of a man.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Izhar's descendants**  - Izhar was a son of Kohath. (UDB 24:22)
  * **Izhar...Shelomith** - See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:15|1 Chronicles 23:18]].
  * **Jahath** - This is the name of a man.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
