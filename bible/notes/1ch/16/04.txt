===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zechariah, Jaaziel, Shemiramoth, Jehiel, Mattithiah, Eliab, Benaiah, Obed-Edom, and Jeiel ... Benaiah and Jahaziel** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **second to him** - This means next to him in authority and position. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **cymbals** - These are two thin, round metal plates that are hit together to make a loud sound. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:13:07|13:8]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
