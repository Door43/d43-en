===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let the sea roar** - AT: "The sea will make a very loud shout." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **that which fills it** - AT: "the sea creatures"
  * **let the trees in the forest shout for joy** - This speaks about the trees as if they were people who could shout for joy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
