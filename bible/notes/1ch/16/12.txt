===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Recall** - "Remember"
  * **his miracles and** - The understood verb may be supplied. AT: "remember his miracles and" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **decrees from his mouth** - Here "mouth" refers to the things that Yahweh spoke. AT: "decrees that he has spoken" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you descendants of Israel his servant, you people of Jacob, his chosen ones** - These phrases share similar meanings and are used for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **His decrees are on all the earth** - Here "all the earth" refers to all the people of the earth. AT: "His laws are for all the people of the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
