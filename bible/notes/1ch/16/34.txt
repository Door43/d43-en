===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from the other nations** - Here "the other nations" represents the people in those nations. AT: "from the people of other nations" or "from the armies of other nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **give thanks to your holy name** - Here Yahweh is referred to by his "holy name." AT: "give thanks to you" or "give thanks to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
