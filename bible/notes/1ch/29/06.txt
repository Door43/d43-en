===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]

  * **Then freewill offerings were made by the leaders ... and by the officials over the king's work.** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Then the leaders ... and the officials over the king's work made freewill offerings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the commanders of thousands and hundreds** - The army had commanders who lead groups of soldiers of different sizes. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:13:01|13|1]]. AT: "the commanders of groups of 1,000 soldiers and the commanders of groups of 100 soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the officials over the king's work** - Here the word "over" is used as an idiom. AT: "the officials in charge of the king's work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **five thousand talents ... and 100,000 talents of iron** - "5,000 talents and 10,000 darics of gold, 10,000 talents of silver, 18,000 talents of bronze, and 100,000 talents of iron." These may be written in modern measurements. AT: "165 metric tons and 84 kilograms of gold, 330 metric tons of silver, 600 metric tons of bronze, and 3,300 metric tons of iron" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
