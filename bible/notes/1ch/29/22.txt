===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **before Yahweh**  - in the presence of Yahweh (see: Idiom)
  * **They made Solomon, David's son, king a second time **- Some time earlier, David had Zadok the priest anoint Solomon to make certain that Solomon would be the son that would succeed David.  This time, Solomon was officially anointed as king before all the Israelites.
