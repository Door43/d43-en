===== translationNotes: =====

  * **before Yahweh** - This is an idiom. AT: "in honor of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a second time** - This means that they anointed him and declared him as king. The first time is described in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:01|23:1]].
  * **anointed him with Yahweh's authority to be ruler** - To do something with "Yahweh's authority" means to do it on his behalf and with his approval. AT: "anointed him to rule over Israel on Yahweh's behalf" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Then Solomon sat on Yahweh's throne as king instead of David his father** - Here the throne of Israel is referred to as "Yahweh's throne," to emphasize that the people of Israel are Yahweh's people. Sitting on the throne represents ruling as king. AT: "So Solomon sat on the throne, instead of his father David, as king over Yahweh's people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
