  * **Gath**  - This is a city of the Philistines.
  * **six**  - "6" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **Rephaim**  - A people group who are giants.
  * **Shimmah**  - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
