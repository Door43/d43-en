===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Abinadab ... Uzza and Ahio** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **David and all Israel** - Here the word "all" is a generalization. AT: "David and all of the Israelites who were present" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **singing with stringed instruments** - "singing while playing stringed instruments"
  * **tambourines** - hand drums with pieces of metal around the side that sound when the instruments are shaken (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **cymbals** - two thin, round metal plates that are hit together to make a loud sound (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
