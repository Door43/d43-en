===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Over the division** - "In charge of the division" 
  * **the second month** - "the 2nd month." This is the second month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of April and the first part of May on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Dodai ... Mikloth ... Ammizabad** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ahoah** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:08:01|8:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **In his division** - "In his group of soldiers"
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the third month** - "the 3rd month." This is the third month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of May and the first part of June on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Jehoiada** - Translate this man's name the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:11:22|11:22]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the thirty** - "the 30." This refers to "David's 30 mightiest soldiers." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **over the thirty** - This is an idiom. AT: "in charge of the 30" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
