===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The commander for the seventh month was Helez the Pelonite, from the people of Ephraim** - "Helez, the Pelonite, from the people of Ephraim, was the commander for the seventh month"
  * **the seventh month** - "the 7th month." This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of September and the first part of October on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Helez ... Sibbekai ... Abi Ezer** - See how you translated the names of these men in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:11:26|11:27-29]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Pelonite ... Hushathite ... Anathothite** - See how you translated the names of these clans in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:11:26|11:27-29]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the eighth month** - "the 8th month." This is the eighth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of October and the first part of November on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]]) 
  * **Zerah** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:02:03|2:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the ninth month** - "the 9th month." This is the ninth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part November and the first part December on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
