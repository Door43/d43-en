===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is the list** - AT: "These are the names"
  * **in various ways** - "in different ways" or "in many ways"
  * **Each military division** - "Each group"
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **throughout the year** - AT: "all year long."
  * **first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Jashobeam son of Zabdiel** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in charge** - "the leader"
