===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the tenth month** - "the 10th month." This is the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of December and the first part of January on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Maharai** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:11:30|11:30]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Netophah ... Pirathon** - These are names of places.
  * **Zerah** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:02:03|2:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the eleventh month** - "the 11th month." This is the eleventh month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of January and the first part of February on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the twelfth month** - "the 12th month." This is the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of February and the first part of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Heldai ... Othniel** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
