===== translationNotes: =====

  * **tenth month** - This is the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Maharai ... Zerah ... Heldai ... Othniel** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Netophah ... Pirathon** - These are names of places. It is during the last part December and the first part January on Western calendars. 
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **eleventh month** - This is the eleventh month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of January and the first part of February on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **twelfth month** - This is the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of February and the first part of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
