===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Azmaveth ... Adiel ... Jonathan ... Uzziah ... Ezri ... Kelub ... Zabdi** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in charge of** - "responsible for guarding"
  * **was over** - This is an idiom. AT: "was in charge of"
  * **fortified towers** - "strong towers"
  * **plowed the land** - This means to dig into or break up the dirt before planting.
  * **Ramathite** - This is the name of a person from the city of Ramah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shiphmite** - This is the name of a person from the city of Shepham. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **wine cellars** - These are the parts of a building that are below the ground and used to store wine.
