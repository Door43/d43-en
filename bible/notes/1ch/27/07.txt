===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The commander for the fourth month was Asahel brother of Joab** - "Asahel, brother of Joab, was the commander for the fourth month."
  * **fourth month** - This is the fourth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of June and the first part of July on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Asahel ... Ikkesh** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **his division** - "his military group"
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **fifth month** - This is the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of July and the first part of August on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **sixth month** - This is the sixth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of August and the first part of September on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Tekoa** - This is the name of a place.
