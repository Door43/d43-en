===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Over the** - This is an idiom. AT: "In charge of the" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **sycamore** - This is a type of tree. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **Baal Hanan ... Shitrai ... Shaphat ... Adlai** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Geder ... Sharon** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **that were pastured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that ate grass in the fields" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
