===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **you, your** - These pronouns are plural in 22:18-19. (See: Forms of 'you')
    * **has given you peace on every side**  - AT: "has arranged to give you peace from war in every place around Israel."
    * **the house built for Yahweh's name**  - AT: "the temple you (plural) will build to honor Yahweh" (See: Metonymy)
