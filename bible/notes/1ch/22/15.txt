===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - David continues speaking to Solomon.

  * **stonecutters, masons** - These are both workers who cut stone and prepare it for builders to use in walls and buildings.
  * **carpenters** - These are persons who work with wood.
  * **skillful craftsmen without number of every kind** - Here "without number" is an exaggeration to emphasize that there were a large number of them. AT: "a very large number of every kind of skillful craftsmen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **may Yahweh be with you** - This is an idiom that implies that Yahweh will help Solomon be successful in the project. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
