===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the commanders over thousands** - Possible meanings are 1) the word "thousands" represents the exact amount of soldiers that each of these commanders and led. AT: "the commanders of 1,000 soldiers" or 2) the word translated as "thousands" does not represent an exact numbers but is the name of a large military division. AT: "the commanders of large military divisions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Obed Edom** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
