===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verses 16-19 describes a second battle when the Arameans who fled from Joab in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:19:14|19:15]] were reinforced by other Arameans and attacked Israel again.

  * **the Arameans saw** - What the Arameans understood is spoken of as what they saw. AT: "the Arameans understood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sent for reinforcements** - "requested more soliders"
  * **Shophak ... Hadadezer** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **When David was told this** - This can be stated in active form. AT: When David's messengers told him that a larger Aramean army was coming" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he gathered ... He arranged** - David did not do these things alone. His official and officers helped him. AT: "David and his officials gathered ... David and his officers arranged" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **all Israel** - This is a generalization. Not every person in Israel came to fight. AT: "a very large number of Israelites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **arranged** - "organized"
  * **they fought him** - Here "him" refers to David's soldiers, in addition to David. AT: "the Arameans fought David and his soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
