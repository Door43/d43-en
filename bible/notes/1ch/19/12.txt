===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Be strong ... show ourselves to be strong** - "Be brave ... behave bravely"
  * **for our people and for the cities of our God** - "for the sake of our people and for the cities of our God" or "to protect our people and the cities of our God"
  * **what is good in his eyes** - Here sight represents judgment or evaluation. AT: "what he considers to be good" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
