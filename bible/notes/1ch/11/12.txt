===== translationNotes: =====

  * **After him** - "After Jashobeam"
  * **Eleazar ... Dodo** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the Ahohite** - This is the name of a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Pas Dammim** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **They stood in the middle of the field** - "David and Eleazar stood in the middle of the field"
  * **cut down the Philistines** - This means that they killed the Philistine soldiers with their swords. AT: "killed the Philistines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
