===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Ashterath, Mithna,Aroer - These are names of towns.
  * chief - This word means leader.
  * Adina,Shiza,Hannan, Maacah,Joshaphat, Uzzia, Shama, Jeiel, Hotham - These are all people's names. (See: Translation Names)
