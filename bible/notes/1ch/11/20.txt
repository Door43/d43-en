===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Abishai** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:02:16|2:16]].
  * **captain over the Three** - This means Abishai was the leader of the three who went and got water for David.
  * **three hundred** - "300 men" or "300 warriors" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **He is mentioned along with the Three** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "People often mentioned him when they spoke of the Three" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Of the Three, he was given double honor and became** - This can be stated in active form. Possible meanings are 1) that he received double the honor that the Three received. AT: "People gave him twice as much honor as they gave the Three and he became" or 2) the Three honored him more than they honored others. AT: "The Three honored him more than they honored others and he became" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
