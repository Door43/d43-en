===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Take it as your own** - This implies that David should take the land without paying for it. AT: "Take it as a gift" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **what is good in your sight** - David's understanding is described as his sight. AT: "whatever you decide to do with it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **threshing sledges** - These are wooden sleds with rocks or metal fitted underneath, dragged by oxen over the grain on the threshing floor to separate the grain from its stalks. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **full price** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:21:21|21:22]].
