===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hebron** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:12|23:12]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jerijah** - This is another way to spell "Jeriah." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:19|23:19]].
  * **counted from the lists** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "their names were on the lists" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **In the fortieth year of the reign of David** - "When David had been king for 40 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **men of ability** - "capable men" (UDB)
  * **Jazer** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **2,700 relatives** - "two thousand seven hundred family members" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
