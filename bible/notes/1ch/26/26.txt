===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thousands and hundreds** - "more than a 1000 men and more than a 100 men" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **plunder** - These are things that were brought back by the army after victory.
  * **Kish** - The name of Saul's father.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Abner son of Ner and Joab son of Zeruiah**  - These men were commanders in David's army.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Everything that was dedicated** - "Everything that was dedicated by previous leaders of the Israelites" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
