===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This begins the list of the gatekeepers.

  * **divisions** - "groups"
  * **Korahites** - See how you translated the name of this people group in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:09:17|19:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Meshelemiah ... Zechariah** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:09:20|9:21]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Kore** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:09:17|9:19]].  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Asaph** - This is an alternative spelling to "Ebiasaph" in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:09:17|9:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jediael ... Zebadiah ... Jathniel ... Elam ... Jehohanan ... Eliehoenai** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the second ... third ... fourth ... seventh** - "the 2nd ... the 3rd ... the 4th ... the 7th" or "number 2 ... number 3 ... number 4 ... number 7." This shows the order in which the sons were born. If it is more natural in your language, you may say "the next" for each son. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
