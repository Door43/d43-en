===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **To Obed Edom was assigned the south gate **- AT: "The south gate was assigned to Obed Edom," (see: Passive/Active)
  * **Shallecheth**  - This is the same as **Shalleketh**  in the UDB. It is a spelling variation.
  * **Watches were established for each family. **- Watches were specific periods of time during which a family member or group would be responsible to watch over or guard a specific gate or area.
