===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ziklag** - This is the name of a place.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **while he was still banished from the presence of**  - AT: "during the time when he could not be in the presence of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **Kish** - This is the name of a man.
  * **They were armed with bows and could use both the right hand and the left** - AT: "Their weapons included bows, and they could use their right hands and left hands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **sling** - to throw an object with a forceful swinging motion
  * **Benjaminites** - This is the name of the tribe of Benjamin's descendants.
