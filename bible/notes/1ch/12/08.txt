  * **They**  - Refers to Gadites.
  * **Faces as fierce as the faces of lions**  - This is another way to show how brave the Gadites were and how they scared thir enemies. (see: Simile)
  * **as swift as gazelles**  - This tells that Gadites could run and climb really fast. (See: Simile)
