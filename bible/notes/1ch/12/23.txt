===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This begins the list of the number of men who joined David from each tribe. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **to turn the kingdom of Saul over to him** - The men making David king in place of Saul is spoken of as if they gave possession of Saul's kingdom to David. AT: "to make David king in place of Saul" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **carried out Yahweh's word** - "made Yahweh's word true" or "fulfilled Yahweh's word"
  * **6,800, armed for war** - "six thousand eight hundred, armed for war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **From the Simeonites** - "From Simeon" or "From the tribe of Simeon"
  * **7,100 fighting men** - "seven thousand one hundred fighting men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
