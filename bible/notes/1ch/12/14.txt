===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The least led a hundred** - This means that the smallest group of the army that a leader lead was 100. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]) 
  * **the greatest led a thousand** - This means that the largest group of the army that a leader lead was 1,000.
  * **first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on Western calendars. It is at the beginning of the spring season when the late rains come. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
