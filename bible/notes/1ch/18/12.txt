===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Abishai, Zeruiah**  - these are names of people (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names)]]
  * **18,000**  -"eighteen thousand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **Valley of Salt**  - name of a place
  * **garrisons**  - "military bases or forts and the soldiers that work there".
