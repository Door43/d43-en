===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The father of Gibeon, Jeiel, whose wife’s name was Maacah, lived in Gibeon**  - AT: "Jeiel, Gibeon's father, lived in Gibeon. His wife's name was Maacah."
  * **His firstborn** - The word "his" refers to Jeiel.
  * **Jeiel, Abdon, Zur, Kish, ..., Zecher** - These are names of men.  (See: Translate Names)
  * **Maacah** - This is the name of a woman.
