===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - All of the names here except Maakah and Gibeon are the names of men. Maakah is the name of a woman, and Gibeon is the name of a town. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **The father of Gibeon, Jeiel, whose wife's name was Maakah, lived in Gibeon** - Here "father of" refers to Jeiel's status as the leading person in the city of Gibeon. AT: "Jeiel, the leader of Gibeon, lived in Gibeon. His wife's name was Maakah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **His first-born** - "Jeiel's first son"
