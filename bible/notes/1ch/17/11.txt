===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will come about…**  - God is speaking. Nathan will give this message to King David.
  * “I” refers to God.
  * “You” refers to David.
  * **when your days are fulfilled for you to go to your fathers**  - when you die (See: [[
en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism ]])
  * **He will build me a house** - "He will build a permanent house for God's name instead of a tent".
