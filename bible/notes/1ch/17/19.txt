===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your servant's sake** - AT: "my benefit"
  * **you rescued from Egypt** - AT: "rescued from slavery in Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **great and awesome deeds** - The words "great" and "awesome" mean basically the same thing and emphasize the intensity of greatness. AT: "very awesome deeds." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
