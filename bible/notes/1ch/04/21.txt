===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Shelah…Er…Lecah…Laadah…Mareshah…Jokim…Joash…Saraph**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **originator**  - a person who began something (in this case, a series of families)
  * **linen**  - a type of fabric
  * **potters**  - people who make containers out of clay
  * **Beth Ashbea … Cozeba … Netaim … Gederah**  - These are the names of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
