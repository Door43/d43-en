===== translationNotes: =====

  * **five hundred men** - "500 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Pelatiah ... Neariah ... Rephaiah ... Uzziel ... Ishi** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the rest of the Amalekite refugees** - "the remaining Amalekite refugees"
  * **refugees** - people who are forced to leave their home country
  * **to this day** - "from then until now." This refers to the day when the author was writing this account.
