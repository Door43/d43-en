===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]]
  * **bore him Perez and Zerah**  - AT: "gave birth to Perez and Zerah"
  * **five sons**  - AT: "5 sons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **in the sight of Yahweh** - AT: "according to Yahweh"
  * **Yahweh killed him** - This means that Yahweh allowed Er to be killed.
  * **daughter-in-law** - This is a reference to the wife of his son.
