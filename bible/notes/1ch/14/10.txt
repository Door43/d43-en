===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **them, they**  - Refers to Philistines.
  * **He, my- **  Refers to David.
  * **Baal Perazim**  - This is the name a place.(See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]] )
  * **by my hand**  - This refers to David's resources. AT: "using my army" (See:[[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metonymy|Metonymy]] )
  * **like a bursting flood of water**  - This is another way to tell that David's army completely overwhelmed and won over the Philitines. AT: " like a rushing wall of water that knocks down everything" (See: [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:simile|Simile]] )
  * **vs 12 that they should be burned**  - Refers to the false gods to be burned.
