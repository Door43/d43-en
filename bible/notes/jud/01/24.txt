===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **24 Now to him that is able to keep you from stumbling, - **"God is able to keep you from falling down."
  * **and to place you before the presence of his glory without blemish in exceeding joy, - "**He places you before the presence of his glory without stain and sin with very great joy."
  * **to the only God our Savior, through Jesus Christ our Lord, be glory, majesty, dominion and power, before all time, and now, and for evermore. Amen. - **"To the only God our Savior, through Jesus Christ our Lord, we give glory, majesty, dominion (or "superiority") and power, before all time, and now, and forever. Amen."
