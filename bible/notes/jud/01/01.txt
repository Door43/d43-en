===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jude** - See "How to Translate Names"
  * **Jude, a servant of** - Alternate translation: "I am Jude, a servant of "
  * **and brother of James,** - "and a brother of James." James (not the apostle) and Jude are half brothers of Jesus. Period after "James."
  * **to those who are called,** - Alternate translation:"I am writing to all whom God has called to him(self)." (UDB)
  * **beloved in God the Father,** - God the Father loves you 
  * **and kept for Jesus Christ:** - "and protected now that they believe in Jesus Christ."
  * **mercy to you and peace and love be multiplied** - Alternate translation: "may mercy, peace and love be increased many times for you." ("You" in this verse refers to all Christians.)
