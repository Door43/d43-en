===== translationNotes: =====

  * **then the valuation of it will be made in proportion to the amount of seed required to plant it** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "then he will estimate its value based on the amount of seed a person would need to plant it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a homer of barley will be valued at** - "The value of one homer of barley will be"
  * **homer** - A homer is 220 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **fifty shekels** - A shekel is 11 grams. "50 shekels" or "550 grams" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
