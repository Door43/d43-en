====== Leviticus 27 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Dedicated to Yahweh ==

This chapter records the manner in which people make vows of dedication to Yahweh. There are many reasons why a person would dedicate something to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:vow]])
