===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **nothing dedicated to Yahweh by a man from anything he has, whether human or animal, or his family land, may be sold or redeemed. Every dedicated thing is holy to Yahweh** - AT: "if someone dedicates anything he has to Yahweh, whether a human, an animal, or his family land, no one may sell it or redeem it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to be destroyed** - "to be executed"
