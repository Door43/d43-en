===== translationNotes: =====

  * **nothing a man sets apart from anything he has, whether human or animal, or his family land, may be sold or redeemed. Everything that is devoted is holy to Yahweh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "if someone dedicates anything he has to Yahweh, whether a human, an animal, or his family land, no one may sell it or redeem it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **No ransom may be paid for the person who is devoted for destruction. That person must be put to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "If Yahweh determines that a person should die because of his sin, no one may pay to set him free" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
