===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone has a skin disease.

  * **the disease** - This refers to the disease on the person's head or chin.
  * **then the priest must pronounce him clean** - Here "him" refers to the person with the disease.
