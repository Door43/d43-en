===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone has a skin disease.

  * **then he must be shaved, but the diseased area must not be shaved** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the person must shave the hair near the sore but not the hair on the sore" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
