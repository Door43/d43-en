===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the life of each creature is its blood** - This means that the blood is enables the creature to be alive. AT: "each creature is able to live because of its blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Whoever eats it must be cut off** - A person being excluded from his community is spoken of as if he had been cut off from his people, as one would cut a piece of cloth or cut a branch from a tree. This can be translated in active form. AT: "Whoever eats blood may no longer live among his people" or "You must separate from his people anyone who eats blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
