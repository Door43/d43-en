===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **that has been torn by wild animals**  - AT: "that wild animals have killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **is native born**  - "is an Israelite"
  * **until the evening**  - "until sunset"
  * **then he must carry his guilt**  - AT: "then he is responsible for his own guilt" or "then I will punish him for his sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
