===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **look at you with favor** - "I will show you favor" or "I will bless you" (UDB)
  * **make you fruitful and multiply you** - Both phrases mean the same thing. They emphasize that Yahweh will give them many children. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **You will eat food stored a long time** - "You will have enough food stored to eat for a long time"
