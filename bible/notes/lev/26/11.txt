===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will place my tabernacle among you, and I will not detest you.**  - This means that Yahweh will make his dwelling place (tabernacle) among the people of Israel; by doing so, he is letting the people know that his presence is among them, and that he does not despise or hate them.
