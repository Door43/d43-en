===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will place my tabernacle among you**  - "I will put my dwelling place among you"
  * **I will not detest you** - AT: "I will accept you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **I will walk among you** - "I will be with you"
  * **I have broken the bars of your yoke and made you to walk standing up straight** - This means that Yahweh has freed the Israelites from slavery. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
