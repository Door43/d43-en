===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will destroy ... cut down ... throw your corpses** - Because God would send an army to do these things, he speaks as if he would do them. AT: "I will send an enemy army to destroy ... cut down ... throw your corpses"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your corpses** - "your dead bodies"
  * **the corpses of your idols** - God speaks of idols not being alive as if they had been alive and then died. AT: "your lifeless idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
