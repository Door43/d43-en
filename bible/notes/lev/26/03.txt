===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walk in my laws**  - "follow the laws I have given you" (see: Idiom)
  * **walk in my laws and keep my commandments and obey them**  - these are three ways of saying and emphasizing the same basic thing: the people need to do what God commands them to do (see: Doublet)
  * **in its season **- at the times of the year when rain is needed to grow the crops (see:  Idiom)
