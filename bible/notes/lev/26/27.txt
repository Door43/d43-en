===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walk against me **-See how this is translated in __26.21__  and __26.23__.
  * **I will will punish you even seven more times as much for your sins.**  - See how "**seven times**" is translated in __26:18__, __26:21__, __26:24__. As the people sin even more against Yahweh, the intensity of the punishment increases.
