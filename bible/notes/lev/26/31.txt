===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will draw out my sword and follow you** - This means Yahweh will continue to cause the enemy army to punish his people.
  * **Your land will be abandoned, and your cities will be ruined** - AT: "You will abandon your land, and your enemies will destroy your cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
