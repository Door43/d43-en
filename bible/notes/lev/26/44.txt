===== Translation Notes: =====
This concludes Yahweh's message to Moses at Mount Sinai regarding the blessings for obedience and the punishments for disobedience.

  * **I will call to mind** - "I will fulfill"
  * **in the sight of the nations** - AT: "in the sight of the people of the nations" or "to show my power to the people of the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
