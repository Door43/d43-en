===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The land will be abandoned by them**  - This refers to the land portion of the covenant made between Yahweh and Abraham (and his descendants).  Even though Israel would be forced out of Canaan and into the land of her enemies, Yahweh would bring Israel back to the land promised to them.
