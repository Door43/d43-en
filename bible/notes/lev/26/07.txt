===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they will fall before you by the sword** - AT: "you will kill them in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Five of you will chase away a hundred, and a hundred of you will chase ten thousand** - This means the Israelites will have victory against larger armies.
  * **Five...hundred...ten thousand** - "5...100...10,000" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
