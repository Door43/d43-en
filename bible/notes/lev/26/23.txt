===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walk against me**  - "rebel against me" or "oppose me"
  * **I will also walk against you** - "I will oppose you"
  * **I myself will hit you with even seven times more blows for your sins** - AT: "I myself will continue to increase your punishment for your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
