===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Nadab and Abihu** - These are the names of Aaron's sons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **censer** - a container for fire and incense which priests used
  * **offered unapproved fire** - "offered fire that was not according to Yahweh's instructions" or "offered fire that Yahweh did not approve"
