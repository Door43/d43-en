===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Moses took them** - Here "them" refers to the fat, thigh, and all of the bread.
  * **from off their hands** - Here "hands" represents the whole person. AT: "from Aaron and his sons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **They were a consecration offering** - "They were an offering for setting Aaron and his sons apart for service to Yahweh"
  * **an offering made by fire to Yahweh** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "a burnt offering to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **produced a sweet aroma** - Yahweh being pleased with the sincere worshiper who offered the sacrifice is spoken of as if God were pleased with the aroma of the burning sacrifice. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:01:07|1:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **breast** - the front part of the animal's body below the neck
  * **ordination** - an official ceremony that makes someone a priest
