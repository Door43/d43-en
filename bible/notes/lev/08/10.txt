===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **sprinkled** - "let drops fall" or "let drops splatter"
    * **seven times** - "the number 7" (See: Biblical Numbers)
    * **utensils**  - "tools for a certain task"
    * **wash basin** - "a large kind of bowl which holds water for washing"
