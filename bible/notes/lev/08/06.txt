===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **brought** - This is a form of the verb 'to bring'.
    * **sash** - "a piece of cloth which ties around the waist"
    * **waist** - "the middle part of the body which crosses the navel"
    * **finely woven waistband** - "a good quality cloth sash for the waist"
    * **bound**  - "tied" or "fastened together"
