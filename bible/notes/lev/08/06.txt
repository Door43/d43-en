===== translationNotes: =====

  * **washed them with water** - This is a symbolic action. It is a ritually cleaning that prepares them to become priests. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **the tunic ... the sash ... the robe ... the ephod ... the finely-woven waistband** - These are special garments that Yahweh commanded the people to make for the priests. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **sash** - a long piece of cloth that is tied around the waist or chest
  * **bound it to him** - "tied it around him"
