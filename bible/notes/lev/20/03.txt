===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I also will set my face against that man** - "I will turn my face away from him." This means Yahweh will reject the person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he has given his child** - "he has sacrificed his child"
  * **profane my holy name** - AT: "dishonor my reputation" or "dishonor me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **close their eyes to** - AT: "disregard" or "ignore" (UDB)
  * **who prostitutes himself in order to play the harlot with Molech** - Often Yahweh compares those who are unfaithful to him and worship false gods to prostitutes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
