===== translationNotes: =====

  * **her bed** - This refers to the woman who is menstruating.
  * **that person will be unclean** - The person whom other people may not touch is spoken of as if he were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **until evening** - "until sunset"
