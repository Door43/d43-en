===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Outside the curtain before the covenant decrees** - Here "covenant decrees" refers to the ark of the covenant which contained the 10 commandments. The ark was located in the most holy place. AT: "Outside of the curtain that separates the holy place from the most holy place" 
  * **from evening to morning** - "from sunset to sunrise"
  * **This will be a permanent command throughout your people's generations** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:lev:03:15|3:17]].
