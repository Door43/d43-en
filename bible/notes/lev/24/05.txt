===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what Aaron must do for tabernacle worship every day.

  * **ephah** - An ephah is 22 liters. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume|biblical volume]])
  * **the table of pure gold before Yahweh** - This table is in the holy place which is before the most holy place.
