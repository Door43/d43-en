===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - God continues telling Moses what they must do.

  * **And he who kills another man must certainly be put to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must certainly put to death anyone who kills another person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **life for life** - You can make clear the implicit information. AT: "one life to replace the other life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
