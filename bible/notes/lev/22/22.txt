===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **disabled, or maimed** - This refers to defects caused by accidents.
  * **warts, sores, or scabs** - These are types of skin diseases.
  * **will not be accepted **- AT: "I will not accept" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **deformed or small** - This refers to defects the animal has from birth.
