===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **keep my commandments and carry them out** - The words "keep" and "carry out" mean the same thing. They emphasize that the people must obey God's commands. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **You must not dishonor my holy name** - AT: "You must honor my holy reputation" or "You must honor me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I must be acknowledged as holy by the people of Israel** - AT: "The people of Israel must acknowledge me as holy" (See:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
