===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Festival of Shelters** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:lev:23:33|23:34]].
  * **when you have gathered in the fruits** - "after you have gathered the crops"
  * **fifteenth day of the seventh month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The fifteenth day is near the beginning of October on western calendars. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
