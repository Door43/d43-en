===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues his instructions for the Festival of Shelters.

  * **branches of palm trees ... willows from streams** - Possible uses for these branches are 1) to make temporary shelters or 2) to wave them as part of their joyous celebration. Some translations state their use clearly; other translations leave it implicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **willows** - trees with long, narrow leaves, which grow near water (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
