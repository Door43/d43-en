===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues his instructions for the Festival of Shelters.

  * **branches of palm trees...willows from streams** - Possible meanings are 1) the people made temporary shelters out of these branches or 2) people waved these branches as part of their joyous celebration.
  * **willows** - trees with long, narrow leaves which grows near water (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
