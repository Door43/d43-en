    * **fifteenth day of the seventh month**  - See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] 
    * **Festival of Shelters**  - A celebration where the people of Israel lived in temporary shelters for seven days as a way to remember the time they spent living in the wilderness after leaving Egypt.
