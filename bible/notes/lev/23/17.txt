===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **made from two tenths of an ephah. They must be made from fine flour and baked with yeast** - AT: "which you must make from two tenths of an ephah of flour and then bake it with yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **two tenths of an ephah** - This represents approximately 4.5 liters.
  * **that will be raised and presented**  - AT: "that the priest will raise and present"
