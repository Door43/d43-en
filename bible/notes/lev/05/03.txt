===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he is unaware of it**  - The word 'unaware' means that "he does not realize it" or "he does not know about it".
  * **whatever it is that a man swears rashly with an oath - ** AT: "Whatever it is that a man swears rashly with an**** oath to do"**** (See: Ambiguous Phrases)
