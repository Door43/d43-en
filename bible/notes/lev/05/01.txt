===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **something about which he is required to testify**  - AT: "something about which a judge has required him to testify" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **the carcass**  - "the dead body"
