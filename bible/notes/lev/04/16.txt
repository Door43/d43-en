===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **The anointed priest will bring some of the blood** - It is implied that the priest caught the blood in a bowl as the blood drained from the bull. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **before the curtain** - It is implied that this is the curtain before the most holy place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
