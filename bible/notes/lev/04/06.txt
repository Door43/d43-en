===== translationNotes: =====

  * **sprinkle some of it** - "drip some of it" or "splatter some of it"
  * **horns of the altar** - This refers to the corners of the altar. They are shaped like the horns of an ox. AT: "the projections at the corners of the altar" 
  * **pour out** - "empty out the rest of the blood"
  * **at the base of the altar** - "at the bottom of the altar"
