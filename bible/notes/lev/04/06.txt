===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sprinkle** - "drip" or "splatter"
  * **horns of the altar** - This refers to the corners of the altar which are shaped like the horns of an ox.
  * **pour out** - "emptied out the blood" or "let the blood run out"
  * **at the base of the altar** - "at the bottom of the altar"
