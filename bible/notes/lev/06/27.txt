===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **its meat **- The pronoun 'its' here refers to the 'meat of sacrifice'.
  * **if the blood is splashed on…..the part that was splashed on**  -"if you splashed the blood onto" ….(See: Active - Passive)
  * **but the clay pot in which it is boiled must be broken **- "you must break the clay pot you boiled it in" (See Active -Passive)
  * **in which it is boiled**  -The pronoun 'it' here refers to 'the meat.
  * **if it is boiled in a bronze pot, it must be scrubbed and rinsed clean in water **- AT: "If you boil it in a bronze pot, you must scrub and rinse it in clean water." (See: Active - Passive)
