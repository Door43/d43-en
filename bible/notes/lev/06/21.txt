===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **it will be made**  - "you will make it" (See: Active - Passive)
  * **when it is soaked**  - "when it is as wet as possible"
  * **baked pieces **- "pieces you will bake in fire"
  * **as commanded **- "as God has commanded you" from the passive form to "as you have been commanded" (See: Active - Passive)
  * **this must always be wholly burned to Yahweh **- "you must always wholly burn it to Yahweh" (See: Active - Passive)
  * **will be wholly burned up **- "you will wholly burn it up" (See: Active - Passive)
  * **it must not be eaten **- AT: "You must not eat the offering" (See: Active - Passive)
