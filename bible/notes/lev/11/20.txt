===== translationWords: =====


===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what he permits the people to eat and what he forbids them to eat.

  * **must be detested by you** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "you must detest" (UDB) or "you must hate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **if they have legs above their feet** - This refers to insects that have jointed or bending legs that permit them to jump instead of crawl on the ground.
  * **locust, katydid, cricket, or grasshopper** - These are small insects that eat plants and can jump . (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **the flying insects that have four feet** - Here the phrase "have four feet" is an idiom that means to crawl on the ground and sets these insects apart from other flying things that have only two feet. AT: "the flying insects that crawl on the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
