===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **must be detested by you** - AT: "you must detest" (UDB) or "you must hate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **locusts...katydids...crickets...grasshoppers**- These are small insects that eat plants and can jump high. (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
