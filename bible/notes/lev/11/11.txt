===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **Since they must be detested** - AT: "Since you must detest them" or "Since you must hate them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **their carcasses must be detested** - AT: "you must detest their dead bodies" or "you must not touch their dead bodies"
