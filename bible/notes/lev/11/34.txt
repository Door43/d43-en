===== translationNotes: =====

  * **All food which is clean** - Food that God has declared acceptable for the people to eat is spoken of as if it were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and permitted to be eaten** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "that you may eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will then be unclean** - Food becomes unacceptable for the people to eat because unclean water has fallen on it is spoken of as if it were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **may be drunk** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "you may drink" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **of the carcass** - "of the dead body"
  * **It must be broken to pieces** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "You must break it to pieces" or "You must shatter it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
