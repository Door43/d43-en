===== translationNotes: =====

  * **unclean** - Food becomes unacceptable for the people to eat because unclean water has fallen on it is spoken of as if it were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **carcass** - "dead body"
  * **must be broken to pieces** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "You must break it to pieces" or "You must shatter it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
