===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **split hoof...chew the cud** - See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:lev:11:03|11:3]].
  * **paws** - an animal foot with claws
  * **until evening** - "until sunset"
