===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron which animals the people are to consider unclean.

  * **Every animal ... is unclean for you** - These animals that God declared to be unfit for the people to eat are spoken of as if they were physically dirty. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **split hoof** - This means a hoof that is split into two parts instead of being one whole. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:11:03|11:3]].
  * **chew the cud** - This means an animal that brings its food up from its stomach and chews it again. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:11:03|11:3]].
  * **Everyone who touches them will be unclean** - A person who is unacceptable for God's purposes because he has touched one of these animals is spoken of as if he were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **paws** - animal feet with claws
  * **until the evening** - "until sunset"
