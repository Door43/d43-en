===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **is to be detested** - AT: "you must detest it" or "you must hate it" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **it must not be eaten** - AT: "you must not eat it"
