===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh finishes telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **for which a distinction is to be made between** - AT: "for which you must distinguish between" (See:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that may be eaten...that may not be eaten** - AT: "that you may eat...that you may not eat"
