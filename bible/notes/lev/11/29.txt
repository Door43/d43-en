===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron which animals the people are to consider unclean.

  * **these are the animals that will be unclean for you** - These animals that God declared to be unfit for people to touch or eat are spoken of as if they were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **weasel** - a small animal with brown fur that eats birds and small animals (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **large lizard, the gecko, the monitor lizard, the lizard, the skink, and the chameleon** - These are different kinds of reptiles with four legs. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **skink** - "sand lizard"
