===== TFT: =====

\v 3 The ones that have hooves that are completely split and that chew their cuds\f + \ft Or: bring their food up from their stomachs to chew it again \f*.
\p
\v 4 There are some animals that chew their cuds but do not have split hooves, and some animals that have split hooves but do not chew their cuds.
