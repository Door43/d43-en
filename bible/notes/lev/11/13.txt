===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **vulture...kite...falcon...raven...screech owl...horned owl...seagull...hawk** - These are birds that are either awake at night or feed on rodents and dead animals (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
