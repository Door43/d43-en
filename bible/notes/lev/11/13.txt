===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what he permits the people to eat and what he forbids them to eat.

  * **vulture ... kite ... falcon ... raven ... horned owl ... screech owl ... seagull ... hawk** - These are birds that are either awake at night or feed on rodents and dead animals. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
