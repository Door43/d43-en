===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement** - God continues telling Moses what he must tell the people.
 
  * **they are my servants** - "your fellow countrymen are my servants"
  * **They will not be sold as slaves** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must not sell them as slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you may buy slaves from them** - "you may by slaves from those nations"
