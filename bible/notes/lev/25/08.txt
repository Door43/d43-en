===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **there will be seven Sabbaths of years** - "they will be seven sets of seven years" 
  * **forty-nine years** - "49 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the tenth day of the seventh month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the end of September on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the Day of Atonement** - On this day each year the high priest would make a sacrifice to Yahweh so that Yahweh would forgive all the sins of the people of Israel. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:23:26|23:27]].
