===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must not conduct...who live with you may gather food** - In 25:5-6 Yahweh means he does not allow the owner of the field to organize his workers and harvest the land like he does the other six years. However, Yahweh does allow individuals to go through the fields to pick and eat the fruit they find.
  * **your unpruned vines** - This means no one has tended to the vines as they do during the other six years.
