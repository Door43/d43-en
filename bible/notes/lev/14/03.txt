===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone is cleansed of a skin disease.

  * **infectious skin disease** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:lev:13:03|13:3]].
  * **the one to be cleansed** - AT: 'the person he is cleansing" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **clean birds** - This means ritually clean birds that God allowed the people to eat and offer as sacrifices.
  * **scarlet yarn** - "red yarn"
  * **hyssop** - an herb with a pleasant smell that was used for medicine (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
