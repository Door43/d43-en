===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone is cleansed of a skin disease.

  * **the bird that was killed** - AT: "the bird that the person killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the person who is to be cleansed** - AT: "the person he is cleansing"
