===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **wring off its head** - "twist off its head"
  * **Then its blood must be drained out** - Moses is telling the priests to make sure the blood is drained out. This can be translated in active form. AT: "Then the priest must drain its blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
