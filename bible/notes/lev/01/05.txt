===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **Then he must kill the bull** - Here "he" refers to the one making the offering.
  * **before Yahweh** - This means beside the altar in front of the tent of meeting.
  * **Then he must skin** - Here "he" refers to the one making the offering.
