===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **Then he must kill the bull** - Here "he" refers to the one making the offering. It can be stated in second person. AT: "Then you must kill the bull" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **before Yahweh** - This means beside the altar in front of the tent of meeting.
  * **will present the blood** - It is implied that the priests would catch the blood in a bowl as it drained out of the animal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **splash it on the altar** - The blood represents life. Throwing the blood on the altar represents God's presence. Splashing the blood on the altar was a symbolic action that indicates God forgiving the person's sin through the sacrifice of the animal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **Then he must skin** - Here "he" refers to the one making the offering.
