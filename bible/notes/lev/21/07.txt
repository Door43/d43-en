===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They must not** - "The priests must not"
  * **You will set him apart** - "You people must treat the priest as holy"
  * **for he offers the bread" of your God** - This can be made explicit. AT: "for he burns the 'food offering' on Yahweh's altar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **He must be holy to you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must regard him as holy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **She must be burned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must burn her to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
