===== translationNotes: =====

  * **among his people** - "among the Israelites"
  * **virgin** - This can also be translated as "young woman"
  * **his virgin sister who has had no husband** - AT: "an unmarried sister who still lives with her father" or "an unmarried sister who is still under her father's authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
