===== translationNotes: =====

  * **corners** - "edges" (UDB) or "any part"
  * **they must be separate** - "they must be holy" or "they must be set apart"
  * **separate for** - "separate for the sake of"
  * **not disgrace the name of their God** - This word "name" is used to represent Yahweh's character. AT: "not disgrace God's reputation" or "not disgrace their God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the food for their God** - "the burnt offering of food to their God." Yahweh does not actually eat these offerings. It is the sincerity of those offering the food that pleases God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
