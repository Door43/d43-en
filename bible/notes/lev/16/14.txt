===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the blood of the bull** - This is the blood Aaron caught with a bowl in 16:11.
  * **sprinkle it with his finger** - He used his finger to splash the blood.
  * **on the front of the  atonement lid** - He did not put the blood on the atonement lid. He put it on the side of the ark of the covenant which faced east.
  * **seven times before the atonement lid** - "seven times below the atonement lid onto the chest"
