===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the blood of the bull** - This is the blood Aaron caught with a bowl in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:16:11|16:11]].
  * **sprinkle it with his finger** - He used his finger to splash the blood.
  * **on the front of the atonement lid** - He put the blood on the top part of the lid. He also put it on the side of the lid that faced him as he entered the most holy place.
  * **before the atonement lid** - Possible meanings are 1) "below the atonement lid onto the chest" or 2) "onto the ground in front of the atonement lid."
