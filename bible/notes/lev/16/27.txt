===== translationNotes: =====

  * **whose blood was brought** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "whose blood Aaron brought" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **must be carried** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "someone must carry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **their hides** - "their skins." Here "their" refers to the bull and the goat.
