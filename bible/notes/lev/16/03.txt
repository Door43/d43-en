===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So here is how** - "This is how"
  * **undergarments** - "underwear." This is clothing worn next to the skin under the outer clothes.
  * **sash** - a piece of cloth that ties around the waist or chest
  * **turban** - This is a head covering made from wrapped strips of cloth.
  * **from the assembly** - "from the congregation"
