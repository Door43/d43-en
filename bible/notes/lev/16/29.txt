===== translationNotes: =====

  * **for you** - The word "you" is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **in the seventh month, on the tenth day of the month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the end of September on the western calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **atonement will be made for you** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Aaron will make atonement for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to cleanse you ... so you will be clean** - People who are acceptable for God's purposes are spoken of as if they were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **It is a solemn Sabbath of rest for you** - This is not the same as the Sabbath they observed every week on the seventh day. This was a special Sabbath on the Day of Atonement.
