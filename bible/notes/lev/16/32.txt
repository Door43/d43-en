===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the one who will be anointed and consecrated** - AT: "the one they will anoint and set apart" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **in his father’s place** - When the high priest died, one of his sons would replace him.
    * **the holy garments** - These are the special clothes the high priest must wear when he enters the most holy place.
    * **for all the people of the assembly** - for all the people of Israel
