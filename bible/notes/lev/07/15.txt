    * **The person presenting** - "The person who offers"
    * **for the purpose of giving thanks** - "for the purpose of thanking Yahweh"
    * **the meat must be eaten...the meat may be eaten** - AT: "he must eat the meat"..."he may eat the meat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
