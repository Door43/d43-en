===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **person presenting** - AT: "person who offers" This implies the words "person 'who is' presenting". (See: Implicit - Explicit) This word comes from the verb 'to present'.
    * **for the purpose of giving thanks** - "when the purpose is to give thanks" (See: Verbal Noun)
    * **the meat must be eaten....the meat may be eaten** - AT: "he must eat the meat"...."he may eat the meat" (See: Active - Passive)
