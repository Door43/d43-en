===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The person presenting** - "The person who offers"
  * **for the purpose of giving thanks** - "for the purpose of thanking Yahweh"
  * **the meat must be eaten ... it may be eaten** - Moses gives clear instructions about when the offering must be eaten. This can be translated in active form. AT: "he must eat the meat ... he may eat it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
