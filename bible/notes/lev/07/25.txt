===== translationNotes: =====


  * **sacrifice by fire** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "burnt offering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that person must be cut off from his people** - A person being excluded from his community is spoken of as if he had been cut off from his people, as one would cut a piece of cloth or cut a branch from a tree. This can be translated in active form. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:07:19|7:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **You must eat no blood** - "You must not consume blood"
  * **in any of your houses** - "in any of your homes" or "wherever you live"
