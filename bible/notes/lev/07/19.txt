===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Any meat that touches an unclean thing must not be eaten** - AT: "No one may eat meat that touches something unclean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **It must be burned** - AT: "You must burn it"(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **that person must be cut off from his people** - AT: "that person may not live among his people" or "you must separate that person from his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
