===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Any meat that touches an unclean thing must not be eaten** - Moses gives instruction about meat that has become unclean. This can be translated in active form. AT: "You must not eat meat that touches something unclean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **It must be burned** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "You must burn it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that person must be cut off from his people** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "that person may no longer live among his people" or "you must separate that person from his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
