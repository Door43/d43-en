===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then Yahweh spoke to Moses, saying, "Speak to the people of Israel and say, 'You must eat no fat** - This has a quotation within a quotation. A direct quotation can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "Then Yahweh spoke to Moses and told him to tell the people of Israel this: 'You must eat no fat" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotesinquotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **died without being a sacrifice** - "died but was not a sacrifice"
  * **the fat of an animal torn by wild animals** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the fat of an animal that wild animals killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **may be used** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "you may use" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
