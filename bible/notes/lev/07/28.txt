===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **to be made by fire** - "that will be a burnt offering"
    * **his own hands must bring it** - "he must bring with his own hands"
    * **He must bring the fat with the breast so that the breast may be an offering raised before Yahweh and presented to him**  - AT: "He must bring the fat with the breast so that he can raise it unto Yahweh and present it to him" (See: Active - Passive)
