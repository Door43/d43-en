===== translationNotes: =====


  * **The offering for ... to be made by fire** - "The part of the burnt offering to Yahweh"
  * **his own hands must bring it** - Translate this so it is clear that he must bring his own offering. AT: "he must bring it himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **so that the breast may be an offering raised before Yahweh and presented to him** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "so that he can raise it to Yahweh and present it to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
