====== Leviticus 07:31-32 ======


===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 31 The Supreme Priest must burn the fat on the altar, but the breast belongs to Aaron and all his sons.
\v 32 You must give to the Supreme Priest the right thigh of the animal that is sacrificed to maintain fellowship with Yahweh.
</usfm>


===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 31 The priest must burn the fat on the altar, but the breast belongs to Aaron and all his descendants.
\v 32 You must give to the priest the right thigh of the animal that you presented to promise friendship with Yahweh.
</usfm>


===== ULB: =====

<usfm>
\v 31 The priest must burn the fat on the altar, but the breast will belong to Aaron and his descendants.
\v 32 You must give the right thigh to the priest as an offering presented out of the sacrifice of your peace offerings.
</usfm>

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **right thigh** - "a part of the animal's leg" God specified that it must be the right thigh.
    * **as an offering presented**  - "and present it as an offering" (See: Active - Passive)
