===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what he must tell Aaron and his sons.

  * **in the place for killing it** - It can be made explicit that this refers to place where the animals for the burnt offerings are killed. See [[:en:bible:notes:lev:01:10|1:10-11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **All the fat in it will be offered** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "The priest must offer all the fat in it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **inner parts** - This is the stomach and intestines.
  * **liver ... kidneys** - See how you translated these words in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:03:03|3:4]].
  * **next to the loins** - This is the part of the body on the sides of the backbone between the ribs and hipbone. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:03:03|3:4]].
  * **all this must be removed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the priest must remove all this" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
