===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not cause judgment to be false** - "Do not judge unfairly"
  * **You must not show favoritism to someone because he is poor, and you must not show favoritism to someone because he is important** - The words "poor" and "important" are combined to mean "anyone." AT: "You must not show favoritism to anyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **judge your neighbor righteously** - "judge everyone according to what is right"
  * **Do not walk around spreading false gossip** - "Do not go around speaking gossip to other people"
  * **seek to protect your neighbor's life** - "seek to protect your neighbors life by telling the truth"
