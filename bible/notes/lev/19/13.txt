===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not oppress your neighbor or rob him** - Here "neighbor" means "anyone." AT: "Do not hurt or rob anyone"
  * **The wages of a hired servant must not stay with you all night until the morning** - The command was for the employer to pay his servant at sundown. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
