===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must NOT do that would defile them.

  * **give any of your children to pass through the fire** - AT: "burn your children alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **profane the name of your God** - AT: "dishonor my reputation" or "bring shame on me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
