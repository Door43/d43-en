===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must not do that would defile them.

  * **You must not give any of your children to pass through the fire** - The phrase "to pass through the fire" means to burn something with fire as a sacrifice. AT: "You must not burn your children alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you must not profane the name of your God** - Here the word "profane" means to dishonor. The word "name" represents God himself. AT: "you must not dishonor your God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
