===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Do not have sexual relations with any sister of yours,"**  - Repeated in verse 11 for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **"whether she is the daughter of your father or the daughter of your mother,"**  - AT: "whether she is related through your father or your mother,"
  * **"whether she was raised at your home or distant from you."**  - AT: "whether she was raised with you or far away."
  * **"Do not have sexual relations with your father's wife's daughter."**  - (See: UDB)
