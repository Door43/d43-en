===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh finishes telling Moses what the people must not do.

  * **the persons ... will be cut off from among their people** - People being excluded from the community is spoken of as if they had been cut off from their people, as one would cut a piece of cloth or cut a branch from a tree. This can be translated in active form. AT: "the persons ... may no longer live among their people" or "you must separate the persons ... from their people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **which were practiced here before you** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "which the people did here before you came" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **by them** - Here "them" refers to the detestable customs.
