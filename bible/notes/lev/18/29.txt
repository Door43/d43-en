===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh finishes telling Moses what the people must not do.

  * **the persons who do such things will be cut off from among their people** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "you must remove from the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **not to practice any of these detestable customs which were practiced here before you** - Translate this to clarify "customs" as the actions of the people. AT: "not to do any of these terrible things which the people did here before you came" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **by them** - Here "them" refers to the detestable customs.
