===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Epaphras** - This is a fellow believer and prisoner with Paul. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **my fellow prisoner in Christ Jesus** - "who is in prison with me because he serves Christ Jesus"
  * **So do Mark, Aristarchus, Demas, and Luke, my fellow workers** - "Mark, Aristarchus, Demas, and Luke, my fellow workers, also greet you"
  * **Mark ... Aristarchus ... Demas ... Luke** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **my fellow workers** - "the men who work with me" or "who all work with me."
  * **May the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit** - The word "your" here refers to Philemon and all who met in his house. The words "your spirit" are a synecdoche and represent the people themselves. AT: "May our Lord Jesus Christ be kind to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
