===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul begins his plea and the reason for his letter.

  * **all the boldness in Christ** - Possible meanings are 1) "authority because of Christ" or 2) "courage because of Christ." AT: "courage because Christ has given me authority"
  * **yet because of love** - Possible meanings: 1) "because I know that you love God's people" 2) "because you love me" or 3) "because I love you"
