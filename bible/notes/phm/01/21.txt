===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Confident about your obedience** - "Because I am sure that you will do what I ask"
  * **your obedience...write to you,...you will do"** - Paul was writing this to Philemon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **knowing** - "and I know"
  * **At the same time** - "Also"
  * **prepare a guest room for me** - "make a room in your house ready for me." Paul asked Philemon to do this.
  * **through your prayers...to visit you** - The words "your""you" here refer to Philemon and the believers that met at his house. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **through your prayers** - This can be translated as "as a result of your prayers" or "because you all are praying for me."
  * **I will soon be able to visit you** - "God will soon make those who are keeping me in prison to set me free so that I can go to you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
