===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul closes his letter and gives final greetings to the believers that met for church in Philemon's house.

  * **Confident about your obedience** - "Because I am sure that you will do what I ask"
  * **knowing** - "and I know"
  * **At the same time** - "Also"
  * **prepare a guest room for me** - "make a room in your house ready for me." Paul asked Philemon to do this for him.
  * **your prayers...visit you** - The words "your" and "you" here refer to Philemon and the believers that met at his house. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
  * **I will soon be able to visit you** - "those who are keeping me in prison will set me free so that I can go to you."
