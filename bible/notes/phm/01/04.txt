===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The word "us" is plural and refers to Paul, Timothy, and Philemon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])

  * **that the fellowship of your faith** - "that your working together with us"
  * **be effective for the knowledge of everything good** - "result in knowing what is good"
  * **in Christ** - "because of Christ" 
  * **the hearts of the believers have been refreshed by you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you encouraged believers" or "you have helped the believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **brother** - "dear brother" or "dear friend." Paul called Philemon "brother" because they were both believers and emphasizing their friendship.
