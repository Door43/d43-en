===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my brothers **- This is not just biological brothers, but all Christians. "fellow Christians"
  * **we will receive greater judgment**  - "teachers of spiritual things are held to a higher standard"
  * **stumble**  - "fail" (See: Metaphor)
  * **his words, he is a perfect man**  - "their words, they are mature"
