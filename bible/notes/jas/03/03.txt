===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now if**  - "when"
  * **horse **- This is a large animal used to carry things.
  * **bits**  - A small piece of metal placed in the horse's mouth to steer the horse.
  * **their mouths**  - "the horses' mouths"
  * **Notice also**  - "Think also about" (UDB)
  * **ships **- This is like a truck that floats on water.
  * **they are so large**  - They refers to the ships.
  * **driven **- "pushed"
  * **rudder **- "tool"
  * **pilot **- "the person steering the ship"
