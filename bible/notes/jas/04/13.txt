===== translationNotes: =====

  * **spend a year there** - James speaks of spending time as if it were money. "stay there for a year" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Who knows what will happen tomorrow** - James uses this question to correct his audience. This can be expressed as a statement. AT: "No one knows what will happen tomorrow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **what is your life** - James uses this question to teach these believers that physical life is not that important. This can be expressed as a statement. AT: "think about your physical lives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **For you are a mist that appears for a little while and then disappears** - James speaks of people as if they were a mist that appears and then quickly goes away. AT: "You live for only a short amount of time, and then you die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
