===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fulfill the royal law **- God originally gave laws to Moses, recorded in the books of the Old Testament. "obey God's law"
  * **your neighbor **- "all people" or "everyone"
  * **favor **- "give special treatment to"
  * **convicted by the law as lawbreakers**  - "breaking God's law"
