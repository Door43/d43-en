===== translationNotes: =====

  * **if you fulfill** - The word "you" refers to the Jewish believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **fulfill the royal law** - "obey God's law." God originally gave laws to Moses, recorded in the books of the Old Testament.
  * **You shall love your neighbor as yourself** - James is quoting from the book of Leviticus.
  * **your neighbor** - "all people" or "everyone"
  * **you do well** - "you are doing well" or "you are doing what is right"
  * **if you favor** - "give special treatment to" or "give honor to"
  * **committing sin** -  "sinning." That is, breaking the law.
  * **convicted by the law as lawbreakers** - Here the law is spoken of as if it were a human judge. AT: "guilty of breaking God's law" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
