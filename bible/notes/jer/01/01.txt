===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hilkiah ... Amon** - men's names (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anathoth**  - name of a town (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came to** - AT: "Yahweh spoke his word to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **came to him **- "came to Jeremiah"
  * **his reign**  - "Josiah's reign"
  * **It also came**  - "The word of Yahweh also came"
  * **of Zedekiah** - "of the reign of Zedekiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
