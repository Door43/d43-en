===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hananiah claims to speak for God.

  * **in the fourth year and the fifth month** - This is the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the dry season. It is during the last part of July and the first part of August on Western calendars. AT: "in the fifth month of his fourth year (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Azzur** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **I have broken the yoke imposed by the king of Babylon** - AT: "I have stopped the control of the king of Babylon"
