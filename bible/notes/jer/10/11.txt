===== Translation Notes: =====

God is speaking to Jeremiah.

  * **The maker of earth in his power established dry land in his wisdom and spread the heavens in his understanding.** - AT: "The Creator is strong and wise. He made  the earth and sky."
  * **His voice makes the roar of waters in the heavens** - AT: "He controls the storms in the sky by speaking"
  * **and he brings up the mists from the ends of the earth**  - AT: "He makes clouds everywhere on earth." 
  * **storehouse**  - a building where things are kept
