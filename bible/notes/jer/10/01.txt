===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh has just reminded the people of Judah as well as Egypt, Edom, Ammon, Moab and all people that they will be punished.

  * **dismayed** - "worried" or "frightened"
  * **for the nations are dismayed by these** - "for the signs in heaven dismay the people of the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] or [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
