===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **written with an iron stylus having a diamond point **- This phrase is an exaggertation used to express how deeply their sin was rooted in their hearts. (See: Hyperbole)
  * **tablet of their hearts **- This phrase compares a "tablet of their hearts" with the innermost part of a person. (See: Metaphor)
  * **on the horns of your altars **- This phrase uses "horns of your altars" as a symbol of their sinful worship practice at altars dedicated to other gods. (See: Metaphor)
