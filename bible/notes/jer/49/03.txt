===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Howl**  - cry out loud
  * **daughters of Rabbah**  - The women in the city of Rabbah
  * **Why are you proud of your strength?**  - AT: "You should not be so proud because you really are not strong." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Who will come against me?**  - "You falsely think that no one can defeat you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
