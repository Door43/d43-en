===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking about what will happen to Damascus.

  * **its**
 - Refers to the Damascus.
  * **Yahweh of Host's declaration**  - See how you translated this in 1:8.
  * **Ben Hadad**
 - This the the name of the king. (See: Translate Names)
