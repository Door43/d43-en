  * **Do you realize that Baalis king of the people of Ammon sent Ishmael son of Nethaniah to murder you?** - Johanan and the commanders use a rhetorical question to warn Gedaliah. It can be translated as a statement. AT: "You need to understand that Baalis king of the people of Ammon sent Ishmael son of Nethaniah to murder you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
