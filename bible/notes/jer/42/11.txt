===== translationNotes: =====

  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **to save you and rescue you** - The words "save" and "rescue" mean the same thing and emphasize that Yahweh will, indeed, save. AT: "to save you completely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **rescue you from his hand** - Here the word "hand" refers to power and authority. AT: "rescue you from his power" or "rescue you from him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
