===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The snorting of his stallions is heard from Dan** - AT: "People in Dan heard the sound of the invading army approaching Jerusalem." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **The whole earth shakes**  - AT: "The people of the land shake with fear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **For they will come**  - The word "they" refers to the invading army.
  * **I am sending out snakes among you**  - The invaders are pictured as snakes and vipers.
  * **They will bite you** - The viper's bite represents the punishment the invading army will bring. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
