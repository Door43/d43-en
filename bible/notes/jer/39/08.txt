===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the king's house** - Zedekiah's house
  * **the people's houses** - the houses that had belonged to the people of Jerusalem
  * **Nebuzaradan** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the king's bodyguards** - "Nebuchadnezzar's guards"
  * **the rest of the people who were left in the city** - This is probably a generalization. AT: "the people who were still living in the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **who had nothing for themselves** - The word "nothing" is probably an exaggeration. AT: "who owned nothing of great value" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
