===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues telling how to destroy the people of Babylon and Chaldea.

  * **her, she**  - The words "her" and "she" refer to Babylon.
  * **her fighting men will be destroyed**  - AT: "I will destroy her fighting men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
