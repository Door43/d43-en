===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stamping**  - See how you translated this in Jeremiah [[en:bible:notes:jer:47:03|47:3]].
  * **you jump around like a calf stamping in its pasture**  - The people of Babylon are compared to a calf who runs around joyfully in a meadow. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **you neigh like a powerful horse**  - Their happiness is compared to the happiness of stallions that are neighing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **So your mother â€¦ the one who bore you will be embarrassed.**  - The same idea is expressed in two different ways for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **a wilderness, a dry land, and a desert**  - These words have basically the same meaning. They are used together for emphasis (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **shudder**  - shake violently due to fear
