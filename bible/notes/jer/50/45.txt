===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the plans that Yahweh has decided against Babylon, the plans that he has planned against the land of the Chaldeans** - These two phrases refer to the same thing and are used together here for emphasis. AT: "the plans that Yahweh has decided for the people of Babylon and the Chaldeans". (See: Parallelism)
  * **They will certainly be dragged away** - This means that the people of Babylon will be taken from their homes whether they like it or not.
  * **At the sound of conquered Babylon the earth shakes**  - This phrase compares the fall of the once powerful nation of Babylon to an extremely loud noise.  (See: Metaphor)
  * **distress** - pain or unhappiness
  * **their shout of distress**  - "their shout of pain"
