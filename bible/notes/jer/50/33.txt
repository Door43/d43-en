===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The people of Israel are oppressed**  - AT: "The Babylonians are oppressing the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **captured them**  - The word "them" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **they refuse**  - The word "they" refers to the Babylonians.
  * **let them go**  - The word "them" refers to the people of Israel.
