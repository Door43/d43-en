===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Only if the highest heavens can be measured and only if the earth's foundation below can be discovered, will I reject all of Israel's descendants** - This is a hypothetical situation that Yahweh never expects to truly happen. AT: "The highest heavens can never be measured, and the earth's foundation below can never be discovered, and I will never reject all of Israel's descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **the highest heavens ... earth's foundation** - These phrase refer to the whole of creation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **the earth's foundation below can be discovered** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "someone can dig all the way down to the earth's foundation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **foundation** - the part of a house on which every other part rests. "deepest part"
  * **reject** - throw away or refuse to take
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
