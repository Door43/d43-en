===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **I will build you up again so you will be built** - AT: "I will make you strong again" or "I will make you prosper again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **virgin Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:18:13|18:13]].
  * **tambourine** - a small musical instrument that a person shakes or hits with their hand used when people danced for joy
