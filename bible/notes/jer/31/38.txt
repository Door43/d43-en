===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look, days are coming**  - AT: "Pay attention! What I am about to talk about will happen soon."
  * **Tower of Hananel ... Corner Gate ... hill of Gareb ... Goah ... Kidron Valley ... Horse Gate** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **set apart**  - AT: "made holy"
