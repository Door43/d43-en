===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **you yourself know me**  - The word "me" refers to Jeremiah.
    * **them**  - The word "them" refers to the people of Israel
    * **wither**  - "dry up"
    * **inhabitants**  - people who live in the land
    * **How long will the land go ... because of the wickedness of its inhabitants?**  - Jeremiah is grieved that the wickedness lasted too long. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
