===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **bare**  - "not covered" or "empty"
    * **Yahweh's sword is devouring**  - Yahweh is using these armies to punish his people (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **From one end of the land to the other**  - this refers all of the Promised Land (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
    * **other. There is no safety in the land for any living creature.**  - AT: "other and no living thing is safe there."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **They have**  - "My people have"
    * **thorn**  - sharp, pointed growth on plants or bushes
    * **bush**  - a plant that is like a tree but smaller 
    * **exhausted** - "worn out" or "tired"
    * **So be ashamed of your gain because of Yahweh's anger.**  - AT:"My people have received this shame because I am very angry with them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
