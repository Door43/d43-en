===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **he was a leading officer** - Here the word "he" refers to Pashur.
  * **Pashur beat Jeremiah** - Possible meanings are 1) that Pashur himself beat Jeremiah, or 2) that Pashur ordered other men to beat Jeremiah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **stocks** - Stocks are a wooden frame with holes in it that people use to lock up a prisoner's hands, feet, and head.
  * **Upper Gate of Benjamin** - This gate is different from the gate in the city wall that shared the same name.
