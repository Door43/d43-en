===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh, the God of hosts**  - AT: "Yahweh, the God of heaven"
  * **It will be like a fire**  -Yahweh's word is being compared to a fire. (See: simile)
  * **this people will be like wood** - the Judean people are being compared to a burning material. (See: simile)
  * **house of Israel–this is Yahweh's declaration** AT: "you Judeans–this is Yahweh's prediction"
  * **It is a lasting nation, an ancient nation**  AT: "It is an old enduring nation" (See: doublet)
  * **It is a nation who language you do not know, nor will you understand what they say** - AT: "It  is a nation whose language you will not understand" (See: parallelism)
