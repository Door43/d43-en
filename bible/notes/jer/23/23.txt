===== Translation Notes: =====
 
    * **Am I only a God nearby... and not also far away?** - AT : "I am a God that is both near and far away." (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Can anyone hide in a secret place so I cannot see him?** - AT: "No one can hide in a secret place so I cannot see him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
    * **Do I not fill the heavens and the earth?** - AT: "I am everywhere, both in the heavens and the earth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
