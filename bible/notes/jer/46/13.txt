===== translationNotes: =====

  * **when Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon** - Here Nebuchadnezzar represents his army. AT: "when the army of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the land of Egypt** - Here "land" represents the people. AT: "the people of Egypt" or "the Egyptians" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Migdol** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:44:01|44:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Memphis ... Tahpanhes** - Translate the names of these cities as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:02:14|2:16]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the sword will devour those around you** - The word "sword" here represents soldiers carrying weapons. The enemy soliders killing people is spoken of as if their swords were devouring people. AT: "your enemies will kill people all around you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
