===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking about what will happen to the captives of Israel.

  * **you**  - Refers to captive Isrealites.
  * **seventy years**  - (See: Numbers) " 70 years".
  * **Yahweh's declaration **  - See how you translated this in 1:8.
