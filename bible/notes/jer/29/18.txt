===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a horrible sight to all the kingdoms on earthx97a horror, an object about curses and  hissed words, and a shameful thing among all the nations** - AT: "make her despised among the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **hissed words** - Hissing sounds like a snake and means strong disgust.
  * **listen** - AT: "obey"
