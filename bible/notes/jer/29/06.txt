===== translationNotes: =====

  * **take wives for your sons** - AT: "have your sons get married"
  * **give your daughters to husbands** - AT: "allow your daughters to marry"
  * **its ... it** - This refers to the city of Babylon.
  * **Seek the peace of the city** - seek to Live peaceably with all people. Implying not to cause trouble and rebel against the authority.
