===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that does not put his neck into the yoke of the king** - Those that do not swear allegiance to the king of Nebuchadnezzar and pay tribute or taxes to the king. 
  * **by his hand** - This phrase refers to Nebuchadnezzar and his armies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - AT: "This is what Yahweh says will happen"
