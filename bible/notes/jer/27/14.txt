===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak through Jeremiah to the king and people of Judah.

  * **Do not listen to the words** - Yahweh is warning the people about all the false prophets that He did not send that are lying to them.
  * **For I have not sent them out** - "For I have not sent them"
  * **in my name** - This phrase means speaking with Yahweh's power and authority or as his representative. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will drive you** - "I will send you away from your home country"
