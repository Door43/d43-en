===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wind of destruction** - AT: "an unstoppable destruction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Leb Kamai** - This is another word for Babylon.  
  * **scatter** - To scatter is to separate and send in different directions.
  * **on the day of disaster** - The phrase "day of" is a Hebrew idiom for "when." AT:"when she is destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
