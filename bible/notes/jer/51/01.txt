===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stir up**  - Raise up. (See: Idiom)
  * **Leb Kamai**  - Leb Kamai is a code name for Babylon.
  * **scatter**  - To scatter is to separate and send in different directions.
  * **all around**  - All around means on every side.
