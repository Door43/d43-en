===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **banner** - Large flag for the soldiers to follow in battle. AT: "flag"
  * **her** - AT: "Babylon"
  * **Minni, Ashkenaz** - These are names of nations or people groups. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **bring up horses like swarming locusts** - Yahweh compares a great number of horses to a swarm of locusts. AT: "bring many horses with soldiers quickly." (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
