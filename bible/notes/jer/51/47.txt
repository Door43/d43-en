===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **see** - AT: "pay attention"
  * **All of her land** - AT: "All of Babylon's people" (See: [[en/ta/vol2/translate/figs_metonymy]])
  * **the heavens and the earth** - Heaven and earth are viewed as if they were people.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Yahweh's declaration** - AT: "what Yahweh has solemnly said"
  * **the killed** - AT: "the killed people" (See: [[en/ta/vol2/translate/figs_metonymy]])
