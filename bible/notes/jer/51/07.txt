  * **Babylon was a golden cup** - AT: "Babylon was a strong nation used for judgment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **insane** - AT: "unable to think clearly"
  * **wail** - This is a loud, long cry of sadness.
