===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **For her guilt reaches up to the heavens; it is piled up to the clouds** - AT: "Babylon is so very guilty" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]]) 
  * **Yahweh has declared our innocence** - Yahweh had punished the Israelites for their sins, but now has let them return to him.
