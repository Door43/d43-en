===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the praise of all the earth** - Praise represents the honor other nations gave Babylon previously. These words are mocking Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
  * **is seized** - The nations have captured Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The sea...roaring waves** - Babylon has been overcome by its enemies. "Waves" represent many people groups overcoming Babylon. The words "sea" and "water" often represent the nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
