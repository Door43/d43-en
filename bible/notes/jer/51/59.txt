===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Seraiah ... Neriah ... Mahseiah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the fourth year of his reign** - "after he had been king for three years." This "fourth" is the ordinal number for four. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **that were written about Babylon** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "that he had written about Babylon"
