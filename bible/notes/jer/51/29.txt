===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the land will shake** - Yahweh promises such great upheaval that the whole nation will tremble. The "land" represents the people of Babylon living there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **be in anguish** - Babylon will be in great distress.
