===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jeremiah was put into an underground cell** - AT: "the officials put Jeremiah into a room that was under the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **brought him** - "brought Jeremiah"
  * **his house** - "the palace of King Zedekiah"
  * **You will be given** - The word "You" refers to King Zedekiah and his people.
  * **given into the hand of the king of Babylon** - See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:32:03|32:4]].
