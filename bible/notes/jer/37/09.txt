===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not deceive yourselves ** - The word "yourselves" refers to King Zedekiah and the rest of the people of Judah.
  * **Surely the Chaldeans are leaving us** - The people of Judah think that they will be safe because the Chaldeans had left. AT: "Surely the Chaldeans are leaving us, so we will be safe." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they would get up** - "the wounded men would get up"
