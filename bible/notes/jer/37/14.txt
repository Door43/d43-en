===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That is not true**  - "Lie" or "That is a lie"
  * **not deserting **  - "not escaping"
  * **Jeriah**  - See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:jer:37:11|37:13]].
  * **officials**  - See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:jer:01:17|1:18]].
  * **were angry** - "were extremely angry" or "were outraged"
  * **Jonathan the scribe**  - "Jonathan who was a scribe." This is the name of a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
