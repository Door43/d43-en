  * **committed Jeremiah to the courtyard of the guard** - "put Jeremiah safely in the courtyard of the guard"
  * **of the guard** - "of the people who were guards"
  * **A loaf of bread was given him** - "His servants also gave Jeremiah a loaf of bread"
  * **from the street of the bakers** - "from the street where the bakers worked"
