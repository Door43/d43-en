===== translationNotes: =====


  * **they went** - "Johanan, his companions, and all the people they rescued went"
  * **Geruth Kimham** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **because of the Chaldeans** - It is implied that they were afraid that the Chaldeans would attack them. AT: "because they thought the Chaldeans may attack them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in charge of the land** - Here "land" represents the people. AT: "in charge of the people of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
