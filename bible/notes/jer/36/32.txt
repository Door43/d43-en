  * **at Jeremiah's dictation** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jer:36:04|36:4]].
  * **burned by Jehoiakim king of Judah** - "which Jehoiakim king of Judah had burned in the fire"
  * **Furthermore, many other similar words were added to this scroll** - AT: "Furthermore, Jeremiah and Baruch added many more words which were similar to the words that had been in the first scroll." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
