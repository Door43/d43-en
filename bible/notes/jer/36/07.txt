===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jeremiah continues to give instructions to Baruch.

  * **Perhaps** - "It is possible that"
  * **their pleas for mercy will come before Yahweh** - "Yahweh will hear them plead for mercy and do as they ask"
  * **their pleas for mercy** - The word "their" refers to the people in the house of Yahweh and the people of Judah who had come to the house of Yahweh from their cities.
  * **from his wicked way** - Yahweh speaks of a person's lifestyle as if it were a "way" or path along which the person walks. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:18:11|18:11]]. AT: "his wicked way of living" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the wrath and fury** - "the anger and rage"
