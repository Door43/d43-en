===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - Here the word "now" is used to draw attention to the important point that follows.
  * **Micaiah son of Gemariah son of Shaphan** - "Micaiah who was the son of Gemariah, who was the son of Shaphan"
  * **Micaiah** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Look** - "Pay attention to what I am going to say"
  * **Elishama ... Delaiah** - These are names of people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shemaiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:26:20|26:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Elnathan son of Achbor** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:26:22|26:22]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gemariah son of Shaphan** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:09|36:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zedekiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:01|1:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Hananiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:28:01|28:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **and all the officials** - "and all of the other officials"
