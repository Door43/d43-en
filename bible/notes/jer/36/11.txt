===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Micaiah** - This is the name of a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Micaiah son of Gemariah son of Shaphan** - "Micaiah who was the son of Gemariah, who was the son of Shaphan"
  * **to the secretary's room** - "to the room of the scribe"
  * **Behold** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. AT: “Indeed”
  * **Elishama...Delaiah** - These are names of people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shemaiah** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:26:20|26:20]].
  * **Elnathan son of Achbor** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:26:22|26:22]].
  * **Gemariah son of Shaphan** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:09|36:10]].
  * **Zedekiah** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:01|1:3]].
  * **Hananiah** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:28:01|28:1]].
  * **and all the officials** - "and all of the other officials"
