===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Micaiah** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:11|36:11]].
  * **reported to them** - The word "them" refers to officials in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:11|36:12]].
  * **in the hearing of the people** - "so that the people could hear" See how similar words are translated in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:02:01|2:2]].
  * **Jehudi ... Nethaniah ... Shelemiah ... Cushi** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in our hearing** - "so we can hear you read it" See how similar words are translated in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:02:01|2:2]].
  * **read the scroll** - "read the scroll aloud"
