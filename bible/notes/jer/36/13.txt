===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Micaiah** - See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:jer:36:11|36:11]].
  * **reported to them** - The word "them" refers to officials in [[en:bible:notes:jer:36:11|36:12]].
  * **in the people's hearing** - "so that the people could hear"
  * **Jehudi...Nethaniah...Shelemiah...Cushi** - These are the names of people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **read the scroll** - "read the scroll aloud"
