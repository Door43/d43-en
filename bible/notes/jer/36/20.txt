===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then they** - The word "they" refers to the officials.
  * **deposited the scroll in the room of Elishama** - "put the scroll safely in the room of Elishama"
  * **Elishama the secretary** - See how you translated "Elishama the scribe" in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:11|36:12]].
  * **these words** - the words of the scroll which Jeremiah had dictated to Baruch
  * **Jehudi** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:13|36:14]].
  * **in the ninth month** - This is the ninth month of the Hebrew calendar. This is at the end of the sowing season and the beginning of the cold season. It is during the last part November and the first part December on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **and a brazier was burning in front of him** - "and the brazier was in front of him with a blazing fire"
  * **a brazier** - a fireplace that people can move
