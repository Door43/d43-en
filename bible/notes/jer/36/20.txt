===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So they** - "Then the officials"
  * **put the scroll in the room of Elishama** - "put the scroll in the room of Elishama so it would be safe"
  * **Elishama the secretary** - See how you translated "Elishama the scribe" in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:11|36:12]].
  * **Jehudi** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:13|36:14]].
  * **in the hearing of the king** - "where the king could hear him" See how similar words are translated in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:02:01|2:2]].
  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here [the narrator] starts to tell a new part of the story.
  * **in the ninth month** - This is the ninth month of the Hebrew calendar. This is at the end of the sowing season and the beginning of the cold season. It is during the last part November and the first part of December on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **a brazier was burning in front of him** - "the brazier was in front of him with a blazing fire"
  * **a brazier** - a fireplace that people can move
