===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the fifth month, on the tenth day of the month** - This is the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the dry season. The tenth day is near the beginning of August on western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the nineteenth year** - "during year nineteen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Nebuzaradan** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **bodyguards** - This refers to people whose job is to protect someone.
