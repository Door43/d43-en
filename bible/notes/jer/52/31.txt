===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the thirty-seventh year of the exile of Jehoiachin** - after Jehoiachin had been an exile for more than 36 years (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the twelfth month, on the twenty-fifth day of the month** - This is the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-fifth day is near the middle of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **It happened** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story.
  * **Awel-Marduk** - He became the king of Babylon after Nebuchadnezzar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
