===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **craftsmen** - This refers to people who makes beautiful objects used in the worship of Yahweh.
  * **Nebuzaradan** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the poorest of the land** - AT: "the poorest people of the land"
