===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It happened** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story.
  * **in the ninth year** - "in year nine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the tenth month, and on the tenth day of the month** - This is the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the beginning of January on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **They camped opposite it** - "Nebuchadnezzar's army camped opposite Jerusalem"
  * **until the eleventh year** - "until year eleven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
