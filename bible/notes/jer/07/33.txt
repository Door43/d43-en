===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **corpses of this people** - "bodies of this people"
  * **beasts of the earth** - "wild animals of the earth"
  * **exaltation and jubilation** - "exaltation and happiness"
  * **the sounds of the groom and bride** - "the sounds of the man and woman getting married" or AT: "the sounds of the wedding party"
