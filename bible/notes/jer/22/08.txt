===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then many nations will pass by this city** - Here "nations" refers to the people of those nations. AT: "Then many people from various nations will pass by this city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **bowed down to other gods and worshiped them** - These two phrases mean the same thing. The phrase "bowed down" describes the posture that people used in worship. AT: "they worshiped other gods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
