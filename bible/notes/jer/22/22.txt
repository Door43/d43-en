===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The wind will shepherd away all your shepherds** - This is a wordplayâ€”Yahweh uses the idea of "shepherd" in two different ways. Here "shepherds" is a metonym for the leaders of Jerusalem, and the wind "leads" them away. The wind represents Yahweh.  AT: "I will take your leaders away if if the wind had blown them away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **go into captivity** - "be taken away as captives" or "go into exile"
  * **the House of the Forest of Lebanon, you who nest among the cedars** - Yahweh speaks of the royal palace as "Lebanon" and "cedars" because it was constructed with a lot of cedar. AT: "a palace made from the cedars of Lebanon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **how you will be pitied** - The meaning of the Hebrew is not clear. Possible meanings are 1) "they will greatly pity you" or 2) "you groan greatly."
  * **when the labor pains of anguish come on you like childbirth** - The pain that the king will feel when he is defeated will be as intense as the pain woman feels in childbirth. AT: "when you experience pain as intense as the pain of a woman in childbirth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
