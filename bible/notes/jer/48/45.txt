===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The ones who flee**  - This refers to the people who were able to run away during the destruction of Moab.
    * **the shadow of Heshbon**  - a safe place within the city of Heshbon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **without any strength**  - tired
    * **for fire will go out from Heshbon, flame from the middle of Sihon**  - These two phrases have similar meaning, and are used together here for emphasis. There will be a fire in the middle of Heshbon, which was the royal city of Sihon, the king of Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) 
    * **forehead of Moab and the top of heads of the boastful people**  - These phrases refer to Heshbon and its prominent leaders. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **forehead**  - This is the part of the face that is above the eyes, but below the hairline. It is also called the brow.  The word "forehead" is used here to refer to Heshbon.
    * **top of the heads of the boastful people**  - This phrase refers to the prominent people of Moab, such as the princes and leaders.
