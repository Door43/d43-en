===== translationNotes: =====


  * **The ones who flee** - This refers to the people who were able to run away during the destruction of Moab.
  * **will stand in the shadow of Heshbon** - Here "in the shadow" represents protection or shelter. AT: "will hide in Heshbon for protection" or "will go to Heshbon for shelter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for fire will go out from Heshbon, flame from the middle of Sihon** - These two phrases have similar meanings and emphasize that Moab's destruction will begin and spread out from Heshbon, where King Sihon once lived. AT: because a fire will burn in Heshbon, which is the city where King Sihon lived long ago (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Heshbon** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:48:01|48:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sihon** - This is the name of an Amorite king who once ruled over Heshbon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **It will devour the forehead of Moab and the top of the heads of the boastful people** - Here "forehead" and "top of the heads" represent the people of Moab as a whole. AT: "It will burn up all the people in Moab who noisily boasted very much" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **forehead** - the part of the face that is above the eyes, but below the hairline
