===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **cliff**  - a steep side of a mountain 
  * **the mouth of a hole in the rocks**  - This refers to an opening in the rocks, such as the entrance to a cave.    
  * **arrogance, haughtiness, pride, self-glory, conceit in his heart**  - All of these words have similar meanings. They are used together to emphasize the extreme pride of the people of Moab.
