===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Chemosh's people are destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The enemy army has destroyed the people who worship Chemosh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Chemosh** - This is the chief god of the Moabites. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:48:06|48:07]].
  * **your sons are taken as captives and your daughters into captivity** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the enemy army has taken your sons and daughters as captives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I will restore the fortunes of Moab** - "I will cause things to go well for Moab again" or "I will cause Moab to live well again." See how similar words are translated in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:29:12|29:14]].
  * **in later days** - "in the future"
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **The judgment on Moab ends here** - "That is the end of what Jeremiah prophesied about Moab"
