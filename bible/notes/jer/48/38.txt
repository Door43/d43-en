===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **every flat roof**  - "every housetop"
    * **plazas**  - These are open public areas, such as open marketplaces.
    * **For I have destroyed**  - The word "I" refers to Yahweh
    * **this is Yahweh’s declaration**  - See how you translated this in 1:8
    * **shattered**  - demolished or destroyed
    * **howl** - a sad and loud cry that a person makes when he is in pain 
    * **So Moab will become an object of derision**  - This means that Moab will become a nation that other nearby nations laugh at.
