===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Heshbon** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:48:01|48:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Elealeh ... Jahaz ... Zoar ... Horonaim ... Eglath Shelishiyah** - These are names of other cities in Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Nimrim** - This is the name of a stream or river near the Dead Sea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
