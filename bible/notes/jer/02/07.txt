===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yet when you came, you defiled my land; you made my inheritance an abomination**  - AT: "There you sinned and made the land I gave you filthy" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **The priest did not say, “Where is Yahweh?” and the experts in the Law did not care about me!** - AT: The priests and other religious leaders did not want to know me." (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **The shepherds transgressed against me** - "Their leaders sinned against me" 
  * **shepherds**  - "leaders"
  * **and walked after unprofitable things.** - AT: and prayed to gods that could not help them"
  * **walked after**  - "obeyed"  (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
