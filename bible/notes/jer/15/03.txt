===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh has just told them he will send some of them to death, some to die by the sword, some to die by famine, and some to be captives.

  * **Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]].
  * **I will make of them a horror to all the kingdoms of the earth, on account of Manasseh son of Hezekiah, king of Judah, because of what he did in Jerusalem**  - AT: "And I will make them a frightful thing to all the kingdoms of the earth because of what Manasseh the son of Hezekiah, king of Judah, did in Jerusalem." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **I will assign them to four groups **- AT: "I will appoint four groups over them"
