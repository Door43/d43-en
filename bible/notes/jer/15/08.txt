===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh has just told them no one will care about them and that he will destroy his people since they would not turn from their wicked ways.

  * **widows**  - women whose husbands have died
  * **more than the sands of the seashore**  - AT: "more than you can count" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **horror**  - "terror"
  * **fall on them**  - "happen to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **waste away**  - "slowly disappear" or "be weak"
  * **gasp**  - "catch her breath" or "sharply draw her breath in"
  * **embarrassed**  - "feel ashamed" or "confused"
