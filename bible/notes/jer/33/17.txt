===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A man from David's line will never be lacking** - "There will always be a man from David's line" 
  * **A man from David's line** - a male descendant of King David
  * **to sit on the throne of the house of Israel** - The throne is a metonym for the king. AT: "to be king over the house of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the house of Israel** - The word "house" is a metonym for the family that lives in the house. In this case it refers to the kingdom of Israel. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:03:17|3:18]]. AT: "Israel" or "the kingdom of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **nor will a man from the Levitical priests be lacking before me to raise burnt offerings** - "and there will always be a man from the Levitical priests to raise burnt offerings"
  * **raise burnt offerings** - "offer burnt offerings"
