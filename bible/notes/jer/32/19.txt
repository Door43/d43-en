===== Translation Notes: =====
Jeremiah continues praying to Yahweh.
  * **For your eyes are open to all the ways of people **  - AT: " You see everything that people do" (See: Simile)
  * **to give to each man what his conduct and deeds deserve - **  AT: " and will reward each one according the way they live and the things they do"
  * **To this present day **  - AT: " To this day" (See: doublet)
  * **With a rised arm**  - This means that God delivered the Israelites from their enemies that were stronger than them.
  * **With a strong hand**  -This means that God protected his people and also reached and destroyed their enemies.
