===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This verse is a continuation from the previous chunk.

  * **for this city has been a provocation of my wrath and fury since the day that they built it.** - The words "wrath" and "fury" mean basically the same thing and emphasize the intensity of his wrath. AT: "for the people of Jerusalem have made me very angry since the day they built their city." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **It has been that right up to this present day** - AT: "They continue to make me angry even now"
  * **from before my face** - AT: "from my presence" or "completely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
