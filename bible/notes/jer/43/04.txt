===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all the people** - This probably did not include every individual there. The word "all" is a generalization that means many people. AT: "many of the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **refused to listen to Yahweh's voice** - Here "voice" represents commands. And here "to listen" means "to obey." AT: "would not obey Yahweh's command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **where they had been scattered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "where Yahweh had scattered them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Nebuzaradan** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:39:08|39:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gedaliah ... Ahikam ... Shaphan** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:39:11|39:14]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Tahpanhes** - See how you translated the name of this city in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:02:14|2:16]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
