===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **threw him into the cistern** - A "cistern" is a deep hole in the ground used for collecting and storing rain water. AT: "put him into the cistern" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They lowered Jeremiah down on ropes** - This tells how they "threw" him into the cistern.
