===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They will not give you over to them** - "The Chaldeans will not give you over to the Judeans." Here "you" refers to Zedekiah. (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **this is what Yahweh has shown me** - Here the word "this" refers to what Jeremiah will say next.
