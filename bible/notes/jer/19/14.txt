===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See** - AT: “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you”
  * **stiffened their neck and refused to listen** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
