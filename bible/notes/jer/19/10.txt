===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Tophet **- This is a "dump" (UDB). This is the place where the people of Judah have sacrificed their children to other gods according to some translations. (See: Translate Names and Explicit/Implicit)
  * **Yahweh's declaration**  - See how this has been translated in 1:8.
  * **shatters - **"breaks into many pieces".
  * **vessel **- A "bowl", "pot", or "jar" (UDB) .
  * **there is no place left for any burial **- AT: "there is no more room to bury dead bodies".
