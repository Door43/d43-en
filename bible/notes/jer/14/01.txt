===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh, through the prophet Jeremiah, has just talked about the adultery of the people.

  * **regarding** - "concerning" or "about"
  * **drought** - lack of water
  * **fall apart** - "fall into pieces"
  * **wailing** - "loud weeping" or "loud crying"
  * **trenches** - long, narrow ditches or holes in the ground
  * **their cries for Jerusalem are going up** - Their cries for Jerusalem are getting louder.
