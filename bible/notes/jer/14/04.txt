===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh, through the prophet Jeremiah, has continued to tell the people about the drought.

  * **plowmen**  - farmers who prepare the ground
  * **doe**  - a female deer, an animal with hard hooves
  * **abandons**  - "leaves"
  * **plains**  - "grasslands"
  * **jackals**  - fierce, wild dogs
  * **pant**  - breathe hard and quickly for air
  * **there is no vegetation**  - "there are no plants"
