===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See** - AT: "Pay attention to this!" See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:05:14|5:14]]
  * **a great storm is beginning from the farthest parts of the earth** - AT: "destruction is coming" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **will on that day extend from one end of the earth to the other** - AT: "will on that day cover the entire earth" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **They will be like dung on the ground **- Possible meanings are 1) there were few or nobody left to bury the people or 2) there was a total lack of concern for the bodies of the dead. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
