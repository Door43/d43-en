===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He roars against his dwelling place** - AT: "He is about to bring punishment and destroy the temple"
  * **to bring a lawsuit** - AT: "to convict the nations" or "to judge and punish the nations"
  * **He will administer justice to all flesh** - All the known nations will experience God's judgment.
  * **to all flesh** - AT: "to all mankind" or "to all people"
  * **hand over the wicked ones to the sword** - AT: "kill the evil people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
