===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **this is Yahweh’s declaration**  - See how you translated this in 1:8.
    * **Who will come down to attack us?**  - AT: "No one will attack us" (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **Who will enter our houses?**  - AT: "No one will enter our houses" (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **I have assigned the fruit of your practices to come against you** - AT: "I will punish you as you deserve because of the things you have done"
    * **the fruit of your practices**  - "the result of your deeds"
