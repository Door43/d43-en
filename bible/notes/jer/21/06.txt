===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **inhabitants of this city**  - those who live in Jerusalem
  * **Zedekiah king of Judah, his servants, the people, and whoever remains in this city after the plague, the sword, and**** the famine**  - Yahweh uses much repetition to emphasize that everyone in Jerusalem will be affected by the coming destruction.
  * **the hand of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon, of their enemies, and of the ones seeking their life**- The attackers of Jerusalem are described in three different ways; this emphasizes the number and power of the enemy that Israel faces.
  * **he will not pity them, spare them, or have compassion**  - The repetition in this phrase emphasizes the cruelty of what they are about to suffer.
