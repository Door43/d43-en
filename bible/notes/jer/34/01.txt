===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is the word that came to Jeremiah from Yahweh****- **"word" means message.
  * **together with all the kingdoms of the earth, the domains under his power **  - AT: " with the armies of all the kingdoms that he ruled." (See: doublet)
  * **waging war**  - "fighting against"
  * **and all of her cities **- "and the other cities in Judah"
  * **to give this city over **- Translate this the same way you translated in Jeremiah 32:28
  * **escape from his hand **- "escape from him" (See: Metaphor)
  * **for you will certainly be seized and given into his hand - **  AT: **" **  they will capture you a nd take you to the king of Babylon " (See: Active or Passive) (UDB)
  * **your eyes will look on the eyes of the king of Babylon **  - AT: "You will meet him"
  * **and his mouth will speak words with your mouth when you go to Babylon **  - AT:" then they will take you to Babylon"
