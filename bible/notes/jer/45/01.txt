===== translationNotes: =====

  * **at Jeremiah's dictation** - "that Jeremiah told him to write"
  * **this was in the fourth year of Jehoiakim son of Josiah** - This statement can be placed at the beginning of 45:1 as in the UDB.
  * **in the fourth year of Jehoiakim** - The words "of the rule" are understood. AT: "in the fourth year of the rule of Jehoiakim" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **fourth year** - The word "fourth" is the ordinal form of the number four. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **he said** - "Jeremiah said to Baruch"
  * **for Yahweh has added agony to my pain** - "for I was already suffering and now Yahweh causes me to be sad as well"
  * **My groaning has wearied me** - The abstract noun "groaning" can be stated as "cry." AT: "I am tired because I cry so much" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
