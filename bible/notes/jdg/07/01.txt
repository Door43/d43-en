===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jerub Baal** - This is another name for Gideon. See how you translated his name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:31|6:32]].
  * **they encamped** - "they set up their camp"
  * **spring of Harod ... hill of Moreh** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **The camp of Midian was to their north** - Here "Midian" represents the Midianite army. AT: "The Midianite army set up their camp to the north of the Israelite army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
