===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sword of Yahweh and of Gideon**  - The word "sword" is used to refer to the army that Yahweh and Gideon lead. The people in the army call themselves swords because that is what they use to fight. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
