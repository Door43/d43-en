===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gideon went up** - Here "Gideon" represents all of his soldiers. AT: "Gideon and his soldiers went up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **He defeated** - Here "He" refers to Gideon who represents all of his soldiers. AT: "Gideon and his soldiers defeated" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Nobah and Jogbehah** - These are names of towns. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zebah and Zalmunna** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:04|8:5]].
  * **panic** - extreme fear or worry that makes someone unable to think or act normally
