===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Nomad**  - people who travel from place to place to find areas where their flocks and herds can eat grass and drink water.
  * **Nobah and Jogbehah** - These are names of towns. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zebah and Zalmunna** - See how you translated their names in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:08:04|8:5]].
  * **panic** - extreme fear or worry that makes someone unable to think or act normally.
