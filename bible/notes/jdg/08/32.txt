===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a good old age** - This is an idiom. AT: "when he was very old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Ophrah** - See how you translated the name of this place in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **the clan of Abiezer** - See how you translated the name of this people group in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **turned again** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "turned away again from God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **prostituted themselves by worshiping the Baals** - This speaks of stopping worshipping Yahweh alone and worshipping a false god instead as if it were prostitution. AT: "gave themselves to worship the images of the god Baal" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Baal Berith** - This is the name of a false god. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
