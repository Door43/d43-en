===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a good old age** - This is an idiom. AT: "when he was very old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **was buried** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they buried him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Ophrah** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **the clan of Abiezer** - Translate the name of this people group as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **It came about** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **turned again** - The people rejecting Yahweh is spoken of as if they physically turned away from him. AT: "they stopped worshiping Yahwheh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **prostituted themselves by worshiping the Baals** - This speaks of worshiping false gods as if it were prostitution. AT: "they sinned against Yahweh by worshiping the Baals" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Baal Berith** - This is the name of a false god. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
