===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **and spoke to the people there in the same way**  - The phrase, "in the same way" refers to the same request for bread that was asked to the people of Succoth.
    * **When I come again in peace**  - This translation means that once he kills the kings of Midian and made peace in the land, then he will return to Penuel to destroy them.
