===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Penuel**  - The name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **and spoke to the people there in the same way**  - AT: "and asked for food there in the same way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis|Ellipsis]])
  * **When I come again in peace**  - The word "peace" refers to the time after Gideon has defeated the other kings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
