  * **Zebah and Zalmunna** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:04|8:5]].

  * **As Yahweh lives** - This idiom is a religious oath used for emphasis that what he is about to say is true. AT: "I promise you that" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
