===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Zebah and Zalmunna** - See how you translated these men's names in (See: [[en:bible:notes:jdg:08:04|8:5]])
  * **Karkor** - The name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **there had fallen** - A person who dies in battle is described as a person who has fallen. AT: "there had been killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]]) 
  * **who had drawn the sword** - To fight in the war is described by the action of pulling out a sword since soldiers would have to draw their sword to fight. AT: "who had fought in the war" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
