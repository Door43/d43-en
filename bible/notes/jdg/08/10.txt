===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the storyline. Here the narrator starts to tell a new part of the story.
  * **Zebah and Zalmunna** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:04|8:5]].
  * **Karkor** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **fifteen thousand men** - "15,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **had fallen** - This is a polite way of referring to people who died in battle. AT: "had been killed" or "had died in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **men who were trained to fight with the sword** - This is a way of referring to soldiers. AT: "soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **with the sword** - Here "sword" represents the swords and other weapons that soldiers used in battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
