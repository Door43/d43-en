===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There was a man** - This is a way to introduce a new person to the storyline. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]])
  * **Micah** - This is the name of a man. It is not the same man who wrote the book of Micah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **that were taken from you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "which someone stole from you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I stole it** - "I was the one who took it"
