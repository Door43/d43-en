===== translationNotes: =====

  * **of Bethlehem** - "from Bethlehem" 
  * **of the family of Judah** - This means that he was living among the family of Judah, that is, the tribe of Judah. AT: "who was living among the tribe of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **He stayed there to fulfill his duties** - "He lived and worked there" 
  * **find a place to live** - "find a different place to live"
  * **where I might live** - It is implied that he is looking for a place to live and work. AT: "where I might live and have a job" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
