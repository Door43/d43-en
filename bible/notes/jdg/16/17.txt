===== translationNotes: =====

  * **razor** - a sharp blade used to cut hair close to a person's skin
  * **from my mother's womb** - Here "from my mother's womb" refers to his entire life. AT: "my entire life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my strength will leave me** - Samson speaks about "strength" as if it were a person who could leave. AT: "my strength will be gone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **shaved** - To have had the hair cut close to the skin with a razor
