===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so you might be humbled**  - In this verse "humbled" refers to being helpless or weak because Samson's strength would be lost. AT: "so you will become helpless."
  * **green bowstrings** - The word "green" describes a young plant that is still moist and hard to break. "Bowstrings" are strong strings used to shoot arrows from a bow.
