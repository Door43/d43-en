===== translationNotes: =====

  * **How can you say, 'I love you,' when you do not share your secrets with me?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "When you say 'I love you,' you are just lying because you do not share your secrets with me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **pressed him hard ... pressured him** - This is an idiom. AT: "tried hard to persuade him ... kept trying to persuade him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that he wished he would die** - The author used a hyperbole, an exaggeration, to emphasize how miserable Sampson felt. AT: "that he was completely miserable" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
