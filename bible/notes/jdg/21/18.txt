===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a wife to Benjamin** - Here Benjamin refers to the male descendants of Benjamin. "a wife to the men of Benjamin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **which is north of Bethel, east of the road that goes up from Bethel to Shechem, and south of Lebonah** - This is background information to explain to the reader where the city of Shiloh is located. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **Lebonah** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
