  * **each one of you should grab a wife ... go back to the land of Benjamin** - It it understood that the Benjamites would take these women back to their own land with them. This can be made explicit. AT: "each one of you should seize on of the girls of Shiloh, and then take them back with you to the land of Benjamin to become your wife" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
