===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Manoah **- See translation of this man's name in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:13:01|13:2]].
  * **works wonders** - "does wonderful things"
  * **the angel of Yahweh went up in the flame of the altar** - "the angel of Yahweh went back up into heaven through the flames of the altar"
  * **lay facedown on the ground**- The front side of their bodies are referred to as their faces. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
