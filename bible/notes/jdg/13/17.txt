===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why do you ask my name?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "You should not ask me what my name is." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **It is wonderful** - This probably means "It is too wonderful for you to understand"
