===== translationNotes: =====

  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - The sight of Yahweh represents Yahweh's judgment or evaluation. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:02:11|2:11]]. AT: "what was evil in Yahweh's judgment" or "what Yahweh considered to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he gave them into the hand of the Philistines** - Here "hand" refers to power to gain victory in battle. AT: "he allowed the Philistines to defeat them" or "he allowed them to be oppressed by the Philistines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **forty years** - "40 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Zorah** - This was the name of a town in Israel. It was in the region of Judah near the border of Dan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Danites** - people from the tribe of Dan
  * **Manoah** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
