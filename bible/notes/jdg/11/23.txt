===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So then Yahweh, the God of Israel, has driven out the Amorites before his people Israel, and should you now take possession of their land?**  - (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **Will you not take over the land that Chemosh, your god, gives you? So whatever land Yahweh our God has given us, we will take over.**- This means that the Israelites are to live in the land that God has given them and the Amorites are to live in the land their god Chemosh has given them. (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **Now are you really better than Balak (the son of Zippor), king of Moab? **- This means that the Israelites are not better and Balak. (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **Did he dare to have an argument with Israel? **- This means that the king of Moab did not have an argument with Israel. **** (See: Rhetorical Questions)
  * **Did he ever wage war against them?**  - This means that the king of Moab did not wage war against the Israelites. (See: Rhetorical Questions)
