===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **has taken vengeance for you against your enemies, the Ammonites** - AT: "has defeated your enemies, the Ammonies, for you"
  * **Let this promise be kept for me** - AT: "Keep this promise for me" or "Keep this promise concerning me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **grieve over my virginity** - AT: "weep because I am a virgin" or "cry because I have never been married"
