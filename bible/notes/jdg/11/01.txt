===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gileadite** - This is someone who is from the region of Gilead. That is father's name is also Gilead is a coincidence. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:10:03|10:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **When his wife's sons grew up** - "When Gilead's wife's sons became adults"
  * **the land of Tob** - Tob is the name of a country.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **they came and went with him** - "they followed him" or "they went everywhere together"
