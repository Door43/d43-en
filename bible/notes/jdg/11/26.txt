===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jephthah's messengers continues to speak.

  * **three hundred years** - "300 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Heshbon** - Translate the name of this city the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:11:19|11:19]].
  * **Aroer** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Arnon** - Translate the name of this city the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:11:12|11:13]].
  * **why then did you not take them back during that time?** - Jephthah is rebuking the king of the Ammonites with this rhetorical question. AT: "you should have taken them back during that time." or "now it is too late; you should have taken them back long ago." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **done you wrong ... doing me wrong** - This is an idiom. AT: "treated you unfairly ... treating me unfairly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
