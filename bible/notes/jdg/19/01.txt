===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In those days** - This phrase introduces the beginning of another event in the storyline. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **remote** - far from where most people live
  * **was unfaithful to him** - This means that she was unfaithful in their relationship and that she began to sleep with other men. This can stated explicitly if necessary. AT: "began to sleep with other men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
