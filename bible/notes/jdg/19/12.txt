===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **turn aside into** - See how you translated "turn aside to" in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:19:10|19:11]].
  * **Gibeah** - place name (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Come** - Unlike the servant's polite request, "Come," in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:19:10|19:11]], this is a command to the servant to keep going (see UDB).
