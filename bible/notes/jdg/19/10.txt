===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that is Jerusalem** - "which was later called Jerusalem"
  * **He had a pair of saddled donkeys** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "He put saddles on his two donkeys" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Come** - This is an idiom used to make a suggestion. AT: "I suggest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **turn aside to** - This mean to take a break from their journey and stop somewhere along the route. AT: "stop at" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
