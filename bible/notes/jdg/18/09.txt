===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Are you doing nothing?** - This rhetorical question is asked sarcastically and means that they should be doing the opposite. This question can be written as a statement. AT: "You should be acting now!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]]) 
  * **Do not be slow to attack** - These two negative words "not" and "slow" together emphasize the positive idea to attack quickly. AT: "Hurry! Attack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]]) 
  * **the land is wide** - "the land is large." This is a description of the size of the land.
  * **that does not lack anything on earth** - The men use a hyperbole, an exaggeration, to emphasize that it is a very desirable place to live. AT: "We will have everything there that we need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])   
  * **not lack** - These two negative words together emphasize a positive idea. AT: "has everything" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
