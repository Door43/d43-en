===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Tola ... Puah ... Dodo** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shamir** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **arose to deliver Israel** - "came to deliver Israel" or "became the leader to deliver Israel"
  * **deliver Israel** - Here "Israel" represents the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **He judged Israel** - Here "judged" means he led the people of Israel.
  * **twenty-three years** - "23 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **was buried** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they buried him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
