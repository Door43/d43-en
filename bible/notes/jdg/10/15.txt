===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They turned away from the foreign gods** - The people no longer worshiping false gods is spoken of as if the people physically turned in a different direction away from the false gods. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **foreign gods they owned** - You can make explicit the implicit meaning of this statement. AT: "foreign gods whose images they owned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **And he became impatient over the misery of Israel** - Here Israel refers to the people of Israel. AT: "And he did not want the people of Israel to suffering any longer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
