===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you abandoned me again** - The people no longer obeying and worshiping Yahweh is spoken of as if they left Yahweh and went somewhere else. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will not keep adding to the times I deliver you** - The phrase "keep adding to the times" is an idiom that means to continue to do something. You can make explicit the implicit meaning of Yahweh's saying. AT: "I will not keep on delivering you again and again" or "You can be sure that I will stop delivering you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
