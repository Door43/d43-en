===== translationNotes: =====

  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - Here "in the sight of Yahweh" represents what Yahweh considers or thinks about something. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:02:11|2:11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh gave strength to Eglon king of Moab** - The abstract noun "strength" can be stated as an adjective. AT: "Yahweh made Eglon king of Moab strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **to Eglon king of Moab to overpower the Israelites** - Here "Eglon king of Moab" represents his army. AT: "to Eglon king of Moab and his soldiers as they attacked the Israelite army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Eglon** - This is the name of a king. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the City of Palms** - This is another name for the city of Jericho. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:01:16|1:16]].
  * **eighteen years** - "18 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
