===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **he raised them up one** - AT: "he gave them someone"
    * **Ehud...Gera** - These are the names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **left-handed** - a person who is better able to use the left side of their body.  They would write and hold a sword with their left hand.
