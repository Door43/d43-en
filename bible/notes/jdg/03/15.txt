===== translationNotes: =====

  * **called out to Yahweh** - Here this means to shout or speak loudly to someone far away. It can also mean to ask someone for help, especially God.
  * **raised up someone** - Yahweh appointing someone to do a special service for him is spoken of as if he raised or lifted up the person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Ehud ... Gera** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **left-handed** - Ehud was better able to hold a sword with his left hand.
