===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he strapped it on under his clothing on his right thigh**  - AT: "he tied it to his right thigh under his clothing"
  * **thigh**  - "upper leg"
  * **Now Eglon was a very fat man**  - Translate this as you would when you are giving additional information in a story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background|Writing Styles - Background Information]])
  * **he left with those who had carried it in**  - AT: "he led away those who brought it in"
