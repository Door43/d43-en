===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ten thousand** - "10,000"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **chosen men** - This is an idiom that means these were particularly good soldiers. AT: "well-trained soldier" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **disaster was close to them** - Here immanent disaster is spoken of as if it was standing very close by them. AT: "they would soon be completely defeated" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
