===== translationNotes: =====

  * **as one man** - This simile speaks of the group as acting as a single person. AT: "as is they were a single man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **from Dan to Beersheba** - This refers to the land as a whole. AT: "from all the elven tribes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **God—400,000 men on foot** - "God and also 400,000 regular soldiers came"
  * **ready to fight** - "capable of going to war." They were not going to fight each other.
