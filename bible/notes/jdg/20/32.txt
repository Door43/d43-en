===== translationNotes: =====

  * **just as at first** - AT: "just as before" or "just like the first two times"
  * **Baal Tamar** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Maareh Gibeah** - This is the name of a place. Other translations may read "fields of Gibeah" or "west of Gibeah" or "Maareh geba." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
