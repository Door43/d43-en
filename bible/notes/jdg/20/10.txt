===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ten men of a hundred ... one hundred of a thousand ... one thousand of ten thousand** - "10 men of 100 ... 100 of 1,000 ... 1,000 of 10,000" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **provisions** - "food" or "supplies"
  * **assembled against the city** - AT: "come together to attack the city"
  * **united with one purpose** - "united as one man" AT: "united as if they were one man"
