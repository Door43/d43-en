===== translationNotes: =====

  * **six hundred** - "600" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **turned back against the people of Benjamin** - These people of Benjamin are not the soldiers who fled to the rock of Rimmon, but the ones who were still in the city.
  * **the entire city** - Here "the ... city" refers to the people in that city. AT: "everyone who was in the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in their path** - This idiom refers to everything that they found as they went toward the city. AT: "they came to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
