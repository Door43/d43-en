===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why did you sit between the fireplaces, listening to the shepherds playing their pipes for their flocks?** - This question is asked to criticize the people of Reuben because they did not decide to come fight in battle.`   AT: "You sit around on the hillside between the lighted fires, listening to the shepherds playing their pipes, and doing nothing of importance." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
