===== Translation Notes: =====

Deborah and Barak's song continues using poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])

  * **Awake, awake** - This word is repeated to add emphasis to it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Deborah**  - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:04|Judges 4:4]].
  * **Barak... Abinoam**  - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
  * **to me among the warriors**  - The word "me" refers to Deborah.
