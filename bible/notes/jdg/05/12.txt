===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Deborah and Barak's song continues using poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]])

  * **Awake, awake** - Possible speakers are 1) the people of Israel or 2) Deborah who is speaking to herself or 3) the poet who wrote the song.
  * **Deborah** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:04|4:4]].
  * **Barak ... Abinoam** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
  * **to me with the warriors** - The word "me" refers to Deborah.
