===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **from Ephraim, whose root is in Amalek **-  People are said to have roots in an area if that is their home and where their family has developed. The development of a tree is used to describe the development of a family. AT: "from Ephraim, who live in the land that once belonged to the descendants of Amalek" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Machir** - This is the place where the descendants of Machir live.  Machir was the son of Manasseh and the grandson of Joseph. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **those who carry an officer's staff** - Military leaders are described by the symbol of their authority. AT: "military leaders" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
