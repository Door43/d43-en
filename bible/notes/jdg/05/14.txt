===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from Ephraim, whose root is in Amalek** - Here "root" refers to the original inhabitants of the land. AT: "from the tribe of Ephraim, who are living where the Amalekites once lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Machir** - This is the place where the descendants of Machir live. Machir was the son of Manasseh and the grandson of Joseph. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **from Zebulun those who carry an officer's staff** - Military leaders are described by the staff, a symbol of their authority. AT: "military leaders from Zebulun" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
