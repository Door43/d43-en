===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jael** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:17|4:18]].
  * **Heber** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:11|4:11]].
  * **Kenite** - Translate the name of this people group as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:01:16|1:16]].
  * **brought him butter** - Here "butter" refers to curdled milk. This was the best milk and a favorite drink among Jael's people. AT: "brought him yogurt" or "brought him curds" 
  * **a dish fit for princes** - This phrase means the dish was of the best quality because princes were given the best things. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
