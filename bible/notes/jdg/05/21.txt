===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Kishon River swept them away** - Because of a summer rain referred to as stars, the river flooded quickly and the water forced the enemy army down stream.
  * **Kishon**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
  * **swept** - to flow quickly and forcefully.
  * **March on my soul** - The author refers to herself by the part of her associated with bravery. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Then the sound of horses' hooves—galloping, the galloping of his mighty ones** - This sentence is used to describe the sound of the enemy solider's retreat. AT: "Then I heard the sound by the retreat of the enemy solider who rode on horses.  It was the sound of a horse's gallop, the sound his mighty warrior's horses made as they galloped away. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **galloping** - when a horse runs.
