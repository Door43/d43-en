===== translationNotes: =====

  * **She put her hand to the tent peg** - "Jael grabbed the tent peg with her left hand"
  * **tent peg** - This is a pointed piece of wood or metal, like a large nail, that is hammered into the ground to hold down a corner of a tent. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:21|4:21]].
  * **her right hand to the workman's hammer** - This can be stated as a complete sentence. AT: "she grabbed a hammer with her right hand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **hammer** - This is a heavy tool made of wood used to hit a tent peg into the ground. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:21|4:21]].
  * **Sisera** - Translate this name the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **limp** - without strength or movement
  * **he was violently killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "she killed him" or "he died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
