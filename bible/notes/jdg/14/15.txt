===== translationNotes: =====

  * **On the fourth day** - "On day 4"
  * **Trick** - mislead or fool someone into doing something they would not want to do
  * **your father's house** - Possible meanings are 1) "the house your father and his family live in" or 2) "your father's family." The word "house" can refer to the people who live in it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
