  * **riddle**  - a game in which the players must discover the answer to a difficult question
  * **can find it out**  - AT: "understand the question"
  * ** thirty linen robes and thirty sets of clothes** - "30 linen robes and 30 sets of clothes" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **linen** - a type of cloth
