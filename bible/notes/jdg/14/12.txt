===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tell you a riddle**  - A riddle is a verbal puzzle that requires a correct response to solve.
  * **can find it out**  - AT: "can figure it out"
  * **we may hear it**  - "can try to figure it out" or "we may think about it"
