===== translationNotes: =====

  * **came on Samson with power** - The phrase "came on" means that Yahweh's Spirit influenced Samson. In this case, he made him very strong. AT: "made Samson very strong" or "made Samson very powerful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **killed thirty of their men** - "killed 30 of their men" - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **their men** - "the men who lived there"
  * **plunder** - things taken by force, usually after a fight or battle
  * **their clothes** - These were from the plunder he had taken from Ashkelon. AT: "the sets of clothing that he had taken" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Burning with anger** - "Very angry"
  * **went up to his father's house** - The phrase "went up" is used here because Samson was at Timnah which is lower in elevation than where his father's house is located. 
  * **Samson's wife was given to his best friend** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "his wife's father gave her to his best friend" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **best friend** - "closest friend"
