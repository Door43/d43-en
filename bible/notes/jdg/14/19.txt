===== translationNotes: =====

  * **came on Samson with power** - This is an idiom. AT: "made Samson very strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **killed thirty men** - "killed 30 men" - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **those people** - the people who live in Ashkelon
  * **plunder** - things taken by force, usually after a fight or battle
  * **furious** - AT: "very angry"
  * **went up to his father's house** - It is implied that he went up the mountainside to his father's house.
