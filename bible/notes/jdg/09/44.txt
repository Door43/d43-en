===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the units** - "the groups of soldiers"
  * **other two** - "other 2" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Abimelech fought ... He tore down** - Here "Abimelech" represents his soldiers. AT: "Abimelech and his soldiers fought ... They tore down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **against the city** - Here "city" represents the people. AT: "against the people of Shechem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **tore down** - "demolished"
  * **spread salt over it** - "spread salt over the land." Spreading salt over land keeps anything from growing there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
