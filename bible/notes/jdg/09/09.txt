===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jotham continues telling his fable where trees represent various people groups.

  * **olive tree** - This speaks of a tree as if it were a person and could hear and speak. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Should I give up my oil ... over the other trees?** - The olive tree is asking this question to refuse to be king. This question can be expressed as a statement. AT: "I will not give up my oil ... over the other trees." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **sway** - This means to move back and forth in the wind.
  * **trees** - This speaks of trees as if they were people who could speak. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **fig tree** - This speaks of a tree as if it were a person and could hear and speak. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Should I give up my sweetness ... over the other trees?** - The fig tree is asking this question to refuse to be king. This question can be expressed as a statement. AT: "I will not give up my sweetness ... over the other trees." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
