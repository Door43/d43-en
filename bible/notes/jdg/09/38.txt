===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zebul** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:28|9:28]].
  * **Where are your proud words now, you** - Zebul is scolding Gaal with this rhetorical question. AT: "You are not speaking proudly now, you" or "You are not proud now, you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **you who said, 'Who is Abimelech that we should serve him?'** - Zebul is quoting Gaal's boast back to Gaal. This can be translated as a statement and as an indirect quote. AT: "you who said that we should not serve Abimelech." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **Are these not the men you despised?** - Zebul is challenging Gaal with this rhetorical question. This question can be translated as a statement. AT: "Here are the men that you despised." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **despised** - "hated" or "disliked"
  * **Gaal** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:26|9:26]].
  * **Many fell with deadly wounds** - This is an idiom. AT: "And many men died of wounds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
