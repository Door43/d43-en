===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and all the men who were with him** - "and all the men accompanying Abimelech" or "and all the men fighting for Abimelech"
  * **they set an ambush against Shechem** - Here "Shechem" represents the people of Shechem. AT: "they hid in order to attack the people of Shechem by surprise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **dividing into four units** - "separating into 4 groups" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Gaal ... Ebed** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:26|9:26]].
