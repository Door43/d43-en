===== translationNotes: =====

  * **His mother's relatives spoke for him to the leaders** - This means that the relatives of the mother of Abimelech spoke to the leaders, suggesting that they make Abimelech their king.
  * **they agreed to follow Abimelech** - "they agreed to let Abimelech be their leader"
  * **the house** - Here "house" represents a temple. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seventy pieces of silver** - This means seventy shekels of silver. A shekel weighs 11 grams. If it is necessary to translate this with modern measurements, you can translate it like this. AT: "almost one kilo of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **seventy** - "70" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Baal-Berith** - This is the name of a false god. See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:32|8:33]].
  * **lawless and reckless** - "violent and foolish"
