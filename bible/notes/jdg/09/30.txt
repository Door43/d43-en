===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zebul** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:31|9:28]].
  * **Gaal ... Ebed** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:31|9:26]].
  * **his anger was kindled** - This speaks of a manner of becoming angry as if it were starting a fire. AT: "he became very angry"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in order to deceive** - This means messengers were sent secretly to Abimelech, deceiving the people of Shechem as to where they were going.
  * **stirring up the city against you** - Here "city" refers to the people of the city. This speaks of the people of the city becoming aroused as if it were liquid in a pot moving around. AT: "The people in the city are being persuaded to rebel against you" or "Gaal is turning the people against you."   (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
