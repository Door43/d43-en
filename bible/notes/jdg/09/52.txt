===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **upper millstone** - Two large round stones were used to grind grain in a mill.  An upper millstone would have been the top one of these stones.
  * **armor-bearer**  - This is the man who carried the weapons of Abilelech.
  * **pierced him through**  - This means the young man put the sword right through the body of Abimelech.
