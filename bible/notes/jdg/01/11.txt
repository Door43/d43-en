===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the name of Debir was previously Kiriath Sepher** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
    * **Debir** - This is the name of a city, it means "a word." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Kiriath Sepher**  - This is the name of a city, it means "city of writing."
    * **Achsah**  - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Othniel...Kenaz**  - These are the names of men.
