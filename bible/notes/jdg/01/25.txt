===== translationNotes: =====


  * **they attacked the city** - Here "city" represents the people. AT: "they attacked the people of the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **with the edge of the sword** - Here "edge" represents the whole sword. AT: "and killed the people of the city with their swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **get away** - This is an idiom. AT: "escape" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Luz** - This new town started in the land of the Hittites was named after the town of Luz, near Bethel, that the man had left.
  * **which is its name to this day** - "which is still its name." Here "to this day" refers to the time when the book of Judges was written.
