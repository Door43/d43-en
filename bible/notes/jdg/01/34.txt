===== Translation Notes: =====
See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]

  * **not allowing them to come down** - stopping them from coming down
  * **plain** - a large area of flat land without trees.
  * **Mount Heres** - a large hill on which the city Aijalon was built. 
  * **Aijalon...Shaalbim...Akrabbim** - These are the names of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the military might of the house of Joseph conquered them** - "but the tribes of people who descended from Joseph were able to conquer them because of their powerful army"
  * **Sela** - This was the name of the capital city of the neighboring country.
