===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Zebulun did not drive out** - Here "Zebulun" represents the men or the soldiers of the tribe of Zebulun. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Kitron ... Nahalol** - These are names of cities in the land of Canaan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **but Zebulun forced** - Here "Zebulun" represents the people of the tribe of Zebulun. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **hard labor** - "difficult labor"
