===== Translation Notes: =====

 
    * **will lead us when we** - The words "us" and "we" represent the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
    * **when we go up** - In the Hebrew language you often go "up" or "down" to a place, or in this case a battlefield. AT: "when we go" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **See** - The word "See" here adds emphasis to what follows. "Behold" or "Look" AT: "Indeed"
    * **that was assigned to us...that was assigned to you**  - "that Yahweh assigned to us...that Yahweh assigned to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
