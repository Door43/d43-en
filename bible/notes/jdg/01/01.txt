===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the note on the Important Term page concerning how to translate this. 
    * **that was assigned to us...that was assigned to you**  - "that Yahweh assigned to us...that Yahweh assigned to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **will lead us** - The word "us" represents the people of Israel.
