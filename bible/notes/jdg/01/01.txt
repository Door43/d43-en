===== translationNotes: =====

  * **death** - Here this means physical death of Joshua.
  * **will attack the Canaanites for us** - The word "us" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **Come up with us** - This probably means that they were to go up to a higher level of ground, such as a hill or plateau. Some languages do not usually indicate whether people were going up or down. AT: "Come with us" or "Go up with us"
  * **See** - "Look." This adds emphasis to what follows. AT: "Indeed"
  * **this land** - This refers to the land where the Canaanites lived. AT: "the land of the Canaanites"
  * **their brothers** - Here this means male people who shares at least one biological parent with another person.
  * **that was assigned to us ... that was assigned to you** - "that Yahweh assigned to us ... that Yahweh assigned to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **we will likewise go with you** - "we will also go with you" or "in the same way we will go with you"
  * **tribe** - Here this means God divided the people of Israel into twelve tribes. Each tribe was descended from a son or grandson of Jacob.
