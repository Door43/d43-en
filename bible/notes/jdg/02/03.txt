===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The angel of Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Israel.

  * **So now I say, 'I will not ... trap for you.'** - This has a quotation within a quotation. This direct quotation can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "So now I tell you that I will not ... trap for you.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotesinquotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **become thorns in your sides** - The Canaanites troubling the Israelites is spoken of as if the Canaanites would be thorns in the side of the Israelites. AT: "cause you trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **thorns** - sharp pieces of wood up to 7 centimeters long that stick out from some plants
  * **their gods will become a trap for you** - The Israelites worshiping the Canaanite gods is spoken of as if the false gods were a hunter's trap that catches an animal and causes it harm. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **shouted and wept** - "cried many tears"
