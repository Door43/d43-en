===== Translation Notes: =====
   
 
  * **Bochim** - This place was named to remember this event. "Bochim" means "crying." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **brought you up from Egypt** - "led you up from Egypt"
  * **break my covenant with you** - "fail to do what I said I would do for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you have not listened to my voice** - "you have not obeyed my commands"
  * **What is this that you have done?** - This question is asked to cause the people of Israel to realize they have disobeyed Yahweh and will suffer because of it. AT: "You have done a terrible thing."  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
