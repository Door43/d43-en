===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh made Sisera's army confused** - "Yahweh made Sisera's unable to think clearly" or "Yahweh made Sisera's army panic"
  * **all his chariots** - Here "chariots" represents the soldiers driving the chariots. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Barak pursued** - Here "Barak" represents his army. AT: "Barak and his soldiers chased" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Harosheth** - Translate this the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **the whole army of Sisera was killed by the edge of the sword** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Barak and his soldiers killed Sisera's whole army with their swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the edge of the sword** - Here "the sword" represents the Israelites who used swords in battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
