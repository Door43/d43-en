===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sisera** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **confused** - "unable to think clearly"
  * **Barak** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
  * **pursued** - "chased"
  * **Harosheth** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **killed by the edge of the sword** - This phrase refers to swords by the part of the sword that is sharp and can be used to kill. AT: "killed with swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
