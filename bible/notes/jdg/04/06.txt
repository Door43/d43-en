===== Translation Notes: =====
 The writer of Judges refers to men, a city, a mountain and a river by their names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Barak...Abinoam**  - The names of men. 
  * **Kedesh**  - The name of a city. 
  * **Mount Tabor**  - The name of a mountain. 
  * **ten thousand men**  - "10,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **draw out**  - to cause people to come away from a safe location.
  * **Sisera...Jabin**  - See how you translated the names of these men in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|Judges 4:2]].
  * **Kishon** - The name of a river.
