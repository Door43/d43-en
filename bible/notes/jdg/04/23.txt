===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God defeated Jabin, the king of Canaan, before the people of Israel** - God causing the Israelites to defeat Jabin and his army is spoken of as is God himself defeated Jabin as the people of Israel watched. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The might** - "The military power"
  * **they destroyed him** - Here "him" refers to Jabin who represents his army. AT: "they destroyed Jabin and his army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
