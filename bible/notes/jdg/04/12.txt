===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When they told Sisera** - Here "they" does not identify anyone specifically. AT: "When someone told Sisera"
  * **Sisera** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **Barak ... Abinoam ... Mount Tabor** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
  * **Sisera called out all his chariots** - Here "chariots" represents the soldiers who drove the chariots. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **nine hundred iron chariots** - "900 iron chariots" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Harosheth Haggoyim** - See how you translated the name of this city in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **Kishon River** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:7]].
