===== Translation Notes: =====

Barak has a discussion with Deborah

  * **Barak**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|Judges 4:6]].
  * **the road on which you are going** - The choice Barak makes is compared to  Barak choosing a road on which he will travel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Sisera **- See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|Judges 4:2]].
  * **by her strength** - "by her leadership" or "by her ability to lead"
  * **Deborah **- See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:04:04|Judges 4:4]].
