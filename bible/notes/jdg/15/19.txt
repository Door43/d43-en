===== translationNotes: =====

  * **split open the hollow place that is at Lehi** - "opened up a hole in the ground at Lehi"
  * **Lehi** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:15:09|15:9]]
  * **his strength returned and he revived** - The author said basically the same thing twice for emphasis. These two statements can be combined. AT: "He got his strength back again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **En Hakkore** - This is the name of a spring of water. The name means "spring of him who prayed." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the days of the Philistines** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "during the time the Philistines controlled Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **for twenty years** - "for 20 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
