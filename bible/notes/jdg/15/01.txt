===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He said to himself** - This is an idiom. "He thought" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will go to my wife's room** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "I will go to my wife's room, so we may sleep together" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **would not allow him to go in** - You can make clear the understood information. AT: "would not permit him to go in to her room" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **so I gave her to your friend** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "so I gave her to be married to your friend" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **is she not?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "I hope you agree." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Take her instead** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "Choose her to be your wife instead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
