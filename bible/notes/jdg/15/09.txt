===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **prepared for battle** - AT: "organized themselves for battle"
  * **Lehi**  - This is the name of a town in Judah. It was given this name because of what happened in this event. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **so we may do to him as he has done to us** - The people knew that Samson had killed many Philistines. AT: "so we may kill him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
