===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from an ephah of flour** - If it is necessary to use modern a modern measurement here is one way of doing it. AT: "with 22 liters of flour" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **broth** - water that has had food, such as meat, cooked in it
  * **them to him** - "them to the angel of God"
  * **angel of God** - This is the same as the angel of Yahweh. AT: "God, who was in the form of an angel" or "God"
