===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh looked at him** - "Yahweh looked at Gideon"
  * **Have I not sent you?** - This can be expressed as a statement. AT: "I, Yahweh, have sent you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **how can I deliver Israel?** - This can be expressed as a statement. AT: "I cannot possibly deliver Israel!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] )
  * **See** - The word "See" is an idiom that means understand. AT: "Please understand." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
