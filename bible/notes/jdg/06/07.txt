===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the house of slavery** - The word "house" is used to describe an area people live in with a certain characteristic.  Egypt is called a house of slavery because when the people of Israel lived in that place, they were slaves. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
