===== translationNotes: =====

  * **may be made complete** - "may become spiritually mature"
  * **in the use my authority** - "when I use my authority"
  * **so that I may build you up, and not tear you down** - Paul speaks of helping the Corinthians to know Christ better as if he were constructing a building. AT: "to help you become better followers of Christ and not to discourage you so you stop following him." See how these words were translated in [[:en:bible:notes:1co:10:07|10:8]].
