===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do you not realize this about yourselves that Jesus Christ is in you?** - Paul asks a question to emphasize his point. AT: "You should know that Jesus Christ is in you!" or "Jesus Christ is in you. You should know this already!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **in you** - Possible meanings are 1) living inside each individual or 2) "among you," part of and the most important member of the group.
