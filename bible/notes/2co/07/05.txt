===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When we came to Macedonia** - Here the word "we" refers to Paul and Timothy but not to the Corinthians or Titus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **our bodies had no rest** - Here "bodies" refers to the whole person. AT: "we had no rest" or "we were very tired" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **we were troubled in every way** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "we experienced trouble in every way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **by conflicts on the outside and fears on the inside** - Possible meanings for "outside" are 1) "outside of our bodies" or 2) "outside of the church." The word "inside" refers to their inward emotions. AT: "by conflicts with other people and by fears within ourselves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **by the comfort that Titus had received from you** - Paul received comfort from knowing that the Corinthians had comforted Titus. AT: "by learning about the comfort that Titus had received from you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
