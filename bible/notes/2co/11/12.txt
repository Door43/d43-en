===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - As Paul continues to affirm his apostleship, he talks about false apostles.

  * **so that I might cut off the opportunity of** - Paul speaks of a situation that allows his enemies to boast as if it were the branch of a tree that he needs to cut off. AT: "so that I might make it impossible for" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **those who desire the opportunity to be found as we are** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "those who desire to have people think that they are like us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **what they are boasting about** - While Paul's boast was that he "freely preached the gospel" ([[:en:bible:notes:2co:11:07|11:7]]), his enemies boasted that they could speak well ([[:en:bible:notes:2co:11:05|11:6]]).
  * **For such people** - "I do what I do because people like them"
  * **deceitful workers** - "dishonest workers"
  * **disguise themselves as apostles** - "are not apostles, but they try to make themselves look like apostles"
