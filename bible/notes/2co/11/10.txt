===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **this boasting … will not be silenced…**  - Paul will continue to boast throughout all of Achaia which is modern day southern Greece. 
  * **And why? Because I do not love you? **- AT: "I will continue to keep you from paying for my needs, because this shows others that I love you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
