===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **had already begun the task with you**  - AT: " had encouraged your giving in the first place".  Paul is referring to the collection of money from the Corinthians for the believers in Jerusalem.
  * **to bring to completion this act of grace on your part**. - AT: "to return to you and encourage you to complete this ministry of giving."
  * **But as you excel in everything **- AT: "Because you do better than expected in so many ways"
  * **in faith,**  - "in your loyalty to God and us,"
  * **in speech, - **"in the way you communicate (or speak"),"
  * **in knowledge,**  - This may mean "comprehension" or "understanding" of many things."
  * **in all diligence,**  - "in all enthusiastic (or "careful attention to"),"
  * **and in your love for us**  - AT: "and in the way you demonstrate of your love for us"
  * **make sure you also excel in this gracious work.**  - AT: "make sure you do better than expected in sacrificially giving for the suffering saints in Jerusalem.
