====== 2 Corinthians 08 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

**Gift to the Church in Jerusalem**

Chapters 8 and 9 are a new section dealing with the gift from the churches in Greece to the poor believers in Jerusalem.

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of 8:15, which are a quotation from the Old Testament.  

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Gift to the Church in Jerusalem**

The year before the church in Corinth had started preparing a gift of money for the poor believers in Jerusalem. The churches in Macedonia have given generously. Now Paul is sending Titus and two other believers to Corinth to encourage the church in Corinth to be generous also. ; He and the other three will carry the money to Jerusalem because they want people to know that it is being done honestly.

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

“gave themselves to the Lord. Then they gave themselves to us by the will of God” meaning they did God wanted and what we wanted them to do.
