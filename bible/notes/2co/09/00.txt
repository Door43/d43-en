====== 2 Corinthians 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. Many English translations indent the line of 9:9, which is a quotation from the Old Testament.  

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Paul encourages them to be generous and get the gift ready**

Paul has told the churches in Macedonia that the Corinthian church was already collecting money for the gift. Now he is send these men ahead to make sure they are ready. They should be generous because the more they give the greater blessing it will be to them from God. Christ became poor for us. God will be praised for their generosity.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Metaphors**

Paul uses three agricultural [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]s: “the one who sows sparingly will also reap sparingly” meaning they just give a little bit they will just receive just a little blessing. “multiply your seed for sowing” means give them more money to give away, “the harvest of your righteousness” meaning the righteousness that they will gain from your generosity.

**Idiom**

He uses one [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]: “in his heart” means as he wants to.

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

“Box the air” means useless activity that does not accomplish anything.
