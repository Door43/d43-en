===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God is able to make every blessing multiply for you** - "God is able to increase every blessing for you." As one gives financially to other believers, God also gives more blessings to the giver so that the giver will have everything he needs.
  * **so that you may multiply every good deed** - "so that you may be able to do more and more good deeds"
  * **It is as it is written** - "This is just as it is written." This can be stated in active form. AT: "This is just as the writer wrote" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
