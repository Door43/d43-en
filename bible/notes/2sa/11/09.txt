===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Have you not come from a journey?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] since David and Uriah both knew that Uriah certainly just came from a journey.   See also: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]].
    * **How then can I go into my house to eat and to drink and to sleep with my wife?**  - This is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] since Uriah felt it would not be right, proper, or respectful for him as a warrier to go home when the army is in danger.
