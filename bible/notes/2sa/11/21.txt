===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Joab continues to explain why the army was so close to the wall. 

  * **Who killed Abimelech ... Jerubbesheth?** - This rhetorical question is used to rebuke David and can be translated as a statement. AT: "Remember how Abimelech and Jerubbesheth were killed!"
  * **Did not a woman cast an upper millstone ... Thebez?** - This rhetorical question is used to rebuke David and can be translated as a statement. AT: "A woman cast an upper millstone on Abimelech ... Thebez and killed him!"
  * **Why did you go ... wall?** - This rhetorical question is used to rebuke David and can be translated as a statement. AT: "You should not have gone ... wall!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Abimelech** - Abimelech was a son of Gideon. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:29|Judges 8:31]] and [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:52|Judges 9:52-53]])
  * **Jerubbesheth** - This is another name for Gideon.
  * **Did not a woman cast an upper millstone on him from the wall, so that he died at Thebez?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "A woman cast an upper millstone ... Thebez." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **millstone** - a heavy stone that would roll, used to crush grain for making bread 
  * **Thebez** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
