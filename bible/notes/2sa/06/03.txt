===== Translation Notes: =====
David and the army of the nation of Israel move the Ark of the Covenant
    * **Abinadab.. Uzzah... Ahio**  - These are male names of people. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **house of Israel** - This refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **tambourines, rattles, and cymbals** - these are musical instruments
