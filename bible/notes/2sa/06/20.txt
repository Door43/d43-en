===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Michal**  - This is the female name of David's wife (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **undresses himself before the eyes of the slave girls** - Michal is rebuking David. David has only been wearing a linen ephod, in dancing he has apparently exposed his genitals to those around him. 
  * **eyes of the slave girls** - Here "eyes" represent the perception of the slave girls. AT: "in front of the slave girls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **crude fellows**  - David is being compared to a common and unrefined person.
