===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he blessed the people in the name of Yahweh of hosts** - To bless "in the name of Yahweh of hosts" means to bless with Yahweh's power and authority, or as his representative. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **cake of raisins** - a baked sweet bread made with dried grapes
