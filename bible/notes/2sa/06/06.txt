===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nakon** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **anger of Yahweh burned** - Here the "anger of Yahweh" is spoken of as if it were fire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for his sin** - It was a sin for Uzzah to touch the ark because Yahweh had commanded that no one should touch the ark. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
