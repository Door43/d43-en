===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When they were** - "When Joab and the men of Judah were"
  * **belt** - a strip of leather or other material used to hold clothing or weapons in place 
  * **sheathed sword** - This means the sword was in its protective covering.
  * **the sword fell** - Joab let the sword fall out to fool Amasa into thinking that he (Joab) was unarmed. Now Joab could get very close to Amasa. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
