===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - "Now" marks a break in the main storyline. This new section gives background information about the men who served King David. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **Joab was over ... Benaiah son of Jehoiada was over ... Adoniram was over** - The phrase "was over" refers to having authority over a group of people. AT: "Joab had authority over ... Benaiah son of Jehoiada had authority over ... Adoniram had authority over" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Jehoiada ... Jehoshaphat ... Ahilud** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:08:15|8:16-18]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Kerethites ... Pelethites** - Translate the names of these people groups the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:08:15|8:16-18]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the men who did forced labor** - "the slave workers"
  * **Adoniram ... Sheva ... Ira** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jairite** - This is the name of a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
