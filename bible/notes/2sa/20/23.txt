===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. This new section gives background information about the men who served King David. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
    * **Jehoiada...Cherethites...Pelethites...Jehoshaphat...Ahilud** - See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:08:15|8:16-18]].
    * **Adoram was over the men who did forced labor** - "Adoram was in charge of the slaves"
    * **Adoram...Sheva...Ira** - These are names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Jairite** - This is the name of a people group. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
