===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It came about after this** - "It happened after this." This phrase is used to introduce a new event to the storyline. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **half-sister** - Amnon and Tamar shared the same father but not the same mother.
  * **full sister** - Absalom and Tamar shared the same father and mother.
  * **Amnon was so frustrated that he became sick because of his sister Tamar** - Amnon was frustrated because he desire to sleep with his sister Tamar. AT:  "Amnon was so frustrated with desire for his sister Tamar that he felt sick" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
