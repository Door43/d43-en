===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jonadab son of Shammah, David's brother** - These are the names of men. Shammah was David's brother. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **shrewd** - crafty or deceptive 
  * **depressed** - in an emotional state of extreme unhappiness
  * **Will you not tell me?** - The missing words may be added to this question. Also, this rhetorical question is a request for Jehonadab to tell him why he is depressed. It may be written as a statement. AT: "Will you not tell me why you are depressed?" or "Please, tell me why you are depressed." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
