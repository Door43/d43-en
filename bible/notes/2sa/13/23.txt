===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It came about after two full years** - This explains that two whole years has passed and introduces the next event in the story line. The phrase "full years" means that they are complete years. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **shearers** - These are people who cut the wool from sheep. 
  * **Baal Hazor** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Look now** - This is a phrase used to draw someone's attention to what is said next. AT: "Listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **your servant** - Absalom is calling himself "your servant" to show respect.
  * **has sheep shearers** - It was customary for people in Israel to have a party after they had sheared their sheep.
  * **may the king** - Although he is talking to his father the king, he calls him "the king" instead of "you" to show respect for him.
