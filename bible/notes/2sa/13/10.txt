===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that I may eat from your hand** - This is a request for Tamar to serve the food to him personally. AT: "and serve it to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **do not force me** - She is referring to sleeping with him. AT: "do not force me to sleep with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **appalling** - very shameful
