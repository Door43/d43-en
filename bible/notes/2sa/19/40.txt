===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Kimham**  - Translate this as you did in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:19:37]].
    * **Why have our brothers, the men of Judah, stolen you away and brought the king and his family over the Jordan, and all David's men with him?** - This question is asked because the people of Israel feel betrayed by the people of Judah. AT: "It is not right that the people of Judah who did not support you as king have the privilege if bringing you back across the Jordan River." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **the Jordan**  - A shortened name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
