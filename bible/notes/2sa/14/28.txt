===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the king's face** - This refers to the king. AT: "the king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Absalom sent word for Joab** - This means that he sent a messenger to Joab with a request. AT: "Absalom sent a messenger to Joab asking him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to send him to the king** - It is implied that Absalom wanted Joab to come to him to see him and to intercede for him so that he would be allowed to see the king. AT:  to come to him and to intercede for him so that he could see the king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **So Absalom sent word a second time** - This means that he sent a messenger to Joab again with the same request. AT: "So Absalom sent a messenger to Joab again with the same request" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
