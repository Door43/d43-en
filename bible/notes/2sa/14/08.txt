===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **may the guilt be on me and on my father's family** - Here there implicit information. The "guilt" refers back to the brother killing the brother. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The king and his throne** - Here the word "throne" refers to any who becomes king in David's place. AT: "the king and his descendants" or "the king and his family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
