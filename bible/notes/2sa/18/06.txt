===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **countryside**  - the land and scenery of a rural area
    * **battle**  - a sustained fight or struggle
    * **forest**  - a large area covered with trees and undergrowth
    * **twenty thousand**  - a number indicating a unit (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
