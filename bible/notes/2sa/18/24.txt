===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main story line and the beginning of a new part of the story.
    * **watchman**  - a person who guards property from thieves
    * **there is news in his mouth** - AT: "he has come to tell us what happened" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
