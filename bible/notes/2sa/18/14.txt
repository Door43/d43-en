===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will not wait for you** - Joab meant that he should not continue talking to the man. AT: "I will not waste anymore time talking to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **heart of Absalom** - Here Absalom's hearts refers to his chest or upper body. AT: "chest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **armor** - This refers to both the armor he would wear to protect himself and to his weapons. AT: "armor and weapons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
