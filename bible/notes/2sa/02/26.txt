===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **called to** - "shouted" or "yelled."
  * **"Must the sword devour forever? **  - AT: "We do not need to keep fighting each other." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Do you not know it will be bitter in the end? How long will it be before you tell your men to stop pursuing their brothers?** - AT: "This will not have a good ending, stop this now so that brothers will not have to kill brother."
