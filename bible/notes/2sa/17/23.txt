===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **saw** - "knew" or "understood"
  * **saddled** - to place a blanket or small leather seat on a donkey or horse for the rider to sit on
  * **set his affairs in order** - He told his family what to do with his possessions after he died and explained to them his death. AT: "He explained his death to his family and got them ready for it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
