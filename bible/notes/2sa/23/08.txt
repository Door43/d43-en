===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jeshbaal** - This is the name of a man. Other versions read Josheb Basshebeth, Jashobeam, Ishbaal, or Ishbosheth, because various ancient manuscripts have these variations. Translators may choose to say this in a footnote to their translation (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Hachmonite** - This is the name of a people group. AT: "the Tahchemonite" or "son of Hachmon"
    * **eight hundred** - "800" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
