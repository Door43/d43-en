===== Translation Notes: =====

The list of men and places they are from concludes at the end of this.

  * **Zelek, Naharai, Gareb**  - Names of men (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Beerothite**  - Translate the same way you did in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:04:01]].
  * **Zeruiah**  - Translate the same way you did in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:02:12]]. 
  * **Ira**  - Translate the same way you did in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:20:23]].
  * **thirty-seven in all**  - A number, 37. The total of those listed previously. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
