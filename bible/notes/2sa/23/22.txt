===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **did these feats** - "did these mighty deeds"
  * **he was named alongside the three mighty men** - AT: "people praise him like they praised the three mighty men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **He was more highly regarded than the thirty soldiers in general, but he was not regarded quite as highly as the three most elite soldiers** - AT: "He was more famous than the other 30 soldiers except for the three best soldiers" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the three most elite soldiers** - This refers to  Josheb Basshebeth, Eleazar, and Shammah. 
  * **his bodyguard** - A group of soldiers in charge of guarding David.
