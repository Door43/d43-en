===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Three of the thirty**  - Refers to the first three (3), in a group totaling 30. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Adullam**  - Name of a place. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Valley of Rephaim**  - A low place between two high hills or mountains in a place called Rephaim. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **cave**  - A natural opening deep into the side of a hill or mountain.
