===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Abishai...Zeruiah** - See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:02:18|2:18]].
    * **captain over the three** - This means Abishai was the leader of the three who went and got water for David.
    * **three hundred men** - "300 men" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **He was often mentioned along with the three soldiers** - AT: "People often mentioned him when they spoke about the three bravest men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **Was he not even more famous than the three?** - The writer uses a question to emphasize that Abishai was more famous than the three bravest soldiers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **three most famous  soldiers** - This refers to  Josheb Basshebeth, Eleazar, and  Shammah. Abishai was not as famous as these soldiers.
