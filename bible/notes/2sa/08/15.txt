===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Joab son of Zeruiah** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:02:12|2:13]].
  * **Ahilud ... Ahitub ... Ahimelech ... Seraiah ... Jehoiada** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ahilud was recorder** - A recorder was a person who spoke to the people and told them the king's announcements. AT: "Ahilud was the herald" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Cherethites ... Pelethites** - These are names of people-groups. These people were David's bodyguards. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
