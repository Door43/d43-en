===== translationNotes: =====


  * **When the Arameans saw that they were being defeated by Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "when the Arameans realized that the Israelites were defeating them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Hadarezer ... Shobak** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **from beyond the Euphrates River** - This means the east side of the Euphrates River.
  * **They came to Helam** - Here "came to" can be translated as "went to" or "gathered at" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **Helam** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **at their head** - This phrase refers to a position of authority. AT: "leading them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
