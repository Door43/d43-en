===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **gathered all Israel together** - Only the army of the nation of Israel was assembled. This is not every person in the nation of Israel that was assembled. AT: "assembled the rest of the army of Israel together." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Helam... Ammon**  - These are the names of places. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
    * **Shobach, Hadarezer**  - These are male names.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
