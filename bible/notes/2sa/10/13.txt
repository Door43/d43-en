===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from Abishai** - Here Abishai represents himself and his soldiers. AT: "from Abishai and his soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **into the city** - Here "the city" refers to Rabbah, the capital city of the Ammonites.
  * **and went back to Jerusalem** - "and returned to Jerusalem"
