===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **take hold of him and kiss him**  - "treated him as a friend and as an equal" (See: symbolicaction)
  * **all Israel**  - this refers to the men of the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **Absalom stole the hearts**  - This phrase means that Absalom was convincing the men to be loyal to him rather than to David. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
