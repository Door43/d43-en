===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who were invited** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "who he had invited" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **went in their innocence** - "went innocently"
  * **he sent for Ahithophel** - This means that he sent a messenger to go get Ahithophel and bring him back to him. AT: "he sent a messenger to go get Ahithophel who was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Ahithophel** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Giloh** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
