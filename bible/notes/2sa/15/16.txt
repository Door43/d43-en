===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to keep the palace** - Here the word "keep" means to care for. AT: "to care for the palace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **at the last house** - This refers to the last house they would come to when leaving the city. AT: "at the last house as they were leaving the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Kerethites ... Pelethites** - Translate the names of these people groups the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:08:15|8:18]].
  * **Gittites** - Translate the name of this people group the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:06:10|6:10]].
  * **six hundred men** - "600 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
