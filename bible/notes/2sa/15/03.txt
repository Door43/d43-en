===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **say to him**  - Refers to the one (anyone) met on the road in the previous verse. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **every man who had any dispute**  - Refers to potential people in the future, not yet in the place.
    * **dispute**  - a disagreement, argument, or debate
    * **Look, your case is good and right** - possessive singular you [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]]
