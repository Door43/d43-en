===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So Absalom arose** - "So Absalom left"
  * **throughout all the tribes of Israel** - Here the places where the tribes of Israel lived are referred to as the tribes themselves. AT: "throughout the land of the tribes of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the sound of the trumpet** - "a trumpet being blown"
