===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Absalom sent spies throughout all the tribes of Israel**  - Here "all the tribes of Israel" means throughout the whole land of the Nation of Israel to all of those in power at levels of government.  See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]].
