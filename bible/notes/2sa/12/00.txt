====== 2 Samuel 12 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the final expansion of David's kingdom and records the end of the war with Ammon.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David's punishment ==
God sent a [[:en:obe:kt:prophet|prophet]] to confront David. He said because David had Uriah killed, there would always be killing in his family and his baby would die. David prayed that the baby be allowed to live but he died. Then, Bathsheba had another son who was named Solomon.
