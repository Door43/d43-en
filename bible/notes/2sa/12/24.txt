===== translationNotes: =====

  * **went in to her, and slept with her** - The phrase "went in to her" is a graphic description of David having sex with his wife. These two phrases have the same meaning are used together to emphasize that they slept together. AT: "slept with her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **he sent word through Nathan the prophet** - Here "word" refers to a message that Yahweh told Nathan to tell David. AT: "he sent Nathan to tell him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Jedidiah** - This is another name for David's son Solomon, which Yahweh chose for him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
