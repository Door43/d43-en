===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **talent** - an ancient measurement of weight
    * **a precious stone** - a rare gemstone such as a diamond, ruby, sapphire, emerald, or opal
    * **plunder** - valuable things taken from a defeated enemy
    * **quantities** - "amounts" or "numbers"
