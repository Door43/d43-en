===== translationNotes: =====

  * **out of the hand of Saul** - Here the word "hand" refers to control. AT: "out of Saul's control" or "from Saul's control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your master's wives into your arms** - Here Yahweh describes David having his master's wive as his own wives, by saying that they are "in his arms." AT: "your master's wives as your own" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I also gave you the house of Israel and Judah** - Here Yahweh speaks of how he gave David his authority as king over Israel and Judah as if he gave him the houses of Israel and Judah as a gift. The phrase "the house of" means "the people of." AT: "I also gave you authority as king over the people of Israel and Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **if that had been too little** - "if I had not given you enough"
