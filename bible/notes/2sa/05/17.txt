===== translationNotes: =====

  * **David had been anointed as king over Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Israel had anointed David as king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they all went out looking for him** - Here "all" is a generalization that means the Philistine army. AT: "the Philistine army went looking for him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]]) 
  * **Valley of Rephaim** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
