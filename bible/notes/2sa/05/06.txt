===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David and the army of Israel attack Jerusalem. 

  * **You will not come here except to be turned away by the blind and the lame** - This can be stated in a positive form. AT: "If you come here, even the blind and the lame will be able to turn you away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **the blind and the lame** - those people who cannot see or walk
