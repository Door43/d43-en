===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Your servant will do all that my master the king commands his servant** - Ziba refers to himself as "your servant" and refers to David as "my master." AT: "I, your servant will do all that you, my king, command me to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **Mika** - This is the name of the son of Mephibosheth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **all who lived in the house of Ziba** - Here "house" represents Ziba's family. AT: "all of Ziba's family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he always ate at the king's table** - Here "table" represents being together with David or in his presence. Eating with the king at his table was a great honor. AT: "he always ate with the king at his table" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **though he was lame in both his feet** - "thouge both of his feet were damaged." Here "feet" represents the ability to walk. AT: "though he was unable to walk" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
