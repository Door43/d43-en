===== translationNotes: =====

  * **King David sent** - It is understood that David sent messengers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Mephibosheth** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:04:04|4:4]].
  * **came to David** - The word "came" can be translated as "went." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **I am your servant** - Mephibosheth refers to himself as "your servant" to show great honor to David.
