===== Translation Notes: =====
David continues his song to Yahweh.

  * **I have kept the ways of Yahweh** - Here "the ways" refers to the ways Yahweh wants his people to act. This means David has done what Yahweh commands.
  * **have been before me** - This means David constantly reads and thinks about God's decrees.
