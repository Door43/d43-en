===== Translation Notes: =====
David concludes his song to Yahweh.
    * **to your name** - Here "name" refers to Yahweh reputation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **And he shows his covenant loyalty to his anointed one** - David is may be referring to the promises Yahweh made in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:07:08|7:8]]
