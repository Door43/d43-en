===== Translation Notes: =====
This continues David's song to Yahweh. He uses parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **For who is God except Yahweh? And who is a rock except our God?** - David uses questions for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **who is a rock** - David compares Yahweh to a rock to emphasize his strength and ability to protect his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he leads the blameless person on his path** - This means Yahweh keeps the blameless person safe and removes anything that may harm him.
