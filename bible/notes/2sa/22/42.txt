===== Translation Notes: =====
David continues his song to Yahweh.

    * **They cried** - "My enemies cried"
    * **They cried out to Yahweh, but he did not answer them** - The time for Yahweh's judgment had come upon them.
    * **like dust on the ground...like mud in the streets** - David completely destroyed his enemies. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
