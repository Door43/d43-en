===== Translation Notes: =====
David continues his song to Yahweh.

  * **pursued my enemies** - "chased my enemies"
  * **I devoured them and smashed them** - AT: "I completely destroyed them like a wild animal devouring its prey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
