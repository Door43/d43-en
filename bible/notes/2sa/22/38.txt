===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David continues his song to Yahweh.

  * **pursued my enemies** - "chased my enemies"
  * **I devoured them and smashed them** - Here David compares himself to a wild animal. AT: "I completely destroyed them like a wild animal devouring its prey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **under my feet** - Here "feet" refers to the power and control of victory over his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
