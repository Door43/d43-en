===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David continues his song to Yahweh.

  * **I can run over a barricade** - Here "barricade" may refer to a group of soldiers or to a stone wall. Either way it means God enables David to defeat his enemies.
  * **I can leap over a wall** - David is exaggerating to emphasize Yahweh's help. AT: "I can climb over the wall that surrounds their city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **The word of Yahweh is pure** - "Everything Yahweh says is true"
  * **He is a shield** - The metaphor "a shield" emphasizes God's power to protect his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
