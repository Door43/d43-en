  * **They crossed** - "Joab and the commanders of the army crossed"
  * **Aroer** - This was a city on the northern edge of the Arnon River. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jazer** - This is a town in Gad. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
