===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **May Yahweh your God multiply the number of people**  - May your God make many the number of people
  * **and may the eyes of my lord the king see it take place. But why does my lord the king want this?**- and may your eyes see it take place, oh King, but, why do you want this?
  * **Nevertheless, the king’s word was final against Joab and against the commanders of the army**. ** - ** Still, the king sent Joab and his officers out. And they left the king to count the number of people in Israel.
