===== translationNotes: =====


  * **villain** - someone who is evil, a criminal or lawbreaker 
  * **man of blood** - This metonym refers to David, who had killed many in battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh has repaid all of you for the blood of Saul's family** - Here the metonym "blood" refers to the deaths caused to the family of Saul. AT: "Yahweh is paying you all back for killing many of Saul's family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **into the hand of Absalom your son** - Here "hand" refers to control. AT: "into the control of Absalom your son." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
