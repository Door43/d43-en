===== translationNotes: =====

  * **villain** - someone who is evil, a criminal or lawbreaker 
  * **man of blood** - Here "blood" refers to all of men he was responsible for killing in battle. AT: "murderer" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh has repaid** - Yahweh repays them by punishing them. This can be stated clearly. AT: "Yahweh has punished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **for the blood you shed within the family of Saul** - Here "blood" refer to people who were killed from Saul's family. The king was responsible for them dying. AT: "for killing many of Saul's family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in whose place you have reigned** - David reigned as king over the same people whom Saul had previously reigned. AT: "in whose place you have reigned as king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **into the hand of Absalom** - Here "hand" refers to control. AT: "into the control of Absalom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
