===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **loyalty**  - a strong feeling of support
    * **Is this your loyalty to your friend? Why did you not go with him?** - Each of these is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]
    * **the one whom Yahweh and this people and all the men of Israel have chosen** - This is [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] for Absalom whom Hushai is addressing and to whom Hushai sincerely but wrongfully believed was chosen by Yahweh.
