===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ziba** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Mephibosheth** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:04:04|4:4]].
  * **two hundred loaves ... one hundred clusters ... one hundred bunches** - "200 loaves ... 100 clusters ... 100 bunches" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **loaves of bread** - "cakes of bread"
  * **clusters of raisins ... bunches of figs** - These phrases refer to raisins or figs pressed together.
  * **raisins** - "dried grapes" 
  * **a skin of wine** - "a wineskin full of wine"
  * **faint** - "very, very tired"
