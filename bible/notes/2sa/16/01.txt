===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ziba**  - a man's name (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Mephibosheth**  - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:04:04|4:4]].
  * **saddled donkeys** - "donkeys wearing small seats for a rider"
  * **two hundred...one hundred** - "200...100" (See: [[en/ta/vol2/translate/translate_numbers]])
  * **loaves of bread** - "cakes of bread"
  * **clusters of raisins...bunches of figs** - a cluster or a bunch refers to cakes of raisins or figs pressed together into larger blocks
  * **raisins**  - AT: "dried grapes" 
  * **faint** - "very, very tired"
