===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bahurim** - Translate the name of this city the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:03:14|3:16]].
  * **Shimei ... Gera** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in spite of** - "even though there was"
  * **bodyguards** - These are men who protect an important person.
