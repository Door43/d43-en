===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now do it** - "So now make David your king"
  * **By the hand of my servant David** - Here "hand" refers to David's power to defeat the Philistines. AT: "I will empower my servant David and" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the hand of the Philistines** - Here "hand" refers to power over Israel. AT: "the power of the Philistines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the hand of all their enemies** - Here "hand" refers to power over Israel. AT: "the power of all of their enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
