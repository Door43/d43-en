===== translationNotes: =====

  * **What have you done?** - Joab asks this question to rebuke David for letting Abner go in peace. "You should not have done this!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Why have you sent him away, and he is gone?** - Joab asks this question to rebuke David for letting Abner escape. This can be translated as a statement. AT: "Abner was here and you let him leave!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Do you not know ... everything you are doing?** - Joab asks this question to convince David that Abner is plotting against David. AT: "Surely you know ... everything you are doing." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **well of Sirah** - "Sarah" is the name of a place where the well was located. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
