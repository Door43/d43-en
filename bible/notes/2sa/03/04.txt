===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fourth son… fifth son… the sixth** - numerical order of David's sons (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **Shephatiah... Ithream**  - These are the male names of David's sons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Haggith... Abital.., Eglah** - These are female names of David's wives.
