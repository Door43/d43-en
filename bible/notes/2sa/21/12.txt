===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jabesh Gilead** - Jabesh is a town in the region of Gilead. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:02:04|2:4]].
  * **the public square** - This is an area near the city gate where people did various kinds of business.
  * **Beth Shan** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gilboa** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:01:06|1:6]].
  * **who had been hanged** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "whom the Gibeonites executed by hanging" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
