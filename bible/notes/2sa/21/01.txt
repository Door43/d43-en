===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **three years** - See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]]
    * **sought the face of Yahweh** - This means David prayed to Yahweh for an answer about the famine. "Face" represents the presence of the other. (See:  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **because of Saul and his murderous family** -  Saul had killed many Gibeonites. Saul's descendants are guilty because of this sin.
