===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who schemed against us** - "who made plans against us"
  * **let seven men from his descendants be handed over to us** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "allow your men to give seven of his descendants to us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **we will hang them** - "we will execute them by hanging"
  * **in Gibeah of Saul** - Saul was from the town of Gibeah.
  * **the one chosen by Yahweh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the one whom Yahweh chose" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
