===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Saul was leaning on his spear** - The phrase, "leaning on his spear," means Saul was attempting to kill himself with his own spear. The spear has been placed butt end against the ground and the head of the spear, placed  against the center of the chest, is driven through one's body with the weight of the body. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
