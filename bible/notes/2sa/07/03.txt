===== translationNotes: =====

  * **do what is in your heart** - Here "heart" means David's desire. AT: "do what you think you should" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **The word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Will you build me a house in which to live?** - Yahweh is almighty and does not need a house to live in, so he is scolding David here. This question can be translated as a statement. AT: "I do not need you to build me a house." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
