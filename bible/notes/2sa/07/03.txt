===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **do what is in your heart** - The word "heart" here refers to David's desire. AT: "do what you think you should." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **Will you build me a house in which to live?**  - Yahweh is almighty and obviously does not need a house to live in, he is scolding David here. AT: "I don't need your to build me a house." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
