===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh describes his promises to King David through the prophet, Nathan.

  * **my people Israel** - This refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and have cut off all your enemies**  - Here the phrase "cut off" means " have killed" AT: "I have killed your enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])
  * **like the name of the great ones who are on the earth**  - The phrase "great ones" means famous persons.
