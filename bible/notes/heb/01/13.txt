===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But to which of the angels has God said at any time...?** - AT: "God never said to any angel what he said to his son...." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
  * **stool**  - a place to rest feet while sitting
  * **those who are about to**  - those who will
  * **Are not all angels spirits** - AT: "All angels are spirits who are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
