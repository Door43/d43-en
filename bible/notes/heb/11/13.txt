===== Translation Notes: =====

The author of Hebrews has just written about God's promise to Abraham about his descendants.


    * **admitted**  - "acknowledged" or "accepted"
    * **strangers and aliens**  - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **aliens**  - "foreigners" or "outsiders"
    * **a fatherland**  - "a country"
