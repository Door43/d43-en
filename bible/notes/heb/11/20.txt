===== Translation Notes: =====

The author of Hebrews has just written about Abraham's test by God to sacrifice his son Isaac.

    * **Jacob worshiped** - "Jacob worshiped God"
    * **his end**  - "his death" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
