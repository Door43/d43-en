===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues to focus on the faith of past leaders in Israel.

  * **went out to** - "left his home to go to"
  * **He went out** - "He left his home"
  * **the land of promise** - "the land God had promised to him"
  * **fellow heirs** - "heirs together"
  * **architect** - a person who designs buildings
