===== Translation Notes: =====

The author is writing to Jewish believers who live in Rome.

  * **Let brotherly love continue**  - AT: "Consistently show your love for other believers as you would for a member of your family"
  * **Do not forget to welcome strangers**  - AT: "Remember to welcome strangers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **welcome strangers** - AT: "treat people you do not know as friends"
  * **by doing this**  - The word "this" refers to welcoming strangers.
