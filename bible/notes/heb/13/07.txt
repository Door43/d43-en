===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the result of their conduct** - "the outcome of the way they behave"
  * **Imitate their faith** - Here the trust in God and the way of life led by these leaders are spoken of as "their faith." AT: "Trust and obey God in the same way they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **is the same yesterday, today, and forever** - Here "yesterday" means all times in the past. AT: "is the same in the past, the present, and in the future forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
