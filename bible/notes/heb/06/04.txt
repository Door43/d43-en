===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who tasted the heavenly gift **- This refers to believers whom God has saved. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **who tasted God's good word**  - This refers to believers who have experienced God's Word for themselves. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **they have crucified for themselves the very Son of God again** - When people turn away from God, it is like they are crucifying Jesus again. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **fell away**  - AT: "turned away from God"
