===== translationNotes: =====

  * **those who were once enlightened** - Understanding is spoken of as if it were illumination. AT: "those who were once made to understand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **who tasted the heavenly gift** - Experiencing salvation is spoken of as if it were the tasting of food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **who tasted God's good word** - Learning God's Word is spoken of as if it were the tasting of food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **sharers in the Holy Spirit** - 
  * **the powers of the age to come** - 
  * **they have crucified for themselves the very Son of God again** - When people turn away from God, it is as though they crucify Jesus again, which is impossible. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **fell away** - "turned away from God"
