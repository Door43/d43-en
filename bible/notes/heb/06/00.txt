====== Hebrews 06 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The audience of this chapter is Christians. This will probably affect how you translate some of the difficult verses. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Abrahamic Covenant ==
God made a covenant with Abraham. In it, God promised to make his descendants into a great nation. He also promised to protect them and to give them land of their own. This covenant is promised based on the character of God.
