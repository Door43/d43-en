===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues the comparison between the first and second covenant. 

    * **first covenant** - Translate this as you did in [[:en/bible/notes/heb/08/06|8:7]].
    * **regulations** - "detailed instructions" or "rules" or "directions"
    * **For** - The author is continuing the discussion from [[:en:bible:notes:heb:08:06|8:7]].
    * **there was a room prepared** - "the Israelites prepared a room"
    * **bread of the presence** - "bread on display before God" (UDB) or "bread the priests presented to God"
