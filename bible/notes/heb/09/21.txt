===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **sprinkled** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:heb:09:18|9:19]]
  * **container** - "object that can hold something" or "vessel"
  * **priestly service** - "work of the priests"
  * **everything is cleansed with blood** - "the priests use blood to cleanse everything"
  * **Without the shedding of blood there is no forgiveness** - "God forgives people's sins only if a priest kills a sacrifice and offers it for them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
