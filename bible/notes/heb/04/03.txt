===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the first quotation is from a Psalm, the second one is from Moses' writings, and the third is again from the same Psalm.

  * **the ones who have believed** - "the ones who believe"
  * **his created works had been finished from the beginning of the world** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he finished his created works from the beginning of the world." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **They will not enter my rest** - "They will not rest with me where I am"
