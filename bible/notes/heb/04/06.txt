===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here we find out that this quotation from the Psalms was written by David. (See: Hebrews [[:en:bible:notes:heb:03:07|3:7-8]])

  * **God's rest is still reserved for some to enter** - The peace and security provided by God are spoken of as if they were rest that he can give, and as if they were a place to which people could go. This thought can be stated in active form. AT: "God still reserves rest his rest for some to enter"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]], [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his voice** - "God" or "what God is saying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **do not harden your hearts** - See how you translated these words in [[:en:bible:notes:heb:03:07|3:8]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
