===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who has passed through the heavens** - "who has entered where God is"
  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **let us firmly hold to our beliefs** - Belief and trust are spoken of as if they were objects that a person could grasp firmly. AT: "let us continue to believe confidently in him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **we do not have a high priest who cannot feel sympathy ... Instead, we have** - This double negative means that, in fact, Jesus does feel sympathy with people. AT: "we have a high priest who can feel sympathy ... Indeed, we have" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **who has in all ways been tempted as we are** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "who has endured temptation in every way that we have" or "whom the devil has tempted in every way that he tempts us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he is without sin** - "he did not sin"
  * **to the throne of grace** - "to God's throne, where there is grace." Here "throne" refers to God ruling as king. AT: "to where our gracious God is sitting on his throne" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **we may receive mercy and find grace to help in time of need** - Here "mercy" and "grace" are spoken of as if they were objects that can be given or can be found. AT: "God may be merciful and gracious and help us in time of need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
