===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **How then can we escape if we neglect so great a salvation?** - The writer uses this question to emphasize that God will certainly judge those who ignore his message. AT: "Since this is true, we will certainly not escape God; he will certainly judge us if we ignore the good news about how he saves us." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **neglect** - "do not care for" or "do not accept"
  * **signs, wonders** - The words "signs" and "wonders" have similar meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
