===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues the  quotation from the same Psalm that was also quoted in [[:en:bible:notes:heb:03:07|3:7]].

  * **For we have become** - Here "we" refers to both the writer and the readers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **if we firmly hold to our confidence in him** - "if we continue to confidently trust in him"
  * **from the beginning** - "from when we first begin to believe in him"
  * **to the end** - This is a polite way of referring to when a person dies. AT: "until we die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **it has been said** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the Psalmist wrote" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Today, if you hear ... as in the rebellion** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:heb:03:07|3:7-8]].
