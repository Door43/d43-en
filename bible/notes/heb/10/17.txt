===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then** - after what was said in [[:en:bible:notes:heb:10:15|10:16]]
  * **No more will I remember** - "I will no longer remember"
  * **sins and acts of lawlessness** - The words "sins" and "acts of lawlessness" mean basically the same thing. Together they emphasize had bad the sin is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **where there is forgiveness for these things, there** - AT: "when God has forgiven these things, offerings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **there offerings are no longer made for sin** - AT: "there people no longer make offerings for sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
