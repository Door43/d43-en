===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **dash you to pieces** - This phrase compares the destruction of Nineveh to breaking a pot into many pieces. Alternate translation: "destroy you" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
    * **remembers his officers** - Possible meaning: 1) "thinks of his officers" or 2) "considers his officers"
    * **eager march** - Possible meaning: "excited march" or "excited walk"
    * **they**  - This refers to the army that will attack Nineveh.
