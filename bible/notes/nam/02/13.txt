===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **See** - "Understand this" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
    * **the sword will devour your young lions.**  - "your soldiers will die violent deaths" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
    * **the sword** - "soldiers with swords" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
    * **devour** - "eat all of"
    * **your young lions** - "your best young men"
    * **I will cut off your prey from your land**  - The word "prey" refers to the nations Nineveh steals resources and treasure from.  This means that Yahweh will destroy Nineveh's ability to steal from other nations. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
