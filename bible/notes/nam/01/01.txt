===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Nahum describes the destruction of Nineveh in poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **The declaration about Nineveh. The book of the vision of Nahum, the Elkoshite** - These words are an introduction to the entire book. This can be stated as a complete sentence. AT: "This is the book of the vision of Nahum, the Elkoshite, which gives a declaration about Nineveh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Elkoshite** - A person from the village of Elkosh (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
