===== Translation Notes: =====

Nahum continues describing the destruction of Nineveh in poetry that began in [[:en:bible:notes:nam:01:01|1:1]]. (See: UDB and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **the city full of blood** - Nineveh's soldiers killed many people. Alternate translation: "the city responsible for many people's deaths" (See:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
