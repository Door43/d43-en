===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **are you better than Thebes**  - Alternate translation: "you are not better than Thebes" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Thebes**  - This refers to Thebes, Egypt, the once capital of Egypt; an important city which was invaded by the Assyrians in 664 B.C.
  * **whose defense was the ocean, whose wall was the sea itself?**  - This is saying the same thing twice in order to make the point clear. Alternate translation: "whose defense was water" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:parallelism|Parallelism]])
