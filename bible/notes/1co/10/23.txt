===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Everything is lawful," but not everything is beneficial**  - AM: "Even if a thing isn't sinful, doing that thing might weaken your faith."
  * **"Everything is lawful," but not everything builds people up**  - AM: "Even if something isn't a sin, people see you do it, and it could weaken their faith or confuse them."
  * **No one should seek his own good. Instead each one should seek the good of his neighbor**
 - AM: "Do what is good for others first, then do what is good for you."
