===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **give no offense to**  - That means they should not offend people for no good reason.
    * **not seeking my own benefit**  - That means he did not focus on doing things that he felt like doing.
    * **but that of the many**  - That means that Paul wants to help the greatest possible number of people to come to Christ or to grow in their faith.
