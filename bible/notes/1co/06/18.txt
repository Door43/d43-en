===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Run away from** - "Immediately remove yourself from" or "Get away from" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **commits** - AT: "does" or "performs"
  * **"Every other sin that a person commits is outside the body"—but the sexually immoral person sins against his own body** - "Every other sin" uses "sin" as a specific event (a noun), whereas "sins against" uses "sin" as an action (a verb).
