===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who prophesies is greater** - Paul is emphasizing that the gift of prophecy is greater than the gift of speaking in tongues. AT: "The one who prophesies has a greater gift" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **interprets** - See how "interpret" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:1co:02:12|2:13]].
  * **how will I benefit you** - This can be a statement. AT: "I will not benefit you" or "I will not have done anything that helps you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
