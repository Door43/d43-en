===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Stephanas, Fortunatus, and Achaicus** - These men were either some of the first Corinthian believers or church elders who have close ties with Paul.
  * **Fortunatus...Achaicus** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **For they refreshed my spirit** - Paul rejoiced and was glad for their visit.
