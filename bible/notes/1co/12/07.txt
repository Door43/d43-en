===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to each one is given** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God gives to each one" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to one is given by the Spirit the word** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "by means of the Spirit God gives to one person the word" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **by the Spirit** - God gives the gifts through the work of the Spirit.
  * **wisdom ... knowledge** - The difference between these two words is not as important here as the fact that God gives them both by the same Spirit.
  * **the word of wisdom** - Paul is communicating one idea through two words. AT: "wise words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
  * **the word of knowledge** - Paul is communicating one idea through two words. AT: "words that show knowldge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
