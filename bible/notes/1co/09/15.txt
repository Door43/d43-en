===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **claimed** -  AT: "demanded" or "asked strongly for"
  * **rights**  - AT: "benefits" or "things that are deserved"
  * **I do not write this so something might be done for me. **- AT: "I am not writing to get something from you." or "I am not writing so you will send money to me."
  * **deprive** - AT: "take away" or "withhold"
  * **I would rather die than anyone deprive me of this boast** - AT: I am proud that I haven't taken money from you." or "I would rather die than lose my pride about this."
  * **I have no reason for boasting** - AT: "I don't have a reason to brag." or "I don't have a reason for boasting."
  * **I must do this** - "this" refers to "preach the gospel"
  * **woe be to me** - AT: "it would be bad for me"
