===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But God will give it body as he chooses, and to each seed its own body - ** AT: "God will decide how the body will change." or "God chooses the changes he will make for each person."
  * **there is one flesh of human beings, and another flesh for animals, and another flesh for birds, and another for fish**  - AT: "the flesh of human beings is different from the flesh of animals." or "human flesh is not like the flesh of animals or bird or fish."
