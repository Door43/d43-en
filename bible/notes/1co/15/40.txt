===== translationNotes: =====

  * **heavenly bodies** - Possible meanings are 1) the sun, moon, stars, and other visible lights in the sky or 2) heavenly beings, such as angels and other supernatural beings.
  * **earthly bodies** - This refers to humans.
  * **the glory of the heavenly body is one kind and the glory of the earthly is another** - "the glory that heavenly bodies have is different from the glory of human bodies"
  * **glory** - Here "glory" refers to the relative brightness to the human eye of objects in the sky.
