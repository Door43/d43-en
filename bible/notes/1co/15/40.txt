===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **heavenly bodies**  - This refers to the sun, moon, stars, and other visible lights in the sky.
  * **earthly bodies**  - This refers to humans.
  * **glory**  - This refers to the relative brightness to the human eye of objects in the sky.
  * **the glory of the heavenly body is one kind and the glory of the earthly is another **- AT: "the glory that heavenly bodies have is different than the glory that humans have." or "the glory that stars have is not the same as the glory that humans have."
  * **There is one glory of the sun, and another glory of the moon, and another glory of the stars, for one star differs from another star in glory.**  - AT: "The stars and the moon differ from each other, and they have different kinds of glory. Humans also differ from each other and have different kinds of glory."
