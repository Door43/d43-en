===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But someone will say, "How are the dead raised? And with what kind of body will they come?" **  - AT: "But some will say that they cannot imagine how God will raise the dead, and what kind of body God would give them in the resurrection." (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **you are so ignorant**  - AM: "you don't know about this" or "you are not informed".
  * **What you sow will not start to grow unless it dies. **- AT: "The information won't change things until you accept it."
