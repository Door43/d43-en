===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **morals**  - rules of right conduct; immoral - violating rules of right conduct; not ethical
  * **"Bad company corrupts good morals."**  - AM: "Spending time with bad friends will make you want to sin." or "Don't spend time with sinful people, or you will start being sinful."
  * **Sober up!**  - AM - "Get serious!", "Think about this!", or "Overcome this problem!"
  * **I say this to your shame - **  AM: "You should be embarrassed by this." or "You should be ashamed that you do not know God."
