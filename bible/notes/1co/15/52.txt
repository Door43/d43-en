===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We will be changed** - "God will change us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **in the twinkling of an eye** - It will happen as fast as it takes for a person to blink his or her eye.
  * **at the last trumpetâ€¦the trumpet will sound** - The blowing of a trumpet often precedes major events. In this case, Paul is referring to the final major event in earth's history.
  * **this mortal must put on immortality** - God will change our mortal bodies to immortal bodies.
