===== Translation Notes: =====

  * The names of "Haggai" and "Zerubbabel" - See (How to Translate Names)
  * **on the twenty-fourth day of the month **- This is based on the Hebrew calendar.
  * **'I will shake the heavens and the earth.**  - Yahweh will cause disruption but doesn't say how.
  * **for I will overthrow the throne of kingdoms **- Govenments will fall apart in the chaos.
  * **The soldiers will kill each other with their own swords.**  - Soldiers will panic.
