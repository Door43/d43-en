===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Haggai ... Zerubbabel** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **on the twenty-fourth day of the month** - AT: "on the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **I will shake the heavens and the earth** - Yahweh will cause disruption in their world.
  * **the heavens and the earth** - AT: "the whole universe" 
  * **for I will overthrow the throne of kingdoms** - Governments will fall apart in the chaos.
  * **the throne of kingdoms** - AT: "government ruled by kings"
  * **brother's sword** - AT: "the sword on a fellow soldier"
