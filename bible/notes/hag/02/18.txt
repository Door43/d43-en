===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month**  - (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]]).
  * **Is there still seed in the storehouse?** - The expected answer is 'no'. This question is used to highlight what the readers already knew. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **the fig tree, the pomegranate, and the olive tree**  - These are fruits grown in the land.
