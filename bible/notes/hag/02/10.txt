===== translationNotes: =====

  * **On the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month** - This is the ninth month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-fourth day is near the middle of December on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the second year of Darius** - "in the second year of the reign of Darius" or "when Darius had been king for more than one year" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Darius ... Haggai** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. AT: "Yahweh gave a message" or "Yawheh spoke this message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **meat that is set apart to Yahweh ... holy** - The words "that is set apart to Yahweh" and the word "holy" translate the same Hebrew word. "holy meat ... holy"
