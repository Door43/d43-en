===== translationNotes: =====

  * **second year of Darius the king** - "second year of the reign of Darius the king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Darius ... Haggai ... Zerubbabel ... Shealtiel ... Jehozadak** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the sixth month, on the first day of the month** - "on the first day of the sixth month." This is the sixth month of the Hebrew calendar. The first day is near the middle of August on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **came by the hand of Haggai** - Haggai was the messenger. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
