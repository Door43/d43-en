===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the second year of Darius the king** - "In the second year of the reign of Darius the king" or "After Darius had been king for more than a year" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Darius ... Haggai ... Zerubbabel ... Shealtiel ... Joshua ... Jehozadak** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the sixth month, on the first day of the month** - "on the first day of the sixth month." This is the sixth month of the Hebrew calendar. The first day is near the middle of August on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. AT: "Yahweh gave a message" or "Yawheh spoke this message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the translationWord page about Yahweh concerning how to translate this.
  * **by the hand of Haggai** - Here the word "hand" refers to Haggai himself. Yahweh used Haggai as the agent to deliver his command. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. AT: "through Haggai" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Yahweh's house** - the temple
