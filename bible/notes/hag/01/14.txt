===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stirred up the spirit** - means to motivate or influence. See: ([[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]). 
  * **remnant**  - The word "remnant" refers to the people that were still alive after they arrived from their captivity in Babylon and started to rebuild Jerusalem.
  * **in the twenty-fourth day of the sixth month, in the second year of Darius the king.** - This is just 23 days after he received the vision. See: ([[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]]).
  * **second year of Darius the king,** - "second year of the reign of Darius the king," or UDB.
