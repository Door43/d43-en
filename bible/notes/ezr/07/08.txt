===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the fifth month** - This is the fifth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of July and the first part of August on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the first day of the first month** - This is near the middle of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the first day of the fifth month** - This is near the middle of July on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Ezra had established his heart to study** - "Ezra committed his life to study"
  * **the good hand of God** - "God" or "God Himself". (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the statutes and decrees of the law of Yahweh** - These were the laws that God passed down to Israel from Moses.
