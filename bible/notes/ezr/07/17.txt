===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This continues the decree that King Artaxerxes gave to Ezra.

  * **So buy in full the oxen, rams and lambs** - The phrase "in full" here represents diligently or faithfully. They were to use the gold and silver to buy what they needed in order to worship God in the the temple. AT: "Faithfully buy the oxen, rams and lambs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to you and your brothers** - The phrase "your brothers" refers to the people who were doing this work with Ezra. AT: "your co-workers" or "your companions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
