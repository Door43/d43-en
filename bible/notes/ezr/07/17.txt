===== Translation Notes: =====

The king desires to please God with the money they bring back.

  * **So buy in full...** - The king authorizes the purchase of animals and food for sacrifices to God.
  * **...please your God with the rest...** - The King grants Ezra latitude use the money as he sees fit to please God.
