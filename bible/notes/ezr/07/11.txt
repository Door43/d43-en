===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This was the decree** - The text following this statement was the decree given by King Artaxerxes.
  * **I am ordering that anyone ... who desires to go to Jerusalem** - In those days people needed permission from the king to resettle and rebuild in an area previously destroyed by the conquering nation.
