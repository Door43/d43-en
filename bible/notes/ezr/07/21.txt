===== translationNotes: =====

  * **anything that Ezra asks from you** - Ezra had the full support of the king in rebuilding the temple.
  * **one hundred** - 100 (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **talent** - A talent is 34 kilograms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **cors** - A cor or kor is 220 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **baths** - A bath is 22 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
