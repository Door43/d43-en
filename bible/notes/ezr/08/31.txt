===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ahava Canal** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:15|8:15]].
  * **twelfth day of the first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. The twelfth day is near the end of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **The hand of our God** - This refers to God providing protection to Ezra's group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he protected us from the hand of the enemy** - "hand" refers to the strength of the person. God kept their enemies from attacking them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the ones who wished to ambush us** - God protected them against thieves and robbers wanting attack them for their treasures. 
  * **three days** - 3 days (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
