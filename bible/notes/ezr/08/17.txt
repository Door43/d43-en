===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Iddo** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Next I sent them to Iddo** - The word "them" refers to the nine leaders and two teachers written about in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:15|8:16]]. AT: "Next I sent those men to Iddo" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Kasiphia** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **I told them what to say to Iddo ... that is, to send to us servants for the house of God** - The words "that is" introduces what he told them to say. AT: "I told them to tell Iddo ... to send us servants for the house of God"
