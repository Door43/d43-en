===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **from the captivity, the people of exile** - These are the Jewish people who were taken captive and resettled in Babylon and choose to come back to Judea and Jerusalem. 
  * **twelve...ninety-six...eventy-seven**  - 12, 96 and 77. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * ** governors beyond the River** - These were the Babylonian officials managing the people east of the Euphrates River which included those residing in Judea.
  * **God's house**  - The temple.
