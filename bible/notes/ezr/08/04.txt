===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The list of men's names continues. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **Of the descendants of Pahath Moab, Eliehoenai son of Zerahiah** - This is the next item in the list. It can be written with the verb "was." AT: "The leader of the descendants of Pahath Moab was Eliehoenai son of Zerahiah" or "Eliehoenai son of Zerahiah was the leader of the descendants of Pahath Moab"
  * **and with him were two hundred males** - "and with Eliehoenai were two hundred males"
  * **Zerahiah** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:07:01|7:4]].
  * **Adin** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:15|2:15]]. 
  * **two hundred ... three hundred ... fifty ... seventy** - "200 ... 300 ... 50 ... 70" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
