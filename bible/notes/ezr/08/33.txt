===== translationNotes: =====

  * **objects were weighed out** - This was a means of accountability for transporting all the temple money and temple objects.
  * **Meremoth ... Jozabad ... Noadiah ...Binnui** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Eleazar** - Translate the same way you did Eleazar in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:07:01|7:5]].
