===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The list of men's names continues. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **Of the descendants of Shephatiah, Zebadiah son of Michael** - This is the next item in the list. It can be written with the verb "was." AT: "The leader of the descendants of Shephatiah was Zebadiah son of Michael" or "Zebadiah son of Michael was the leader of the descendants of Shephatiah" 
  * **Shephatiah** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:03|2:2]].
  * **Michael** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **and with him were listed eighty males** - "and with Zebadiah were listed eighty males"
  * **Bebai** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:11|2:11]]. 
  * **eighty ... twenty-eight** - "80 ... 28" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
