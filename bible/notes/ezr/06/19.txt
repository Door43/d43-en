===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fourteenth day of the first month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. The fourteenth day is near the beginning of April on western calendars. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **purified themselves** - "purified themselves according to the law of Moses" Before priest can make sacrifices for others, they first must make sure they are pure before God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
