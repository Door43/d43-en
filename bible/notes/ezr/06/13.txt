===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Tattenai, Shethar Bozenai** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:05:03|5:03]].
  * **The house** - "God's house" or "The temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the third day of the month of Adar** - "Adar" is the name of the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the cold season. The third day is near the middle of February on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
