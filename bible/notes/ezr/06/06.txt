===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues the record of King Cyrus's command that the Jews rebuild God's temple in Jerusalem, which began in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:06:03|6:3]].

  * **Tattenai ... Shethar Bozenai** - Darius writes directly to these men. Translate their names as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:05:03|5:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the Province Beyond the River** - This is the name of the province that was west of the Euphrates River. It was across the river from the city of Susa. See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:04:09|4:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
