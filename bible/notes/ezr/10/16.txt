===== translationNotes: =====

  * **did this** - The people investigated who married non-Jewish wives.
  * **the first day of the tenth month** - This is the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. The first day is near the middle of December on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **first day of the first month** - This is near the middle of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
