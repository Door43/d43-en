===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ninth month and twentieth day**  - measurement of time  
  * **in the square**  - A large open courtyard in front of the temple.
  * **committed treason**  - The crime of betraying one's country; not being loyal. Ezra equated marrying foreign women as an act of treason.
