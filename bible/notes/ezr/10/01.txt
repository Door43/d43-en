===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Shekaniah** - Translate the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:04|8:7]].
  * **Jehiel** - Translate the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:08|8:9]].
  * **We have been unfaithful to our God** - This was made as a public confession, not as a boastful claim.
  * **But in spite of this, there is still hope for Israel** - Shekaniah is making a statement of faith that God is and will be merciful to them.
