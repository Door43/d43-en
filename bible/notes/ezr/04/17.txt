===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the Province Beyond the River** - This is the name of the province that was west of the Euphrates River. It was across the river from the city of Susa. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:04:09|4:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **The letter that you sent me has been translated and read** - This can be translated in active form, in which case you will need to make explicit who translated and read the letter to the king. AT: "I have had my servants translate and read the letter that you sent to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Rehum** - man's name. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:01|2:2]].
  * **Shimshai** - man's name. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:04:07|4:8]].
  * **the River** - the Euphrates River
