===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For this we also pray**  - "We also pray"
  * **we**  - the pronoun "we" refers to Paul, Silas and Timothy. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **we also pray continually for you**  - "frequently". (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **you **  - All second person plural pronouns “you” refer to the believers in the Thessalonian church. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
  * **fulfill every desire of goodness **  - "make you able to do good in every way that you desire". (UDB)
  * **that the name of our Lord Jesus may be glorified by you**  - "that you may glorify the name of our Lord Jesus" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **and you by him**  - "and Jesus may glorify you" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis|Ellipsis]]) (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **because of the grace of our God**  - "because of our God's grace"
