===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - As Paul continues, he talks about God being just.

  * **It is righteous for God** - "God is right" or "God is just"
  * **relief to you** - You can make it explicit that God is the one who returns relief. AT: "for God to return relief to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the angels of his power** - "God's powerful angels"
  * **In flaming fire he will take vengeance on those who do not know God and on those who** - "He will punish with blazing fire those who do not know God and those who" or "Then with blazing fire he will punish those who do not know God and those who"
