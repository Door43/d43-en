====== 2 Thessalonians 03 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Idleness and Laziness ==
In Thessalonica, there apparently was a problem with people in their church who were able to work but refused to do so. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])

== What should you do if "your brother sins"? ==

In this chapter, Paul teaches that it is important for Christians to live in a way that honors God. Believers should also encourage one another and hold each other accountable for their actions. The church is also responsible for encouraging believers to [[:en:obe:kt:repent|repent]] if they sin.
