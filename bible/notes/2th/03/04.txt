===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the Lord** - "joined to the Lord" (UDB)
  * **we **- “we” refers to Paul, Silas and Timothy but not their audience. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **concerning **- "about"
  * **you**  - “you” and "your" refer to the Thessalonian believers. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you|Forms of 'You']])
  * **direct your hearts**  - This is an expression that refers to the believers not just a body part. "guide your hearts" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
