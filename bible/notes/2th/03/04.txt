===== translationNotes: =====

  * **We have confidence** - "We have faith" or "We trust"
  * **direct your hearts** - Paul describes the Lord motivating Christians to love God as if the Lord were guiding them down a path. AT: "help you know" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **hearts** - This is a metaphor for the emotions or will, which control love and faithfulness. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **to the love of God and to the endurance of Christ** - Paul speaks of God's love and Christ's endurance as if they were destinations on a path. AT: "how much God loves you and how much Christ has endured for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
