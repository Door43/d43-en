===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But** - Paul uses this word here to contrast the lazy believers with the hard-working believers.
  * **you, brothers** - The word "you" refers to the Thessalonian believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **do not lose heart in** - To "lose heart" is an idiom that means to get tired, weary or discouraged. AT: "do not be discouraged in" or "do not stop" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **take note of him** - "publicly identify that person" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **so that he may be ashamed** - Paul views shaming a believer who is in sin as proper disciplinary action.
