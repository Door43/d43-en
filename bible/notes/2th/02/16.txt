===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul ends with a blessing from God.

  * **Now** - Paul uses this word here to mark a change in topic.
  * **may our Lord ... who loved us and gave us** - The words "our" and "us" refer to all believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **Lord Jesus Christ himself** - Here "himself" gives additional emphasis to the phrase "Lord Jesus Christ." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **comfort and establish your hearts in** - "hearts" refers to the seat of emotions. AT: "comfort you and strengthen you for" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
