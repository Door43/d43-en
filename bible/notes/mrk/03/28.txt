===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Truly I say to you** - This indicates that the statement that follows is especially true and important. 
  * **the sons of men** - "those who have been born of man." This expression is used to emphasize peoples' humanity. AT: "people" 
  * **utter** - "speak"
  * **they were saying** - "the people were saying"
  * **has an unclean spirit** - This is an idiom that means to be possessed by an unclean spirit. AT: "is possessed by an unclean spirit" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
