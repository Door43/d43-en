===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to whom he gave** - The phrase "to whom" refers to both James son of Zebedee and his brother John.
  * **the name Boanerges, that is, sons of thunder** - Jesus called them this because they were like thunder. AT: "the name Boanerges, which means men who are like thunder" or "the name Boanerges, which means thunder men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Thaddaeus** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who would betray him** - "who would betray Jesus" The word "who" refers to Judas Iscariot.
