===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **General Information:** - Here the words "they" and "them" refer to the people who were under the control of the unclean spirits which were demons.

  * **You are the Son of God** - Jesus' power over unclean spirits is related to his title, "Son of God."
  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
