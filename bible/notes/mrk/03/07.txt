===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continued to heal people as great crowd of people followed him when he wanted to get away.

  * **the sea** - This refers to the Sea of Galilee.
  * **Idumea** - This is the region, previously known as Edom, which covered the southern half of the province of Judea. 
  * **a great crowd, when they heard everything he was doing, came to him** - This can be written as a separate sentence. "Sidon. When the great crowd heard everything he was doing, they came to him"
  * **heard everything he was doing** - Here "everything" refers to the miracles Jesus was performing. AT: "heard about the great miracles that Jesus was performing"
  * **came to him** - "came to where Jesus was"
