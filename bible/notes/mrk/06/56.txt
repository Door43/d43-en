===== translationNotes: =====

 
  * **Wherever he entered** - "Wherever Jesus entered"
  * **they would put** - Here "they" refers to the people. It does not refer to Jesus' disciples.
  * **the sick** - This phrase refers to people. AT: "the sick people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **They begged him** - Possible meanings are 1) "The sick begged him" or 2) "The people begged him."
  * **let them touch** - The word "them" refers to the sick.
  * **the edge of his garment** - "the hem of his robe" or "the edge of his clothes" 
  * **as many as** - "all those who"
