===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"When they had crossed over"**  - "When they had crossed over the Sea of Galilee" or "After they went further around the Sea of Galilee" (UDB)
  * **stretchers-**  A stretcher is a device used to carry a sick person or someone who is unable to walk.  They may "stretch out" on it and lie down while they are carried.
