===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they saw them leaving** - "the people saw Jesus and the apostles leaving"
  * **on foot** - They people are described as going on foot, by land, which is in contrast to how the disciples went by boat.
  * **he saw a great crowd** - "Jesus saw a great crowd"
  * **they were like sheep without a shepherd** - Jesus compares the people to sheep who are confused when they do not have their shepherd to lead them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
