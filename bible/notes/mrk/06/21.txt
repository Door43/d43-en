===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The author continues to give background information about Herod and the beheading of John the Baptist. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **he made a dinner for his officials ... of Galilee** - Here the word "he" refers to Herod and is a metonym for his servant whom he would have commanded to prepare a meal. AT: "he had a dinner made for his officials ... of Galilee" or "he invited his officials ... of Galilee to eat and celebrate with him"
  * **a dinner** - a formal meal or banquet
  * **Herodias herself** - The word "herself" is a reflexive pronoun used to emphasize that it was significant that it was Herodias' own daughter who danced at the dinner. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **came in** - "came into the room"
