===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **many were coming and going -**  Could be translated as "many people were seeking out the apostles."  There was always someone wanting to see them, night and day.
  * **they took no time apart for leisure - **  Could be translated as "the apostles were unable even to rest."  They had no schedule for rest, and could not even have a relaxed, private conversation.
