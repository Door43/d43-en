===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They went out** - The word "they" refers to the Twelve and does not include Jesus. Also, it may be helpful to state that they went out to various towns. AT: "They went out to various towns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **They cast out many demons** - It may be helpful to to state that they cast the demons out of people. AT: "They cast many demons out of people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
