===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These signs will go with those who believe**  - Jesus is saying that those who believe and proclaim his name will be able to act with his authority and they will have authority over physical and spiritual things in the same way that Jesus did.
  * **They will pick up serpents with their hands, and if they drink anything deadly it will not hurt them.- ** In the Old Testament people would be put on trial by picking up serpents or drinking poison. If the serpent stuck and the person died they would be assumed guilty. This statement says that one will be protected by the power of God, which is more powerful than a serpents bite or poison
