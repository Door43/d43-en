===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he was taken up into heaven and sat** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God took him up into heaven, and he sat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **at the right hand of God** - "where the one to whom God gives the most honor and power sits"
  * **confirmed the word** - This idiom means they proved that their message was true. AT: "showed that his message, which they were speaking, was true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
