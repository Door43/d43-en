===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus had been buried in the tomb that Joseph from Arimethea had provided three days earlier.

  * **bought aromatic spices, that they might come and anoint Jesus body for burial- ** Anointing a body for burial was customary at that time.
  * **aromatic** - "sweet smelling"
  * **first day of the week** - Sunday was the first day of the week.
