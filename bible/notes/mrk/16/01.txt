===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - On the first day of the week, women come early because they expect to use spices to anoint Jesus' body. They are surprised to see a young man who tells them Jesus is alive, but they are afraid and do not tell anyone.

  * **When the Sabbath day was over** - That is, when the sun had set on the seventh day and the first day of the week had begun.
  * **aromatic** - "sweet-smelling"
