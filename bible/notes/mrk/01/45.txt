===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But he went out** - The world "he" refers to the leper that Jesus had healed in the previous verse
  * **started to tell everyone and he spread the word so much** - This is a doublet stating the same idea in two ways for reinforcement.  (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **started to tell everyone** - This is a hyperbole meaning that he was telling everybody he was meeting. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
  * **Jesus was unable to go freely into town** - "The crowds prevented Jesus from moving freely in towns" 
  * **from everywhere ** - "from all over the region" (UDB)
