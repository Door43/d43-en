===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A leper came to him; he was begging him as he knelt down. He said to him** - "A leper came to Jesus; the leper was begging Jesus as the leper knelt down. The leper said to Jesus"
  * **If you are willing** - "If you desire to make me clean"
  * **you can make me clean** - "you can heal me" People with leprosy were considered unclean and were cast out from society, but when the leprosy was gone, the man could return to society. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I am willing** - "I desire to make you clean"
