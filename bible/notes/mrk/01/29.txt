===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - After healing the demon-possessed man, Jesus healed Simon's mother-in-law as well as many other people.

  * **Now Simon's mother-in-law was lying sick with a fever** - The word "Now" introduces Simon's mother-in-law to the story and gives background information about her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **the fever left her** - It can be shown clearly who healed her. AT: "Jesus healed her of the fever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **she started serving them** - It is implied that food was served. AT: "she provided them with food and drinks" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
