===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - After Jesus' baptism, he is in the wilderness for 40 days and then goes to Galilee to teach and call his disciples.

  * **compelled him to go out** - "drove Jesus out forcefully"
  * **He was in the wilderness** - "He stayed in the wilderness"
  * **forty days** - "40 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **He was with** - "He was among"
