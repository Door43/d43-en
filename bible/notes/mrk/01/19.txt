===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **mending the nets** - "fixing the nets"
  * **They and them** - The pronoun "they" and "them" refer to James and John.
  * **They left their father ** - This means they left their father in the boat as well as the life they had with him to follow Jesus.
  * **hired servants** - These are people that were making their money working for the family.
