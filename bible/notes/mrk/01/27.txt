===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they asked each other, "What is this? A new teaching with authority?** - The peoples response can be written with statements instead of rhetorical questions. AT: "This is amazing! Not only does he teach in a new and authoritative way, but he also commands the evil spirits and they obey him!" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **He commands** - "Jesus commands"
  * **He commands even the unclean spirits** - Alternate translation: "He is capable of commanding the unclean spirits"
  * **Immediately the news** - "In a short time the news" (See: [[en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
