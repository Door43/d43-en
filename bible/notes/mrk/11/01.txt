===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they**  - This pronoun refers to Jesus and his disciples.
  * **Bethphage**  - This is the name of a village near Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **you**  - This pronoun refers to the disciples Jesus sent into the village to get a colt.
  * **If any one asks you**  - "If a person asks you"
