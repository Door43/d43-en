===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they were thinking within themselves** - "the scribes were thinking"
  * **"Why are you thinking this in your hearts?"** - Jesus is using a rhetorical question to scold the scribes who doubted his authority. Alternate translation: ""You scribes are questioning my authority."" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **What is easier to say to the paralyzed man,** - "Is it easier to say to the man who cannot walk".
