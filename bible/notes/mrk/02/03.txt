===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **four people were carrying him** - "four of them were carrying him." It is likely that there were more than four people within the group that brought the man to Jesus.
  * **were bringing a paralyzed man** - "were bringing a man who was unable to walk or use his arms"
  * **could not get near him** - "could not get close to where Jesus was"
