===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Seeing their faith** - "Knowing that the men had faith"
  * **the paralyzed man** - "the man who could not walk"
  * **Son, your sins are forgiven** - "Son" is used figuratively because the man believed. Alternate translation: "My child, your sins are forgiven".
  * **reasoned in their hearts** - "were thinking to themselves"
  * **How can this man speak this way?** - "This man should not speak this way." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **Who can forgive sins but God alone?** - "Only God can forgive sins." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
