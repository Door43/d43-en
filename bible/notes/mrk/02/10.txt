===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But in order that you may know** - "But so that you may know." The word "you" refers to the scribes and the crowd.
  * **that the Son of Man has authority** - Jesus refers to himself as the "Son of Man." AT: "that I am the Son of Man and I have authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **in front of everyone** - "while all the people there were watching"
