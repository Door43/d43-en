====== Mark 02 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== "Sinful people" ==
Mark refers to a group of people as "[[:en:obe:kt:sin|sinful]]." The Jewish leaders thought these people were sinful, but in reality it was the leaders were truly being sinful. This can be taken as [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]].

== Fasting and Feasting ==
Fasting was often done during a time of repentance. It was not done during joyous times. This is used in contrast to weddings, which were a time of great joy and had a large feast. Having Jesus on the Earth was a cause of celebration for mankind.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Rhetorical Questions ==
[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical questions]] are used several times in this chapter. The Jewish leaders used them to express disbelief and anger. Jesus used them to show the Jewish leaders their ignorance.
