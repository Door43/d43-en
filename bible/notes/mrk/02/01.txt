===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - After preaching and healing people throughout Galilee, Jesus returns to Capernaum where he heals and forgives the sin of a paralyzed man.

  * **it was heard that he was at home** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the people there heard that he was staying at his home" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so many were gathered there** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "so many people gathered" or "so many people came to the house"
  * **there was no more space** - This refers to there being no space inside the house. AT: "there was no more room for them inside" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
