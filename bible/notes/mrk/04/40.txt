===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus and his disciples were crossing the lake when a violent storm started.

  * **Why are you afraid?** - "I am disappointed that you are so afraid." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Who then is this** - "We need to think carefully about who this man really is!"  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
