===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **some seeds fell upon the wayside **- Could be translated "some seeds fell on the pathway."
  * **fell on the wayside, fell on the rocky soil **- a farmers of this time sowed seed before plowing. The wayside or pathway would not be plowed and until plowing was done, rocky ground would not be visible. Therefore the seed would be wasted.
