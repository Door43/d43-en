===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus explains the parable to his disciples.

  * **Do you not understand this parable? How then will you understand the rest of the parables?** - "If you are unable to understand this parable, you will not be able to understand the rest of the parables."  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
