===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He entered into a house and he did not want anyone to know he was there.**  - Jesus did not want anyone to know that he was in the house because he wanted to rest.
  * **Now the woman was a Greek, a Syrophoenician by race"**  - This woman was a gentile, not a Jew, and to this point in time, Jesus' ministry was primarily for a Jewish audience.
