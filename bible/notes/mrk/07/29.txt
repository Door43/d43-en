===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you are free to go** - "you may go now" or "go home" 
  * **The demon has gone out of your daughter** - Jesus has caused the unclean spirit to leave the woman's daughter. This can be expressed clearly. AT: "I have caused the evil spirit to leave your daughter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
