===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came through** - "traveled through"
  * **Decapolis** - “the Ten Towns” (see UDB), a region to the southeast of the Sea of Galilee. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who was deaf** - "who was not able to hear"
  * **had a speech impediment** - "could not speak clearly"
