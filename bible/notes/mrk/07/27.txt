===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He said to her** - Jesus speaks the woman with a metaphor, comparing the Gentiles to dogs and the Jews to children. AT: "So, suggesting that he should help the Jews first and not the Gentiles whom some Jews called dogs, he said to her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Let the children first be fed** - Here Jesus compares the Jews here to children. This may be stated in active form.  AT: "I must feed the children first" or "I must serve the Jews first" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **bread** - This refers to food in general. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **even the dogs under the table eat the children's crumbs** - The Gentiles are here compared to dogs. AT: "you can serve me, a Gentile, in this small way." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **crumbs** - very small pieces of bread
