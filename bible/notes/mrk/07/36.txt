===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the more he ordered them** - The refers to him ordering them not to tell anyone about what he had done. AT: "the more he ordered them not to tell anyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the more abundantly** - "the more widely" or "the more"
  * **extremely** - "utterly" or "exceedingly" or "beyond all measure"
  * **the deaf ... the mute** - These refer to people. AT: "deaf people ... mute people" or "people who cannot hear ... people who cannot speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
