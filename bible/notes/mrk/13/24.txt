===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the sun will be darkened** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the sun will become dark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the moon will not give its light** - Here the moon is spoken of as if it were alive and able to give something to someone else. AT: "the moon will not glow" or "the moon will be dark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the stars will fall from the sky** - This does not mean that they will fall to earth but that they will fall from where the are now. AT: "the stars will fall from their places in the sky" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the powers that are in the heavens will be shaken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will shake the powers that are in the heavens" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the powers** - This is a descriptive name for the planets and may include the stars. AT: "the planets" or "the heavenly bodies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **in the heavens** - "in the sky"
  * **Then they will see** - "Then people will see"
  * **with great power and glory** - "powerfully and gloriously"
  * **he will gather** - The word "he" refers to God and is a metonym for his angels, as they are the ones who will gather the elect. AT: "they will gather" or "his angels will gather" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the four winds** - The whole earth is spoken of as "the four winds," which refer to the four directions: north, south, east, and west. AT: "the north, south, east, and west" or "all parts of the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **from the ends of the earth to the ends of the sky** - These two extremes are given to emphasize that the elect will be gathered from the entire earth. AT: "from every place on earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
