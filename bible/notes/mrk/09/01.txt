===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus has just been talking to the people and his disciples about following him. Six days later, Jesus goes with three of his disciples into a mountain where he looks as he will in the kingdom of God. 

  * **he was transfigured** - "he changed into another form" or "he appeared very different" (UDB)
  * **radiantly brilliant** - "exceedingly white"
  * **whiter than any bleacher on earth could bleach them** - Bleaching describes the process of making natural white wool even whiter by using chemicals like bleach or ammonia. AT: "whiter than any person on earth could whiten them"
