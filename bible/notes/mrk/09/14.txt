===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - When Peter, James, John, and Jesus came down from the mountain, they found the scribes arguing with the other disciples. 

  * **they came back to the disciples** - Jesus, Peter, James, and John returned to the other disciples who had not gone with them up the mountain.
  * **scribes were arguing with them** - The scribes were arguing with the disciples who had not gone with Jesus.
  * **was amazed** - It may be helpful to state why they were amazed. AT: "was amazed to see Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
