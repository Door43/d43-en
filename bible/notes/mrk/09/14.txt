===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - When Peter, James, John, and Jesus came down from the mountain, they found the scribes arguing with the other disciples. 

  * **scribes were arguing with them** - The scribes were arguing with the disciples who remained behind.
  * **the whole crowd was amazed** - There were many more people present than just the scribes and Jesus' disciples.
