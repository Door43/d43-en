===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus had taken Peter, James, and John up a high mountain where Jesus appeared in bright white clothes along with Moses and Elijah.

  * **So they kept the matter between themselves** - "So they did not talk about these things with anybody who did not also see them happen". 
  * **"to rise from the dead"** - "to come alive after dying"
