===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus had just told the demon to come out of the boy.

  * **It cried out and convulsed the boy** - "The demon cried out and caused the boy to have a convulsion"
  * **The boy looked like one who was dead** - "The boy appeared dead" or "The boy seemed dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
