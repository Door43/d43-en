  * **"for he was teaching" **- Jesus wanted time to teach without the distraction from crowds
  * **"after three days, he will rise again" **- the disciples had no teaching or background before this to understand Jesus' crucifixion, burial and resurrection. But Jesus was preparing them for it anyway, before these things happened.
