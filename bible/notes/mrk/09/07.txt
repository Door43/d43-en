===== translationNotes: =====

  * **came and overshadowed** - "appeared and covered"
  * **Then a voice came out of the cloud** - The "voice" is a metonym for God. Also, the "voice" is described as "coming out of the cloud," meaning that they heard God speaking from the cloud. AT: "Then God spoke from the cloud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **This is my beloved Son. Listen to him** - God the Father expresses his love for his "beloved Son," the Son of God.
  * **beloved Son** - This is an important title for Jesus, the Son of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **when they looked** - Here "they" refers to Peter, James, and John.
