===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They also had** - Here the word "they" is used to refer to Jesus and his disciples.
  * **he gave thanks for them** - "Jesus gave thanks for the fish"
  * **They ate** - "The people ate"
  * **they picked up** - "the disciples picked up"
  * **the remaining broken pieces, seven large baskets** - It may be helpful to add the missing information. AT: "the remaining broken pieces of bread and fish, which filled seven large baskets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **And he sent them away** - It may be helpful to clarify when he sent they away. AT: "And after they ate, Jesus sent them away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they went into the region of Dalmanutha** - It may be helpful to clarify how they got to Dalmanutha. AT: "they sailed around the Sea of Galilee to the region of Dalmanutha" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Dalmanutha** - This is the name of a place on the northwestern shore of the Sea of Galilee. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
