===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have eyes, do you not see? You have ears, do you not hear?** - Jesus is expressing his frustration with their lack of understanding thru the use of these rhetorical questions.  AT: "You have eyes, but you do not understand what you see!  You have ears, but you do not understand what you hear."  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]}
  * **Do you not remember? When** - Jesus is reminding them of the miracle they had seen.  AT: "Remember when" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **five loaves among the five thousand** - "5 loaves among the 5000" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Twelve** - "12"  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
