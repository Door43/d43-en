===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He looked up** - "The man looked up" 
  * **I see men who look like walking trees** - The man sees men walking around, yet they are not clear to him, so he compares them to trees. AT: "Yes, I see people! They are walking around, but I cannot see them clearly. They look like trees." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Then he again** - "Then Jesus again"
  * **and the man opened his eyes, his sight was restored** - The phrase "his sight was restored" can be written in active form. AT: "restoring the man's sight, and then the man opened his eyes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
