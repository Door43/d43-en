===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **What does it profit a person to gain the whole world… **- First part of a rhetorical question that could be stated "Even if a person gets everything they want in the world it will not benefit him…
  * **…and then forfeit his life? **- Second part of a rhetorical question that could be stated "…if he loses his life anyway."
  * **What can a person give in exchange for his life? **- Could be translated as "What will buy or ransom a person's life from God?"
