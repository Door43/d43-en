===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He asked them** - "Jesus asked his disciples"
  * **He commanded the crowd to sit down on the ground** - This can be written as a direct quote. "Jesus commanded the crowd, 'Sit down on the ground'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **sit down** - Use your language's word for how people customarily eat when there is no table, whether sitting or lying down.
