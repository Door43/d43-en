===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he sent to them another servant** - "the owner of the vineyard sent to the grape farmers another servant"
  * **they wounded him in the head** - the grape farmers hit and injured the servant in the head"
  * **and treated him shamefully** - "and showed no respect to the servant" or "and insulted the servant" (UDB)
