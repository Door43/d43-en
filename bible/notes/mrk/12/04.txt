===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he sent to them** - "the owner of the vineyard sent to the vine growers"
  * **they wounded him in the head** - This can be written more clearly. AT: "they beat that one on the head, and they hurt him terribly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **yet another ... many others** - These phrases refer to other servants. AT: "yet another servant ... many other servants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **They treated many others in the same way** - This refers to servants that the owner sent. The phrase "in the same way" refers to them being mistreated. This can be written clearly. AT: "They also mistreated many other servants whom he sent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
