===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **them** - These are Jewish religious leaders.
  * **A man planted a vineyard** - "A man planted grape plants"
  * **dug a pit for a wine-press** - This means that he made a hole in the ground for a container to hold the grapes so that people may walk on the grapes to crush them and have grape juice.
  * **leased the vineyard** - The owner made people pay to use his land to grow grapes.
  * **vine dressers** - "the people who grow the grapes" or "grape farmers"
  * **went away on a journey** - "traveled" or "left on a trip"
  * **to receive from them some of the fruit of the vineyard** - "to get his share of the grapes"
  * **they took him** - "the vine dressers grabbed the servant"
