===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He asked him, "What is your name?"- **  Jesus asked the man "What is your name?"
  * **The man replied "My name is Army, for we are many."- **  The spirits inside of the man say that there is not just one evil spirit in this man, but many.
  * **He implored him over and over not to send them out of the region.-** The unclean spirit implored Jesus many times not to send them out of the region.
