===== translationNotes: =====


  * **He had been bound many times** - This can be written in active form. AT: "People had bound him many times" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **his shackles were shattered** - This can be written in active form. AT: "shattered his shackles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **shackles** - "bonds on his feet" or "metal bindings attached to his ankles to confine him"
  * **chains** - "handcuffs" or "chains attached to his wrists to confine him"
  * **No one had the strength to subdue him** - The man was so strong that no one could subdue him. AT: "He was so strong that no one was strong enough to subdue him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **subdue him** - "control him"
