===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The UDB uses a verse bridge to combine these two verses in order to write the events in the order that the occurred. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_versebridge]])

  * **He cried out** - "The unclean spirit cried out"
  * **What do I have to do with you, Jesus, Son of the Most High God?** - The unclean spirit asks this question out of fear. This can be written as a statement. AT: "Leave me alone, Jesus, Son of the Most High God! There is no reason for you to interfere with me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Jesus...do not torment me** - Jesus has the power to torment unclean spirits. 
  * **Son of the Most High God** - This is an important title for Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **I beg you by God himself** - Here the unclean spirit is swearing by God as he makes a request of Jesus. Consider how this type of request is made in your language. AT: "I beg you before God" or "I swear by God himself and beg you"
