===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He cried out** - "The unclean spirit cried out"
  * **What do I have to do with you** - AT: "I have nothing to do with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **do not torment me** - "do not torture me" (UDB)
  * **Jesus, Son of the Most High God? I beg you by God himself, do not torment me.** - Jesus, the "Son of the Most High God," has the power to torment unclean spirits. Translate this description of Jesus so that the reader clearly understands that the name Jesus, the Son of God, is the one with power over evil spirits.
