===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **What do I have to do with you, Jesus, Son of the Most High God? I beg you by God himself, do not torment me.- **  This is the unclean spirit that is living within the man who is calling out to Jesus and begging for mercy.
  * **For Jesus was saying to him, "Come out of the man, you unclean spirit"- **  Jesus does not torment the unclean spirit, but frees the man by sending the unclean spirit away from him.
