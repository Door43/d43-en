===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who touched my clothes?-**  Even though Jesus was surround by a group of people Jesus knew that someone intentionally touched his clothes for the purpose of being healed.
  * **You see this crowd pressed around you, and you say, "Who touched me?"- ** The disciples ask this rhetorical question because they do not understand how Jesus could distinguish between someone running into him and someone intentionally touching him.
