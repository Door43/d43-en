===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"The woman knew what had happened to her"**  - "The woman knew that she had been healed" or "The woman knew what she had done"
  * **she came in fear and trembling- **  This is an expression that means she was very afraid and may have even been trembling
  * **"Daughter, your faith has made you well."- **"Daughter, your act of faith has allowed you to be healed" or "Daughter, because you have believed that I could heal you, I have now healed you" (UDB)
