===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Pilate again asked him** - "Pilate asked Jesus again"
  * **Do you give no answer** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "Do you have an answer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **See how many charges they are bringing against you?** - This can be written as a statement. AT: "They are bringing many charges against you!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **See** - "Listen." This is a word used to direct Jesus' attention to the charges brought against him. 
  * **that amazed him** - It surprised Pilate that Jesus did not reply and defend himself.
