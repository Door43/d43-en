===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For he knew that it was because of envy ... Jesus over to him** - This is background information about why Jesus was handed over to Pilate. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **it was because of envy that the chief priests** - They envied Jesus, probably because so many people were following him and becoming his disciples. AT: "the chief priests were envious of Jesus. This is why they" or "the chief priests for envious of Jesus' popularity among the people. This is why they" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **stirred up the crowd** - The author speaks of the chief priest rousing or urging the crowd as if the crowd were a bowl of something that they were stirring. AT: "roused the crowd" or "urged the crowd" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **released instead** - The missing information may be added. AT: "released instead of Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
