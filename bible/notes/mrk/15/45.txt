===== Translation Notes: =====
Joseph of Arimathea, a respected member of the Jewish Council, had asked Pilate to give him the body of Jesus.

    * **centurion** - a Roman soldier in charge of 100 men. See how you translated this in 15:39.
    * **a tomb that previously had been dug** - "a tomb that someone had previously dug" (See: Active/Passive)
    * **linen** - a cloth made from a flax plant
    * **where Jesus' body was placed** - "where Joseph and the others placed Jesus' body" (See: Passive/Active)
