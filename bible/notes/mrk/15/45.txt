===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When he learned** - "When Pilate learned"
  * **he granted his body to Joseph** - "he permitted Joseph to take Jesus' body"
  * **linen** - Linen is cloth made from the fibers of a flax plant. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mrk:14:51|14:51]].
  * **He took him down ... Then he rolled a stone** - Joseph had help from other people to take Jesus down from the cross, preparing it for the tomb, and closing the tomb. AT: "He and others took him down ... Then they rolled a stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a tomb that had been cut out of a rock** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "a tomb that someone had previously cut out of solid rock" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a stone against** - "a huge flat stone in front of" 
  * **Joses** - This Joses was not that same person as the younger brother of Jesus. See how you translated the same name in [[:en:bible:notes:mrk:06:01|6:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the place where Jesus was buried** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the place where Joseph and the others buried Jesus' body" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
