===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The soldiers brought Jesus to Golgotha where they crucified him with two others. Many people mocked him. 

  * **Place of a Skull** - "Skull Place" or "Place of the Skull." This the name of a place. It does not mean that there are lots of skulls there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Skull** - the head bone
  * **wine mixed with myrrh** - It may be helpful to explain that myrrh is a pain-relieving medicine. AT: "wine mixed with a medicine called myrrh" or "wine mixed with a pain-relieving medicine called myrrh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
