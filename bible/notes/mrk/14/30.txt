===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **before the rooster crows twice**  - A rooster is a male domesticated bird; they crow, or holler loudly, at the breaking of dawn or some other obvious or unseen stimulating event.
  * **you will deny me** - Jesus is saying Peter will say he does not know or follow Jesus.
