===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus continues with his disciples, except for Judas, in the Garden of Gethsemane.

  * **and found them sleeping** - "and found Peter, James, and John sleeping" 
  * **Simon, are you asleep** - "Simon, you are asleep when I told you to stay awake" (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Could you not watch** - "You could not even stay awake" (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **The spirit indeed is willing, but the flesh is weak** - "Your body cannot do want your mind wants you to do." 
  * **flesh** - "body" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
