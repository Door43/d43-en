===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Judas betrays Jesus with a kiss, and the disciples all flee.
  * **General Information:** - Verse 44 gives background information about how Judas had arranged with the Jewish leaders to betray Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Now his betrayer** - This refers to Judas.
  * **he is the one** - Here "the one" refers to the man that Judas was going to identify. AT: "he is the one you want" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he kissed him** - "Judas kissed him"
  * **laid hands on him and seized him** - These two phrases have the same meaning to emphasize that they seized Jesus. AT: "grabbed Jesus and seized him" or "seized him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
