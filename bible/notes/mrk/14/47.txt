===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who stood by** - "who was standing nearby"
  * **Jesus said to them** - "Jesus said to the crowd"
  * **Do you come out, as against a robber, with swords and clubs to capture me?** - Jesus is rebuking the crowd. This can be written as a statement. AT: "It is ridiculous that you come here to seize me with swords and clubs, as if I were a robber!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **But this was done that** - "But this has happened so that"
  * **All those with Jesus** - This refers to the disciples.
