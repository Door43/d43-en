===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus had just been seized in the garden by those sent by chief priests, scribes, and elders.

  * **Do you come out, as against a robber, with swords and clubs to capture me?** - "You come out, as against a robber, with swords and clubs to capture me." (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
