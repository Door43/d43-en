===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Connecting Statement:** - As Jesus and his disciples continue walking toward Jerusalem, Jesus heals blind Bartimaeus, who then walks with them.

  * **the son of Timaeus, Bartimaeus, a blind beggar** - "a blind beggar named Bartimaeus, the son of Timaeus." Bartimaeus is the name of a man. Timaeus is his father's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **When he heard that it was Jesus** - Bartimaeus heard people saying that it was Jesus. AT: "When he heard people saying that it was Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Son of David** - Jesus is called the Son of David because he is a descendant of King David. AT: "You who are the Messiah descended from King David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Many** - "Many people"
  * **all the more** - "even more"
