===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why do you call me good? No one is good except God alone.- ** Jesus uses this rhetorical question, followed by an answer to the man's question to point to God's goodness and without expressly saying that he is connected to God he implies it by answering the question that was posed.
  * **"do not kill"**  - "do not take another's life" or "do not murder anyone" (UDB)
  * **"do not falsely testify"**  - "Do not lie about another person in court" or "do not give false testimony"
  * **"do not defraud"-**  "do not cheat anyone" or "do not take advantage of people"
