===== Translation Notes: =====

While Jesus and his disciples had been walking toward Jerusalem, the blind man outside of Jericho had been calling out to Jesus.

  * **commanded him to be called** - "commanded others to call him" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Be brave** - "Don't be afraid"
