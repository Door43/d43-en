===== translationNotes: =====

  * **whoever** - "if anyone"
  * **as a little child** - Jesus is comparing how people must receive the kingdom of God to how little children would receive it. AT: "in the same manner as children would" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **will not receive the kingdom of God** - "will not accept God as their king"
  * **definitely not enter it** - The word "it" refers to the kingdom of God.
  * **he took the children into his arms** - "he hugged the children"
