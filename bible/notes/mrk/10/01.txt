===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that place**  - Capernaum
  * **the region of Judea**  - (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **the jordan river**  - (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **the crowds came to him again**  - People Jesus had already taught came to him again.
  * **to divorce his wife**  - "to legally separate from his wife" or "to send his wife away"
  * **certificate of divorce**  - The document, mentioned in Deuteronomy 24:1-4, that a husband writes his displeasing wife before sending her from his house.
