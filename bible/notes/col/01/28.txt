===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he**- The pronoun "he" refers to Christ.
  * **we**- The pronoun "we" refers to Paul and Timothy and probably all the apostles.
  * **him**- The pronoun "him" refers to Christ.
  * **complete**- This word means spiritually mature; one in whom the work of is finished.
  * **I **- The pronoun "I" refers to Paul.
  * **his **- The pronoun "his" refers to Christ.
  * **role **- The word refers to the working of Christ in Paul.
  * **me **- The pronoun "me" refers to Paul.
