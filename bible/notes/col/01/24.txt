===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I **- The pronoun "I" is referring to Paul in the first two verses.
  * **my **- This pronoun is also referring to Paul.
  * **you **- The pronoun "you" is referring to the believers in Colossae in the first two verses.
  * **afflictions **- The Greek word refers to both physical and emotional suffering and pain.
  * **his **- The pronoun refers to Christ.
  * **serve the church **- The Greek word could indicate minister to the church like a pastor or wait on the church like a waiter does at a restaurant.
  * **me**- This pronoun is referring to Paul.
  * **the secret truth **- This phrase could also be translated as mystery which is a sacred truth that can only be known by revelation of God, not by human wisdom.
  * **ages**- This word refers to all time from the beginning until the time in which the letter was being written.
  * **revealed**- This word means to show or make <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>something</font>    visible; to make it clear; revelation.
  * **mystery**- A sacred secret that is not known by human reason, but is only known by revelation of God.
  * **confidence**- This word means an absolutely sure hope.
