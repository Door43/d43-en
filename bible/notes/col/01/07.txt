===== translationNotes: =====

  * **our beloved ... our behalf ... to us** - The words "our" and "us" do not include the Colossians. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **gospel as you learned it from Epaphras, our beloved fellow servant, who** - "gospel. It is exactly what you learned from Epaphras, who is our beloved fellow servant and who" or "gospel. It is exactly what Epaphras, our beloved fellow servant, taught you. He"
  * **Epaphras, our beloved fellow servant, who is a faithful servant of Christ on our behalf** - Here "on our behalf" means that Epaphras was doing work for Christ that Paul himself would have done if he were not in prison.
  * **Epaphras** - the man who preached the gospel to the people in Colossae (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **your love in the Spirit** - Paul speaks of the Holy Spirit as if he were a place in which the believers were located. AT: "how the Holy Spirit has enabled you to love believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
