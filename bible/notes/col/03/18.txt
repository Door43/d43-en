===== Important Terms: =====


 

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wives submit to **  - “wives obey”.
  * **it is appropriate **  - “it is proper” or “it is right”.
  * **do not be bitter against **  - "do not be harsh with" or "do not be angry towards".
  * **for this is well-pleasing in the Lord.**  "for the lord is well-pleased with you when you obey your parents". (See: [[:en:ta:translate:ellipsis|Ellipsis]])
  * **provoke your children **  - "aggravate your children" or “stimulate your children to anger”
