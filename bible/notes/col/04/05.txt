===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Live in wisdom toward** - "Be wise toward"
  * **who are outsiders** - "who are not believers"
  * **use your time wisely** - The word "you" refers to the Colossian believers.
  * **use your time wisely** - "be wise about what you do"
  * **Let your words always be with grace. Season them with salt** - AT: "Let your conversation always be gracious and attractive." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **know how you should answer** - "you should know how to answer questions about Jesus Christ from anyone"
