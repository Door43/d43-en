===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Aristarchus ** - He was in prison with Paul in Ephesus when Paul wrote this letter to the Colossians.
  * **"If he comes** - "if Mark comes"
  * **Jesus who is called Justus ** - This is a man who also worked with Paul.
  * **These alone of the circumcision are my fellow workers for the kingdom of God ** - "These three men are the only Jewish believers working with me to proclaim God as king through Christ Jesus" (UDB)
  * **These alone of the circumcision** - "These men, Aristarchus, Mark, and Justus, alone of the circumcision"
