===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **him **- <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The pronoun "him" is referring to Christ Jesus in both verses.</font>
  * **Be firmly planted in himâ€‹ **- This is a metaphor showing the need to develop a faith that is strong, just like a plant needs deep roots to grow well. (See metaphor)
  * **be built upon him, be established in faith **- This is a doublet that is used to express how important it is to live your entire life based upon your faith in Jesus Christ. (See doublet)
  * **abound **- This word in the Greek means to have in excess or to overflow.
