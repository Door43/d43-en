===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **robbed of a prizeâ€‹ **- This is a metaphor showing the loss of a spiritual reward can result from being deceived by false teachers just like someone <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>on earth</font>    being disqualified from a contest because they did not follow the rules. (See metaphor)
  * **false humility **- This phrase indicates doing things that make you appear to be humble to others rather than true humility. The literal Greek translation is "voluntary humility" and it could be translated "pious self-denial".
  * **obsesses **- This word means thoughts that constantly control your mind or something you cannot stop thinking about.
  * **fleshly thinking **- Thinking as a natural person rather than as a spiritual person.
  * **hold on to **- Keeping the mind on Christ and his provision, just as a child holds on to their parent.
  * **Christ, the head, from whom the whole body is supplied and held together through joints and ligaments **- This is a metaphor to show that Christ provides for His church and holds it together just as the head of a physical body directs the functioning of the entire human body. (See metaphor)
