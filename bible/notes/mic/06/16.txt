===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **The regulations made by Omri have been kept** - "You have done what Omri commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Omri ... Ahab** - Both of these men were kings over the northern kingdom of Israel. God considered both of them to be very wicked. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **You walk by their advice** - "you have done the evil things that Omri and Ahab told the people to do
