===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Obadiah his message to Edom.
    * **men of your alliance** - "men of your partnership" or AT: "your partners"
  * **prevailed against you** - "have overcome you" or  "have conquered you"
  * **There is no understanding in him** - Here, "him" refers to those who humble Edom.
  * **understanding** - "reason" or "wisdom"
  * **and understanding out of the mountain of Esau? - **  Obadiah is not really expecting an answer here. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **mountain of Esau** - The "mountain of Esau" is another way to say the "land of Edom." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **your mighty men will be dismayed, Teman** - "Teman" was a descendant of Esau. This is just another way to refer to the people of Edom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **your mighty men will be dismayed** - AT: "these things will discourage your strong men" or "these things will terrify your strong men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
