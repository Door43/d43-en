===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David dies and Solomon takes his place as the new king of Israel.

  * **slept with his ancestors** - David dying is spoken of as if he had fallen asleep. AT: "died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **David ... and was buried** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "David ... and they buried him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The days that David reigned over Israel were** - "The time that David reigned over Israel was" or "David reigned over Israel for"
  * **sat on the throne of his father David** - The throne represents the authority of the king. AT: "became king, as his father David had been" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **his rule was firmly established** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Yahweh had firmly established Solomon's rule" or "no one else dared to try to become king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
