===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to put my name there forever** - Possible meanings are 1) "to dwell there forever" or 2) "to claim possession of it forever." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my eyes and my heart will be there** - Possible meanings are 1) "I will be there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) or 2) "I will protect and care for it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my eyes and my heart** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
