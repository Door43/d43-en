===== translationNotes: =====

  * **King Solomon built** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped Solomon do this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a fleet of ships** - "a large group of ships"
  * **420 talents of gold** - A talent is a unit of weight equal to about 33 kilograms. AT: "about 14,000 kilograms of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
