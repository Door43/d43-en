===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He walked in the ways of Asa, his father** - A person's behavior is spoken of as if that person walked along a path. AT: "He did the same things that Asa, his father, had done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh** - Here "eyes" represents seeing and Yahweh's opinion is spoken of as if he were able to see something. AT: "what Yahweh considered to be right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the high places were not taken away** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he did not take away the high places" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
