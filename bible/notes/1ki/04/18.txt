===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The list of Solomon's officials and the areas of which they were in charge continues.

  * **Shimei ... Ela ... Geber ... Uri ... ... Og** - men's names  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Benjamin** - tribal land named for a son of Israel  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gilead ... Bashan** - names of pieces of land  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the country of Sihon** - "the land that in earlier years belonged to Sihon"
  * **Amorites** - the name of a people group  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the land** - Here "the land" refers to the land of Judah; the previous officials were over different parts of Israel.
