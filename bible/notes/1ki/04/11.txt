===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The list of Solomon's officials continues, and the list of the areas of which they were in charge begins.

  * **Ben-Abinadab ... Ben-Geber ... Jair ... Manasseh ... Ahinadab ... Iddo** - men's names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in all Naphoth Dor ... in Taanach ... in Ramoth Gilead ... in Mahanaim** - "who was the officer in charge of all Naphoth Dor ... who was the officer in charge of Taanach ... who was the officer in charge of Ramoth Gilead ... who was the officer in charge of Mahanaim"
  * **Taphath** - woman's name (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **Dor ... Beth Shan ... Zarethan ... Jezreel ... Beth Shan to Abel Meholah ... Jokmeam ... Ramoth Gilead ... Argob ... Bashan ... Mahanaim** - place names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
