===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Continuation of Solomon's List of Officials:

  * **Ben Hur ... Ben Deker ... Ben Hesed** - These are the names of men. Note that "Ben" before a name means "son of" so "Ben Hur" means "the son of Hur." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the hill country ... in Makaz ... in Arubboth** - "who was the official in charge of the hill country ... who was the official in charge of Makaz ... who was the official in charge of Arubboth"
  * **Ephraim ... Makaz ... Shaalbim ... Beth Shemesh ... Elon Bethhanan ... Arubboth ... Socoh ... Hepher** - These are names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
