===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God gave Solomon great wisdom and understanding** - God enabling Solomon to be wise and to understand many things is spoken of as if God were giving Solomon physical objects. The abstract nouns "wisdom" and "understanding" can be translated as verbs. AT: "God made Solomon able to be wise and to understand many things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **wideness of understanding like the sand on the seashore** - Solomon was able to understand many things about many different subjects. The abstract noun "wideness" can be translated as an adjective. AT: "Solomon was able to understand many things about a wide range of subjects" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Solomon's wisdom exceeded the wisdom of all the people** - "Solomon was wiser than all the wise people"
  * **the people of the east** - This refers to people from countries east of Israel such as Arabia and Mesopotamia.
  * **Ethan ... Heman ... Kalkol ... Darda ... Mahol** - men's names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
