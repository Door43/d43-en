===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Solomon made ... he made ... he made ... He overlaid ... he spread** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped Solomon do these things. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **lintel** - beam across the top of a door frame
  * **indented sections** - toothlike notches on each of five sections
  * **overlaid** - "covered" (UDB)
