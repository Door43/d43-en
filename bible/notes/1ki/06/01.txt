===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The narrator lists the dimensions of the temple.

  * **Solomon began to build** - Solomon would have commanded his workers to do the building. AT: "Solomon commanded his workers to begin building" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **480th ... fourth** - These are the ordinal forms of 480 and 4. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the month of Ziv, which is the second month** - "Ziv" is the name of the second month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of April and the first part of May on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **sixty cubits long, twenty cubits wide, and thirty cubits high** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "27.6 meters long, 9.2 meters wide, and 13.8 meters high" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
