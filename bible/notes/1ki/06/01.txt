===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Scripture lists the dimensions of the temple.

  * **Solomon began to build** - Solomon did not do the building himself; his servants did the work. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **480th ... fourth** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the month of Ziv, which is the second month** - "Ziv" is the name of the second month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of April and the first part of May on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **cubits** - A cubit is 46 centimeters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
