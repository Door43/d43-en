===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Solomon prepared ... Solomon overlaid** - Solomon would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "They prepared ... They overlaid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **twenty cubits** - "9.2 meters"
  * **covered the altar with cedar wood** - This altar would be used for burning incense.
