===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Solomon made ... He carved ... he evenly overlaid** - Solomon would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "They made ... They carved ... they evenly overlaid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **In this way, Solomon also made for the temple entrance doorposts of olive wood having four indented sections and two doors of cypress wood** - "Solomon also made doorposts of olive wood for the temple entrance, with four indented sections and two doors of cypress wood, in the same way"
  * **indented sections** - tooth-like notches
  * **The two leaves of the one door** - This means each door had two sections connected by hinges so they could fold together.
