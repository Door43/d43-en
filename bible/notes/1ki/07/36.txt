===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Huram engraved ... He made** - Huram would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "they engraved ... He commanded them to make" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they were surrounded** - Here the word "they" refers to the cherubim, lions, and palm trees.
  * **they were surrounded by wreaths** - The word "wreaths" refers to spiral-shaped pieces of bronze. This can be stated in active form. AT: "there were wreaths all around them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **All of them were cast in the same molds** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Huram cast all of the stands in the same mold" or "They cast all of the stand in the same mold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they had one size, and the same shape** - "all of the stands were the same size and shape"
