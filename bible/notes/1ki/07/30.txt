===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues to describe the bronze stands that Huram made.

  * **every stand**  - This refers to each of the 10 stands that Huram made. The stands were movable and had wheels, so the stands may also be called carts. (See UDB)
  * **four bronze wheels and axles**  - "four bronze wheels and 2 axles" (UDB)
  * **axle**  - a shaft, pole or spindle that holds the wheels of a vehicle
  * **its four corners**  - "its" is a possessive pronoun. AT: "the four corners of each stand". (see [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns|personal pronouns)]]
  * **its four corners had supports beneath for the basin**  - Since the basin is on top of the stand, the four corners and supports mentioned are at the top of the stand. AT: "the top four corners of each stand had supports beneath to hold a basin"
  * **cast**  - see 7:23
  * **round like a pedestal**  - the opening is compared to the top of a pedestal that is round (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]]
  * **a crown that rose up**  -this was a frame to hold the basin that resembled a crown or circular collar
  * **cubit**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **Upon the opening were engravings**  - AT: "There were engravings around the opening"
  * **their panels**  - their is a possessive pronoun. AT: "the panels of the stands"
