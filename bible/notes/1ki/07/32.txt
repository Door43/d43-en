===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues to describe the bronze stands that Huram made.

  * **axle**  - see 7:30
  * **their housings**  - "their" is a possessive pronoun that refers to housings of the axles.
  * **housings**  - coverings that protect the axles and moving parts
  * **stand**  - see 7:27
  * **cubit**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **the wheels were forged**  - Huram (and his helpers) made the wheels (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **forged**  - metal is heated and then hammered into the shape wanted
  * **The wheels were forged like chariot wheels.**  - The wheels of the stand resembled the wheels of a chariot. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **their housings, rims, spokes, and hubs**  - Here "their" is a possessive pronoun that refers to the housings, rims, spokes and hubs of the wheels.
  * **cast**  - see 7:23
