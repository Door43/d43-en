===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues to write about bronze items that Huram made for the temple.

  * **pomegranate**  - a fruit with a hard rind or skin and many juicy seeds inside.
  * **capital**  - In this verse, capital refers to a decoration on top of the column or pillar.
  * **lily/lilies**  - a trumpet-shaped flower. In this verse lilies are bronze decorations that look like this flower.
  * **portico**  - see 6:3
