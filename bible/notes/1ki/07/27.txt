===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer describes the bronze carts that Huram made.

  * **stands**  - "wheeled tables or carts" (See UDB)
  * **cubit**  - (See:[[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **The work of the stands was like this**  - This means the author will describe the stands in the following or next verses.
  * **They had panels**  - Here "they" refers to the stands.
  * **They had panels that stood between frames**  - AT: "On the sides of the stands there were panels set in frames." (UDB)
  * **on the panels and on the frames were lions, oxen, and cherubim.**  - AT: The workers decorated the panels and frames with figures of lions, bulls and cherubim.
  * **Above and below the lions and oxen were wreaths of hammered work.**  - The workers hammered or beat metal into wreath designs and put the wreath decorations above and below the lions and oxen.
