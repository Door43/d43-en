===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The author continues to write about the palace area.

  * **Solomon's house in which he was to live, in another courtyard within the palace grounds, was similarly designed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Solomon had someone design the house in which he was to live, in another courtyard within the palace grounds, in the same way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **He also built** - Solomon would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "He also commanded them to build" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
