===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cast**  - Here cast means the method used to shape the metal. Melted metal is poured into a hollow container or clay mold having the shape wanted, and when the metal cools it will be the shape of the container.
  * **cubit**  - (See:[[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **brim**  - top edge of a vessel or container
  * **from brim to brim**  - the distance across the top of a container
  * **circumference**  - the distance around something
  * **encircling**  - all the way around
  * **gourds**  - see 6:18
