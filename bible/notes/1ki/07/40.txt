===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Huram made ... he finished** - Huram would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "Huram had them make ... they finished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the bowl-like capitals** - The capitals were shaped like bowls. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **decorative latticework** - "crossed metal strips woven together"
