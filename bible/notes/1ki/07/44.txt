===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He made ... Huram made** - Huram would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "They made ... They made" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all the other implements** - "all the other tools"
  * **polished bronze** - bronze that was polished so that it would reflect light
