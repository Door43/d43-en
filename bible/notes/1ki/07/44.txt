===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues to list items for the temple that Huram made of bronze.

    * **He** - He refers to Huram and his helpers. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **He had made** - This phrase can be left out, and then continue the list after the semicolon from the previous verse.
    * **the sea**  - a large water tank (See how you translated this in verse 23)
    * **under it** - Place a semicolon after this phrase to separate items in the list. AT: "under it;"
    * **also the pots,** - Leave out the word "also." AT: "the pots,"
    * **implements** - tools
    * **-Huram made them for King Solomon, for the temple of Yahweh, of burnished bronze.** AT: "All the things that Huram and his workers made for King Solomon for the temple were made of polished bronze."
