===== translationNotes: =====

  * **two hundred pomegranates** - "200 pomegranates." A pomegranate is a fruit with a hard, red rind and many juicy seeds inside. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:07:18|7:18]] (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **He raised up the pillars** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped Huram do this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The pillar on the right was named Jakin** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The name of the pillar on the right side was Jakin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the pillar on the left was named Boaz** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The name of the pillar on the left side was Boaz" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The fashioning of the pillars was done** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Huram fashioned the pillars" or "Huram's men fashioned the pillars" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
