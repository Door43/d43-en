===== Translation Notes: =====

The author writes about the Hall of the Throne, also called the hall of justice.

  * **hall of the throne where he was to judge**  and **the hall of justice**  - This where the king sat to make judgments. Both references mean the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **from floor to floor**  - Some ancient translations have "from the floor to the rafters (ceiling). (See UDB).
