===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh listened to the voice of Elijah**  - AT: "Yahweh answered Elijah's prayer".
  * **life came into him again and he revived**  - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **down the steps**  - It is implicit that Elijah's room was upstairs, perhaps on the roof, and not part of the main house. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **Now I know**  - This suggests she had doubts but was now convinced.
