===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the woman's son, the woman who owned the house** - "the son of the woman who owned the house"
  * **no more breath left in him** - This is a polite way of saying the boy died. AT: "he stopped breathing" or "he died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **man of God** - The phrase "man of God" is another title for a prophet.
  * **of my sin** - This means sins in general not a specific sin. AT: "of my sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
