===== translationNotes: =====

  * **walk in my ways to keep** - Living one's life is spoken of as walking on a path. AT: "live as I want you to live and obey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **lengthen your days** - Here "days" represent Solomon's life. AT: "make you live a long time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
