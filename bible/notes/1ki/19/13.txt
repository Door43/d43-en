===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he wrapped his face in his cloak** - It is implied that Elijah hid his face out of respect, shame, or fear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **What are you doing here, Elijah?** - Yahweh asks this question to remind Elijah of his duty and that he should not hide or be afraid. AT: "I did not tell you to come here" or "this is not a place for a prophet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) (See 19:9-10)
  * **forsaken**  - AT: "abandoned"
