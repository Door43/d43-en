===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all that Elijah had done and how he had killed all the prophets**  - These two phrases are similar. How he killed the prophets would be included in "all that Elijah had done." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **So may the gods do to me, and more also**  - This is an oath used as emphasis. AT: "The gods can kill me"
  * **if I do not make your life like the life of one of those dead prophets**  - This is a threat and means Jezebel planned to have someone kill Elijah. AT: "if I do not kill you like you killed those prophets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
