===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Tirzah** - This is the name of a city. See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:14:17|14:17]].
  * **Shemer** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **two talents of silver** - You may convert this to a modern measure AT: "68 kilograms of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **He built a city** - The word "He" refers to Omri. He commanded people to build the city. AT: "Omri had his people build a city" or "Omri commanded and his workers built a city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_smetonymy]])
  * **after the name of Shemer** - This is an idiom that means "to honor Shemer" or "so that people would remember Shemer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
