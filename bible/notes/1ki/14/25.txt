===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the fifth year of King Rehoboam** - This refers to the fifth year of Rehoboam's reign as king. AT: "in the fifth year that Rehoboam was king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the fifth year** - "in year 5" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Shishak king of Egypt came up against Jerusalem** - "Shishak king of Egypt" represents himself along with the Egyptian army. AT: "Shishak king of Egypt, and his army with him, came up against Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Shishak** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:11:40|11:40]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **came up against** - This is an idiom that means marched against or attacked. AT: "came to attack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **He took everything away** - This is a generalization that indicates every valuable thing that could be found was taken away. AT: "He took away many valuable things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **He took** - The word "He" represents Shishak and the soldiers who were with him. AT: "Shishak and his army took" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **that Solomon had made** - Here "Solomon" refers to the craftsmen who worked for Solomon to make the shields. AT: "that Solomon had his workers make" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
