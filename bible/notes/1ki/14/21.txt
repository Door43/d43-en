===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **forty-one years old...seventeen years** - AT: "41 years old...17 years" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **to put his name** - AT: "to live and claim possession of it." See how you translate this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:09:03|9:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **His mother's name** - Here the word "His" refers to Rehoboam.
