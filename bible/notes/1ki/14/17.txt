===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Tirzah** - This is the name of the city where king Jeroboam lived. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **All Israel buried him and mourned for him** - This is a generalization that means the people of Israel buried him and mourned for him. AT: "A great number of the people of Israel were present when people buried him, and the people of Israel mourned for him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **just as it was told to them by the word of Yahweh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "just as Yahweh told them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the word of Yahweh** - Here "word" represents a message. AT: "the message of Yahweh" or "Yahweh's message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
