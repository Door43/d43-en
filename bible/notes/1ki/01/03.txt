===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So they searched**  - "So the king's servants searched"
  * **within all the borders of Israel**  - "in all of Israel" or "throughout Israel"
  * **Abishag the Shunammite**  - "the young virgin from the city of Shunen who served King David"
  * **the king**  - "King David"
