===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Adonijah sacrificed** - AT: "One day Adonijah went to sacrifice"
    * **fattened calves** - "young cows specially prepared for sacrifice" 
    * **stone of Zoheleth** - This is a rocky area near Jerusalem; "stone of Zoheleth" means "Snake Rock." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]].)
    * **Enrogel** - This was the name of a spring where people got water. 
    * **all his brothers, the king's sons** - Both of these phrases refer to the same people, Adonijah's brothers.  AT: "most of his brothers" All of his brothers were invited except Solomon. 
    * **men of Judah, the king's servants** - Both of these phrases refer to the same people, officials who served the king.
