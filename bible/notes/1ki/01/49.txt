===== Translation Notes: =====

Adonijah becomes terrified of King Solomon.

  * **Adonijah ... took hold of the horns of the altar** - The "horns of the altar" symbolized the strength and protection of Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
