===== Translation Notes: =====

Adonijah's attempt to seize power over the kingdom continues.


  * **He conferred with**  - "Adonijah discussed his plans with"
  * ** Joab, Zeruiah, Abiathar, Zadok, Benaiah, Jehoiada, Nathan, Shimei, Rei** - See [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:other:names|How to Translate Names]].
  * **followed Adonijah and helped him**  - "supported and helped Adonijah" or AT: "promised to support and help Adonijah"
