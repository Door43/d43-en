===== Translation Notes: =====

King David continues to speak on behalf of Solomon as the one who will become king.

  * **So let it be!** - They agree and will do what King David said. (See: [[en:obe:kt:amen]])
  * **make his throne greater than the throne of my master King David** - "make his power greater than King David's power" or "make his kingdom greater than King David's kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]].)
