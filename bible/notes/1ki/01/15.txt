===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Abishag the Shunammite** - This is the young virgin who King David's servants brought to him to care for him.
  * **bowed and prostrated herself before the king**  - "bowed close to the ground in front of the king"
  * **what do you desire?**  - "what do you want?" or "what can I do for you?"
  * **you swore to your servant by Yahweh your God**  - "you promised me before Yahweh your God"
  * **saying, 'Surely Solomon your son shall reign after me, and he shall sit on my throne'** - Bathsheba is reminding King David of the words he spoke to her.
  * **shall**  - "will"
  * **he shall sit on my throne**  - "he will be the king"; This phrase describes a king or ruler of the people.
