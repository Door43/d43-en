===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Go and tell your master that Elijah is here** - See how you translated these words in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:18:09|18:11]].
  * **your master** - Here "master" refers to King Ahab.
  * **As Yahweh of hosts lives** - This is an oath to emphasize that what he will say is true.
  * **before whom I stand** - To "stand before" is an idiom for being in someone's presence and ready to serve him. AT: "whom I serve" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
