===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **It happened that in a little while** - AT: "In a little while this happened"
    * **rode and went**  - These two words share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. AT: "rode in his chariot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **the hand of Yahweh was on Elijah**  - Here "hand" represents guidance and strength. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **belt**  - a band of leather or material worn around a person's waist or chest
    * **ran before Ahab to the entrance of Jezreel**  - This was meant to be a sign of respect to Ahab. AT: "ran in front of Ahab's chariot all the way to the entrance of Jezreel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
