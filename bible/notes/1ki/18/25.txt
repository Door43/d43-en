===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **prepare it**  - "make it ready to be sacrificed"
  * **you are many people**  - Here the word "you" is plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you|Forms of You]])
  * **the bull that was given to them**  - AT: "the bull that someone gave them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **But there was no voice, nor anyone who answered**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. AT: "But Baal did not say or do anything" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
