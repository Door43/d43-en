===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Solomon talks with King Hiram about building the Temple.

  * **Hiram had always loved David** - "Hiram had always been a close friend of King David" (UDB)
  * **Yahweh was putting his enemies under the soles of his feet** - To put an enemy under someone's foot means to conquer them. AT: "Yahweh was helping David to defeat his enemies" or "David was busy since Yahweh was giving him victory over his enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for the name of Yahweh** - The word "name" is a metonym for the person, and "for the name" refers to worshiping the person.See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:03:01|3:2]]. AT: "in which people would worship Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **because of the wars that surrounded him** - Another possible meaning is "because of the wars with which his enemies surrounded him" or "because he was fighting enemies on all sides"
  * **Yahweh was putting his enemies under the soles of his feet** - This speaks of Yahweh giving David complete control over his enemies as putting them under his feet. AT: "Yahweh was enabling David to completely defeat his enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
