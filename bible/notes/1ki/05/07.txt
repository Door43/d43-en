===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - King Hiram answers Solomon.

  * **the words of Solomon** - "what Solomon said"
  * **May Yahweh be blessed today** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I praise Yahweh today" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cypress** - Cypress is another kind of valuable wood that would be used for building the temple.
