===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This section continues the background information that began in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:11:14|11:15]].

  * **They left Midian** - Here the word "They" refers to Hadad and the other Edomites mentioned in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:11:14|11:17]].
  * **Midian ... Paran ... Egypt** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Tahpenes** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
