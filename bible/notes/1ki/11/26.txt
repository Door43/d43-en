===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jeroboam, Nebat** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zeredah, Millo** - These are the names of locations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zeruah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **he lifted up his hand against the king** - "Hand" refers to authority, power and control. "Lifting up against" refers to opposing someone by using authority, power and control. This is a metonymy used as a common idiom. AT: "rebelled against the king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Solomon built up the place at Millo** - See how you translated "the Millo" in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:09:15|9:15]].
