===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ashteroth ... Milcom** - These are the names of false gods. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sidonians** - This is the name of a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - The phrase, "in the sight of" refers to someone's opinion. AT: "what Yahweh considered to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he followed Milcom** - "Milcom" is perhaps another name for the idol "Molech." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
