====== 1 Kings 08 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The ark placed in the new temple.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

When the temple building was finished, Solomon told all the people to come to Jerusalem. They took the ark out of the tent and brought it to the temple. Then Solomon prayed that God would hear and answer prayers made to him towards this temple. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==
Solomon prayed that God would respond to people's prayers using an [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]:  "that your eyes may be open to the request of your servant."
