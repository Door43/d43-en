    * **all Israel with him** - This may refer either 1) to the people whom Solomon called to Jerusalem and who are listed in [[en:bible:notes:1ki:08:01|8:1]], or 2) to those who traveled to Jerusalem for the feast, not necessarily to every person who lived in Israel.
