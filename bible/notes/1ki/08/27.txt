===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But will God actually live on the earth?** - AT: "But surely, it cannot be that God will actually live on the earth!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **this prayer of your servant and his request** - The words "prayer" and "request" mean basically the same thing and emphasize the earnestness of his prayer. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) 
  * **respect this prayer of your servant ... listen to the cry and prayer that your servant prays** - The first and last parts of this sentence say the same thing. Both parts intensify Solomon's request. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
