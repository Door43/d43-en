  * **mildew**  - a usually white substance that grows on the surface of things in wet, warm conditions
  * **locusts**  - a type of grasshopper that travels in very large groups and that can cause great destruction by eating crops
  * **knowing the plague in his own heart**  - AT: "knowing in their own heart the plague was caused by their sin". (see UDB)
