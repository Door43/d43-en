===== translationNotes: =====

  * **leave us or forsake us** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize Solomon's desire for Yahweh to be present with the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **incline our hearts to him** - Here their "hearts" refer to the people's desires and emotions. Desiring to please someone is spoken of as inclining the heart toward that person. AT: "make us want to please him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **live in all his ways** - Here "in his ways" is an idiom that refers to the way he wants people to live. AT: "live as he requires us to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
