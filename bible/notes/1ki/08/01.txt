===== translationNotes: =====

  * **assembled the elders of Israel** - "called together the leaders of Israel"
  * **All the men of Israel** - This may refer either 1) to the people whom Solomon called to Jerusalem and who are listed in 8:1 or 2) to those who traveled to Jerusalem for the feast, not necessarily to every male person who lived in Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **at the feast** - This is a reference to the Feast of Succoth, also know as the Feast of Tabernacles or Feast of Shelters (UDB).
  * **in the month of Ethanim, which is the seventh month** - "Ethanim" is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of September and the first part of October on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
