===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to sit on the throne of Israel** - The throne is a metonym for the activity of the one who sits on the throne. AT: "to rule over Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **walk before me ... have walked before me** - The way a person lives is spoken of as if that person were walking on a path.AT: "live as I want you to ... have lived as I want you to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
