===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look now**  - These words are used to draw attention to what they will say. AT: "Consider this" or "Think about this" or "Listen to us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **put sackcloth around our waists and ropes around our heads**  - This was a sign of surrender.
  * **Is he still alive?**  - AT: "I am glad he is alive" or "It is good that he is still alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **He is my brother**  - AT: "I will not harm him"
