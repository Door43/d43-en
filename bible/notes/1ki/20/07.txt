===== translationNotes: =====

  * **of the land** - "The land" represents all the people living there. AT: "of the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **take note and see** - "Take note" is an idiom to observe carefully. AT: "pay close attention to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I have not refused him** - This can be expressed positively. AT: "I have agreed to his demands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
