===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all their days** - This refers to the entire time they reigned as kings. AT: "the whole time that they reigned over Judah and Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **acted aggressively against Judah** - "attacked Judah"
  * **built up Ramah** - It is implied that the army of Baasha first captured Ramah. The full meaning of this statement can be made clear. AT: "captured and fortified Ramah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
