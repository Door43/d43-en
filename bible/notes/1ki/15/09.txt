===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the twentieth year of Jeroboam king of Israel** - This refers to the twentieth year of Jeroboam's reign. AT: "After Jeroboam had been the king of Israel for almost twenty years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **In the twentieth year** - "In year 20" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **forty-one years** - "41 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh** - The eyes here represent seeing, and seeing represents judgment. Yahweh saw and approved of Asa's actions. AT: "what Yahweh judges to be right" or "what Yahweh considers to be right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
