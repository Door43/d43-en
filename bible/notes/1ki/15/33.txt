===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - AT: "what Yahweh considered to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **walked in the way of Jeroboam** - AT: "lived in the way of Jeroboam" or "did the same things that Jeroboam had done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in his sin** - Here the word "his" refers to Baasha.
