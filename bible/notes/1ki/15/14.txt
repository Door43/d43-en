===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But the high places were not taken away** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "But Asa did not command the people to take away the high places" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Asa's heart was completely devoted** - The heart represents the person. AT: "Asa was completely devoted" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **all his days** - This refers to the whole period of time that Asa lived. AT: "the whole time that Asa lived" or "throughout Asa's whole life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
