===== translationNotes: =====

  * **120 talents of gold** - "one hundred twenty talents of gold." A talent is a unit of weight equal to about 33 kilograms. AT: "about 4,000 kilograms of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **No greater amount of spices ... was ever given to him again** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "No one ever again gave to King Solomon more spices than the queen of Sheba gave to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
