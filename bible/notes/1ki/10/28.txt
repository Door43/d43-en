===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that had been bought from Egypt** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that his merchants had bought from people in Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Kue** - This is the name of a region. Some think that Kue was the same as Cilicia, in Asia Minor. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Chariots were purchased** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "His merchants purchased chariots" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **six hundred shekels of silver ... 150 shekels** - A shekel is a unit of weight equal to about 11 grams. AT: "about 6.6 kilograms of silver ... about 1.7 kilograms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **six hundred shekels of silver** - "600 shekels of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Many of these were then sold** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "His merchants then sold many of these" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
