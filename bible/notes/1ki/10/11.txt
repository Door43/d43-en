===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The king made** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped Solomon do this. AT: "The king told his people to make" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **almug wood** - a type of wood, possibly one with a pleasant scent (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **or been seen again** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "nor has anyone ever seen such a great quantity again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to this day** - This means to the day that the author was writing this.
