===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Solomon's fame concerning the name of Yahweh** - Here Yahweh is represented by his "name." Possible meanings are 1) AT: "Solomon's fame, which glorified Yahweh" or 2) AT: "Solomon's fame, which Yahweh had given him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all that was in her heart** - This is a generalization. AT: "everything she wanted to know" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
