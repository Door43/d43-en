===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **thought in his heart** - Here "heart" represents the mind. This phrase also suggests that strong emotions were attached to his thoughts.
    * **the heart of these people** - Here "heart" refers to the loyalty or commitment of the people.
    * **these people will turn again to their master. They will...return to Rehoboam** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
