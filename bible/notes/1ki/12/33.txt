===== translationNotes: =====

  * **went up to the altar** - "Went up" is an idiom for going to a sacred place to worship since these were located on high locations. AT: "offered sacrifices on the altar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the month he had planned in his own mind** - "in the month that he had determined"
