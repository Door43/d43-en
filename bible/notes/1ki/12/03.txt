===== translationNotes: =====

  * **called him** - Here the word "him" refers to Jeroboam.
  * **made our yoke heavy** - A heavy yoke is a metaphor for very difficult labor and requirements. AT: "treated us cruelly" or "forced us to work very hard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
