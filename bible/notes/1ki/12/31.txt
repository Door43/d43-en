\v 32 He also established Adonijah and two priests, Elishama and Jehoram. ===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **made priests** - AT: "chose men to be priests" or "appointed men to be priests"
    * **all the people** - This refers to men in the general population of the northern tribes of Israel, but excluded men from the tribe of Levi.  
    * **went up to the altar** - AT: "went to the place where the altar was"
    * **He did so at Bethel** - AT: "He celebrated the feast at Bethel."
    * **sacrificing to the calves** - "He offered sacrifices to the calves", "He made sacrifices to the calves" or " He sacrificed to the calves"
