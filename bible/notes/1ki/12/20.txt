===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **when all Israel heard**  - AT: "when some of the people of Israel heard" or "when the leaders of the tribes of Israel heard" (See: Hyperbole)
    * **king over all Israel** - This refers to the northern kingdom of Israel, composed of 10 of the 12 tribes of Isreal. (See: Explicit - Implicit)
    * **only the tribe of Judah** - The tribe of Benjamin also followed the family of David, but is not listed here. (See: Explicit - Implicit)
