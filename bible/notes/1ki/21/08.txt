===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wrote letters in Ahab's name** - Possible meanings are 1) she wrote Ahab's name on the letters. AT: "wrote letters and signed them with Ahab's name" or 2) the word "name" is a metonym for authority. AT: "she wrote letters on behalf of Ahab" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the wealthy who sat with him** - The word "wealthy" refers to wealth people. AT: "the wealthy people who sat with Naboth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **seat Naboth above the people** - Having Naboth sit in a place of honor is spoken of as if it were having him sit above the rest of the people who were present. AT: "have Naboth sit in a place of honor among the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
