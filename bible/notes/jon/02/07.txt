===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a continuation of Jonah's prayer that started in [[:en:bible:notes:jon:02:01|2:2]].

  * **I called Yahweh to mind** - Since Jonah was praying to Yahweh, it might be more clear in some languages to say "I thought about you, Yahweh" or "Yahweh, I thought about you." 
  * **then my prayer came to you, to your holy temple** - Jonah speaks as if his prayers could travel to God and his temple. AT: "then you in your holy temple heard my prayer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Those who pay attention to useless gods** - "People who pay attention to useless gods"
  * **reject your faithfulness toward themselves** - "are rejecting you, who would be faithful to them"
