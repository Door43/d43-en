===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lucius, Jason, Sosipater...Tertius** -  These are men's names (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **Tertius, who write this epistle down** - Tertius is the man who wrote down what Paul spoke.
  * **greet you in the Lord** - AT: "greet you as a fellow believer in the Lord"
