===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Tryphaena...Tryphosa...Persis**  - These are the names of women. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
    * **Rufus...Asyncritus...Phlegon...Hermes...Patrobas...Hermas**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
    * **chosen in the Lord** - This can be translated with an active verb: "whom the Lord has chosen." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
    * **his mother and mine** - "his mother, whom I also think of as a mother"
