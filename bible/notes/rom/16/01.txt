===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I**  - The pronoun "I" in 16:1 refers to Paul.
  * **Phoebe**  - Phoebe was a woman in the church at Cenchrae. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **Cenchrea**  - This is a town near Corinth in Greece. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **in a manner worthy of the saints**  - "in a way worthy of the saints"
