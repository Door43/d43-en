===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I commend to you** - "I especially want to mention to you." Here the word "I" refers to Paul. The word "you" is plural and refers to the believers in Rome. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
  * **Phoebe**  - Phoebe was a woman in the church at Cenchrae. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **our sister** - "our sister in Christ." Here "our" refers to Paul and includes all believers. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **Cenchrea**  - This is a town near the city of Corinth in the country of Greece.
  * **receive her in the Lord** - "welcome her because we all belong to the Lord"
  * **in a manner worthy of the saints**  - "in the way that believers should welcome other believers"
  * **and that you stand by her** - This could be translated as a new sentence: "I also want you to help her."
  * **has become a helper of many, and of myself as well** - "has helped many people, and she has helped me as well."
