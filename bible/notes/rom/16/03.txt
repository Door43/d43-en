===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul is greeting many of the believers in Rome by name. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])

  * **Prisca and Aquila** - Prisca, also known as Priscilla, was the wife of Aquila.
  * **my fellow-workers in Christ Jesus** - AT: "who work with me to tell people about Christ Jesus"
  * **Greet the church that is in their house** - AT: "Greet the believers who meet in their house to worship"
  * **Epaenetus**  - This is the name of a man.
  * **first fruit of Asia** - This phrase means that Epaenetus was the first person in Asia to believe in Jesus.
