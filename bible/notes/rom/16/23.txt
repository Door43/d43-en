===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gaius ... Erastus ... Quartus** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the host** - This refers to Gaius, the person in whose house Paul and his fellow believers gathered for worship.
  * **the treasurer** - This is a person who takes care of the money for a group.
  * **May the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all** - Here the word "grace" refers to the Lord's blessings upon his people. AT: "May the Lord Jesus Christ continue to act kindly toward you all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
