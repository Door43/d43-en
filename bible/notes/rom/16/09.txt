===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Urbanus...Stachys...Apelles...Aristobulus...Herodian...Narcissus** - These are the names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
    * **the approved in Christ**  - The word "approved" refers to someone who has been tested and proved to be genuine. AT: "whom Christ has approved"
