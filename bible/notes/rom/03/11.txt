===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There is no one who understands** - There is no one who understands what is right. AT: "No one really understands what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **There is no one who seeks after God** - Here the phrase "seeks after God" means to have a relationship with God. AT: "No one sincerely tries to have a right relationship with God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **They have all turned away** - This is an idiom that means the people do not even want to think about God. They want to avoid him. AT: "They have all turned away from God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They together have become useless** - Since no one does what is good, they are useless to God. AT: "Everyone has become useless to God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
