===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There is none who understands** - AT: "No one really understands God's truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **There is none who seeks after God** - AT: "No one sincerely tries to have a righteous relationship with God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **turned away** - Here this means to stop doing something. AT: "rejected God and his righteous will for them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **have become useless** - AT: "have become worthless as far as God's will for them is concerned"
