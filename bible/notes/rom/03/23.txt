===== translationNotes: =====

  * **come short of the glory of God** - Here the "glory of God" is a metonym that refers to the image of God and his nature. AT: "have failed to be like God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **they are freely justified by his grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus** - Here "justified" refers to being made right with God. You can translate this in an active form. AT: "God has made them right with himself as a free gift, because Christ Jesus has set them free" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
