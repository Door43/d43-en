===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he…his**  - "God...God's"
  * **containers of wrath...containers of mercy**  - AT: "people who deserve wrath...people who deserve mercy" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **riches of his glory**  - AT: "his glory, which is of great value"
  * **which he previously prepared for glory**  - AT: "whom he prepared ahead of time to give glory"
  * **also us**  - Paul and fellow believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **called** - Here this means God has appointed or chosen people to be his children, to be his servants and proclaimers of his message of salvation through Jesus
