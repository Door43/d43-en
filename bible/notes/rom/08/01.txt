===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul gives the answer to the struggle he has with sin and good.

  * **There is therefore now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus** - Here "condemnation" refers to punishing people. AT: "God will not condemn and punish those who are joined to Christ Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **therefore** - "for that reason" or "because what I have just told you is true" 
  * **the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus** - This refers to God's Spirit. AT: "God's Spirit in Christ Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **the law of sin and death** - This refers to our sinful nature that forces us to sin.
