\v 29 They strongly desire to do all kinds of unrighteous deeds. They strongly desire to do all kinds of evil things to others. They strongly desire to take things that belong to others. They strongly desire to harm others in various ways. Many are constantly envying other people. Many constantly desire to murder people. Many constantly desire to cause arguments and quarrels between people. Many constantly desire to deceive others. Many constantly desire to speak hatefully about others. Many speak evil things about others.
