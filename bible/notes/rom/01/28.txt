===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As they did not approve of having God in their awareness** - AT: "They did not think it was necessary to know God"
  * **they...their...them** - the "mankind" of [[en:bible:notes:rom:01:18|1:18]]
  * **he gave them up to a depraved mind** - AT: "God allowed their mind filled with immorality to control them"  
  * **not proper** - AT: "disgraceful" or "indecent" or "sinful"
