===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sin...deceived me, and...killed me.**  - As in [[:en:bible:notes:rom:07:07|7:7-8]], Paul is viewing sin as though it were a person who could act. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **taking the opportunity through the commandment**  - When God commands us not to do something, it is because we have been told not to do it that we want to do it more. Translate this the same way you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:rom:07:07|7:8]].â€‹
  * **through the commandment it killed me**  - "because I disobeyed God's commandments, I died spiritually"
