===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - The pronoun "I" refers to Paul.
  * **you…you…your** - The pronouns "you" and "your" refer to the Gentile believers.
  * **in order that you will not be wise in your own thinking** - "so that you will not think you are wiser than you are:" The Gentile believers may believe they are wiser than the Jewish unbelievers. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Implicit]])
  * **that a partial hardening has occurred** - Paul is saying that some of the Jews have hardened their hearts and refused to accept God's way of salvation. However, some Jews have believed and more will believe in Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
