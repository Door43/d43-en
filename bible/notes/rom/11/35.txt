===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **him**  - The pronoun "him" in 11:35 refers to the person who gives to God.
    * **so it will be paid back to him**  - AT: "so God will pay it back to him"
    * **him**  - All occurrences of "him" in 11:36 refers to God.
