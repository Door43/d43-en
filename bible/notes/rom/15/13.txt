===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the God of confidence** - Here "confidence" refers to one's ability to trust in God to do what he has promised to do. AT: "God, whom you trust to fulfill his promises" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **fill you with all joy and peace** - Paul exaggerates here to emphasize his point. AT: "fill you with great joy and peace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **you may abound in confidence** - "you may be completely confident"
