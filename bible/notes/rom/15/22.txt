===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul tells the believers in Rome about his personal plans to visit them and asks the believers to pray.

  * **I was also hindered** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "they also hindered me" or "people also hindered me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I no longer have any place in these regions** - Paul implies that there are no more places in these areas where people live who have not heard about Christ. AT: "there are no more places in these regions where people have not heard about Christ" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
