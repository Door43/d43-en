===== translationNotes: =====

  * **root of Jesse** - Jesse was the physical father of King David. AT: "descendant of Jesse" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The Gentiles will have confidence in him** - Here "him" refers to the descendant of Jesse, the Messiah. Those who are not Jews will also trust him to fulfill his promises. AT: "The people who are not Jews can trust him to do what he has promised" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
