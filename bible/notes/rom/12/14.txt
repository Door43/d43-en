===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Be of the same mind toward one another **  - AT: "Agree with one another" or "Live in unity with each other" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Do not think in proud ways**  - AT: "Do not think that you are more important than others"
  * **accept lowly people** - AT: "welcome people who do not seem important"
  * **Do not be wise in your own thoughts**  - AT: "Do not think of yourselves as having more wisdom than everyone else"
