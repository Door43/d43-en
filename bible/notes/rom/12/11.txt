===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Concerning diligence, do not be hesitant. Concerning the spirit, be eager. Concerning the Lord, serve him** - "Do not be lazy in your duty, but be eager to follow the Spirit and to serve the Lord"
  * **Rejoice in the confidence you have about the future** - Paul implies here that the believers can be confident that God will help them. AT: "Be happy because you are confident that God will always help you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Be patient in your troubles** - "Wait patiently whenever you have troubles"
  * **Share in the needs of the believers** - This is the last item in the list that began in [[:en:bible:notes:rom:12:09|12:9]]. "When fellow Christians are in trouble, help them with what they need"
  * **Find many ways to show hospitality** - "Always welcome them into your home when they need a place to stay"
